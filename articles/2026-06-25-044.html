Sky Equity Tops Out on Upper East Side Condos

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:29:21 +0000

SUMMARY

Sky Equity Group has topped out construction on its first Manhattan condominium development, a 60-unit project located at 260 E. 72nd St. on the Upper East Side. Sales for the luxury condominium are expected to launch in the fall. Sky Equity Group assumed the lead developer role for the project in 2024 following a restructuring and recapitalization.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Address: 260 E. 72nd St., New York City, NY

Units: 60

Project Notes: The site was previously overseen by Chetrit Group before Sky Equity Group assumed lead developer role in 2024.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Sky Equity Group — Simon Dushinsky, Founder and CEO

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Chetrit Group
