Terry Black’s BBQ owners plan $63M hotel in San Antonio

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 19:55:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Black Family Hospitality, led by the owners of Terry Black's BBQ, is planning a $63 million hotel and restaurant development in San Antonio. The project, named The Mandeville, will feature a three-story hotel with over 40 rooms, a Terry Black's BBQ outpost, a separate non-barbecue restaurant, and a 9,800-square-foot bathhouse called The Depths. Construction is slated to begin in September and conclude by June 2028.
