Greenstone Partners Brokers Sale of Skokie Retail Center

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:38:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Greenstone Partners has brokered the sale of Gross Point Crossing, a 19,000-square-foot retail center in Skokie, Illinois, for $3.875 million. The property was 67% occupied at the time of sale, offering a value-add opportunity through lease-up and rent optimization. The transaction highlights continued strong buyer interest in well-located, value-add retail assets in supply-constrained suburban markets.
