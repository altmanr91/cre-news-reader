RCLCO Fund Advisors Teams with Pension Fund for SFR Investments

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Tue, 12 May 2026 22:05:16 +0000

SUMMARY

RCLCO Fund Advisors (RFA) has established a programmatic joint venture with a top 50 domestic pension fund to invest in purpose-built single-family rental communities across various U.S. markets. The partnership aims to capitalize on the strong demand for high-quality, affordable single-family housing, with an initial acquisition of an 82-home community. The strategy targets communities ranging from 50 to 250 homes, located within a 30-minute drive of major employment centers, and primarily featuring three-bedroom-plus products, often with amenities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Units: 82

Project Notes: Initial investment for a programmatic joint venture targeting core-plus returns.

EQUITY/FINANCING: Top 50 domestic pension fund
