Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of 49-Room Hotel in Clarendon, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of a 49-room Best Western Plus hotel in Clarendon, Texas. The property, built in 2004 on 2.4 acres, features an indoor pool, business center, fitness center, and meeting space. Brokers with Marcus & Millichap represented the seller and procured the buyer in this transaction.
