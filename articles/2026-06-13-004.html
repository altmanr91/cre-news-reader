Midwood Pays $41M for West Village Retail Swath at 403 Avenue of the Americas

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 16:58:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Midwood Investment & Development has acquired a collection of retail storefronts in Manhattan's West Village for $41 million in an all-cash transaction. The properties, located at 403 and 405 Avenue of the Americas and 1 Greenwich Avenue, were purchased from Millsmith, an entity with ties to Samco Properties. This acquisition expands Midwood's presence in the West Village, adding to its diverse national portfolio of retail, office, residential, and development assets.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 403 Avenue of the Americas, New York City, NY

Sale Price: $41.0M

Notable Features: Located at the corner of Greenwich Avenue, running toward Christopher Street, two blocks northwest of Washington Square Park.

Project Notes: The seller, Millsmith, acquired the commercial plot in 2007.
