Tech Firm Bluebook Cities Signs 6K-SF Lease at 412 Broadway

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 21:00:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Bluebook Cities, a technology firm focused on self-governed communities, has signed a 6,300-square-foot lease for the entire top floor at 412 Broadway in Manhattan's Tribeca neighborhood. The asking rent for the space was $50 per square foot. The landlord's broker noted a significant increase in demand from AI and AI-adjacent technology companies in Tribeca over the past year, indicating a strong market for such firms.
