D-Tech Signs 83,482 SF Industrial Lease in West San Antonio

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 15:09:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Divine Technologies Aerospace Corp. (D-Tech) has signed an 83,482-square-foot industrial lease at Mid-City Business Park in San Antonio. The transaction involved Welcome Group as the landlord and was facilitated by Partners Real Estate, with brokers representing both parties.
