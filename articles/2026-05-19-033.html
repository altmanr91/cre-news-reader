Hillwood, Vanderbilt take larger bite of marquee Las Colinas office complex

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 18 May 2026 19:44:59 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Vanderbilt Office Properties and Hillwood Urban has increased its ownership stake in the 1.4 million-square-foot Towers at Williams Square office complex in Irving, Texas. The transaction, which also brought in TriPost Capital Partners as a new equity partner, represents one of the largest office trades in Dallas-Fort Worth this year. The property, known for its prominent location in the Las Colinas master-planned community and its strong tenant mix, was financed by Beal Bank.

DATA POINTS

Address: 5205 North O’Connor Boulevard, Irving, TX

Site Area: 18 acres

Notable Features: Three-building office campus with two 14-story buildings and a 26-story high-rise, known for its bronze mustang statues and located in the Las Colinas master-planned community.

Project Notes: The previous ownership group acquired the building for $330 million in 2015 and completed a $30 million renovation in 2024.
