Triten Real Estate Delivers 400,000 SF Distribution Center in Humble, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 15:09:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Triten Real Estate Partners has completed Kenswick at 1960, a two-building, 400,000-square-foot distribution center in Humble, Texas. The project features 36-foot clear heights, office space, and extensive parking, including over five acres of outdoor storage. CBRE has been appointed as the leasing agent for the newly delivered industrial facility.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Kenswick at 1960

Address: 1960 Kenswick, Humble, TX

Size: 400,000 SF

Notable Features: Two-building distribution center with 36-foot clear heights, 9,000 square feet of office space, and over five acres of outdoor storage.
