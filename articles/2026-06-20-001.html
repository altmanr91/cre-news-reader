Skanska Breaks Ground on $134M Academic Project at Texas A&M University in College Station

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 14:55:11 +0000

SUMMARY

Skanska has commenced construction on the $134 million AgriLife Meat Science & Technology Building at Texas A&M University in College Station. This 85,600-square-foot academic facility will feature modern labs, classrooms, and processing spaces for research and education, along with a public retail outlet. The project is slated for completion in 2028 and will anchor a new agriculture district on the university's West Campus.
