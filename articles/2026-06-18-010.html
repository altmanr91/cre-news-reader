JLL Negotiates Sale of 655,852 SF Industrial Portfolio Near Port of Virginia

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 14:30:49 +0000

SUMMARY

NorthPoint Development has acquired a four-building industrial portfolio totaling 655,852 square feet near the Port of Virginia in the Hampton Roads region. The properties, built between 2005 and 2012, were 82 percent occupied by 10 tenants at the time of sale. JLL represented the seller, an affiliate of Equus Capital Partners Ltd., in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 6900, 6920 and 6950 Harbour View Blvd, Suffolk, VA; 2601 Indian River Road, Chesapeake, VA

Size: 655,852 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 82.0%

Notable Features: Portfolio of distribution facilities located near the Port of Virginia.

Project Notes: Properties were built between 2005 and 2012.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: NorthPoint Development

SELLER: Equus Capital Partners Ltd.

SELLER BROKER: JLL — Bill Prutting; Chris Dale; Craig Childs; Ginna Wallace; Andrew Baquero
