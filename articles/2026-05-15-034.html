Distressed Loop LaSalle Street office high-rise hits market

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 14 May 2026 15:44:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A distressed, half-empty office tower at 2 North LaSalle Street in Chicago's Loop is being marketed for sale by JLL. The 26-story, 713,000-square-foot property, currently owned by special servicer Torchlight Loan Services, is only 50% occupied, with the city of Chicago as its primary tenant. The sale is banking on the "Google effect" and recent area momentum to attract a buyer willing to take on the property's challenges, including a nearly $138 million loan balance, despite its prime location near City Hall and the redeveloped Thompson Center.
