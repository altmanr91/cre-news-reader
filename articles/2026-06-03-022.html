Tishman Speyer fully leases former Macy’s building

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Tishman Speyer has fully leased The Wheeler, a 10-story office redevelopment in Downtown Brooklyn, with Brooklyn Defender Services signing a 31-year lease for 212,000 square feet. This transaction brings the building to full occupancy, concluding a multi-year leasing effort for the property which was built atop a former Macy's department store.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: The Wheeler

Address: 422 Fulton Street, Brooklyn, NY

Size: 212,000 SF

Current Occupancy: 100.0%

Notable Features: Office redevelopment built atop a former Macy's flagship store, preserving the cast-iron facade.

Project Notes: Tishman Speyer acquired the upper portion of the property from Macy's in 2015 for $270 million. The project secured a $301 million refinancing from Starwood Property Trust in 2024.
