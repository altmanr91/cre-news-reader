CoStar to Acquire Homebuilding Data and Marketplace Platform Zonda for $800M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 29 May 2026 17:23:47 +0000

SUMMARY

CoStar Group has announced an $800 million agreement to acquire Bora and its subsidiary, Zonda, a property and construction data provider, from private equity firm MidOcean Partners. This strategic acquisition, paid in cash, will expand CoStar's presence in the residential real estate data and software market, building on its previous acquisitions of Homesnap, Homes.com, and Matterport. Zonda offers a subscription-based data platform, virtual home tour software, and new construction discovery platforms, serving over 3,000 customers in the construction industry.

DATA POINTS

Sale Price: $800.0M

Project Notes: Acquisition expected to close in the second half of 2026.
