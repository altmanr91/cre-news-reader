Design and Preconstruction Startup LightTable Raises $22M Series A

Commercial Observer — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 12:45:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

LightTable, a startup focused on reducing design and preconstruction risk through an AI-native quality assurance and control platform, has successfully raised a $22 million Series A funding round. The company aims to scale its product nationally and expand its architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) support team. The platform identifies errors and omissions in construction drawings, which typically lead to costly change orders, requests for information, and potential lawsuits during the construction phase. LightTable's CEO highlighted the company's ability to identify significantly more critical issues in drawings compared to general AI models, attributing this to their integration of AEC experts with AI technology.
