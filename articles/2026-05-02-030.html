Burk Interests tweaks Preston Hollow high-rise zoning request

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Burk Interests and Greenway Investment Company have submitted a revised zoning proposal for their $800 million mixed-use project in Dallas, scaling back the height of the planned high-rises in response to local opposition. The updated plan for the 7-acre site at 5924 Royal Lane now proposes a maximum height of 299 feet, reducing the tallest tower to 25 stories and the second tower to 21 stories. The project will feature a hotel, condominiums, luxury apartments, office space, retail, dining, and significant green space, and is currently under review by city staff.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

8300 Douglas Avenue: Mixed-use complex with a 12-story office tower and a 17-story residential high-rise, developed by Ramrock Real Estate, Lincoln Property Company, HKS, and Willow Bridge Property Management.
