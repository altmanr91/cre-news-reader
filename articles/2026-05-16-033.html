Land purchases inch embattled Thakkar family’s Mustang Square closer to completion

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 15 May 2026 13:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Thakkar Developers has sold additional commercial and residential parcels within its Mustang Square mixed-use project in Plano, TX, bringing the development closer to full build-out. Foundry Plano, an office development company, acquired a 3.5-acre lot to construct 13 luxury office condominiums. Separately, homebuilder D.R. Horton purchased four more homesites, adding to its existing holdings in the residential section of the development. The sales occur as Thakkar Developers faces ongoing legal challenges, including a tax lawsuit in New York and federal fraud charges against its principals.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Mustang Square

Address: 4524 Community Ct, Plano, TX*

Site Area: 3.5 acres

Project Notes: The residential component of the development was previously planned for townhomes but changed to detached single-family homes. A fitness center is also under construction within the development.
