The Missner Group celebrates completion of 180,000-square-foot industrial development in Chicago’s Fuller Park neighborhood

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 10:49:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Missner Group, in partnership with Thackeray Partners, has completed a 180,000-square-foot speculative industrial development in Chicago's Fuller Park neighborhood. The project, located within the Chicago Stockyards Industrial Park, is designed to accommodate up to four tenants and is pursuing LEED Silver certification. This development addresses the ongoing demand for modern industrial and logistics space on Chicago's South Side, leveraging the area's established infrastructure and labor pool.

DATA POINTS
