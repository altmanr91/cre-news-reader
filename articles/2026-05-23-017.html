Ex-Washington Commanders owner relists massive estate for $10M off

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 22 May 2026 14:34:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

Former Washington Commanders owner Dan Snyder and his wife Tanya have relisted their River View estate in Alexandria, Virginia, for $49.9 million, a $10 million reduction from its initial asking price in June 2024. The Snyders acquired the 16.5-acre property in October 2021 for $48 million, which remains the most expensive residential sale in the Washington, D.C. area's history. The price adjustment reflects a strategic move to attract a buyer as the couple continues to divest their real estate holdings in the region following the sale of their football team in 2023.
