Ashford Hospitality Trust sells another hotel in its expansive portfolio for $17M

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 15 May 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Ashford Hospitality Trust has sold the 150-key Embassy Suites by Hilton Dallas to DG Lodging for $17 million, or approximately $133,000 per key. This transaction is part of Ashford's strategy to divest six hotels from its portfolio, with this sale marking the fifth completed disposition since April. The REIT aims to streamline its holdings, with one additional sale pending.
