Contract inked for Oceana PH asking $24M tops slow week in Miami-Dade

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 16:14:08 +0000

SUMMARY

A penthouse at Oceana Bal Harbour, asking nearly $24 million, secured a buyer during an otherwise slow summer week in Miami-Dade County. The 4,900-square-foot upper penthouse with four bedrooms and six bathrooms was listed for $23.95 million, or nearly $4,900 per square foot. The property previously sold in 2021 for $15 million.
