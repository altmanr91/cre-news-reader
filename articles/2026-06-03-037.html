Paceline moves deeper into San Jose office market with $33M buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 21:15:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Paceline Investors, in partnership with New York Life Insurance, has acquired a 142,700-square-foot office building at 350 East Plumeria Drive in San Jose for $33 million. The property, formerly a tech company headquarters, was sold by Galaxy Properties and is currently available for lease. This acquisition marks Paceline's continued investment in the San Jose office market, following another recent purchase in the area.

Overall office vacancy in the San Jose region dropped from 16.1 percent to 15.4 percent in the first quarter. North San Jose had a vacancy rate of 16.7 percent across more than 15 million square feet of office space.
