Housing Notes: Bed Bath & Beyond… Brokerage?

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 19:35:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

This article discusses the acquisition of Fathom Holdings, a cloud-based residential real estate brokerage, by Bed Bath & Beyond (BBB), which has relaunched as an online home retailer. The author argues that this acquisition is not unusual, fitting into a broader trend of companies seeking to own the entire customer journey in the homebuying space. This strategy, dubbed "Everything Home" by BBB, aims to integrate services like home purchase, financing, insurance, furnishing, and ongoing home services. The article highlights similar efforts by other industry players like Opendoor, Zillow, ICE, and Compass, all striving to create comprehensive ecosystems or "super apps" to streamline the homebuying process and capture greater market share. However, the author notes the significant challenge in these integrated models: real estate agents' reluctance to adopt in-house ancillary services due to perceived risks, additional burdens, and a desire to maintain control over their client relationships and commission splits.
