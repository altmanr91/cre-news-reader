Peachtree Group Provides $32M Construction Loan for Alabama Hotel

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Fri, 22 May 2026 15:36:50 +0000

SUMMARY

Peachtree Group has provided a $32.36 million construction loan to Spandrel Development Partners for the development of a 154-room AC Hotel by Marriott in Huntsville, AL. The hotel will be located within the Bridge Street Town Centre, a part of Cummings Research Park. The project is expected to benefit from strong demand drivers in the area, including government and defense-related activities, as well as significant economic and employment growth over the past decade.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Huntsville, AL has experienced 23% employment growth over the past decade. Recent economic boosts include Blue Origin's expansion in Cummings Research Park and Eli Lilly and Company's new $6 billion manufacturing facility. The U.S. Space Command is also developing a permanent headquarters at Redstone Arsenal.
