HUD Section 223(f) Loan Refinances Chula Vista Multifamily

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Wed, 27 May 2026 21:44:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Walker & Dunlop arranged a $101.6 million HUD Section 223(f) loan to refinance Enclave Heritage Flats, a 312-unit multifamily community in Chula Vista. This financing, secured for The Baldwin Company, replaces existing debt that Walker & Dunlop had previously arranged in 2024. The transaction highlights the HUD Section 223(f) program's appeal for high-quality multifamily assets, offering long-term, fixed-rate financing with favorable leverage and execution certainty.

DATA POINTS
