Wells Fargo Provides $46M Refi for SoCal Shopping Center

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 21:55:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Wells Fargo has provided $46 million in fixed-rate, interest-only permanent financing for Rosemead Place, a 336,718-square-foot retail center in Rosemead, CA. The shopping center, anchored by a major retailer and featuring a strong tenant mix, is owned by MPM Partners. The transaction was completed in less than 60 days, despite some near-term lease rollover, reflecting the strong lender appetite for well-located, anchored retail assets.
