Bain, 11North spend $300M on open-air retail portfolio

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 28 May 2026 17:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Bain Capital Real Estate and 11North Partners has acquired a portfolio of five open-air retail centers across multiple states for approximately $300 million. The portfolio, totaling 757,000 square feet, boasts a strong tenant mix and 93 percent occupancy. This acquisition aligns with the partners' strategy of investing in grocery-anchored retail, which they view as offering compelling risk-adjusted fundamentals in the current real estate market.
