Major League Soccer Unveils Global Headquarters at Vornado’s PENN 2

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:18:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Major League Soccer (MLS) has established its new global headquarters at Vornado Realty Trust's PENN 2 in Midtown Manhattan. The 126,000-square-foot, two-floor space is designed by TPG Architecture with an "office as a stadium" concept, featuring elements like a "player arrival" entry and a "Midfield" collaboration hub. JLL, which represented MLS in the long-term lease, noted that PENN 2's extensive amenities, its location within the evolving Penn District, and the lease's long-term flexibility made it an ideal choice for the league.
