Matt Mullins Launches Bricks and Brand, a Strategic Real Estate Advisory Firm Bridging Market Insight, Development, and Brand Execution

REJournals — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 13:02:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Matt Mullins, a real estate veteran with 25 years of experience, has launched Bricks and Brand, a new advisory firm. The firm aims to provide strategic consulting services to private developers, investors, landowners, and public-sector clients across Minnesota, the Midwest, and nationwide. Bricks and Brand differentiates itself by integrating traditional real estate advisory with brand strategy, storytelling, and go-to-market execution, ensuring projects are financially viable and thoughtfully positioned to resonate with target audiences and communities.
