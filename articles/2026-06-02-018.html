Christie’s International dumps tri-state affiliate

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 17:20:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Christie's International Real Estate has terminated its franchise agreement with Christie's International Real Estate Group LLC, the exclusive affiliate for New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut. This decision, made by the brokerage's parent company, impacts an operation that Compass acquired in December 2024 as part of a larger $450 million deal. The former affiliate, led by Ilija Pavlović, encompasses approximately 30 offices and over 1,000 agents. Christie's International Real Estate stated the termination was in the best interest of its brand and global network, reaffirming its commitment to reestablishing its presence in the New York and Northern New Jersey markets.
