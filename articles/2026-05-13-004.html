Realterm Acquires Bronx IOS Site at 935 Garrison Avenue

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 12 May 2026 20:01:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Realterm, an investment manager specializing in transportation-focused properties, has acquired a 1-acre industrial outdoor storage (IOS) site at 935 Garrison Avenue in the Bronx's Hunts Point neighborhood. This acquisition marks Realterm's fifth industrial property in the Bronx, a market characterized by strong demand for IOS facilities due to e-commerce growth and its critical role in the city's supply chain. The property includes a 9,400-square-foot maintenance building and is currently vacant, with Realterm planning capital improvements to attract tenants.

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Steven Siegel; Philip Biancavilla; Jakub Nowak

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Demand for industrial outdoor storage (IOS) facilities has grown over the last few years due to the expansion of e-commerce and the need for efficient, cost-effective storage solutions. The Bronx, particularly Hunts Point, is a core node in New York City's supply chain with minimal supply of functional IOS maintenance facilities and significant barriers to developing comparable sites.
