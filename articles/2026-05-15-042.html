Pegasus puts commercial leasing data toward AI joint venture

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 14 May 2026 15:35:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Two Los Angeles-based companies, Fore Enterprise and Pegasus, have formed a joint venture called Fore Real to streamline tedious and costly commercial real estate tasks using artificial intelligence. Fore Real offers services including property tax appeals, insurance compliance, lease abstraction, and tenant communications. Pegasus, a commercial real estate advisory platform with $4 billion in assets under management, contributed its extensive portfolio data to train the AI models, while Fore Enterprise provided its technical AI expertise. The collaboration aims to achieve high accuracy in automated processes, with Fore Real claiming 98-99% accuracy, which Pegasus CEO David Chasin affirmed as "closest to perfection." The platform employs a multi-agent AI approach with human review to ensure reliability, addressing concerns about AI accuracy that have deterred firms from adopting other solutions.
