Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 64-unit apartment property in Lawrenceburg

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 14 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has facilitated the sale of Waterview Apartments, a 64-unit multifamily property in Lawrenceburg, Indiana. The transaction involved a Wisconsin-based limited partnership as the seller and a Wisconsin-based LLC as the buyer. The property, built in 2012, offers a mix of one-, two-, and three-bedroom units.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Waterview Apartments

Address: 1200 Sycamore Estates Drive, Lawrenceburg, IN

Units: 64

Year Built: 2012

Site Area: 16 acres

Notable Features: Amenities include a clubhouse, fitness center, business center, picnic area, and playground.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Matthew Whiteside, Investment Specialist; Julia Evinger, Indiana Broker of Record

SELLER: Wisconsin-based limited partnership
