Jeffrey Soffer and Barry Sternlicht Land $104M for Oceanfront Condo in South Florida

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 20:00:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Fontainebleau Development and Starwood Capital Group have secured $104 million in financing for their 1 Homes Jupiter Island luxury condominium project in Tequesta, FL. BDT & MSD Partners provided the debt, which refinances an existing loan and adds approximately $29 million in new capital. The project, located on a 5.4-acre oceanfront site, will feature 26 units with prices ranging from $11 million to $27 million.

Notable Features: Oceanfront luxury condo development near Cato's Bridge, across the Intracoastal Waterway from Jupiter.

Project Notes: The 26-unit development is part of a larger site where local officials have also approved plans for another 34-unit building. The developers previously acquired two existing condo buildings on the property between 2021 and 2022 for a combined $130 million. An $80.5 million loan was provided by Motcomb Estates in 2022.
