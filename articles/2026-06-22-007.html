Trident Capital Group Grows Columbus-Area Industrial Portfolio by 1.9 MSF

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:52:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Trident Capital Group has significantly expanded its industrial footprint in the greater Columbus, Ohio region through a series of five transactions and developments, adding 1.9 million square feet to its portfolio. This expansion includes the completion and full lease-up of Columbus Works Commerce Center Phase I, the acquisition of the Ease Logistics Distribution Center, and the ongoing construction of Columbus Works Commerce Center Phase II and two large speculative industrial projects in Lockbourne, Rickenbacker Industrial Center II and III, developed in partnership with O'Connor Capital Partners.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Columbus Works Commerce Center Phase I (Building C): 191,077-square-foot industrial facility completed in Q4 2025 and fully leased to three tenants.

Columbus Works Commerce Center Phase II (Building D): 177,607-square-foot industrial facility under construction on 16.5 acres, featuring 32-foot clear height, 21 dock doors, and 144 car parking spaces.

Ease Logistics Distribution Center: 308,358-square-foot build-to-suit facility in Marysville, delivered in 2024 and fully leased to Ease Logistics Services LLC, situated on 25 acres within 33 Innovation Park.

Rickenbacker Industrial Center III: 958,740-square-foot speculative industrial project under construction in Lockbourne, slated for completion in Q1 2027, featuring 40-foot clear height, 104 dock doors, and potential rail access.

Rickenbacker Industrial Center II: 303,670-square-foot industrial facility in Lockbourne delivering in June 2026, featuring 32-foot clear height, 30 dock doors, and 76 trailer stalls.
