CenterPoint Acquires Two Industrial Buildings Totaling 192,318 SF in South Houston

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 26 May 2026 14:34:17 +0000

SUMMARY

CenterPoint Investment Management has acquired two newly constructed, fully leased shallow-bay industrial buildings totaling 192,318 square feet in South Houston. The properties offer frontage along Beltway 8 and include a significant outdoor storage yard, appealing to industrial users seeking modern facilities with strategic access.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 11217 Telephone Road, Houston, TX

Size: 192,318 SF

Year Built: 2024

Notable Features: The buildings feature 28-foot clear heights, a combined 52 dock-high doors, four drive-in doors, and a 1.3-acre outdoor storage yard, with frontage along Beltway 8.

Project Notes: The buildings were fully leased at the time of sale.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: CenterPoint Investment Management
