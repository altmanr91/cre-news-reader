Rudin makes splash outside of NYC with Stamford apartment buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Rudin, a family firm primarily known for its New York City holdings, is in contract to acquire 1 Harbor Point, a 239-unit multifamily complex in Stamford, Connecticut, from Building and Land Technology for approximately $150 million. This acquisition marks a significant expansion for Rudin outside its traditional market, with the property boasting 96% occupancy and proximity to public transit. The deal, if closed at the reported price, would value the property at over $627,000 per unit.
