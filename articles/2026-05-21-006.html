Walker & Dunlop Arranges $128.5M Acquisition Loan for Houston Apartment Complex

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 20 May 2026 15:09:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Walker & Dunlop has arranged a $128.5 million acquisition loan for The Arno, a 168-unit apartment complex in Houston's River Oaks neighborhood. The financing was secured through Hudson Bay Capital on behalf of Sade Real Estate, facilitating the acquisition of this multifamily asset.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Arno

Units: 168

Loan Amount: $128.5M

Loan Per Unit: $764,881/unit (calculated)

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

MORTGAGE BROKER: Walker & Dunlop — Reimer; Aaron Appel; Jonathan Schwartz; Keith Kurland; Adam Schwartz; Dustin Stolly; Sean Bastian

LENDER: Hudson Bay Capital

SPONSOR: Sade Real Estate
