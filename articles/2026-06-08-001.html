Canyon Partners Real Estate Adds Four Executives to Investment Platform

Commercial Observer — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 13:00:50 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Canyon Partners Real Estate has expanded its executive team with four key appointments, including Scott Swisher as head of Western region credit originations, Adam Ostrowsky as managing director of capital markets, Scott San Filippo as senior director of bridge debt, and Spencer Schlee as head of opportunistic equity. These strategic hires and promotions are intended to strengthen the firm's real estate platform and capitalize on opportunities in both lending and opportunistic investing, reflecting an ongoing evolution of the firm's capabilities.
