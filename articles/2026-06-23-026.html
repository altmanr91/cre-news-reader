Austin sued by property owners over ETJ reversal affecting 170 properties

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Property owners in Travis County have filed a lawsuit against the city of Austin, alleging that the city unlawfully reversed its decision to release 170 properties from its extraterritorial jurisdiction (ETJ). The plaintiffs contend that once the city issued initial notices of release, it lacked the authority to void those releases, which they claim was done based on a misinterpretation of a state law exception related to military bases. The lawsuit seeks to compel Austin to honor the original ETJ releases, which would allow developers to operate under county regulations rather than city ordinances.
