Domus lands approval for standard-breaking Fulton Market apartments

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Chicago City Council has approved a 29-story, 347-unit apartment tower in Fulton Market, a decision that could establish a new density benchmark for the area. The project, developed by Domus Real Estate Group, received a DX-16 zoning designation, typically reserved for skyscrapers, after the developer acquired an adjacent parcel for a public park, fulfilling a condition of the city's approval. While some critics expressed concern that this high-density approval deviates from previous planning efforts and could strain infrastructure, proponents highlighted the urgent need for more housing in the Chicago area and the benefit of additional green space.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 215 North Racine Avenue, Chicago, IL

Units: 347

Notable Features: 29-story apartment tower with a public park component.

Project Notes: The DX-16 zoning designation is exclusively tied to the current ownership entity with a "no-flip" clause, preventing Domus from selling the property.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Domus Real Estate Group
