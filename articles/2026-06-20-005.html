Cushman & Wakefield adds director to St. Louis office

REJournals — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 15:34:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Cushman & Wakefield has appointed Matt Hiatt as Director in its St. Louis office, where he will join the office leasing team. With over two decades of commercial real estate experience, Hiatt will provide advisory services to both occupiers and landlords in St. Louis and other national markets. His expertise includes negotiation, site selection, sales, and portfolio strategy, focusing on delivering client-centric real estate solutions.
