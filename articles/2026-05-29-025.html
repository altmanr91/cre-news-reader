MIA pays $19M for Duty Free warehouse amid $14B airport expansion

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 28 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Miami International Airport (MIA) acquired a 68,000-square-foot industrial warehouse from Duty Free Americas for $19.2 million. The property, located at 1800 Northwest 70th Avenue, will initially serve as indoor storage and a construction staging site for MIA's extensive $14 billion modernization and expansion plan. Following the completion of the modernization work, the airport intends to reposition the asset for revenue-generating commercial uses. The transaction, which took nearly two years to finalize due to various hurdles including permitting and zoning, was brokered by WGS Realty.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Miami-Dade's industrial market saw 3.2 million square feet leased in Q1, with asking rents rising 1.5% year-over-year to $17.04 per square foot. Industrial developments completed during the quarter totaled 1.4 million square feet, increasing vacancy slightly to 7.1% from 6.9%. Another 2.9 million square feet of industrial space is currently under construction across South Florida. Industrial cap rates in South Florida averaged 6.3% in Q1.
