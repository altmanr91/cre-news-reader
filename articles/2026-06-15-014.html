CBRE brokers sale of 112,862-square-foot industrial property in Elkhart

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Vault Equity Partners has acquired a fully leased 112,862-square-foot industrial property in Elkhart, Indiana, from Industrial Commercial Properties (ICP). The property is part of the redeveloped Concord Business Park, a former mall that ICP converted into a modern industrial facility. This transaction highlights ICP's successful strategy of transforming obsolete retail spaces into industrial hubs, leveraging existing infrastructure and creating new opportunities for manufacturing and distribution.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 3701 S. Main Street, Elkhart, IN

Size: 112,862 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Notable Features: Redeveloped from a former mall, featuring increased clear heights, new loading docks, and drive-in doors.

Project Notes: The property was part of the former Concord Mall, which was redeveloped into Concord Business Park.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Vault Equity Partners
