Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of 306-Unit Self-Storage Facility in Denison, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 14:39:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of A-Alpha Self Storage, a 306-unit facility in Denison, Texas. The property, built between 1996 and 2008, spans 41,959 net rentable square feet. The transaction involved an undisclosed seller and a Dallas-Fort Worth-based owner-operator as the buyer.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: self-storage

Property Name: A-Alpha Self Storage

Address: 2216 Woodlawn Blvd, Denison, TX*

Size: 41,959 SF

Year Built: 1996

Site Area: 4.2 acres

Notable Features: Includes five permanent shipping containers and one office space.

Project Notes: Built in phases between 1996 and 2008.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
