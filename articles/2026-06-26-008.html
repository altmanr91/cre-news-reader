PCC Acquires 252-Unit Multifamily Complex in San Antonio, Plans Capital Improvements

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 15:12:05 +0000

SUMMARY

Platte Canyon Capital (PCC) has acquired Northwood Apartments, a 252-unit multifamily complex in New Braunfels, northeast of San Antonio. The investment firm plans to undertake capital improvements to the amenity spaces, building exteriors, and parking areas. The property was 70 percent occupied at the time of sale.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Northwood Apartments

Address: 902 N Academy Ave, New Braunfels, TX*

Units: 252

Occupancy at Sale: 70.0%

Year Built: 1998

Notable Features: Built in phases between 1998 and 2017, offering one- and two-bedroom units.

Project Notes: The seller was the property's original developer.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Platte Canyon Capital (PCC)
