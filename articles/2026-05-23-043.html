Grocery REIT Considering Sale After Unsolicited Takeover Bid

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 22 May 2026 12:33:07 -0400

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Slate Grocery REIT, a Canadian investor with a 15.2M SF portfolio of grocery-anchored properties across 23 U.S. states, is exploring strategic alternatives, including a potential sale, after receiving an unsolicited takeover bid from affiliates of its external manager, Slate Asset Management. The REIT's board has formed a special committee to evaluate options aimed at maximizing unitholder value, noting that the public market valuation may not fully reflect the underlying portfolio's worth. This move comes amidst a trend of public landlord mergers and acquisitions in the commercial real estate sector, driven by economic instability and a valuation gap between public and private real estate assets.
