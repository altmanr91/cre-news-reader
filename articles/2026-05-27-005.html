Gary Barnett’s Extell Buys 165 East 56th Street for $39M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 26 May 2026 17:49:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Extell Development, led by Gary Barnett, has acquired the eight-story office building at 165 East 56th Street in Midtown for $39 million from the American Jewish Committee (AJC). The acquisition is part of Extell's ongoing strategy to assemble properties along the East 55th Street corridor for potential redevelopment, with the site marketed for office-to-residential conversion. The seller, AJC, had owned the property since 1959 and plans to relocate its offices.
