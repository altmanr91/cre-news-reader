Detroit’s Invictus Builders adds brother team to board of directors

REJournals — Fri, 29 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Invictus Builders LLC, a Detroit-based commercial construction management firm, has appointed brothers Nate Beene and Dr. Justin Beene to its board of directors and investor group. This strategic move is part of the company's "East-meets-West" leadership vision, aiming to expand its footprint across Michigan and the Midwest. The Beene brothers bring extensive experience in workforce solutions, construction entrepreneurship, and community leadership, having previously collaborated with Invictus on the 1515 Madison Center of Innovation project.
