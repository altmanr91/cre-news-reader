Investor Offloads Dunwoody Office Park for $27M

SALE

Connect CRE — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:04:20 +0000

SUMMARY

Grubb Properties has sold the 284,000-square-foot Park at Perimeter Center East in Dunwoody, GA, to Network Realty Partners for $27.15 million. Grubb Properties had acquired the 19.4-acre site in 2015 for $32.65 million and had previously attempted to redevelop the property with a large mixed-use project, but those plans did not receive local approval. The sale marks Grubb Properties' exit from this particular asset, as the company is also relocating its Atlanta office from the Dunwoody property to another office it owns in Smyrna.
