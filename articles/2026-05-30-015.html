St. Louis’ Lawrence Group adds head of finance and accounting

REJournals — Fri, 29 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

The Lawrence Group, a St. Louis-based architecture, design, and development firm, has appointed Susan Garvey as its new Head of Finance and Accounting. Garvey brings over two decades of financial leadership experience within the AEC industry, with a focus on aligning financial strategy with organizational objectives. In her new role, she will be responsible for overseeing the firm's financial operations and guiding strategic financial planning to support its ongoing growth and long-term success.
