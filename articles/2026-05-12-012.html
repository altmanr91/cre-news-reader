Realterm acquires industrial outdoor storage facility in Bridgeview

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Tue, 12 May 2026 09:47:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Realterm has acquired an industrial outdoor storage and maintenance facility located in Bridgeview, Illinois. The 3.9-acre property features a 28,985-square-foot building with 10 oversized drive-in doors and a 6,000-square-foot office, and is fully leased to a global HVAC and refrigeration solutions provider. This acquisition enhances Realterm's portfolio in a key industrial market with strong highway access and proximity to a major air cargo hub.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 9655 Industrial Drive, Bridgeview, IL

Size: 28,985 SF

Site Area: 3.9 acres

Notable Features: Industrial outdoor storage maintenance facility with 10 oversized drive-in doors and a 6,000-square-foot office. The site is fully secured with two points of ingress and egress and offers direct access to major highway infrastructure.
