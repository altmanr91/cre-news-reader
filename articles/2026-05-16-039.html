Hollywood Multifamily Sale Reaches Three-Year High in Pricing

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:24:40 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers brokered the sale of Sophia Hollywood, a 28-unit Class A multifamily community, for approximately $16 million. This transaction represents the highest price per unit achieved in Hollywood in over three years, signaling a growing trend among investors who view current market conditions as a buying opportunity. The property's newer construction, condominium-quality design, and larger floor plans were key differentiators, making it attractive to investors despite elevated development costs and interest rates.
