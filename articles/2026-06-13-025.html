$17M in escrow funds gone overnight: Palm Beach law firm sues bank over cyber attack

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 18:00:39 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A prominent Palm Beach real estate law firm, Rabideau Klein, is suing First Horizon Bank after more than $17 million was stolen from its escrow account in a cyber attack. The firm alleges the bank violated state law governing fund transfers, breached its contract, and was negligent, leading to the unrecovered loss of approximately $6.5 million. The lawsuit details how an unauthorized third party gained access to the firm's online banking platform, changed passwords, created new user IDs, and even obtained a new security token by impersonating a firm attorney, despite numerous red flags that the bank allegedly ignored.
