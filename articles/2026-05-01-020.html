Former Extell CEO snaps up Scarsdale retail hub from Saks Global

ACQUISITION

Latest New York Real Estate News — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Sagehall, a fund managed by former Extell Development CEO Sush Torgalkar, has acquired a 200,000-square-foot retail property in Scarsdale, New York, from Saks Global for $80 million. The transaction, which closed approximately three weeks ago, represents the second deal between Sagehall and Saks Global in recent months. The property, formerly home to a Saks OFF 5th and Lord & Taylor, is currently 64 percent occupied with a diverse mix of tenants.
