Foundry Park nears $202M for infrastructure from City Hall

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 16:06:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

JDL Development and Kanye Anderson Real Estate's $3 billion Foundry Park megadevelopment is poised to receive $202 million in tax-increment financing (TIF) from the city, pending approval from the Community Development Commission. These funds are designated to reimburse the developers for infrastructure improvements, including new roadways and park space, addressing critical issues that were also a concern for the site's previous development proposal. The project, located on the former Lincoln Yards site, will feature 3,200 residential units alongside retail and office components.
