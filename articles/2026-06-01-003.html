Patrick Southern of Serhant: 5 Questions

Commercial Observer — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 11:00:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article features an interview with Patrick Southern, a veteran residential broker who recently joined Serhant to expand his new development sales business in Jersey City, NJ. Southern discusses his 20-year career in the market, highlighting its evolution from an affordable option to a thriving urban center. He emphasizes his high-volume transaction approach, having closed 376 contracts in 2025, and explains how Serhant's platform provides the necessary support to pursue larger-scale projects that were previously out of reach.
