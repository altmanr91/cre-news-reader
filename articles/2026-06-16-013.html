SVN|Chicago Commercial brokers sale of 18,000-square-foot industrial property in Chicago

SALE

REJournals — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 09:40:00 +0000

SUMMARY

SVN|Chicago Commercial recently facilitated the sale of an 18,000-square-foot industrial property located at 3450 N. Kostner Ave. in Chicago for $1.433 million. The buyer, a Canadian millwork company, acquired the facility to support its expansion into the Chicago market. The property's robust infrastructure and strategic location were key factors in its appeal as an owner-user opportunity.
