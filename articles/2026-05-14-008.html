Kennedy Funding Provides $4.4M Loan for Refinancing of Residential Development Site in Spicewood, Texas

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:41:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Kennedy Funding has provided a $4.4 million loan for the refinancing of a 38.8-acre residential development site in Spicewood, Texas. The loan proceeds will be used by the undisclosed borrower to access capital for the next phase of the project, which consists of six adjoining parcels.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: land

Site Area: 38.8 acres

Project Notes: The site consists of six adjoining parcels.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LENDER: Kennedy Funding — Edwin Urrego
