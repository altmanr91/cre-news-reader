Highland Park Mediterranean-style mansion sells after asking $12M

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 06 May 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A Mediterranean-style mansion in Highland Park, Dallas, was sold by former finance and oil executive Lee Edgar Mikles and his wife Lori Mikles to Michael and Stacey Watson. The property, which last asked $11.5 million, was previously listed for nearly $12 million in September. The buyers secured an $8.1 million loan for the purchase.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Address: 3600 Beverly Drive, Dallas, TX

Size: 7,842 SF

Loan Amount: $8.1M

Loan Per SF: $1,033/SF (calculated)

Year Built: 2001

Site Area: 0.4 acres

Notable Features: Mediterranean-style home with five bedrooms, eight bathrooms, two wet bars, four fireplaces, and a second-story balcony overlooking a walled backyard.

Project Notes: The sellers previously acquired the home in 2023 from Paul Loyd, when it was asking $8 million.
