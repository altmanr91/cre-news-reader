San Jose La Quinta latest Bay Area hotels in default

LOAN

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 28 May 2026 18:46:06 +0000

SUMMARY

The La Quinta Inn & Suites in San Jose has defaulted on a $16 million construction loan provided by Central Valley Community Bank. The 59-room hotel, completed in 2023, now faces potential foreclosure. This default is part of a broader trend of distress in the Bay Area's lodging sector, with numerous hotels experiencing loan defaults and foreclosures since 2023.
