Judge orders $205M foreclosure on South Beach’s Goodtime Hotel

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 13 May 2026 20:20:12 +0000

SUMMARY

A Miami-Dade Circuit Court judge has ordered a $204.7 million foreclosure on the Goodtime Hotel in South Beach, following a lawsuit filed by lender CIM Group against the hotel's ownership entity, Washington Squared Owner. The judgment includes the principal balance, interest, default interest, late fees, and attorney fees. An auction for the property is scheduled for July 1, unless the owner pays the full amount prior to that date. The hotel, completed in 2021, has faced financial distress and other allegations of unpaid bills.
