Rosen roadblock? SL Green sues RFR over Madison Avenue building access

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

SL Green is suing RFR Holding to gain access to adjacent properties at 350 Madison Avenue and 10 East 45th Street, which SL Green claims is necessary to proceed with the demolition and below-grade construction phases of its planned 850,000-square-foot office tower at 346 Madison Avenue. SL Green alleges RFR has repeatedly ignored requests for access, causing significant delays to the project. The REIT is seeking a court-ordered two-year license to enter portions of RFR's properties to complete the work.
