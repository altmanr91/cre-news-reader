Court revives Eli Karp’s loan-to-own claim against Madison Realty Capital

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 04 May 2026 21:05:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A Brooklyn appellate court has reinstated Eli Karp's 2021 lawsuit against Madison Realty Capital, allowing the developer to pursue claims of a "loan-to-own" scheme related to the Hello Nostrand project. Karp alleges Madison made false promises to fund the development, deliberately pushing it into default to seize control. Madison Realty Capital ultimately acquired the property at 1580 Nostrand Avenue with a $70 million credit bid after Karp's plans for an apartment complex stalled, with only one rental building completed.
