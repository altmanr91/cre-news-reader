Pacific Urban Investors Acquires 140-Unit Multifamily Community in San Diego

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 13:20:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Pacific Urban Investors has acquired Casas by the Sea, a 140-unit oceanfront multifamily community in San Diego's Bird Rock/La Jolla neighborhood. The property, built in 1970, features a mix of studio, one-, two-, and three-bedroom apartments, along with two single-family homes. Approximately 86 percent of the units offer ocean views.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Casas by the Sea

Address: 5060 La Jolla Blvd, San Diego, CA*

Units: 140

Year Built: 1970

Notable Features: Oceanfront property adjacent to Tourmaline Beach; approximately 86 percent of units offer ocean views.
