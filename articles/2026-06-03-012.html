Lamar Companies, Mid-America boost leasing activity at Geneva retail center

LEASE

REJournals — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 10:02:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lamar Companies and Real Capital Solutions, in collaboration with Mid-America Asset Management, have significantly boosted leasing activity at Geneva Commons in Geneva, IL, securing 45,951 square feet of new retail and restaurant leases. This effort has increased the lifestyle center's occupancy from 88% to 95% since its acquisition in April 2024. The new leases and over 20 renewals, including long-term extensions with key retailers, underscore the property's strong market position and the ownership's successful strategy to enhance its tenant mix and appeal.

DATA POINTS

Notable Features: Anchored by Dick's Sporting Goods, Barnes & Noble, Sephora, Gap, and Banana Republic, the center benefits from strong surrounding traffic drivers including Fresh Market and Northwestern Medicine Delnor Hospital.

Project Notes: Lamar Companies previously acquired the 440,370 square foot lifestyle center in April 2024 for $63.75 million and has since invested over $2.4 million in capital and common area improvements.
