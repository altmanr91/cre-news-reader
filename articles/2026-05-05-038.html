Kennedy Wilson’s Mac Bernie Sees Deal Market Become Increasingly Competitive

Connect CRE — Mon, 04 May 2026 21:11:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Mac Bernie, managing director of Kennedy Wilson's debt investment group, observes an increasingly competitive deal market, particularly since early 2025, driven by the re-entry of banks, insurance companies, and other alternative lenders. He notes a shift towards more property sales compared to a few years ago when refinancing was more prevalent, with take-outs now split evenly between sales, agency/longer-term debt, and bridge financing. Kennedy Wilson maintains a consistent, conservative underwriting approach and specializes in senior non-recourse multifamily construction debt, typically lending $75 million to $150 million at 60-65% loan-to-cost, often allowing subordinate debt.
