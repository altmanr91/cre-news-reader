Lake Forest estate leads slower April for Chicagoland luxury homes

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 06 May 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Chicagoland's luxury residential market experienced a slowdown in April, with only four homes selling for over $5 million, a decrease from seven in March. The top transaction was the $6.3 million sale of a 7,300-square-foot English Country estate in Lake Forest, previously owned by the late Kraft Foods and Quaker Oats CEO Robert Morrison. Other notable sales included an off-market deal for a Lincoln Park home sold by Shannon Abloh for $6.1 million, a $5.5 million home in Winnetka's Hubbard Woods neighborhood, and a $5.1 million duplex at One Magnificent Mile sold by trading card mogul Matthew Bayer.
