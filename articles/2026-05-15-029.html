Edition Residences condo buyers sue Two Roads over stalled project

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 14 May 2026 18:34:26 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Three buyers are suing Two Roads Development to recover approximately $2.5 million in deposits for five units at the planned Edition Residences in Miami's Edgewater neighborhood. The buyers allege that the developer has anticipatorily breached their contracts, which stipulated a summer 2028 completion, as construction has not yet begun and it is now impossible to meet the deadline. The developer maintains that the claims are without merit and that they are moving forward with the project, having recently filed a certificate of termination for the existing Biscayne 21 condo association.
