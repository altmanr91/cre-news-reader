“90% there”: Controversial project outside UDB delayed again. But is it inching closer?

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:40:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

A controversial proposal for an industrial facility outside Miami-Dade County's Urban Development Boundary (UDB) has been delayed again, but appears to be inching closer to approval. Kelly Tractor, a heavy machinery supplier, seeks to relocate its headquarters to a 200-acre tract it owns, a move opposed by environmental groups due to its location beyond the UDB. The project, which would create approximately 1,300 jobs, requires a supermajority of nine votes from the county commission to proceed, and the developer is working to secure the final vote by addressing concerns about wetlands preservation and the necessity of building outside the UDB.
