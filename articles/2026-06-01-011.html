Hanley Investment Group closes $5.3 million sale of retail center in Terre Haute

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hanley Investment Group Real Estate Advisors facilitated the off-market sale of Eastview Commons, a newly constructed, fully occupied retail center in Terre Haute, Indiana, for $5.3 million. The property, anchored by a high-volume Chipotle, features a diverse tenant mix and benefits from its strategic location adjacent to a major retail anchor, drawing significant traffic to the area. The transaction highlights the continued investment interest in well-located, internet-resistant retail assets in growing markets.
