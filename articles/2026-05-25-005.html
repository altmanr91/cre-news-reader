Keeping up with the Thakkars, the embattled Dallas developer family

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Sun, 24 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

This article details the ongoing legal and development challenges faced by Dallas-based developer Poorvesh Thakkar. While he recently achieved a milestone with the sale of a portion of his Mustang Square mixed-use project in Plano, his development track record remains largely unproven due to numerous delayed or aborted projects. Concurrently, Thakkar is embroiled in a lawsuit in New York City, where the ground lease owner of a Midtown Manhattan office building he acquired is seeking his eviction for alleged unpaid property taxes and penalties totaling nearly $28 million. Thakkar, who purchased the building for a significant discount with plans for a residential conversion, has filed a countersuit, claiming he was hindered from his conversion project.
