Greenstone Partners lists office property in Chicago’s Woodlawn neighborhood

REJournals — Fri, 01 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Greenstone Partners is marketing a fully leased urban infill office property in Chicago's Woodlawn neighborhood for $4.75 million. The single-story building, occupied by a government tenant since 1993, offers stable income and significant future development potential due to its favorable zoning and large lot size. The listing agent highlighted the property's high cap rate and long-term government tenancy as key investment drivers.
