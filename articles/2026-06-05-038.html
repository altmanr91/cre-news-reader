Aggieland gives thumbs up to Terafab tax break

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 17:31:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Grimes County officials have approved a new tax investment zone and a 100% tax abatement for Terafab, a joint venture by Elon Musk's companies SpaceX, Tesla, and xAI. This move, which establishes SpaceX Reinvestment Zone No. 1, will eliminate property taxes on over a thousand acres designated for the semiconductor chip manufacturing facility. The project represents a significant investment in the rural county northwest of Houston, with an initial commitment of $55 billion that could potentially reach $119 billion upon full completion. A project representative indicated that Terafab would support local schools and cover road damages incurred during construction, while also committing to create approximately 1,800 jobs and generate its own power.
