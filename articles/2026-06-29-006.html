Marcus & Millichap Brokers $6.5M Sale of Cambridge Apartment Building

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 13:10:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of a 10-unit apartment building in Cambridge, Massachusetts, for $6.5 million. The 13,000-square-foot property, located across from Harvard University, was originally built in 1925 and features a mix of one-, two-, and five-bedroom apartments. Both the seller and buyer chose to remain anonymous in the transaction.
