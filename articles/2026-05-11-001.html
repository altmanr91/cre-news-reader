A Rare Offering of Scale: 325,000 RSF at 1285 Avenue of the Americas

Commercial Observer — Mon, 11 May 2026 04:00:40 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Lease

RXR is marketing a rare, large-scale office leasing opportunity at 1285 Avenue of the Americas in Midtown Manhattan. The offering comprises approximately 325,000 rentable square feet of contiguous space across five floors, providing a unique chance for global firms to establish a significant corporate presence in a prime business corridor. The building, designed by Skidmore, Owings & Merrill, offers expansive and flexible floor plates, high ceilings, and direct connectivity to Rockefeller Center, catering to the evolving needs of large tenants seeking a dominant headquarters in a prestigious location with extensive transit access.
