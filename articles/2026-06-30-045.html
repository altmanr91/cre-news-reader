CIM Group Combines With Nontraded REIT, Explores Public Offering

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 17:39:00 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

CIM Group, a Los Angeles-based real estate firm, has combined with its nontraded REIT, CIM Real Estate Finance Trust, in a strategic move that could lead to a public stock exchange listing. CIM Group transferred its assets and investments, taking a 67.5% share in the combined entity, which will operate under the name CIM Group Inc. This consolidation aims to create a larger, more flexible real assets manager with enhanced resources, while also providing an exit strategy for existing REIT shareholders and potentially opening CIM's portfolio to public investment.
