Us Construction’s Sophia Reyes: Inside the soaring demand for experiential retail and mixed-use projects

REJournals — Fri, 29 May 2026 10:14:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Sophia Reyes, Director of Relationships at Us Construction, highlights the robust demand for experiential retail and mixed-use developments, particularly in South Florida, driven by an influx of residents seeking live-work-play environments. She notes that institutional investors are readily financing these larger mixed-use projects, while smaller, independent retailers and restaurants face significant challenges in securing funding due to rising construction costs. Reyes observes a post-COVID surge in experiential retail, with consumers prioritizing interactive experiences over traditional bar or club settings, leading to a resurgence in physical retail spaces.
