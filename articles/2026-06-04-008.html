SurePoint Breaks Ground on 861-Unit Self-Storage Facility in The Woodlands, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 14:49:55 +0000

SUMMARY

SurePoint Development has broken ground on an 861-unit self-storage facility in The Woodlands, Texas. The project will feature 90,000 net rentable square feet of climate-controlled space and is expected to be completed in spring 2027. This marks SurePoint's tenth facility in the Houston metropolitan area.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: self-storage

Size: 90,000 SF

Units: 861

Completion: spring 2027

Notable Features: Climate-controlled space.

Project Notes: This is SurePoint's 10th facility in the Houston area.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: SurePoint Development

OWNER: ExtraSpace Storage
