It’s Still Early Innings for AI, Crypto in Commercial Real Estate: Forum

Commercial Observer — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 20:43:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

A recent forum hosted by Commercial Observer explored the nascent integration of AI and cryptocurrency within the commercial real estate sector. Grant Cardone of Cardone Capital discussed his hybrid investment model, which combines stable multifamily real estate returns with the volatility of Bitcoin, aiming to offer investors better, more stable returns than traditional REITs. Separately, a panel featuring experts from Lev, BXP, and Brookfield Asset Management emphasized a pragmatic approach to AI adoption, advocating for specialized, task-specific implementations to achieve cost and time efficiencies rather than broad, unspecialized deployments. The panelists highlighted the importance of establishing a unified data source and a robust framework to ensure AI systems learn and compound effectively over time.
