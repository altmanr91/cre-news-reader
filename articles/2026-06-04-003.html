AI Startup Phia Signs 9K-SF Lease at Zar Property’s 37 East 18th Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 21:22:03 +0000

SUMMARY

AI startup Phia, co-founded by Phoebe Gates and Sophia Kianni, has signed a lease for 8,900 square feet on the penthouse level of 37 East 18th Street in Manhattan. The deal represents a relocation for the company, which develops an AI-powered shopping assistant, and includes access to a portion of the building's roof deck. The landlord, Zar Property NY, was represented in-house, while JLL brokered the deal for the tenant.
