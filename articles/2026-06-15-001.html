South Florida’s Billionaires Wave Is Swamping the Region for Good and Ill

Commercial Observer — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:00:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

South Florida's real estate market is experiencing a significant boom, particularly in the luxury residential sector, driven by an influx of wealthy individuals and tech billionaires. This migration, fueled by factors like low taxes and a desirable lifestyle, has led to record-breaking property transactions and is now impacting the commercial real estate market. While this wealth migration presents opportunities for developers, it also exacerbates an affordability crisis, pushing out working and middle-class residents and creating a shortage of essential workers. Developers are responding by building luxury properties and amenities, and also investing in infrastructure like educational institutions to support the growing high-earning population.
