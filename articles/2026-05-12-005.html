New Rexford Industrial Realty CEO Laura Clark On the Southern California Market

Commercial Observer — Mon, 11 May 2026 13:22:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Laura Clark, the new CEO of Rexford Industrial Realty, is navigating the company through a strategic pivot from aggressive acquisitions to capital recycling, emphasizing maximizing returns through share repurchases and property sales. This shift comes amidst pressure from activist investor Elliott Investment Management and the departure of co-founders. Clark is focused on optimizing Rexford's existing portfolio, particularly through the lease-up of ongoing developments and repositionings, to drive net operating income growth. Despite a challenging Southern California industrial market with falling rents and rising vacancies, Clark maintains strong conviction in the long-term upside of infill industrial properties in the region.
