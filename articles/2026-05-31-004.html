SUMMARY

REO / Foreclosure

The article details the ongoing financial fallout for lenders involved in the collapse of Yissocher "Izzy" Rotenberg's Chicago multifamily portfolio. Despite foreclosures, lenders are incurring significant costs due to property damage, utility bills, and legal disputes. The situation highlights the challenges faced by bridge lenders in distressed multifamily markets, where property repossession often leads to further financial burdens.
