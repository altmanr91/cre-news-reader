Omnichannel Retailer Cozey Will Open First U.S. Store in Flatiron District

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 19 May 2026 21:23:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Omnichannel furniture retailer Cozey is expanding its presence in the U.S. market by opening its first permanent store in Manhattan's Flatiron District. The Canadian direct-to-consumer brand signed a 10-year lease for 10,146 square feet at 160 Fifth Ave., a mixed-use building owned by RFR. This move signifies a growing trend of digitally native brands establishing physical retail footprints in key urban markets like New York City.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 160 Fifth Ave., New York City, NY

Size: 10,146 SF

Notable Features: Space previously occupied by Club Monaco and Ralph's Coffee.

Project Notes: This marks Cozey's first permanent U.S. store, following a pop-up in 2024.
