LISTEN: The RED Awards, a night unlike any other

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 18:05:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article provides a critical review of the RED Awards, an industry event created by Selman Yalcin, highlighting its unique and often bizarre characteristics. The event, while attracting major real estate figures, is noted for its unconventional awards, 90s-esque advertisements, and a high-energy atmosphere that draws a younger, more diverse professional crowd. Despite questions about its 'pay-for-play' nature, the event is seen as valuable for the sense of recognition and networking opportunities it provides, and it is expanding internationally with a Forbes partnership.
