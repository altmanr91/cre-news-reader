PMG, Lndmrk score $126M construction loan for Wynwood condos

LOAN

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 01 May 2026 16:27:44 +0000

SUMMARY

PMG and Lndmrk Development secured a $126 million construction loan from Madison Realty Capital and Siguler Guff for their 233-unit condominium project in Miami's Wynwood neighborhood. The project, Twenty Sixth & 2nd Wynwood Residences, will feature short-term rental-friendly units with deeded office space and is already 73 percent presold, with completion anticipated in 2028. The developers are acquiring the site from ASG Equities, which will retain ownership of the retail component and a minority stake in the residential portion.
