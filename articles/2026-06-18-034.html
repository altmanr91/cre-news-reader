Murphy, Creek Lane list Fulton Market office amid debt distress

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 18:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A joint venture between Murphy Development Group and Creek Lane Capital is marketing its 104,900-square-foot office building in Chicago's Fulton Market, following the maturity of a $43.3 million loan from PGIM Real Estate. The developers completed the property in 2020, and it is currently 78% leased with a weighted average lease term of 7.6 years. The sale is expected to test investor appetite for discounted assets in the Fulton Market area, as the property is likely to trade below its original mortgage value.
