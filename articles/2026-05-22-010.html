US Merchants Buys 453,960 SF Industrial Facility in Surprise, Arizona

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 21 May 2026 13:49:40 +0000

SUMMARY

US Merchants has acquired Building One at West Summit at Surprise, a 453,960-square-foot industrial facility in Surprise, Arizona, from developer Mohr Capital for $63.9 million. The property, completed in the third quarter of 2024, is part of a larger two-building industrial park and features modern amenities suitable for logistics and distribution operations.
