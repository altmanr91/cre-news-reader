Chef Andrew Sargent Inks Deal at 16 West 18th Street for His First NYC Restaurant

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 18:19:45 +0000

SUMMARY

Chef Andrew Sargent, winner of Netflix's "Next Gen Chef," has signed a 10-year lease for 3,133 square feet at 16 West 18th Street in Manhattan's Flatiron District to open his first New York City restaurant, Caroline. The space includes ground floor and basement square footage, with an asking rent of $150 per square foot. The restaurant is anticipated to open in spring 2027, offering a tasting menu and à la carte bar program, and is expected to enhance Manhattan's dining scene.
