Life Time breaks ground on flagship club in Maple Grove

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 09:51:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Life Time has broken ground on a new 120,000-square-foot athletic country club in Maple Grove, Minnesota, which will serve as a flagship location and nearly double the size of its existing club in the city. This development is part of a land exchange agreement with the City of Maple Grove, where the city will acquire the current Life Time club to expand its community center once the new facility is operational. The project signifies Life Time's continued investment in premium, resort-style destinations, with a targeted opening in late 2027 and future plans for Life Time Living luxury residences.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: fitness

Size: 120,000 SF

Completion: Late 2027

Notable Features: Two-story facility with resort-style outdoor pool, beach club, dedicated pickleball courts, and full-service LifeSpa.

Project Notes: The project is part of a land exchange agreement with the City of Maple Grove, finalized in late 2024. The city will take ownership of the current Life Time club to expand its adjacent community center after the new club opens. Future phases include Life Time Living luxury residences.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Life Time

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Knutson Construction
