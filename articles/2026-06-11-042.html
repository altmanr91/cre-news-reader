Marcus & Millichap Closes Deal for Bronx Industrial with Redevelopment Potential

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 21:02:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of an industrial property in the Bronx for $10.275 million. The asset, which includes a warehouse on a substantial lot, was acquired by Realterm. The transaction highlights the strong demand for industrial outdoor storage and last-mile logistics facilities in New York City, with investors willing to pay a premium for scarce vacant industrial land that offers redevelopment potential.
