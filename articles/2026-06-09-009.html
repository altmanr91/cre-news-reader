JLL Negotiates 75,000 SF Office Lease at APEX at Legacy in Plano

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 14:45:44 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has successfully brokered a 75,000-square-foot office lease at APEX at Legacy in Plano, Texas, with a cloud-based educational software company. This significant transaction boosts the 220,000-square-foot building's occupancy to 97 percent, underscoring strong demand for prime office space in the area.
