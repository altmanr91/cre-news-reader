Matthews Brokers $8.5M Sale of Retail Strip Center in East Nashville

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 12:22:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Matthews has brokered the $8.5 million sale of a recently built retail strip center in East Nashville's Five Points district. The property, constructed in 2023, features a strong tenant mix with long-term triple-net leases. A locally based private investor acquired the asset as part of a 1031 exchange, securing a five-year acquisition loan from a local lender.
