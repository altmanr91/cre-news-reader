SUMMARY

This article is a weekly digest of real estate news and data points, covering several distinct topics. The report also examines institutional investor activity in the housing market, noting a pullback in acquisitions of existing homes and a shift towards building rental properties, with their purchases typically focusing on lower-priced, older homes. Additionally, the article points out the increasing number of cities where starter homes are valued at $1 million or more, particularly in New York, New Jersey, and California, indicating a growing barrier for first-time homebuyers.
