Financing locked in for Preston Center high-rise project

LOAN

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Willow Bridge Property Company, Ramrock Real Estate, and Lincoln Property Company has secured construction financing for a 12-story mixed-use high-rise at 8300 Douglas Avenue in Dallas. The project, slated for completion in 2028, has already secured Fifth Third Bank as an anchor tenant for its regional headquarters. The financing package, including both equity and construction funds, was provided by an undisclosed lender.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Downtown Dallas is experiencing a decline in office occupancy, with companies relocating out of the area. Fifth Third Bank is moving its regional headquarters from Comerica Bank Tower. The Dallas Mavericks and Dallas Stars NBA/NHL franchises are planning to move out of American Airlines Center, and the Neiman Marcus store at 1618 Main Street is closing permanently.
