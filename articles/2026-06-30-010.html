Scannell Breaks Ground on 1.2 MSF Spec Industrial Building Near Columbus, Ohio

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 13:50:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Scannell Properties has commenced construction on a 1.2 million-square-foot speculative industrial building in Commercial Point, a southern suburb of Columbus, Ohio. This project represents the initial phase of a new logistics park and is expected to be completed by April 2027. A Colliers broker is managing the leasing efforts for the new facility, which is Scannell's ninth development in the central Ohio market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Size: 1,200,000 SF

Completion: April 2027

Notable Features: 40-foot clear height, 60 dock doors, four drive-in doors.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Scannell Properties

LEASING AGENT: Colliers — Joel Yakovac
