Trepp’s Lonnie Hendry Emphasizes Discipline, Patience and Data

Connect CRE — Mon, 04 May 2026 22:17:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Lonnie Hendry, Chief Product Officer at Trepp, highlights key trends and opportunities in the current commercial real estate market, emphasizing the need for discipline, patience, and data-driven decision-making. He notes a significant bifurcation in asset performance across all property types, with Class-A assets and strong operators outperforming. Hendry also points to refinancing risk as a major concern, as even healthy assets struggle to refinance at previous leverage levels, leading to an increase in unresolved loans at maturity. Despite a lag in transaction volume due to capital markets' confidence resetting, he anticipates an increase in activity throughout 2026 and beyond, with opportunities emerging for investors and lenders who can underwrite realistic rents and conservative leverage, particularly in non-gateway markets.
