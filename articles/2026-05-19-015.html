Nuveen adds to Tempe office-to-industrial pipeline with 300K sf redevelopment plans

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 18 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Nuveen Real Estate, through its affiliate USCIF Sky Harbor LLC, has acquired the 19-acre Sky Harbor Innovation Park in Tempe, AZ, for $23.2 million. The former office complex is slated for redevelopment into a three-building industrial park totaling approximately 300,000 square feet, with plans to retain about 60,000 square feet of office space. This acquisition and planned conversion highlight a growing trend in metro Phoenix where obsolete office properties are being redeveloped into industrial and logistics facilities, driven by strong demand for such space and the area's strategic location near the airport and major freeways.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

First-quarter office vacancy in metro Phoenix fell to 23.5 percent from 24.5 percent quarter over quarter. Since 2020, over 8 million square feet of office space in the region has been designated for conversion, with nearly 5.7 million square feet earmarked for industrial redevelopment.
