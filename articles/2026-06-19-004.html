Vilson Lumaj Plans Two 99-Unit Buildings in the Bronx After $14M Deal

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 20:26:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Housing developer Vilson Lumaj has acquired a vacant property in the Bronx for $13.5 million, including air rights, with plans to construct two 99-unit residential buildings. The development strategy of building two separate 99-unit structures is intended to circumvent prevailing wage requirements under the 485-x tax program, a successor to the expired 421-a abatement, which has been criticized for increasing construction costs and hindering housing supply.

DATA POINTS
