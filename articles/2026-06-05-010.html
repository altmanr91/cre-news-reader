Nestle USA Signs 524,252 SF Industrial Lease Renewal in North Fort Worth

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 15:11:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Nestle USA has renewed its lease for a substantial 524,252-square-foot industrial distribution facility located at 13600 Independence Parkway in North Fort Worth. The U.S. affiliate of the Swiss foodmaker will maintain its operations at this namesake property. Cushman & Wakefield represented the tenant, while Holt Lunsford Commercial acted on behalf of the landlord, Clarion Partners.
