South Florida’s top construction permits: Week ending June 24, 2026

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The largest new building permit in South Florida for the week ending June 24, 2026, was issued for a $130 million construction project at the Link at Douglas mixed-use development in Miami. This permit is for the fourth and final tower of the project. Additionally, Keiser University secured an $8 million permit for a new commercial building in West Palm Beach, while another $7.05 million commercial permit was issued for a project in West Palm Beach linked to affordable housing and a specialty grocery store. The top residential permit was for seven new homes in PulteGroup's Domaine at SoSo community, with an estimated construction cost of $3.12 million.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Link at Douglas

Address: 3070 Southwest 37th Avenue, Miami, FL

Total Project Cost: $130.0M
