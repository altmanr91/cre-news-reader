Town Lane, Gillon Property Acquire 460,000 SF Retail, Office Property in Allen, Texas

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 06 May 2026 14:54:31 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership between Town Lane and Gillon Property Group has acquired Watters Creek Village, a 460,000-square-foot retail and office property in Allen, Texas. The property, built in 2008 on 46 acres, includes a significant retail component anchored by a grocery store and boutique office space. The new owners plan to implement capital improvements.

DATA POINTS

Site Area: 46 acres

Notable Features: Anchored by a Market Street grocery store; includes 360,000 square feet of retail and 100,000 square feet of boutique office space.
