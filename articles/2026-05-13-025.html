Palm Beach mansion asking $50M tops luxury contracts

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 12 May 2026 19:46:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A Maurice Fatio-designed mansion in Palm Beach, owned by a longtime media executive, was the top luxury contract signed last week with an asking price of $49.5 million. The 9,000-square-foot property at 115 Via La Selva was built in 1928 on 0.8 acres and features eight bedrooms, nine bathrooms, a gym, pool, and guest house. Another notable contract was for a 5,000-square-foot home at 231 Coral Lane, asking $15.9 million, which was built in 2008 on 0.3 acres.
