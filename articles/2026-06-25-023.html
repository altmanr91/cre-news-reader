Swiss firm plans Breitling tower as swelling branded condo pipeline tests Miami’s limits

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 11:05:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Partners Group, in collaboration with Empira Group, is developing the $220 million B Residences, a 70-story Breitling-branded condominium tower in Miami's Brickell Financial District. The project will feature over 300 units and a 45,000-square-foot private social club, with construction slated to begin in late 2028 and completion targeted for 2031. This development adds to Miami's rapidly expanding pipeline of branded residences, a trend that is testing the market's absorption capacity, though the developers express confidence in the project's unique 'Swiss quality standards' and Breitling's distinct style.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Miami had 48 completed branded condo projects and another 55 planned as of last fall, with the pipeline increasing since then. Planned projects include Elle Residences in Edgewater, Palm Tree Residences Miami, Anantara Hotels & Resorts, and Faena Residences on the Miami River. OKO Group and Oak Row Equities are also planning a branded residential project in Brickell as part of a larger 4.3-acre bayfront redevelopment.
