C-PACE Lending Expanding in Size, Scope as Regulations Adjust

Commercial Observer — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 10:29:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article details the significant expansion of Commercial Property Assessed Clean Energy (C-PACE) financing, which has evolved from primarily funding sustainability upgrades to also supporting new construction, recapitalizations, and refinancing. The industry saw record originations of $2.42 billion across 214 deals in 2024, with preliminary 2025 data indicating cumulative originations exceeding $10 billion. Lenders like Nuveen Green Capital and Peachtree Group are setting new records for C-PACE loan sizes, driven by higher interest rates and the ability of C-PACE to provide a substantial portion of the capital stack. The growth is further fueled by the increasing acceptance of retroactive C-PACE loans and expanded state programs, allowing property owners to finance past energy efficiency and resiliency upgrades.
