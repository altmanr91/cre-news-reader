The Doran Group begins construction 128-unit seniors housing community in Hudson

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 10:10:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Doran Group has commenced construction on Linden House – Hudson, a 128-unit seniors housing community in Hudson, Wisconsin. The project, which secured $26 million in construction financing from Veridian Credit Union, represents the launch of the developer's second senior housing brand, targeting regional hubs and growing smaller cities in the Upper Midwest. The community is designed to offer upscale living with a mix of spacious one- and two-bedroom homes, each featuring private outdoor balconies and underground heated parking.

Notable Features: Upscale setting with an outdoor central terrace, pool, clubroom, wellness center, craft spaces, pet run and spa, and grand lobby. Units range from 690 to 1,380 square feet with private outdoor balconies and underground heated parking.

Project Notes: This project marks the launch of a second senior housing brand for The Doran Group, specifically designed for strong regional hubs and growing smaller cities across the Upper Midwest.
