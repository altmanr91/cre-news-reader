“Wishy-washy” buyers scuttled one in every six Texas home transactions last month

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Housing Report

Texas homebuyers canceled more than one in six home transactions last month, a trend attributed to the state's historically high housing supply and permissive contract laws. Fort Worth, San Antonio, Dallas, and Houston were among the top U.S. metros for contract cancellations, with rates ranging from 16.9% to 18.1%. Industry experts suggest that Texas's contract law, which allows buyers an inexpensive termination option, combined with an abundant housing supply, empowers buyers to be less committed to initial offers, leading to increased cancellations.
