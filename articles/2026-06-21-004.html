Forget cash. These Bay Area sellers want AI stock

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Sat, 20 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article explores the emerging trend of Bay Area homeowners attempting to sell luxury residential properties in exchange for pre-IPO shares of artificial intelligence companies like OpenAI and Anthropic. While several high-profile listings have garnered significant attention, the article highlights the challenges and complexities of such transactions, including valuation discrepancies, capital gains tax implications, and the unpredictable nature of IPOs. Despite the difficulties, some sellers are genuinely seeking to tap into the anticipated wealth creation from the AI boom, even if it means ultimately pursuing traditional cash deals or separate stock transactions.
