Premier PV Signs 265,400 SF Industrial Lease in Southwest Dallas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 14:43:58 +0000

SUMMARY

Premier PV, a solar photovoltaic products and services provider, has signed a 265,400-square-foot industrial lease in southwest Dallas. The building, located at 4715 Mountain Creek Parkway, was constructed in 2004 and recently renovated. The landlord, CanTex Capital, was represented by Holt Lunsford Commercial, while Site Selection Group represented the tenant.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 4715 Mountain Creek Parkway, Dallas, TX

Size: 265,400 SF

Year Built: 2004

Notable Features: Features 34.5-foot clear heights and six acres for future expansion or outdoor storage.

Project Notes: Building was renovated in 2025.
