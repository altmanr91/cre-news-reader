Hunington plans $33M multifamily project in booming Fulshear

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 15:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hunington Properties is developing a 348-unit multifamily project in Fulshear, a rapidly expanding town in Fort Bend County, west of Houston. The $33 million development, spanning 444,607 square feet across 12 three-story buildings and a clubhouse, will feature amenities such as a pool court, outdoor exercise area, and a dog park. Construction is slated to commence in September 2026 and conclude by March 2027, contributing to the area's growing housing supply amidst significant single-family development.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Fulshear ranked second among the nation's fastest-growing cities with populations of 20,000 or more last year, with its population growing by 21 percent to 64,630 between July 2024 and July 2025.
