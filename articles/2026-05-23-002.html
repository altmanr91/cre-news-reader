Dutch Investor Pays $110M For New Rental in Aventura, Fla.

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 22 May 2026 16:35:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Dutch investor Breevest has acquired Avida Aventura, a newly constructed 266-unit multifamily property in Aventura, FL, for $110 million. The seller, Fifield Companies, completed the eight-story building in 2024 after securing a $112 million construction loan from Ladder Capital in 2021. This transaction marks Breevest's entry into the South Florida market, with BDT & MSD Partners providing $85 million in acquisition financing.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

South Florida's multifamily investment market has seen increased activity, despite a slowdown in rent growth since the pandemic's peak. Recent transactions include the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints' real estate arm acquiring a 456-unit complex in Boca Raton for $240 million, and LeFrak Organization purchasing a waterfront rental tower in Fort Lauderdale for $180 million.
