Austin in line for $30M of libertarian development devoted to Ayn Rand

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 22 May 2026 17:51:58 +0000

SUMMARY

The Ayn Rand Museum and Archives Foundation, an arm of the Ayn Rand Institute, is developing a two-story, 27,144-square-foot Ayn Rand Center in Austin, TX. The $30 million project, located near the University of Texas at Austin campus, will feature a library, exhibition room, offices, and higher education classroom spaces. Construction is slated to begin in July and conclude in October 2028, marking the institute's and foundation's migration to Austin.
