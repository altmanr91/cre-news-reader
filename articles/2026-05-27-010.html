Taroko Sports to Open 11,740 SF Entertainment Venue in Pflugerville, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 26 May 2026 14:24:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Taroko Sports, a baseball-themed entertainment concept, is set to open an 11,740-square-foot venue in Pflugerville, a northern suburb of Austin. This will be the company's second location in Texas and is scheduled to open early next year within the Stone Hill mixed-use development.

DATA POINTS

Address: 500 Limestone Commercial Dr, Pflugerville, TX*
