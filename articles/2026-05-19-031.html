Carter Cadillac of Chicago set to open in 2027

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 18 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Jack Carter Automotive Group is developing the first Cadillac dealership within Chicago city limits, located at 1400 West North Avenue. The group acquired the 2.2-acre site for $15.5 million from Sterling Bay, with an anticipated opening in late 2027. The property was originally part of Sterling Bay's ambitious Lincoln Yards megaproject, which faced significant challenges and ultimately saw much of its land returned to lenders and sold off.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 1400 West North Avenue, Chicago, IL

Sale Price: $15.5M

Site Area: 2.2 acres

Notable Features: First individual Cadillac dealership within Chicago city limits.

Project Notes: Site was previously part of Sterling Bay's Lincoln Yards megaproject and was acquired by Sterling Bay seven years prior to this transaction. Sterling Bay had previously envisioned a 100,000-square-foot medical office building for the site.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
