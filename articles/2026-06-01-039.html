Converted Belmont Mill Trades for $55.5M

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 14:42:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Harbor Group International has acquired Chronicle Mill, a 238-unit adaptive-reuse apartment complex with 9,000 square feet of commercial space in Belmont, NC, from Armada Hoffler Realty Trust for $55.5 million. This transaction is part of a larger $562 million portfolio acquisition by Harbor Group, involving 11 multifamily properties from Armada Hoffler. The Chronicle Mill, originally built in 1901, underwent a $60 million redevelopment before this sale.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Chronicle Mill

Address: 96 Catawba St., Belmont, NC

Units: 238

Sale Price: $55.5M

Total Project Cost: $60.0M

Year Built: 1901

Notable Features: Adaptive-reuse apartment complex with commercial space, offering studio, 1-bed, and 2-bed units.

Project Notes: The 2.6-acre site was previously acquired by Armada Hoffler Realty Trust for $1.4 million in 2020. This property was the 28th textile mill built in Gaston County and the first in Belmont.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Harbor Group International

SELLER: Armada Hoffler Realty Trust
