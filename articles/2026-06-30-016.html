Supreme Court rebuffs Trump bid to oust Fed governor

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

The Supreme Court has rejected former President Donald Trump's attempt to remove Federal Reserve Governor Lisa Cook, ruling five to four in her favor. Cook had sued, arguing her firing was not "for cause" as required by federal law, and alleged it was a politically motivated move. This decision slows Trump's efforts to exert greater influence over the independent central bank, though a separate ruling granted him more power to fire leaders of other regulatory agencies.
