Rivani sells Wynwood Jungle for $26M to fund next Miami Beach buy

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Robert Rivani's firm sold the Wynwood Jungle, a nearly 39,000-square-foot retail plaza in Miami, for $26 million to a Dallas-based investment group. The sale price is nearly double its acquisition cost five years prior, despite the property being 50 percent vacant and facing ongoing lease disputes with former tenants. The proceeds from this sale will be used to finance Rivani's upcoming acquisition of an iconic office building in Miami Beach, signaling a strategic shift away from retail and hospitality assets.
