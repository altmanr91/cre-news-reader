Strada picks up SKS office building in Showplace Square for $103M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Strada Investment Group has acquired the 1 De Haro Street office building in San Francisco's Showplace Square for $103 million, or approximately $771 per square foot. The seller was SKS Partners, which completed the 133,427-square-foot property in 2021. The four-story building is fully leased to a wireless networking company, which occupies the entire structure for its global headquarters. This transaction represents a significant vote of confidence in San Francisco's recovering office market, as fully leased, revenue-generating properties have been rare in recent sales.
