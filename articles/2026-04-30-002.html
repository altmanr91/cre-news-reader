ESRT Reports Strong Balance Sheet Despite Temporary Headwinds in Q1

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 20:01:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Empire State Realty Trust (ESRT) reported strong first-quarter 2026 revenues of $190.3 million, exceeding expectations and marking its 19th consecutive quarter of positive leasing spreads. The REIT's commercial portfolio reached 93.2 percent leased, with its office portfolio maintaining over 90 percent occupancy for the 13th straight quarter. ESRT also expanded its retail holdings with a $46 million acquisition in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, adding six new storefronts to its existing portfolio in the area.
