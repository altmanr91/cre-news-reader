Summit Agrees Not To Pursue Rent Arrears In Pinnacle Portfolio

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 19:12:30 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Summit Properties USA, the new owner of a 93-building, 5,000-unit rent-stabilized multifamily portfolio in New York City, has agreed to waive millions in unpaid rent arrears for tenants. This decision follows a successful campaign by the Union of Pinnacle Tenants (UPT), which had organized a rent strike due to hazardous living conditions and numerous violations under the previous ownership. Summit Properties USA has also committed to a five-year, $30 million capital expenditure plan, with $10 million allocated for the first year, to address long-standing maintenance issues and improve living conditions across the portfolio.
