Related tweaks plans for delayed Austin office that would be firm’s first mass timber build

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 13 May 2026 20:16:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Related Companies has revised its plans for a mass timber office project at 901 South Congress Avenue in Austin, reverting to a five-story design after previously expanding it to seven stories. The project, initially announced in 2022, was conceived as the developer's first mass timber construction, a method gaining popularity in Austin for its speed, cost-effectiveness, and sustainability. While the latest filing did not specify the square footage, earlier iterations included ground-floor retail and residential components, which may or may not be part of the current plan.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 901 South Congress Avenue, Austin, TX

Notable Features: Originally planned as a mass timber construction, a method known for speed, cost-effectiveness, and sustainability.

Project Notes: Initial plans in 2022 called for a five-story, 107,000 SF project. Plans were revised in April 2023 to seven stories and 260,000 SF. The current filing reverts to a five-story design.
