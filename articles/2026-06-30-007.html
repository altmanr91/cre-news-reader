NewQuest Breaks Ground on 163,456 SF Retail Project in Northwest Houston

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 14:20:00 +0000

SUMMARY

NewQuest has commenced construction on a 163,456-square-foot retail development in northwest Houston. The project, part of the larger $90 million Grand at 249 development, will include two junior anchor spaces and is expected to be ready for occupancy by mid-2027. A strong tenant mix has already preleased the majority of the space.
