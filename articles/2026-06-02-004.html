Wave Sports & Entertainment Signs 8K-SF Lease at Feil’s 145 West 30th Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 20:40:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Wave Sports & Entertainment, a digital media company, has signed a long-term lease for 8,320 square feet on the entire 11th floor of 145 West 30th Street in Midtown Manhattan. The Feil Organization, the landlord, secured the deal with an asking rent of $48 per square foot. This transaction reflects a broader trend of tenants seeking high-quality office spaces with elevated finishes and full-floor opportunities, according to the landlord's leasing head.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 145 West 30th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 8,320 SF

Rental Rate: $48.00/SF/yr

Notable Features: Full-floor layout with elevated finishes and an easy location for commuters near Penn Station.
