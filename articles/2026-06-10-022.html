David Werner closes One Dag purchase with $250M JPMorgan loan

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 20:16:28 +0000

SUMMARY

David Werner Real Estate Investments has acquired One Dag Hammarskjöld Plaza, a 50-story office tower in Midtown East, for $270 million from Rockpoint Group. The acquisition was financed with a $250 million loan from JPMorgan. Werner plans an additional $60 million investment for upgrades and leasing, bringing the total capitalization to over $330 million, aiming to improve the building's nearly 30 percent vacancy.
