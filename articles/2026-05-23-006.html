Provident Industrial Completes 161,408 SF Project in Arlington

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 22 May 2026 15:38:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Provident Industrial has completed the A20 Logistics Center, a 161,408-square-foot industrial project in Arlington, designed to accommodate up to four tenants. The development includes infrastructure improvements such as an extended city roadway and a new traffic signal at the property entrance, enhancing accessibility and functionality for future occupants.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: A20 Logistics Center

Address: 500 E Bardin Rd, Arlington, TX*

Size: 161,408 SF

Notable Features: Designed to accommodate up to four tenants with knockout panels and doors for central entrance configuration.

Project Notes: Infrastructure improvements included an extended city roadway and a new traffic signal at the property entrance.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Provident Industrial
