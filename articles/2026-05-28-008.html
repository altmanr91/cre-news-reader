Smurfit Westrock Signs 78,683 SF Industrial Lease in Northwest Houston

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 27 May 2026 15:16:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Smurfit Westrock, a packaging services provider, has signed a 78,683-square-foot industrial lease in northwest Houston. The company will occupy space within Patriot Business Park, a three-building industrial development totaling 462,591 square feet. The transaction highlights continued demand for industrial space in the Houston market.
