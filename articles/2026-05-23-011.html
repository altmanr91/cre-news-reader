JLL closes sale of 244-room hotel in Houston’s CITYCENTRE district

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 22 May 2026 16:06:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL's Hotels & Hospitality Group has completed the sale of The Moran Hotel CITYCENTRE, a 244-room lifestyle hotel located within Houston's 47-acre CITYCENTRE mixed-use district. Midway sold the asset to MetroNational in a joint venture acquisition. The hotel, which underwent a multimillion-dollar renovation in 2024, is a focal point of the walkable CITYCENTRE development and includes 22 condominium units and ground-floor retail space.

The hotel completed a multimillion-dollar renovation in 2024 and includes 15,750 SF of meeting space, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center. It is situated at the intersection of Interstate 10 and Beltway 8.

Project Notes: The property opened in 2009 as Hotel Sorella before rebranding as the Moran in 2020. The offering included 22 condominium units on floors 11-12 and 14,645 SF of ground floor retail.
