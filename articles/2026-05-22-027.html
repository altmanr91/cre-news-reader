Penn-Florida sets stage for Mandarin Oriental hotel dev site auction

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 21 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Penn-Florida Companies is preparing for an auction of its Mandarin Oriental hotel development site in Boca Raton, FL, although the firm is still pursuing a recapitalization or refinancing to avoid the sale. The 1-acre site, where a 12-story, 164-key hotel is under construction and structurally complete, is subject to Chapter 11 reorganization filings by Penn-Florida affiliates. The primary creditor is owed $130.2 million, with additional unsecured and secured claims totaling over $100 million.
