NYC’s top deals: Sioni Group buys Flatiron office for $31M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 30 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Sioni Group has acquired a 12-story office building in New York City's Flatiron District for $31 million. The 68,800-square-foot property, located at 38 West 21st Street, was sold by Jack Vogel Associates, who had owned the asset for several decades. The transaction values the property at approximately $450 per square foot.
