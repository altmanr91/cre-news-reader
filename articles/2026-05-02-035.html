Atlanta agency weighs $200M investment and public-private partnership for former CNN Center

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Atlanta — Fri, 01 May 2026 15:41:48 +0000

SUMMARY

The Atlanta-Fulton County Recreation Authority is considering a public-private partnership with CP Group to acquire and redevelop The Center at 190 Marietta Street, formerly the CNN Center. The authority would purchase the 1.2 million-square-foot complex using tax-exempt revenue bonds, while CP Group would contribute $200 million to $300 million in equity for renovations and continue to operate the property. This strategic move aims to prevent disrepair and maintain public influence over the high-profile downtown asset, which is transitioning from a single-tenant media hub to a mixed-use project.
