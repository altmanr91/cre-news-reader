Brookfield sheds River North hotel for $29M after pandemic pickup in bankruptcy

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 11 May 2026 18:22:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Brookfield Asset Management has sold the Homewood Suites Chicago-Downtown, a 233-room extended-stay hotel, for $29 million to an entity tied to investor Prakash Patel. This transaction represents Brookfield shedding assets acquired during the 2021 bankruptcy of Hospitality Investors Trust, where Brookfield took control of a large portfolio. The sale price of nearly $125,000 per key indicates a stronger performance compared to other recent Chicago-area hotel deals, with the property offering significant value-add potential through property improvements and operational enhancements.
