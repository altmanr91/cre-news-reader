East Bay senior housing facilities trade for $62M in latest “silver tsunami” demand play

ACQUISITION

San Francisco - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CareTrust REIT, through its affiliates, has acquired two senior housing facilities in the East Bay for a total of $61.7 million in all-cash transactions. The properties, located in Fremont and Pleasant Hill, specialize in skilled nursing, long-term care, and rehabilitation services. This acquisition reflects the ongoing demand for senior housing in the Bay Area, driven by the aging baby boomer population.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Bay Area senior housing market is experiencing increased demand due to the aging baby boomer population, leading to significant transaction activity. Recent comparable sales include Crescent Oaks Memory Care and Silver Oaks Memory Care for nearly $24.3 million in total, and Ivy Park at Los Gatos for $54 million.
