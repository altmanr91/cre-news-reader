Boston Developers Sue Bank For Disclosing Financial Information

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 15:19:00 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A group of Boston developers has filed a lawsuit against Eastern Bank, alleging the bank improperly disclosed their personal financial information on the online debt sale platform DebtX. The developers claim that sensitive data, including personal financial statements, tax returns, and details on over 50 active properties, was published without their consent or legitimate business purpose. This alleged disclosure occurred as Eastern Bank sought to sell a construction loan related to a substance abuse treatment center in Weston, a loan originally issued by HarborOne Bank before its acquisition by Eastern Bank.
