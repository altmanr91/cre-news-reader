Patterson Arranges Construction Loan for 165-Unit Multifamily Project in Sugar Land, Texas

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 01 May 2026 14:09:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Patterson Real Estate Advisory Group has arranged a construction loan for The Westermark, a 165-unit multifamily project in Sugar Land, Texas. The development will feature two five-story buildings offering a mix of studio, one-, two-, and three-bedroom units, along with amenities such as a pool, fitness center, resident lounge, and coworking space. Wintrust Commercial Real Estate provided the financing to developer Elm Real Estate Group.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Westermark

Size: 165 units

Notable Features: Two five-story buildings with a pool, fitness center, resident lounge, and coworking space.
