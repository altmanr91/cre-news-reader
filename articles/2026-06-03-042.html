Gantry Secures $12M Loan for 389K-SF Illinois Retail Center

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 21:27:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Gantry has secured a $12 million permanent loan to refinance maturing debt for Rock River Plaza, a 389,375-square-foot retail power center in Moline, Illinois. The five-year, fixed-rate, non-recourse loan was provided by a CMBS lender and features full-term interest-only payments. The center's strong performance and roster of credit tenants were key factors in securing the low-leverage financing for the private real estate investor.
