BKV Group completes work on 290-unit rental high-rise in downtown San Antonio

REJournals — Mon, 04 May 2026 10:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

BKV Group has completed The Continental Residences, a 290-unit rental high-rise developed by Weston Urban in downtown San Antonio. The 16-story building, located on the 1.9-acre Continental Block, features a design that integrates with the historic Zona Cultural district, including a brick podium and residential levels that complement adjacent historic structures. The project offers a range of apartment types and extensive amenities, with leasing and initial move-ins now in progress.
