Pulte, Tri Pointe pay $63M for state-owned land near Phoenix as prices fall

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 16:03:15 +0000

SUMMARY

PulteGroup and Tri Pointe Homes have finalized the acquisition of a 274-acre parcel from the state of Arizona for $63 million, completing their land assembly for the 1,200-acre Aloravita master-planned residential community near Phoenix. The joint venture plans to develop 568 homes on this site, with Tri Pointe building 275 and Pulte constructing 293, to be delivered in phases. This acquisition follows their successful bid in October 2024 and subsequent entitlement work with the City of Peoria.
