Creation Completes 137,000 SF Industrial Project in Haltom City, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 15:09:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Creation has completed Triad 820, a three-building industrial project totaling 137,000 square feet in Haltom City, a northern suburb of Fort Worth. The development, which began construction in early 2025, offers modern features designed for industrial users. NAI Robert Lynn is handling the leasing for the property.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Triad 820

Address: 4901 Anderson Blvd, Haltom City, TX*

Size: 137,000 SF

Completion: June 2026

Notable Features: Features 28-foot clear heights, 125-foot truck court depths, electrical vehicle charging stations, and a secured yard.

Project Notes: Construction began in early 2025.
