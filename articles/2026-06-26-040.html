SUMMARY

Promotion

Cresa has appointed Julie Northcutt-Dunn as president, markets, a newly created executive role focused on driving growth across the company's market operations. She will be based in Cresa's Chicago headquarters. The Inland Real Estate Group has expanded its institutional asset management platform, Inland Institutional, with the appointment of Jacqueline Dagan as associate general counsel and Dhaval Parikh as head of institutional capital formation. PREMIER Design + Build Group has promoted Stacy Reid to vice president of its new Reno office, marking a significant step in the company's national expansion. JLL has appointed Chris Fernandez to lead its workplace management business across the Americas, overseeing teams and enhancing operational performance. PsiQuantum has hired Josh Koons as vice president of facilities in Chicago, where he will lead the development of the company's facility at the Illinois Quantum and Microelectronics Park. Cushman & Wakefield has added Matt Hiatt as director to its office leasing team in St. Louis. Sartorius's Center of Excellence for Bioanalytics in Ann Arbor, Michigan, has received LEED v4 Gold certification, recognizing its sustainable construction and energy-efficient operations.
