Free at last? Charles Cohen pays off $187M Fortress debt

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 21:34:47 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Billionaire Charles Cohen has satisfied a debt of over $187 million to Fortress Investment Group, stemming from personal guarantees on a defaulted $534 million loan portfolio. This payment concludes a significant legal battle that began in 2022 and led to Cohen selling several Manhattan properties to generate funds. Despite this resolution, Fortress indicates it will continue to pursue other claims against Cohen.
