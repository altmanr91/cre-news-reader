AI reshaping retail real estate, but not how you might think

REJournals — Mon, 18 May 2026 09:52:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Retail Report

A new report from Colliers indicates that artificial intelligence (AI) is not diminishing the importance of brick-and-mortar retail, but rather enhancing it. The report, "How AI Is Redefining Retail Real Estate in 2026," highlights that physical stores are evolving into crucial fulfillment centers, logistics hubs, and technology-driven customer experience destinations, supporting both digital and in-person commerce. This shift is driving 71% of retailers to expand their physical footprints in 2026, as one in four online orders is already fulfilled through a physical store, a figure projected to rise to 35.4% by 2030. The increasing consumer adoption of AI for product recommendations and purchasing decisions is also compelling retailers to invest significantly in in-store technology and integrated data infrastructure, creating new demands for landlords and developers.
