Chicago megadevelopment owners considering project merge

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 28 May 2026 18:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development / Project Planning

Discussions are underway regarding a potential merger of two significant Chicago lakefront megaprojects: Bob Dunn's One Central and the Michael Reese hospital site development. The Michael Reese site, developed by Fairpoint Development as part of the GRIT consortium, faces a city deadline to revise its infrastructure plans. While the city is aware of the negotiations and Dunn's interest in expanding his lakefront holdings, the Michael Reese ownership group is reportedly resistant to partnering with Dunn.
