Virtual Reality Simulator SkiPod to Open Store at 572 11th Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 28 May 2026 21:06:44 +0000

SUMMARY

SkiPod, a virtual reality skiing and snowboarding simulator, has leased a 3,790-square-foot corner storefront at 572 11th Avenue in Manhattan's Hell's Kitchen. This transaction marks one of the mixed reality company's initial retail locations. The leasing agent noted strong demand in the submarket, driven by new development, and this deal brings the retail space at 572 11th Avenue to full occupancy.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Oskar

Address: 572 11th Avenue, New York City, NY

Size: 3,790 SF

Rental Rate: $100.00/SF/yr

Current Occupancy: 100.0%

Notable Features: Corner storefront in the Hell's Kitchen neighborhood.

Project Notes: The retail space is located below Oskar, a 12-story luxury rental building. The Moinian Group originally developed the building and sold a 70% stake in 2024.
