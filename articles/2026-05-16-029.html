Tishman Speyer lists 357-unit Union West apartments in Fulton Market

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 15 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Tishman Speyer has listed its 357-unit Union West apartment building in Chicago's Fulton Market for sale, three years after acquiring it for $128 million. The 15-story property, located at 939 West Washington Boulevard, is currently 96% leased, with its 12,436-square-foot retail component fully occupied. The decision to sell comes amidst a robust Chicago multifamily market, characterized by strong demand and significant investment activity, including numerous office-to-residential conversions.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Chicago's multifamily market has seen $6 billion in acquisitions over the past year. Demand for apartment space is strong, leading to an increasing number of office-to-residential conversions in Downtown Chicago. Fulton Market, a former meatpacking district, continues to experience accelerated growth in high-end retail, apartment, and office developments.
