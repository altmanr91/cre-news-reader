SVN|Chicago Commercial closes $1.95 million sale of Itasca office property

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 08 May 2026 14:02:00 +0000

SUMMARY

SVN | Chicago Commercial has brokered the $1.95 million sale of a 15,818-square-foot office building located at 100 E Pierce Road in Itasca, Illinois. The property, which was well-maintained and had two tenants in place, generated significant buyer interest even before being formally marketed, leading to an off-market transaction.
