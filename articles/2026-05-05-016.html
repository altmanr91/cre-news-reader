AI investor launches data center land venture

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 04 May 2026 16:35:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Coatue Management, an AI investor, has launched Next Frontier, a new venture focused on acquiring land for data center development. The firm is prepared to invest tens of billions of dollars and has already begun acquiring land for a 430-megawatt data center complex in New Lebanon, Indiana. Next Frontier has also formed a joint venture with Fluidstack, a provider of computing services for AI firms, to explore potential development projects.
