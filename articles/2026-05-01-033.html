BGO drops $164M on north San Jose office campus

ACQUISITION

San Francisco - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 21:42:14 +0000

SUMMARY

BGO, an affiliate of BentallGreenOak, has acquired the seven-building Rio Tech Office Park in San Jose for $164.3 million. The fully leased 375,000-square-foot campus, previously owned by Washington Holdings, is home to a strong tenant mix of hardware and R&D firms. BGO plans to further develop the property to cater to companies focused on hardware innovation, capitalizing on the Bay Area's established human capital in this sector.

Another recent transaction in North San Jose involved Kayne Anderson purchasing the 95,000-square-foot Pactrust Business Center South for $30 million in December.
