Chicago Commercial Real Estate Awards Event Raises $1M for Hunger Relief

Connect CRE — Wed, 20 May 2026 20:20:22 +0000

SUMMARY

Association / Leadership News

The 38th Annual Chicago Commercial Real Estate Awards, presented by Associated Bank, recently convened over 800 commercial real estate professionals at The Old Post Office. The event successfully raised $1.1 million for the Greater Chicago Food Depository, marking it as the Food Depository's largest annual fundraiser. This long-standing partnership, initiated in 1989, has generated millions of dollars to combat hunger in Chicago and Cook County.

DATA POINTS

Funds Raised: $1.1 million

Event Attendance: 800+

Event Anniversary: 38th Annual
