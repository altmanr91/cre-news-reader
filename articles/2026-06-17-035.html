Adult website founder sells three SF buildings amid legal battle

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 22:04:10 +0000

SUMMARY

The founder of an adult entertainment website sold three commercial buildings in San Francisco for a combined $6.7 million to an affiliate of Urban Rock Properties. The sale occurred amidst an ongoing legal dispute involving the seller over an easement violation at a separate property, with the plaintiff's attorney suggesting the sales were an attempt to divest assets securing a judgment.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 201 Ninth Street, San Francisco, CA

Sale Price: $6.7M

Project Notes: The property at 201 Ninth Street formerly hosted the AsiaSF nightclub and is slated to become a new nightclub called 201 Bodega.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: A Star Holdings LLC — Peter Acworth

BUYER: Urban Rock Properties — Brenton Wickham, CEO
