The numbers are in: How Texas metros compare on population growth

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 14 May 2026 21:27:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Population Report

New Census Bureau data reveals significant population growth across Texas metros, with North Texas leading the charge. Fort Worth has officially entered the top 10 largest U.S. cities, now ranking as the fourth-largest in Texas. Several Dallas and Houston suburbs, including Celina and Fulshear, are among the fastest-growing cities nationally by both numerical and percentage increases. This rapid expansion is fueling a robust housing development market, particularly in the northern Dallas suburbs.
