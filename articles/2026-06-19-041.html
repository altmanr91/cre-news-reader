Colliers Facilitates Sale of 118-Unit Apartment Property in Bozeman

SALE

Connect CRE — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 21:28:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers facilitated the sale of Silver Creek Apartments, a 118-unit multifamily community in Bozeman, Montana. The property, built in 2021, benefits from its strategic location near Downtown Bozeman, Montana State University, and key employment and retail corridors, which are expected to drive strong, sustainable demand and long-term value in the growing market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Silver Creek Apartments

Address: 1481 North 25th Avenue, Bozeman, MT

Units: 118

Year Built: 2021

Notable Features: Located near Downtown Bozeman, Montana State University, and major employment and retail corridors.
