Texas Stock Exchange hints at Y’all Street office plans with stock ticker request for Uptown tower

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 07 May 2026 20:09:51 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The Texas Stock Exchange is reportedly in discussions to establish its headquarters at the Bank of America Tower at Parkside in Uptown Dallas. The potential move is contingent on the exchange's request to install a prominent stock ticker on the building, symbolizing the shift of economic influence to Texas. This development aligns with a broader trend of financial institutions expanding their presence in the Uptown area, which is emerging as a significant financial hub.
