Speaker Spotlight: Adam Greene of RXR

Commercial Observer — Fri, 29 May 2026 04:00:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Adam Greene, Executive Vice President of Development at RXR, discussed the future of New York City's real estate market at Commercial Observer's Future of New York Forum. He highlighted the growing momentum for office-to-residential conversions, the sustained demand for high-quality office space, and the impact of evolving industries like artificial intelligence on new development. Greene emphasized the need for more housing, strategic reinvestment in prime assets, and the construction of next-generation offices to maintain New York's economic advantage. He noted that the 467m program is effectively incentivizing conversions in Manhattan's core, particularly in areas like Midtown South, which is transforming into a dynamic live-work-play district.
