Partnership Agrees to Buy Church Land in Manhattan’s Upper West Side, Develop Mixed-Income Project

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:23:42 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership between Rockefeller Group and Atlas Capital Group has reached an agreement to acquire church-owned land on Manhattan's Upper West Side from the Roman Catholic Church of Holy Name of Jesus and Saint Gregory the Great. The $96 million transaction will facilitate the development of a mixed-income housing project, with 25 percent of the residential units designated as affordable. The project will repurpose underutilized portions of the church campus, including former school and convent buildings, while the active church continues its operations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 200 W. 97th St., New York City, NY

Sale Price: $96.0M

Notable Features: 25 percent of residences will be subject to income restrictions; includes retail space.

Project Notes: Involves underutilized portions of a church campus, including former school, convent, and recreation buildings, while the active church continues its ministry.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Rockefeller Group

BUYER: Atlas Capital Group

SELLER: Roman Catholic Church of Holy Name of Jesus and Saint Gregory the Great
