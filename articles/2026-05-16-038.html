Realterm Acquires Hunts Point Outdoor Storage Facility

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:37:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Realterm, a global investment manager specializing in transportation-focused properties, has acquired a one-acre industrial outdoor storage (IOS) maintenance facility in The Bronx's Hunts Point submarket. The property, which includes a 9,400-square-foot maintenance building, is currently vacant and will undergo capital improvements to enhance its functionality and appeal. This acquisition aligns with Realterm's strategy of investing in transportation-advantaged IOS facilities within supply-constrained markets like Hunts Point, which serves as a critical supply chain hub for New York City.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 935 Garrison Ave., New York City, NY

Size: 9,400 SF

Site Area: 1 acres

Notable Features: Fully paved, lit, and secure industrial outdoor storage facility with three drive-in bays.

Project Notes: Property is currently vacant and available for lease. Realterm plans targeted capital improvements.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Realterm — Ed Brickley, Managing Director, Senior Fund Manager
