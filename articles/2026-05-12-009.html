Highland Properties Negotiates 25,000 SF Industrial Lease Renewal in Arlington

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 11 May 2026 14:53:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Highland Properties has successfully negotiated a 25,000-square-foot industrial lease renewal in Arlington, Texas. The tenant, a manufacturer of advanced honeycomb core components for aircraft, will continue to occupy the space at 2800 E. Randoll Mill Road. This renewal underscores the continued demand for industrial space in the Arlington market.
