Berkadia Lines Up Construction Loan for Seachange Partners

LOAN

Connect CRE — Tue, 19 May 2026 21:05:58 +0000

SUMMARY

Berkadia has secured $15.6 million in non-recourse construction financing for Seachange Partners, facilitating the development of two affordable housing projects in Los Angeles. The five-story developments, located in the Sawtelle and Palms neighborhoods, will collectively provide 79 deed-restricted affordable units. The lender was Genesis Capital LLC, a private residential construction lender based in Los Angeles. The projects are currently under construction and are expected to be completed in 2027.
