Gilbane, CBRE Investment Begin Leasing 703-Bed Student Housing Development Near University of Florida

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 12:26:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Gilbane Development and CBRE Investment Management have commenced leasing for Zaya, a 703-bed student housing development in Gainesville, FL. The project, which broke ground last year, is scheduled for completion in fall 2027 and is located less than one mile from the University of Florida campus. Zaya will feature 352 units with bed-to-bath parity and a comprehensive amenity package.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Property Name: Zaya

Address: 3600 S.W. 20th Ave, Gainesville, FL

Units: 352

Beds: 703

Completion: fall 2027

Notable Features: Located less than one mile from the University of Florida campus and Butler Center, a retail destination. Amenities include three courtyards, study rooms on each floor, a coworking lounge, rooftop resort-style pool, pickleball court, clubhouse, 24-hour fitness center, yoga studio, and an indoor sauna.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Gilbane Development

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: CBRE Investment Management
