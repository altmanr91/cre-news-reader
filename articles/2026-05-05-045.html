Olema Pharmaceuticals Leases New South San Francisco HQ

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 04 May 2026 18:18:27 -0400

SUMMARY

Olema Pharmaceuticals has leased 38,100 SF of office space at Kilroy Oyster Point in South San Francisco for its new corporate headquarters. The biotech firm will occupy 25,000 SF initially, with an additional 13,100 SF for future expansion, relocating from its existing lab and office spaces in San Francisco. The seven-year lease includes options for extension and additional space, with Kilroy Realty undertaking tenant improvements expected to be completed by mid-September.

Notable Features: The second phase of Kilroy Oyster Point includes two seven-story buildings and one eight-story building, along with 50,000 SF of amenities such as an open-air amphitheater, outdoor meeting spaces, and a dedicated food and beverage hub.

Project Notes: Kilroy Realty acquired the development site at Oyster Point and Marina boulevards in 2018. Hathaway Dinwiddie broke ground on the second phase in June 2021, delivering 865,000 SF.
