JLL Brokers Sale of 271,689 SF Industrial Campus in Cedar Park, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 06 May 2026 14:48:17 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has brokered the sale of New Hope, a newly constructed three-building industrial campus totaling 271,689 square feet in Cedar Park, Texas. The campus was sold by a joint venture of Central Texas investment firms to San Francisco-based Stockbridge. The property features modern shallow-bay, rear-load buildings with high clear heights and ample loading and parking.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: New Hope

Address: 200 W New Hope Dr, Cedar Park, TX*

Size: 271,689 SF

Year Built: 2024

Site Area: 28.5 acres

Notable Features: Three shallow-bay, rear-load buildings with 32-foot clear heights, 200-foot truck court depths, 17 percent office finishes, 82 dock-high doors, and 579 parking spaces.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
