Fading momentum? Not in Detroit, according to the Detroit Commercial Real Estate Mid-Year Forecast

REJournals — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 10:03:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Forecast

The Detroit-area commercial real estate market continues to show strong momentum across industrial, retail, multifamily, and healthcare sectors, with a brighter outlook emerging for the office sector. This positive sentiment was a key takeaway from Michigan Real Estate Journal's 11th annual Detroit Commercial Real Estate Mid-Year Forecast. The event highlighted ongoing development projects reshaping the city's skyline and surrounding communities, indicating sustained construction activity. Experts noted rising demand for apartments in both urban and suburban areas, underscoring the multifamily market's strength.
