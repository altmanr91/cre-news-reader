AI Agent Platform Parloa Quickly Snaps Up More Office Space at 1245 Broadway

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 20:50:30 +0000

SUMMARY

AI agent platform Parloa has expanded its office footprint at 1245 Broadway in Midtown, New York City, just six months after its initial lease. The company added 8,300 square feet, bringing its total occupancy to 23,031 square feet under a new five-year lease. The expansion, which saw asking rents rise to $125 per square foot, signifies strong demand for modern, sustainable office environments and has brought the building to full occupancy.
