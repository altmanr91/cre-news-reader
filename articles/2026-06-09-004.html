Berkadia Provides $35M Refi for Detroit-Area Multifamily Apartment Complex

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 20:40:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Fenton Management Company has secured a $35.2 million fixed-rate loan to refinance the Indian Lake Village Apartments, a 394-unit multifamily community located in Lake Orion, Michigan. Berkadia originated the 10-year loan, which will be securitized by Freddie Mac. The property's strong fundamentals and resident demand in a desirable suburban market near Detroit contributed to favorable financing terms.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Indian Lake Village Apartments

Address: 3001 Lake Village Boulevard, Lake Orion, MI

Units: 394

Loan Amount: $35.2M

Project Notes: The property was built between 1995 and 1997.
