Billionaire Randal Kirk sells Manalapan estate for $63M after slashing price

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 07 May 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Billionaire Randal Kirk sold his Manalapan estate at 820 South Ocean Boulevard for $62.5 million to Westview Holdings, a Delaware LLC. The property, initially listed for $134 million, saw significant price reductions before closing at less than half its original asking price. The 2.3-acre compound features a 15,500-square-foot main house, a guest house, two pools, a tennis court, a dock, and 215 feet of beach frontage.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Manalapan has become a hub for trophy real estate deals, attracting high-net-worth individuals due to its large estates offering both beachfront and Intracoastal Waterway access, suitable for large yachts. The market also benefits from its proximity to Palm Beach, where comparable properties are scarcer and more expensive. Recent notable sales include a $105 million oceanfront land assemblage and a $51.7 million ocean-to-lake estate.
