Former Spectra Energy HQ in Houston’s Galleria area for sale after foreclosure

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:56:15 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The former Spectra Energy Headquarters, a nine-story, 632,000-square-foot office building in Houston's Galleria area, is now being marketed for sale by JLL after a foreclosure. The property, previously owned by PTAD Realty, was transferred to lender Argentic on June 2 following the expiration of Enbridge's lease and the transfer of a $52.5 million loan to special servicing. The building's appraised value has significantly declined from $84.5 million in 2014 to $20.7 million, reflecting the impact of its primary tenant vacating and broader office market conditions.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

An older office building at 919 Milam Street, with 20% occupancy, was recently purchased and may be converted to residential. The 3000 Post Oak Boulevard building is also facing foreclosure after being 99% vacant in 2023, with residential conversion also a possibility.
