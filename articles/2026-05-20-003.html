Benchmark Buys Upper West Side Apartment Building From Heller for $42M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 19 May 2026 21:06:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Benchmark Real Estate Group has acquired a 90-unit prewar apartment building on Manhattan's Upper West Side from The Heller Organization for $42 million. This transaction marks Benchmark's second deal with Heller in less than a year, following a $66 million acquisition in September 2025. The property, built in 1925, is located at 698 West End Avenue and features one-bedroom apartments starting at $3,200 per month.
