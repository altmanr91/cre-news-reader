ZSD bets on family-sized condos in West Loop

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 17:45:07 +0000

SUMMARY

ZSD is developing 1282 Washington, a five-story condominium building in Chicago's West Loop, featuring 16 family-sized units with at least four bedrooms and outdoor space. The developer acquired the 18,000-square-foot site for $5 million in April and has already pre-sold 12 of the units, with prices ranging from $1.75 million to $3.2 million. The project is strategically located near highly-rated schools, appealing to families, and is expected to be completed by fall 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: 1282 Washington

Address: 1282 Washington Boulevard, Chicago, IL

Units: 16

Completion: fall 2027

Site Area: 0.41 acres

Notable Features: Each unit will have at least four bedrooms and outdoor space. The development is located two blocks from Mark T. Skinner Elementary West.

Project Notes: The 18,000-square-foot site was previously owned by a toy company and was acquired by ZSD for $5 million in April. A demolition permit was received on June 17.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Loop area is experiencing significant changes with office-to-residential conversions due to hybrid work schedules and tenant departures. Downtown rents are spiking, and buildings are trading at steep discounts, indicating a market in transition.
