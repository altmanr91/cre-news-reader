Hyatt in SoCal’s Inland Empire Secures $104M to Finance Upgrades

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Fri, 15 May 2026 20:30:33 +0000

SUMMARY

National Core, a nonprofit development firm, has secured $103.6 million in tax-exempt bond financing for the renovation of the Hyatt Regency hotel in Ontario, CA. The financing package, which includes tax-exempt hotel revenue bonds and C-PACE bonds, will fund a significant redevelopment of the previously distressed property. The project will reduce the room count from 309 to 295 by enlarging suites, and will also include a lobby redesign, new food and beverage options, and upgraded amenities.
