TPG Joins Norges, Canadian Pensions On $2B Grocery-Anchored Bet

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 11:17:16 -0400

SUMMARY

A consortium led by TPG Real Estate, alongside PSP Investments, La Caisse, and Norges Bank Investment Management, has acquired Echo Realty, a prominent operator of grocery-anchored retail centers across the Midwest and Southeast. The transaction values Echo Realty at approximately $2 billion. The new ownership group plans to expand Echo's business in both existing and new markets, leveraging its established platforms in leasing and property management. This acquisition underscores a continued institutional investor focus on resilient, needs-based retail segments.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Size: 16,000,000 SF

Sale Price: $2.00B

Notable Features: Portfolio includes over 230 retail centers primarily anchored by dominant grocery tenants such as Giant Eagle, Publix, Harris Teeter, Safeway, Acme Markets, and Whole Foods, as well as convenience store anchors.
