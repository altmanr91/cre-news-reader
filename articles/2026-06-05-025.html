Fairfield home sets non-waterfront record with $12M sale

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 20:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A newly rebuilt seven-bedroom, seven-bathroom home in Fairfield, CT, sold for $12.3 million, setting a new record for a non-waterfront property in the town. The sellers, who purchased the 1.3-acre property in 2020 for $2.3 million, undertook a significant renovation, effectively rebuilding the home. The sale reflects the booming housing market in New York City suburbs, where median sales prices have surged 86% from 2016 to 2025.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The median home price in Southport, part of Fairfield, more than doubled to $1.4 million between 2020 and 2026. Median sales prices in New York City suburbs increased by 86% from 2016 to 2025, compared to a 43% increase for homes within the city during the same period.
