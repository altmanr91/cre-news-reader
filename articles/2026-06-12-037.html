Bluffview’s priciest spec home lists for $7M as builders bank on lure of Preston Hollow

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Spec builder Catena Homes has listed a newly constructed luxury residence in Dallas' Bluffview neighborhood for $6.8 million, making it the most expensive new home in the area. The builder is banking on continued corporate relocations to the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex to attract high-end buyers, despite a broader market where mid-tier homes are seeing price reductions. The property, located at 4405 Wildwood Road, was built this year and features six bedrooms and nine bathrooms across 7,000 square feet. The developer acquired the lot in October 2024 for $1.9 million, highlighting the rapid appreciation of land values in secondary luxury neighborhoods.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

DFW topped the list of national corporate relocation hotspots, netting 11 new headquarters in 2025. The typical Dallas-Fort Worth home sold for 98.4 percent of its asking price in May 2021 and 104.7 percent in May 2022. A similarly sized homesite in Bluffview listed for $2.2 million and sold for $2.6 million months after Catena's acquisition.
