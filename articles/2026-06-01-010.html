SimonCRE Acquires 10 Acres to Develop Prasada East Retail Project in Surprise, Arizona

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:24:40 +0000

SUMMARY

SimonCRE has acquired a 10-acre parcel in Surprise, Arizona, to develop Prasada East, a 98,000-square-foot open-air retail center. The project, which will break ground in spring 2026, will be anchored by a Whole Foods Market and include several other confirmed tenants, complementing the larger Prasada master-planned development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Prasada East

Size: 98,000 SF

Site Area: 10 acres

Notable Features: Open-air retail center anchored by Whole Foods Market, complementing the existing Village at Prasada and Prasada North developments.

Project Notes: Initial phase will feature a 35,100-square-foot Whole Foods Market.
