Malabar Hill Breaks Ground on 27,000 SF Retail Project in Manvel, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 19 May 2026 14:24:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Malabar Hill Capital has commenced construction on The Market at Meridiana, a 27,000-square-foot neighborhood shopping center in Manvel, Texas. The project is strategically located at the entrance of the Meridiana master-planned community and is already 54 percent preleased, indicating strong tenant demand in the area.
