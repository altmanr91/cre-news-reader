Joseph Nicholas Construction breaks ground on Porsche dealership in Barrington

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Tue, 12 May 2026 16:29:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Joseph Nicholas Construction has broken ground on a new 133,960-square-foot Porsche dealership in Barrington, Illinois, for Murgado Automotive Group. This two-story facility, set to open in late spring 2027, will be the largest Porsche dealership in Illinois and among the largest nationally. The project will relocate Murgado's existing Porsche dealership from the Motor Werks of Barrington auto mall to a dedicated, expanded facility, enhancing showroom capacity, service offerings, and on-site inventory storage.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Barrington Porsche

Address: 24 E. Dundee Road, Barrington, IL

Size: 133,960 SF

Completion: late spring 2027

Notable Features: Largest Porsche dealership in Illinois, one of 16 Manthey Certified dealers in the country, includes a basement level for 90 inventory cars.

Project Notes: New facility for Murgado Automotive Group's existing Porsche dealership, which is currently operating within the Motor Werks of Barrington auto mall.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Joseph Nicholas Construction

OWNER: Murgado Automotive Group
