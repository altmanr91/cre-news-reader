Menashe strikes again with massive discount on LaSalle Street tower

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:46:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Menashe Properties is under contract to acquire the 38-story office tower at 180 North LaSalle Street in Chicago for an estimated $55 million to $60 million. This marks the firm's third downtown Chicago office acquisition in less than three years, continuing its strategy of purchasing distressed assets at a significant discount. The seller, Canadian pension fund La Caisse, is taking a substantial loss on the property, which it acquired for $198.5 million in 2016. Menashe Properties is betting on the building's proximity to Google's future Chicago headquarters to drive a rebound in the central Loop office market.
