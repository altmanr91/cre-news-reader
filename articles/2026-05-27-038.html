Another Sonoma County estate comes to market with local price record in crosshairs

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 26 May 2026 22:25:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Two luxury estates in Sonoma County, "The Summit" and "Ghost Donkey Ranch," have been listed for sale, potentially setting new price records for the region. The Summit, a 1,119-acre property with a 10-bedroom home, is asking just under $40 million, while the 227-acre Ghost Donkey Ranch, featuring a 4,200-square-foot home and a working cattle ranch, is listed for $35 million. Both properties are poised to surpass the current county record of $30 million, set in 2010.
