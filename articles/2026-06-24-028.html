Skokie home breaks sale record with $2M-plus closing

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A single-family home in Skokie, IL, sold for $2.25 million, setting a new record for the Chicago suburb. The property, built in 2006, features a home theater, gym, and a backyard opening onto the Evanston Golf Club. The sale price of $401 per square foot reflects the custom-built nature of the residence.
