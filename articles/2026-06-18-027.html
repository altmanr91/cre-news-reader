Kolter starts Delray Live Local rental project with $92M financing

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 23:16:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Kolter Multifamily has commenced construction on Alton Delray, a 386-unit multifamily development in Delray Beach, after securing $91.7 million in financing from U.S. Bank. The project, located at 2101 South Congress Avenue, will dedicate 40% of its units to workforce housing under Florida's Live Local Act, allowing the developer to proceed without rezoning on the industrial-zoned site. The development will feature five mid-rise buildings totaling 460,000 square feet, with amenities including a clubhouse, pool, and dog park, and is expected to deliver its first apartments in late 2027.
