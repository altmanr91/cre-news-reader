NBCUniversal offshoot locks down 250K sf in Midtown Manhattan

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 27 May 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Versant, an NBCUniversal spinoff, has expanded its office footprint at 229 West 43rd Street in Midtown Manhattan, adding 85,000 square feet to its existing 165,000 square feet, bringing its total to 250,000 square feet. The 18-year lease for the additional space, which includes floors 11, 12, and 14, was secured with landlord Columbia Property Trust. The property is a significant media hub, maintaining a high occupancy rate in a strong Manhattan office market.

DATA POINTS

Notable Features: The space will include an outdoor terrace and custom-built studios for media brands.

Project Notes: This expansion adds to 165,000 square feet taken a year earlier, bringing the total footprint to 250,000 square feet. Columbia Property Trust acquired the office portion of the building in 2015 for $516 million.
