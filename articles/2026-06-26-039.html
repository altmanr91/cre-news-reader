Connect Midwest Multifamily Trends 2026: Navigating Capital Flow & Deal Dynamics

Connect CRE — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 22:08:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The multifamily sector in the Midwest is experiencing a return of investment competition, driven by sellers adjusting pricing expectations and a steady resurgence of buyer interest. Industry experts at Connect Midwest Multifamily Trends 2026 discussed strategies for navigating capital flows, securing financing, and managing loan maturities. Key insights included the increasing competitiveness for capital in larger deals, the use of high-leverage financing for quick value creation, and the growing acceptance of PACE as a mainstream financing solution to mitigate bank participation risk. Despite market shifts, the fundamentals of the Midwest multifamily market, particularly in Chicago, remain strong.
