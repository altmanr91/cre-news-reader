IQHQ Lands Full-Building Lease At Andover Life Sciences Campus

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 11:02:00 -0400

SUMMARY

IQHQ has secured a significant full-building, long-term lease with clinical-stage radiopharmaceutical company AdvanCell for 128,000 square feet at 1 Corporate Drive within its Innovation Park campus in Andover, MA. This transaction brings the second phase of the life sciences campus to full occupancy, marking a notable success for the developer in a challenging Greater Boston life sciences market. The campus, acquired by IQHQ in 2020 for $44 million, has undergone extensive renovations to include common area improvements and amenities, enhancing its appeal to life sciences tenants.
