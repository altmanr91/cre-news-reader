JLL Arranges Sale of 76,426 SF Shopping Center in North Dallas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 12 May 2026 14:43:31 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has facilitated the sale of Shops at Mockingbird, a 76,426-square-foot retail center in North Dallas. The property, anchored by a fitness tenant and featuring a strong tenant mix, was 94.7 percent occupied at the time of sale. JLL represented the seller, Dunhill Partners, in this transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Shops at Mockingbird

Address: 4550 W Mockingbird Ln, Dallas, TX*

Size: 76,426 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 94.7%

Year Built: 2008

Site Area: 6.4 acres

Notable Features: Anchored by a fitness tenant; located adjacent to Dallas Love Field Airport.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
