REPORT: Blackstone In Talks To Buy H&R REIT

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 16:45:26 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Blackstone is reportedly in discussions to acquire H&R REIT, a Toronto-based company with a diversified portfolio of over 20M SF of North American real estate. The potential takeover talks, which began last year, previously included TPG and Crestpoint Real Estate Investments, but Blackstone is now the sole remaining suitor. H&R REIT has been strategically repositioning its portfolio, divesting from office and retail assets to focus on multifamily and industrial properties in the U.S. and Canada, with residential assets now comprising 60% of its portfolio.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

REIT mergers and acquisitions have seen increased activity in the past year. Blackstone and partners acquired Alexander & Baldwin, the largest owner of grocery-anchored shopping centers in Hawaii, for $1.5B in December. Blackstone also launched a data center mortgage REIT that raised $1.75B. AvalonBay Communities and Equity Residential agreed to a merger in May, creating a 180,000-unit entity with a $69B enterprise value.
