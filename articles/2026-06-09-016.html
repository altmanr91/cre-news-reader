LISTEN: The real estate deep dive into artificial intelligence

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 21:51:19 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article summarizes a podcast discussion on the potential impact of artificial intelligence on the real estate industry. The conversation explores how AI is influencing residential transactions, with clients using AI-generated information to inform their decisions. In the commercial sector, AI presents both challenges, such as potential disruption in the office market as tenants delay long-term plans, and opportunities for increased efficiency and new demand from growth-oriented companies. The overall sentiment is that while AI's long-term effects are still uncertain, it is poised to significantly reshape real estate.
