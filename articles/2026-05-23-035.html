Tech firm snags foreclosed San Jose office building at 24% discount

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 22 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A tech firm, Rocket EMS, acquired a foreclosed 78,200-square-foot research and office building at 5729 Fontanoso Way in San Jose, CA, for $18.8 million. The property was sold by asset management firm Evergreen Advantage, which had taken possession of the building after it went into default and foreclosure. The sale price represents a significant discount from its 2024 appraised value of $24.5 million, highlighting the distress in the Bay Area commercial real estate market.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Other recent office transactions in San Jose include BGO's acquisition of Rio Tech Office Park for $164.3 million (a slight discount from its 2021 price) and Paceline Investors' purchase of 2581 Junction Avenue for $12.2 million, a 61.9% discount from its 2018 sale price.
