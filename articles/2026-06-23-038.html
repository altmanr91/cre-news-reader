Passco Cos. Founder And DST Pioneer Bill Passo Dies At 84

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 12:01:18 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Obituary

William "Bill" Passo, the founder and CEO of multifamily investment firm Passco Cos., passed away at the age of 84. Passo was a pioneer in fractionalized 1031 ownership structures, including tenancy-in-common (TIC) and Delaware statutory trust (DST) commercial real estate investments, which significantly shaped the market for securitized 1031 exchanges. His leadership helped advance these investment models, opening new pathways for real estate investors.
