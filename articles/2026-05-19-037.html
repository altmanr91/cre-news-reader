Harbor Associates, Farallon Acquire Yorba Linda Industrial Campus Off-Market

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Mon, 18 May 2026 21:55:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Harbor Associates and Farallon Capital Management have acquired the Yorba Linda Commerce Center, a 280,000-square-foot industrial campus in Yorba Linda, CA, for $81 million in an off-market transaction. The property, which was 98% leased at closing, offers local businesses a high-quality, irreplaceable location with a mix of suite sizes to support current and future operational needs. The acquisition was financed with a $56.3 million loan from an affiliate of QuadReal Property Group.
