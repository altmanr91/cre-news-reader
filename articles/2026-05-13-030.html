Glenn Beck’s nonprofit inks massive Las Colinas lease

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 12 May 2026 22:00:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Glenn Beck's nonprofit, Mercury One, has signed a significant lease for the entire 172,089-square-foot office building at 6655 MacArthur Boulevard in Irving, TX. This transaction represents the largest direct new office lease in Las Colinas in the past five years. The humanitarian aid charity plans to relocate its American Journey Experience Museum to the new space, with the lease commencing in November and extending through October 2038.
