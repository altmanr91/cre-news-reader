Marcus & Millichap Launches Network of Preferred Partners

Connect CRE — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 11:33:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Marcus & Millichap has launched its Preferred Partner Program, an initiative to connect its commercial real estate clients with a curated network of third-party service providers. This program aims to support clients throughout the investment lifecycle by offering expertise in areas such as insurance, tax strategies, operational services, and risk management. The firm's CEO stated that the program extends their value-added approach, helping clients and investment professionals maximize returns by accessing valuable professional resources for informed decision-making and long-term investment success.
