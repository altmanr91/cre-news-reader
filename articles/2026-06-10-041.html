Ameriprise Signs 10-Year Renewal for One World Trade Center Space

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 20:56:21 +0000

SUMMARY

Ameriprise Financial, Inc. has renewed its 37,704-square-foot lease on the 78th floor of One World Trade Center in New York City for another 10 years. The global financial services firm has maintained a corporate presence in the building since 2016. This recommitment highlights the building's appeal to financial services firms and the overall strength of the Lower Manhattan office market, which is currently 97% leased.
