SFA Agency Extends, Expands Tenancy at One Battery Park Plaza

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 21:14:37 +0000

SUMMARY

SFA Agency LLC, an affiliate of Northwestern Mutual, has extended its lease and expanded its footprint at One Battery Park Plaza in Lower Manhattan. The financial services company will now occupy 14,071 square feet in a 10-year lease extension, adding 5,170 square feet to its existing space. This transaction highlights the continued demand for high-quality, well-located office space in Lower Manhattan, bringing the building to near full occupancy.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: One Battery Park Plaza

Size: 14,071 SF

Current Occupancy: 99.0%

Project Notes: SFA Agency has been a tenant since 2023 and will relocate within the building in early 2027.
