Industrial and dev sites drive South Florida i-sales boom, but party’s over for multifamily

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 18 May 2026 21:20:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Investment Sales Report

Investment sales activity in South Florida surged by nearly 30% year-over-year in the first quarter, reaching $4.3 billion across 244 transactions, according to an Avison Young report. This growth was primarily driven by a 102.4% increase in industrial deal volume, totaling $1.3 billion, and a 123% jump in development site sales, which hit $503.9 million. Retail and office sectors also saw increases, while multifamily transactions experienced a notable decline after several years of strong performance, attributed to a slowdown in new resident influx and a surge in new unit completions.
