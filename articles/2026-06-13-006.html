Harbert South Bay Breaks Ground on 162-Unit Seniors Housing Project in Dallas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 15:15:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Harbert South Bay has commenced construction on The Marianne on Inwood, a 162-unit seniors housing community in Dallas. This 294,000-square-foot facility will provide a continuum of care options, including independent living, assisted living, and memory care. The project is slated for completion in 2027 and will feature a comprehensive suite of amenities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: seniors housing

Property Name: The Marianne on Inwood

Size: 294,000 SF

Units: 162

Completion: 2027

Notable Features: Offers independent living, assisted living, and memory care residences. Amenities include multiple dining venues, indoor and outdoor pools, a gym, hair salon, media lounge, and a golf simulator. The site is adjacent to the Highland Park area.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Harbert South Bay

ARCHITECT: Arcadis

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Bozzuto Construction

OWNER: Momentum Senior Living
