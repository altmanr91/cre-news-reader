Broward County drops pursuit of Spirit Airlines Dania Beach HQ

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Broward County has withdrawn its motion to bid on a portion of Spirit Airlines' former 615,600-square-foot headquarters in Dania Beach, Florida, which is slated for auction next month. The county had considered acquiring the property for a new government building, citing potential savings of $350 million compared to new construction. However, the motion was withdrawn due to zoning concerns and the lack of prior written approval from Kimco Realty, the owner of the larger mixed-use development Dania Pointe where the headquarters is located. The property is being sold as part of Spirit Airlines' liquidation efforts following its cessation of operations.
