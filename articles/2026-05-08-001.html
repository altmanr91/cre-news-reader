Soloviev Group’s 9 West 57th Street Seals $1.8B CMBS Refi from Bank of America

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 07 May 2026 21:49:21 +0000

SUMMARY

The Soloviev Group has secured a $1.8 billion CMBS refinancing for its 9 West 57th Street office tower in New York City, marking the largest CMBS loan in the city this year. The loan, led by Bank of America, reflects the building's strong tenant demand and increased occupancy, which rose from 72.6 percent at the time of acquisition in 2020 to 89.9 percent by the end of 2025. The refinancing is expected to value the property at $2.9 billion upon stabilization, underscoring the asset's robust performance in the Midtown Manhattan market.
