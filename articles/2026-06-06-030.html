Dickies Arena looking to wrangle new naming rights sponsor

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A legal dispute has emerged over the naming rights for Dickies Arena in Fort Worth, TX, as the managing nonprofit, Trail Drive Management, seeks to terminate its agreement with the Dickies brand. The core of the conflict stems from two changes in ownership of the Dickies brand since the 2017 naming rights deal, with Trail Drive alleging that the current owner, Bluestar Alliance, is attempting to avoid contractual obligations. Trail Drive is now actively searching for a new naming rights sponsor for the 560,000-square-foot arena, which opened in 2019.
