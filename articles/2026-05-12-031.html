Pritzker pitches housing plan on Matt Laricy podcast as real estate alliance deepens

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 11 May 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Illinois Governor JB Pritzker is actively promoting his "Building Up Illinois Developments" (Build) plan, a legislative package aimed at increasing housing supply and affordability across the state. The plan, which includes proposals to mandate small multi-unit buildings on residential lots and legalize accessory dwelling units statewide, is being advanced through a strategic alliance with the real estate industry. Pritzker recently appeared on a prominent Chicago real estate podcast to discuss the plan, emphasizing its goal to add more homes without creating massive density increases, and addressing concerns from local governments about potential overreach.
