Lombard’s LSC Demolition adds director of operations

REJournals — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 09:54:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Lacey McKenzie has been appointed as the Director of Operations at LSC Demolition, a Lombard, Illinois-based company. In her new role, McKenzie will be responsible for executive oversight of operations, supporting strategic growth, and enhancing the company's commitment to safety, quality, and client service. Her extensive background in construction and operations management is expected to drive operational excellence within the organization.
