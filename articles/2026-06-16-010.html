Partnership Breaks Ground on 809,141 SF Industrial Park in Durham

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:04:48 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Hines and an Ares Real Estate fund has broken ground on Parkside Commerce Center, an 809,141-square-foot industrial park in Durham, NC. The project will be developed in two phases, with the first phase expected to deliver in the fourth quarter of 2027. Foundry Commercial has been retained to manage leasing and marketing for the new industrial development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Parkside Commerce Center

Size: 809,141 SF

Completion: 4Q 2027 (Phase I)

Phase I: Building 1 (237,824 SF) and Building 2 (283,724 SF)

Notable Features: Adjacent to Research Triangle Park. Phase I buildings will feature 36-foot clear heights and 63 trailer stalls. Phase II buildings will have 32-foot clear heights.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Hines

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Ares Real Estate

LEASING AGENT: Foundry Commercial
