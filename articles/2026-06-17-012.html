Peerless Development begins pre-leasing at 1880 N. Milwaukee apartment development in Chicago

REJournals — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Peerless Development has commenced pre-leasing for 1880 Milwaukee, a new 44-unit boutique apartment community in Chicago's Bucktown neighborhood, with first residents expected in August 2026. The five-story mid-rise will offer one- and two-bedroom floor plans, along with 3,200 square feet of ground-level retail space. The development is designed to complement the neighborhood's character and offers strong transit-oriented access, addressing the demand for contemporary housing in an area with limited new multifamily construction.
