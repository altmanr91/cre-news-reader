Cravey Real Estate Arranges Sale of 13,014 SF Industrial Building in Corpus Christi

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 15:18:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Cravey Real Estate Services has facilitated the sale of a 13,014-square-foot industrial building located at 3930 Bratton Road in Corpus Christi, TX. The property, which includes 1,000 square feet of office space, was sold by a family estate to Victory Investment Portfolio LLC.
