“Straight out of Las Vegas”: Developers complete E11even Residences in Miami

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

E11even Partners and PMG have completed the first phase of their E11even-branded condo development in Miami, with nearly all 457 units in the initial tower having closed. The 65-story E11even Club Hotel & Residences Miami, located in the Park West district, was the first high-rise condo tower in Miami to sell out in the post-pandemic market, achieving 90 percent presales within two months in 2021. The project's total sellout value is approximately $375 million, and it features extensive amenities including multiple pools, a casino-style sports lounge, and a Deepak Chopra spa. The developers repaid nearly $150 million in construction financing from Starwood Property Trust in May.
