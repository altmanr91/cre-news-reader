The NRP Group marks groundbreaking of 348-unit affordable housing community in Southeast Austin

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 15 May 2026 16:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The NRP Group and the Housing Authority of Travis County have broken ground on Sanara, a 348-unit affordable housing community in Southeast Austin, TX. This development, located on a 56-acre site with 18 acres dedicated to residential use, will provide housing for households earning up to 60 percent of the Area Median Income. The project is strategically located near major employment centers like Tesla's Gigafactory and Samsung's semiconductor facilities, and features a unit mix heavily weighted towards family-oriented floorplans with three- and four-bedroom layouts. This marks The NRP Group's first tax credit partnership and third overall collaboration with the Housing Authority of Travis County, underscoring a commitment to expanding affordable housing in high-growth areas.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Sanara

Address: 4401 E. Slaughter Lane, Austin, TX

Units: 348

Site Area: 56 acres

Notable Features: All units are reserved for households earning up to 60 percent of the Area Median Income (AMI); more than 60 percent of residences offer three- and four-bedroom layouts; 18 acres dedicated to residential use, with the remaining floodplain land dedicated to the City of Austin Parks Department for trail connectivity.

Project Notes: This is The NRP Group’s first tax credit partnership with the Housing Authority of Travis County and its third collaboration overall with the agency. Safehold is participating as a ground lessor, marking the first use of its ground lease structure for an affordable multifamily development in Texas.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
