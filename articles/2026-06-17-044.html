'We've Taken Control Of Our Destiny': QuadReal Unveils Plan For BTR Growth

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 05:01:24 -0400

SUMMARY

QuadReal Property Group, the real estate arm of British Columbia Investment Management Corporation, is significantly expanding its build-to-rent (BTR) and purpose-built student accommodation (PBSA) portfolios in the UK and Ireland. This strategic growth follows its acquisition of long-term partner Realstar and its resident-facing brand Uncle last year, creating a vertically integrated residential business in Europe. The company plans to grow through both acquisitions of operational real estate and selective development, leveraging its global experience and focusing on direct operational control to manage risk and enhance service offerings. QuadReal recently announced a £120M forward-funding deal for a 241-unit BTR development in Woolwich, London, and is actively exploring further opportunities in markets like Dublin, Leeds, Manchester, and Birmingham, driven by strong fundamentals and acute undersupply in the living sectors.
