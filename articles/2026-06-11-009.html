SkyWalker Buys 73,747 SF Office Building in North Fort Worth

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 15:19:42 +0000

SUMMARY

SkyWalker Property Partners has acquired Alliance Commerce Center, a 73,747-square-foot office building in North Fort Worth, from Group O. The property was 90 percent leased at the time of sale and is situated within the AllianceTexas development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Alliance Commerce Center

Address: 2301 Eagle Pkwy, Fort Worth, TX*

Size: 73,747 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 90.0%

Notable Features: Located within AllianceTexas.
