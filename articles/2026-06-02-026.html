Scotiabank opens Dallas expansion with MapleMark Bank acquisition

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 21:53:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Scotiabank, a major Canadian financial institution, is expanding its presence in the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex by acquiring Maple Financial Holdings, the parent company of MapleMark Bank. This acquisition will allow Scotiabank to offer FDIC deposit insurance to its clients and further establish its regional hub in Uptown Dallas. MapleMark Bank primarily serves middle-market companies and high-net-worth individuals in the Dallas-Fort Worth area.
