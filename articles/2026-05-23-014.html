The Missner Group acquires three industrial properties in Northwest Indiana, Indianapolis markets

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Fri, 22 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Missner Group has significantly expanded its industrial portfolio by acquiring three Class-A industrial properties totaling over 840,000 square feet across Northwest Indiana and the Indianapolis market. This strategic acquisition nearly doubles the company's industrial holdings to more than 2.2 million square feet. The properties, which include a two-building portfolio in Merrillville and a logistics facility in Whitestown, are fully leased to a strong tenant mix, underscoring their appeal in active distribution corridors.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Size: 843,531 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Notable Features: The Merrillville properties offer direct access to I-65, I-80/I-94, and I-90. The Whitestown facility features 32-foot clear heights, ESFR sprinklers, LED warehouse lighting, 30 dock doors, and trailer parking.

Project Notes: The Merrillville portfolio was built in 2021 and 2022. The Whitestown property was completed in 2023.
