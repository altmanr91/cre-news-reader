Pulte set to pay $14M to develop Naperville duplexes in unincorporated wetlands

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 16:15:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Pulte Home Company is poised to acquire a 32-acre undeveloped wetland property in Naperville, IL, from the DuPage Water Commission for $14 million. The developer plans to construct a 94-unit duplex neighborhood, named "Naperville Ridge," targeting empty nesters and pre-empty nesters. The project faces local opposition due to its wetland location bordering a forest preserve and concerns over tree removal, despite Pulte's adjustments to preserve some wetland areas.
