Terra, BH to pay $100M for billionaire Jeff Greene’s West Palm land

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 22:06:22 +0000

SUMMARY

Terra and BH Group are set to acquire a 1.6-acre waterfront site in West Palm Beach from billionaire Jeff Greene for approximately $100 million. The developers plan to construct two 31-story condominium towers on the property, which will include 281 residential units, retail space, and a shared underground parking garage. The site's value has seen a significant increase since its last sale in 2014, reflecting strong demand for prime waterfront development opportunities in the area.
