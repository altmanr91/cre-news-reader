One of NYC’s Largest Residential Co-Ops Repositions Its 50K-SF Retail Portfolio

Commercial Observer — Wed, 27 May 2026 12:00:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Leasing / Portfolio Management

Seward Park Cooperative, one of New York City's largest market-rate residential co-ops, has engaged Lee & Associates NYC to manage and reposition its extensive 50,000-square-foot retail portfolio in the Lower East Side. This strategic move aims to modernize the co-op's commercial holdings, capitalize on the neighborhood's evolving retail landscape, and reduce operational costs. The Lee & Associates team plans to attract larger brands and establish the portfolio as a destination, while continuing to service the local community.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

New York City's retail market sustained strong leasing momentum in 2025, with availability hitting a 10-year low. However, the Lower East Side and East Village had a 15.43% retail vacancy rate in December, the second-highest among Manhattan's community districts.
