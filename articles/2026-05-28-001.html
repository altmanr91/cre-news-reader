Law Firm Taft Inks 17K SF at Durst’s 1155 Avenue of the Americas for First NYC Office

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 28 May 2026 12:00:59 +0000

SUMMARY

National law firm Taft has secured its first New York City office, leasing 16,563 square feet on the entire 24th floor of The Durst Organization's 1155 Avenue of the Americas. The firm, specializing in employment law and corporate transactions, chose the Midtown tower for its strategic location near Bryant Park and major transit hubs, aiming to establish a strong foundation for nationwide growth. The new office will house an energy, infrastructure, and project finance group, marking a significant expansion for Taft following recent mergers that boosted its attorney count and revenue.
