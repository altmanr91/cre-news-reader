Partnership Acquires 549,170 SF Pinnacle Tower Office Building in North Dallas

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:15:38 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership between Estein USA and Vanderbilt Office Properties has acquired Pinnacle Tower, a 549,170-square-foot office building in North Dallas, from New York Life. The 24-story building was 88 percent leased at the time of sale and features a strong amenity package. JLL has been appointed as the new leasing agent for the property.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Pinnacle Tower

Address: 5005 Lyndon B Johnson Fwy, Dallas, TX*

Size: 549,170 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 88.0%

Notable Features: The 24-story building offers amenities such as a café, tenant lounge, conference center, coffee bar, outdoor amenity area, and a fitness center.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Estein USA

BUYER: Vanderbilt Office Properties

SELLER: New York Life

LEASING AGENT: JLL
