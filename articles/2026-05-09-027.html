Investors accuse Joe Farrell of using Wellington project as “personal piggy bank”

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 08 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Investors in a 23-acre single-family home development in Wellington, FL, are suing developer Joe Farrell, alleging he misappropriated approximately $6 million in investor funds. The lawsuit, filed in Palm Beach County, claims breach of contract, negligence, and conspiracy to commit fraud, asserting that funds were improperly redirected to pay salaries and vendors for other projects and to enrich the defendants. Farrell denies all allegations, stating that all funds were properly allocated and that the lawsuit was triggered by an investor's attempt to secure a "garbage loan" for the project, which he rejected.
