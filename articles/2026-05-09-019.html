Soloviev winning streak rages on with $1.8B refinancing

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:02:15 +0000

SUMMARY

The Soloviev Group is securing a $1.8 billion refinancing loan for its trophy office property at 9 West 57th Street in New York City. The five-year loan, provided by a syndicate of major banks, will replace an existing $1.2 billion mortgage and fund closing costs, landlord obligations, and provide significant cash to the owner. This refinancing follows a recent record-setting office lease at the property, underscoring the building's strong market position and the owner's strategy to achieve premium rents.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 9 West 57th Street

Address: 9 West 57th Street, New York City, NY

Loan Amount: $1.80B

Notable Features: The building features a renovated lobby, upgraded elevators and technology systems, a fitness center with two pools and a golf simulator, and ground-floor art installations by notable artists.

Project Notes: The refinancing is expected to close by the end of May 2026. The building is valued at $3.9 billion upon stabilization. The previous mortgage was $1.2 billion at a 2.85% interest rate. The new loan has an interest rate of 4.97%.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

In 2025, there were 28 office deals in New York City at $200 per square foot or higher, including six deals above $250 per square foot. A recent record-setting lease at 9 West 57th Street was for 5,063 square feet on the 50th floor at $327.50 per square foot for a 10-year term. The previous record was held by SL Green's One Vanderbilt in 2022, with a 9,871 square-foot deal exceeding $300 per square foot.
