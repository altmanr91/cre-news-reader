MMCC Secures Financing for El Cajon Multifamily

LOAN

Connect CRE — Mon, 11 May 2026 21:32:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap Capital Corporation (MMCC) has secured $3.96 million in financing for Penny Lane Apartments, a 14-unit multifamily property in El Cajon, CA. The 30-year, fully amortized loan was arranged with a national bank on behalf of a private client, achieving a 65% loan-to-value. The property's diverse unit mix and strategic location with access to the San Diego employment base attracted competitive lender interest, aligning with the client's long-term investment goals.

DATA POINTS

Notable Features: Offers units ranging from studios to three bedrooms, pet-friendly, with amenities including balconies or patios, a barbeque and picnic area, laundry facilities, a courtyard, and a dog run. Located approximately 17 miles from downtown San Diego.

Project Notes: The loan is fully amortized over 30 years at 65% loan-to-value.
