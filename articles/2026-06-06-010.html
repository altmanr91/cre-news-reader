Panepinto Properties Begins Leasing 53-Story Apartment Tower in Jersey City

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 14:40:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Panepinto Properties has commenced leasing at 505 Summit, a 53-story apartment tower in Jersey City's Journal Square. The development offers 605 residential units along with street-level retail space and a range of amenities. Construction on the project, financed by Pacific Life, began in the third quarter of 2023 and topped out last summer.
