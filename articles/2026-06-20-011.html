Kidder Closes Hollywood Multifamily Sale Despite Softening Rental Trends

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 20:36:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Kidder Mathews successfully brokered the $7.6 million sale of The Carlton Apartments, a 36-unit multifamily property in Hollywood. Despite a softening rental market in the area, the property attracted significant buyer interest, underscoring the importance of persistent brokerage efforts to connect sellers with suitable investors.
