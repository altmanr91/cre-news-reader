“There is no ceiling”: Nadim Ashi’s latest Surf Club condos near $400M sellout

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:09:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Fort Partners has achieved a nearly $400 million sellout for its recently completed Seaway North condo building, part of the larger Surf Club complex in Surfside, FL. The 10-unit building, located at 9165 Collins Avenue, boasts an unparalleled average sale price of $38.6 million per unit in Miami-Dade County's new development market. This success is attributed to the collaboration with the Four Seasons brand, high-end finishes, and the boutique nature of the project, which has garnered "it-building" status amid a significant wealth migration to South Florida.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The South Florida condo market is experiencing high demand for ultraluxury sales, driven by wealth migration to the region. The Surf Club complex is achieving some of the highest prices per square foot in the market, with brokers predicting $6,000 per square foot will soon be the norm within the complex due to limited inventory and strong buyer interest.
