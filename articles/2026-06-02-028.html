Second Downtown Austin hotel heads to foreclosure

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 17:08:27 +0000

SUMMARY

The Hyatt Centric hotel in Downtown Austin is slated for a foreclosure auction due to a default on an $85 million loan. This marks the second downtown hotel facing foreclosure in recent days, with The Line hotel also scheduled for auction. The foreclosure proceedings are attributed to significant revenue drops for downtown hotels following the closure of the Austin Convention Center for expansion, impacting the market value of these properties.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Austin Convention Center was razed last year and is closed until 2029 for expansion and reconstruction, leading to double-digit, year-over-year revenue drops for many downtown hotels. Recent foreclosure auctions in downtown Austin have seen properties exchange hands for significantly less than their appraised values.
