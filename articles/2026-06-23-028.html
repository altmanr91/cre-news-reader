Bread of Life starts another YIGBY project in Houston

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 20:03:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Bread of Life Incorporated, a charitable organization, is set to begin construction next month on The Weirton, a seven-story, 99-unit supportive housing facility in Houston. The 82,000-square-foot development, located at 3208 Austin Street, is estimated to cost $20 million and is slated for completion in September 2027. This project marks the organization's fifth development in the Houston area, continuing its mission to provide supportive housing.
