Miamians Sue Trump Over Plan To Include Hotel With Presidential Library

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 14 May 2026 11:01:20 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Miami residents, a Miami Dade College student, and an urban farm owner have filed a lawsuit against Donald Trump, his library foundation, Florida Governor Ron DeSantis, and Miami Dade College, seeking to block the construction of a planned 47-story presidential library tower. The plaintiffs argue that the project, which includes a hotel and is planned for a 2.6-acre site gifted by the state of Florida, violates the U.S. Constitution's emoluments clause by allowing a federal official to receive gifts from foreign or state governments and to monetize the property for private gain. The lawsuit contends that the land transfer, valued at $67 million, already constitutes enrichment and that the proposed development would negatively impact the neighborhood.
