JV Snaps Up Philip Chong’s SoHo Office Building for $36M via Deed-in-Lieu

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Thu, 14 May 2026 18:26:25 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture has acquired a 103,500-square-foot office and retail assemblage in SoHo for $36 million through a discounted note sale followed by a deed-in-lieu of foreclosure. The property, previously owned by HCRE's Philip Chong, was burdened by approximately $60 million in debt. The transaction was brokered by Meridian Capital Group, facilitating the transfer from the lender to the new ownership group.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 261-267 Canal Street, New York City, NY

Size: 103,500 SF

Sale Price: $36.0M

Notable Features: Assemblage of two separate office buildings connected by a defunct breezeway and basement corridor, both with ground-floor retail space.

Project Notes: The transaction involved a discounted note sale followed by a conveyance of the deed-in-lieu. The previous owner was HCRE's Philip Chong. The debt on the property was roughly $60 million.
