Realterm CEO Bob Fordi Is Transforming the IOS Business

Commercial Observer — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 10:30:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Realterm, a multinational transportation and logistics real estate investment firm, has experienced significant growth under CEO Bob Fordi, tripling its assets from $4.5 billion to $14 billion since 2019. The firm, a pioneer in industrial outdoor storage (IOS) and a leader in air cargo facilities, has expanded its global footprint to include Amsterdam, Singapore, Australia, and soon, Japan. Realterm's strategy focuses on acquiring and developing 'high flow-through' properties designed to move freight efficiently, establishing a foundational grip on critical transportation logistics assets.
