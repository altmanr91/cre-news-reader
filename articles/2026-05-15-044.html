Three Trends Shaping the Multifamily Outlook

Connect CRE — Fri, 15 May 2026 05:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

A recent analysis by Marcus & Millichap identifies three key trends shaping the multifamily outlook: tapering construction, a slowdown in job creation, and growing homeownership costs. While Sun Belt markets are currently experiencing elevated supply and sluggish rent growth due to a surge in new units, a significant decline in multifamily starts is expected to balance the market. The slowdown in job creation and international migration is impacting household formation and apartment absorption in the short term. However, long-term demographic trends and the increasing unaffordability of homeownership are anticipated to drive future apartment demand, leading to falling vacancies and strengthening rent growth once short-term headwinds subside.
