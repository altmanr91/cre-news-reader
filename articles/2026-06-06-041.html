Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of 160-Unit Multifamily Property in Wisconsin

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 20:36:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Orchard Courts Apartments, a 160-unit multifamily property in Kenosha, Wisconsin. The property, which includes 20 acres of developable land, attracted significant investor interest due to its stable operations, recent capital improvements, and future development potential. The transaction involved two private investors, with the buyer being procured by Marcus & Millichap.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Orchard Courts Apartments

Address: 985 Wood Road, Kenosha, WI

Units: 160

Site Area: 34.2 acres

Notable Features: Includes approximately 20 acres of developable land adjacent to the University of Wisconsin–Parkside.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Jake Lenti, Associate Director of Investments; Ryan D. Engle, Investment Specialist; Andrean Angelov, Investment Specialist

SELLER: Private Investor

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Kenosha market is benefiting from major employment growth driven by companies such as Eli Lilly, Microsoft, and Uline.
