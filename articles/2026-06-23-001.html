The ‘Best’ Broker No Longer Automatically Wins

Commercial Observer — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 12:00:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This opinion piece argues that the traditional methods of business development for commercial real estate brokers are being augmented, if not supplanted, by digital visibility and artificial intelligence. The author contends that while relationships and reputation remain crucial, the ability for potential clients to discover a broker's expertise through online content and AI search is now a critical factor in securing new assignments. The article emphasizes the importance of a robust digital footprint, including articles, videos, and research, to establish credibility before initial client contact. The author concludes that firms restricting their professionals from building personal brands and thought leadership online risk becoming invisible in an increasingly AI-driven search environment.
