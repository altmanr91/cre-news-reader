Stars CEO says tear down American Airlines Center once teams leave

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 22:13:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The CEO of the Dallas Stars has publicly advocated for the demolition of the American Airlines Center once both the Stars and Mavericks vacate the arena in 2031. This recommendation comes as both teams plan to construct new, separate arenas: the Mavericks in Valley View, Dallas, and the Stars in Plano. The Stars' proposed $3 billion arena and entertainment district in Plano, developed by Centennial Real Estate and Cawley Partners, recently received city council approval for preliminary studies, with Plano offering significant financial incentives. Dallas city officials, however, are exploring options to retain the Stars within the city.
