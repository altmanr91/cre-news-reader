CF Capital: Multifamily Investment Requires Connecting the Right Dots for Each Deal, Not Riding Market Waves

Connect CRE — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 21:48:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

CF Capital, a Louisville-based multifamily investment firm, emphasizes that successful multifamily investing stems from disciplined underwriting, execution, governance, and investor transparency rather than attempting to time the market or follow broad trends. The firm, which has nearly doubled its revenue in the last two years and manages 1,500 units across two states, prioritizes long-term advantages in apartment communities that offer downside protection and appreciation potential across cycles. They utilize a scoring system for property characteristics to maintain discipline in their pursuit of deals, ensuring higher return expectations for lower-scoring opportunities. The firm cautions against oversimplifying distressed deals, noting that many require a repositioning of the resident profile, not just interior upgrades, which can create operational friction and impact returns if not properly underwritten.
