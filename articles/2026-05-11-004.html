Triten Real Estate Partners completes 400,000-square-foot distribution center in Humble

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Mon, 11 May 2026 09:57:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Triten Real Estate Partners has completed Kenswick at 1960, a two-building, Class-A distribution center totaling nearly 400,000 square feet in Humble, Texas. The development is strategically located near George Bush Intercontinental Airport and major freight corridors, offering efficient access to the Houston metropolitan area and beyond. The project features a campus-style layout, 36-foot clear heights, extensive trailer parking, and the potential for over five acres of secured outdoor storage, catering to a diverse range of industrial users.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Kenswick at 1960

Address: 8311 FM 1960 Bypass Road, Humble, TX

Size: 400,000 SF

Notable Features: Dual-building campus-style environment with 36-foot clear heights, ample dock-door count, approximately 9,000 square feet of Class A office space, and above-standard trailer parking with potential for over five acres of secured outdoor storage. Located approximately one-half mile from George Bush Intercontinental Airport's air cargo facilities, with immediate connectivity to U.S. Highway 59 (Interstate 69), Beltway 8, and Grand Parkway (SH 99).
