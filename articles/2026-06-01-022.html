Runyon Group’s plan for record Water Mill retail buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Runyon Group, in a joint venture with River Oaks Properties, has acquired a 50,000-square-foot retail portfolio in Water Mill, NY, for $39 million. This acquisition, comprising 670 and 760 Montauk Highway, represents 84% of the leasable retail space in the hamlet and sets a new price record for the area. The buyers plan to revitalize the properties by restoring the buildings, curating a mix of both essential and high-end tenants, and enhancing the outdoor spaces to create a more inviting and community-focused shopping experience by summer 2027. The developers aim to balance luxury with daily needs, drawing on their experience in creating successful retail destinations in other affluent markets.
