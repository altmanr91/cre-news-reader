Visitt, BGO Partner to Deploy AI-Enabled Property Software Across Canadian Portfolio

Commercial Observer — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 20:52:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Visitt, an AI-driven property operations platform, has partnered with BGO Properties to deploy its technology across 46 million square feet of BGO's Canadian portfolio, encompassing approximately 300 properties. This collaboration aims to streamline work orders, implement predictive and preventive maintenance, and manage equipment lifecycles, ultimately enhancing tenant services in both residential and commercial properties across BGO's Canadian markets. The platform will offer bilingual services to cater to regions including Ontario, British Columbia, Alberta, Manitoba, and Quebec.
