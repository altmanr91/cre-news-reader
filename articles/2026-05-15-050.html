Top Retail Investor Says Cash Is King When Analysing Deals

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 14 May 2026 12:12:24 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

A top retail investor emphasizes a laser focus on true income levels when underwriting new deals, a significant shift from pricing in yield movements or estimated rental value changes. This approach, which involves detailed analysis of footfall and customer spending to ensure retailers are not overrented, allows for growth in net operating income. The retail real estate sector has shown strong operational performance recently, driven by factors like increased consumer savings and the shakeout of underperforming retailers. This positive trend is attracting investor attention, with capital reallocation to retail and office properties anticipated once pricing adjusts to the increased cost of capital.
