Affinius-led investor group finalizes nearly $4B Veris Residential takeover

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 28 May 2026 21:30:07 +0000

SUMMARY

An investor group led by Affinius Capital and Vista Hill Partners has finalized its $3.5 billion acquisition of Veris Residential, a real estate investment trust. The all-cash transaction, which includes debt, was priced at $19 per share and marks the culmination of Veris Residential's five-year strategic shift from a diversified office and apartment portfolio to a solely residential enterprise. The acquisition includes a portfolio of Class A multifamily properties located across the New York City metro area, Boston, and New Jersey.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Veris Residential Portfolio

Sale Price: $3.50B

Project Notes: The investor buyout was priced at $19 per share of Veris common stock. Veris Residential was formerly known as Mack-Cali Realty Corp and had been divesting suburban office properties over the last five years to focus on residential assets.
