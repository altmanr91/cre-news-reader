Silicon Valley rents to rise as much as 7% over the next year: CoStar

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Multifamily Forecast

Silicon Valley's multifamily market is poised for significant rent growth, with projections indicating a 6-7% increase over the next year, driven by the booming artificial intelligence sector. This surge in demand, coupled with a dramatic slowdown in new apartment construction, is expected to push vacancy rates to a decade-low of approximately 3% across Santa Clara and San Benito counties. Despite incentives from cities like San Jose and San Francisco to stimulate development, high interest rates, tight capital markets, and elevated construction costs continue to hinder new supply, creating a landlord-favorable environment.
