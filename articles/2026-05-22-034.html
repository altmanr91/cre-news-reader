Hillwood moving low-and-slow with 1,000-acre master plan in Lockhart

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 21 May 2026 20:05:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Hillwood Communities is progressing with a master-planned community on a 1,110-acre tract in Lockhart, Texas, located between Dry Creek Road and U.S. Highway 183. The development, which is primarily within Lockhart's extraterritorial jurisdiction, is moving at a deliberate pace due to an acreage expansion and ongoing municipal utility district approvals. This project aligns with a broader trend of developers targeting Austin's growing suburban markets.
