Vacant lot from Bob Vila’s former Palm Beach estate fetches $39M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A vacant 0.84-acre lot at 690 Island Drive in Palm Beach, formerly part of Bob Vila's estate, sold for $39.2 million, marking a $6.2 million appreciation from its previous sale a year ago. The 1949-built home on the property was demolished last year. The transaction highlights the increasing demand and premium prices for vacant land and teardown properties in Palm Beach, driven by scarcity and the trend of replacing older estates with modern mansions.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: land

Address: 690 Island Drive, Palm Beach, FL

Site Area: 0.84 acres

Notable Features: The lot includes 175 feet of intracoastal water frontage and a private dock.

Project Notes: The 1949-built home on the property was torn down two months after its sale last June for $33 million.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
