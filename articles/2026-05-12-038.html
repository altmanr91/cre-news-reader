Belmont apartments offer bright spot for Bay Area multifamily after $21M trade

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 11 May 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A private family ownership group has sold the El Dorado Apartments, a 69,960-square-foot multifamily property in Belmont, CA, for $20.5 million. The transaction, which was part of a 1031 exchange, attracted a competitive buyer pool due to the property's well-maintained condition and its role in providing quality vintage housing. The sale highlights the continued demand for Class B and C multifamily assets in the San Mateo metro area, which are experiencing strong occupancy rates and steady rent growth.
