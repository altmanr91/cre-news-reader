Developers Want To Copy The Battery Atlanta Success Story. Experts Say It's Not As Easy As It Looks

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 04 May 2026 13:06:02 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The Battery Atlanta, a 2.2M SF mixed-use project anchored by Truist Park, generated a record $97M in revenue last year, prompting other sports teams to explore similar developments. However, experts caution that replicating this success is challenging, requiring significant governmental support and a deep understanding of market demand for retail and restaurant offerings. The project's success is attributed to careful programming and strategic planning, rather than a 'build-it-and-they-will-come' approach. Collegiate sports programs are also looking to mixed-use stadium developments to generate revenue, with some universities focusing on enhancing the fan and campus experience.

Colleges and universities are also exploring mixed-use stadium developments to offset financial pressures from player compensation rules.
