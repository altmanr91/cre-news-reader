Gantry provides $60 million loan for big-box warehouse facility in Fort Worth

REFINANCE

REJournals — Fri, 01 May 2026 13:47:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Gantry secured a $60 million loan for a private real estate investor to refinance a 1.4-million-square-foot big-box warehouse facility in Fort Worth, Texas. The two-year, fixed-rate, interest-only loan, with two one-year extension options, was provided by a correspondent insurance company lender and will be serviced by Gantry. This financing helps strategically position the fully occupied logistics asset during a legacy portfolio restructuring.
