Billionaire video game developer pays $71M for McMackins’ waterfront Manalapan estate

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Billionaire video game developer Gabe Newell, through Building Florida Home LLC, acquired a 22,900-square-foot waterfront estate in Manalapan, FL, from Cindy and Ron McMackin for $70.9 million. The transaction was financed with a $46 million mortgage from Morgan Stanley. The property, built in 2020, features a unique tunnel connecting the mansion to a beach bungalow and was sold for approximately 17 percent below its most recent asking price.
