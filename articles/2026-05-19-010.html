Foss Real Estate Arranges $7M in Bridge Financing for Arlington Industrial Property

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 18 May 2026 14:09:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Foss Real Estate Partners has arranged $7 million in bridge financing for Oakhollow Business Park, an 84,469-square-foot industrial property in Arlington, Texas. The loan was structured with a 70 percent loan-to-cost ratio and a fixed interest rate of 6.5 percent. The property was built in 1984 and renovated in 2020.
