Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of 46,822 SF Retail Center in Irving

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 14:45:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Market Square, a 46,822-square-foot retail center located in the Las Colinas district of Irving, Texas. The property, built in 1984, features a strong tenant mix operating under triple-net leases. Both the seller and buyer were private investors who chose to remain anonymous.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Market Square

Address: 5910 N MacArthur Blvd, Irving, TX*

Size: 46,822 SF

Year Built: 1984

Notable Features: Houses a mix of service-oriented tenants under triple-net agreements.
