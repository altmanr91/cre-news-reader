Watch mogul’s ex-wife finds buyer for waterfront Gables Estates home

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 14:32:59 +0000

SUMMARY

A waterfront mansion in Gables Estates, previously owned by Martha Cecilia Hernandez Quintero and her ex-husband Rick De La Croix, has found a buyer. The nearly 12,000-square-foot luxury home was asking $28 million and was among the top contracts signed in Miami-Dade County last week. Rick De La Croix is a prominent watch marketer and entrepreneur.
