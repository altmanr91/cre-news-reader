The Plan: 99 Park Avenue Returns to Its Art Deco Roots in Midtown

Commercial Observer — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 10:00:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

Global Holdings is undertaking a $30 million repositioning of its 26-story, 600,000-square-foot Class A office tower at 99 Park Avenue in Midtown Manhattan. The renovation, led by Vocon, focuses on restoring the building's original Art Deco aesthetic in the lobby and adding a new 5,600-square-foot amenity center called Below Park, which includes a speakeasy-style lounge, conference facilities, a golf simulator, bowling alley, and barbershop. The project, which began 18 months ago, is expected to complete in Q2 2026 and aims to attract new tenants and retain existing ones by offering a hospitality-inspired environment.
