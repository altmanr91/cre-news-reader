Senior Housing Operator Omni Lifestyle Living Buys Midtown Marriott for $40M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 21:20:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Omni Lifestyle Living, an Ohio-based senior living provider, has acquired the Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites New York Manhattan hotel in Midtown for $39.9 million from Lam Generation. The company, which specializes in converting hotels into assisted senior living facilities, secured $37.3 million in acquisition financing from Wells Fargo. This purchase marks Omni's expansion into the New York City market, adding to its portfolio of 10 existing assets and eight planned facilities.
