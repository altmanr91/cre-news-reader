Proposed UES mansion tops Manhattan’s luxury contracts

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 04 May 2026 15:17:04 +0000

SUMMARY

A proposed luxury mansion on the Upper East Side, formed by combining two existing homes, topped Manhattan's luxury contract market with an asking price of $24.5 million. The transaction for the 12,500-square-foot property, which includes city-approved plans for a five-story, eight-bedroom residence, was the highest of 29 luxury homes to go into contract last week. This deal highlights continued demand for high-end residential properties in the borough, even as the overall number of luxury contracts dipped slightly.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Address: 34-36 East 70th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 12,500 SF

Sale Price: $24.5M

$/SF: $1,960/SF (calculated)

Notable Features: Plans include eight bedrooms, seven bathrooms, a back garden, and two terraces, with city Landmarks Preservation Commission approval.

Project Notes: The property last traded for $18.3 million in 2018. It was initially listed in March 2024 for $36 million.
