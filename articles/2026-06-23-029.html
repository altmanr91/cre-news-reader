Health scanning concept takes four floor lease as Union Square bounces back

LEASE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Midjourney, an AI image-generating company, has leased 23,000 square feet across four floors at 300 Grant Avenue in San Francisco's Union Square to open its first health imaging spa. The new division, Midjourney Medical, will offer full-body medical scans within a futuristic, gold-plated bathhouse setting, aiming to provide a luxurious experience rather than a clinical one. The 10-year lease marks a significant expansion for the company into medtech and contributes to the ongoing revitalization of the Union Square luxury retail district.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 300 Grant Avenue, San Francisco, CA

Size: 23,000 SF

Year Built: 2021

Notable Features: The facility will feature 10 full-body scanners and a 24/7 spa with cold plunges, saunas, hot tubs, and amber lighting, designed with curved surfaces, waterfalls, and water ripples.

Project Notes: The building was one of the first new ground-up properties in Union Square in decades when it opened in 2021. The spa is expected to open late next year.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Union Square's foot traffic increased by 9.6% last month compared to a year prior, and vacancies fell by 2.8%. The submarket experienced a vacancy rate of 22% during the pandemic.
