PACE Loan Group closes $11.6 million in financing Irving data center conversion

LOAN

REJournals — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 16:13:00 +0000

SUMMARY

PACE Loan Group has provided $11.6 million in C-PACE financing for the conversion of a vacant industrial-flex property in Irving, TX, into the Vesgro Data Center. The project, developed by Infrascale Systems, will transform the 113,406-square-foot building into a 3.0 MW colocation data center with approximately 45,000 square feet of raised-floor data hall space. The financing will fund significant upgrades to HVAC, lighting, electrical infrastructure, generators, and the building envelope, with completion anticipated by September 2026. The project leverages the site's substantial power allocation and strategic location to meet the growing demand for Tier IV data centers, a trend noted by the lender in the current market.
