Podcast | How CRE is Implementing Agentic Workflows, with Lev CEO Yaakov Zar

Commercial Observer — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 19:42:45 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

This article features a podcast interview with Yaakov Zar, CEO of Lev, a software platform designed to streamline commercial real estate workflows. Zar discusses how Lev, which began as a tech-enabled brokerage, has evolved into an agentic workflow platform that assists lenders, brokers, and investors in managing deals and processing unstructured data more efficiently. He highlights the challenges within the CRE financing process that inspired Lev's creation and elaborates on the platform's capabilities in modernizing real estate operations.
