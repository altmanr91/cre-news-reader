'So Excited And So Terrified': Sternlicht Sees Opportunity Despite Earnings Miss

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 08 May 2026 10:50:52 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Earnings Report

Starwood Property Trust reported mixed first-quarter results, with total revenue of $512 million but a net income dip to $52 million. CEO Barry Sternlicht acknowledged global volatility and macro concerns but expressed optimism about commercial real estate markets, noting a shift from headwinds to tailwinds and robust investor demand for their equity funds. The REIT deployed $4 billion in investments in 2026, leveraging its $10.4 billion loan capacity and over $2 billion in liquidity and corporate debt capacity to capitalize on market dislocation. The company is rebalancing its portfolio, which included the sale of a multifamily property at a loss and the foreclosure of three assets in Dallas and Phoenix, while also repurchasing $20 million in stock, believing its public pricing undervalues its assets.
