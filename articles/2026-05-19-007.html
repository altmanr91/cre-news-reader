KCC Underway on 266,788 SF Industrial Build-Out in Hutchins, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 18 May 2026 14:19:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Krusinski Construction Co. is currently undertaking a 266,788-square-foot tenant build-out for an HVAC products and systems provider at the Wintergreen Distribution Center in Hutchins, Texas. The industrial project, delivered in 2024 by IAC Properties, is expected to be completed in June.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Wintergreen Distribution Center

Address: 300 W Wintergreen Rd, Hutchins, TX*

Size: 266,788 SF

Year Built: 2024

Completion: June 2026

Site Area: 34 acres

Notable Features: Features 40-foot clear heights, four drive-in doors, and parking for 328 cars and 151 trailers.
