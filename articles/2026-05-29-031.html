ICE planning to add more San Antonio office space as workforce dramatically increases

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 28 May 2026 23:17:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Government Leasing

U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) is seeking an additional 65,000 square feet of office space in San Antonio, within the Loop 1604 boundaries, to accommodate a significant workforce increase. This expansion comes as the agency's budget has grown by nearly $80 million, enabling a planned 120 percent increase in personnel. The agency is also converting a recently acquired 640,000-square-foot warehouse into a detention center, a move that has faced strong opposition from local advocacy groups and city leaders.
