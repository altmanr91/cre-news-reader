Atria Health Leases Entire Commercial Space at One High Line

LEASE

Connect CRE — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 21:15:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Atria Health Institute has signed a lease for the entire 52,000-square-foot commercial component of One High Line, a mixed-use development in Manhattan's West Chelsea neighborhood. The five-story space connects the development's East and West towers and is expected to open in 2027 as Atria's second New York Institute. The landlord noted that Atria's focus on innovative preventive care aligns with the project's vision for a premier lifestyle destination.
