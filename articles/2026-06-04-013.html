Commercial construction company TEXBUILT launches in Texas

REJournals — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 16:19:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

TEXBUILT has officially launched as a new commercial construction company focused on the Texas market. As an employee-owned entity under BLDR Holdings, TEXBUILT aims to leverage local leadership and a dedicated Texas-based team to deliver commercial construction services across various sectors. The company's leadership emphasizes a commitment to the region and building on existing relationships established through its affiliate, EMJ Construction, to serve the rapidly growing Texas market.
