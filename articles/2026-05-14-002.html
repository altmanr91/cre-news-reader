Law Firm Cole, Scott & Kissane Adds Second Dadeland Office in 21K-SF Lease

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 13 May 2026 21:00:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Law firm Cole, Scott & Kissane has signed a 10-year lease for 21,000 square feet at One Datran office building in Dadeland, FL, marking an expansion for the firm which will retain its existing office nearby. The new space will accommodate the firm's growing team and enhance client services, with a planned opening in January after a buildout.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: One Datran

Address: 9100 South Dadeland Boulevard, Dadeland, FL

Size: 21,000 SF

Notable Features: 18-story office building adjacent to the Dadeland South Metrorail station, located between Palmetto Expressway and Route 1.

Project Notes: The building was previously acquired for $150 million in 2016.
