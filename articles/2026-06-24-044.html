Amazon Turns Buyer As Logistics Market Throws Up Opportunities

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 12:59:58 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Amazon is actively seeking to acquire and lease logistics and industrial properties across Europe, particularly in the UK and Germany, capitalizing on current market uncertainties to secure long-term assets. The e-commerce giant prioritizes proximity to major population centers, access to labor, and increasingly, access to power for its automated facilities and electric vehicle fleets. This strategic shift towards property ownership reflects Amazon's long-term investment approach in a market where core capital is largely hesitant, creating opportunities for well-capitalized players.

DATA POINTS

UK industrial investment 2025: £10B

UK industrial investment Q1 2026: £1.9B

Q1 2026 investment volume change year-over-year: down 15%

Industrial yields: around 5%

Yield increase: 25 basis points

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

UK industrial investment reached a three-year high of £10B in 2025, but Q1 2026 volumes were £1.9B, a 15% decrease year-over-year. Industrial yields have risen 25 basis points to approximately 5%. There is a significant gap between prime and secondary yields, and a lack of new development in the sector over the past five years continues to support rental growth.
