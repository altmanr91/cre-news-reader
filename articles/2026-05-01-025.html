Eichners’ Continuum, Turkish partner score $350M financing for North Bay Village project

LOAN

South Florida - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 16:27:34 +0000

SUMMARY

The Continuum Company, in partnership with Aksoy Holding, secured $350 million in financing for the Continuum Waterfront District, a significant mixed-use development in North Bay Village. The financing package, provided by S3 Capital, includes $261 million for the construction of the first phase and $83 million for the predevelopment of the second phase. This project, which will feature luxury condominiums, a hotel, office space, and retail, represents the largest financing deal in North Bay Village to date and is expected to be completed in phases by 2030.
