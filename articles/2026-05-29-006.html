JBG Smith Begins Office-to-Residential Conversion Project in Metro D.C.

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 28 May 2026 14:45:20 +0000

SUMMARY

JBG Smith and Declaration Partners have commenced the conversion of 2200 Crystal Drive, an 11-story office building in Arlington, VA, into a 195-unit multifamily community. The project involves repositioning the 315,000-square-foot office structure, originally built in 1968, into residential use. BNY is providing construction financing for the development, which is located in the National Landing neighborhood.
