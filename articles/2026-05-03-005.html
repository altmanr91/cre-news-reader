SUMMARY

This article highlights several notable real estate transactions in New York City. The most expensive commercial sale was an apartment complex in the Bronx, acquired by an LLC linked to Joel Weiss and Isaac Silberstein from a Great Neck-based company. In the residential sector, a trust purchased a penthouse in Downtown Brooklyn, while statistician Nate Silver and Robert Gauldin acquired a condo in Noho from Justin Blatstein. Additionally, Lisa Bardack bought a newly constructed condo in Yorkville.
