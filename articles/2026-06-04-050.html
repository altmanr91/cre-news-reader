Bisnow Wins 10 NAREE Journalism Awards For 2025

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 15:35:44 -0400

SUMMARY

Association / Leadership News

Bisnow received 10 awards at the National Association of Real Estate Editors Journalism Competition, matching its highest annual total. The newsroom earned silver for Best Real Estate Web Site, with individual reporters recognized across various categories. Notable wins included silver for Best Architecture Story for an exploration of commercial glass recycling, silver for Best Real Estate E-Newsletter for The D.C. Rundown, and an honorable mention for an investigation into the 2026 World Cup's potential impact on affordable housing. Editor-in-Chief Mark F. Bonner emphasized the importance of uncompromising journalism in commercial real estate, citing the consistent NAREE recognition as proof of the newsroom's commitment to cutting through spin and focusing on the story.
