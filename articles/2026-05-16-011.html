Joseph Nicholas Construction breaks ground on Cadillac dealership in Chicago’s former Lincoln Yards site

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:39:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Joseph Nicholas Construction (JNC), in partnership with Jack Carter Auto Group, has broken ground on Carter Cadillac of Chicago, a new flagship dealership located at 1400 W. North Ave. in Chicago. The development, part of the former Lincoln Yards site, will feature a two-story showroom and a six-level parking garage with 490 spaces. This facility is slated to be the brand's third flagship store globally and is expected to open in late 2027, aiming to accelerate business development and job creation in the city.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Carter Cadillac of Chicago

Address: 1400 W. North Ave., Chicago, IL

Completion: late 2027

Notable Features: Third Cadillac flagship store globally; authorized to sell the 2026 Cadillac CELESTIQ ultra-luxury sedan.

Project Notes: Located on a vacant lot at the corner of North Avenue and Besly Court, part of the former Lincoln Yards site.
