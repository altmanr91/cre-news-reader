Burlington Office Building Sells For One-Third Its Prior Price

SALE

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 20 May 2026 16:04:57 -0400

SUMMARY

K & L Realty Co. acquired the 116,000-square-foot office building at One Burlington Woods Drive in Burlington, MA, for $8.5 million. The sale represents a significant discount from its prior sale price in 2013, reflecting the broader decline in suburban office values. The property is 60% occupied, and its low acquisition cost is expected to enable the new owner to offer competitive rents, potentially attracting tenants from further out on Route 495.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Office vacancy in the 128 North submarket, which includes Burlington, was 14% last quarter, lower than the 17.8% vacancy across the entire Route 128 corridor. However, leasing activity in the submarket was the lowest in the corridor, with approximately 73,000 SF of deals signed in Q1.
