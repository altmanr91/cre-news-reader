JLL Capital Markets brokers sale of five-property retail portfolio in Dallas, Fort Worth markets

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 04 May 2026 10:04:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets brokered the sale of the N3 DFW Strip, a five-property retail portfolio located across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, to Curbline Properties. The portfolio includes retail strip centers in Flower Mound, Hudson Oaks, Southlake, Red Oak, and Waco. The properties are strategically positioned in rapidly growing suburban markets, offering strong connectivity and favorable demographic trends, which are crucial for retail success. The transaction generated intense competitive bidding from institutional capital due to the limited availability of unanchored strip center portfolios at scale.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex is experiencing robust economic growth, with expanding populations and strong consumer spending supporting retail fundamentals. The region's diversified economy, major transportation infrastructure, and favorable business climate make it a highly sought-after market for retail investment.
