The Cooper Commercial Investment Group brokers sale of 36,966-square-foot retail center in North Olmsted

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Cooper Commercial Investment Group has brokered the sale of Bentley Coe Plaza, a 36,966-square-foot multi-tenant retail center in North Olmsted, Ohio. The property, located in a strong retail corridor, sold for $4.25 million at a 7.38% capitalization rate. The buyer was identified through targeted outreach before the property was formally marketed.
