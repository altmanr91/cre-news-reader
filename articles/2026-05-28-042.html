Uncommon Developers Takes Community-Building Approach

Connect CRE — Wed, 27 May 2026 22:30:40 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Uncommon Developers, a vertically integrated real estate platform, focuses on community-building through its mixed-use and multifamily projects in Los Angeles. Co-founder Ryan Hekmat emphasizes job creation and strengthening local communities economically and socially by providing housing, opportunities, public spaces, and experiences that enhance quality of life. The company handles all aspects of development in-house, from acquisition to property management, and leverages modular construction to navigate market instability and rising costs. Hekmat notes that while governments aim to streamline approvals, issues like unpredictable fee schedules can undermine project viability.
