Colliers-branded TIC deals spark state, federal probes

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 06 May 2026 19:18:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A series of tenant-in-common (TIC) deals sponsored by Millcreek Commercial Properties and its principal, Kevin Long, are under state and federal investigation following allegations that retirees were sold high-risk medical office building investments under the guise of a global brokerage brand. Investors claim they were misled by inflated valuations and illusory leases to fast-growing healthcare tenants, many of whom later filed for bankruptcy without occupying space or paying rent. The investments, often part of 1031 exchanges, have resulted in significant losses for retirees, leading to at least 14 lawsuits across five states and probes by the SEC and Utah's Division of Real Estate.
