Bill Koch’s Aspen estate heads to auction asking $99M

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 27 May 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Billionaire industrialist Bill Koch is sending his 52-acre Elk Mountain Lodge compound in Aspen, CO, to an online auction with a current asking price of $99 million. The property, which includes a nearly 17,000-square-foot main lodge and seven guest cabins, was previously listed for $125 million. The move to auction is intended to cast a wider net for ultra-high net worth buyers and determine the property's true market value, given the limited buyer pool for such high-value listings.

Aspen luxury market sales volume (last year): $2.5 billion

Aspen luxury market sales volume increase (last year): almost 40 percent

Aspen median sale prices increase (2025 to current year): 13 percent

Aspen median sale price (current year): over $5.5 million

Record Aspen mansion sale (2024): $108 million

Priciest listing in America (Aspen): $300 million

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Aspen luxury market has experienced significant growth, with sales volume increasing by nearly 40% last year to over $2.5 billion. Median sale prices in the area rose 13% from 2025 to over $5.5 million. Restrictive zoning measures have limited new supply, contributing to the market's strength. A recent record sale in 2024 saw a mansion trade for $108 million, and the market currently features the priciest listing in America at $300 million.
