Dov Stark sets record with $55M Fort Lauderdale estate listing

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 22:20:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Dov Stark, founder and CEO of Prestige Homes Development, has listed Ember House, a newly constructed 15,000-square-foot waterfront estate in Fort Lauderdale, for $55 million, setting a new record for the highest-priced residential listing in Broward County. Stark acquired the property in 2024 for $5.5 million and invested over $35 million in construction costs over 15 months. The listing reflects the evolving luxury market in Fort Lauderdale, which is experiencing increased demand for high-quality homes and trophy estates, attracting out-of-state buyers and driving up waterfront land values.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Fort Lauderdale's luxury market, often called the 'Venice of America,' has historically lagged behind Miami and Palm Beach but is now experiencing an evolution due to development infrastructure and increased amenities. Prime waterfront properties are driving market growth, with land values reaching unprecedented levels. The city is attracting an influx of out-of-state buyers, primarily from New York, New Jersey, California, Washington, D.C., and Chicago.
