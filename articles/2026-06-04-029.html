Advocate Medical Group fuels Chicago medical office surge with pair of property buys

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Advocate Medical Group has acquired two properties in the Chicago metropolitan area, signaling a surge in the medical office market. One acquisition involved a 23-acre parcel in South Shore from Related Midwest for $15 million, where Advocate plans to develop a 40-bed hospital. The second purchase was an outpatient medical facility in Naperville, previously a Planet Fitness, which was redeveloped by Capital Healthcare Properties. These transactions highlight the robust demand for medical office assets in the region, driven by strategic developments and a growing need for healthcare services.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Address: 7931 South Brandon Avenue, Chicago, IL

Sale Price: $15.0M

Site Area: 23 acres

Notable Features: The South Shore site is adjacent to Related Midwest's forthcoming $9 billion quantum computing campus and near the Obama Presidential Library.

Project Notes: The Naperville property was redeveloped from a Planet Fitness into an outpatient medical facility, including an ambulatory surgery treatment center with one procedure room and cardiac services.
