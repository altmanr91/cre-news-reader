NYC’s top deals: Witkoff, Access Industries snag $24M for One High Line penthouse

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Sat, 27 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Shinko Co. acquired a six-story, mixed-use building at 245 Eldridge Street on the Lower East Side of New York City for $24 million. The property, which includes 10 apartments and ground-floor retail, was previously purchased by the seller in December for $16.5 million. This transaction represents the top commercial deal recorded in New York City during the reporting period.
