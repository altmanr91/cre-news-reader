AI Marketing Platform Hightouch Takes 18K SF at 275 Seventh Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Tue, 19 May 2026 20:44:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Hightouch, a data and AI marketing platform, has relocated its New York City operations to a full-floor, 18,000-square-foot space at 275 Seventh Avenue in Midtown South. The new office will serve as the company's operational base for its sales, engineering, finance, and executive teams, allowing for expanded engagement with its significant customer base in New York. The move represents an expansion for Hightouch, which previously occupied smaller offices in the area.
