CRE Pricing Spread Between Cycle's 'Winners And Laggards' The Widest Ever

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 12:00:25 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Pricing Report

A recent report from Altus Group highlights a record-setting divergence in commercial real estate pricing between top-performing and struggling asset classes. Industrial property pricing has surged 88.5% since pre-pandemic levels, while office pricing has only increased by 36.6% over the same period, indicating a structural repricing rather than a mere recovery gap. Despite anticipated headwinds like elevated interest rates, geopolitical tensions, and tech sector volatility, overall CRE prices saw gains in Q1 2026, with all property types except hospitality reaching new highs. The report notes that industrial remains the most affordable on a per-square-foot basis at $110, while multifamily is the most expensive at $150.
