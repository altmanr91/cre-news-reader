Thakkars claim Safehold cut them out of office-to-resi conversion

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 21 May 2026 21:10:49 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

The Texas-based owners of a Midtown office tower are suing their ground landlord, Safehold, alleging wrongful termination of their 104-year ground lease at 135 West 50th Street. Thakkar Developers claims Safehold attempted to cut them out of a planned office-to-residential conversion after they brought in MetroLoft as a development partner. The lawsuit seeks to block the lease termination and demands over $500 million in damages, following Safehold's own suit to eject the Thakkars over alleged unpaid property taxes.
