AI Is Filling Office Towers. It's Also Likely To Empty Them

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 15:08:31 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The article explores the paradoxical impact of artificial intelligence on the U.S. office market, noting a concentrated rebound in leasing activity within high-quality properties in select markets, primarily driven by AI-focused tech firms. This surge in demand for premium space coexists with significant tech sector layoffs, as companies prioritize AI investments over hiring and seek efficiencies. Experts suggest that while AI adoption is leading to downsizing and a flat outlook for office-using job growth in the short term, it is also expected to create new job categories and business opportunities as the technology matures, particularly in areas like compliance and data security.
