Austin politicos sell Westlake estate after asking $7M

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 19:19:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Texas politicos David and Elizabeth White sold their 8,370-square-foot personal residence at 600 Logans Lane in Austin to the Piccolo Bambino Trust. The property, last listed for $6.5 million, features five bedrooms, seven bathrooms, a pool, an outdoor kitchen, and six covered terraces on a 2.1-acre lot in the affluent Westlake neighborhood. The sale went under contract in just 33 days, indicating a significant acceleration in Austin's luxury housing market, which previously experienced longer selling periods.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The typical Austin luxury home went under contract in 104 days last fall, ranking fourth in the country and first in Texas for the highest median days on market. During the three months ending April 30, Austin luxury homes sold in a median of 57 days, a 16-day drop year-over-year in days on market and the second-biggest decrease in the country.
