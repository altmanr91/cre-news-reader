117-year-old family affair over on Union Square

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 20:37:49 +0000

SUMMARY

A historic San Francisco family has sold its majority stake in a 32,400-square-foot retail building in the Union Square district for $19.2 million. The Gump family, who originally purchased the property in 1908, divested their 60.4% interest to a local jeweler, Victor Wu, owner of J. Stella. This transaction marks a significant vote of confidence in the city's retail core, which has faced challenges in recent years, and reflects a return to pre-pandemic investment patterns for trophy assets in the area.
