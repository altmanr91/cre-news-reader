Clear Height Properties closes disposition of 354,000-square-foot manufacturing facility in Kenosha

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 10:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Clear Height Properties and Buligo Capital Partners have sold a 354,000-square-foot manufacturing and distribution facility located in Kenosha, Wisconsin. The disposition follows Clear Height's successful execution of a long-term lease extension with a corporate guarantee, which stabilized the property and enhanced its investment-grade credit profile. The facility's strategic location offers strong connectivity to both the Chicago and Milwaukee metropolitan areas.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 10200 55th Street, Kenosha, WI

Size: 354,000 SF

Notable Features: Equipped with integrated labs, lighting paint lines, and customized production infrastructure.

Project Notes: Clear Height executed a long-term lease extension with a corporate guarantee and S&P A- investment grade credit enhancement prior to sale.
