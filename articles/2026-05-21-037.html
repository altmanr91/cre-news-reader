Bank OZK expanding in Houston by nearly tripling office space

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 20 May 2026 18:43:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Bank OZK is significantly expanding its office footprint in Houston, nearly tripling its space at Brookhollow Central III from 15,000 square feet to 40,000 square feet. This expansion, expected to finalize in the fourth quarter of 2026, reflects the bank's strong growth in the Houston market, particularly within its corporate and institutional banking group. The move will also accommodate the addition of 15 new employees, increasing its local workforce from approximately 45 to 60.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Brookhollow Central III

Address: 2950 North Loop West, Houston, TX

Size: 40,000 SF

Completion: 4Q 2026

Project Notes: Bank OZK's original lease was for 7,800 square feet before a prior expansion to 15,000 square feet.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

TENANT: Bank OZK — Jake Munn, President of Corporate and Institutional Banking

OWNER: Meneses Holdings

OWNER: Dominus Commercial

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Houston and Dallas economies are experiencing significant growth, particularly in the commercial and industrial sectors. Bank OZK issued $3 billion in real estate construction loans in 2023, exceeding its closest competitors by over $1 billion.
