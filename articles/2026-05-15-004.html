Vacant Former WeWork Property Sees Valuation Double to $33M

Commercial Observer — Thu, 14 May 2026 19:30:20 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Valuation Report

A vacant 11-story office building in Greenwich Village, formerly fully occupied by WeWork, has seen its valuation double to $32.9 million from $16.4 million in June 2025. This significant increase, despite the property being distressed and having a defaulted CMBS loan, suggests a potential residual effect from strong demand in Manhattan's Class A office market, pushing some tenants to consider lesser-tier spaces. The property was taken over by a CMBS trust in October 2025 after the previous owner defaulted on its loan.
