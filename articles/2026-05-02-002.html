Advanced Real Estate Buys Two of Hollywood’s Five Apartment Towers

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:40:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Advanced Real Estate has acquired two multifamily towers, Columbia Square Living (now Sky Hollywood) and Jardine, in Hollywood, CA, from Kilroy Realty for $202 million. The transaction, which includes 393 units, represents the largest multifamily deal in Southern California this year and significantly expands Advanced Real Estate's portfolio in the region. The buyer secured $141.4 million in 10-year, fixed-rate Freddie Mac loans to finance the acquisition, underscoring confidence in the Hollywood market's future growth driven by major events and the entertainment industry's return.

Notable Features: Both properties feature rooftop pools and fitness centers, and contain penthouses leasing from $12,000 to $20,000 per month.

Project Notes: The properties were acquired with two 10-year Freddie Mac loans totaling $141.4 million at a 5.17% fixed rate with interest-only payments. Advanced Management Company will manage the properties and R³ Construction Services will complete cosmetic upgrades.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Hollywood market has a limited supply of high-rise apartment units, with these two properties representing over half of the existing five apartment towers. The construction pipeline for multifamily remains at historic lows in the region.
