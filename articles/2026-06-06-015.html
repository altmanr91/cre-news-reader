Skender tops out at new Westfield police headquarters

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 13:26:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Skender, serving as general contractor, has topped out construction on the new Westfield Police Department headquarters, a $35 million facility developed in partnership with Garmong. The 50,000-square-foot building, located on an 11.7-acre site along Grand Park Boulevard in Westfield, Indiana, is designed to replace the city's outdated public safety building and support future growth. The project, which began in late 2025, is anticipated to be completed in 2027 and will consolidate police and informatics departments, enhancing public safety and technology functions.
