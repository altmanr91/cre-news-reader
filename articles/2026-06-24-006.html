Berkadia Arranges $124.6M Loan for Refinancing of Irving Mixed-Use Property

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 15:13:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Berkadia has arranged a $124.6 million loan for the refinancing of Alesio Urban Center, a mixed-use property in Irving, TX. The property, owned by Harbor Group International, features 908 apartments and 55,499 square feet of commercial space. Blackstone provided the financing for the asset located in the Las Colinas district.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Alesio Urban Center

Address: 385 Las Colinas Blvd E, Irving, TX*

Units: 908

Loan Amount: $124.6M

Loan Per Buildable Unit: $137,225/buildable unit (calculated)

Year Built: 1987

Site Area: 17.3 acres

Notable Features: Developed in phases between 1987 and 1995, the property includes 14 residential buildings, a structured parking garage, and 23 commercial spaces, with amenities such as five pools, a fitness center, business center, game lounge, running trail, and media/theater room.
