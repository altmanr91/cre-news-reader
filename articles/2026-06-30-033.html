Mystery bidder on Hawthorne Race Course facility could end planned “racino” development

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hawthorne Race Course, the last professional horse racing track in the Chicago area, is poised for a $90 million sale to an unknown corporate buyer represented by ALLIMAC 2023 LLC, following a Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing earlier this year. The sale, if approved by a bankruptcy judge, could lead to the closure of the racetrack, despite previous plans for a 'racino' development. The property's heavy industrial zoning and proximity to Chicago have attracted significant buyer interest, with potential redevelopments including data centers.
