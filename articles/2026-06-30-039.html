Heitman lists 84-acre Fremont shopping center as Silicon Valley retail market recovers

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 18:11:11 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Heitman has listed Pacific Commons, an 84-acre, nearly 900,000-square-foot shopping center in Fremont, for sale. The property is 92 percent leased with a strong tenant mix and is located near a significant employment base of advanced manufacturing companies and affluent households. The listing comes as Silicon Valley's retail market shows improving fundamentals, with declining vacancy and positive absorption, despite a slow investment sales market due to high borrowing costs.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Silicon Valley's retail vacancy dropped to 3.8 percent in Q2, with nearly 590,000 square feet absorbed over the past year. Elevated borrowing costs are keeping buyers on the sidelines, creating a disconnect between strong leasing fundamentals and muted transaction volume.
