Centerbridge Commits to Two Floors at Rudin’s 345 Park Ave.

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 26 May 2026 22:15:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Centerbridge Partners has committed to a 15-year lease for 75,826 square feet across two full floors at Rudin's 345 Park Avenue, establishing its new New York City headquarters. The private investment firm will relocate from its current nearby office in the second quarter of 2027. The landlord noted that the property offers a prime Plaza District location, best-in-class service, and an upcoming amenity suite, making it an ideal environment for leading companies.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 345 Park Ave.

Address: 345 Park Ave., New York City, NY

Size: 75,826 SF

Notable Features: 44-story Plaza District office tower with a top-tier amenity suite planned.

Project Notes: Centerbridge will occupy the entire 22nd and 23rd floors and relocate from 375 Park Avenue in Q2 2027.
