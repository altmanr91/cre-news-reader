Greek Real Estate Partners JV Seals $79M Financing Package for N.J. Industrial Dev

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 21:35:25 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture led by Greek Real Estate Partners (GREP) secured a $79 million financing package for the development of a 281,215-square-foot Class A industrial facility in Parsippany, NJ. The financing includes a $49 million recapitalization and a $30 million construction loan provided by Provident Bank. The project, which involves redeveloping a site previously occupied by an office building, is expected to achieve substantial completion in the first quarter of 2027, addressing strong demand for modern logistics space in a constrained market.
