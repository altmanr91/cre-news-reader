ESD pitches $5B plan to complete Pacific Park

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 23:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Empire State Development and the Atlantic Yards Community Development Corporation board have proposed a $5 billion plan to complete the Pacific Park megaproject in Brooklyn, New York City. The ambitious buildout, which has been delayed for over two decades, includes more than 5,600 residential units, with over 1,200 designated as income-restricted. The plan also features the decking over of railyards, exceeding previously approved open space requirements, and the construction of 1,000 condo units across three buildings, all to be built with union labor. A joint venture between Cirrus Real Estate Partners and LCOR, which acquired the remaining sites in a foreclosure auction, is slated to lead the development, leveraging their experience with large-scale projects and capacity to secure financing.
