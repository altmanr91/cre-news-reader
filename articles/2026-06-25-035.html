Meta nails down another sublease at Sixth and Guadalupe space in Downtown Austin

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lease

Meta has subleased 32,000 square feet at the Sixth and Guadalupe tower in Downtown Austin to The Malin, a New York-based coworking firm. This transaction marks another step in Meta's effort to sublease a significant portion of the 589,000 square feet it pre-leased in 2021 but never occupied. Despite this new sublease, a substantial amount of space remains available in the building, reflecting the broader challenges in Austin's office market as hybrid work models persist.

TENANTS AT PROPERTY

Weaver & Tidwell

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Meta still has 488,225 square feet available for sublease at Sixth and Guadalupe. The Austin office market has seen challenges post-pandemic due to hybrid work schedules and some tech companies returning to Silicon Valley, leading to multiple landlords holding vacant space. Google recently occupied its space at the Sail Tower, while Indeed put space in its namesake tower up for sublease.
