Endeavor points with bird dog certainty toward “Dog’s Head” development potential

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 20 May 2026 21:23:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

The Austin City Council is considering the annexation of a 2,614-acre parcel known as the "Dog's Head" into city limits, which would enable future mixed-use development by Endeavor Real Estate Group. The project is anticipated to generate $3.5 billion in property tax revenue for the city over three decades. The development is planned to include residential, commercial, and industrial components, with a commitment to affordable housing and a public trail along the Colorado River.
