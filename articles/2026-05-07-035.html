Architect buys Bayou Woods spec mansion asking $11M

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 06 May 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Levant Luxury Homes sold a newly built 12,145-square-foot spec mansion in Houston's exclusive Bayou Woods neighborhood to architect Dan Boggio and Susan Boggio. The property, which was asking $10.5 million, features six bedrooms, 10 bathrooms, a four-car garage, and terraces overlooking a pool, all situated on an acre lot. This sale follows Levant Luxury Homes' previous high-value residential transactions in the area, underscoring the continued demand for luxury homes in this prominent Houston enclave.

DATA POINTS

Notable Features: Six-bedroom, 10-bathroom home with a four-car garage and upstairs terraces overlooking the pool, located in the upscale Bayou Woods subdivision with 24-hour security and a strong school district.

Project Notes: The home was built in 2025 and was initially listed for $10.5 million in June 2025, relisted in December 2025 after a brief removal.
