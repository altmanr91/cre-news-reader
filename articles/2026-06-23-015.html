New development marketing guru Nancy Packes dies at 80

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Obituary

Nancy Packes, a pioneering figure in New York City's new development marketing, passed away at 80. After a career as a criminal trial lawyer, she founded Feathered Nest brokerage in 1977, building it into a top residential firm before selling it to Brown Harris Stevens in 1998. Packes was renowned for her consulting work with major developers, influencing design and buyer profiles for projects like Trump Tower, the Epic, the Helena, and Ohm. Her firm, Nancy Packes Inc., and its data services arm continued to be a significant resource for rental market information.
