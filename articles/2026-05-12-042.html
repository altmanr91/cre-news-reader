Barings Provides $58M to Refinance Newly Built Bushwick Apartments

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Mon, 11 May 2026 21:46:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Barings has provided a $58 million refinancing loan for 1333 Broadway, a recently completed mixed-use multifamily property in Brooklyn's Bushwick neighborhood. The transaction was arranged by JLL Capital Markets on behalf of a joint venture between Ekstein Development Group and Standard Real Estate Investments. The property's strong location, transit access, and tax benefits contributed to a favorable financing outcome.
