IPA Arranges $29M in Construction Financing for Northwest Houston Industrial Project

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 27 May 2026 15:25:20 +0000

SUMMARY

Institutional Property Advisors (IPA) has secured $29 million in construction financing for Lifestyle Industrial, an 84-unit industrial condominium project in northwest Houston. The development, undertaken by X Space Group, is designed to integrate office, retail, and entertainment elements. The financing will support the construction of these condo-style industrial units.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Lifestyle Industrial

Units: 84

Loan Amount: $29.0M

Loan Per Unit: $345,238/unit (calculated)

Notable Features: Condo-style industrial units designed to incorporate elements of office, retail, and entertainment.
