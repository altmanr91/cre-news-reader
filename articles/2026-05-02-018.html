Google’s 723K sf warehouse lease delivers boosts Charlotte industrial absorption

LEASE

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 01 May 2026 16:01:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Google has signed a significant lease for a 723,000-square-foot warehouse in Rowan County, North Carolina, a deal that substantially boosted Charlotte's industrial absorption in the first quarter. The tech giant's commitment to KC 100, located within the Overlook 85 industrial park, includes options for renewal and first rights on an adjacent planned 1 million-square-foot facility, indicating potential for further expansion. This transaction highlights the scarcity of large industrial spaces in the Charlotte market, which is experiencing sustained demand and declining vacancy rates.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: KC 100

Address: 1894 Old Beatty Ford Road, Charlotte, NC

Size: 723,000 SF

Notable Features: Located within the Overlook 85 industrial park; tenant has first rights on an adjacent planned 1 million-square-foot facility (KC 300).

Project Notes: Lease term is 88 months with two five-year renewal options.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Charlotte's industrial sector recorded over 1 million square feet of net absorption in Q1 2026, marking the third consecutive quarter above this threshold. Regional industrial vacancy dipped to 8.1%, with spaces larger than 500,000 square feet being notably scarce.
