Krusinski Construction Company tackling tenant-improvement project for auto, clean energy company in Chicago

REJournals — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 09:59:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

Krusinski Construction Company has been selected by Dayton Street Partners to undertake a tenant improvement project for a multinational automotive and clean energy company in Chicago. The project at the Loomis facility will convert the existing building into an electric-vehicle repair facility, involving extensive renovations including new overhead doors, ADA-compliant restrooms, enhanced exterior lighting, and significant mechanical and drainage upgrades. Construction is slated for completion in July 2026.
