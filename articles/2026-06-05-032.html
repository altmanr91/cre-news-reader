Chicago’s top construction permits: Week ending May 29, 2026

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 21:14:54 +0000

SUMMARY

This article details the top construction permits issued in Chicago for the week ending May 29, 2026, highlighting significant projects across various sectors. The largest permit is a $250 million revision and expansion of Terminal 3 at O'Hare International Airport, adding 35,960 square feet. Other notable permits include a $5.2 million interior renovation for an "Experience Center" at 141 West Jackson Boulevard, a $5 million renovation at Saint Joseph Hospital Chicago to expand sterile storage, and a $5 million mechanical plant replacement at Clissold Elementary School. Additionally, a $4.7 million exterior restoration is planned for the historic Edgewater Beach Apartments, and a $4.3 million office buildout is underway for the Illinois Housing Development Authority at 225 North Michigan Avenue.
