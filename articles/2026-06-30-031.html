Extensively rehabbed Winnetka mansion sells for $8M, highest local price so far this year

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 21:37:59 +0000

SUMMARY

A Winnetka mansion designed by Mayo & Mayo, extensively renovated by its sellers, has sold for $7.8 million, marking the highest residential sale in the suburb this year. The sellers, Greta and Joseph Cacciatore, acquired the property in 2023 for $3.05 million at a foreclosure auction and undertook a comprehensive renovation, including adding a third-floor living space, a pool, and enhancing the landscaping. The listing agent praised the sellers for maintaining the original architectural vision while significantly upgrading the home's amenities.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The $7.8 million sale is the third-highest across Chicago this year and the highest in Winnetka for 2026. Last year, multiple Winnetka homes sold in the $30 million range, with two homes exceeding $9.5 million by this time in 2025.
