Sotheby’s sues seller over $56M Hamptons deal

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 19:54:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Sotheby's International Realty has filed a lawsuit against seller Nancy Silverman and her LLC, alleging an unpaid commission of $840,000 from the $56 million sale of a Southampton property. The brokerage claims its agent, Harald Grant, was instrumental in securing the buyer, Roger Barnett, and salvaging the deal, which closed in August 2025. Silverman, however, refused to pay the agreed-upon 1.5% commission, leading to the litigation.

DATA POINTS
