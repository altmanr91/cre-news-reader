Mapletree to Develop 952,720 SF Industrial Project in Manalapan, New Jersey

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 12 May 2026 14:34:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Mapletree, a Singapore-based development and investment firm, is set to develop a substantial 952,720-square-foot industrial project in Manalapan, New Jersey. The project will comprise two buildings on a 96.8-acre site, with construction slated to begin in the coming months and conclude in early 2028. This development aims to enhance industrial capacity in Central New Jersey, strategically located near a major transportation artery.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 370 Smithburg Road, Manalapan, NJ

Size: 952,720 SF

Completion: early 2028

Site Area: 96.8 acres

Notable Features: Two buildings totaling 300,220 SF and 652,500 SF, 40-foot clear heights, 172 dock doors, and parking for 366 cars and 141 trailers. Located near Exit 8 off the New Jersey Turnpike.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Mapletree
