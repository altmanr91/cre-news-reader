SF’s great AI lease-up continues with latest firm growing nearly sixfold in SoMa

LEASE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 20:13:45 +0000

SUMMARY

LangChain, an AI tools company, has significantly expanded its San Francisco office footprint by subleasing 70,000 square feet at 303 Second Street in the South of Market neighborhood from Sony Interactive Entertainment. This move represents a nearly sixfold increase in space for LangChain, which previously occupied 11,800 square feet nearby. The transaction highlights the continued strong demand from artificial intelligence companies for office space in San Francisco, a trend that is driving the city's office market recovery.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 303 Second Street, San Francisco, CA

Size: 70,000 SF

Current Occupancy: 66.1%

Notable Features: The building is a 785,000-square-foot office structure.

Project Notes: LangChain's new lease comes about a year after it moved into an 11,800-square-foot office at 501 Second Street. Sony PlayStation signed a lease for 130,000 square feet across three floors in the building in 2019, and LangChain's sublease includes two of those three floors.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Tenant requirements for office space in San Francisco are up to approximately 9.7 million square feet, a 50 percent increase from a year ago. Tech firms account for roughly half of this demand, with AI companies alone making up about a quarter. Overall demand has returned to 2018 levels.
