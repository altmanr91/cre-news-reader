Blue Diamond buys Fremont Hyatt for $13M as hotel bargains draw investors

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Blue Diamond Hospitality has acquired the 151-room Hyatt Place Fremont/Silicon Valley hotel in Fremont, CA, for $13.2 million from RLJ Lodging Trust. The transaction reflects a significant discount, with the sale price being 34% below the property's last assessed value, indicating a trend of bargain hunting amidst a wave of hotel foreclosures and declining values in the Bay Area.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Bay Area hotels have experienced a steep drop in values, leading to numerous foreclosures in downtown San Jose, downtown Oakland, and San Francisco. This market condition is attracting new investment from buyers seeking bargains.
