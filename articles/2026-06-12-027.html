Michael Stern’s JDS to bring on partner Jeff Soffer at Mercedes-Benz Miami project, working on $1B loan

LOAN

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JDS Development Group is reportedly close to securing a new partnership with Fontainebleau Development and an over $1 billion financing package for its Mercedes-Benz branded condominium project in Miami. This deal is expected to recapitalize the project, resolve ongoing litigation with the previous lender, and allow construction to proceed on the nearly 800-unit development. The financing includes a C-PACE component, emphasizing sustainable design and materials.
