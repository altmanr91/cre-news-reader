San Jose motel eyed for housing conversion as Mahan touts lodging-to-living push

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 19 May 2026 21:50:35 +0000

SUMMARY

A motel in north San Jose is slated for conversion into a residential complex, aligning with the city's strategy to meet state housing goals. The project involves transforming the existing 58-room Caravelle Inn & Suites into 58 apartments, followed by the construction of a new five-story, 45-unit apartment building on the same parcel. This initiative is part of San Jose Mayor Matt Mahan's broader effort to convert motels into housing across the city.
