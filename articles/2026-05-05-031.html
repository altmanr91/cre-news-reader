Sacked Fermi CEO hits Texas REIT with wrongful termination lawsuit

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 04 May 2026 17:18:50 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Former Fermi CEO Toby Neugebauer has filed a wrongful termination lawsuit against the Texas-based data center REIT, alleging he was fired without cause and that the board misled investors about his departure. This legal action comes amidst a challenging period for Fermi, which has seen its stock price plummet and faced criticism for its ambitious, unleased Project Matador development. Neugebauer is advocating for an immediate sale of the company.
