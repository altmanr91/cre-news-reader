South Bay mobile home parks trade hands in swath of deals

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 26 May 2026 21:43:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Three mobile home parks in the South Bay area of California have recently traded hands for a combined total of $23.2 million. A group led by Mountain View developer Peter Wang acquired two properties, Mobilodge of Milpitas and Old Orchard Mobile Park in San Jose, for $9.1 million and $8.2 million respectively. Wang also sold the Mayfair Trailer Park in San Jose for $5.9 million to a Sacramento-based affiliate led by John McDougall of Monte Christo Communities. These transactions reflect continued investor interest in mobile home parks in the region.
