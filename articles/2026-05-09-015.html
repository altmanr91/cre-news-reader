Illinois Tool Works moves into 60,000-square-foot industrial space in Bartlett

LEASE

REJournals — Fri, 08 May 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Illinois Tool Works has leased a 60,050-square-foot industrial space at Blue Heron Business Park in Bartlett, Illinois. The company's Electronic Component Solutions group will utilize the facility for its ITW Formex and ITW Lumex brands, focusing on specialized safety and technology products for various sectors. The lease transaction was the culmination of a seven-month process, with the landlord, Cratos Industrial, represented by Brown Commercial Group.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Blue Heron Business Park

Address: 2305-2325 Kenyon Road, Bartlett, IL

Size: 60,050 SF

Notable Features: The building features 32-foot clear height ceilings, 36 exterior truck docks, and three drive-in doors, with convenient access to IL Route 25 and US Route 20.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

TENANT: Illinois Tool Works
