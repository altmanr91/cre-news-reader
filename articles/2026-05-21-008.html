PAGEWOOD Breaks Ground on Adaptive Reuse Project in Downtown Houston

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 20 May 2026 14:45:22 +0000

SUMMARY

PAGEWOOD has broken ground on the first phase of East Blocks, an adaptive reuse project in Houston's East Downtown neighborhood. This initial phase involves converting two 15,000-square-foot warehouses into a mixed-use district of retail, office, and green spaces. The project is a partnership with Wile Interests and is expected to deliver in August.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: East Blocks

Address: 1107 Hutchins St. / 2202 Dallas St., Houston, TX

Size: 30,000 SF

Completion: August 2026

Phase I: Conversion of two 15,000-square-foot warehouses

Notable Features: Adaptive reuse project transforming a 10-block stretch of mid-20th century warehouses into a walkable district.
