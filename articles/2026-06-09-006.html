How Developers Can Prioritize Successful Adaptive Reuse Lending Relationships

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article by John Garrett of Enterprise Bank & Trust discusses the unique financial challenges and opportunities in adaptive reuse projects, particularly for sub-institutional transitional developments. It highlights how these projects transform underutilized commercial spaces into new, functional uses like residential or mixed-use, offering value-add opportunities for developers. The author emphasizes the importance of strong, flexible banking partnerships, especially with local banks, to navigate the complexities of these projects, which often involve limited interim cash flow and specific lending needs. Garrett advises developers to be "loan ready" by demonstrating experience, a strong understanding of market numbers, and a commitment to a banking relationship beyond just seeking the lowest interest rate, while also managing dependencies, ensuring sufficient equity, and avoiding over-rapid expansion.
