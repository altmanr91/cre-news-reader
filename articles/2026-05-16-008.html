LanCarte Commercial Arranges Sale of 33,000 SF Industrial Building in South Fort Worth

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 15 May 2026 14:07:24 +0000

SUMMARY

LanCarte Commercial has facilitated the sale of a 33,000-square-foot industrial building located in South Fort Worth's Carter Industrial Park. The single-tenant property at 7610 Will Rogers Blvd. was acquired by an undisclosed buyer who intends to utilize the facility for manufacturing operations. Sarah LanCarte represented the undisclosed seller in this transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 7610 Will Rogers Blvd, Fort Worth, TX

Size: 33,000 SF

Notable Features: Single-tenant building located within Carter Industrial Park.
