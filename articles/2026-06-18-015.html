Lawrence Group completes work on free-standing clinic in Austin

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 16:12:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lawrence Group has completed the development of ARC South Specialty, a new 63,000-square-foot, two-level free-standing clinic in Austin, TX, for Austin Regional Clinic (ARC). The facility, which opened on June 1, consolidates a wide range of specialty, primary, and surgical care services into a single campus location. Designed with a patient-centered approach, the clinic incorporates locally sourced materials and sustainable features, aiming for an Austin Energy Green Building 3 Stars rating.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Property Name: ARC South Specialty

Address: 708 West Ben White Blvd., Austin, TX

Size: 63,000 SF

Completion: June 2026

Notable Features: Designed to achieve an Austin Energy Green Building (AEGB) 3 Stars rating; incorporates locally sourced limestone veneer and wood panels; includes lab and radiology services, plus cardiology labs.

Project Notes: Lawrence Group performed a detailed feasibility study in June 2024, which led ARC to opt for new construction over adaptive reuse. The facility will also house ARC Surgery Center South, expected to open in summer 2026.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Austin Regional Clinic (ARC)

ARCHITECT: Lawrence Group

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Rogers-O’Brien Construction

SPONSOR PIPELINE

ARC Greenlawn Specialty: Opened in Round Rock, TX in January 2026, and will soon include ARC Surgery Center North.
