BHI Provides Condo Inventory Loan for Ground-Up Luxury Project

LOAN

Connect CRE — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:19:03 +0000

SUMMARY

BHI, the U.S. branch of Bank Hapoalim, B.M., provided a $200 million condo inventory loan to Malabar Residences, a newly constructed ultra-luxury residential condominium and ground-floor retail building in New York City. The financing will retire the project's existing construction loan as it approaches completion and transitions into the temporary certificate of occupancy (TCO) phase. This loan underscores BHI's confidence in the New York City market and its commitment to supporting well-conceived developments.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: Malabar Residences

Address: 126 E. 57th St., New York City, NY

Units: 145

Loan Amount: $200.0M

Loan Per Unit: $1,379,310/unit (calculated)

Notable Features: Ultra-luxury residential condominium with ground-floor retail, units ranging from 508 SF to 2,992 SF.

Project Notes: The loan retires an existing construction loan as the project nears completion and enters its TCO phase. Projected sale prices range from $1.36 million to $13.5 million per unit.
