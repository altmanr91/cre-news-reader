Chicago Housing Authority board power player, tax commissioner jump into mayoral race

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 20:20:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

This article reports on two real estate-adjacent figures, Matthew Brewer and George Cardenas, formally entering the race for Chicago mayor. Both candidates bring significant real estate experience, with Brewer having served on the Chicago Housing Authority board and Cardenas as a Cook County Board of Review Commissioner. The city's housing crisis and a substantial construction pipeline logjam are expected to be central issues in the upcoming election.
