Hedge funder seeks record $153M for Sagaponack estate

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 14:11:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Hedge fund founder Zach Schreiber has listed his Sagaponack estate at 39 Fairfield Pond Lane for $152.5 million, a price that would set a new Hamptons record for a single parcel if achieved. The property features a renovated, fully-furnished two-story home with extensive amenities and over 360 feet of beach frontage. This listing comes amidst a strong luxury market in the South Fork, which saw a significant increase in high-end transactions and median sale prices for luxury homes in the first quarter of the year.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

During the first three months of this year, transactions above $5 million notched a record share of deals in the South Fork, and the median sale price for luxury homes jumped 30 percent to $13 million. Sales of $10 million or more generated $560 million in volume during the first quarter, up from $523 million in the same period in 2020.
