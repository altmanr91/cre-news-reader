Big Tech Says Data Centers Must Change To Survive Local Opposition

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 17:06:19 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Executives from Microsoft and Oracle argue that the data center industry must fundamentally change its approach to design and development to overcome growing local opposition. They contend that the industry's current strategy of dismissing community concerns as misinformation or NIMBYism is unsustainable, especially as AI-driven development expands into residential and rural areas. Instead, developers and hyperscalers need to proactively address issues like noise, emissions, visual impact, and resource consumption through innovative architectural solutions and community engagement. The executives emphasize that many of these problems are solvable with existing engineering and design principles, but the industry has been slow to adapt, leading to a negative public perception that will be difficult to reverse.
