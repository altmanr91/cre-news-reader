An insatiable demand? The data center sector still red hot throughout Texas

REJournals — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 10:24:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Data Center Outlook

Newmark's recent data center outlook report highlights Texas as a prime market for data center development due to its scale, flexibility, and lower-cost power, which supports a robust "powered industries ecosystem." This ecosystem, where digital infrastructure, power generation, advanced manufacturing, and supply chain activities are interconnected, is driving significant industrial demand. The report quantifies that each operating data center in Texas is associated with approximately 531,000 square feet of supporting industrial space, with data-center-related industrial leasing reaching nearly 14 million square feet in 2025. The primary challenges to faster growth include power constraints, grid connection delays, equipment bottlenecks, and increasing community opposition over water use and infrastructure strain. Developers are responding by adopting water-efficient cooling systems and collaborating with local officials to address concerns.
