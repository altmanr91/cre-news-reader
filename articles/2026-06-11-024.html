Trilogy wins over $1M in suit over leaking “Frankenstein” roof at luxury auto club

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Miami-Dade Circuit Court judge awarded Trilogy Real Estate Group $1.2 million in damages and other awards in a lawsuit against Link Construction Group, the general contractor for its luxury motorsports club. The litigation centered on extensive roof leaks and other defects at six auto lofts within The Concours Club, which significantly delayed its planned opening. The court found Link Construction Group in breach of contract and held it primarily responsible for the defects and associated losses, including liquidated damages for lost rent and the cost of re-roofing. The judge also determined that Trilogy was justified in retaining nearly $2.5 million from Link's contract balance to cover repair expenses.
