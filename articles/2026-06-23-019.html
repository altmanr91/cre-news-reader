Hotel mogul makes $4M-plus in five years on Fisher Island condo sale

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hospitality executive Keith Menin and his wife, Evelyn Menin, sold their 3,600-square-foot condo unit at Palazzo Della Luna Residences on Fisher Island for $10.5 million. The off-market transaction, handled by ONE Sotheby's International Realty, represents a significant gain for the sellers who acquired the unit in 2021 for $6.1 million. This sale is part of a robust luxury condo market in Miami, with Fisher Island continuing to command premium prices.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Miami's luxury condo market is experiencing its third-strongest first quarter ever for the $1 million+ market, with 424 closed sales and an average sale price of $1.84 million. The $2 million+ market saw 204 closed sales and a median sale price of $3.6 million, a 5 percent increase from the previous year. Fisher Island was ranked the nation's wealthiest zip code last year.
