Meta pumps brakes on Menlo Park mixed-use megaproject amid 61% profit jump

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Meta Platforms has indefinitely shelved its ambitious Willow Village mixed-use development in Menlo Park, citing a challenging real estate market and evolving space requirements. The project, which received city approval in 2022, was designed to include nearly 1.3 million square feet of office space, 1,730 residential units (with 300 designated as affordable), and various retail amenities. This decision aligns with the tech giant's broader strategy of reducing its office footprint and workforce, despite reporting a significant 61% increase in first-quarter profits.
