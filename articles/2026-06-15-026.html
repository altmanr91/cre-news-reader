SUMMARY

The article highlights an unexpected positive outcome of Dallas hosting the World Cup: an influx of international visitors sharing positive experiences of Downtown Dallas on social media. This has generated a sense of civic pride among locals, contrasting with recent news of major companies relocating from the downtown area. The article also briefly covers the Houston Astros' acquisition of a Salvation Army shelter for a parking garage, Texas Governor Greg Abbott's push for data center regulations, and Silver Star Properties' second Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing.
