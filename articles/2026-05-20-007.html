Newmark Arranges Sale, Financing of 1.4 MSF Office Campus in Irving

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 19 May 2026 14:43:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Newmark has facilitated the sale and acquisition financing for The Towers at Williams Square, a 1.4 million-square-foot office campus located in Irving's Las Colinas district. The property, comprising four buildings, was 76 percent leased at the time of closing. A joint venture between Vanderbilt Office Properties, Hillwood, and TriPost Capital Partners acquired the asset, which recently underwent $25 million in renovations to its common areas and amenity spaces.
