Greystar, University of Texas at Austin Break Ground on 340-Bed Student Housing Project

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 12 May 2026 14:49:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Greystar and the University of Texas at Austin have commenced construction on Law School Village, a 14-story student housing development. This public-private partnership will deliver 340 beds across 280 units, offering a mix of studio, one-, and two-bedroom layouts. The project is designed to cater specifically to law school students and is anticipated to be ready for occupancy by the 2028-2029 academic year.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Property Name: Law School Village

Address: 1900 Burton Dr, Austin, TX*

Units: 280

Beds: 340

Notable Features: Amenities include a library-style sky lounge, café, fitness and yoga area, courtyard, two rooftop decks, and ground-floor retail space.

Project Notes: Developed through a public-private partnership with the University of Texas at Austin.
