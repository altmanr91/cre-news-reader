Karis Critical taking aim at vacant former BP campus in Naperville

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Karis Critical is exploring development options for the vacant 169-acre former BP campus in Naperville, IL, following a previous rejection of a data center proposal. The firm met with the Naperville Development Partnership and city officials to discuss potential uses, with an advanced manufacturing facility being one option. Karis Critical has indicated a willingness to invest in site cleanup, and city officials are keen to attract a viable project to the long-vacant property.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Hoffman Estates rezoning request: Rezoning request for land owned by Karis Critical, not recommended by village planning commissioners due to potential data center use. Proposed for manufacturing and industrial buildings.
