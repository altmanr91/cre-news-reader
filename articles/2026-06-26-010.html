Madison’s Momentum: Development, retail and innovation continue to fuel Wisconsin’s capital city

REJournals — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 10:21:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Madison, WI continues to experience robust commercial real estate activity across multiple sectors, driven by strong economic fundamentals, population growth, and municipal policies encouraging density. Mixed-use developments, particularly in downtown areas, are thriving with over 60 projects in various stages, emphasizing walkability and community spaces. The retail sector is a strong performer with low vacancy rates and positive absorption, attracting national brands and local operators, while industrial real estate has rebounded after a temporary slowdown. Despite elevated construction and borrowing costs, developers remain confident, pushing forward with projects and eyeing major redevelopment opportunities downtown.
