Steve Madden Inks 60K-SF Office Lease at ESRT’s 501 Seventh Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 20:16:29 +0000

SUMMARY

Steve Madden has signed a 13-year office lease for 60,003 square feet at Empire State Realty Trust's 501 Seventh Avenue in Midtown Manhattan. This transaction represents a relocation for the shoe and accessories retailer's corporate headquarters. The landlord was represented in-house and by Cushman & Wakefield, while Savills brokered the deal for Steve Madden.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 501 Seventh Avenue

Address: 501 Seventh Avenue, New York, NY

Size: 60,003 SF

Year Built: 1923

Notable Features: 18-story office property located near Pennsylvania Station.

Project Notes: Asking rent was in the low $60s per square foot. Steve Madden is relocating its corporate headquarters from Long Island City, Queens.
