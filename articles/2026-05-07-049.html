Ideaya Biosciences Expanding Into 80K SF In South San Francisco

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 06 May 2026 18:06:25 -0400

SUMMARY

Ideaya Biosciences has expanded its footprint at 5000 Shoreline Court in South San Francisco with a 25,000 SF lease expansion, bringing its total occupancy in the building to 80,000 SF. The clinical-stage cancer research company initially leased 44,000 SF in June 2023, followed by an 11,300 SF expansion in 2024, and now this latest addition. The property owner, DivcoWest, acquired the vacant 140,000 SF waterfront building in 2022 and has been converting it into laboratory space, investing significantly in tenant improvements and infrastructure upgrades.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: lab

Address: 5000 Shoreline Court, South San Francisco, CA

Size: 25,000 SF

Rental Rate: $64.20/SF/yr

Completion: March 1, 2027

Notable Features: Waterfront building at Sierra Point, outfitted with a new 1,200-kilowatt generator system and upgraded electrical systems.

Project Notes: DivcoWest previously acquired the vacant 140,000 SF building for $165M and converted it into laboratory space. Ideaya Biosciences' total occupancy in the building is now 80,000 SF, including prior expansions. DivcoWest will contribute $6.8M to tenant improvements for the current expansion.
