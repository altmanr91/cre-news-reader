One of Texas’ priciest homes trades hands

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 08 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A mystery entity, Lost River Trust, has acquired a luxury mansion at 4815 Saint Johns Drive in Highland Park, Dallas, from entrepreneurs Melbourne and Jamie O’Banion. The property was listed for $24.9 million, though the final sale price was not disclosed. This acquisition follows the trust's purchase of another high-end home in the area just six months prior, indicating a pattern of significant investment in the Dallas luxury residential market.
