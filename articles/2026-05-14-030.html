Judge tosses OC Ventures’ bankruptcy, clearing path for Stephen Bus’ discounted UIC student housing deal

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 13 May 2026 20:07:29 +0000

SUMMARY

A federal bankruptcy judge dismissed the Chapter 11 case of an affiliate of OC Ventures, clearing the path for Stephen Bus' firm, Up Campus Holdings, to acquire The Letterman, a 482-bed student housing property in Chicago's West Loop. The dismissal allows Up Campus Holdings to proceed with the forced sale of the property for $44 million, an $8 million discount from its initial offer. The ruling is a significant setback for OC Ventures' principal, Shangxuan Tan, who had sought to use bankruptcy to nullify the sale contract and find a higher bidder, but failed to provide sufficient evidence to the court.

An additional $8 million in debt was provided by Monroe Capital and Daili Xiao, which triggered the foreclosure.
