Can JLL smooth out Sundance Square leasing as Sasha and Ed Bass’ step back?

The Real Deal — Texas — Sat, 16 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Leasing Assignment

JLL has been appointed to manage retail leasing for Sundance Square, a 35-block mixed-use district in Downtown Fort Worth. This move follows criticism of the previous stewardship by Sasha and Ed Bass, who took control in 2019 and faced challenges including high vacancy rates and tenant complaints about untenable rent increases and unfulfilled marketing services. The Basses also appointed LanCarte Commercial Real Estate to handle office leasing a few months prior, signaling a shift in management strategy for the prominent downtown area.
