Google, HouseCanary roll out home listing search results to 50 states

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 17:06:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Google is expanding its home listing display feature to all 50 states, allowing mobile search results to directly show property listings. This initiative, developed in partnership with AI-enabled brokerage HouseCanary, relies on data from participating Multiple Listing Services (MLSes) and direct partnerships with brokerages. The program aims to provide brokers and agents with a free method to increase the discoverability of their listings, offering prominent attribution and a click-to-contact button for consumers. This move is seen as a significant challenge to established real estate portals, potentially altering how listings are shared and monetized in the industry.
