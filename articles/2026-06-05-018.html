Housing Notes: If the mortgage fits, wear the rate

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 20:01:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article analyzes the current state of the residential mortgage market, noting that 2026 mortgage volume is expected to slightly exceed 2025, but overall expectations are lower than pre-Iran war forecasts due to rising mortgage rates. The author highlights that a significant increase in refinance activity is largely a statistical artifact of comparing to a very low base year in 2025, rather than a true surge in demand. The piece also discusses the 2024 rebranding of mortgage lender Guaranteed Rate to 'Rate,' which included an AI-focused strategy, and the company's recent expansion into athleisure clothing as a potential diversification strategy in a volatile market.
