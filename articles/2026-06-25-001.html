Apollo, Affinius Capital, RXR Invest $785M in Debt and Equity to Build 175 Third Street

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:09:39 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture has secured $785 million in debt and equity financing for the construction of 175 Third Street, a 1.1 million-square-foot mixed-use residential complex in Brooklyn. This project, which will be the fifth building in the Gowanus Wharf campus, will deliver 1,100 units, with 25% designated as affordable. The financing package includes $600 million in debt from Apollo and Affinius Capital, alongside a $185 million equity investment from RXR. The development team has a strong track record in the rapidly growing Gowanus neighborhood, having previously developed several successful residential projects in the area.

Project Notes: The site was acquired approximately one year ago from RFR Holding. This is the fifth building developed by the sponsorship team in the Gowanus Wharf campus since 2019, following the Gowanus rezoning.
