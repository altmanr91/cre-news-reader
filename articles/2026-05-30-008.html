Stout Street Hospitality Receives Refinancing for Magnolia Hotel Denver

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 29 May 2026 13:54:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Stout Street Hospitality has secured refinancing for its flagship Magnolia Hotel Denver, a Tribute Portfolio Hotel by Marriott, located in downtown Denver's central business district. JLL Hotels & Hospitality arranged the loan through PPM America for the borrower. The property, originally built in 1910 as a bank, was converted into a boutique hotel in 1995 and also houses Stout Street Hospitality's corporate headquarters.
