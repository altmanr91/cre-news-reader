Dallas Mavericks To Purchase Valley View Mall Site For New Arena

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 17:55:06 -0400

SUMMARY

The Dallas Mavericks announced plans to purchase 104 acres of the former Valley View Center mall site in Dallas, TX, for a new mixed-use district. This development will be anchored by a new arena for the NBA team, which aims to open by 2031 when its current lease at American Airlines Center expires. The project is expected to include restaurants, entertainment, public green spaces, and family-friendly experiences, aligning with existing zoning for the property.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Valley View Center mall site

Site Area: 104 acres

Notable Features: Site of former Valley View Center mall, located along Interstate 635.

Project Notes: Most of the mall was demolished in 2019, with remaining structures removed by 2023. The new arena is targeted for a 2031 opening.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Dallas Mavericks — Rick Welts, CEO

SELLER: Beck Ventures — Scott Beck, CEO

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Anthem Development
