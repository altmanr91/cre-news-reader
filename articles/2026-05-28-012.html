General manager with Chicago’s Draper and Kramer wins property manager award

REJournals — Wed, 27 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Kelli Stuart, a general manager and assistant vice president at Draper and Kramer, Incorporated, received the 2026 Certified Property Manager (CPM) Excellence Award from the Institute of Real Estate Management (IREM)'s Chicago chapter. The award recognizes her excellence in real estate management, leadership, community service, and engagement with IREM. Stuart, who has 17 years of industry experience, currently manages three high-profile residential properties in Chicago's Gold Coast neighborhood, demonstrating versatility in delivering exceptional residential experiences and asset performance.
