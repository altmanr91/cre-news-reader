Apple strikes again in Sunnyvale with a $162M office pickup

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 22:18:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Apple has acquired an office building at 684 West Maude Avenue in Sunnyvale, CA for $162.2 million. The tech giant purchased the property from Union Investment Real Estate GmbH and Metzler Real Estate Advisors, securing a significant discount from its previous sale price in 2022. This acquisition continues Apple's trend of expanding its real estate footprint in the Silicon Valley region, particularly in Sunnyvale and Cupertino.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 684 West Maude Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA

Sale Price: $162.2M

Project Notes: The property was previously acquired in 2022 for $222 million. LinkedIn leased the building in 2022, but it is unclear if they ever occupied the space.
