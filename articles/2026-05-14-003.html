Retailer Garage to Open 9K-SF Corner Store at RFR’s 160 Fifth Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 13 May 2026 20:41:50 +0000

SUMMARY

Fashion retailer Garage has secured a 9,023-square-foot lease for its second New York City store at RFR Holding's 160 Fifth Avenue in the Flatiron District. The space, formerly part of a larger retail footprint, was divided to accommodate modern brand requirements. The ground floor asking rent was $500 per square foot, with the lower level at $150 per square foot.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 160 Fifth Avenue, New York City, NY

Size: 9,023 SF

Year Built: 1891

Notable Features: Historic building designed by R.H. Robertson with large showrooms and loft offices, featuring a restored facade and a new 3,300-square-foot rooftop terrace amenity.

Project Notes: The retail space was previously occupied by Club Monaco and was vertically sliced into two shops to accommodate current brand requirements.
