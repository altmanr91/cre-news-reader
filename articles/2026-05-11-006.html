Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 36-unit apartment property in Falcon Heights

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 11 May 2026 09:49:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Falcon View Apartments, a 36-unit multifamily property in Falcon Heights, Minnesota, which traded for $5.09 million. The property, consisting of two buildings, offers a mix of one-, two-, and three-bedroom floor plans.
