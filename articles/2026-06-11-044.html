Fun Is The New Foot Traffic As Trampoline Parks, Challenge Rooms Plot Huge Expansion

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 17:27:59 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Retail Trends

Family entertainment concepts, including trampoline parks and challenge rooms, are demonstrating significant resilience and expansion in the retail real estate sector, driven by consumer demand for affordable local outings over costly theme park vacations. This trend is creating a reliable demand pipeline for brick-and-mortar retail, particularly for backfilling large-format vacancies. Operators and landlords view these entertainment venues as new anchors that generate foot traffic and serve as core drivers for retail strategy, especially in suburban areas and the Sun Belt. The sector's growth is also fueled by investors seeking to reposition empty mall anchors and second-generation retail boxes, transforming disused spaces into vibrant community hubs.
