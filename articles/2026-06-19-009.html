HDA Completes Design of 23,000 SF Nissan Dealership in Herculaneum, Missouri

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 12:49:16 +0000

SUMMARY

HDA has completed the design of the AutoCenters Nissan dealership in Herculaneum, a suburb of St. Louis. The 23,000-square-foot facility includes 8,500 square feet for office, administrative, and customer-facing functions, alongside 14,500 square feet dedicated to service, parts, and detailing.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: AutoCenters Nissan

Size: 23,000 SF

Notable Features: Features a full curtainwall system for the showroom and a signature red Nissan entry tablet.

ARCHITECT: HDA
