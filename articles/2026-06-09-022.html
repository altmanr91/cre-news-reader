Seaport’s “Sky House” penthouse asking $20M snags contract

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 15:47:28 +0000

SUMMARY

A penthouse unit at 150 Nassau Street, known as "Sky House," has gone under contract with an asking price of $20 million, making it the priciest Manhattan home to find a buyer last week. The 6,400-square-foot quadplex, featuring unique amenities like a four-story stainless-steel slide and multiple terraces, was previously renovated by its sellers, former technology executives Craig and Kristen Nevill-Manning. This transaction is part of a strong luxury market in Manhattan, which saw 36 properties asking $4 million or more secure contracts last week.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Manhattan's trophy market has been on a hot streak, with an average of 34 luxury homes per week going into contract over the last three months. Approximately a quarter of these deals were for homes asking $10 million or more. The typical luxury home in contract last week was on the market for over a year and saw a 4 percent discount.
