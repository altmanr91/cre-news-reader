Luxury Jewelry Brand David Yurman Takes 685 Fifth’s Entire 23K-SF Retail Condo

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 19:21:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Luxury jewelry brand David Yurman has leased the entire 23,000-square-foot retail condominium at 685 Fifth Avenue in Midtown Manhattan, taking over space previously occupied by a global fashion house. This significant lease comes shortly after a $160 million loan on the property was transferred to special servicing due to tenant departures. The landlord, GGP, expressed confidence in the asset's ability to attract high-quality brands, and the new flagship store is expected to create lasting value at this iconic corner.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: 685 Fifth Avenue

Address: 685 Fifth Avenue, New York City, NY

Size: 23,000 SF

Notable Features: Located at the base of a 29-story building that also houses 69 luxury residential condos.

Project Notes: A $160 million loan on the property was transferred to special servicing last month due to concerns of financial default as previous tenants vacated.
