Cronheim Hotel Capital Arranges $50.4M Financing for Two Hotels on Amelia Island in Florida

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 28 May 2026 14:27:11 +0000

SUMMARY

Cronheim Hotel Capital has arranged $50.4 million in financing for two hotels on Amelia Island, Florida. The financing includes a $29.9 million loan for the refinancing and conversion of the Ocean Coast Hotel into a Holiday Inn Resort, and a separate $20.5 million loan for the refinancing of the Amelia Hotel. Both loans were originated with a regional bank and incorporate USDA and PACE components.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: Ocean Coast Hotel / Amelia Hotel

Loan Amount: $50.4M

Notable Features: Amelia Hotel is an independent beachfront hotel. Ocean Coast Hotel is being converted to a Holiday Inn Resort.

Project Notes: Loans include USDA and PACE components.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

MORTGAGE BROKER: Cronheim Hotel Capital

SPONSOR: Innisfree Hotels

SPONSOR: RREAF Holdings
