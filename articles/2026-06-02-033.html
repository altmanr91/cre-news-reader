Gale Associates Opens Rhode Island Office in Providence

Connect CRE — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 21:28:11 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Consulting engineering firm Gale Associates, Inc. has established a new office in Providence, Rhode Island, located within The Foundry, a historic commercial campus. The expansion aims to enhance client service and strengthen relationships across Southern New England, with Senior Project Manager Jason Wagner leading the new location.
