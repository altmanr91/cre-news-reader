Q&A: Cresa’s new CEO Ray Anderson talks raising capital, AI and weaponizing brokerage conflicts

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Ray Anderson, the new CEO of Cresa, is spearheading a growth strategy focused on North American acquisitions, recruiting top brokers, and leveraging technology. Anderson, who comes from a consulting and tech background, is rolling out "Cresa Core," an AI-powered platform designed to assist tenants with lease administration, accounting, site selection, and labor analytics. He emphasizes Cresa's tenant-only model as a key differentiator, arguing that it addresses potential conflicts of interest in the commercial real estate brokerage industry, a concern he believes is amplified by recent scrutiny in the residential sector. Anderson's approach is centered on scaling the firm through strategic M&A and technological integration, rather than preparing it for sale.
