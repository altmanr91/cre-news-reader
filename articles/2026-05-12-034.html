JPMorgan Chase lends $400M for AT&T’s Plano headquarters

LOAN

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 11 May 2026 22:08:40 +0000

SUMMARY

JPMorgan Chase has provided a $400 million construction loan to KDC for the development of AT&T's new $1.4 billion headquarters in Plano, TX. This financing marks a key step in the project, which will consolidate AT&T's operations into a 54-acre campus. The move follows AT&T's decision to relocate from its longtime Dallas headquarters, influenced by concerns over downtown quality of life and a significant economic incentive package from the City of Plano.
