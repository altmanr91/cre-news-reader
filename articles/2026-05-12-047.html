Eli Simon Touts $2B Development Pipeline In First Earnings Call As Simon Property CEO

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 11 May 2026 12:42:49 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Earnings Report

Eli Simon, the new CEO of Simon Property Group, announced a robust development pipeline totaling $2 billion, with $1 billion in projects currently underway and another $1 billion slated to begin this year. These projects primarily involve redeveloping existing anchor spaces into retail and restaurants, or adding hotels and apartments to existing centers. The REIT also reported strong first-quarter results for 2026, with increased funds from operations, a raised quarterly dividend, and a slight uptick in portfolio occupancy to 96%.
