Waterfront Miami Beach mansion of late Broadway legend sells for $32M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 20 May 2026 23:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The waterfront Miami Beach mansion of the late Broadway legend Harold Prince has been sold for $31.5 million by his daughter, Daisy Chaplin. The 9,000-square-foot estate, built in 1937 on a 0.8-acre lot with 150 feet of bay frontage, is expected to be redeveloped. This transaction continues a trend of high-value sales on North Bay Road.
