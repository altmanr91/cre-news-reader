Aby Rosen selling “Anna Delvey” building for $80M+

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 14:29:29 +0000

SUMMARY

RFR, led by Aby Rosen, is in contract to sell the historic 281 Park Avenue South building in New York City for $81.5 million. The Beaux-Arts landmark, formerly home to Fotografiska, is currently vacant. The sale price of approximately $2,000 per square foot represents a significant premium compared to recent neighborhood transactions, reflecting RFR's substantial investment in overhauling the building's systems since acquiring it in 2014.
