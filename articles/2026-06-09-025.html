South Florida’s top deals: Finance exec sells Surf Club condo for $27M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 22:02:34 +0000

SUMMARY

This article is a roundup of the top residential sales in South Florida. The priciest transaction involved financier Todd Green selling a condo at the Four Seasons at The Surf Club in Surfside for $27.3 million. Another notable sale was a waterfront property on Hibiscus Island in Miami Beach, which traded for $18.5 million. Additionally, a home in Palm Beach sold for $15.9 million, and a Palm Beach Gardens residence was acquired for $11 million.
