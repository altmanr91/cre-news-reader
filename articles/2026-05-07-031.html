Shorewood scoops up wiped-clean Walgreens campus in Deerfield for $13M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:53:58 +0000

SUMMARY

Shorewood Development Group acquired the former 37.5-acre Walgreens office campus in Deerfield, IL, for $13.1 million from Orion Properties. The site, which was recently cleared of its outdated office buildings, is slated for a future mixed-use development incorporating retail, medical, and multifamily components. This acquisition marks another significant suburban redevelopment for Shorewood, following a trend of transforming large, vacant corporate sites.

Project Notes: The previous office buildings were demolished by the seller in late 2025 after Walgreens stopped paying rent in August 2023. A prior sale attempt to LG Development Group failed in 2023.
