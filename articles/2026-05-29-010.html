Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of Restaurant in Buford, Georgia Leased to Whataburger

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 28 May 2026 14:23:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap's Taylor McMinn Retail Group has brokered the sale of a Whataburger-occupied restaurant in Buford, GA. The property is subject to a 15-year ground lease with 10% rent increases and extension options. The transaction involved an out-of-state buyer utilizing an all-cash 1031 exchange, with the seller being a repeat client developer for Whataburger.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Notable Features: Property is subject to a 15-year ground lease with 10% rent increases in the initial term and extension options.

Project Notes: The buyer purchased the restaurant in an all-cash 1031 exchange.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Don McMinn; Andrew Koriwchak
