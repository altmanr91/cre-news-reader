Steve Ross buying Boca Raton’s ex-IBM campus, plots mixed-use “micro city”

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Related Ross is nearing a deal to acquire the 1.7 million-square-foot Boca Raton Innovation Campus (BRiC) in Boca Raton, FL, with plans to transform the former IBM campus into a mixed-use "micro city." The acquisition leverages existing 2023 city approvals for a multi-phased development that could include over 1,200 residential units, medical offices, retail space, an entertainment venue, and a potential 140-key hotel. The campus, known for its brutalist architecture and historical significance as the birthplace of the first personal computer, has attracted new tenants due to South Florida's business-friendly environment.
