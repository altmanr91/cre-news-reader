South Florida’s top deals: El-Ad buys Miami condo property for $46M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 27 May 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

El-Ad National Properties acquired Chateau Grove, a 25-unit condominium complex in Miami, for $45.5 million from an LLC managed by Jose Canero of The Canero Group. The 1960s-era property is located at 3265 Virginia Street. This transaction represents the top commercial deal recorded in South Florida during the period.
