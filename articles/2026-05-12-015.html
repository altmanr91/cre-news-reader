Mack moves in on more acreage near chipmaker’s north Phoenix campus

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 11 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Mack Real Estate Group is seeking to develop an additional 1,473 acres of Arizona State Land Department property in north Phoenix, adjacent to its existing Halo Vista master-planned community. The developer plans to entitle and master plan the site, which is envisioned as an expansion of Halo Vista's technology district. This move highlights the intense developer interest in land surrounding Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company's rapidly expanding campus, which has spurred significant speculative land planning in the metro Phoenix area.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Halo Vista: 2,300-acre master-planned project east of TSMC's plant along Interstate 17, with planned technology district, advanced manufacturing, research parks, and mixed-use commercial development.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company's (TSMC) Arizona campus, initially a $12 billion commitment, has expanded to a projected $165 billion investment. This expansion has led to nearly 20,000 acres of land around the campus, much of it formerly state-controlled, now being under development or in entitlement processes.
