Bellforge Arts Center Breaks Ground at Former Medfield Hospital Campus

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:20:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Bellforge Arts Center has commenced a $30 million preservation and renovation project at the former Medfield State Hospital campus, aiming to transform the historic Lee Chapel and Infirmary into a year-round arts and education hub. The initiative is funded through a capital campaign and historic tax credits, with doors anticipated to open in fall 2027. This development is part of a broader redevelopment effort for the former hospital campus, supported by state and local infrastructure investments.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: cultural

Property Name: Bellforge Arts Center (Lee Chapel and Infirmary)

Total Project Cost: $30.0M

Completion: Fall 2027

Notable Features: Preservation and renovation of historic Lee Chapel and Infirmary into a year-round arts and education campus.

Project Notes: Project is part of the larger redevelopment of the Medfield State Hospital campus.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Bellforge Arts Center — Jean Mineo, Executive Director

ARCHITECT: DBVW Architects

FINANCING

Funded through Bellforge's $25 million capital campaign and historic tax credits through the Massachusetts Historic Commission.
