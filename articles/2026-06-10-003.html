France’s Junk Smash Burgers Enters U.S. With New Store in SoHo

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 22:33:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Junk Smash Burgers, a French burger concept, has signed a 10-year lease for its first U.S. location at 452 West Broadway in New York City's SoHo neighborhood. The new 2,150-square-foot retail space is owned by Thor Equities. The deal signifies SoHo's continued appeal to international brands entering the American market.
