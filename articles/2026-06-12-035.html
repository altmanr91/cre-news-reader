Brinks swaps sprawling Farmers Branch headquarters for leaner office setup

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 20:02:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Brinks, a home security company, is relocating its North Texas headquarters to a significantly smaller space in Farmers Branch, TX. The company signed a 10-year lease for 27,000 square feet at the newly opened Park West building, an 80% reduction from its previous 165,000-square-foot facility. This move reflects Brinks' strategy to optimize costs and enhance value per square foot, accommodating hybrid work models for its Dallas-Fort Worth based employees.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Park West

Address: 1501 Lyndon B. Johnson Freeway, Farmers Branch, TX

Size: 27,000 SF

Completion: June 2026

Notable Features: Located on the seventh floor of a new building with amenities including a Starbucks, conference center, and full-service cafe. Offers easy access to DFW International Airport, Downtown Dallas, and the Las Colinas business district.

Project Notes: Brinks' previous headquarters at 1990 Wittington Place, Farmers Branch, TX, was 165,000 square feet and is being demolished. The move was driven by a desire for less space to accommodate hybrid work schedules.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

TENANT: Brinks — Dinesh Kalwani, Senior Vice President and Chief People Officer

LEASING AGENT: Forge Commercial

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Park West buildings offer 260,932 square feet of office space at approximately $22 per square foot per year. There is a trend of companies moving out of downtown Dallas to areas like Farmers Branch for headquarters, as seen with recent departures of AT&T and Fifth Third Bank.
