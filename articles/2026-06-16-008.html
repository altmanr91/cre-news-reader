Marcus & Millichap Brokers $7.1M Sale of Two Long Island Office Buildings

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:14:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the $7.1 million sale of two office buildings totaling 28,714 square feet in Roslyn, NY. The properties, located at 200-220 S. Service Road, were sold by an undisclosed seller to two separate undisclosed buyers. Marcus & Millichap represented the seller and one of the buyers, while Global Realty Services USA procured the buyer for the second building.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 200-220 S. Service Road, Roslyn, NY

Size: 28,714 SF

Sale Price: $7.1M

Site Area: 1.5 acres

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Michael Tuccillo; Anthony Cerrone

BUYER BROKER: Global Realty Services USA
