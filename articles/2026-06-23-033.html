Mott Haven Development Site Set for One or More Residential Buildings

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:40:49 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has arranged the $21.7 million sale of a 172,774-square-foot residential development site in the Mott Haven section of the Bronx. The Altmark Group sold the property to Nalcorp, which intends to develop one or more residential buildings on the site. The transaction highlights the strong investor interest in the area, driven by substantial development rights, excellent connectivity to Manhattan, and robust multifamily market fundamentals, according to JLL.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 122 Bruckner Blvd., New York City, NY

Size: 172,774 SF

Notable Features: Located just south of the Major Deegan Expressway and two blocks east of the Willis Avenue Bridge, offering strong connectivity to Manhattan.
