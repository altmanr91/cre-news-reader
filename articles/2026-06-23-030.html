Pulte moves forward with 147 townhomes near Apple HQ

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

PulteGroup is advancing plans for Parvia, a 147-townhome development in Santa Clara, near Apple's headquarters. The project, which will replace an existing strip mall, includes 90 four-story and 57 three-story townhomes, with 22 units designated as affordable housing. The development also features 5,000 square feet of retail space and a 6,000-square-foot park. Construction is anticipated to begin early next year, with completion by early 2028, pending final city approvals.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Parvia

Address: 3521-3591 Homestead Road, Santa Clara, CA

Units: 147

Site Area: 5.5 acres

Notable Features: Located between Koreatown and Apple Park, the project includes 5,000 square feet of shops and a 6,000-square-foot park.

Project Notes: 22 units are set aside as affordable housing. The project is pending approvals by the Santa Clara Planning Commission and City Council. Pulte is under contract for the retail property and will close the deal after all approvals are secured.
