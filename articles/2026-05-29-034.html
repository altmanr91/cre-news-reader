The die is cast: Tilman Fertitta to buy Caesars Entertainment in $17B-plus deal

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 28 May 2026 16:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Tilman Fertitta's Fertitta Entertainment has agreed to acquire Caesars Entertainment in an all-cash deal valued at approximately $17.6 billion, including the assumption of $12 billion in debt. This acquisition, which includes a "go-shop" period for Caesars to consider other offers until July 11, would expand Fertitta Entertainment's domestic casino resort portfolio to 60 properties. Fertitta has pursued Caesars for nearly a decade, previously proposing a merger with his Golden Nugget casino chain.
