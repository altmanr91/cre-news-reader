Willow Bridge eyes 300 more apartment offerings in CityGate Centre

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Willow Bridge plans to expand its CityGate Centre campus in Naperville, IL, with an additional 297 luxury multifamily units. This development aims to capitalize on the existing complex's low vacancy rate and strong demand in the I-88 business corridor. The new four-story building will feature a mix of one- and two-bedroom apartments, targeting young professionals, and will be integrated into the existing mixed-use campus which includes offices, restaurants, and a hotel. Despite some local opposition regarding density, the project received endorsement from the majority of planning and zoning commissioners.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: CityGate Centre

Address: 2135 City Gate Ln, Naperville, IL*

Units: 297

Current Occupancy: 95.0%

Notable Features: Luxury apartments designed to attract young professionals, integrated into a mixed-use campus with offices, restaurants, and a hotel.

Project Notes: Projected rents for one-bedroom apartments range from $1,900 to $2,400, and two-bedroom apartments from $3,000 to $4,000. The development is intended to rejuvenate the I-88 corridor, diversify housing stock, increase property tax base, and support new jobs.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The I-88 business corridor in Naperville has a 5% vacancy rate for existing complexes. A cancelled data center site in Naperville has a proposal for 260 townhomes and rowhouses by Pulte Homes and Franklin 1960 Lucent Lane LLC. The Solomon Organization recently acquired a 640-unit complex for $136 million in the area.
