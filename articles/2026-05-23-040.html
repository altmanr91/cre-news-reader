Family Office Collective Teams with Delshah, A.M. Properties on Midtown Office Condo

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 22 May 2026 19:38:27 +0000

SUMMARY

REALM, a collective of 130 family offices, has partnered with Delshah Capital and A.M. Properties to acquire the 377,000-square-foot office condominium portion of CitySpire, located at 156 W 56th St. in Midtown Manhattan. The property, which is 98% occupied, was purchased at an 8.5% cap rate. The buyers noted strong fundamentals in premier Midtown assets, with leasing activity and occupancy outperforming the broader cautious capital markets for office properties.

DATA POINTS

Notable Features: Comprises 24 floors within a 70-story tower, features renovated common areas and high-end finishes.

Project Notes: Approximately $22 million in capital improvements have been made to the property, with no near-term capital expenditures required.
