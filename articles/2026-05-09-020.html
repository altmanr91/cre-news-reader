Americold announces $1.3B JV, boosting depressed stock

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 08 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Americold Realty Trust has formed a $1.3 billion joint venture with EQT, which will acquire a 70 percent interest in a portfolio of 12 cold storage facilities. Americold will retain a 30 percent stake and continue to manage the properties. The deal is expected to provide Americold with $1.1 billion in cash to reduce its debt, a move that analysts believe validates the asset value and will accelerate deleveraging. This transaction comes as Americold faces pressure from activist investors due to its declining stock price and high debt load, amidst an industry grappling with oversupply and high energy costs.
