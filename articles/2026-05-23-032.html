Texas Capital Bank moving into Stonelake’s 415 Colorado in Austin

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 22 May 2026 20:06:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Texas Capital Bank has signed a lease for the entire 17th floor and a portion of the 16th floor at 415 Colorado, a 47-story mixed-use tower in downtown Austin. The bank will relocate its San Jacinto Center branch to this new location. The building, owned by Stonelake Capital Partners, is now 50% leased for its office component, with its residential units at 86% occupancy.
