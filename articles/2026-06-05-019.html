Major Philly-area tenant Vanguard exits 88K sf office

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lease

Vanguard, a major investment management firm, is vacating its 88,000-square-foot office space at 45 Liberty Boulevard in Malvern, PA, leaving a significant vacancy in the building. The landlord, FLD Group, which acquired the property in December 2021 for $36.3 million, is actively negotiating with a prospective tenant for 65,000 square feet of the space and is also discussing an early renewal with its second-largest tenant, Microsoft, whose 43,000-square-foot lease expires in February 2028. This departure highlights the ongoing challenges in the Philadelphia-area office market, even as some suburban areas see large lease expansions.
