Another Milpitas hotel joins lineup of recent lodging sales across Bay Area

SALE

San Francisco - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 19:28:34 +0000

SUMMARY

The 194-room Element by Marriott San Jose Milpitas hotel has been sold to an undisclosed buyer, with the new owner set to assume management in late June. Developed by Dynamic City Capital and opened in 2022, the five-story select-service hotel is located at 521 Alder Drive. This transaction is part of a broader trend of hotel sales in the Bay Area, driven by elevated borrowing costs and rising operating expenses impacting valuations.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Bay Area and Silicon Valley hotel markets have seen increasing distress and sales in recent years. New hotel construction has slowed, with 15 hotels totaling 1,610 rooms under construction in 2025, a 34.3% decrease in rooms and 28.6% decline in properties year-over-year compared to 2024's 21 projects with 2,452 rooms. The Sheraton San Jose Hotel in Milpitas sold last year for $20.1 million.
