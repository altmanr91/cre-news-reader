An Evening of Cocktails and Celebration at CO’s 2026 Power Young Professionals Gala

Commercial Observer — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 18:38:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Commercial Observer hosted its 2026 Power Young Professionals Gala, celebrating 75 emerging leaders in the real estate industry. The event, held at 120 Broadway in New York City, recognized professionals across various sectors including brokerage, finance, development, and architecture. Editor-in-Chief Max Gross highlighted the growth of the awards from its initial focus on brokers to its current broader scope, acknowledging the significant contributions of young talent to the industry. Joseph Artusa of Silverstein Properties emphasized the importance of relationships in real estate and the potential of the honorees to shape the future of the industry.

DATA POINTS

Number of honorees: 75
