Larkspur Capital Begins Leasing Townhome Project in Dallas’ Cedars District

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 14:53:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Larkspur Capital has commenced leasing for Phase I of Victoria Row, a 155-unit townhome development in Dallas' Cedars District. The project, designed by Architecture Demarest and built by Acadian Group, offers one-, two-, and three-bedroom floor plans across 29 buildings on an eight-acre site. Amenities include a pool, dog park, and multiple courtyards, with the developer also recently delivering units in Phase II.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Victoria Row

Address: 1100 Parker St, Dallas, TX

Units: 155

Notable Features: Amenities include a pool, dog park, and multiple courtyards.

Project Notes: Phase I consists of 155 residences across 29 buildings. Phase II units were recently delivered.
