Cook County foreclosure map: Loop’s $45M student housing deal tops defaults

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Foreclosure Report

Cook County saw 1,633 foreclosure lawsuits initiated in March and April, totaling $482.7 million in mortgaged value. The largest action was Wells Fargo's pursuit of a $45.3 million loan against affiliates of Versity Investment for The Buckingham, a 27-story student housing tower in Chicago's Loop. This period's foreclosure activity highlights a concentration of distress in the lower end of the market, with the majority of properties valued under $500,000.
