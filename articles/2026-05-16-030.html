Gresham James Hotels purchases Home2 Suites location on the River Walk

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 15 May 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Gresham James Hotels has acquired the 128-key Home2 Suites by Hilton San Antonio Riverwalk, marking a strategic expansion for the Dallas-based firm outside its primary North Texas market. The acquisition was financed with a $16.5 million loan, and the new owner plans to undertake renovations to reposition the hotel for increased corporate and leisure travel. This transaction occurs amidst a challenging period for San Antonio's tourism industry, which has seen declines in hotel revenue and occupancy.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

San Antonio's tourism industry experienced a 7 percent drop in hotel revenue in 2025, with 2.5 million room nights sold in Q4 2025, a decrease of approximately 220,000 room nights year-over-year from 2024. Hotel occupancy also declined by more than 5 percent.
