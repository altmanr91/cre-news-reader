Nonprofit Embrace Boston Plans Redevelopment of Two Downtown Crossing Properties

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 20:21:21 +0000

SUMMARY

Embrace Boston, a nonprofit organization, has acquired two adjacent office buildings totaling 34,300 square feet in Boston's Downtown Crossing for $9.55 million. The organization plans to redevelop the properties into a 35,000-square-foot cultural and civic hub, featuring a theater, exhibition rooms, a podcast studio, and a cafe, with a targeted opening in 2030. Avison Young brokered the sale, representing Embrace Boston in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 33-41 West St., Boston, MA

Size: 34,300 SF

Sale Price: $9.6M

Notable Features: Redevelopment into a cultural and civic hub with a theater, exhibition rooms, podcast studio, cafe, and restaurant.

Project Notes: Targeted opening in 2030.
