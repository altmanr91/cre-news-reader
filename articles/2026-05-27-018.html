Slate Grocery REIT evaluates future after takeover offer

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 26 May 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Slate Grocery REIT is exploring strategic alternatives after receiving an unsolicited takeover bid from private affiliates of its external manager, Slate Asset Management. The Toronto-based REIT's board of trustees has formed a special committee to evaluate options aimed at maximizing unitholder value. The company's portfolio comprises 15.2 million square feet of grocery-anchored retail properties across 23 U.S. states, valued at $2.4 billion, with nearly half located in the Southeast.
