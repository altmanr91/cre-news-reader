NYC’s top deals: Williamsburg commercial properties sell for $50M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Norworth Holdings LLC acquired a portfolio of commercial properties in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, for $50 million. The portfolio includes a nearly 38,000-square-foot commercial condo, a nearly 70,000-square-foot warehouse, and a vacant lot, collectively occupying most of a city block. The sellers were companies managed by Abraham Follman.
