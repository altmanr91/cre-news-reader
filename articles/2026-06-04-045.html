Avison Young Negotiates Relocation for Mercedes-Benz of Beverly Hills

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:32:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Avison Young has brokered a multi-decade lease for a 37,561-square-foot automotive dealership at 8825 Wilshire Blvd. in Beverly Hills, facilitating the relocation of a prominent luxury car brand. This transaction secures one of the last remaining automotive-zoned properties in the area with both sales and full-service bay capabilities, representing a strategic move for the tenant to expand its presence in the core of Beverly Hills.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 8825 Wilshire Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA

Size: 37,561 SF
