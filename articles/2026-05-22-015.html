MAG Capital Partners acquires two temperature-controlled facilities from FreshEdge

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Thu, 21 May 2026 16:39:00 +0000

SUMMARY

MAG Capital Partners has acquired two temperature-controlled industrial facilities from FreshEdge in a sale-leaseback transaction. The portfolio includes FreshEdge's 104,114-square-foot headquarters in Indianapolis and a 28,800-square-foot warehouse also in Indianapolis. This transaction allows FreshEdge to monetize its real estate while continuing operations under a lease agreement.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 4501 Massachusetts Avenue, Indianapolis, IN

Size: 132,914 SF

Site Area: 16.51 acres

Notable Features: The Indianapolis headquarters facility features 23'-34' clear height, 39 dock doors, and one drive-in door, situated along I-70. The second warehouse has 21' clear height and four drive-in doors, located minutes from I-465.

Project Notes: The transaction was structured as a sale-leaseback.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: MAG Capital Partners

SELLER: FreshEdge

SELLER BROKER: Colliers — Todd Heine; Brendan Keely; Daniel LaPierre; Noah Shutan
