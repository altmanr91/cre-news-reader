Weitzman LP Acquires 87,857 SF Shopping Center in Arlington

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 19 May 2026 14:27:09 +0000

SUMMARY

A limited partnership managed by Weitzman has acquired Cooper Street Annex, an 87,857-square-foot shopping center in Arlington, Texas. The property was fully leased at the time of sale, featuring a strong tenant mix. Weitzman's internal team represented the buyer, while SummitRE represented the undisclosed seller in the transaction.

Size: 87,857 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

SELLER BROKER: SummitRE — Hudson Lambert
