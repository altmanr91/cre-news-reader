Shorenstein snags 274K sf office campus in Plano from Spear Street Capital

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Shorenstein Properties has acquired The Tennyson, a fully leased, two-building Class A office campus in Plano, from Spear Street Capital. The property, valued at approximately $70.6 million by the Collin Central Appraisal District, recently underwent significant upgrades to its amenities. The campus benefits from its prime location within Legacy Business Park, offering walkability to major retail and dining destinations and proximity to several corporate headquarters.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: The Tennyson

Address: 6105 Tennyson Parkway, Plano, TX

Size: 273,574 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Year Built: 2012

Notable Features: Class A office campus with recent upgrades to fitness center, lobby, tenant lounge, and conference rooms. Located in Legacy Business Park, walking distance to Legacy West and The Shops at Legacy.

Project Notes: Renovations completed in 2024. Weighted average lease term is more than six years.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Shorenstein Properties

SELLER: Spear Street Capital

SELLER BROKER: Newmark — Chris Murphy, Vice Chairman; Robert Hill, Vice Chairman; Gary Carr, Vice Chairman; Austin Sheahan, Director

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Plano, TX is experiencing a boom in corporate headquarters, with Samsung moving its U.S. corporate headquarters to the city and Las Vegas Sands opening a tech office. The Collin Central Appraisal District valued The Tennyson at approximately $70.6 million.
