South Florida Dirt: Inside the first week of broker George Pino’s trial

The Real Deal — Miami — Sun, 14 Jun 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

This article reports on the ongoing criminal trial of commercial broker George Pino, CEO of State Street Realty, who is facing manslaughter and vessel homicide charges stemming from a 2022 boat crash that resulted in one fatality and multiple injuries. The trial's first week was marked by emotional testimony and legal arguments regarding Pino's recollection of the accident and whether his actions meet the legal definition of recklessness. Prosecutors contend Pino's failure to take basic precautions led to the collision, while the defense argues it was an accident and not a result of recklessness.
