St. Louis’ S.M. Wilson & Co. earns 2026 Quality Concrete Award

REJournals — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 13:24:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

S. M. Wilson & Co. received a 2026 Quality Concrete Award from the Concrete Council of St. Louis & American Concrete Institute (ACI) Missouri Chapter for its work as Construction Manager at Risk on the Northwest R-1 School District's Proposition S Bond Program. The program involved updates across all district campuses, notably including multi-purpose storm shelter additions at several schools constructed with precast concrete panels. These panels were recognized for their strength, sustainability, and durability, allowing the spaces to double as severe weather shelters.
