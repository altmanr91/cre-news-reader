Bancap Self Storage Group Brokers Sale of Two Properties Totaling 315 Units in Corning, California

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 29 May 2026 13:52:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Bancap Self Storage Group has brokered the sale of two self-storage facilities, Airport Mini Storage and Affordable Mini Storage, located in Corning, California. A private family trust sold the fully occupied portfolio, comprising 315 units and nearly 52,000 net rentable square feet, to a local investment group. The transaction price was not disclosed.
