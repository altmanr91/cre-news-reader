Houston’s upcoming Ritz-Carlton Residences selling condos at record pace

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 15 May 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

The Ritz-Carlton Residences in Houston's Uptown district has achieved over $203 million in pre-construction condo sales within four months of its announcement, including a $30 million penthouse that may be the highest-priced condo sale in Texas history. The 45-story tower will feature 112 branded residences and a 156-key hotel, with completion anticipated in fall 2029. The strong sales, primarily from local buyers, highlight the increasing demand for luxury branded condominiums in the Houston market.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

The Woodlands Ritz-Carlton project: Another Ritz-Carlton project being developed by Howard Hughes Holdings in The Woodlands.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Satya's St. Regis condo building at 102 Asbury Street previously held the record for most expensive condo sold in Houston at $14.5 million. The Randall Davis Company is developing a 15-story, 33-unit condo building nicknamed 'Chaucer' at 2360 Rice Boulevard for $65 million.
