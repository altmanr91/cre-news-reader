The Doran Group begins construction 128-unit seniors housing community in Hudson

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Thu, 21 May 2026 10:10:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Doran Group has commenced construction on Linden House – Hudson, a 128-unit seniors housing community in Hudson, Wisconsin. The project, which also secured $26 million in construction financing from Veridian Credit Union, represents the launch of the developer's second senior housing brand, targeting regional hubs and growing smaller cities in the Upper Midwest. The community is designed to offer upscale living with a variety of amenities and larger one- and two-bedroom homes.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: seniors housing

Property Name: Linden House – Hudson

Address: 201 Carmichael Ridge, Hudson, WI

Units: 128

Loan Amount: $26.0M

Loan Per Unit: $203,125/unit (calculated)

Completion: late 2027

Notable Features: Offers larger one- and two-bedroom homes ranging from 690 to 1,380 square feet, private outdoor balconies, and underground heated parking. Amenities include an outdoor central terrace with a pool, clubroom, wellness center, and pet run and spa.

Project Notes: Leasing is expected to begin in late 2026.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: The Doran Group

LENDER: Veridian Credit Union
