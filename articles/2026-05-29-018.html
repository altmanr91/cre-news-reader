Rotem Rosen nabs $135M in Israeli bond financing for NYC hotel, Miami site

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 28 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

MRR Development, led by Rotem Rosen, secured $135 million in bond financing from the Israeli market to refinance its Hotel Indigo property on New York City's Lower East Side and a development site in Miami. This financing, obtained at a 6.2 percent interest rate, replaces a previous $113 million bond issuance from 2022 that carried a lower 4.5 percent rate. The transaction highlights the continued, albeit more cautious, appeal of the Israeli bond market for U.S. real estate developers, particularly those with existing ties to the country.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel / development site

Property Name: Hotel Indigo

Address: 171 Ludlow Street, New York City, NY

Project Notes: The Miami development site was acquired in 2022 and allows for up to 1.9 million SF of mixed-use development. The Hotel Indigo was purchased in 2018.
