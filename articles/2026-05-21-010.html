Piazza Auto Group Breaks Ground on 270-Unit Multifamily Project in Ardmore, Pennsylvania

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 20 May 2026 14:29:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Piazza Auto Group, in partnership with Radnor Property Group, has broken ground on The Plaza at Ardmore, a 270-unit multifamily project in Ardmore, Pennsylvania. The development will also include 30,000 square feet of ground-floor retail space. Financing for the project is being provided by Bank OZK and Affinius Capital, with completion anticipated in early 2028.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Plaza at Ardmore

Size: 30,000 SF

Units: 270

Completion: early 2028

Notable Features: Amenities will include a pool, outdoor grilling and dining stations, a fitness center, and dedicated coworking and private study spaces.
