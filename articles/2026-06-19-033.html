Trademark lassos Whole Foods for Southlake mixed-use development

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 16:34:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Trademark Property Company has officially secured Whole Foods Market as the anchor tenant for its Shivers Farm mixed-use development in Southlake, TX. The 40-acre project, approved by the Southlake City Council, will feature retail, office space, single-family lots, and a hotel or additional retail pad. Construction is slated to begin this summer and conclude by the end of 2027, bringing the first major grocer north of State Highway 114 to the area.
