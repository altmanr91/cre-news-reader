Glenstar, Halloran complete bargain purchase of 46-story Loop office tower for under $100M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Glenstar and Patrick Halloran has acquired the 46-story office tower at 500 West Monroe Street in downtown Chicago for less than $100 million, a significant discount from its previous sale price. The buyers plan to invest in renovations and new amenities to boost leasing momentum, as the building was approximately 75% leased last year and faces a major lease expiration in 2027. The previous owner, Spear Street Capital, surrendered the property via deed-in-lieu of foreclosure after defaulting on a $270 million loan.
