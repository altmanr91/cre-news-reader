Lee & Associates closes 7,635-square-foot industrial lease in Carrollton

LEASE

REJournals — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates Dallas-Fort Worth facilitated a new lease agreement for a 7,635-square-foot industrial property in Carrollton, Texas. The transaction involved a tenant expanding and renewing its commitment at the location, with brokers from Lee & Associates representing the tenant and JSC Realty Investment Services representing the landlord.

Project Notes: This transaction represents a lease expansion and renewal for the tenant.
