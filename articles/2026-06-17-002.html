The Plan: Suburb Meets City at 487 Henry Street in Brooklyn

Commercial Observer — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 10:00:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Eckstrom NYC has completed the renovation of 487 Henry Street, a luxurious 6,300-square-foot townhouse in Brooklyn's Cobble Hill neighborhood. The property, which was originally built in the mid-1800s, now features five bedrooms, six bathrooms, and a range of high-end amenities including multiple outdoor spaces, a sauna, cold plunge, and a workout area. The developer aimed to blend city and suburban living, with the property listed for $14.5 million.
