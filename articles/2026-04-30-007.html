Vertical Construction Begins on $1.2B Hospital Development in North Charleston

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:47:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Vertical construction has commenced on Roper St. Francis Healthcare's new $1.2 billion hospital development in North Charleston, South Carolina. The Barton Malow | Edifice joint venture has begun structural steel work on the 27-acre campus, which will feature an 805,000-square-foot hospital with 328 inpatient beds, a 47-bay emergency department, and 18 operating rooms. The project is designed to significantly expand healthcare services in the region, with completion anticipated in 2029.
