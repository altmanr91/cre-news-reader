Senate Advances Housing Bill After Striking Deal With House Leaders

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 17:13:44 -0400

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

The Senate has advanced the 21st Century Road to Housing Act, a significant federal housing bill that has garnered bipartisan support and is expected to pass both chambers. The legislation, which aims to increase housing supply and improve affordability, represents a compromise after extensive negotiations, notably excluding controversial build-to-rent (BTR) regulations that would have mandated sales to single-family buyers within seven years. While the bill still includes provisions to curb large investors' purchases of single-family homes, some ambiguity remains regarding the resale of BTR properties. Proponents believe the bill, despite being a collection of smaller measures, will be a crucial step towards addressing housing challenges across the U.S.
