Chinese firm tees up partial multifamily conversion for Wacker Drive office high-rise

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 08 May 2026 16:55:55 +0000

SUMMARY

An affiliate of Chinese firm Titan Power Overseas Investment, operating as TP Management, acquired the 23-story office high-rise at 205 West Wacker Drive in Chicago for $15 million through a deed-in-lieu of foreclosure. The property, formerly owned by Alvarez & Marsal, was burdened by $27.5 million in unpaid mortgage debt. Titan Power plans a phased conversion of the upper floors into high-quality boutique apartments, aiming to address the building's 43% occupancy rate and capitalize on Chicago's strong multifamily market.
