Berkshire Hathaway pays $6.8B for homebuilder Taylor Morrison

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Berkshire Hathaway, under its new leadership, has agreed to acquire Arizona-based homebuilder Taylor Morrison for $6.8 billion, or $72.50 per common share, representing a 24 percent premium. This acquisition is significant as it signals a strategic shift for Berkshire Hathaway to unify its homebuilding operations into a combined platform, integrating Taylor Morrison with its existing homebuilder, Clayton Homes. The deal is expected to close in the second half of the year and reflects a long-term investment in the housing market despite recent declines in new residential construction.
