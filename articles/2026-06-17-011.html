Colliers closes sale of 324,800-square-foot industrial facility in Indianapolis

SALE

REJournals — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers has brokered the sale of County Line Commerce Park Building II, a 324,880-square-foot industrial facility in Indianapolis. The property, developed by a joint venture between Citimark and Gershman Partners, was acquired by Alfa Laval, which uses it as its North American manufacturing, testing, and R&D headquarters. This transaction represents the first completed building within the larger County Line Commerce Park, a master-planned industrial development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: County Line Commerce Park Building II

Address: 5820 Meritage Parkway, Indianapolis, IN

Size: 324,880 SF

Year Built: 2025

Site Area: 26.84 acres

Notable Features: Features 36-foot clear heights, 25 dock-high doors, 330 parking spaces, and building systems designed for advanced manufacturing and R&D operations, with immediate access to Interstate 65.

Project Notes: This is the first completed building within the 117-acre, two-phase County Line Commerce Park, which is planned to include four industrial buildings totaling approximately 1.34 million square feet, two retail outlots, and a hotel site.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Citimark

SELLER: Gershman Partners

BUYER: Alfa Laval

SELLER BROKER: Colliers — Alex Cantu; Alex Davenport; Jeff Devine; Steve Disse; Tyler Ziebel

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Peterson Construction
