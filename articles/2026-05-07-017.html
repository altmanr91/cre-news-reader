Boston-area university sells half of its campus for luxury development

SALE

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:31:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Salem State University is selling 23 acres of its south campus to AvalonBay Communities and Winn Development Company for a 340-unit luxury multifamily project. The sale allows the university to consolidate its operations and avoid over $50 million in deferred maintenance costs. This initial phase, known as Avalon Salem, is part of a larger Forest River Residences project that will eventually include 145 additional units.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Avalon Salem

Address: 8 Harrison Road, Salem, MA

Units: 340

Site Area: 23 acres

Notable Features: Luxury housing development with over 500 parking spaces.

Project Notes: This is the first phase of the broader Forest River Residences project, which will include another 145 units. The sale is contingent on the university completing its campus consolidation by fall 2027.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: AvalonBay Communities

BUYER: Winn Development Company

SELLER: Salem State University
