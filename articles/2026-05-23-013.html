Younger Partners negotiates sale of 182 acres in Howe

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 22 May 2026 14:09:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Younger Partners brokered the sale of the 182-acre Hightower tract in Howe, Texas, from the Hightower family to Cherukuru Investments at Howe LLC. The property, located at the northeast corner of FM 902 and Bennett Road, is positioned for a phased mixed-use development with residential and retail components, capitalizing on the rapid growth along the U.S. 75 corridor. The buyer plans to begin with infrastructure improvements, followed by residential and commercial construction over the next five to ten years.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: land

Property Name: Hightower tract

Site Area: 182 acres

Notable Features: Located outside Howe city limits, partially within Sherman and Howe ETJ, less than two miles from U.S. 75.

Project Notes: The land has been owned by the Hightower family for approximately 140 years. Potential plans include a phased mixed-use development with residential and retail components.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Cherukuru Investments at Howe LLC — Ravi Cherukuru

SELLER: Hightower family

SELLER BROKER: Younger Partners — John St. Clair, Executive Managing Director; Ethan Peck, Associate
