MBK Opens Murrieta Rentals Following Strong Demand in Pre-Leasing Phase

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 20:39:16 +0000

SUMMARY

MBK Rental Living has officially opened Vintage Farms, a 330-unit apartment community in Murrieta, CA, following strong pre-leasing demand. The developer emphasized creating a differentiated community with modern amenities and a strong connection to its natural surroundings, integrating hillside views and outdoor gathering spaces into the resident experience. The project also incorporates sustainable features, reflecting the company's commitment to environmentally conscious development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Vintage Farms

Address: 28749 Clinton Keith Rd, Murrieta, CA*

Units: 330

Completion: June 2026

Site Area: 15 acres

Notable Features: Community features EV charging stations, energy-efficient LED lighting and Energy Star appliances, low or no VOC materials, bicycle racks, and a weekly recycling program.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
