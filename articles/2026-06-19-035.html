SF landlord accused of killing tenant to clear Sunset home for sale

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 21:31:46 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A San Francisco landlord is facing a wrongful death lawsuit alleging he murdered his tenant to clear a Sunset District home for sale. The suit claims the landlord engaged in a months-long campaign to force the tenant out, including issuing improper notices, threatening eviction, and refusing rent payments, culminating in the tenant's fatal shooting. The landlord has been arrested and charged with murder, arson, and weapons offenses.
