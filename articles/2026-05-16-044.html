Related Midwest Associate Returns To Competition That Helped Him Break Into CRE

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 15 May 2026 14:47:50 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article highlights the Eisenberg Real Estate Challenge as a crucial platform for young talent to enter the commercial real estate industry, especially in a tightening job market. DuWarren Gibson, an associate at Related Midwest and a former participant, returned as a judge, emphasizing the competition's role in providing real-world development experience and fostering valuable industry connections. The challenge, now in its 16th year, tasks student teams with creating project proposals for actual development sites, such as an 8.1-acre parcel within Related's The 78 megadevelopment in Chicago. The article underscores the increasing importance of relationship-based hiring and networking for recent graduates, particularly as AI impacts the job market and entry-level CRE positions decline.
