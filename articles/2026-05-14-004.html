CRE Industry Awaits Warsh’s Fed Policies After Senate Approval

Commercial Observer — Wed, 13 May 2026 20:40:19 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The commercial real estate industry is closely watching Kevin Warsh, following his Senate confirmation as the new Federal Reserve chair. Warsh's appointment comes as President Trump advocates for lower interest rates, a stance that could influence the Fed's monetary policy. Industry leaders anticipate Warsh will address issues critical to CRE finance, including interest rates, capital availability, and proposed bank capital requirements for CRE loans, aiming to foster a resilient and healthy finance system.
