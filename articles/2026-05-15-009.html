Walker & Dunlop Provides $53.7M Agency Refinancing for Student Housing Community in Daytona Beach

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 14 May 2026 13:48:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Walker & Dunlop has provided a $53.7 million Freddie Mac loan for the refinancing of OnShore, a 636-bed student housing community in Daytona Beach, Florida. The seven-year, floating-rate loan includes a four-year interest-only period. The borrower is a partnership between L3 Campus and Aureon Partners.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Property Name: OnShore

Address: 1100 N Halifax Ave, Daytona Beach, FL*

Units: 210

Beds: 636

Loan Amount: $53.7M

Year Built: 2020

Notable Features: Amenities include a fitness center, study areas, an infinity pool, and a flight simulator. Located near Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University.
