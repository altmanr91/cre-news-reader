Portman to develop Marriott hotel in downtown Cincinnati

LOAN

REJournals — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 09:11:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Portman, in partnership with the City of Cincinnati, Hamilton County, the State of Ohio, Cincinnati Center City Development Corp., The Port of Greater Cincinnati, and Visit Cincy, has secured $540 million in financing for the development of the Cincinnati Downtown Marriott hotel. This full-service hotel will feature 700 rooms, 60,000 square feet of meeting space, and a 17,000-square-foot events terrace, serving as a key anchor for the city's revitalized Convention District. The project is a critical component of Cincinnati's $828 million Convention District Revitalization Plan, which aims to enhance the city's ability to attract national conventions and major events.
