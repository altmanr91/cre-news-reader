Touch of Bay: Former Grateful Dead studio in San Anselmo hits market

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 27 May 2026 20:35:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A historic Marin County home, formerly a recording studio for renowned musicians, has been listed for sale at $4.4 million. The property, which includes two connected church buildings and a separate cottage, has been extensively renovated by the current owners, venture capitalist Doug Penman and his wife Samantha Lyman, since their purchase in 2014. The residence offers five bedrooms and four bathrooms across 4,300 square feet, retaining some of its original aesthetic with vaulted ceilings and exposed steel beams.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The median home price in Marin County is $1.5 million. A 40,000-square-foot compound in Belvedere, known as "The Crest," is currently the priciest listing in Marin County at $50 million.
