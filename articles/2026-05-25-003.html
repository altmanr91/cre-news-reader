The Hammocks: Inside South Florida’s biggest HOA fraud

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 25 May 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

This article details a massive homeowners association fraud scheme at The Hammocks, South Florida's largest HOA. Marglli Gallego, the former president, orchestrated a scheme with her husband and other board members to siphon $11 million from HOA funds, diverting money to themselves and associates. The fraud involved retaliatory tactics against homeowners, invalidating recall petitions, and significantly hiking annual assessments. Gallego and her husband recently pleaded guilty, with Gallego receiving a seven-year prison sentence and a lifetime ban from the Hammocks, while her husband received probation and paid restitution.
