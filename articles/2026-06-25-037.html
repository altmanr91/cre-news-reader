Bascom Group’s $52M Houston apartment refi includes millions for fresh renovations

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Bascom Group secured a $52 million cash-out refinancing for Cantera at Towne Lake, a 366-unit apartment complex in Cypress, a northwest Houston suburb. The loan includes $4.4 million designated for property renovations, which are expected to enable rent increases. This strategy aligns with market predictions for positive rent growth in 2027 as the Houston multifamily market absorbs its current development pipeline.
