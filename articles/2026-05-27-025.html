Inside the latest in separate deadly incidents in Miami waters tied to real estate

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 26 May 2026 23:31:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

Two separate boating incidents in Miami waters, both involving prominent real estate figures and resulting in teenage deaths, are proceeding with different legal outcomes. Commercial broker George Pino, CEO of State Street Realty, faces a felony vessel homicide charge following a 2022 crash that killed a 17-year-old and severely injured another. His defense team is seeking to exclude evidence related to his alleged alcohol consumption and statements made before Miranda rights were read. In a separate 2024 incident, 15-year-old Ella Riley Adler, daughter of Matthew Adler of Adler Real Estate Partners, died after being struck by a boat while wakeboarding. The captains of both the towing yacht and the striking boat have accepted plea deals for misdemeanor charges, with the Adler family approving the outcome.
