Kinective Fitness Club to Open 50,859 SF Gym in Plano

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:35:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Kinective Fitness Club is set to open a new 50,859-square-foot gym in Plano, Texas, with an anticipated opening in early 2027. The facility's design, spearheaded by RDC, will feature seven specialized studios for various fitness disciplines, including metabolic conditioning, HIIT, group fitness, immersive cycling, yoga, meditation, and Pilates reformer.
