Hydrogen Fitness Signs 17,000 SF Retail Lease in Midtown Manhattan

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:11:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Hydrogen Fitness has signed a 15-year retail lease for 17,000 square feet in Midtown Manhattan's Murray Hill submarket. The space, located at 145 E. 32nd St., will span the ground, lower, and mezzanine levels of the building. The new fitness center is expected to open in January.
