Developer Moses Hershko, From California to South Florida: 5 Questions

DEVELOPMENT

Commercial Observer — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:35:22 +0000

SUMMARY

Moses Hershko Development, a luxury homebuilder known for its work in California, is expanding its operations to South Florida with plans for high-end condos and mansions. The firm is developing a 28-unit boutique condominium project in Miami Beach, with units priced between $20 million and $200 million. Additionally, the company is in the design phase for a single-family mansion in Indian Creek, where land costs alone can reach $70 million to $80 million. The firm recently launched MHD Capital and raised $250 million from investors to support its real estate ventures.
