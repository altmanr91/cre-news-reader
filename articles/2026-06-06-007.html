Arvato USA Signs 269,600 SF Industrial Lease in Denton, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 15:08:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Arvato USA, a Germany-based third-party logistics provider, has signed a 269,600-square-foot industrial lease for the entirety of Denton Logistics Center in Denton, Texas. The newly constructed building offers 32-foot clear heights, providing modern specifications for logistics operations.
