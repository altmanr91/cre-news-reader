Ricchi Group puts 15-story Dallas office on auction block with nearly $3M opening bid

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 26 May 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The Ricchi Group is auctioning off the 15-story, 268,000-square-foot office building at 1111 West Mockingbird Lane in Dallas, with an opening bid of $2.75 million. The property, formerly known as Regions Tower, is approximately 50 percent leased and sits on a six-acre parcel with significant development potential. Its strategic location near major highways and key Dallas destinations is highlighted as a notable feature.
