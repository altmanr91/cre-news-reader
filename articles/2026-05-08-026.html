Integra wins approval for Fort Lauderdale beach development — again

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 07 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Integra Solutions secured city approval for the third iteration of its Sereno development in Fort Lauderdale's North Beach. The updated plan for the 14-story mixed-use project increases the number of condos from 76 to 88 and reintroduces 113 hotel rooms, along with 1,800 square feet of commercial space. The project, which previously eliminated hotel rooms due to post-pandemic financing considerations, now features a two-building design with a rooftop pool deck and amenity levels.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Sereno

Address: 2901 East 9th Court, Fort Lauderdale, FL

Size: 1,800 SF

Units: 88

Keys: 113

Original Plan: 12-story, 100 hotel rooms, 54 multifamily units (2022); 14-story, 76 multifamily units (2024)

Notable Features: 14-story east building with rooftop pool deck; west building with four levels of parking and a fifth level of amenities.

Project Notes: Development site previously acquired for $7.2 million in 2021. The project was formerly known as Ocean Park Residences.
