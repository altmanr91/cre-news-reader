Josh Zegen, Adi Chugh talk private credit and writing big checks

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 07 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article features a discussion between Josh Zegen of Madison Realty Capital and Adi Chugh of TYKO Capital, two prominent figures in the private credit real estate sector, at The Real Deal's annual New York City Forum. They discussed the evolution of private credit, with Zegen highlighting Madison Realty Capital's pioneering role over two decades. Chugh, a newer entrant, shared insights from his rapid rise, including significant office loans in Miami and Manhattan. Both leaders offered perspectives on current market trends, including the potential shift of capital from corporate credit to real estate credit, the viability of office-to-residential conversions, and the importance of flexibility in lending.
