Celestica doubles down on Richardson with lease renewal, planned expansion

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 19:57:51 +0000

SUMMARY

Celestica, a data center infrastructure company, has renewed its 10-year lease for its 600,000-square-foot manufacturing facility at 2801 Telecom Parkway in Richardson, TX. The company also plans a significant expansion into an additional 343,000 square feet of office space by next year. This move is supported by a $3 million tenant improvement grant from the City of Richardson, which anticipates the retention of 400 jobs and the creation of 2,300 new permanent jobs, alongside a $300 million capital investment from Celestica.
