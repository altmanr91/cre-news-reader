Brandon Miller’s East Village project lands first office tenants

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 20:34:19 +0000

SUMMARY

Two firms have signed 10-year leases totaling over 16,000 square feet at 1 St. Mark’s Place, a nine-story boutique office project in New York City's East Village. An investment firm leased 9,500 square feet, while a technology-focused investment firm took 6,800 square feet. These deals represent a significant step forward for the 60,000-square-foot property, which faced challenges including the developer's death and pandemic-era market conditions.
