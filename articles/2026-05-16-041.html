Colliers Completes Sale of 321K-SF Kansas City Office Building

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:02:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers has facilitated the sale of 2323 Grand Boulevard, a 321,000-square-foot office building in Kansas City's Crown Center submarket. Stanton Road Capital, LLC sold the 11-story property to Crain Company, a Wichita-based multifamily acquisition and development firm. The transaction highlights the area's strong long-term potential, driven by its prime location, panoramic downtown views, and proximity to significant new developments like the Kansas City Royals stadium.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 2323 Grand Boulevard, Kansas City, MO

Size: 321,000 SF

Year Built: 1985

Notable Features: 11-story building with panoramic downtown views, covered and surface parking, and walkable access to hotels, retail, and dining.

Project Notes: Property was acquired by Stanton Road Capital in 2017.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Stanton Road Capital, LLC

BUYER: Crain Company

SELLER BROKER: Colliers — Evan Warwick, Senior Vice President
