ZOM’s Arizona debut price tag: $323M in legal penalties

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 22 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Maricopa County jury ordered ZOM Living to pay $323 million in damages to the now-defunct Gray Development Group, finding ZOM liable for breaching a joint venture agreement. Gray Development Group accused ZOM of using its proprietary information, market intelligence, and relationships to launch its first multifamily projects in Arizona, effectively freezing Gray out of a planned $1.4 billion development pipeline. The dispute centered on four apartment developments in Phoenix and Scottsdale that began construction last year and are slated for completion next year.
