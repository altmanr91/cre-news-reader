$47M capital improvement plan for NRG Park to make key fixes across property

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 20:28:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

The Harris County Sports & Convention Corporation has approved a $47.14 million capital improvement plan for NRG Park in Houston, TX, addressing 24 priority projects. These improvements include a $12 million roof replacement for NRG Center, $4.5 million for window repairs at NRG Stadium, and $6.81 million for elevator repairs across both buildings. The plan, which also allocates funds for emergency communications and drainage, has been submitted to the Harris County Commissioners Court for funding approval. This initiative follows a $55 million allocation in 2025 for elevator and escalator upgrades at NRG Stadium.
