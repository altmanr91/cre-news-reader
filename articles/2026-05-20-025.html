Development likely for almost 500 acres of Weatherford “horse country”

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 19 May 2026 15:29:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Aledo-based homebuilder Kenmark Homes, operating as NTX Crowder Ranch Development LLC, has acquired a 487-acre estate in Weatherford, TX, from the heirs of John and Helen Crowder. The undeveloped property, previously listed for $11 million, sold above its asking price and is expected to be developed for residential use. The transaction highlights the increasing demand for land in Parker County, a region known for its equestrian community.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: land

Site Area: 487 acres

Notable Features: Largely unimproved ranch with stock tanks, a hay barn, hilly terrain, and mature hardwood coverage.

Project Notes: The land was listed for $11 million and sold above its asking price.
