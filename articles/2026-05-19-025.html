Rockstar Energy billionaire finds new buyer for waterfront North Bay Road home

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 18 May 2026 16:02:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Billionaire Russell Savage has found a new buyer for his waterfront home at 5300 North Bay Road in Miami Beach, which was gut-renovated by Keith Menin. The property, asking $28 million, leads the 22 luxury home contracts signed in Miami-Dade County between May 11 and May 17. This transaction highlights the continued activity in Miami-Dade's luxury residential market, where properties spent an average of 176 days on the market.
