QuikTrip Expanding Chicago Footprint with Three New Locations

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:52:24 +0000

SUMMARY

QuikTrip is expanding its presence in the Chicago metropolitan area with the recent opening of a new convenience store and gasoline station in Minooka, Illinois. The company also has plans to open two additional locations in Bridgeview and South Holland later this year, further solidifying its footprint in the region. These new stores are part of QuikTrip's broader strategy to grow its network of convenience stores and travel centers.
