Under contract: Al Capone’s former Miami Beach property

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:07:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A waterfront lot on Palm Island in Miami Beach, formerly owned by Al Capone and demolished in 2023, is under contract with an asking price of $23.5 million. The seller, an LLC named after the property, had acquired the lot in 2021 for $15.5 million. This transaction is part of a broader trend of luxury home contracts in Miami-Dade County, with 18 properties totaling $146.3 million in asking dollar volume going under contract last week.
