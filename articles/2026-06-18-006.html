April Housing Reopens Three Affordable Housing Communities in East Austin Following $60M Rehabilitation

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 15:13:12 +0000

SUMMARY

April Housing, the affordable housing division of Blackstone Real Estate, has completed a $60 million rehabilitation of three affordable housing communities in East Austin, totaling 654 units. The extensive renovations, undertaken in partnership with the Housing Authority of the City of Austin, included updated unit interiors, building systems, and amenity spaces across all properties. These communities provide housing for seniors and families earning between 30% and 80% of the area median income.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Heritage Point / Eagle's Landing / Village at Collinswood

Units: 654

Total Project Cost: $60.0M

Notable Features: Affordable housing for seniors and families earning 30-80% of AMI.

Project Notes: Rehabilitation project across three existing communities.
