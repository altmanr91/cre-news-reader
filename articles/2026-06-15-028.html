KETTLER, Stillman Trade 276-Unit N. Charleston Rental Asset

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 15:02:21 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between KETTLER and The Stillman Group has sold Waverly Place, a 276-unit multifamily community in North Charleston, SC, to the Charleston Workforce Housing Fund, an impact fund led by Ascent Housing in partnership with Housing Collaborative. The acquisition aims to preserve the affordability of the units. Newmark facilitated both the sale and the acquisition financing for the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Waverly Place

Address: 1900 Waverly Place Lane, North Charleston, SC

Units: 276

Notable Features: Garden-style apartment and townhome community with a clubhouse, fitness center, resort-style swimming pool, outdoor grilling areas, playground, and tennis/racquetball courts.

Project Notes: Acquired by an impact fund with the goal of maintaining affordability.
