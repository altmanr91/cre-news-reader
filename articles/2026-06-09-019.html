TPG leads investor acquisition of retail landlord Echo Realty

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture led by TPG, alongside PSP Investments, La Caisse, and Norges Bank Investment Management, has acquired retail landlord Echo Realty for approximately $2 billion. Echo Realty's portfolio comprises over 230 grocery-anchored retail centers, primarily located in the Midwest and Southeast. The acquisition underscores strong investor confidence in the necessity-based retail sector, with the new ownership planning to expand Echo's business across existing and new markets, including its leasing, property management, and brokerage operations.
