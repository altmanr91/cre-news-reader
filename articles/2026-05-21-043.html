Colliers Closes 208K-SF Industrial Facility Sale in Ohio

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 20 May 2026 21:21:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers has facilitated the sale of Innovation Industrial Park Building 2, a 208,000-square-foot Class A industrial facility in Rossford, Ohio. The property, delivered in 2022 and fully leased, was acquired by Glen Una Management Company from seller Scannell Properties. This transaction highlights sustained investor interest in newly constructed industrial assets with strong tenancy and potential for rent growth, particularly in key Midwest logistics corridors.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Innovation Industrial Park Building 2

Address: 1901 Innovation Avenue, Rossford, OH

Size: 208,000 SF

Year Built: 2022

Notable Features: Class A industrial facility with immediate access to Interstate 75 and Interstate 80/90.
