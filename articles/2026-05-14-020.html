Eli Karp wins reprieve in $15M guarantee fight tied to East Flatbush foreclosure

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:09:39 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Brooklyn developer has secured a temporary reprieve in a legal battle over a $15 million personal guarantee tied to a foreclosed East Flatbush rental property. A judge vacated a prior judgment against the developer, allowing him to contest the deficiency judgment after he argued his former attorney failed to file opposition papers without his knowledge. The core of the dispute involves a significant discrepancy between two appraisals of the property, prepared by the same firm, which the developer alleges is part of a "loan-to-own" scheme by the lender.
