NADG Lands $120M to Build Rental on Farmland in Palm Beach County

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 16:55:05 +0000

SUMMARY

North American Development Group (NADG) has secured a $120 million construction loan from Wells Fargo for NUVO Delray Beach, a 476-unit multifamily development in Palm Beach County, FL. The garden-style project is being built on a 33-acre parcel within the county's Agricultural Reserve, with 12 acres dedicated to outdoor recreation and a bird sanctuary. Construction has commenced and is slated for completion by late 2028.

Notable Features: 12 acres reserved for outdoor recreation, including a bird sanctuary.

Project Notes: The 33-acre site was previously acquired by the developer for just under $42 million four years ago. The development is located within Palm Beach County's 22,000-acre Agricultural Reserve.
