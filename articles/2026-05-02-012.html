Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 64,200-square-foot shopping center in San Antonio

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 01 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap brokered the sale of Rigsby Avenue Shopping Center, a 64,200-square-foot multi-tenant retail center in San Antonio, Texas. The fully leased property, anchored by Dollar Tree, was sold by a private investor to another private investor. The center benefits from a strong tenant mix including retail, service, and medical providers, and is strategically located along a high-traffic corridor.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 2000 SE Loop 410 Access Road, San Antonio, TX

Size: 64,200 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Year Built: 2002

Site Area: 6.09 acres

Notable Features: Anchored by Dollar Tree and located along a high-traffic corridor near Walmart, with on-site parking and monument signage.
