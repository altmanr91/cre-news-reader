Napa Valley estate blows past recent local listings with $100M ask

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 23:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Glendale Ranch, a historic 2,000-acre wine estate in Napa Valley, has been relisted for sale with an asking price of $100 million. The property, which has been owned by the same family for generations, features four homes, 40 acres of established vineyards, nearly 800 additional acres suitable for planting, and a private lake. This nine-figure asking price significantly exceeds other recent luxury wine estate listings in the St. Helena area.
