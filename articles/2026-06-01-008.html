PCCP, Alliance Residential Buy 184-Unit Multifamily Community in Riverside, California

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:26:54 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between PCCP Multifamily and Alliance Residential Co. has acquired The Hawthorne, a 184-unit multifamily community in Riverside, California, for $65.3 million. Completed in 2023, the property offers a mix of one-, two-, and three-bedroom floor plans with modern finishes and a comprehensive amenity package.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Hawthorne

Address: 9170 Indiana Ave, Riverside, CA

Units: 184

Sale Price: $65.3M

$/Buildable Unit: $354,891/buildable unit (calculated)

Year Built: 2023

Site Area: 6.7 acres

Notable Features: Units feature quartz countertops, kitchen islands, stainless steel appliances with gas ranges, in-unit washers/dryers, vinyl plank flooring, and private patios or balconies. Community amenities include a pool and spa with cabanas, landscaped courtyards with barbecues and fire pits, a clubhouse, and a fitness center.
