Barcel USA Signs 24,178 SF Industrial Lease in Irving

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:00:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Barcel USA, a snack manufacturer, has signed a 24,178-square-foot industrial lease for space within Building II of Freeport Business Park in Irving, Texas. The transaction involved representation from CBRE for the tenant and Holt Lunsford Commercial for the landlord, ATCAP Partners.
