$25M Renovation Planned for Historic Jewelers Building in Chicago

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 21:14:46 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture led by Clayco, CRG, The Prime Group, Inc., and TP Management Co. announced a $25 million renovation plan for the historic Jewelers Building in Chicago. The project will introduce new amenities including a fine-dining restaurant, lobby and rooftop bars, expanded fitness and wellness facilities, and reimagined conference and coworking spaces. Construction is slated to begin in fall 2026, with new food and beverage concepts expected to open in 2027, coinciding with strong leasing activity at the property.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Jewelers Building

Address: 35 E. Wacker Drive, Chicago, IL

Total Project Cost: $25.0M

Year Built: 1927

Completion: 2027

Notable Features: Historic landmark building with planned fine-dining restaurant, lobby and rooftop bars, expanded fitness and wellness amenities, and modernized conference and coworking spaces.

Project Notes: Renovation includes modernized elevators, upgraded security systems, and enhanced parking garage.
