South Florida by the numbers: MLS mega-merger metrics

South Florida - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 19:38:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Industry Merger

Miami Realtors and Broward, Palm Beaches and St. Lucie Realtors (RWorld) are merging to form Miami and South Florida Realtors, which will become the largest local Realtor association globally. This consolidation aims to unify the region's real estate professionals and streamline operations across Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, and St. Lucie counties. The combined entity will also create one of the country's largest Multiple Listing Services, reflecting the increasingly integrated nature of the South Florida real estate market.

DATA POINTS

Total members after merger: 93,000

Number of state associations larger than: 47

Total real estate volume sold last year (both associations): $69 billion

Years Teresa King Kinney served as CEO of Miami Realtors: 33
