Will Miami commissioners put Live Local Act in legal crosshairs?

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Miami City Commissioner has sponsored a measure to challenge Florida's Live Local Act, a state law designed to incentivize affordable housing development through tax breaks and zoning exemptions. The commissioner argues that while housing initiatives are welcome, the city must protect residents from development pressures and policies that override local planning and undermine neighborhood character. This move aligns Miami with other localities, such as Hollywood and Hillsborough County, that have initiated legal action against the controversial law, which opponents claim erodes local control.
