Riverside launches retail brokerage division as demand soars

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 08 May 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Austin-based commercial real estate firm Riverside has launched a new retail brokerage division, appointing Travis Robertson to lead the initiative. This strategic move allows Riverside to expand its in-house capabilities and capitalize on Austin's robust retail market, which is experiencing significant demand driven by population growth and low vacancy rates. The firm will continue to collaborate with external brokers for specific projects, while leveraging Robertson's extensive experience in retail leasing.

DATA POINTS

Austin retail vacancy rate (end of 2025): lowest in Texas Triangle

Austin retail vacancy rate (past 4 years): below 3.5%

Austin retail space expected to open (by end of 2026): 1.5 million square feet (projects > 25,000 SF)

Austin retail construction pipeline: over 3 million square feet underway

Austin national ranking for retail construction pipeline: 3rd nationally

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Austin's retail construction pipeline, with over 3 million square feet underway, ranks third nationally, behind Dallas and Houston. The market ended 2025 with the lowest retail vacancy rates in the Texas Triangle, remaining below 3.5 percent for four consecutive years.
