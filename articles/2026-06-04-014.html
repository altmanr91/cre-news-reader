Kraus-Anderson completes $100 million renovation, addition at New Town casino

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Kraus-Anderson has completed a $100 million renovation and addition project at 4 Bears Casino & Lodge in New Town, North Dakota. The extensive project involved demolishing the previous hotel, remodeling the existing casino, and constructing a new seven-story hotel tower that added 90 guest rooms, bringing the total to 264 units. The upgrades also included expanding the gaming area, converting a restaurant into a sports bar, and adding a ballroom, meeting rooms, a fine dining steakhouse, and a new spa.
