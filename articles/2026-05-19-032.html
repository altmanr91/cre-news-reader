Life after IBM: Brandywine tees up $31M in renovations for 66-acre Austin office campus

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 18 May 2026 20:47:45 +0000

SUMMARY

Brandywine Realty Trust is undertaking a $31 million renovation of Building 902 at its 66-acre Uptown ATX campus in North Austin, following IBM's planned departure in mid-2027. The project, which includes interior and exterior upgrades to the 172,000-square-foot office building, is expected to begin by July 2026 and conclude by the end of 2027. This renovation marks a strategic shift for Brandywine, which initially planned to demolish existing structures at the former Broadmoor campus but has pivoted to extensive renovations across three buildings totaling approximately 500,000 square feet, reflecting the softening Austin office market.

The campus was formerly known as Broadmoor office campus.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Austin office market vacancy increased from 22.1% in Q4 2025 to 22.4% in Q1 2026. Overall asking rents dropped from $46.20 per square foot to $45.45 per square foot during the same period.
