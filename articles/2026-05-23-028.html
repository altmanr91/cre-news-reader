Judge orders Chicago MLS to restore Zillow access to listings

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 22 May 2026 20:54:23 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A federal judge has ordered Midwest Real Estate Data (MRED), the Multiple Listing Service operator for northern Illinois, to immediately restore Zillow's access to its Chicagoland listing feeds. This ruling grants a temporary restraining order, reinstating approximately 43,000 listings to Zillow's platform and temporarily halting a dispute with MRED and brokerage Compass. The conflict arose after MRED cut Zillow's data feed, allegedly in response to Zillow's "Listing Access Standards" which restrict privately marketed listings, a practice heavily utilized by Compass.
