JLL Arranges $91.3M Sale of 122,167 SF Retail Center in Southern California

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 15:55:03 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has facilitated the $91.3 million sale of The Beacon, a 122,167-square-foot neighborhood retail center in Carlsbad, California. Asana Partners divested the asset to 11North Partners. The property, originally built in 1982, underwent significant renovations between 2020 and 2023 and is anchored by a luxury fitness brand alongside a diverse mix of other tenants.
