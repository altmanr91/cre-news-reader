LaSalle Acquires Student Housing Complex Near Michigan State University

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 20:59:23 +0000

SUMMARY

LaSalle Investment Management, on behalf of its LaSalle Value Partners IX fund, has acquired Red Cedar Flats, a 190-unit, 436-bed student housing community in East Lansing, Michigan. The property, completed in 2021, is strategically located near Michigan State University. This acquisition marks the fund's initial foray into the student housing sector, with LaSalle noting the attractive fundamentals of campus-adjacent assets at growing universities with limited new development.
