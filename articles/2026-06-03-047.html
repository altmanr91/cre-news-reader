Simpson Thacher Taking Nearly 1M SF On Fifth Avenue: The N.Y. Deal Sheet

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 12:21:37 -0400

SUMMARY

Law firm Simpson, Thacher & Bartlett signed Manhattan's largest office lease in May, taking 916,000 square feet in Extell Development’s planned 29-story office and retail tower at 570 Fifth Avenue. This significant lease is roughly 200,000 square feet larger than initial discussions and represents a major commitment to the under-construction building, which is expected to deliver in 2028. The deal highlights continued demand for prime office space in Midtown, even as new developments come online.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 570 Fifth Ave.

Address: 570 Fifth Ave., New York City, NY

Size: 916,000 SF

Completion: 2028

Notable Features: 29-story office and retail tower under construction.

Project Notes: The building is partially owned by the investing arm of Ikea's parent company, and Ikea plans to open an 80,000 SF store in the building. Extell Development refinanced the project in March with a $417M loan from JPMorgan Chase.
