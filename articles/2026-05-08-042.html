Duke Of Westminster's Firm To Sell U.S. Real Estate Portfolio

SALE

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 07 May 2026 12:11:04 -0400

SUMMARY

Grosvenor Group Holdings Ltd., the British property company owned by the Duke of Westminster, is divesting its directly owned U.S. real estate portfolio, valued at approximately $954 million, over the next three to five years. This strategic shift follows a $147 million loss in North America last year and aims to refocus the firm's U.S. strategy on joint venture investments, while prioritizing direct investments in Europe. The company recently sold a 54,700 SF office building in San Francisco for $32.8 million and secured a significant lease in Washington, D.C.
