John Novak strengthens grip along Chicago River with two more purchases

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 20 May 2026 18:56:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Novak Construction has acquired two additional properties at 1685 North Elston Avenue and 1907 North Mendell Street in Chicago, further expanding its holdings around the former Lincoln Yards megadevelopment site. These purchases follow Novak's earlier acquisition of 18.4 acres of the Lincoln Yards site for $34 million. The firm is strategically consolidating its position along the Chicago River, though specific development plans for these newly acquired satellite properties remain undisclosed.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Lincoln Yards

Sale Price: $6.0M

Project Notes: The acquired properties are satellite parcels near the former Lincoln Yards megadevelopment. Novak previously acquired 18.4 acres of the Lincoln Yards site for $34 million. The original Lincoln Yards project was planned as a $6 billion, 53-acre mixed-use district spanning 14.5 million square feet.
