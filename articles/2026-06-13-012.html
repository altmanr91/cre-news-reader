Talonvest Capital provides $48 million in financing for six self-storage properties.

REFINANCE

REJournals — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Talonvest Capital has arranged $48 million in non-recourse refinancing for a portfolio of six self-storage properties on behalf of BRB Development. The financing, provided by a life company, features a seven-year term with full-term interest-only payments and a flexible prepayment structure. The properties are geographically diverse, located across Florida, New Jersey, Minnesota, Connecticut, and Illinois, and benefit from strong local market dynamics.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: self storage

Units: 5,006

Loan Amount: $48.0M

Notable Features: Portfolio includes two retail units.

Project Notes: The financing included an early rate lock and a 12-month open prepayment window, resulting in over $500,000 in interest savings.
