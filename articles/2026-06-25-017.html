Trump holds bipartisan housing bill hostage

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 16:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

President Donald Trump has unexpectedly canceled a signing ceremony for the bipartisan 21st Century ROAD to Housing Act, reportedly using the housing legislation as leverage to push for the passage of his SAVE America Act, which focuses on election overhauls. Despite earlier White House support for the housing bill, which aims to address affordability by banning institutional investors from purchasing single-family homes, streamlining environmental reviews, and expanding manufactured housing definitions, Trump now deems it of "minor importance." This move creates uncertainty for the housing bill, which had strong bipartisan backing in both the House and Senate.
