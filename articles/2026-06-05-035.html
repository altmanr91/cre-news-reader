Starwood moves ahead with dormant Del Valle master-planned community

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Starwood, through its subsidiary Spur Mirador Land LP, is moving forward with the next phase of the Sun Chase master-planned community in Del Valle, east of Austin. The $15.8 million project will develop a 97-acre subdivision with 263 single-family lots, with construction slated to begin in September 2025 and complete by September 2028. This development is part of Starwood's larger 1,400-acre tract, which it acquired in 2025 from Hines, marking the third developer to own the land originally planned by Qualico Communities.
