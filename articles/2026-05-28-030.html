DRA Advisors snaps up Plainfield apartments for $95M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 27 May 2026 22:15:42 +0000

SUMMARY

DRA Advisors acquired The Enclave at 127th, a 340-unit apartment complex in Plainfield, IL, from JVM Realty for $95 million. The transaction, which saw DRA Advisors assume an existing $64 million loan from Prudential Life Insurance, reflects continued investor confidence in the Chicago multifamily market. The property consists of over a dozen townhome-style buildings, each containing 20 units.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Chicagoland multifamily market is experiencing steady rent growth and has not seen the same value fluctuations as Sun Belt markets. Investors are increasingly drawn to the area for its stability. Nuveen affiliate acquired Uptown La Grange, a 254-unit complex, for $87.8 million in 2025. Continental Properties sold the 292-unit Springs at Weber Road complex in Romeoville for $76 million in March 2026 and Authentix McHenry, a 288-unit complex, for $55.3 million in 2025. A proposed $150 million development in St. Charles for nearly 500 units is facing challenges due to the city's inclusionary housing ordinance requiring 10% affordable units.
