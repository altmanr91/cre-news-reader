PolicyPro: Budget gridlock drags on, Queens landlords push for faster evictions

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 11 May 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Queens landlords are appealing a lower court's decision regarding the pace of eviction proceedings, arguing that housing courts are legally obligated to process cases more quickly. The coalition of 16 property owners, led by Argentine Leasing Limited Partnership, contends that chronic delays due to backlogs and staffing shortages harm both landlords and tenants by increasing arrears. While the appeals court panel acknowledged the dysfunction, they questioned whether the judiciary is the appropriate branch to mandate policy changes related to court resources and staffing.
