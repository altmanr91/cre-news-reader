National developer adds $44M multifamily project to stacked San Antonio pipeline

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 13 May 2026 19:38:09 +0000

SUMMARY

NRP Group is expanding its San Antonio multifamily portfolio with a new $44 million project, NRP 301 Lofts, located at 13388 West Hwy 90. The development will feature 12 three-story apartment buildings totaling 382,486 square feet, along with a clubhouse, pool, and recreational areas. Construction is slated to begin in November 2026 and conclude in July 2028, further solidifying the developer's presence in the Texas market.
