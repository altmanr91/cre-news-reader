Boutique Hotel Planned for Chicago’s Northalsted Neighborhood

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 19:47:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Backbeat Hotel has unveiled the detailed design for its upcoming 50-room independent boutique hotel in Chicago's Northalsted neighborhood. The six-story, 41,000-square-foot property, which will replace the former Yoshi's Café, is slated for demolition later this year with an anticipated opening in 2028. The hotel will feature 12 suites, a rooftop pool and bar with skyline views and a retractable roof, a street-level restaurant, and a below-ground lounge, aiming to serve both guests and the local community.

DATA POINTS
