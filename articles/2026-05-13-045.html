£400M UK REIT Bought At A Discount

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 12 May 2026 05:32:48 -0400

SUMMARY

A consortium led by LondonMetric Property and Schroder Real Estate Investment Trust has reached an agreement in principle to acquire Picton Property Income for approximately £403 million. The transaction, which will be paid in shares, values Picton at a 7% premium to its closing share price but a 24% discount to its last published net asset value. Picton's portfolio, valued at £699 million including debt, is primarily composed of industrial assets (67%), with offices (21%) and retail/leisure (12%) making up the remainder. The deal aims to address challenges faced by smaller listed property companies, offering Picton shareholders a premium and reinvestment into larger, more scaled UK-listed REITs.

DATA POINTS

Sale Price: $403.0M

Notable Features: Picton's portfolio is valued at £699M including debt, with 67% in industrial assets, 21% in offices, and 12% in retail and leisure.

Project Notes: The offer values Picton at 78.2 pence per share, representing a 7% premium on its closing share price and a 24% discount to its last published net asset value. LondonMetric will acquire approximately 46% of Picton’s gross asset value, and Schroder Real Estate Investment Trust will take the remaining 54%.
