H-E-B to close Austin store in move that spotlights company’s evolution into real estate empire

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 22 May 2026 15:06:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Company Strategy

H-E-B is closing its Spicewood Springs Road store in Austin, TX, by July 31 as its lease expires. This closure underscores the company's strategic shift from a traditional grocer to a significant real estate owner and developer, as it now owns the land and buildings for its modern, larger stores. The company is actively expanding its footprint across Texas, particularly in high-growth areas like Dallas-Fort Worth, by acquiring substantial acreage and developing new, larger format stores.
