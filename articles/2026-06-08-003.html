Hey, Retailers, the Soccer Ball’s in Your Court This World Cup

Commercial Observer — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 10:40:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article argues that the FIFA World Cup presents a significant opportunity for retailers, restaurateurs, and U.S. downtowns to attract foot traffic and new customers. The author emphasizes that this is not a passive opportunity, but requires intentional strategies focused on fan behavior before and after games, such as extending hours, offering specials, and implementing outdoor activations. Partnerships between businesses and downtown organizations, like those seen in New York City, are highlighted as crucial for maximizing economic impact by spreading visitor movement beyond single anchor destinations. The piece also suggests tactics like country-themed menus, viewing parties, limited-time merchandise, and pop-up shops, while stressing the importance of data analysis for staffing and security.
