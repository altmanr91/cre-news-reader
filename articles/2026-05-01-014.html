Academy Sports + Outdoors to occupy former Macy’s space at Fairview Town Center

LEASE

REJournals — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:09:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Academy Sports + Outdoors has signed a lease to occupy the former Macy's space at Fairview Town Center in Fairview, Texas. This significant backfill of a large-format retail space is expected to open in Spring/Summer 2026, reinforcing the center's position as a strong retail destination in the North Texas market. The deal highlights retailer confidence in the community and the collaborative efforts to attract a nationally recognized brand.
