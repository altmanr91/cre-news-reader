Italian luxury brand Cipriani, growing in South Florida, was in Epstein’s network

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 11 May 2026 23:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

This article details the historical connections between the luxury Italian brand Cipriani and convicted sex trafficker Jeffrey Epstein, as revealed in Department of Justice records. The records show Epstein's presence at Cipriani venues, his association with individuals in Cipriani's social circles, and discussions for a potential business venture in London involving Giuseppe Cipriani Jr. and Livio Bisterzo. While a Cipriani spokesperson stated no deal materialized, emails suggest a friendly relationship between Epstein and Cipriani Jr. The article also notes Cipriani's significant expansion in South Florida, including a hotel and condo project in Miami Beach and several Mr. C branded developments across the region.
