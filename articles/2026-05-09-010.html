TKF Burnside Buys 400,000 SF Office Building in White Plains, New York

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 08 May 2026 15:04:47 +0000

SUMMARY

TKF Burnside Real Estate Partners has acquired a 400,000-square-foot office building at 360 Hamilton Avenue in White Plains, NY. The property, located in the downtown area, houses a strong tenant mix. The new owner plans to undertake capital improvements to the asset.
