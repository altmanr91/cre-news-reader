Ambrose completes 190,000 square feet of industrial leases in Cincinnati, Denver and Orlando

LEASE

REJournals — Tue, 12 May 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Ambrose has completed three industrial leases totaling over 190,000 square feet across facilities in Cincinnati, Denver, and Orlando. In Denver, a catering logistics firm leased 16,640 square feet at Ascent Commerce Center. In Cincinnati, a manufacturing firm secured 69,659 square feet at Cincinnati Logistics Park – Airport West. Additionally, a technology subsidiary of a Fortune 500 company fully leased a 103,878-square-foot building at Orlando Logistics Park at LeeVista. These transactions highlight strong demand for modern industrial space in key logistics hubs.
