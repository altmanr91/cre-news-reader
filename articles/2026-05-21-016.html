Scion Group, Ares buy Harrison Street’s $910M student housing portfolio

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 20 May 2026 20:49:54 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Scion Group and Ares Management has acquired a 12-property student housing portfolio from Harrison Street Asset Management for $910 million. The portfolio comprises 7,578 beds across a dozen college towns, with properties located near major universities such as Auburn University, Arizona State University, Ohio State University, and Washington State University. This acquisition represents a significant investment in the student housing sector, which is experiencing steady demand with rising college enrollment and strong pre-leasing activity, despite a deceleration in rent growth.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Beds: 7,578

Sale Price: $910.0M

Project Notes: The portfolio traded for approximately $120,000 per bed.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Scion Group

BUYER: Ares Management

SELLER: Harrison Street Asset Management

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

College enrollment increased by 1.8% nationally in Fall 2025. Pre-leasing rates for student housing are outpacing last year's figures. Average student housing rents reached $925 per bed in February, representing a 0.4% year-over-year increase, a deceleration from the 3.5% growth observed in February 2025.
