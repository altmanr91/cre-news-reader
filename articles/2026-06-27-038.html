Holland buys San Jose apartments for $1M over 2018 price

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 23:18:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Holland Partner Group has acquired the 218-unit Meridian at Midtown apartment complex in San Jose, CA, for $105.3 million from Essex Property Trust. The transaction, which equates to over $483,000 per unit, reflects a modest appreciation of 1.2% since its last sale in 2018, indicating a relatively flat market for rental housing in the South Bay. Despite recent market challenges, analysts predict an uptick in the South Bay apartment market, with average rents expected to rise by 4.4% this year.
