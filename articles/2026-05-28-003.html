Bridge City Capital Provides $21M Loan for Greenpoint Dev Site

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Wed, 27 May 2026 21:15:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Bridge City Capital has provided a $21 million acquisition and construction loan to Rogers Builders & Development for the purchase of a parking lot in Greenpoint, Brooklyn. The developer plans to construct a 99-unit mixed-use multifamily project on the site, taking advantage of the 485x tax abatement program. The financing package, with a 12-month term and extension options, represents an 80 percent loan-to-value ratio, reflecting a growing trend in construction financing where developers face challenges in securing equity.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 72 West Street, Brooklyn, NY

Units: 99

Loan Amount: $21.0M

Loan Per Unit: $212,121/unit (calculated)

Notable Features: Site includes an obsolete water tower and offers 54,375 buildable square feet as-of-right, or 70,687 square feet with Universal Affordability Preference program.

Project Notes: The 74-foot by 200-foot parcel was recently subdivided by the seller. The planned 99-unit project is designed to skirt the 485x tax abatement program's wage requirement for new construction.
