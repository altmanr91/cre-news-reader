NRP Group Breaks Ground on 297-Unit Affordable Housing Project in Denton, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 04 May 2026 15:06:07 +0000

SUMMARY

The NRP Group has commenced construction on Arbor Ranch, a 297-unit affordable housing development in Denton, Texas. The project, situated on a 22-acre site, will feature nine three-story buildings offering one- to four-bedroom units for households earning 30 to 70 percent of the area median income. Truist Bank provided a $68 million construction loan, and the first units are anticipated to be ready for occupancy early next year.

Notable Features: Amenities include a pool, playground, barbecue and picnic areas, children’s activity room, and a community lounge, with onsite social services available.

Project Notes: Units are reserved for households earning between 30 and 70 percent of the area median income.
