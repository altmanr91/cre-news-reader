Sprawling Arthur Andersen relic hits market in suburban Chicago

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 01 May 2026 22:45:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The Q Center, a former corporate training campus and hotel in St. Charles, IL, has been listed for sale by JLL. The sprawling 15-building property, a relic of the Arthur Andersen era, is being marketed as both a hospitality asset and a redevelopment opportunity, with potential for residential or other uses. The offering comes as the corporate retreat model faces challenges due to remote work trends and tightened travel budgets.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The corporate retreat model is facing a national reckoning, with major players like General Electric and Boeing offloading similar legacy training centers in 2024 in New York and Missouri, respectively. The former Pheasant Run Resort, a few miles from the Q Center, is also undergoing redevelopment, with Vequity proposing a retail village and a new hotel.
