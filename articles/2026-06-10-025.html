“Parents’ worst nightmare”: First boat crash witnesses take the stand in broker George Pino’s trial

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 21:19:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

The trial of commercial real estate broker George Pino is underway, focusing on a 2022 boat crash that resulted in the death of a 17-year-old and injuries to ten others. Pino, CEO of State Street Realty, faces charges of manslaughter and vessel homicide. Witnesses, including the victim's father, testified about the events leading up to and immediately following the collision, which occurred during a Labor Day Weekend outing in the Florida Keys. The defense is arguing that Pino's account of a 'phantom boat' was a result of a concussion sustained in the crash, and that any distraction does not equate to legal recklessness.
