Houston-based Hines scoops up Chicago shopping center for $70M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 18 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hines Global Income Trust has acquired the Wicker Park Commons shopping center in Chicago from DWS Group for $70 million. The 218,000-square-foot retail property, anchored by a grocery store and a home improvement retailer, was purchased at a significant discount from its 2015 sale price. This acquisition marks Hines' second-largest in Chicago in recent months, reflecting their continued investment in the market.
