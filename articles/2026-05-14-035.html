Jeff Bezos’ Blue Origin eyes Austin suburb of Hutto for $650M industrial megaproject

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:00:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Blue Origin, Jeff Bezos' aerospace company, is reportedly planning a massive industrial campus in Hutto, Texas, an Austin suburb. The project, referred to as "Project Blue Hub," is expected to encompass 1.3 million square feet for manufacturing, R&D, warehousing, and logistics. This development could bring over 2,000 jobs to Williamson County, with an estimated capital investment exceeding $650 million, potentially reaching $1 billion. The Hutto Economic Development Corporation has discussed potential financial incentives for the project, which would further establish Central Texas as an aerospace hub.
