Blackstone sells nine Broward County warehouses for $99.6 million

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 20:05:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Blackstone, through its subsidiary Link Logistics, sold a nine-building industrial portfolio totaling 419,300 square feet in Broward County, Florida, to Dalfen Industrial for $99.6 million. The portfolio, which was 83% leased at the time of sale, includes properties across Fort Lauderdale and Weston. This transaction is part of Blackstone's broader strategy of industrial dispositions, while Dalfen Industrial continues to expand its last-mile logistics holdings in the Southeast.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Broward County's industrial market saw net absorption fall to 1.1 million square feet over the past year, with vacancy rising to 6.8% in Q1. Despite this, sales activity remains above long-term historical averages.
