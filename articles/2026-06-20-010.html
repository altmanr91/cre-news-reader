Bronx STEAM Sets Up Shop at Simone’s Hutchinson Metro Center Atrium

LEASE

Connect CRE — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 20:50:27 +0000

SUMMARY

The New York City School Construction Authority has signed a long-term lease for 54,245 square feet at the Hutchinson Metro Center Atrium in the Bronx to establish the new Bronx STEAM Center. This initiative represents a significant public-private partnership, with Bronx STEAM and Montefiore Medical Center collaborating to offer career-connected education in high-demand healthcare and technology fields. The project is expected to create long-term opportunities for students and the borough.
