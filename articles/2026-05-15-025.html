Kimco hit with damages suit for allegedly “destroying” Fort Lauderdale café business

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 14 May 2026 22:02:49 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Kimco Realty is facing a $9 million lawsuit from Sheer Hospitality, alleging that extensive construction and demolition work at its Cypress Creek Station shopping plaza in Fort Lauderdale effectively destroyed the tenant's Drip Coffee business. The lawsuit claims Kimco began gutting the plaza days after Drip Coffee opened, and later expanded work into the café's unit, forcing its closure. Sheer Hospitality alleges it was not adequately informed of the scope of the construction, which includes demolishing an LA Fitness and movie theater to make way for a Target, and was not offered alternative space despite continuing to pay rent.
