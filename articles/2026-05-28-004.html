Barnes & Noble Inks 11K-SF Retail Lease for New East Village Store

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 27 May 2026 20:45:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Barnes & Noble has signed an 11,300-square-foot retail lease for a new store in Manhattan's East Village, located at the base of the Steiner East Village condominium building. The new store, which is expected to open in November, marks a significant expansion for the bookstore chain, which plans to open 60 new locations across the U.S. in 2026.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Steiner East Village

Address: 181 Avenue A, New York City, NY

Size: 11,300 SF

Completion: November 2026

Notable Features: Located at the base of an 82-unit condominium building with amenities including a residents lounge, fitness center, swimming pool, and landscaped rooftop terrace.
