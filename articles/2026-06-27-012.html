HDA celebrates renovation of St. Louis Jewish Community Center building in Chesterfield

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

HDA has completed a comprehensive renovation of the St. Louis Jewish Community Center's Marilyn Fox Building in Chesterfield, Missouri. The 87,930-square-foot project modernized the community hub with expanded amenities, enhanced accessibility, and new spaces including an Adult Day Center. The renovation, which began in April 2024, was phased to allow continuous member access and preserve the building's community spirit.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: community center

Property Name: Marilyn Fox Building

Address: 16801 Baxter Rd, Chesterfield, MO*

Size: 87,930 SF

Year Built: 1996

Completion: June 2026

Notable Features: Includes a new Adult Day Center with capacity for 36 participants, new locker rooms, redesigned lobby, upgraded infrastructure, renovated gymnasium, fitness center, Early Childhood Center, expanded kitchen facilities, and dedicated pickleball courts.

Project Notes: Originally constructed in 1996 with a minor renovation in 2008. Construction began in April 2024 and was phased to allow continuous member access.
