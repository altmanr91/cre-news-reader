Rancho Cucamonga Neighborhood Retail Sells for $6M Off-Market

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 19:48:04 +0000

SUMMARY

An Orange County-based private investor has acquired Allmark Plaza, a 16,926-square-foot multi-tenant neighborhood retail center in Rancho Cucamonga, for $6 million in an off-market transaction. The buyer was drawn to the property's strong historical occupancy, diverse tenant mix, and strategic location with convenient freeway access. The acquisition was viewed as a compelling opportunity to secure a fully occupied retail asset with durable long-term fundamentals.
