CBRE closes sale of 39,627-square-foot medical outpatient building in Hinsdale

SALE

REJournals — Tue, 12 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CBRE facilitated the sale of the 11 Salt Creek Medical Building, a 39,627-square-foot medical outpatient facility in Hinsdale, Illinois. The property, fully leased to UChicago Medicine AdventHealth, was sold by MedProperties Group. The health system recently expanded its presence in the building, underscoring its commitment to the location and its outpatient delivery model.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Property Name: 11 Salt Creek Medical Building

Address: 11 Salt Creek Lane, Hinsdale, IL

Size: 39,627 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Notable Features: Recently renovated medical outpatient building, 100% leased on a long-term triple-net basis to an investment-grade health system, located less than one mile from a 261-bed hospital.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Hinsdale medical outpatient market boasts a 91% occupancy rate across 23 outpatient medical buildings, indicating limited availability of competing properties.
