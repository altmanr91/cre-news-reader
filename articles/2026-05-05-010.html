JLL Arranges $109.5M Loan for Refinancing of Apartment Building in Edgewater, New Jersey

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 04 May 2026 14:36:31 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has arranged a $109.5 million bridge loan for the refinancing of Edgewater Riello, a 226-unit apartment building in Edgewater, New Jersey. The three-year, floating-rate loan was provided by MF1 Capital to the sponsors, Skylight Real Estate Partners and PCCP. The property recently underwent renovations and offers a variety of unit sizes and amenities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Edgewater Riello

Address: 99 Gorge Rd, Edgewater, NJ*

Units: 226

Loan Amount: $109.5M

Loan Per Unit: $484,513/unit (calculated)

Notable Features: Newly renovated building with one-, two-, three- and four-bedroom units, penthouse suites, fitness center, sauna, golf simulator, resident party room, and outdoor lounge.
