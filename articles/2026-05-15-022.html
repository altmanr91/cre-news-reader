PBC inks 120K SF lease at 10 Bryant with Baker McKenzie

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:05:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Property & Building Corp. (PBC) has secured a significant 122,000-square-foot lease expansion with a prominent law firm at 10 Bryant in New York City. This transaction marks a major step in the building's recovery following the departure of its former anchor tenant in 2024, bringing the 30-story tower to full occupancy. The law firm will pay approximately $150 per square foot, a rate substantially higher than the Midtown Class A office average, reflecting the strong demand for amenity-rich office space in the tightening Manhattan market.
