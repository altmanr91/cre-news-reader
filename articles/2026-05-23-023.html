Seller tied to investor Randall Smith sells Palm Beach mansion for $93M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 22 May 2026 18:39:45 +0000

SUMMARY

A company linked to Randall Smith of Alden Global Capital sold a newly constructed, 13,000-square-foot mansion on Everglades Island in Palm Beach for $93.3 million. The 1-acre waterfront property was purchased by a trust managed by attorney Mark Meland, who secured a $70 million mortgage from Bank of America. This transaction represents the second-priciest lakefront home sale in Palm Beach without ocean frontage.
