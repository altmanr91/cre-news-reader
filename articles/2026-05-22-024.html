Growing law firm leases keep Manhattan’s office market humming

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 21 May 2026 20:11:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Manhattan's office market continues to be significantly influenced by law firm activity, with two major firms recently expanding their footprints. Willkie Farr & Gallagher added 53,000 square feet at 787 Seventh Avenue, increasing its total occupancy to 368,000 square feet. Concurrently, Kirkland & Ellis expanded its space at 900 Third Avenue by 52,000 square feet, bringing its total to over 309,000 square feet. These expansions highlight a trend of law firms renewing and growing their presence in the market, as evidenced by a recent Savills report indicating that 70% of first-quarter law firm leasing activity in Manhattan involved renewals or expansions.
