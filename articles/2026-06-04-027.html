Billionaire Adam Neumann supersizes Flow high-rise in Aventura

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 18:35:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Adam Neumann and his partner, Canada Global, received unanimous approval from Aventura city officials to expand their planned Flow-branded multifamily development at Aventura Corporate Center. The revised plans for the 8.6-acre site now call for 720 units across two 32-story towers, an increase from the previously approved 675 units in three 29-story buildings. The developers also committed $5 million to a city charter school and will provide community vehicles and bicycles in exchange for the conditional use approval.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Flow

Address: 20801, 20803 and 20807 Biscayne Boulevard, Aventura, FL

Units: 720

Site Area: 8.6 acres

Notable Features: Two 32-story towers rising 367 feet; 20 units designated as 'hero housing' for city employees, first responders, and teachers; short-term rentals not allowed.

Project Notes: Developers pledged $5 million toward a city-run charter school, along with 25 community vehicles and 50 bicycles for residents. The 8.6-acre site was previously acquired for $116.2 million in 2024. The existing 525,000 SF office complex will be retained, with one parking garage replaced and another expanded.
