Don’t paint yourself into a corner: How to take a smarter approach to healthcare facility design

REJournals — Mon, 18 May 2026 09:59:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article argues that healthcare facility design must prioritize flexibility and foresight to accommodate future changes in patient volumes, care delivery, and technology. The author emphasizes the importance of comprehensive site master planning to evaluate maximum building footprints, parking ratios, and infrastructure capacity upfront, rather than addressing these issues reactively. By making strategic, cost-effective investments in expandable systems and thoughtful building layouts, healthcare organizations can avoid costly retrofits and ensure seamless growth without disrupting patient experience.
