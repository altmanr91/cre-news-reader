NAI Robert Lynn helps bring Mediterranean restaurant to Fort Worth

LEASE

REJournals — Thu, 14 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Avé Mediterranean, an upscale dining concept, has secured a 6,000-square-foot lease at Montgomery Plaza in Fort Worth, Texas. The new restaurant will offer lunch, dinner, and weekend brunch, aiming to blend refined dining with a vibrant atmosphere in the city's rapidly growing Cultural District. The location was chosen for its proximity to major cultural attractions, which is expected to draw a broad clientele.
