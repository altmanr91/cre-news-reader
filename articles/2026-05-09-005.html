Howard Hughes Holdings’ Q1 Earnings Finds a Growing Company in Transition

Commercial Observer — Fri, 08 May 2026 17:19:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Howard Hughes Holdings (HHH) reported solid first-quarter 2026 earnings, with net operating income from total operating assets increasing to $73.1 million, a 2% rise year-over-year. The company's master-planned communities (MPCs) saw a 33% increase in revenue, primarily driven by strong land sales at its Bridgeland community in Houston, TX. This performance comes as HHH transitions into a diversified holding company, a strategy underscored by its pending $2.1 billion acquisition of Vantage Group Holdings, an insurance and reinsurance firm, expected to close in Q2 2026.
