TruCore Industrial Acquires 72,710 SF Industrial Portfolio in Houston, San Antonio

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 15 May 2026 14:23:22 +0000

SUMMARY

TruCore Industrial has acquired a two-property industrial portfolio totaling 72,710 square feet, with assets located in Houston and San Antonio. Both properties were fully leased to a power distribution and control equipment manufacturer at the time of sale. The transaction details, including the seller and sale price, were not disclosed.
