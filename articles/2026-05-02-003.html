Jump Trading Inks 99K SF at Related Companies’ 50 Hudson Yards

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:40:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Jump Trading, an algorithmic and high-frequency trading firm, has signed a lease for 99,305 square feet at 50 Hudson Yards, a prominent office tower owned by Related Companies in Manhattan. This new location will double Jump Trading's New York City office footprint and headcount, marking its 13th global office. The building, which opened in 2022, is one of New York City's largest commercial office towers and has attracted several high-profile tenants.
