Health Platform Tennr Takes 125K SF at Hudson Square Properties’ 345 Hudson Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:13:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Health care technology platform Tennr has signed a 124,733-square-foot office lease at 345 Hudson Street, a property owned by Hudson Square Properties. This transaction represents one of the largest office leases in Manhattan for April, indicating continued activity in the Lower Manhattan market.
