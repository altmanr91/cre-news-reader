Kaufman Under Contract to Buy Savanna’s Madison Square Park Properties for $125M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 20:34:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Kaufman Investments is under contract to acquire the Madison Square Collection, comprising 24-28 West 25th Street and 48 West 25th Street, from Savanna for approximately $125 million. The transaction, which includes two office properties in Manhattan's Flatiron District, is expected to close within the next couple of weeks. Rialto Capital is providing the acquisition financing for Kaufman Investments.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Madison Square Collection

Address: 24-28 West 25th Street and 48 West 25th Street, New York City, NY

Sale Price: $125.0M

Notable Features: Located just west of Madison Square Park in Manhattan's Flatiron District.

Project Notes: Savanna previously acquired 48 West 25th Street in 2018 for $90 million and 24 and 28 West 25th Street in 2019 for $107.35 million.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Savanna — Chris Schlank; Nick Bienstock
