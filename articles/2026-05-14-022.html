The Daily Dirt: Get Steve Roth some media training

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 13 May 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Editorial

This article critiques the public communication strategies of Vornado's Steven Roth and Mayor Zohran Mamdani regarding New York City's fiscal policies and real estate development. The author argues that while development is a politically easier way to increase city revenue by attracting urban professionals, both Roth and Mamdani made missteps in their public statements. Roth's comparison of "tax the rich" to racial slurs overshadowed his positive remarks about the mayor, while Mamdani's pointed video targeting a wealthy individual was deemed reckless, especially in light of recent safety concerns among the city's affluent. The piece concludes that effective, non-reckless public messaging is crucial for both sides to achieve their goals and foster a mutually beneficial environment for the city's growth and real estate market.
