Fidelis wins piece of Hutto’s long-stalled Cottonwood project amid city’s ongoing lawsuit with Midway

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 11 May 2026 20:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Fidelis Realty Partners has entered into a purchase-and-sale agreement with the Hutto Economic Development Corporation for approximately 58 acres of the 250-acre Cottonwood property in Hutto, TX. Fidelis plans to develop the site into "Cottonwood Marketplace," a retail-focused project featuring shopping, dining, and entertainment. This agreement represents a significant step forward for the long-stalled Cottonwood tract, which has been subject to previous development challenges and ongoing litigation with a former master developer.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Cottonwood Marketplace

Site Area: 58 acres

Notable Features: Retail-focused project with shopping, dining, and entertainment components.

Project Notes: The 58-acre parcel is part of a larger 250-acre Cottonwood property. The city is currently in litigation with former master developer Midway over the broader site.
