Ansin starts Miramar megaproject as Sunbeam pushes deeper into real estate

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 28 May 2026 21:29:51 +0000

SUMMARY

Sunbeam Properties, in partnership with Stiles, has commenced construction on Miramar Cove, a significant mixed-use megadevelopment in Miramar, FL. The 125-acre project will feature nearly 3,000 residential units, substantial retail and office space, and a 185-key hotel. This development marks a deeper push into real estate for the Ansin family's Sunbeam, building on their long-standing presence in the region.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Miramar Cove

Address: 11125 Miramar Pkwy, Miramar, FL*

Size: 525,000 SF

Units: 2,874

Keys: 185

Completion: Q4 2028

Site Area: 125 acres

Notable Features: Project centerpiece is a 5.5-acre water basin, with 5 miles of walking paths and over 30 acres of open park space.

Project Notes: The 125-acre site was purchased approximately 70 years ago for about $100 per acre. Sunbeam proposed the project in 2024 and secured city and commission approvals.
