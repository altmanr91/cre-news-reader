BH Group closes on 151-acre golf course buy after settling litigation with Straub

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 27 May 2026 21:20:51 +0000

SUMMARY

BH Group has finalized the acquisition of the 150.5-acre Polo West Golf Course in Wellington, Palm Beach County, for $20.8 million, following the resolution of litigation with seller Glenn Straub. The Aventura-based firm plans to redevelop the shuttered golf course into a luxury single-family home community. The property, which previously hosted PGA Tour events, had been idle and subject to code citations due to Straub's unsuccessful redevelopment efforts.
