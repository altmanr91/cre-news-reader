Houston Rodeo partially breaks from NRG Park with $300M livestock campus

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 20:34:02 +0000

SUMMARY

The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo is developing a new 1 million-square-foot livestock complex in Houston, TX, valued at $300 million. This facility, located at Highway 288 and Airport Boulevard, will host various livestock and agricultural events, complementing the main rodeo and concert events that will continue at NRG Park. The expansion is part of the Rodeo's long-term vision, offering more flexible planning and year-round event capabilities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: special purpose

Size: 1,000,000 SF

Total Project Cost: $300.0M

$/SF: $300/SF (calculated)

Notable Features: The complex will include two barns totaling 860,000 square feet, a 140,000-square-foot central barn, an outdoor cattle yard, and a combination auction hall and sales convention center.

Project Notes: This project was initially discussed and mapped out in 2019 but was delayed due to the pandemic. Some operations will remain at NRG Park, and the Rodeo is working on a new lease with the Houston Texans and Harris County.
