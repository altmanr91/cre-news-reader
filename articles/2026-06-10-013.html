Fairstead to rehab 80-unit affordable housing community in Lexington

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Fairstead is set to begin a comprehensive rehabilitation of Malabu Manor, an 80-unit affordable housing community in Lexington, KY, catering to seniors and residents with disabilities. The project, which includes extensive in-unit, exterior, and property-wide upgrades, is financed through 9% Low-Income Housing Tax Credits from the Kentucky Housing Corporation and a $750,000 Green and Resilient Retrofit Program grant from HUD. These improvements will enhance energy efficiency, climate resiliency, and resident experience, with renovations expected to conclude by December 2026.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Malabu Manor

Address: 137 Malabu Drive, Lexington, KY

Units: 80

Completion: December 2026

Notable Features: Affordable housing for seniors and residents with disabilities, all units reserved for households earning up to 60% AMI. Includes backup generators, energy-efficient appliances, business center, library, outdoor gazebo, dog park, and free Wi-Fi/Cable TV.

Project Notes: Fairstead acquired Malabu Manor in 2022 as part of a long-term strategy to preserve housing affordability. The project is supported by a $750,000 Green and Resilient Retrofit Program (GRRP) Elements grant from HUD for energy and climate resiliency improvements.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Fairstead

LENDER: Berkadia

EQUITY/FINANCING: Kentucky Housing Corporation

EQUITY/FINANCING: U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD)

EQUITY/FINANCING: PNC Bank

FINANCING

Financed through 9% Low-Income Housing Tax Credits (LIHTCs) and a $750,000 Green and Resilient Retrofit Program (GRRP) Elements grant.
