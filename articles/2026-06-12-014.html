RMK Management Corp. announces renovations at Naperville rental community

REJournals — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

RMK Management Corp. has announced a renovation initiative for its 348-unit Bristol Station rental community in Naperville, IL. The upgrades, handled primarily by the in-house maintenance team, focus on modernizing kitchens, bathrooms, paint, and flooring. The rolling renovation schedule aims to complete three units per month, with 40 apartments already updated.
