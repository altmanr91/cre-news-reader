Barry Diller's People Inc. Offers To Buy MGM Resorts For $18B

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 12:24:09 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

People Inc., led by media executive Barry Diller, has submitted a nonbinding proposal to acquire MGM Resorts International for approximately $18 billion, or $48.30 per share. People Inc. already holds a significant stake in MGM, and Diller, who serves on MGM's board, expressed confidence in the casino giant's real-world assets and digital growth potential, believing the market undervalues them. This proposed acquisition follows closely on the heels of another major casino deal, with Fertitta Entertainment's bid to acquire Caesars Entertainment.
