Anagram eyes River North office tower at 75% discount after Blackstone default

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Anagram Real Estate and Glendon Capital Management are reportedly in negotiations to acquire 350 North Orleans Street, a 1.3 million-square-foot office tower in Chicago, out of foreclosure. The potential sale price, estimated between $90 million and $100 million, represents a significant discount from the $378 million paid by the previous owner, Blackstone, in 2015. This transaction highlights the ongoing trend of distressed office asset sales in downtown Chicago, driven by post-pandemic shifts in office demand and higher interest rates.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Downtown Chicago office buildings are being purchased at significant discounts, with 500 West Monroe Street selling for a 76 percent discount. Nationally, some office buildings are selling at 90 percent discounts or more, such as the Denver Energy Center, which sold for $5.3 million after a 2013 sale of $176 million.
