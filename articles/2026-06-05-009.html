Reports: Samsung to Relocate U.S. Headquarters from New Jersey to Plano

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 15:21:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Samsung is reportedly relocating its U.S. headquarters from Englewood Cliffs, New Jersey, to Plano, Texas, a move expected to be completed by the end of the year. This strategic decision aims to consolidate Samsung's U.S. operations in Texas, where the company already has a significant presence with a semiconductor manufacturing plant in Taylor. The relocation comes less than a year after Samsung opened its new headquarters facility in New Jersey, though it is unclear if that facility will be entirely vacated.
