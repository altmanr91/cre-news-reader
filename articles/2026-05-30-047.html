United Overseas Bank Takes Over Landmark Midtown Tower

REO

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 29 May 2026 13:19:45 -0400

SUMMARY

United Overseas Bank has taken ownership of the Bush Tower, a 29-story neo-Gothic office building in Midtown Manhattan, from Vanke US in a transaction valued at $58.1 million. This acquisition by the Singapore-based lender, which provided a $120 million loan on the property in 2018, represents a significant loss on the original financing. Vanke US will continue to manage the building for United Overseas Bank, which intends to sell the property. The deal highlights the ongoing trend of lenders taking back office assets at reduced valuations as the commercial real estate market adjusts to post-pandemic conditions.
