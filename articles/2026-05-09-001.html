Gary Barnett’s Extell Lands $500M Acquisition of 405-417 Park Avenue

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:02:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Extell Development, led by Gary Barnett, has acquired a full-block development site at 405-417 Park Avenue in Midtown Manhattan for $500 million. This acquisition represents the largest component of Barnett's ongoing assemblage in the area, with the potential for up to 700,000 square feet of rentable office space. The deal underscores Barnett's continued confidence in the New York City market and his vision for shaping its skyline.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: development site

Address: 405-417 Park Avenue, New York City, NY

Sale Price: $500.0M

Notable Features: Prime block-long development site with potential for up to 700,000 square feet of office space.

Project Notes: Extell also acquired 103,000 square feet of air rights from Central Synagogue for $20 million, with a put option for an additional 30,000 square feet. The site was jointly marketed by multiple owners.
