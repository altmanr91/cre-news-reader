The Plan: WeWork Is Growing Up at 511 Fifth Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 13 May 2026 10:00:41 +0000

SUMMARY

WeWork is implementing a significant design shift at its new 37,000-square-foot lease at 511 Fifth Avenue in New York City, moving towards a boutique hotel or members-only club aesthetic. The coworking giant, which leased four floors earlier this year, is creating a more intimate and private workspace with features like burgundy carpets, warm color palettes, and custom fixtures. This new design philosophy, developed in collaboration with landlords Wharton Properties and Aurora Capital Partners, aims to cater to a Midtown clientele of boutique hedge funds, financial services, and accounting firms, reflecting a "gradient of privacy" in its layout.
