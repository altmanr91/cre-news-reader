Tavros Signs Chelsea Piers Fitness to Mixed-Use Lower Manhattan Development

LEASE

Connect CRE — Thu, 14 May 2026 22:46:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Tavros has secured a long-term lease agreement with Chelsea Piers Fitness for a significant fitness club within its planned mixed-use tower at 250 Water St. in Lower Manhattan. The 76,000-square-foot facility, spanning five stories and including outdoor space, will be a key amenity for the Seaport neighborhood. This marks the second collaboration between Tavros and Chelsea Piers Fitness, with construction on the tower expected to commence this winter.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 250 Water St, New York City, NY

Size: 76,000 SF

Notable Features: Includes 9,000 square feet of outdoor space.

Project Notes: Tavros and Atlas Capital acquired the property in February. Construction is expected to break ground in winter 2026.
