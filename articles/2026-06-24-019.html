NYC’s top construction permits: Week ending June 19, 2026

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Permit Filings

This article summarizes the top construction permit applications filed in New York City during the week ending June 19, 2026, highlighting significant new building, major alteration, and demolition projects across various boroughs. Notable new residential developments include a 127-unit project in Crotona Park East and a 108-unit project in Kew Gardens. Major alteration permits were filed for an office expansion in Graniteville and a significant renovation in Midtown. The largest demolition permit was for a partial teardown on the Upper East Side to make way for a new condominium development.
