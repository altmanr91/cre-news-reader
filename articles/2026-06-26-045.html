REIT's Liquidation Plan Upended After $280M Sale Falls Through

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 11:25:45 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / REIT News

Elme Communities, a multifamily REIT undergoing liquidation, has withdrawn its shareholder payout estimate after a $280 million deal to sell its largest remaining asset, the 1,222-unit Riverside Apartments in Alexandria, VA, collapsed. The terminated contract, which included adjacent undeveloped land, introduces uncertainty regarding the timing and proceeds of the property's sale, impacting the REIT's ability to fully repay a $520 million term loan from Goldman Sachs. This development has led to a significant drop in Elme's stock value and necessitates renewed marketing efforts for the property.
