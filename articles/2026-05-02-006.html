Shopoff Realty Breaks Ground on Westminster Mall Redevelopment Project in Orange County

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 01 May 2026 14:30:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Shopoff Realty Investments has commenced construction on Bolsa Pacific at Westminster, a significant mixed-use redevelopment project in Orange County. This ambitious undertaking will transform the former Westminster Mall into a vibrant downtown destination featuring a substantial residential component, retail and restaurant space, a hotel, and extensive public open areas. The project's scope expanded in 2024 to encompass the entire 83.3-acre former mall site, allowing for a comprehensive reimagining of the property into a connected mixed-use district.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Bolsa Pacific at Westminster

Size: 220,000 SF

Size: 2,250 units

Keys: 120

Site Area: 83.3 acres

Notable Features: Located along the 405 Freeway and 10 minutes from the Los Angeles County border. Includes market-rate and affordable housing, as well as for-sale townhomes.

Project Notes: Demolition of the former Westminster Mall is underway. The existing Target will remain open during construction, with a new store planned and relocated within the overall development. The project's scope expanded in 2024 from a 25.8-acre assemblage to the full 83.3-acre site.
