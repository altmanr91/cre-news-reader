Groundwork gears up first phase of $1B Tallgrass community in Burleson

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Groundwork Development Partners is preparing to break ground on the first phase of Tallgrass, a $1 billion master-planned community spanning 621 acres in Burleson, south of Fort Worth. The initial phase will encompass 200 acres and include 645 residences, 45 acres of parks, and an elementary school. The overall development is slated to feature approximately 4,000 homes of various types, 90 acres of parks, and one million square feet of commercial space, aiming to create a multigenerational community with diverse housing options.
