Byron Trott and Michael Dell’s firm pays feds $80M for Old Post Office

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 14:43:51 +0000

SUMMARY

BDT & MSD Partners, the firm co-founded by Byron Trott and Michael Dell, has acquired the Old Post Office building and its underlying land at 1100 Pennsylvania Avenue NW in Washington, D.C., from the General Services Administration for $80 million. The firm, which already held a $285 million note and had purchased the leasing rights for $100 million at a prior foreclosure auction, is consolidating its ownership of the property, which operates as a Waldorf Astoria hotel. The federal government initiated the process to sell the property earlier this year.
