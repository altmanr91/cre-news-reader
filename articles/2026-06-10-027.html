Sant Ambroeus to anchor Kengo Kuma building in Miami Design District

LEASE

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 19:31:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Sant Ambroeus, a Milanese Italian restaurant and coffee shop, has signed a 5,000-square-foot lease for a new full-service restaurant with a coffee bar and outdoor dining space at the Mirai Design District in Miami. The upcoming mixed-use building, designed by Japanese architect Kengo Kuma, recently broke ground and is expected to be completed in 2028. The developers are optimistic that Miami's continued growth in financial and creative firms will drive demand for boutique office space in the Design District.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Mirai Design District

Address: 4218 Northeast Second Avenue, Miami, FL

Size: 5,000 SF

Completion: 2028

Notable Features: Designed by Japanese architect Kengo Kuma, the mixed-use development will include 41,000 square feet of Class A office space and 16,000 square feet of retail, restaurant, and hospitality space, along with a 2,000-square-foot standalone sculptural structure.

Project Notes: The project recently secured an $85 million construction loan from Monroe Capital and has started construction. The office portion is targeted toward family offices, small and midsize investment firms, and design-focused businesses.
