Opus begins construction of Continental mixing facility in Mount Pleasant

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Tue, 26 May 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opus has commenced construction on an 80,000-square-foot advanced manufacturing facility for ContiTech, a division of Continental, in Mount Pleasant, Iowa. The new compounding center will integrate production, warehouse, and office operations, including a three-story mixing facility, an automated high-bay warehouse, and office/lab space, all connected to the existing plant. Opus, acting as the design-builder, proposed a precast concrete system to meet the substantial load demands and improve efficiency for the specialized vertical manufacturing process.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Size: 80,000 SF

Completion: June 2027

Notable Features: Includes a three-story 17,472-square-foot mixing facility, automated high-bay warehouse, and 4,250-square-foot office and lab space, all linked to an existing facility. Designed with a precast concrete system to accommodate substantial load demands for a specialized vertical manufacturing process.

Project Notes: A portion of the mixing facility is expected to be ready by fall 2026 for the installation of critical mixing equipment.
