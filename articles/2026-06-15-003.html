Tishman Speyer Snaps Up $40M Mezz Loan on New York’s One Dag

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 12:00:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Tishman Speyer's debt platform has acquired a $40 million mezzanine loan on One Dag Hammarskjöld, an 817,000-square-foot office building in Midtown Manhattan. This transaction follows the recent acquisition of the property by 601W Companies and David Werner Real Estate Investments, for which J.P. Morgan provided the senior financing. Tishman Speyer's global head of debt noted the compelling opportunity to invest in a premier asset with an experienced institutional sponsor, reflecting continued confidence in the Manhattan office sector.

Project Notes: The property was recently acquired by 601W Companies and David Werner Real Estate Investments for $270 million. J.P. Morgan provided a $215 million whole loan for the acquisition, which included the $40 million mezzanine component.
