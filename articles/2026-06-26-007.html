Transwestern Completes 321,104 SF Industrial Project in North Houston

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 15:20:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Transwestern has completed Imperial Crossing, a two-building industrial project totaling 321,104 square feet in North Houston. The development offers flexible space configurations, with one building featuring a cross-dock design and 36-foot clear heights, and the other a front-load design with 32-foot clear heights. The project is designed to accommodate a range of tenant sizes, from full-building users to those needing as little as 35,000 square feet.

DATA POINTS
