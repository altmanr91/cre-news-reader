Cushman & Wakefield Negotiates Sale of Publix-Anchored Shopping Center in Central Florida

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 14 May 2026 13:49:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Graphite Real Estate has acquired Shoppes of Sweetwater, a 64,253-square-foot, Publix-anchored shopping center in Longwood, Florida. The property was 94% leased at the time of sale and includes an outparcel leased to McDonald's, along with an undeveloped 0.6-acre parcel. Cushman & Wakefield represented the seller, Shoppes of Sweetwater Inc., in the transaction.
