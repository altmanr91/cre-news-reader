South Florida’s top deals: Publix drops $78M in Boynton Beach

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 21 May 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A subsidiary of the supermarket chain Publix acquired the Fountains of Boynton shopping center in Boynton Beach for $78 million. The property, anchored by a Publix store, was previously purchased by Union Investment in 2021 for $79.5 million. This transaction represents a significant commercial deal in South Florida.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Fountains of Boynton

Address: 6545, 6627, 6661 and 6667 Boynton Beach Boulevard and 9811 South Job Road, Boynton Beach, FL

Sale Price: $78.0M

Notable Features: Anchored by a Publix supermarket.

Project Notes: The property was previously acquired by Union Investment in 2021 for $79.5 million.
