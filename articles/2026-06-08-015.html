Movers: Five-person Avison Young industrial team jumps to JLL

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

A five-person industrial brokerage team, including Managing Director Adam Haefner, Executive Vice Presidents Marty Mikaitis and Zeke Rowan, Vice President Nick Fazio, and Sales Coordinator Anne McGrath, has moved from Avison Young to JLL in Chicago. The team will focus on industrial clients in the O'Hare submarket, bringing a combined deal volume of over $2.5 billion across 50 million square feet. Additionally, Core Industrial Realty hired Alexander "AJ" Smith as a new broker, Lee & Associates promoted Tony Russo to Executive Vice President and Matthew Androwich to Vice President, and McHugh Construction added Eric Engstrom as Project Executive, Grant Peoples as Director of Risk Management, and Amit Patel as Project Manager. Jordan Multak also joined Greenstone Partners, specializing in multifamily and mixed-use sectors.
