Rockpoint, LCOR plot historic 299-unit resi complex in Georgetown

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 21:05:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Rockpoint, LCOR, and Potomac Investment Properties are partnering in a joint venture to redevelop two existing properties at 1000 and 1050 Thomas Jefferson Street NW in Washington, D.C.'s Georgetown neighborhood. This project will create 299 rental units across two buildings, marking the largest multifamily development in Georgetown in over a century. The development will also include 18,000 square feet of retail space and preserve existing parking.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 1000 and 1050 Thomas Jefferson Street NW, Washington, D.C.

Size: 349,000 SF

Units: 299

Notable Features: Proposed amenities include resident lounges, a rooftop pool, a fitness center, a pet spa, and a library. The project will also include 18,000 square feet of retail space and preserve 282 below-grade parking spaces.

Project Notes: The joint venture received approval for the conversion from the Old Georgetown Board and the U.S. Commission of Fine Arts. This is the first multifamily project in Georgetown to exceed 150 units in over a century.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Rockpoint

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: LCOR

OWNER: Potomac Investment Properties

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Washington, D.C. has become a hub for conversions, with 32 adaptive reuse projects currently underway, totaling approximately 7 million square feet. This is a significant increase from the average of four conversions per year between 2016 and 2024.
