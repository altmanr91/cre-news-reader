Who’s guiding Compass’ new era

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 04 May 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Compass has appointed Neda Navab as its new president, tasking her with guiding the company into a new era following its significant $1.6 billion acquisition of Anywhere Real Estate. Navab, who previously served as chief of staff to CEO Robert Reffkin, is undertaking a 100-day listening tour to engage with agents across the country. This leadership transition and strategic integration come as Compass navigates the complexities of merging with Anywhere, which includes integrating thousands of new agents, franchise businesses, and a substantial debt load, while also working to unify corporate culture and technology platforms.

DATA POINTS

Sale Price: $1.60B

Project Notes: Compass acquired Anywhere Real Estate, which included brands like Sotheby's International Realty, Corcoran, and Coldwell Banker, along with $3 billion in debt from Anywhere's balance sheets.
