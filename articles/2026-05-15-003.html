Tech Services Firm Audible Difference Takes 47K SF at Brooklyn Army Terminal

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 14 May 2026 19:59:35 +0000

SUMMARY

The New York City Economic Development Corporation (NYCEDC) has secured over 58,000 square feet of new leases at the Brooklyn Army Terminal (BAT) in Sunset Park, including a significant 47,106-square-foot deal with audiovisual firm Audible Difference. This expansion for Audible Difference, a Brooklyn-based company, underscores the appeal of BAT's industrial and creative ecosystem. Additionally, smart locker company GoLocker, camera and lens rental company Otto Nemenz International, and clean tech manufacturer EcoLogin also signed leases, contributing to the city's ongoing efforts to transform the office and manufacturing hub.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Brooklyn Army Terminal

Size: 47,106 SF

Site Area: 59 acres

Notable Features: City-owned campus managed by NYCEDC, part of a 59-acre campus.

Project Notes: Audible Difference signed a five-year lease. GoLocker signed a three-year lease for 7,178 SF. Otto Nemenz International signed a five-year lease for 4,350 SF. EcoLogin signed a seven-year lease for 3,854 SF. An additional 3,840 SF lease was signed at MADE Bush Terminal with Makelab, bringing the total activated space across two city-owned campuses to over 66,000 SF.
