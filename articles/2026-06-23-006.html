Draper and Kramer VP earns industry honor

REJournals — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 09:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Draper and Kramer, a national real estate firm, announced that Kathryn Jorgensen, Vice President of Investment Management, has been recognized by GlobeSt. as one of CRE's Aspiring Leaders of 2026. The award acknowledges professionals under 40 who significantly contribute to their company's success and the commercial real estate industry. Jorgensen was specifically lauded for her adept handling of complex challenges, including navigating anchor tenant departures and environmental issues during asset dispositions, as well as her retail expertise in repositioning commercial spaces within multifamily properties.
