Fort Lauderdale’s luxury sales are booming, but insiders are cautious

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 13 May 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Fort Lauderdale's luxury residential market is experiencing a resurgence in sales, driven by renewed buyer confidence and a strong boating culture. However, brokers and lenders remain cautious due to potential oversupply and a buyer demographic that is less inclined to pay ultra-luxury prices compared to neighboring Miami and Palm Beach. Despite a slowdown last year following tariff announcements, recent high-value transactions indicate a turning tide, with demand focused on new construction, turnkey waterfront homes that accommodate yachts.
