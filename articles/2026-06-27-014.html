Marcus & Millichap negotiates sale of 43,125-square-foot industrial property in Nicholasville

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of a 43,125-square-foot industrial property in Nicholasville, Kentucky. The asset, fully occupied by a mission-critical tenant under a new 16-year absolute net lease, was sold by a local client to an Ohio-based buyer. The property's location within a key industrial corridor and its proximity to major transportation routes and regional employers were highlighted as significant advantages.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 400 Enterprise Drive, Nicholasville, KY

Size: 43,125 SF

Site Area: 6.04 acres

Notable Features: Fully occupied by a mission-critical tenant with a new 16-year absolute net lease extending through 2041. Located within Nicholasville's industrial corridor, benefiting from proximity to major transportation routes, regional employers, and growing retail, residential, and population density within the Lexington-Fayette metro area.
