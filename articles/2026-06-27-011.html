KeyBank provides $56 million of financing for seniors housing in Ohio

LOAN

REJournals — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

KeyBank Community Development Lending and Investment (CDLI) has provided a comprehensive financing package totaling $56 million for the new construction of Clover Glen II, a 96-unit affordable seniors housing project in Galloway, Ohio. The financing includes a $16.5 million taxable construction loan, a $9.4 million federal Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) equity investment, and an $8.2 million state LIHTC investment. Additionally, KeyBank Commercial Mortgage Group provided an $8.2 million Freddie TEL permanent loan, and KeyBanc Capital Markets underwrote $13.82 million in tax-exempt bonds for the project, which is being developed by National Church Residences.

Notable Features: Affordable housing for seniors aged 55+, with units restricted to 50%, 60%, and 70% AMI. Features senior-oriented design and accessibility, including handrails and grab bars, a clubhouse, fitness center, and limited-access entry.

Project Notes: The project is a new construction senior LIHTC development. KeyBank and National Church Residences have a long-standing affordable housing partnership. Clover Glen I, an adjacent parcel, was financed by KeyBank CDLI in October 2022.
