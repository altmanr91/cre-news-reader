2 Investment Giants Now Predict 3 Rate Hikes This Year

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 11:19:13 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Two major investment firms, Bank of America and PGIM, have revised their economic forecasts to predict three interest rate hikes by the Federal Reserve this year. This shift reflects a growing consensus that the central bank will need to take more aggressive action to combat persistent inflation, which has been exacerbated by rising oil prices due to geopolitical events and strong demand-side pressures from artificial intelligence. Despite earlier hopes for rate cuts, the Fed's own projections from its June meeting also indicated that rates are unlikely to fall in 2026, challenging commercial real estate investors who have been anticipating rate relief for over two years.

PGIM Rate Hike Forecast: 3 rate hikes in 2026
