$14M Refinance Secured for Newly Delivered Wicker Park Multifamily

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 20:45:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Arrow Real Estate Advisors has arranged a $14.2 million refinance loan for The Ash, a recently completed 40-unit multifamily property in Chicago's Wicker Park neighborhood. The financing, provided by Associated Bank, was secured while the property was still in its lease-up phase, having already achieved over 70% occupancy. The strong demand for new multifamily developments in this competitive market, coupled with a 30-year tax abatement, contributed to favorable loan terms including a future rate step-down feature upon stabilization.
