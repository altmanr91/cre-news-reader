St John’s U. Sells Staten Island Campus to Wagner College

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 13 May 2026 22:18:58 +0000

SUMMARY

St. John's University has sold its Staten Island Campus to Wagner College, ensuring the property will continue to be used for higher education and community benefit. Savills represented St. John's in the transaction, which was described as a mission-driven sale requiring a balance of financial, institutional, and community considerations. The sale allows St. John's to reinvest in its main Queens campus and other global sites, while Wagner College plans to integrate the campus into its existing institutional footprint.
