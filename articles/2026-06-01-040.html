Inland Waterfront Destination Slated for Miramar

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 14:07:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Sunbeam and Stiles have broken ground on Miramar Cove, a 125-acre mixed-use development in Miramar, FL, which they are positioning as South Florida's first inland waterfront destination. The project, with an estimated cost of up to $1 billion, will feature a significant water component, including a Blue Mar Basin with translucent blue water, surrounded by retail, office, residential, and hospitality components. The development aims to create a vibrant community hub with dining, entertainment, and gathering spaces centered around the waterfront.
