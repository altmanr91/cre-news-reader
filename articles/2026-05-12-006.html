Southern Land Breaks Ground on 953,000 SF Mixed-Use Project in Houston

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 11 May 2026 15:52:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Southern Land Co. has commenced construction on a 953,000-square-foot mixed-use development in Houston's River Oaks neighborhood. The project will feature a 10-story building with office and retail components, alongside a 38-story apartment tower offering 331 residential units. Completion is anticipated in 2028.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Size: 953,000 SF

Units: 331

Completion: 2028

Notable Features: Residential amenities include a rooftop lounge with a pool, a seventh-floor garden deck connecting the buildings, a residential spa, guest suites, and a fitness center accessible to both residents and office users.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Southern Land Co.

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Andres Construction
