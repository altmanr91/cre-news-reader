Jameis Winston, Mark Ingram Among Investors In Modular Building Firm

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 28 May 2026 12:40:55 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Kinexx Modular Construction, a Chicago-based modular home company, is raising capital through a crowdfunding campaign to expand its operations into eight new urban markets across the country. The company specializes in building on challenging urban infill lots that traditional construction firms often avoid. The expansion aims to address the significant number of vacant lots in cities like Detroit, Baltimore, and St. Louis, leveraging modular construction as a solution for affordable housing development.

DATA POINTS

Modular construction share of total U.S. construction (2024): 5.1%

Projected annual growth of modular construction: 4.5%

Vacant lots in cities like Detroit, Baltimore, Cleveland, St. Louis, and Columbus, OH: 250,000+

Initial target for expansion into 8 urban markets: 25,000 vacant lots
