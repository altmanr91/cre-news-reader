Kennedy Wilson Forges €2-Billion JV to Own, Develop Irish Apartments

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 11:23:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Kennedy Wilson has established a €2 billion (US$2.31 billion) residential joint venture with APG, acting on behalf of Dutch pension fund ABP, to develop and manage a portfolio of over 3,400 private rented homes in Ireland. This strategic partnership will significantly expand Kennedy Wilson's Irish residential footprint to approximately 6,900 units upon completion of the new developments. The venture includes Kennedy Wilson acquiring a minority equity interest in APG's existing 1,100-unit Cherrywood portfolio and a minority equity interest in 2,300 new rental units across three development sites, which Kennedy Wilson will develop and deliver. These projects are expected to transform communities and contribute to Ireland's housing supply goals.
