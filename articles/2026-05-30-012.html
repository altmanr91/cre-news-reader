HDA completes 155,000-square-foot Forsythia on the Park development in Clayton

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 29 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

HDA has completed Forsythia on the Park, a 155,500-square-foot luxury condominium development in Clayton, Missouri. The four-story building features 38 two- and three-bedroom residences designed to maximize views and natural light, with expansive private terraces connecting indoor living to the adjacent Shaw Park. The project emphasizes a balance of architecture, environment, and livability, offering residents a high-end experience with refined and functional interiors.
