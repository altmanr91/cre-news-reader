Gersh Autism Academy Signs 21K-SF Bronx Sublease at 425 College Avenue

Commercial Observer — Thu, 21 May 2026 20:11:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Lease

Gersh Autism Academy, a nonprofit serving autistic children, has subleased a 21,000-square-foot building at 452 College Avenue in the Mott Haven neighborhood of the Bronx. The space was formerly occupied by Brilla Schools, which is consolidating its operations. The transaction reflects strong demand for educational facilities in the area.
