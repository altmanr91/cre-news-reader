Samsung Latest Corporate Giant To Bring HQ Operations To Plano

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 17:11:34 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Samsung Electronics America is relocating its U.S. headquarters from Englewood Cliffs, New Jersey, to its existing campus in Plano, Texas, by the end of the year. This strategic move aims to enhance operational alignment and focus on key business priorities, building on the company's three-decade presence in the state. Approximately 1,000 employees from the New Jersey headquarters are expected to be reassigned to the Plano location, which already supports nearly 2,000 employees.
