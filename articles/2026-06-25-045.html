Sea Breeze Properties Opens Rental Project in San Marcos’ North City District

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:13:05 +0000

SUMMARY

Sea Breeze Properties has opened 222 North City, a new 12-story residential project in San Marcos' North City district. The community, designed by Safdie Rabines Architects, features contemporary architecture, hospitality-inspired living, and over an acre of open space with various amenities. The developer aimed to create a culturally vibrant project that fosters connections through design, walkability, art, and shared experiences.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: 222 North City

Address: 222 N City Dr, San Marcos, CA*

Notable Features: Features over an acre of open space, a resort-style pool, two spas, two clubhouses, outdoor pavilions, kitchens, and sun decks. Residences range from studios to three-bedroom homes, with premium '128 Collection' residences on higher floors.
