What CRE Executives Want In An Increasingly Fraught Job Market

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 16:39:49 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Commercial real estate executives are seeking new college graduates with a broader and more diverse skill set than in previous decades, as the job market for recent grads faces its highest unemployment rate in a decade. Developers emphasized the need for new hires to possess multidisciplinary expertise, including financial engineering, design, sustainability, and proficiency in integrating artificial intelligence into workflows. Some firms are also prioritizing candidates with prior government experience, particularly in affordable housing, due to the increasing complexity and nuanced nature of such projects. The consensus among industry leaders is that while specialized knowledge remains important, the ability to think critically, adapt to new technologies, and demonstrate a wide range of transferable skills is crucial for new entrants to stand out.
