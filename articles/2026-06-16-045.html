Shorenstein Investment Advisers Buys Premium Office Campus In Major Dallas Market

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 11:42:19 -0400

SUMMARY

Shorenstein Investment Advisers has acquired The Tennyson, a 273,000-square-foot Class-A office campus in Plano's Legacy business park, from Spear Street Capital. The fully leased, two-building campus, originally developed in 2012 as a corporate headquarters, recently underwent significant capital improvements. Shorenstein plans further amenity enhancements, aligning with its strategy to invest in submarkets poised for rent growth and strong demographic tailwinds. The acquisition reflects continued investor appetite for high-quality office product in growing Sun Belt markets, particularly assets with recent reinvestment and strong market positioning.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Plano's office market is experiencing growth due to corporate relocations, including AT&T moving its global headquarters to Plano in 2028 and Samsung Electronics America relocating its U.S. headquarters to Plano by the end of 2026. AT&T's new headquarters will be a $1.35 billion project, more than double the size of its current 1 million SF building. The Dallas Stars also announced plans for a mixed-use district anchored by a new arena in Plano.
