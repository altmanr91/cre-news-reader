Locally Based Logistics Firm Scales Up with Menifee Warehouse Acquisition

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 20:13:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Southwest Traders, a Temecula-based logistics and distribution company, acquired Gateway at Menifee, a 229,934-square-foot industrial facility in Menifee, CA, for $40.5 million. The acquisition, which closed rapidly after escrow opened in May 2026, will enable Southwest Traders to expand its regional logistics footprint. The company plans an additional $25 million investment to convert a portion of the building into a refrigerated distribution center.
