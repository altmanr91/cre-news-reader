Kohan Retail completes heavily-discounted purchase of former Citadel Center

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 15:31:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Kohan Retail Investment Group has acquired the former Citadel Center, a 37-story office tower in Chicago, for $137 million. The New York-based firm, known for revitalizing distressed properties, financed the acquisition with $65 million in debt from BayernLB. This purchase marks Kohan's third office acquisition in Chicago within approximately a year, demonstrating their strategy of acquiring discounted assets in the market. The building, which was 45% vacant at the time of the agreement in February, has recently secured new leases, and its naming rights are available for a significant tenant.
