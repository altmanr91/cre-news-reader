Senior Living Investment Brokerage closes sale of two-community skilled-nursing portfolio in Nebraska

SALE

REJournals — Wed, 13 May 2026 16:29:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Senior Living Investment Brokerage facilitated the sale of a two-community skilled nursing portfolio in Bridgeport and Hemingford, Nebraska. The transaction involved a Denver-based owner/operator selling the properties to a Utah-based owner/operator, who acquired one community and assumed a leasehold interest with a purchase option on the other. This deal represents the second closing in a larger four-property disposition strategy for the seller, allowing the buyer to establish and expand its presence in Nebraska.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: skilled nursing

Beds: 87

Project Notes: This transaction represents the second closing within a larger four-property portfolio disposition strategy. The Hemingford community was structured as a lease-option transaction.
