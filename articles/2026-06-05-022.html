NYC’s top deals: Cohen offloads two properties to ESRT for $114M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Empire State Realty Trust acquired two office properties in New York City from Cohen Brothers Realty for a combined total of $113.5 million. The transaction included a 35-story office building at 1400 Broadway in the Garment District and a 25-story office building at 112 West 34th Street in Midtown. This acquisition represents a significant commercial real estate transaction in the city.
