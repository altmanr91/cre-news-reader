Where do the San Antonio Spurs live off the court?

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

This article provides an overview of the residential real estate holdings of current and former San Antonio Spurs players and coach, noting a shift from the traditional enclave of The Dominion to more dispersed locations, primarily in north San Antonio near the team's practice facility. It highlights specific property transactions and preferences, such as Keldon Johnson's ranch in Boerne and Gregg Popovich's penthouse in Southtown. The article also touches on the team's upcoming new arena in Downtown San Antonio, which is part of a larger development plan.
