Stonemont Starts Work on 100K-SF Palm Beach Gardens Medical Campus

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 14:07:02 +0000

SUMMARY

Stonemont has commenced construction on a 100,000-square-foot medical campus in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, in partnership with The Sina Companies and Fengate Asset Management. The project, slated for completion by 2028, will feature a 53,370-square-foot neighborhood hospital with emergency services and 29 inpatient beds, alongside a 47,000-square-foot medical outpatient building offering specialized care. This development is part of the larger Avenir master-planned community and represents a significant expansion of Jupiter Medical Center's services in the region.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Size: 100,000 SF

Completion: 2028

Notable Features: The campus includes a two-story neighborhood hospital with 24-hour emergency services and 29 inpatient beds, and a three-story medical outpatient building for various specialists.

Project Notes: The facilities are located within the 4,752-acre Avenir master-planned community.
