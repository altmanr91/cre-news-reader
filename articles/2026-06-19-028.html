Lake Geneva’s historic Allegheny estate lists for $24M as priciest in southern Wisconsin

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 16:09:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The historic Allegheny estate in Lake Geneva, Wisconsin, has been listed for $24 million, making it the most expensive active listing in southern Wisconsin. The nearly 21-acre waterfront property features a 102-year-old Colonial-style mansion and 560 feet of Geneva Lake shoreline. The estate, designed by architect Howard Van Doren Shaw, includes original architectural details and is protected by a conservation easement preventing subdivision. A sale at this price would be one of only a few transactions in the area to exceed $20 million.
