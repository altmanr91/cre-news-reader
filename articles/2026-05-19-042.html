Value Of Tishman Speyer's Long Island City Office Complex Slashed In Loan Workout

REFINANCE

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 18 May 2026 15:40:15 -0400

SUMMARY

Tishman Speyer has secured a three-year extension on the $425 million CMBS loan for The Jacx, a 1.2 million-square-foot office complex in Long Island City, New York. The extension, which includes a 1.5-year option, was granted after Tishman Speyer injected an additional $20 million in equity. The property's appraised value has been significantly reduced to $480 million, a 44% decrease from its 2021 valuation, reflecting challenges such as a major vacancy from a large tenant that never occupied its space and another tenant's lease abandonment. Despite these hurdles, Tishman Speyer expressed confidence in the quality of the development and the surrounding neighborhood.
