Chetrits face more distress in the Financial District

LOAN

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The $290 million CMBS loan backed by the Standard Oil Building at 26 Broadway in New York City's Financial District has been transferred to special servicing. The Chetrit Group, owner of the office building, acknowledged it would discontinue loan payments due to a significant drop in net cash flow and occupancy, coupled with rising operating costs. The property's occupancy has fallen from 83% in 2021 to 75% by the end of Q1, and its debt service coverage ratio has collapsed to 0.76x.
