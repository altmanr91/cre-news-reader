20 Times Square Loan Returns to Special Servicing

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:15:06 +0000

SUMMARY

The $647.5 million CMBS loan secured by a 99-year ground lease on the 20 Times Square property has returned to special servicing after missing its May 2026 maturity date. The loan, originated by Natixis in 2018, previously entered special servicing in November 2022 before receiving an extension in late 2023. The property includes a 454-room hotel and largely vacant retail space, with the retail portion having previously housed a significant tenant that closed shortly after its 2018 debut.

The hotel component was shuttered during the COVID-19 pandemic and reopened in June 2021.
