Dermody sells portion of former Allstate HQ to Realty Income for $175M

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 22:10:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Dermody has sold a portion of the former Allstate headquarters campus in Glenview, IL, to Realty Income for $175 million. This transaction is part of Dermody's ongoing redevelopment of the 232-acre site into an industrial campus, which it acquired in 2022. The sale includes two parcels within the larger industrial park, which is planned to feature 10 warehouses totaling 3.2 million square feet. Dermody also sold a separate industrial property in Elmhurst, IL, to Realty Income for $41 million, bringing Realty Income's total investment in Dermody's Chicago-area industrial properties to $216 million.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Allstate headquarters

Address: 2775 Sanders Road, Glenview, IL

Sale Price: $175.0M

Phase I: 1.2 million square feet across five warehouses

Total Project (All Phases): 3.2 million square feet across 10 warehouses

Site Area: 232 acres

Notable Features: Former corporate headquarters campus being redeveloped into an industrial park.

Project Notes: Dermody acquired the campus in 2022 for $232 million. The Village of Glenview agreed to an incentive package for the developer, expecting to generate $135 million in tax revenue. The property was refinanced for $27 million with a loan from New York Life Insurance in early 2025.
