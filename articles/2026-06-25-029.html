601W Companies’ Blackstone-originated $343M loan for 1 South Wacker Drive enters default

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 19:47:01 +0000

SUMMARY

REO / Foreclosure

601W Companies has defaulted on a $343 million loan for its 1.2 million-square-foot office tower at 1 South Wacker Drive in Downtown Chicago. The loan, originated by Blackstone Mortgage Trust in 2018, entered default on June 9. The property, which 601W acquired in 2019 for $310 million, is currently 73 percent occupied. This default highlights the ongoing challenges in the Downtown Chicago office market, which continues to struggle with post-pandemic recovery.
