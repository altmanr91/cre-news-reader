Texas Family Office Snaps Up Grocery-Anchored El Cajon Retail

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 21:07:48 +0000

SUMMARY

A Texas-based family office has acquired The Shops at Parkway Plaza, a 122,484-square-foot grocery-anchored retail center in El Cajon, for $31.5 million. The property features a strong tenant mix and is strategically located adjacent to a regional shopping mall. The sale highlights the continued robust demand for well-performing daily needs retail centers with potential for net operating income growth.
