Lenny’s Repair Takes 14K SF at Red Hook’s 590 Smith Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 19:45:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Lenny's Repair, a phone wholesaler, has signed a five-and-a-half-year lease for 14,000 square feet of flex warehouse and office space at 590 Smith Street in Red Hook, Brooklyn. The deal represents a consolidation for the repair business, which will occupy an upper floor of the three-story building. The landlord, Yeshiva of Kasho, also recently leased 7,000 square feet to medical wholesaler CW Health, reflecting Red Hook's appeal to industrial and flex tenants seeking more affordable options compared to redeveloping areas like Gowanus.

DATA POINTS
