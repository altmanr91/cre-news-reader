Hunter Advisors Arranges Sale of 141-Room Aloft San Antonio Airport Hotel

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 08 May 2026 15:29:46 +0000

SUMMARY

Hunter Advisors has facilitated the sale of the 141-room Aloft San Antonio Airport Hotel. The Marriott-branded, select-service property, built in 2009, was acquired by Armada Investments LP from an undisclosed seller. The hotel offers a range of amenities including a pool, business center, fitness center, conference facilities, and airport shuttle service.
