Billionaire Jeff Greene’s latest West Palm Beach resi plans tap Live Local

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 28 May 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Billionaire developer Jeff Greene has proposed a 25-story, 366-unit apartment tower in West Palm Beach, marking his first project under Florida's Live Local Act. The development will incorporate a prefabricated mass timber system, making it one of the tallest of its kind in Florida, and will preserve two historic facades of a former fire station on the site. The project aims to address the demand for workforce housing, with 148 units (40%) designated as affordable, integrated throughout the building alongside market-rate units.
