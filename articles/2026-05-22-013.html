Gerdom Realty and Investment helps bring fitness center to Novi

LEASE

REJournals — Fri, 22 May 2026 09:47:00 +0000

SUMMARY

OrangeTheory Fitness has secured a 4,800-square-foot lease at Novi 10 Plaza in Novi, Michigan. The fitness center will occupy the former Hallmark space within the Busch's Grocery-anchored retail center, enhancing the tenant mix at this well-located property.
