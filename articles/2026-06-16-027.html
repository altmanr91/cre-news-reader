Oak Brook medical office space purchased by its tenant from Harrison Street and MedProperties Group

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A Northwestern Medicine affiliate has acquired a seven-story medical office building in Oak Brook, Illinois, for $58 million, transitioning from tenant to owner. The property, located at 1001 Commerce Drive, was sold by its developers, Harrison Street and MedProperties Group. This transaction highlights the strong demand for medical office space in the Chicagoland area, a sector that continues to see consistent activity and investment despite broader office market challenges.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Medical office and lab property deals in Chicagoland totaled over $600 million in May. The demand for outpatient space remains consistent, contrasting with general office properties that have struggled with vacancy due to hybrid work trends. The medical office market is stable due to an aging population and low supply, though investors are cautious with federally funded offices.
