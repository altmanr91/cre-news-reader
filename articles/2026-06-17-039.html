Bradford Allen Closes 60K-SF Office Lease in Lincolnshire

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 20:46:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Bradford Allen has completed a 60,000-square-foot office lease renewal with an office supply retailer at 300 Tri State International Drive in Lincolnshire, Illinois. The property is part of the Tri State Lincolnshire office complex, which Bradford Allen has owned and operated since 2017, investing over $30 million in significant renovations and upgrades to enhance the tenant experience and building infrastructure.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Tri State Lincolnshire

Address: 300 Tri State International Drive, Lincolnshire, IL

Size: 60,000 SF

Notable Features: Part of a five-building, 560,000-square-foot office complex with a 135-seat auditorium and on-site fitness center, offering convenient access to downtown Chicago and O'Hare International Airport.

Project Notes: Bradford Allen has invested over $30 million in improvements since acquiring the complex in 2017, including upgrades to lobbies, conference rooms, elevators, roofs, and cooling towers.
