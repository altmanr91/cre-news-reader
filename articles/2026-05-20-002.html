25 Kent Offers Companies A Cutting-Edge Office Experience Along The Williamsburg Waterfront

Commercial Observer — Wed, 20 May 2026 04:00:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Office Leasing

25 Kent, a 500,000-square-foot Class A office building on the Williamsburg waterfront in Brooklyn, is attracting a strong mix of creative and technology companies, particularly those in AI. The building offers flexible floor plans, high-end prebuilt spaces, and custom buildouts, allowing tenants to scale their operations. With amenities like a fitness center, rooftop terrace, and independent HVAC systems, 25 Kent provides a cutting-edge office experience in a desirable location with limited comparable Class A space. The building is currently 72% leased, with asking rents at $76 per square foot, demonstrating its appeal to companies seeking a dynamic live-work environment outside of Manhattan.
