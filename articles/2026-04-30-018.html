AvalonBay, Equity Residential in early merger talks

National - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 15:47:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Corporate Merger / Market Commentary

AvalonBay Communities and Equity Residential, two of the largest multifamily REITs, are reportedly in early discussions regarding a potential merger. This combination would create one of the largest real estate deals in U.S. history, significantly reshaping the rental housing market. Both companies have extensive portfolios across multiple states, and a merger could face antitrust scrutiny due to their substantial market shares.
