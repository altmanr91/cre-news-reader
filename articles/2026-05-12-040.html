Durst Markets 600K-SF Block of Midtown Space for Lease

Connect CRE — Mon, 11 May 2026 22:17:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Lease

The Durst Organization is marketing a 600,000-square-foot block of Class A office space at 114 W. 47th St. in Midtown, representing the largest contiguous availability in the submarket. The 26-story tower, originally built by Durst, offers a unique opportunity for a single tenant to occupy the entire building. The property is undergoing a significant renovation, including a reimagined lobby and amenity spaces designed by HOK, with the potential for building naming rights for a major tenant.
