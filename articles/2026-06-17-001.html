Colleen Wenke of Taconic Partners: 5 Questions

Commercial Observer — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 12:00:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Taconic Partners has successfully leased up The Ellery, a 330-unit luxury apartment tower in Times Square, demonstrating the viability of residential development in previously non-traditional neighborhoods. According to Colleen Wenke, president and COO, the project's success stems from creating a "calm oasis" within the bustling area, featuring amenities like an indoor garden and pool, coupled with excellent mass transit access. This approach caters to a growing demand for living close to work and highlights the potential for converting functionally obsolete Class B office buildings into residential uses to address New York City's housing shortage. Taconic is also seeing strong performance at The Miramar, a mixed-income development in Inwood, which benefits from transit access, waterfront proximity, and relative affordability within Manhattan.
