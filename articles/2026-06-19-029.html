Oakley Home Builders snags Burr Ridge equestrian estate for well below $10M ask

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 15:39:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Oakley Home Builders acquired Saddle Hill Farm, a 7.6-acre equestrian estate in Burr Ridge, IL, for $6 million, representing a 40 percent discount from its original $10 million asking price. The developer plans to demolish the existing structures and construct a 16-home active-adult subdivision for buyers aged 55 and older. The project will feature 3,700-square-foot homes with first-floor primary suites and will include 1.5 acres of green space, a pond, pocket parks, and a walking path, pending a necessary zoning change.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The $6 million closing is the second-priciest Burr Ridge transaction in five years, following a $6.9 million sale in 2022. Developer appetite for multi-acre estates in Burr Ridge has increased, with another 4.7-acre property on Madison Street selling for $3.5 million in February.
