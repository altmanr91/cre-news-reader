Interra Realty closes sale of two multifamily buildings in Chicago market

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 11 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Interra Realty recently facilitated the sale of two fully occupied multifamily buildings in the Chicago market for a combined total of $2.98 million. The transactions included a six-unit property in Schaumburg, which sold for $1.08 million, and a 12-unit building in Chicago's O'Hare submarket, which traded for $1.9 million. Both properties were fully occupied at the time of sale, reflecting strong demand in the area.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Units: 18

Sale Price: $3.0M

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Notable Features: The Schaumburg property includes six two-bedroom apartments with balconies or terraces, in-unit laundry, and two assigned parking spaces, located near downtown Schaumburg and Woodfield Mall. The Chicago property features one studio and 11 one-bedroom layouts, most with balconies, and is close to various retail, dining, and entertainment options, as well as O'Hare International Airport.

Project Notes: The 8755 W. Foster Ave. property had not traded hands in nearly 40 years.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Interra Realty — Patrick Kennelly, Managing Partner; Paul Waterloo, Managing Partner; Nathan Zito, Director

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Interra Realty has closed eight multifamily deals in Chicago's O'Hare submarket since 2023.
