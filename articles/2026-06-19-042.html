ICE Looks To Offload $700M In U.S. Warehouses As Detention Overhaul Unravels

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 15:31:08 -0400

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The Department of Homeland Security is reversing its controversial plan to convert industrial warehouses into detention facilities, now seeking to sell or transfer seven of the 11 properties it acquired for over $700 million. This marks a significant shift from the agency's initial $38.3 billion expansion strategy, which aimed to increase detention capacity nationwide. The decision follows a halt to the program and an inspector general probe into its implementation, as well as legal challenges in several states regarding environmental reviews and property conversions. The properties, which were purchased at a premium, now face an uncertain return to the market, potentially at a discount due to their association with the federal government's contested real estate initiative.
