AI’s Dark Side: Flagging Legitimate Multifamily Renter Applicants

Connect CRE — Fri, 01 May 2026 05:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article explores the challenges multifamily owner-operators face when using artificial intelligence (AI) systems for tenant screening, highlighting the potential for these systems to unfairly flag legitimate applicants. Steve Carroll, Co-Founder and CEO of Findigs, discusses how AI biases can lead to incorrect responses and fair housing issues, emphasizing the need for operators to monitor approval rates, applicant disputes, and ensure vendors provide transparency and impact testing. He also notes the increasing demand from owner-operators for accountability and guarantees from screening platforms, citing recent legal precedents and regulatory guidance from HUD and state-level legislation like Colorado's Artificial Intelligence Act.
