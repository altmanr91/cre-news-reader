Paul Singer’s Elliott Management Buys Coconut Grove Hotel for $110M: Updated

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 17:45:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Elliott Management, led by Paul Singer, acquired the Mayfair House Hotel & Garden in Miami's Coconut Grove from Brookfield Asset Management for approximately $110 million. The 180-room hotel, originally built in 1982, was previously purchased by Brookfield in 2019 for $40 million. Acore Capital provided a $79 million loan to the buyer for the transaction.
