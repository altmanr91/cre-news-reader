The Plan: In Long Island City, an Old Paint Factory Pops as Condos

Commercial Observer — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 10:00:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

ZD Jasper Realty is developing the Paragon, a 23-story, 182-unit luxury condominium project in Long Island City, Queens. Located at 40-45 Vernon Boulevard, the building is on the site of a former paint factory and offers residences ranging from studios to four-bedrooms, priced from $685,000 to $3.5 million. The project aims to blend the area's industrial heritage with modern residential needs, featuring bold design elements and a wide array of amenities, including a landscaped rooftop, sky lounge, fitness center, pickleball court, and children's play center. Sales have been strong, particularly for larger units, indicating a trend of young families choosing to remain in Long Island City.
