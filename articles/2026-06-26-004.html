S3 Capital Lends $101M on Luxury Resort Project Near Orlando

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 16:50:49 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Urban Network Capital Group and Vertical Developments secured $101 million in construction financing for the Visions Orlando Resort & Spa, a master-planned luxury resort community in Davenport, FL. S3 Capital provided the loan for the 70-acre project, which will feature single-family homes, townhomes, condos, and condo-hotel units, alongside extensive resident amenities and retail. The project's presale-driven capitalization and strong tourism dynamics in the Orlando market were key factors in the lender's decision.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Visions Orlando Resort & Spa

Address: 7007 Osceola Polk Line Road, Davenport, FL

Loan Amount: $101.0M

Loan/Acre: $1,442,857/acre (calculated)

Loan/Land SF: $33.12/land SF (calculated)

Site Area: 70 acres

Notable Features: Master-planned luxury resort community with a 10,000-square-foot clubhouse, fitness center, pool, sports courts, wellness facilities, restaurant, bar, movie theater, and golf simulator.

Project Notes: Phases I, II, III, and V, totaling 460 residential units, have sold out. Phase IV includes 277 condo-hotel units. The community is expected to expand through Phase VIII and will include a 4-acre commercial component.
