Blackstone hit by foreclosure on $90M loan for North Dallas apartments

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Blackstone faces foreclosure on a $90 million loan for the 75 West Apartments in North Dallas, with Ares Management initiating the foreclosure proceedings. The alleged default on the 490-unit multifamily property, which Blackstone acquired in late 2021, highlights the challenges in the Dallas-Fort Worth multifamily market, particularly for Class B assets, due to a significant influx of new supply that has suppressed rents. The property, built in 2000, is currently offering concessions to attract tenants.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Dallas-Fort Worth multifamily market has seen over 30,000 new apartment units delivered annually, leading to a 1.9 percent drop in rent rates over the last 12 months, compared to a 0.2 percent national increase. The region had nearly 43,000 units in the pipeline at the beginning of the year.
