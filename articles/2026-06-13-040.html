Clovis 7-Eleven C-Store/Gas Station Trades in 1031 Exchange

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 20:51:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Hanley Investment Group Real Estate Advisors has completed the $5 million sale of a 7-Eleven convenience store and gas station in Clovis, CA. The single-tenant, fee-simple property was acquired by a 1031 exchange investor from Bakersfield. The transaction highlights the strong demand from private investors for properties offering bonus depreciation eligibility, absolute triple-net lease structures, and strong tenant performance, according to the seller's broker.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: 7-Eleven C-Store/Gas Station

Address: 394 W. Ashlan Ave, Clovis, CA

Sale Price: $5.0M

Notable Features: Absolute triple-net lease structure; grocer anchor.

Project Notes: Acquired by a 1031 exchange investor; eligible for 100% bonus depreciation.
