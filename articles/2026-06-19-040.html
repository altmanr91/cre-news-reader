Survey Reports Robust Leasing and Design Investments in the Legal Office Sector

Connect CRE — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 21:37:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Legal Sector Report

The legal sector is demonstrating robust performance in the office market, driven by strong demand for services, revenue growth, and a commitment to in-person work. A recent survey by Cushman & Wakefield indicates that 4.5 million square feet of legal office space was leased in Q1 2026, with firms increasingly expanding and facing higher renewal rates due to tight supply. Law firms are prioritizing office design that emphasizes employee well-being, collaborative spaces, and areas for focused work, while also integrating technology like artificial intelligence for document generation and analytics, despite concerns about AI's accuracy. The sector anticipates continued employment growth and geographic expansion, with successful firms expected to align their real estate, workforce, and technology strategies.

DATA POINTS

Legal space leased in Q1 2026: 4.5 million square feet

Law firms requiring 3+ in-office days per week: 9 out of 10

Law firms using AI: 62%

Law firms planning to adopt AI: 21%

AI hallucinations in court documents (2025 vs 2024): 10 times higher
