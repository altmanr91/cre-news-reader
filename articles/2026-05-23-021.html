Cain, Alchemy-ABR score $321M refi for Billionaires’ Row office tower

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 22 May 2026 17:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Cain and Alchemy-ABR Investment Partners secured a $321 million refinancing package for their recently completed 30-story office tower at 125 West 57th Street in New York City. The financing, provided by JPMorgan Chase and Hudson Bay Capital, replaces a $250 million construction loan. The Class A tower, built on spec, has attracted strong tenant interest with recent leases exceeding $300 per square foot, reflecting the robust demand for trophy office space in Manhattan's Plaza District.
