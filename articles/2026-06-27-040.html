Walker & Dunlop Arranges Wells Fargo Refi for Rosemead Center

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 20:15:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Walker & Dunlop has arranged a $46 million refinance loan for Rosemead Place, a 336,718-square-foot retail center in Rosemead, CA. The fixed-rate, interest-only permanent debt was provided by Wells Fargo on behalf of a family office. The transaction closed in less than 60 days despite some near-term lease rollover, demonstrating strong lender confidence in well-located, anchored retail assets.
