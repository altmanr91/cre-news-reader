Distress wave hits S2 Capital with $250M in CMBS loans flagged for special servicing

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 28 May 2026 14:15:10 +0000

SUMMARY

REO / Foreclosure

Over $250 million in CMBS loans tied to three multifamily properties owned by S2 Capital have been transferred to special servicing due to underperformance. The properties, located in Grand Prairie, TX, Houston, TX, and Glendale, AZ, failed to maintain net cash flow at the levels required by their underwriting. This financial distress follows a recent capital call by S2 Capital, which aimed to raise $70 million in preferred equity but secured only $30 million, leading to warnings of a full loss of capital for equity investors in the REIT.
