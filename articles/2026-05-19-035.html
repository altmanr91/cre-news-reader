Rubicon Point wades into Santa Clara with $87M office campus pickup

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 18 May 2026 22:05:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Rubicon Point Partners has acquired a four-building office campus in Santa Clara, CA, for $87.2 million, marking a significant entry into the South Bay market. The 201,100-square-foot complex, fully occupied by a medical device manufacturer, was purchased at a discount to its assessed value. This acquisition represents the initial phase of Rubicon Point's planned billion-dollar investment strategy across the Bay Area.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 3003 Bunker Hill Lane, Santa Clara, CA

Size: 201,100 SF

Sale Price: $87.2M

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Project Notes: The sale price was 15.7% less than the assessed value of $103.4 million.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Rubicon Point Partners — Ani Vartanian, Co-founder

SELLER: TG USA Development

LENDER: BMO Bank

SELLER BROKER: CBRE

TENANTS AT PROPERTY

Shockwave Medical

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Bay Area office market has experienced a value tumble after a post-pandemic peak.
