Gould Property Co., Konterra Realty Break Ground on 400-Acre MD Project

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Tue, 19 May 2026 20:27:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Gould Property Co. and Konterra Realty have broken ground on the initial phase of the Konterra Town Center East project in Prince George's County, Maryland. This first phase includes the construction of 220 townhomes, with the first units expected to deliver in fall 2026, and two apartment buildings totaling 500 units, slated for completion in 2028. The development is part of a larger 1,400-acre master-planned community, transforming a former mining site into a significant mixed-use destination.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Konterra Town Center East

Units: 720

Site Area: 400 acres

Notable Features: Part of a larger 1,400-acre master-planned community on both sides of I-95, on a site formerly used for sand and gravel mining operations.

Project Notes: The Gould family has owned the land since the early 1980s. The first phase includes 220 townhomes and 500 apartment units.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Gould Property Co.

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Konterra Realty

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Caruso Homes

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Bozzuto Construction
