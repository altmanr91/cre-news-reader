JLL Negotiates 691,667 SF Industrial Lease in Adairsville, Georgia

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 15:53:21 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has brokered a full-building industrial lease for Pactra USA Inc., a third-party logistics firm, at the International Commerce Center in Adairsville, Georgia. The facility offers modern specifications and strategic access to major transportation infrastructure, which was a key factor in securing the large-scale distribution space.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: International Commerce Center

Address: 7634 Adairsville Hwy, Adairsville, GA*

Size: 691,667 SF

Notable Features: Features 40-foot clear heights, 124 dock-high doors, four drive-in doors, 190-foot truck courts, 388 auto parking spaces, and 254 trailer parking spaces, with proximity to the I-75 corridor and the Appalachian Regional Port.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
