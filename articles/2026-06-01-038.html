Crescent Debuts 151-Unit Lawrenceville BTR Community

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:15:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Crescent Communities has opened Harmon Cedar Run, a 151-unit build-to-rent community in Lawrenceville, GA. This project marks Crescent's 13th multifamily investment in the Atlanta market and its first under the Harmon brand. The community offers a mix of townhomes and single-family houses for rent, featuring two-car garages and a range of amenities including a pickleball court and EV charging.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Harmon Cedar Run

Address: 1575 Cedars Rd SE, Lawrenceville, GA*

Units: 151

Notable Features: Includes a mix of townhomes and single-family houses, two-car garages, pickleball and basketball court, large pool, playground, pet spaces, and EV outlets in each garage.

Project Notes: This is the first project under Crescent Communities' Harmon brand. The Harmon portfolio is supported by a joint venture partnership with an account managed by Heitman LLC, which recently increased its commitment from $235 million to $345 million.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
