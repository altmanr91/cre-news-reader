SL Green Sells 49% Stake in 346 Madison Avenue to Mori Building Co.

SALE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 27 May 2026 22:08:40 +0000

SUMMARY

SL Green Realty has sold a 49% stake in 346 Madison Avenue to Mori Building Company, valuing the office tower at $175 million. The two companies will form a joint venture to develop a new 46-story, 850,000-square-foot office tower at the Midtown site. SL Green will retain a 51% stake and manage the development and leasing, aiming to meet strong tenant demand for high-quality, well-located office space in East Midtown.
