Major Anthropic backer ditches Silicon Valley HQ for downtown SF

LEASE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 07 May 2026 19:43:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Lightspeed Venture Partners, a major investor in AI firms, is relocating its headquarters from Silicon Valley to downtown San Francisco, signing a 12-year lease for over 43,000 square feet at 149 New Montgomery Street. This move signifies the growing influence of the artificial intelligence sector on San Francisco's office market, as AI companies continue to expand and compete for space. The firm will maintain a presence in Menlo Park and will be closer to many of its AI portfolio companies.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 149 New Montgomery Street, San Francisco, CA

Notable Features: The building was acquired through a deed-in-lieu of foreclosure in September.

Project Notes: Lightspeed Venture Partners will maintain a presence at 2200 Sand Hill Road in Menlo Park.
