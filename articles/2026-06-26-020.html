Donald Trump Jr, wife put down roots in West Palm Beach

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Donald Trump Jr. and Bettina Anderson have purchased a double lot in West Palm Beach's Prospect Park neighborhood, where they intend to construct an eight-bedroom residence. This acquisition follows their recent marriage and marks their decision to establish roots in the area. The move also signifies a continued expansion of the Trump family's real estate footprint in South Florida.

Notable Features: Double lot intended for an eight-bedroom home, suitable for entertaining and political fundraisers.
