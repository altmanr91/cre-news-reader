“Reckless”: George Pino breaks down at start of his manslaughter trial over 2022 boat crash

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 22:30:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

George Pino, CEO of State Street Realty, experienced an emotional breakdown on the first day of his manslaughter trial related to a 2022 boat crash that resulted in the death of a 17-year-old girl. Prosecutors allege Pino was reckless and negligent, piloting his boat erratically and accelerating before crashing into a channel marker in Biscayne Bay. The defense argues Pino would never intentionally endanger lives and had safely navigated the channel many times before.
