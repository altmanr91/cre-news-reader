Realty Income scoops up Chicagoland industrial center for $124 million

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 26 May 2026 17:33:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Realty Income acquired a 910,800-square-foot industrial warehouse in University Park, IL, for $124 million. The property, fully leased to Central Steel & Wire, was sold by Affinius Capital and Venture One Real Estate. This transaction represents the largest single-property industrial sale in Chicagoland in the past five years, excluding data centers.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

This transaction is the largest industrial sector sale in Chicagoland in more than five years, not involving a data center. The last deal to exceed it was a $126.5 million purchase of a Michelin tire distribution center in Wilmington five years ago. Venture One Real Estate recently sold a 32-property portfolio for over $200 million to DRA Advisors and a 54-property portfolio for $369 million in 2023, with at least 40 properties in the Chicago area.
