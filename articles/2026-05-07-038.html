Foreclosure imminent for Bechtel’s old Houston office

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 06 May 2026 18:23:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Lenders are initiating foreclosure proceedings on the 19-story, 441,523-square-foot office tower at 3000 Post Oak Boulevard in Houston, owned by Korea-based AIP Asset Management and Stamford, Connecticut-based Five Mile Capital Partners. The building has faced significant challenges since its anchor tenant, an engineering and construction company, vacated in 2023, leaving the property largely empty. The $80 million loan, originated by JPMorgan Chase & Company in 2020, matured in March 2025 and had been transferred to special servicing twice due to the tenant's departure and the borrowers' payment struggles.
