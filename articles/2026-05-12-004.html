KeyBank Provides $54M Refi for 298-Unit Houston-Area Multifamily

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 11 May 2026 15:41:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Rockstar Capital has secured a $54 million fixed-rate refinancing for Lakeview at Westpark, a 298-unit multifamily property in Richmond, TX. KeyBank provided the 35-year loan, which was arranged by Marcus & Millichap Capital. The financing converted a floating-rate bridge loan into federal HUD financing, structured in conjunction with a local Housing Finance Corporation, demonstrating strong lender confidence in the Houston-area submarket.
