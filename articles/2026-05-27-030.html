Wicker Park Connection buildings list in West Town amid selling spree

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 26 May 2026 19:42:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The Wicker Park Connection, a two-building mixed-use property in Chicago's West Town, has been listed for sale. Developed by Centrum Realty & Development's Arthur Slaven and John McLinden, the property includes 207 apartment units and 23,855 square feet of retail space, with a significant portion occupied by a major retailer. Brokers are highlighting the opportunity for continued rent growth in the area, as the listing comes amidst a surge in multifamily property sales in Chicagoland driven by strong rent growth and limited new construction.

DATA POINTS

Chicagoland property sales increase (past year): 30 percent

Chicagoland property sales increase vs. national rates: 25 percent more

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Chicagoland multifamily landlords are actively listing properties due to swelling rent growth and a lag in new construction. Chicago property sales have increased by 30% in the past year, outpacing national rates by 25%.
