Life Time inks NYC’s biggest retail deal in April

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Lease Roundup

Life Time signed the largest retail lease in New York City in April, taking 71,000 square feet in a new development in Williamsburg. Other significant retail leases included a 22,000-square-foot restaurant concept in the Plaza District, a 16,000-square-foot clothing brand lease in the Upper West Side, and a 15,300-square-foot relocation for another clothing retailer in Grand Central. The activity highlights continued demand across various retail categories in key Manhattan and outer-borough submarkets.
