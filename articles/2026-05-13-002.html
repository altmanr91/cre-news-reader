Sun River Health Renews 7K-SF Medical Office at Simone’s 2510 Westchester Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Tue, 12 May 2026 20:45:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Sun River Health has renewed its 6,704-square-foot medical office lease at 2510 Westchester Avenue in the Bronx, maintaining full occupancy at the three-story building. The long-term renewal underscores strong tenant retention and sustained demand for high-quality medical office space in the Bronx market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Address: 2510 Westchester Avenue, Bronx, NY

Size: 6,704 SF

Notable Features: Property is a three-story building located between Seabury and Commerce avenues in Westchester Square.

Project Notes: Sun River Health has occupied the space since at least 2020, following a merger.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LANDLORD: Simone Development Companies — Joe Simone, President

TENANT: Sun River Health

TENANT REP: JLL — Michael A. Aievoli

TENANTS AT PROPERTY

Sun River Health

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Average asking rent for medical office space in the Bronx ranges between $30 and $60 per square foot.
