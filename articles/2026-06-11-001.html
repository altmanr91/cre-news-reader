New York City Is Leading the U.S. in Apartment Construction. Yes, Really.

Commercial Observer — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 12:00:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

New York City is experiencing a significant surge in apartment construction, leading the nation in multifamily units under construction in Q1 2026, despite its high building costs and low vacancy rates. This boom is largely attributed to city and state initiatives, including residential-friendly rezonings in areas like Gowanus and Long Island City, and tax incentives such as the 467-m tax break for conversions. While 2025 saw the highest number of housing units completed since 1965, experts suggest that sustained policy changes, including further aggressive rezonings and improved tax incentives beyond the current 485-x program, are crucial to address the city's long-term housing shortage and maintain this development momentum.
