Menashe Properties closes on another Loop tower for $57M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 21:09:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Menashe Properties has acquired 180 North LaSalle Street, a 38-story office tower in Chicago, for $56.5 million. This marks the firm's third office acquisition in Chicago in four years, demonstrating a strong belief in the market's recovery. The buyer is banking on the "Google effect" from the nearby Thompson Center redevelopment to attract new tenants, with Stream Realty Partners continuing to manage the property's business operations.
