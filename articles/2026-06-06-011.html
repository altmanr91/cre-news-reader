Entre Commercial Realty closes 55,488-square-foot industrial lease in Lake Zurich

LEASE

REJournals — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Entre Commercial Realty facilitated a 55,488-square-foot industrial lease at 1290 Ensell Road in Lake Zurich, Illinois. The tenant, represented by Entre Commercial, sought a larger facility to accommodate its expansion requirements. The property's features, including clear height ceilings and loading docks, made it a suitable choice for the tenant's operational needs.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 1290 Ensell Road, Lake Zurich, IL

Size: 55,488 SF

Notable Features: Features 25-foot clear height ceilings, 8,248 square feet of office space, four interior loading docks, and three oversized drive-in doors.
