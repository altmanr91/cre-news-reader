Cooper Group Brokers $4.3M Sale of Retail Center in North Olmsted, Ohio

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 12:50:20 +0000

SUMMARY

The Cooper Commercial Investment Group has brokered the sale of Bentley Coe Plaza, a 36,966-square-foot retail center in North Olmsted, Ohio, for $4.3 million. The transaction was completed at a 7.4 percent capitalization rate, with the buyer identified prior to the property being formally marketed for sale.
