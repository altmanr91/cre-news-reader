Tishman Speyer nets Loop office wins with Databricks, Alliant leases

LEASE

Chicago Real Estate News - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Tishman Speyer secured two significant office leases at The Franklin, a 1.6 million-square-foot skyscraper in Chicago's Loop. Data and AI firm Databricks expanded its presence by leasing 50,000 square feet, while insurance brokerage Alliant committed to 20,000 square feet. These deals provide a crucial boost to the property's occupancy amidst a challenging downtown office market, demonstrating continued demand for Class A, amenity-rich spaces.
