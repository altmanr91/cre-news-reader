Ballast’s Pac Heights hot streak continues with another $35M apartment buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 08 May 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Ballast, a San Francisco-based multifamily owner-operator, has acquired the 80-unit Grosvenor Atrium at 1690 Broadway in Pacific Heights for $35.3 million. The seller was an affiliate of Grosvenor Properties, which is divesting multifamily assets in the city. This acquisition continues Ballast's active buying streak in San Francisco, often in partnership with Carlyle Group, and reflects a broader institutional bet on the city's post-pandemic apartment market recovery.
