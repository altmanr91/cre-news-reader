Stream Realty Partners celebrates groundbreaking of Northfield Logistics Center in Houston

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 15 May 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Stream Realty Partners has broken ground on Northfield Logistics Center, a new Class A industrial development in Houston's infill Northwest submarket. The project will deliver over 300,000 square feet of distribution space in a supply-constrained corridor, addressing strong occupier demand for modern logistics facilities. The development is strategically located with excellent access to major transportation routes and a dense labor pool, making it a significant addition to the area's industrial offerings.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Northfield Logistics Center

Address: 4330 Northfield Lane, Houston, TX

Size: 300,000 SF

Completion: Q1 2027

Notable Features: Class A distribution space with direct access to U.S. 290 via W 43rd Street and secondary access at Langtry Street, located within a dense labor pool and surrounded by retail and dining amenities.
