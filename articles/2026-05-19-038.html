Haussmann Development Closes on Construction Loan for Manhattanville Mixed-Income

LOAN

Connect CRE — Mon, 18 May 2026 21:47:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Haussmann Development secured a $40.75 million construction loan from Affinius Capital and Axonic Capital for an 84-unit mixed-income multifamily building at 16-20 Convent Ave. in Manhattanville. The project, located in an Opportunity Zone near Columbia University, will provide permanently affordable housing units under New York City's 485-x tax abatement program and utilize the Universal Affordability Preference density bonus. This marks Haussmann Development's fifth project in Northern Manhattan.
