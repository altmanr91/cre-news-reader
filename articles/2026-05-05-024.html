Adam Neumann’s Flow buys 50% stake in Wynwood apartments

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 04 May 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Adam Neumann's Flow has acquired a 50% stake in Society Wynwood, a 318-unit multifamily property in Miami, from PMG. Flow will now co-own the asset with Greybrook, with plans to renovate and rebrand the property as Flow Wynwood. The acquisition involved an initial $15 million investment, with an additional $10 million allocated for capital expenditures and rebranding efforts.

Project Notes: The property was rebranded as Flow Wynwood and came under Flow's management immediately after the acquisition. The forecasted net operating income from the property is estimated at $300 million.
