InterFace Panel: Affordable Housing Deal Flow Gains Momentum as Brokers Turn Bullish

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 11:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The affordable housing transaction market in the Southeast is experiencing a significant resurgence after a period of slower activity, driven by strong renter demand, persistent housing shortages, and improving capital market conditions. Despite ongoing challenges such as elevated borrowing costs and interest rate volatility, investors are showing renewed urgency to deploy capital, leading to a notable increase in transaction volume. Brokers and developers are adapting to a "new normal" where traditional refinancing options are less available, pushing owners to sell legacy assets, particularly older acquisition-rehab properties that require substantial capital investment.

DATA POINTS
