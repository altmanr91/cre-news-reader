Atkins Diet: Zenith IOS’s CEO On Industrial’s Hottest Niche

Commercial Observer — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 10:30:46 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article features an interview with Ben Atkins, co-founder and CEO of Zenith IOS, an industrial outdoor storage (IOS) investment and management platform. Atkins discusses his career trajectory from law to commercial real estate, including his time at Simpson Thacher & Bartlett and Vornado, before co-founding Zenith IOS. He highlights the firm's rapid growth to over 100 properties and $1.2 billion in asset value within six years, attributing success to early identification of the IOS sector's institutional potential and strategic joint ventures with J.P. Morgan Asset Management, as well as a significant equity partnership for acquisitions and development.
