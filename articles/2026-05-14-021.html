NYC’s top deals: Coney Island rehab center trades for $17M

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 13 May 2026 12:28:26 +0000

SUMMARY

A drug and alcohol rehabilitation center in Coney Island, Brooklyn, was sold for $17.2 million. The 40,300-square-foot, four-story property at 2316 Surf Avenue was acquired by an LLC tied to Joel Basch from King David of Brooklyn, an entity associated with nursing home operator Robert Lichtschein. This transaction represents the highest-priced commercial deal recorded in New York City during the reporting period.
