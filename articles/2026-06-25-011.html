Newmark negotiates sale for advanced manufacturing facility in Rossford

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 10:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Newmark negotiated a development agreement and sale for a new 563,803-square-foot advanced specialized manufacturing facility in Rossford, Ohio. The facility, which includes a 250,000-square-foot expansion pad on 53 acres, will be developed by Scannell Properties as a build-to-suit for OPmobility. This project is expected to create up to 1,000 new jobs and was facilitated by state and local incentive packages, underscoring the region's appeal for significant corporate investment.

DATA POINTS
