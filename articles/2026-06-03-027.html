TPG Angelo Gordon offloads 5-building Chicago industrial portfolio for $70M

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 22:18:07 +0000

SUMMARY

TPG Angelo Gordon has sold a five-building industrial portfolio in the Chicago area to Cannon Industrial for $70 million. The portfolio includes properties in Chicago, Wheeling, and Broadview. While the overall profit for TPG Angelo Gordon on this portfolio is unclear, the transaction occurs during a period of significant activity in Chicago's industrial sector, which has seen a surge in inventory and strong investor interest.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Sale Price: $70.0M

Project Notes: The portfolio includes properties at 3700 South Kedzie Avenue, 3200 South Kilbourn Avenue, 4400 West 25th Place in Chicago; 1351 Wheeling Road in Wheeling; and 1900 25th Avenue in Broadview. TPG Angelo Gordon's predecessor, Angelo Gordon & Company, acquired the 3700 South Kedzie property for approximately $20 million in 2018 and the South Kilbourn and West 25th Place properties for a combined $26 million in 2019. TPG acquired Angelo Gordon in 2023. At least one property was subject to a sale-leaseback agreement.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: TPG Angelo Gordon

BUYER: Cannon Industrial

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Chicago's industrial market saw a 10 basis point improvement in vacancy quarter-over-quarter to 5.9% in Q1 2026, compared to just over 6% in Q1 2020.
