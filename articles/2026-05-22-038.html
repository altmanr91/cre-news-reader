Sand Hill affiliate plots hundreds of apartments, townhomes in East Palo Alto

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 21 May 2026 21:31:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

An affiliate of Sand Hill Property Company, Woodland Park Communities, has submitted pre-applications for two significant residential projects in East Palo Alto, proposing nearly 800 new apartments and townhomes. The O’Keefe-Manhattan and West Bayshore-Newell Improvements projects aim to renovate existing apartments, construct new for-sale townhomes, and develop new apartment buildings across multiple sites. The developer, which has owned the properties since 2016, plans to avoid displacing current tenants by offering replacement rent-controlled units at stabilized rates, with formal applications anticipated this summer and construction phased over several years.
