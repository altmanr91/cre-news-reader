Lee & Associates Negotiates Sale of Four Industrial Outdoor Storage Sites in Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 18 May 2026 14:15:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates has brokered the sale of a four-property industrial outdoor storage (IOS) portfolio across Texas, totaling 56.2 acres and 230,000 square feet. The properties are located in Dallas, Houston, San Antonio, and Austin. Transport Properties acquired the portfolio, which also included an additional site in Chicago, though the seller and sale price were not disclosed.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Size: 230,000 SF

Site Area: 56.2 acres

Project Notes: Portfolio included an additional industrial outdoor storage facility in Chicago.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Transport Properties

BUYER BROKER: Lee & Associates — Alex Wilson
