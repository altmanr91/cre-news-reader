Korean Chef Jiho Kim to Open Flagship Restaurant at 21 West 17th Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 17:40:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Korean chef Jiho Kim has secured 3,120 square feet at 21 West 17th Street in Manhattan's Flatiron District for his new flagship restaurant, Jiho. The New American eatery with a Korean-inspired menu will occupy ground floor and basement space. The landlord was represented by Ripco Real Estate, while Meridian Capital Group negotiated on behalf of the tenant.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 21 West 17th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 3,120 SF

Rental Rate: $120.00/SF/yr

Year Built: 2024

Notable Features: Located within Louie, a two-building condominium complex developed by Vinbaytel Property Development, which includes 21 full-floor condo units.

Project Notes: The building's second retail space at 16 West 18th Street was leased to chef Andrew Sargent earlier in June 2026.
