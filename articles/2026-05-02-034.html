Bay Club buys entire city block in Northern Waterfront for $65M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:53:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Bay Club, a fitness and hospitality chain, has acquired three adjacent parcels in San Francisco's Northern Waterfront for $65 million, securing the entire city block surrounding its flagship location. The acquisition from LaSalle Investment Management includes a 130-year-old building, Bay Club's existing facility, and a parking garage, totaling over 150,000 square feet. This purchase solidifies Bay Club's long-term commitment to San Francisco, with plans to expand its footprint at the site.
