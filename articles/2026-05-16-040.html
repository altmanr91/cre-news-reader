Chicago-Based Stotan Industrial Launches NASA Causeway Logistics Center in Florida

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:10:08 +0000

SUMMARY

Stotan Industrial, in partnership with CrossHarbor Capital Partners, has acquired 28.63 acres in Titusville, Florida, for the development of the NASA Causeway Logistics Center. This project will feature two industrial buildings totaling 306,980 square feet, strategically located near the Kennedy Space Center and other key infrastructure. The development aims to capitalize on the strong regional demand driven by major space, port, and aviation operators.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: NASA Causeway Logistics Center

Size: 306,980 SF

Site Area: 28.63 acres

Notable Features: Located within Vector Space Business Park, adjacent to the NASA Causeway and approximately five miles from the Kennedy Space Center, providing access to Space Coast's launch, port, aviation, and highway infrastructure.

Project Notes: The project will include two divisible industrial buildings: Building A (215,460 SF) and Building B (91,520 SF).

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Stotan Industrial

EQUITY/FINANCING: CrossHarbor Capital Partners

SELLER BROKER: Lightle Beckner Robison
