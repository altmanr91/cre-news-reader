KKR lands $600M refi for OpenAI-leased Mission Bay office

REFINANCE

San Francisco - The Real Deal — Wed, 29 Apr 2026 18:40:14 +0000

SUMMARY

KKR has secured a $600 million refinancing package for its four-building office complex at 1800 Owens Street in San Francisco's Mission Bay. The new debt, provided by a syndicate of lenders, replaces an existing loan of the same amount. The property, which KKR acquired in 2021 for over $1 billion, recently saw a significant portion of its space subleased to a major artificial intelligence company, which is expanding its footprint in the area.
