Aaron Amitin of Good Company: 5 Questions

Commercial Observer — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 12:00:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Good Company, a leasing, marketing, and management services firm launched by The Domain Companies in January 2025, has successfully leased up over 2,000 new development units in New York City within its first 18 months. The firm, which operates as a 'one-stop shop' for real estate services, primarily serves Domain's extensive housing portfolio but has also expanded into third-party brokerage, leveraging its integrated operations and ownership mentality to outperform submarkets. Good Company's success includes the lease-up of a 600-unit housing project in Jamaica, Queens, for partner Goldman Sachs, demonstrating its ability to replicate its strategies for external clients.
