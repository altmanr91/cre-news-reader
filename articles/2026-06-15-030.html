Dalfen Adds 419K-SF Broward County Industrial Portfolio to Warehouse Holdings

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:07:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Dalfen Industrial has acquired a nine-building industrial portfolio in Broward County, Florida, from Blackstone and Link Logistics for $99.6 million. The 419,253-square-foot Broward Logistics Portfolio is 83% leased and offers strong connectivity to major transportation arteries and logistics hubs, making it attractive for distribution and trade-related users in South Florida. This acquisition expands Dalfen Industrial's holdings in the Southeast to 11 million square feet.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Broward Logistics Portfolio

Size: 419,253 SF

Sale Price: $99.6M

Occupancy at Sale: 83.0%

Notable Features: Portfolio features buildings ranging from 10,000 to 68,500 square feet, with direct access to Interstate 95 and the Florida Turnpike, and close proximity to Port Everglades and Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport.

Project Notes: Acquired at 55% replacement cost.
