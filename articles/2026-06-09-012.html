St. Louis’ Case Engineering adds three to its roster

REJournals — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 10:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Case Engineering, a St. Louis-based firm, has expanded its team by hiring three new engineers. Adam Barney joins as an Electrical Engineer, Luke Markovich as a Mechanical Engineer, and Spencer Bearden as a Structural Engineer. These additions bring a mix of recent graduates and experienced professionals to the firm, enhancing its capabilities across various engineering disciplines.
