Academy Sports + Outdoors to Open 130,000 SF Store in Fairview, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 01 May 2026 14:03:15 +0000

SUMMARY

Academy Sports + Outdoors is set to open a new 130,000-square-foot retail store in Fairview, Texas, a suburb northeast of Dallas. The retailer will occupy a former department store space within the Fairview Town Center, a large power center. The lease was facilitated by the Fairview Economic Development Corp., and the store is expected to open in the coming weeks following ongoing remodeling work.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Fairview Town Center

Size: 130,000 SF

Year Built: 2009

Notable Features: Located within a 921,075-square-foot power center.

Project Notes: The store will backfill a former Macy's location.
