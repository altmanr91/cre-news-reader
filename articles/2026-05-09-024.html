Dog track site goes to resi in Kadima Developers’ Hallandale Beach plans

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 08 May 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Kadima Developers and Persea Ventures are planning Greenzone, a mixed-use development in Hallandale Beach, FL, on a portion of the former Big Easy Casino parking lot. The project will feature two eight-story buildings with 278 residential units and nearly 18,000 square feet of retail space. Kadima Developers previously acquired a 12-acre portion of the former greyhound racing site from Jeffrey Soffer for $31.1 million in December, subsequently flipping the 3.1-acre project site to Persea Ventures for $12 million.
