Bawabeh seeks to rezone East Flatbush development site

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 04 May 2026 19:13:02 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Bawabeh Holdings is seeking a rezoning approval for a nine-building, 1.2 million-square-foot development in East Flatbush, Brooklyn. The project aims to deliver 972 housing units, with 194 to 292 units designated as affordable housing. The site is currently zoned for industrial use, necessitating the rezoning for the proposed 14- to 15-story residential buildings. The development is planned in two phases, with completion anticipated by 2035.
