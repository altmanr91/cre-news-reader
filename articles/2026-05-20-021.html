Vinayaka Hospitality snags “Hotel of Presidents” for $43M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 19 May 2026 20:44:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Vinayaka Hospitality has acquired the historic 355-key Blackstone hotel in Chicago's Loop for $43 million from Fundamental Advisors and Sage Hospitality Group. The sale represents a significant discount from its previous purchase price in 2016, despite substantial renovations made by the sellers. The transaction occurs amidst a broader effort by Chicago's tourism board to boost the hospitality sector through a new surcharge.
