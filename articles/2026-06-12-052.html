Timberland REIT Selects Buckhead For HQ: The Atlanta Deal Sheet

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 16:46:00 -0400

SUMMARY

Rayonier Inc., a timberland REIT, is establishing its new headquarters at Terminus 100 in Buckhead, signing a 36,000 SF lease. This move follows its merger with PotlatchDeltic Corp. and strategically positions the company in the U.S. South, close to a significant portion of its landholdings. The new Atlanta headquarters is expected to open in the first quarter of 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Name: Terminus 100

Address: 3280 Peachtree Rd NE, Atlanta, GA*

Size: 36,000 SF

Notable Features: Located in the heart of Buckhead's financial district, a 27-story, 590,000 SF tower.
