South Florida’s top deals: McDonald’s franchisee sells Palm Beach estate for $37M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 19 May 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A waterfront estate in Palm Beach, Florida, was sold for $37.1 million by Violet Hill Development Company Ltd., an entity connected to the late McDonald's franchisee George Cohen. The 8,600-square-foot mansion, featuring six bedrooms and seven and a half bathrooms, was acquired by Elland Properties LLC. This transaction represents the top home sale recorded in South Florida for the period.
