BOMA of Greater Minneapolis elects board chair, directors

REJournals — Mon, 04 May 2026 10:04:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

The Building Owners and Managers Association (BOMA) of Greater Minneapolis has elected new leadership, with Raymond Vaughn of Ryan Companies named Board Chair. Additionally, Xue Yang of Hempel, Kellee Vinge of Capital Partners, and Umberto Rivera of Transwestern were elected to the Board of Directors. These new leaders are expected to bring diverse perspectives and experience to further BOMA's mission of providing education, networking, and advocacy for commercial real estate professionals.
