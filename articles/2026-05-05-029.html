KSL Capital Partners acquires Irving-based Invited Clubs

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 04 May 2026 21:34:47 +0000

SUMMARY

KSL Capital Partners has re-acquired Invited Clubs, a national operator of golf and membership clubs, from Apollo Global Management for approximately $3 billion, including debt. This marks the second time KSL has owned the company, which operates over 150 country and city clubs across the U.S. The transaction highlights continued private equity interest in the golf and leisure sector.

DATA POINTS

Property Name: Invited Clubs (corporate acquisition)

Sale Price: $3.00B

Notable Features: Invited Clubs operates over 150 country and city clubs with over 300,000 members.

Project Notes: KSL Capital Partners previously owned Invited Clubs (then ClubCorp) from 2006 to 2013, taking it public. Apollo Global Management took the company private in 2017 for $2.2 billion.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: KSL Capital Partners

SELLER: Apollo Global Management

OWNER: Invited Clubs
