REBNY Spotlights Creativity with 82nd Annual Most Ingenious Commercial Deal Awards

Connect CRE — Tue, 12 May 2026 21:52:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Association / Leadership News

The Real Estate Board of New York (REBNY) honored the most ingenious commercial real estate deals of 2025 at its 82nd annual awards. Geoffrey Newman of Savills won the top Henry Hart Rice Achievement Award for assembling a complex Gramercy Park frontage over 13 years, including acquiring a 34-unit cooperative building and utilizing cantilever rights. CBRE's team secured the second-place Robert T. Lawrence Memorial Award for orchestrating a 336,000-square-foot headquarters lease for Universal Music Group at PENN 2. Cushman & Wakefield's team earned the third-place Edward S. Gordon Memorial Award for representing Goodwin in a 300,000-square-foot lease at 200 Fifth Avenue, focusing on the law firm's long-term workplace strategy.
