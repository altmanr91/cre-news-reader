Hudson Valley Property Group Expands California Presence with Elk Grove Acquisition

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 22 May 2026 19:20:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Hudson Valley Property Group (HVPG) has acquired Mosa Apartment Homes, a newly constructed 387-unit affordable multifamily community in Elk Grove, CA, for $83.6 million, including assumed debt. This acquisition significantly expands HVPG's West Coast portfolio and its strategy to preserve affordability in newly delivered communities, complementing its work on repositioning legacy properties. The transaction involved three Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) partnerships, with HVPG acquiring the general partner interests.
