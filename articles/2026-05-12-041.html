Colliers Finalizes Apartment Sale in Supply-Constrained 605 Corridor

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 11 May 2026 22:02:33 +0000

SUMMARY

B.A.G. Investments, Inc. has acquired Parc @ 5, a 104-unit garden-style apartment community in North Downey, from Parc @ 5 Downey, LLC. The transaction represents a significant multifamily sale in the supply-constrained 605 Corridor submarket, attracting the buyer due to its strong cash flow and value-add potential. The property's size and quality in an infill location with high barriers to entry made it a rare and attractive investment.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Parc @ 5

Address: 9140 Brookshire Ave, Downey, CA*

Units: 104

Notable Features: Garden-style apartment community, one of the few 100-plus-unit communities in North Downey.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The 605 Corridor submarket is supply-constrained and consistently performs well for rent growth and investor returns due to limited new supply.
