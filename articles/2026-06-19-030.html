Local firms snag distressed Dallas-Fort Worth apartments after foreclosure sales

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 21:09:40 +0000

SUMMARY

Two distressed multifamily properties in North Texas, the 152-unit Apex Apartments in Fort Worth and the 149-unit Residences at White Rock Lake in Dallas, were recently sold after undergoing foreclosure auctions. The Residences at White Rock Lake was acquired by an entity linked to Ironridge Capital, while Apex Apartments was purchased by Ninety9 Capital. Both transactions involved the disposition of properties by entities that had previously acquired them through foreclosure.
