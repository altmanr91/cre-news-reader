Integritas Capital, Kriss Capital Provide $220M Construction Loan in Jersey City

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 19:11:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Integritas Capital and Kriss Capital have provided a $220 million construction loan for Imperial Tower, a 56-story mixed-use development in Jersey City's Journal Square neighborhood. The project, which will include residential units, a hotel, and retail space, is being developed by Kuldeep Kumar and Mohan Myneni. The financing supports a significantly upzoned project that will become one of Jersey City's tallest buildings, addressing the market's demand for housing and hospitality assets in a transit-oriented location.

Notable Features: 56-story mixed-use development with market-rate and affordable residences, a Marriott-branded hotel, and ground-floor retail; located next to the Journal Square PATH station; amenities include a rooftop infinity pool, fitness center, coworking lounges, theater room, library, children's playroom, and pet facilities.

Project Notes: Construction was paused at eight stories to pursue a significant upzoning, which doubled the size of the building columns from 18 inches to 36 inches to support the new height.
