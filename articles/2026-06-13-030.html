Prominent Chicago developer Richie Stein dies at 87

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 19:09:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Obituary

Richard "Richie" Stein, a prominent Chicago developer, passed away at 87. He was known for his risk-taking in business and his commitment to employing minority and woman-owned contractors. Stein & Company played a significant role in shaping Chicago's skyline, including the expansion of McCormick Place and the development of the United Center and AT&T Corporate Center. His work on the AT&T building notably championed diversity in contracting, setting a precedent for the city.
