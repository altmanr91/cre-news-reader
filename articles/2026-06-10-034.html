Texas’ top construction permits for the week ending June 9, 2026

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 19:28:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Houston First Corporation has filed plans for a $320 million expansion of the George R. Brown Convention Center in Houston, part of a larger $1 billion redevelopment. The project will involve interior build-out and exterior envelope construction for a 700,000-square-foot multipurpose section, with work scheduled from September 2026 to September 2028. This represents the largest construction permit filed in Texas for the week ending June 9, 2026.
