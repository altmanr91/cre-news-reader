Top Partners Industrial Broker Travis Land Dies At 45

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 20 May 2026 14:23:02 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Obituary

Travis Land, a prominent industrial broker and partner at Partners Real Estate, passed away at the age of 45 after a battle with cancer. Land was a top producer for the firm, achieving the No. 1 overall producer ranking in 2021, 2023, and 2024, and closing over 30M SF of transactions during his 22-year career. He was also a significant shareholder and board member, known for his sharp wit and insightful counsel.
