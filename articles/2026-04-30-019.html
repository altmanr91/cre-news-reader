Blackstone takes loss on Phoenix-area apartments after five years

SALE

National - The Real Deal — Wed, 29 Apr 2026 23:19:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Blackstone has sold the 412-unit Arrowhead Summit apartment complex in Glendale, Arizona, to an entity linked to NALS Apartment Homes for just under $101.4 million. This transaction represents an approximately 18 percent loss for Blackstone, which had acquired the property in 2021 for $123.6 million. The sale reflects broader challenges in the multifamily market, including elevated interest rates, moderated rent growth, and tighter capital markets compared to the robust conditions seen during the pandemic-era buying spree.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The sale of Arrowhead Summit is the third multifamily transaction above $100 million in the Phoenix area this year. Other notable sales include the 253-unit Cortland Biltmore for $125 million and the 2016-built Avant at Fashion Center for nearly $110.3 million.
