Olmstead Buys 19 West 44th Street From Savanna at a 45% Haircut

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 16:45:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Olmstead Properties, in partnership with its investment platform Vertex, has acquired the 18-story office building at 19 West 44th Street in Midtown Manhattan for $108 million. The seller, Savanna, incurred a significant loss on the transaction, having purchased the property for $195 million in 2017. The new ownership plans amenity upgrades and a prebuilt suite program to attract new tenants, building on recent capital improvements and existing strong tenancy.
