Affordable housing plans emerge for San Jose site long in limbo with $15M land buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 06 May 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Green Valley Corporation and Pacific Housing have acquired a 13-acre site at 1655 Berryessa Road in San Jose for approximately $15 million, with plans to develop a 260-unit affordable housing project. The acquisition concludes decades of ownership by the Facchino family and is expected to deliver much-needed affordable housing near the Berryessa BART station. The project, named Berryessa Family Apartments, will offer 257 units at below-market rates for families earning 30 to 70 percent of the area median income.
