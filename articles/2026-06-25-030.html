O’Hare office owners test market with $20M-plus ask in Des Plaines

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Deer Park Investors and Marc Realty are testing the market with a $20 million-plus asking price for 1350 Touhy Plaza, a 244,049-square-foot office building in Des Plaines, near O'Hare International Airport. The joint venture, which acquired the property out of foreclosure for approximately $6 million, is seeking to capitalize on recent renovations and the building's 90% occupancy. The sale comes as the owners face a July 2027 maturity deadline on a $15.1 million mortgage, with a strategy to sell now rather than exercise a three-year loan extension, anticipating lower interest rates.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The O'Hare area has seen significant transaction activity, including R.I.G. Capital's $167 million acquisition of the 1,115-unit Pavilion Apartments in May, and Fortress's $50 million purchase of the Westin O'Hare at $95,000 per key. Claire's also recently relocated its headquarters to Columbia Centre III in the vicinity.
