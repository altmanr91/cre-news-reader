REPORT: Peter Thiel's Family Office Breaks Miami Rent Record With Brickell Lease

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 11:31:12 -0400

SUMMARY

Thiel Capital, the family office of billionaire Peter Thiel, has signed a record-setting office lease for 18,000 square feet at 830 Brickell in Miami. The deal, valued at approximately $250 per square foot, marks the most expensive office lease in Miami's history, surpassing a previous record in the same building. The space became available after a Brazilian bank vacated the premises amid a fraud investigation. This move further solidifies Peter Thiel's growing presence in South Florida, following other ventures and his personal relocation to the area.
