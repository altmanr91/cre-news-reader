The White House Slashes Tariffs On Construction Equipment To 15%

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 14:56:52 -0400

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

The White House has announced a temporary reduction in tariffs on certain construction equipment, effective June 8, to stimulate national industrial investment. Import taxes on equipment made from aluminum, steel, and copper, including forklifts and residential HVAC systems, will decrease from 25% to 15% until the end of next year. This move aims to alleviate the strain on real estate development caused by rising construction material costs, which have seen significant increases in recent months due to global events. The administration hopes the tariff cuts will encourage private investment in nonresidential buildings, which has been declining, while residential construction has seen an uptick.
