Owner of shuttered Lake Point Tower restaurant Jiazhao Chen hit with another $14M foreclosure suit

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 08 May 2026 23:40:01 +0000

SUMMARY

A lender has filed a new foreclosure lawsuit against high-rise restaurateur and South Side landlord Jiazhao Chen, seeking to recover a $14.2 million loan tied to 10 properties primarily located in Chicago's Chinatown and Bridgeport neighborhoods. This action adds to a growing number of lawsuits against Chen, who is now facing over $93 million in alleged defaults across a portfolio of 55 properties with more than 230 rental units. The properties' combined appraised value is significantly less than the outstanding debt, highlighting the financial distress of the portfolio.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Units: 230

Loan Amount: $14.2M

Project Notes: The loan was originated by Encore SFR Finance in April 2024 and transferred to RRM RE Holdings in January 2026. The borrower allegedly stopped making payments in August 2025.
