Netflix To Buy Storied Hollywood Studio At 78% Price Cut

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 09:38:07 -0400

SUMMARY

Netflix is under contract to acquire the historic Radford Studio Center in Los Angeles for approximately $400 million from a group of lenders led by Goldman Sachs. This acquisition represents a significant discount from its 2021 appraisal of $1.8 billion, reflecting the challenges faced by studio owners due to rising interest rates and reduced production activity. The streaming giant, which has historically leased studio space, is consolidating its productions into a single owned facility.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: studio

Property Name: Radford Studio Center

Address: 4024 Radford Ave, Studio City, CA*

Size: 1,100,000 SF

Site Area: 55 acres

Notable Features: Historic studio lot famous for hosting productions including Seinfeld and Gilligan’s Island.

Project Notes: The property was previously owned by Hackman Capital Partners, which defaulted on its loan last year after failing to reach a restructuring or refinancing deal with bondholders. The studio was appraised at $1.8 billion in 2021.
