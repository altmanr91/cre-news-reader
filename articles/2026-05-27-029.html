Relu Stan’s Fulton Market hotel site returns to market with bonus air rights attached

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 27 May 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The site of an approved 296-key hotel project in Chicago's Fulton Market, previously home to Fulton Market Kitchen, has returned to the market for sale. Investor and developer Relu Stan is offering the property with an option to acquire additional air rights from an adjacent property managed by R2 and another Stan-owned building, potentially doubling the developable square footage to 151,000 square feet. The original 15-story hotel project approval is set to expire in January, with a one-year extension available.
