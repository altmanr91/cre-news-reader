Broward beach resort headed to foreclosure sale over $40M debt

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Hillsboro Beach Resort in Hillsboro Beach, FL, is facing a foreclosure sale in July due to a default on a $26 million loan from an Emerald Creek Capital affiliate. The ownership entities, controlled by BH3 Management principals Daniel Lebensohn and Gregory Freedman, defaulted on the loan by failing to pay real estate taxes and not repaying the loan at maturity. The total debt, including accrued interest and fees, has grown to $40 million, leading to the scheduled auction of the 81-room oceanfront property.
