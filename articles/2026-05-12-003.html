Phillips Auction House Inks 23K-SF Deal at 950 Third Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 11 May 2026 16:47:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Phillips Auction House has signed a 23,000-square-foot lease for two full floors at 950 Third Avenue in Midtown East, representing the larger of two new deals totaling 30,000 square feet at the Class A office tower. The transaction, alongside a 7,000-square-foot lease by investment firm Backcast Partners, contributes to a recent surge of leasing activity at the property, which has seen 55,000 square feet of new leases over the past several quarters.
