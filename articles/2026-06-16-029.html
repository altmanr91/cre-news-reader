Huntington Bank goes against the grain in the Loop

LEASE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 18:09:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Huntington Bank has significantly expanded its office footprint in Chicago's Loop, signing a 10-year lease for 60,900 square feet at 222 North LaSalle Street. This move nearly doubles the bank's previous space in the building, bucking the trend of office space reduction in the market. The expansion is attributed to recent renovations by the building owners, which have enhanced the property's appeal to tenants.
