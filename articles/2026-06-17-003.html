Inside the AI Revolution Quietly Reshaping CRE’s Slowest Data Layer

Commercial Observer — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 04:00:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Crexi has integrated Zoneomics, an AI-enabled zoning data platform, into its analytics product, Crexi Intelligence. This integration allows users to instantly access zoning classifications, permitted uses, and parcel boundary geometry directly on property detail pages, eliminating the need to navigate fragmented municipal databases. Users can now pose plain-language queries about development potential and receive source-backed answers, significantly streamlining the property evaluation process. This move addresses the long-standing challenge of fragmented zoning data in commercial real estate, providing a competitive edge in a rapidly evolving market with increasing legislative changes and office-to-apartment conversions.
