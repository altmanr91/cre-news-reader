Marcus & Millichap brokers sale of 94,532-square-foot retail property in Wichita Falls

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 14:33:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Parker Square Shopping Center, a 94,532-square-foot multi-tenant retail property in Wichita Falls, Texas. The center, which was 97% occupied at the time of sale, was acquired by a Dallas-based high-net-worth individual from one of the largest privately held real estate firms in the United States. The buyer secured traditional financing and intends to hold the property as a long-term investment.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Parker Square Shopping Center

Address: 2901 Kemp Blvd, Wichita Falls, TX

Size: 94,532 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 97.0%

Year Built: 1955

Site Area: 12 acres

Notable Features: Anchored by Crunch Fitness, with approximately 68% of the center leased to national or regional tenants on a triple-net basis. The center is shadow-anchored by Cinemark Theatres and benefits from strong visibility and freeway access at a signalized intersection.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Chris Gainey, Investment Specialist; Philip Levy, Investment Specialist

SELLER: One of the largest privately held real estate firms in the United States

BUYER: Dallas-based high-net-worth individual
