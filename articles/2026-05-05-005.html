Intuit TurboTax Signs 2.5K-SF Retail Lease at One Willoughby Square

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:00:05 +0000

SUMMARY

Intuit TurboTax has signed a long-term lease for 2,500 square feet of retail space at One Willoughby Square in Downtown Brooklyn. The financial services company will occupy a ground-floor storefront in the 35-story office tower, which is owned by JEMB Realty in a joint venture with AVRS Partners and KSR Capital. This transaction highlights the asset's appeal and Downtown Brooklyn's growth as a desirable location for businesses.
