Stonelake Advancing Lamar Blvd. Mixed-Use Venture

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Fri, 22 May 2026 19:28:22 +0000

SUMMARY

Stonelake is proceeding with a mixed-use development on South Lamar Boulevard, which will feature approximately 470 residential units for lease and 10,000 square feet of ground-floor food and beverage space. The project is planned as a six-story building, though recent zoning changes allow for construction up to 90 feet. Demolition of existing structures on the site is expected to precede a Q1 next year groundbreaking.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 1700 S. Lamar Blvd., Dallas, TX

Size: 10,000 SF

Units: 470

Completion: Q1 next year (groundbreaking)

Notable Features: Six-story building with potential for up to 90 feet in height due to recent zoning changes.

Project Notes: Three existing properties, including commercial buildings and a duplex, will be demolished.
