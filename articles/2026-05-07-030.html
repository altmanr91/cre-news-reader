Integra sells bayfront Miami-Dade rental complex for $151M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 06 May 2026 18:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

RPM Living has acquired Biscayne Shores, a 380-unit bayfront apartment and townhome complex in Miami-Dade County, from Integra Investments for $151.4 million. Completed in 2024, the property consists of a 15-story apartment building and three-story townhomes, and was fully leased at the time of sale. This transaction represents one of the largest multifamily trades in Miami-Dade County this year, with brokers noting the rarity of large waterfront apartment developments in the market.
