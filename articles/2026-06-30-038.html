Silicon Valley office vacancy declining as Sunnyvale sees surge: Colliers

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 20:45:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Fortinet, a cybersecurity firm, has acquired two office buildings at 140 and 144 Commercial Street in Sunnyvale, CA, for $15 million. This transaction adds approximately 26,000 square feet to the company's growing real estate portfolio in Silicon Valley, particularly around its Sunnyvale headquarters. The acquisition reflects Fortinet's continued expansion in its home market, driven by significant revenue growth.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 140 and 144 Commercial Street, Sunnyvale, CA

Size: 26,000 SF

Sale Price: $15.0M

Year Built: 1957

Project Notes: One building is 12,000 SF and built in 1957, the other is 14,000 SF and built in 1975. These properties are located across the street from Fortinet's headquarters campus.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Fortinet

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Silicon Valley office vacancy fell to 14.4% in Q1 and is expected to continue declining. The artificial intelligence boom is increasing demand in local housing and office markets.
