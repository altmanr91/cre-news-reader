Owner/User Acquires Corona Drive-Thru Restaurant

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:33:49 +0000

SUMMARY

A private investor has acquired a 2,230-square-foot vacant drive-thru restaurant building in Corona, CA, for $2.45 million. The buyer plans to open a new Troy's Burgers restaurant at the location. The transaction was structured with seller carry financing, which provided the buyer with a lower initial equity requirement and offered the seller a predictable income stream with future monetization options. The property's high-visibility location and flexible financing made it an attractive opportunity, reflecting strong demand for well-located drive-thru restaurant properties in the Inland Empire.
