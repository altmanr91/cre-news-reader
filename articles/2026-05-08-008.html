PAGEWOOD Buys 14,000 SF Office, Retail Building in West Houston, Plans Redevelopment

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 07 May 2026 14:35:34 +0000

SUMMARY

PAGEWOOD has acquired a 14,000-square-foot office and retail building in West Houston with plans for redevelopment. The company intends to relocate its headquarters to 6,000 square feet within the building, with the remaining space to be leased to food-and-beverage or fitness tenants. The project, named The Outpost at Brittmoore, is slated for completion in the fourth quarter.
