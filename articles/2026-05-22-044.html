Landlords Are Turning Offices Into Art Galleries To Win Tenants

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 21 May 2026 16:25:36 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Office landlords are increasingly investing millions in art installations and curated collections as a key strategy to attract and retain tenants, particularly in a competitive post-pandemic market. This trend moves beyond mere decoration, with landlords commissioning significant works and integrating art into building architecture to create 'enriched environments' that enhance employee productivity and engagement. The investment is proving effective, as prime office buildings with substantial renovations and art programs are achieving higher occupancy rates and better leasing figures compared to the broader market.
