Sherwin Belkin, prominent New York landlord-side attorney, to retire

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 22 May 2026 22:56:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Sherwin Belkin, a co-founding partner at Belkin Burden Goldman and a prominent New York landlord-side attorney, is set to retire in October after a 50-year legal career. Belkin was a key figure in rent-stabilization law, helping landlords deregulate units and navigate the city's evolving housing regulations. He noted that the environment for property owners, managers, and developers has become increasingly challenging due to the volume of regulations, particularly for small and mid-sized owners. His work shifted significantly after the 2019 Housing Stability and Tenant Protection Act, moving from tenant buyouts and deregulation to due diligence for buyers and sellers to assess potential overcharge liabilities.
