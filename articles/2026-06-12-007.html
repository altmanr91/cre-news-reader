Marcus & Millichap Arranges Sale of 62,400 SF Retail Center in East San Antonio

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 14:58:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has facilitated the sale of Rigsby Avenue Shopping Center, a 62,400-square-foot retail property in East San Antonio. Both the seller and the buyer were private investors who chose to remain anonymous. The center, built in 2002, features a strong tenant mix.
