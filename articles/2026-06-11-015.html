Red Oak Capital provides $11.10 million in financing for Dayton assisted living community

LOAN

REJournals — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Red Oak Capital Holdings, LLC provided $11.10 million in bridge financing for the acquisition and capital improvements of Cottages of Clayton, a 90-unit assisted living community in Dayton, Ohio. The loan, structured under Red Oak's Core-Plus Bridge Loan Program, has an 18-month initial term and a loan-to-stabilized value of 69.0%. The borrower, Clayton Realco, LLC, plans to use the funds to complete targeted capital improvements, achieve HUD compliance, and enhance operational efficiency through their existing management platform.
