Prologis flips Tastykake’s Philadelphia headquarters

SALE

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 12 May 2026 20:38:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Bridge Investment Group, a subsidiary of Apollo Global Management, has acquired the 346,000-square-foot Tastykake headquarters and distribution center at the Philadelphia Navy Yard from Prologis for $87 million. The property is fully occupied by Tastykake, which has nine years remaining on a 26-year lease. Prologis had acquired the asset in 2020 through its merger with Liberty Property Trust, which originally developed the build-to-suit facility for Tastykake in 2009.
