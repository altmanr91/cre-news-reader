Bridgeton offloads SoMa commercial property for more than its pre-pandemic value

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Bridgeton has sold the 36,000-square-foot commercial portion of Museum Parc, a mixed-use property in San Francisco's South of Market neighborhood, to Southern California-based DPI Retail for nearly $19.3 million. The property, which includes retail and restaurant space below luxury residences, remained fully leased throughout the pandemic, a notable achievement in the city's recent commercial market. The buyer was attracted to the asset's location, strong tenant mix, stable cash flow, and long-term outlook as San Francisco's commercial market shows signs of recovery.
