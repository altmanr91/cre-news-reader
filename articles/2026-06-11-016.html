City Realty goes after lender for alleged online personal disclosures

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Boston developers Fred Starikov, Stephen Whalen, and Matthew Zuker, co-founders of City Realty Group, have filed a lawsuit against Eastern Bank, alleging the bank disclosed their confidential personal financial information on a loan sale platform. The lawsuit claims that personal financial statements and other sensitive data were posted on DebtX, a portal for loan sales, when Eastern Bank attempted to sell a $29 million construction loan and a $2 million line of credit originally obtained from HarborOne Bank (later acquired by Eastern). The developers are seeking monetary damages and a preliminary injunction to compel the bank to remove their information from the platform.
