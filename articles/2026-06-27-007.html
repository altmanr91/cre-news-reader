JLL Negotiates Sale of 669-Unit Self-Storage Portfolio in Arlington

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 14:51:12 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has facilitated the sale of a three-property self-storage portfolio in Arlington, Texas, comprising 669 units across 80,216 net rentable square feet. BreakChain Capital Investments acquired the portfolio from an undisclosed publicly traded storage REIT. The properties, all constructed in the 1970s, are located at 3016 S. Cooper St., 2331 S. Collins St., and 2306 N. Collins St.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: self-storage

Address: 3016 S. Cooper St., Arlington, TX

Size: 80,216 SF

Year Built: 1970

Notable Features: Portfolio includes three facilities built in the 1970s.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: BreakChain Capital Investments

SELLER BROKER: JLL — Steve Mellon; Brian Somoza; Adam Roossien; Matthew Wheeler
