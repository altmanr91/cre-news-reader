Pinecroft Breaks Ground on $16.2M Healthcare Project in Shenandoah, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 18 May 2026 14:12:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Pinecroft has commenced construction on a $16.2 million medical office building in Shenandoah, Texas, north of Houston. The 39,883-square-foot facility is fully preleased to various specialty healthcare providers, with completion anticipated in early 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Size: 39,883 SF

Total Project Cost: $16.2M

$/SF: $406/SF (calculated)

Completion: early 2027

Notable Features: Fully preleased to specialty healthcare providers.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
