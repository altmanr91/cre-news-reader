Miami tees up legal fight over Live Local Act

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 20:41:18 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Miami officials have unanimously approved a measure to challenge Florida's Live Local Act, a state law offering tax breaks and zoning incentives for affordable housing developments. City leaders argue the act undermines local zoning authority, specifically Miami 21, by allowing high-density projects to bypass established review processes and potentially harm historic neighborhoods. While acknowledging the need for workforce housing, the city attorney has been directed to explore legal options to contest the law, following similar actions by other Florida municipalities.
