Jewelers Building bets on luxury amenities to keep office tenants coming

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 28 May 2026 21:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRG and Prime Group are investing $25 million into renovating the historic Jewelers Building in Downtown Chicago, adding luxury amenities such as a fine dining restaurant, coworking space, and rooftop bars. This renovation aims to attract and retain office tenants, building on recent leasing momentum including a significant expansion by Clayco and new headquarters for Sheridan Capital. The project reflects a strategic bet on high-end amenities to counter broader office market challenges, following the building's acquisition by CRG's parent company, Clayco, and Prime Group in summer 2024 after a foreclosure.
