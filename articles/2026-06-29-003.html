Truist Bank Supplies $300M Credit Facility for JIOS’s Expanding IOS Portfolio

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 12:00:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Jadian IOS (JIOS), an affiliate of Jadian Capital, has secured a $300 million acquisition credit facility from Truist Bank to support its continued expansion in the industrial outdoor storage (IOS) sector. The financing, arranged by Cooper-Horowitz, is initially seeded by 11 IOS properties and provides capital for JIOS to acquire additional assets nationwide. This facility highlights the growing maturity of the IOS debt market and institutional lenders' appetite for strong sponsors in this specialized real estate sector.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial outdoor storage

Loan Amount: $300.0M

Project Notes: The facility is seeded by 11 industrial outdoor storage properties.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SPONSOR: Jadian IOS (JIOS)

LENDER: Truist Bank — Alex Rownd, Director, Real Estate Corporate Banking Group

MORTGAGE BROKER: Cooper-Horowitz — Justin Horowitz

SPONSOR PIPELINE

46-property IOS portfolio: Refinanced with a $226 million loan from Blackstone Real Estate Debt Strategies.

Avenel, NJ: Acquired a 4.3-acre IOS site in May.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

JIOS has acquired more than 70 properties so far in 2026. The platform currently has around $2 billion in assets across 30 markets. Truist Bank provided a $344 million loan facility to Alterra IOS in July 2025 and a $100 million refinancing for a portfolio owned by Triten and TPG Angelo Gordon in October 2025. Jadian Capital raised over $2 billion for its second opportunistic equity fund in March 2025, exceeding its target by 40%.
