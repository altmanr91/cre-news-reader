Related, Battery Park City Authority to Expand Affordable Housing at Tribeca Park

Commercial Observer — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 20:13:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Related Companies and the Battery Park City Authority (BPCA) have partnered to preserve and expand affordable housing at Tribeca Park, a 27-story residential building in Lower Manhattan. The agreement amends the building's ground lease, extending the affordability of 81 existing units and adding 20 new units, bringing the total to 101 income-restricted residences through 2069. This initiative is part of BPCA's commitment to increasing affordable housing opportunities in the neighborhood and also includes increased ground rent payments to the authority.
