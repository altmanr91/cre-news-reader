Battery Club owners’ historic Oakland building slated for private school campus

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 13 May 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The Julia Morgan School for Girls is exploring a move into the former Bellevue Club building in Oakland, proposing to convert the historic 40,000-square-foot Beaux-Arts property into a private school campus. The building, previously acquired by the founders of The Battery social club with plans for a members-only establishment, was put up for sale last year after those ambitions did not materialize. This potential transaction highlights how Oakland's challenging commercial real estate market is creating opportunities for educational institutions to acquire discounted space.
