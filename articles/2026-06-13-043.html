Sentinel Real Estate Acquires Luxury Apartment Community in Potomac

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 20:41:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Sentinel Real Estate has acquired The Perry, a luxury multifamily community in Potomac, Maryland. The property, completed in 2016, features 297 residential units and 8,610 square feet of ground-floor retail. This acquisition represents a rare opportunity to invest in a premier residential community within one of the nation's most affluent submarkets.
