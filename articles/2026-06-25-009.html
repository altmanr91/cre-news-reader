Lument Provides $14.4M Agency Acquisition Loan for Northwest Houston Multifamily Property

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 14:37:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Lument has provided a $14.4 million Fannie Mae acquisition loan for Aspen Forest, a 144-unit multifamily property in northwest Houston. The loan features a fixed interest rate, a five-year term with partial interest-only payments, and a 30-year amortization schedule. The property, built in 1985 and renovated in 2020, consists of 36 buildings with two-bedroom units.
