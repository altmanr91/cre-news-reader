CBRE Arranges Acquisition Financing for 353,000 SF Retail Property in San Antonio

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 14:44:51 +0000

SUMMARY

CBRE has arranged acquisition financing for The Legacy, a 353,000-square-foot retail property in San Antonio. The financing was secured on behalf of a partnership between Rio Capital Investments and Triangle Capital Group, who acquired the asset. The property, built in 2006, features a strong tenant mix.
