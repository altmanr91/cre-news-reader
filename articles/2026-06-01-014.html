Ryan Companies, Washington Capital Management start construction of 190,000-square-foot spec industrial facility in Bellwood

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Ryan Companies US, Inc. and Washington Capital Management, Inc. have commenced construction on a 190,000-square-foot speculative industrial facility in Bellwood, Illinois. This project, located at the former SureBuilt building site, continues Ryan's strategy of redeveloping underutilized industrial land. The facility is designed to attract a variety of industrial users, offering Class A amenities and strategic access to major interstates and O'Hare International Airport, addressing the demand for modern industrial space in a supply-constrained submarket.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 840 25th Avenue, Bellwood, IL

Size: 190,000 SF

Completion: Spring 2027

Notable Features: Class A amenities including 32-foot clear height, ESFR fire protection, and exterior bike racks, with flexible design for warehousing, distribution, and light manufacturing. Immediate access to Interstates 290, 294, and 88, and proximity to O'Hare International Airport.

Project Notes: Located on the site of the former SureBuilt building.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Ryan Companies US, Inc.

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Washington Capital Management, Inc.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Pullman Crossings industrial park: Five industrial facilities totaling over 1 million square feet, including a Midwest Distribution Center and a last-mile distribution center.
