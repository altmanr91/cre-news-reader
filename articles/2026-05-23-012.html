Haven Housing acquires 309-unit multifamily community in Kyle

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Fri, 22 May 2026 15:11:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Haven Housing, the multifamily and affordable housing platform of BH Properties, has acquired Hays Park Apartments, a 309-unit garden-style multifamily community in Kyle, Texas. This acquisition expands Haven Housing's portfolio in a rapidly growing market.
