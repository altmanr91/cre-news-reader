Lee & Associates negotiates sale of 70,497-square-foot office property in Oak Brook

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 16:09:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates facilitated the sale of a 70,497-square-foot office property in Oak Brook, IL. KMMRD Enterprises, LLC sold the asset to Urban Commercial Property Group (UCPG). The new owner plans substantial capital investments for renovations and deferred maintenance to improve the tenant experience and reposition the property in the market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 915 Harger Road, Oak Brook, IL

Size: 70,497 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 52.0%

Notable Features: Located in a prominent business corridor with convenient access to regional transportation networks, retail, and dining amenities.

Project Notes: The property had a 48% vacancy rate at the time of sale.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: KMMRD Enterprises, LLC

BUYER: Urban Commercial Property Group (UCPG)

SELLER BROKER: Lee & Associates — Tony Russo, Executive Vice President
