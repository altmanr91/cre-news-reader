Oak Cliff affordable senior project lands financing, moves toward construction

LOAN

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:41:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Onyx Legacy Group and Lincoln Avenue Communities have secured initial financing for Tabor Village, an affordable senior housing project in Dallas. The development will feature 229 one- and two-bedroom apartments, with a significant portion reserved for low-income seniors, allowing them to remain in their established neighborhood. The project aims to provide safe, energy-efficient housing for seniors on fixed incomes amidst market uncertainties.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Tabor Village

Address: 6200 Baraboo Drive, Dallas, TX

Units: 229

Site Area: 19 acres

Notable Features: Adjacent to a library and fire station, with trails, a dog park, and a water feature.

Project Notes: 15% of units restricted to tenants at or below 50% of median income, 75% at 60% of median income, and 10% at market rate. Land formerly owned by Mt. Tabor Baptist Church.
