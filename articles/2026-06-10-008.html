Bradford Arranges Sale of 41,780 SF Industrial Flex Building in Haltom City, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:03:18 +0000

SUMMARY

Bradford Commercial Real Estate Services has facilitated the sale of a 41,780-square-foot industrial flex building in Haltom City, Texas. The property, previously occupied by Sunair Products, was acquired by Flannery Inc. from First Sunday Property Management Group. This transaction marks a new operational base for the steel and aluminum products manufacturer.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 2525 Minnis Drive, Haltom City, TX

Size: 41,780 SF

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: First Sunday Property Management Group

BUYER: Flannery Inc.

SELLER BROKER: Bradford Commercial Real Estate Services — Luke Clardy
