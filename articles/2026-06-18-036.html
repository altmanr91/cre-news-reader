Compass grows Austin presence with North Mopac office lease

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 19:32:45 +0000

SUMMARY

Compass, a national residential real estate brokerage, has expanded its presence in Austin, TX, by leasing 12,000 square feet at Stonebridge, a two-building office campus. This new lease at 9600 North Mopac Expressway consolidates two smaller offices, increasing the firm's total footprint in the market. The expansion reflects Compass's consistent agent growth and broader regional expansion efforts across Central Texas.

DATA POINTS

Project Notes: This lease consolidates two smaller offices for Compass, increasing their cumulative office space in Austin.
