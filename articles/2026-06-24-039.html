Trident Capital Group Expands Industrial Portfolio by 2 M SF in Ohio

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 22:08:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Trident Capital Group has significantly expanded its industrial portfolio in the Columbus, Ohio region by adding approximately 1.94 million square feet through a combination of new developments and acquisitions. The expansion includes a recently delivered and fully leased industrial facility, a second phase currently under construction, and a distribution facility acquired in April that is also fully leased. Additionally, Trident Capital Group, in partnership with O'Connor Capital Partners, is developing two large speculative industrial centers, one of which is nearing completion.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Project Notes: This article describes multiple transactions and developments by Trident Capital Group, including the acquisition of a 308,358 SF distribution facility in April 2026, the delivery of a 191,077 SF industrial facility in 2025, the ongoing construction of a 177,607 SF industrial facility, and the development of two industrial centers totaling 1,262,410 SF.
