22-Property NJ Workforce Portfolio Refinanced for $38M

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 19:32:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Benmark Capital has provided a $38 million loan to Ania Management for the refinancing of a 22-building workforce housing portfolio in Paterson, NJ. The financing was structured to support the sponsor's refinancing strategy and prepare the asset for future agency financing. The portfolio, which includes 203 residential units and six commercial units, is nearly fully occupied.
