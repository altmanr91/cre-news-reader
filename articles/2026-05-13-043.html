BREIT Raises $1.2B In Q1, Redemptions Decline By 41%

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 12 May 2026 11:31:48 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Fund Performance

Blackstone Real Estate Income Trust (BREIT) reported a strong first quarter, raising $1.2 billion in capital, marking its highest quarterly raise in three years. This surge in inflows was accompanied by a 41% decline in redemption requests compared to the previous year, indicating a significant rebound for the fund. BREIT also sold 64 properties for approximately $2 billion in net proceeds during the quarter, contributing to a reduced net loss compared to the prior year.
