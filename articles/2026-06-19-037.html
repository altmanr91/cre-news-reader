Can Brownfield Sites Support Data Centers?

Connect CRE — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 05:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article explores the potential for brownfield sites to host data centers, highlighting both the advantages and challenges. While brownfields offer benefits like proximity to power grids, favorable zoning, and existing infrastructure, they also present significant environmental liabilities. The authors, legal experts from Bracewell LLP, emphasize the need for thorough due diligence, clear allocation of environmental risks in contracts, and consideration of voluntary cleanup programs. They also touch upon policy efforts to streamline environmental permits for data center development, suggesting that developers should monitor new incentives while utilizing existing strategies for brownfield redevelopment.
