Sidley Austin Set To Anchor New 1M SF Related Midwest Office Tower

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 13:34:54 -0400

SUMMARY

Law firm Sidley Austin has signed on as the anchor tenant for Related Midwest's planned 45-story trophy office tower at 725 W. Randolph St. in Chicago's Fulton Market. The firm will occupy more than half of the 1 million-square-foot building, with an expected move-in date in late 2030. This significant lease marks a major step forward for new office development in Chicago, signaling renewed confidence in the market after a period of limited construction activity.

Notable Features: 45-story trophy office building designed by Kohn Pedersen Fox Associates.

Project Notes: The project requires city council approval. Related Midwest previously proposed a mixed-use residential-and-hotel tower on the site in 2023. Construction is expected to begin in 2027.
