Lawrence Group transforming 1800s train depot into retail incubator in Buda

REJournals — Fri, 22 May 2026 10:21:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Lawrence Group, in partnership with Logic Builders Group, is transforming a historic 1800s train depot in Buda, Texas, into "The Depot on Main," a 2,000-square-foot retail incubator and community marketplace. This design-build project aims to foster small businesses and local products, serving as a launchpad for entrepreneurs and a community hub. The redevelopment, which received Buda City Council approval in March, involves relocating the original depot building to the Downtown Buda Greenbelt and is expected to be fully completed in early 2027, with portions of the plaza potentially opening by December 2026.
