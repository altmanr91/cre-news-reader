Advanced Real Estate Buys 126-Unit Casa La Paz Apartment Community in Rowland Heights, California

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 15:53:22 +0000

SUMMARY

Advanced Real Estate, through its Advanced Fund 24-3, has acquired the 126-unit Casa La Paz apartment community in Rowland Heights, California. The transaction utilized a 721 tax-deferred "exchange and contribution" structure, where the previous owners contributed the property in exchange for partnership interests in the fund. Advanced Real Estate plans a value-add renovation and will rebrand the property as The Rowland, with its affiliated companies handling construction and property management.
