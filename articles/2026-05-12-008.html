Valrock, Evergen Equity Acquire 194,255 SF Industrial Building in Von Ormy, Texas

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 11 May 2026 14:56:19 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership between Valrock and Evergen Equity has acquired a 194,255-square-foot industrial building in Von Ormy, a southwestern suburb of San Antonio. The property was fully leased to a packaging company at the time of sale. CBRE represented the undisclosed private seller in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 10600 Fischer Road, Von Ormy, TX

Size: 194,255 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Notable Features: Fully leased at the time of sale.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Evergen Equity
