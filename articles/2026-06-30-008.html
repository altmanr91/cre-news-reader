Lee & Associates Negotiates 78,272 SF Industrial Lease in North Fort Worth

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 14:10:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates has successfully brokered a 78,272-square-foot industrial lease in North Fort Worth. The transaction involved Wistron Corp., a high-tech communications provider, securing space in Building H at 35 Eagle Center. Lee & Associates represented the tenant, while CBRE acted on behalf of the landlord, Alpha Industrial Properties.
