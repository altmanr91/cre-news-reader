Commercial tenant drops Cushman suit over data breach

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:50:05 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A commercial tenant has voluntarily dismissed a potential class-action lawsuit against Cushman & Wakefield, less than a month after it was filed. The lawsuit alleged that the brokerage firm failed to protect clients' personal information, which was compromised in a recent cyberattack. While the reason for the dismissal was not disclosed, it was filed without prejudice, allowing for the possibility of future re-filing.
