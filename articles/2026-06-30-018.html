UK private equity firm acquiring Kayne Anderson Real Estate

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 15:02:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Bridgepoint Group, a UK private equity firm, is acquiring Kayne Anderson Real Estate (KARE), valuing the investment platform at nearly $1.4 billion. The transaction involves Kayne Anderson shareholders receiving 189 million newly issued shares of Bridgepoint and $759 million in cash. This acquisition significantly expands Bridgepoint's presence in the United States and is expected to enhance its earnings per share. KARE's co-founder, Al Rabil, will continue to lead the business under the new Kayne Bridgepoint brand, aiming to capitalize on growth opportunities in alternative real estate sectors.
