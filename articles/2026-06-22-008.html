LCG Capital Management Acquires 81,700 SF Office Campus Near St. Louis

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:52:11 +0000

SUMMARY

LCG Capital Management LLC has acquired Colony Square and Williamsburg Center, an 81,700-square-foot office campus in O'Fallon, Illinois. This acquisition marks LCG's strategic entry into mission-critical office space, targeting defense contractors and government services firms supporting Scott Air Force Base. The firm plans to capitalize on below-market rents and existing vacancy to enhance the property's value.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Colony Square and Williamsburg Center

Size: 81,700 SF

Notable Features: Seven-building office campus located near Scott Air Force Base, headquarters of U.S. Transportation Command and Air Mobility Command.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: LCG Capital Management LLC

BUYER: JRN Investments LLC

EQUITY/FINANCING: Alberici Corp.
