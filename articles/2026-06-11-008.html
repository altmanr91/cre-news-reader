LifeCare Development Underway on 117-Unit Seniors Housing Project in College Station

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 15:20:54 +0000

SUMMARY

LifeCare Development has commenced construction on The Blake at College Station, a 117-unit seniors housing project in College Station, Texas. The community will feature a mix of assisted living and memory care units, complemented by a range of culinary and wellness amenities. Blake Management Group will oversee the property's operations upon its scheduled completion in 2028.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: seniors housing

Property Name: The Blake at College Station

Address: 2324 Harvey Mitchell Pkwy S, College Station, TX*

Units: 117

Completion: 2028

Notable Features: 73 assisted living units and 44 memory care units; includes culinary and wellness amenities.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
