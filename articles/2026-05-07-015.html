Lee & Associates negotiates 10,773-square-foot industrial lease in Fort Worth

LEASE

REJournals — Wed, 06 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates Dallas-Fort Worth has brokered a new industrial lease for a 10,773-square-foot space at Northern Cross-Building D in Fort Worth, Texas. Premier Medical Distribution will occupy the space, with Lee & Associates representing the tenant and Holt Lunsford Commercial representing the landlord, ULF Northern Crossing LP.
