JLL Arranges Construction Financing for 111,923 SF Industrial Project in North Fort Worth

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 14:50:33 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has arranged construction financing and an equity investment for Signature 35, a 111,923-square-foot industrial project in North Fort Worth. The facility, a partnership between Indco Partners and Holley Development Co., is expected to be completed in the first quarter of 2027. Simmons Bank provided the construction loan, while an undisclosed family office supplied the equity.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Signature 35

Size: 111,923 SF

Completion: Q1 2027

Site Area: 5.2 acres

Notable Features: Features 32-foot clear heights, 36 dock doors, 122-foot truck court depths, and two oversized ramped doors.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

MORTGAGE BROKER: JLL — Jarrod McCabe; Brennan Fewin; Ben Pollack

LENDER: Simmons Bank

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Indco Partners

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Holley Development Co.
