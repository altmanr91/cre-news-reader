McDowell Housing, Miami Jewish Health Open $139M Affordable Senior Living Community

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:43:31 +0000

SUMMARY

McDowell Housing Partners and Miami Jewish Health have completed Douglas Gardens Senior Health and Living, a $139 million affordable senior living community in Pembroke Pines, Florida. The newly opened community features 410 units for residents aged 55 and older, with income restrictions set at 30, 60, and 100 percent of the area median income. This project represents the third collaboration between the two partners, offering amenities such as walking paths, a theater room, and various indoor and outdoor gathering spaces.
