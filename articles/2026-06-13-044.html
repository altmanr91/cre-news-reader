Vornado Closes on 49% Stake in Fisher Brothers’ Park Avenue Plaza

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 20:28:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Vornado Realty Trust has finalized its acquisition of a 49% stake in Park Avenue Plaza, a 1.2-million-square-foot Class A office tower in Midtown Manhattan. The transaction valued the property at $1.1 billion, with Vornado acquiring its interest subject to its share of the existing $575 million loan. Fisher Brothers will retain its 51% ownership and continue to manage and lease the property, with both parties holding joint control over major decisions.
