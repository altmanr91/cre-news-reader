Sares Regis scoops up foreclosed Los Gatos multifamily development site for $12M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 01 May 2026 21:11:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Sares Regis Group of Northern California has acquired a foreclosed development site in Los Gatos, CA for $11.8 million, securing an $11.3 million loan from Stearns Bank. The 1.7-acre parcel, previously approved for a 78-unit housing development, was sold by an unnamed lender who had foreclosed on the property last August. The site has a history of legal disputes involving the prior developer and was notable for its reliance on state laws to streamline housing approvals.

DATA POINTS

Address: 405 Alberto Way, Los Gatos, CA

Sale Price: $11.8M

Loan Amount: $11.3M

Loan/Acre: $6,647,059/acre (calculated)

Loan/Land SF: $152.60/land SF (calculated)

Site Area: 1.7 acres

Notable Features: Site previously approved for a 78-unit housing development, including affordable units.

Project Notes: The unnamed lender foreclosed on the property in August for $11.3 million. The site was previously involved in legal disputes and was subject to state laws (SB 330) to bypass planning obstacles.
