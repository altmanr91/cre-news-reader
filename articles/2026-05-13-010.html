Optimism on tap during Illinois Real Estate Journal’s seventh annual Women in Real Estate Summit

REJournals — Wed, 13 May 2026 10:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The seventh annual Women in Real Estate Summit in Chicago conveyed a positive outlook on the local commercial real estate market, despite existing challenges. Speakers highlighted the continued strength of the multifamily and industrial sectors, a solid retail market, and emerging optimism for Class-A office properties with strong amenities. The event also celebrated the increasing presence and success of women in leadership roles within the Chicago CRE industry, with participants expressing confidence for a stronger market in the coming year.
