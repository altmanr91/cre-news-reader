Blackstone takes 54% loss on Seattle tower as it exits office game

SALE

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 14:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Blackstone has agreed to sell the U.S. Bank Center, a 44-story office tower in Seattle, to Spear Street Capital for approximately $280 million. This transaction represents a 54% loss for Blackstone, which acquired the property in 2019 for $612 million. The sale reflects the ongoing challenges in the Seattle office market, where demand has significantly declined post-pandemic due to increased remote work.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Seattle office market saw $255 million in sales in the first four months of this year. In contrast, the Manhattan office market recorded $2.8 billion in sales, San Francisco had $1.5 billion, and Dallas saw $1 billion over the same period.
