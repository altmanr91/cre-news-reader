Law Firm Gallet Dreyer & Berkey Inks 14K SF at BGO’s 685 Third Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 08 May 2026 18:48:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Law firm Gallet Dreyer & Berkey has signed a 12-year, full-floor lease for 14,000 square feet at 685 Third Avenue in Midtown East, a 31-story office tower owned by BGO. The firm's commitment to a full floor in this Midtown location underscores its confidence in the area as a vibrant business corridor and reflects its long-term presence in New York City.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The average office asking rent in Midtown was $84.77 per square foot in April. Legal sector office leasing increased 9 percent nationwide in the fourth quarter of 2025, up to 4.3 million square feet.
