Krusinski Construction Company begins construction of 32,000-square-foot addition to food distribution hub in Aurora

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Krusinski Construction Company has commenced construction on a 32,000-square-foot addition to the Loaves & Fishes Community Services Food Distribution Hub in Aurora, Illinois. This expansion will nearly double the facility's size to 62,000 square feet, significantly increasing storage capacity, improving distribution efficiencies, and enhancing collaboration with partner pantries. The project, part of the organization's Nourish Together campaign, aims to address the more than 50% increase in food insecurity in the surrounding counties since 2019, enabling Loaves & Fishes to serve two to three times more individuals weekly.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Loaves & Fishes Community Services Food Distribution Hub

Address: 580 Exchange Court, Aurora, IL

Size: 32,000 SF

Phase I: civil engineering work on site and getting a portion of the new East parking lot turned over

Notable Features: Expansion includes new and expanded freezer (2,300 SF) and cooler (3,200 SF) space, a larger depressed dock for semi-trucks, and an improved site layout for parking and recipient pick-up.

Project Notes: The construction is being executed in four phases to minimize disruption to ongoing operations. The facility is designed with a Class III commodity classification.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

OWNER: Loaves & Fishes Community Services — Mike Havala, CEO & President

ARCHITECT: Powers Brown Architecture
