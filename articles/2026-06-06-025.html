Related Group, Russell Galbut part of Fisher Island team at center of fuel depot battle

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 18:35:35 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership including Related Group, Russell Galbut, and Raycliff Capital, operating as HRP Group, acquired a nearly 10-acre fuel depot site on Fisher Island for $180 million in October. The developers plan to redevelop the property into "One Fisher Island," but face significant opposition from Miami-Dade County and Fisher Island residents. Miami-Dade County, which previously considered acquiring the site for $400 million, is now pursuing eminent domain to secure continued fuel supply for PortMiami cruise ships, a move the developers are aggressively fighting. Residents also oppose the fuel depot and have filed lawsuits against both the county and HRP Group.
