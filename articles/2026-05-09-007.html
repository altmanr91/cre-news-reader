JLL Negotiates Sale of 153,671 SF Office Building in West Houston

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 08 May 2026 15:32:26 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has brokered the sale of 5300 Memorial Drive, a 153,671-square-foot office building in West Houston. An affiliate of Equus Capital Partners sold the property to CAMCO Investment Group. The building, which was 90 percent leased at the time of sale and last renovated in 2019, also secured acquisition financing through Cornerstone Capital Bank, arranged by JLL.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 5300 Memorial Drive, Houston, TX

Size: 153,671 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 90.0%

Notable Features: Building was most recently renovated in 2019.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
