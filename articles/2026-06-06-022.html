Casino mogul picks up $36M mansion next to his Fort Lauderdale home

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 21:40:21 +0000

SUMMARY

Casino mogul James F. Allen and his wife, Shelly Allen, acquired a 38,000-square-foot waterfront mansion adjacent to their current residence in Fort Lauderdale for $35.8 million in an off-market transaction. The property, which previously traded in 2021 for $7.3 million, had an older home demolished and a new three-story, five-bedroom house constructed on the lot. This purchase highlights the strong rebound in Fort Lauderdale's luxury real estate market.
