JLL Arranges $19.5M Loan for Refinancing of Industrial Building in Westchester County

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:16:33 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has secured a $19.5 million refinancing loan from Webster Bank for a 118,991-square-foot industrial building in Croton-on-Hudson, NY. The property, fully leased to a single tenant, was purpose-built in 1995 for tire distribution and is owned by a partnership between Monday Properties and KPR Centers.
