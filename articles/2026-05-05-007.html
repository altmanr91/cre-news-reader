NewcrestImage Completes Renovation of 296-Room Hotel in Dallas

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 04 May 2026 14:51:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

NewcrestImage, a Dallas-based hospitality investment and management firm, has completed a comprehensive renovation of Hotel Mockingbird, a 296-room hotel in the Highland Park area of Dallas. The upgrades include significant property enhancements and the introduction of two new food and beverage concepts, Verse Kitchen and Bar and Raven. NewcrestImage acquired the property in October 2024.
