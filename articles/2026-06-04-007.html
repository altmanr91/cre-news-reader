Dallas Stars Plan New Arena, Mixed-Use Entertainment District in Plano

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 14:55:22 +0000

SUMMARY

The Dallas Stars are planning a new hockey arena and mixed-use entertainment district in Plano, Texas, at The Shops at Willow Bend. The project, estimated at $1 billion, will include sports-themed retail, restaurants, entertainment, and public spaces. The City of Plano is expected to contribute approximately $700 million towards the financing and will own the arena and site, entering into a 30-year lease with the team's parent company. This move follows a similar announcement by the Dallas Mavericks, as both teams' leases at American Airlines Center expire in 2031.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: The Shops at Willow Bend

Address: 6121 W Park Blvd, Plano, TX

Total Project Cost: $1.00B

Notable Features: The project includes a new hockey arena, sports-themed retail, restaurants, entertainment uses, and public gathering spaces.

Project Notes: The City of Plano is expected to contribute approximately 70% of the financing and will own the arena and site, entering into a 30-year lease with the team's parent company. The Shops at Willow Bend is a 1.4 million-square-foot shopping, dining, and entertainment destination currently undergoing redevelopment.
