Panattoni buys former State Farm campus in Tempe with industrial redevelopment plans

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 14 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Panattoni Development Company has acquired the former State Farm regional headquarters campus in Tempe, AZ, for $37.5 million from JDM Partners. The 25-acre property, which includes 462,000 square feet of office space and two parking garages, is slated for redevelopment into a two-building industrial project. The Tempe City Council has already approved Panattoni's plans to convert the site into 355,000 square feet of warehouse, industrial, and manufacturing space, along with 89,000 square feet of offices, reflecting a broader trend of suburban office conversions to higher-value uses.
