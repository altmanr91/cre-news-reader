Marcus & Millichap brokers sale of two Sherwin-Williams properties in Austin, San Antonio markets

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap brokered the sale of two newly constructed retail properties, both leased to Sherwin-Williams, in the Austin and San Antonio metropolitan areas of Texas. The properties, totaling nearly 9,000 square feet, were acquired by separate limited liability companies. One transaction in Jarrell involved new financing, while the other in Helotes was an all-cash purchase.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Size: 8,955 SF

Year Built: 2026

Notable Features: Both properties are newly constructed and leased to Sherwin-Williams under new 10-year leases with 10% rental increases every five years and multiple renewal options.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Mark Ruble; Chris Lind; Zack House

SELLER: North Carolina limited liability company

BUYER: Texas limited liability company

BUYER: Florida limited liability company
