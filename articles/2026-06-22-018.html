Movers & Shakers: Chez Realty expands in South Florida, acquiring Elev8

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 14:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

This article reports on several personnel changes and a corporate acquisition within the real estate industry. Chez Realty expanded its South Florida presence by integrating The Elev8 Group, adding six agents and over $40 million in sales volume. American Landmark Apartments appointed Elizabeth Roy as Chief Investment Officer to oversee its investment strategy and portfolio management. Separately, Bed Bath & Beyond is set to acquire Fathom Holdings for approximately $53.4 million in a stock exchange deal, aiming to integrate Fathom's real estate services into its 'Everything Home' ecosystem.

DATA POINTS

Sale Price: $53.4M

Project Notes: The acquisition of Fathom Holdings by Bed Bath & Beyond is an all-stock transaction.
