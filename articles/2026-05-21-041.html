22-bedroom East Bay estate trades for near-record price

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 20 May 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Fieldhaven Estate, a 22-bedroom mansion in Alamo, CA, has sold for $21.5 million, nearing a record price for Contra Costa County. The 21-acre property, originally built by tech co-founder David Duffield, was purchased by an unnamed East Bay family. The sale reflects a strong luxury market in the East Bay, driven by an influx of wealth from the booming artificial intelligence sector in San Francisco and Silicon Valley.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The luxury market in the East Bay is experiencing an influx of money from San Francisco and Silicon Valley, largely attributed to the booming artificial intelligence sector, leading to increased home prices and a mansion shortage in San Francisco.
