Frank Carone arrested on federal bribery charges

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

Frank Carone, former chief of staff to ex-mayor Eric Adams, was arrested on federal bribery charges in connection with an alleged migrant shelter contract. His brother, Anthony Carone, Long Island City hotel owner Yan Po Zhu, and Zhu's employee Crystal Chen were also arrested. Federal prosecutors have been scrutinizing Carone's business dealings and his time in City Hall since early 2024, with the investigation reportedly focusing on real estate partnerships and other business ventures tied to Carone.
