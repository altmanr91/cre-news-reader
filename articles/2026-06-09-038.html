11K sf Saratoga estate hits market for $19M as local housing market heats up

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 19:22:46 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A luxurious 11,000-square-foot estate in Saratoga, CA, has been listed for sale at $19 million. Completed in 2013 by Conrado Home Builders, the property features high-end amenities including a theater, wine storage, gym, and an 85-foot infinity-edge pool. The sellers, who commissioned the home while working abroad, are now downsizing, a trend observed among affluent Bay Area families.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Home prices in Saratoga were up 12.3 percent in the three months ending in April compared to the same period last year, with a median price of $4.1 million. Silicon Valley and the Bay Area are experiencing a surge of high-net-worth buyers, many linked to the artificial intelligence sector.
