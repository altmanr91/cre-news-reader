Glenstar acquires 500 W. Monroe office tower in Chicago

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Glenstar, in a joint venture with a private investor, has acquired the 966,924-square-foot, Class-A office tower at 500 W. Monroe in Chicago's West Loop through an all-cash purchase. The acquisition aligns with Glenstar's strategy of investing in high-quality, well-located buildings in the downtown market. Glenstar plans extensive renovations to modernize the lobby and common areas, introduce new amenities, and implement its tenant engagement program, Glenstar Connect. The firm will also assume asset and property management responsibilities for the 44-story building, which offers large, column-free floor plates and excellent connectivity to transit and major expressways.

Project Notes: Acquired in an all-cash purchase. Glenstar will launch a hospitality-driven renovation including a full lobby transformation, common area upgrades, and new amenities. Glenstar Connect, an app-based tenant engagement program, will also be introduced.
