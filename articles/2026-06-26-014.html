Adolfson & Peterson Construction begins renovations at Chisholm Hall at UTSA

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Adolfson & Peterson Construction has commenced a $6 million renovation project at Chisholm Hall, the largest and oldest residence hall at the University of Texas at San Antonio. The project, slated for completion in July 2026, involves comprehensive upgrades to modernize building systems, enhance resident comfort, and improve efficiency across the 120,860-square-foot facility.
