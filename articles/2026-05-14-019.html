Brookfield, Qatar  jump on trophy refinancing boom

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 13 May 2026 15:11:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Brookfield and the Qatar Investment Authority are securing a $1.9 billion refinancing loan for their trophy office tower, Two Manhattan West, located in Hudson Yards. The 58-story, 2-million-square-foot property, which opened in 2023, is 97 percent leased with strong in-place rents. This refinancing is expected to generate a significant cash return of $273 million for the ownership, highlighting a trend of landlords leveraging high-quality office assets for substantial payouts.
