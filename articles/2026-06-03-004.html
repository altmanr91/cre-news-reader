eBay Takes 28K SF at Bromley Companies’ 122 Fifth Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 20:57:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Digital retail giant eBay has signed an eight-year lease for 27,902 square feet, encompassing the entire second floor of Bromley Companies' 122 Fifth Avenue in Manhattan's Flatiron District. The company will relocate from its current New York City office at 625 Avenue of the Americas. The landlord noted that the lease marks the completion of a significant redevelopment of the historic building, which now offers modern infrastructure and amenities designed to attract forward-thinking companies.
