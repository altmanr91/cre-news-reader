Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 22,178-square-foot office property in Naperville

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap brokered the sale of a 22,178-square-foot office property in Naperville, Illinois, for $3.7 million. The property, built in 2015, is fully occupied by its original tenant, a consulting group, and features a mix of office and industrial flex space. The transaction involved a repeat client as the seller and a non-1031 investor as the buyer.
