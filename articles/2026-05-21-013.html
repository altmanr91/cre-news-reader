Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 242-unit self-storage property in Janesville

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 21 May 2026 10:02:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Lark Self Storage, a 242-unit self-storage facility in Janesville, Wisconsin, for $2.86 million. The property, which includes both traditional self-storage units and RV/boat parking, was sold by a local seller to a local business owner. The new ownership plans to operate the facility as Interstate Secure Storage.
