Pac Heights’ Apple orchard grows with another exec mansion buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 20 May 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Apple executive Mike Fenger has acquired a historic mansion at 2649 Green Street in San Francisco's Pacific Heights neighborhood for $13 million. The transaction highlights the continued strength of the city's luxury housing market, particularly in Pacific Heights, which has seen a significant recovery in high-end sales.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Sales of homes priced at $10 million or more in San Francisco jumped over 60 percent year over year in April. Pacific Heights has led the city's pricing leaderboard this year, with recent eight-figure sales including 2350 Broadway ($29.9 million), 2830 Pacific Avenue ($27.5 million), and 2606 Jackson Street ($24 million).
