South Florida’s top deals: Businessman sells Coral Gable home for $26M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

American Infrastructure Partners acquired a four-building shopping center and an additional commercial property in Lantana, FL, from ESJ Capital Partners for a combined $21.4 million. The shopping center, totaling 11,100 square feet, sold for approximately $10.9 million, while the second property, home to a maritime academy, traded for $10.5 million. This transaction represents the top commercial deal recorded in South Florida for the period.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 1330 and 1450 West Lantana Road, Lantana, FL

Size: 11,100 SF

Sale Price: $21.4M

Project Notes: The shopping center component previously sold in 2017 for $7.2 million. The second property at 600 East Coast Avenue is home to the Palm Beach Maritime Academy.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: American Infrastructure Partners

SELLER: ESJ Capital Partners
