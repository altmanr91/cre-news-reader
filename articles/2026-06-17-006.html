MetroNational Buys 244-Room Moran Hotel at CITYCENTRE in West Houston

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 15:06:54 +0000

SUMMARY

MetroNational, a local owner-operator, has acquired The Moran Hotel, a 244-room property located within the 47-acre CITYCENTRE mixed-use development in West Houston. The hotel recently underwent renovations and features extended-stay accommodations, a pool, fitness center, and extensive meeting and event space. JLL represented the seller, Midway, in this transaction.
