Blackstone Targets $1.75B IPO For Data Center REIT

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 04 May 2026 11:57:01 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Blackstone is planning an initial public offering for Blackstone Digital Infrastructure Trust, a new REIT focused on acquiring newly constructed data centers. The firm aims to raise $1.75 billion by issuing 87.5 million shares at $20 each, with an affiliated Blackstone entity indicating interest in purchasing up to $200 million in shares. The REIT will target data centers valued between $250 million and $1.5 billion, occupied by investment-grade hyperscalers, and anticipates returns of 5.75% to 7% or higher. This move comes as data center development surges but sales of existing assets have declined, positioning BXDC to capitalize on the growing market for stabilized data center assets.
