Bill Hutchinson to pay two women damages in split verdict on sexual assault case

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 23:26:19 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

Dallas developer Bill Hutchinson was ordered to pay $860,000 in damages to two women in a civil sexual assault lawsuit. The jury's verdict came after a two-and-a-half-week trial in Downtown Dallas, where the women had sought $120 million. Hutchinson, chairman and CEO of Dunhill Partners, was not criminally charged in these incidents, though he previously pled guilty to a misdemeanor sexual battery charge in California that was later expunged.
