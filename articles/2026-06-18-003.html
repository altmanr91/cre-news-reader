Hawkins Way Capital Drops $28M on 81 East Third Street Apartments, Student Housing

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 20:02:58 +0000

SUMMARY

Hawkins Way Capital has acquired a mixed-use property at 81 East Third Street in New York City for $28 million. The 13-story building includes both student housing and market-rate apartments, with approximately two-thirds of the space currently serving as a dormitory for the New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts. The student housing component will remain, while the future of the market-rate apartments is yet to be determined. This acquisition follows Hawkins Way Capital's recent purchase of another student housing facility in the Upper West Side.
