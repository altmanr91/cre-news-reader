Texas Icon Mat Volz: Building a real estate firm from the ground up

REJournals — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 09:40:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article profiles Mat Volz, a managing partner at Pagewood, a Houston-based real estate owner-operator and investment firm. Volz, alongside founder Paul Coonrod, has grown the firm to manage over 80 properties totaling approximately $350 million in assets across industrial, housing, retail, and office sectors within the Houston metropolitan area. The firm's growth has been deliberate and self-funded, a strategic choice that has shaped its operational philosophy and emphasizes relationships, shared accountability, and a sustainable approach to development.
