Adolfson & Peterson Construction wraps interior build-out of Arlington children’s clinic

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Wed, 20 May 2026 16:43:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Adolfson & Peterson Construction has completed the interior build-out of a new 6,450-square-foot pediatric primary care clinic for Cook Children's in Arlington, TX. The facility, which began construction in August 2025, features 15 exam rooms, treatment and laboratory spaces, and a waiting area with a playroom, enhancing access to pediatric care in the rapidly expanding Arlington community.
