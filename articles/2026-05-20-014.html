JLL Capital Markets closes $141 million refinancing for Wolf Point West in Chicago

REFINANCE

REJournals — Tue, 19 May 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has arranged a $141.817 million refinancing loan for Wolf Point West, a 48-story luxury multifamily tower in Chicago's River North neighborhood. The three-year, floating-rate loan was secured through New York Life Insurance Company on behalf of the borrower, a partnership between Hines, AFL-CIO Building Investment Trust (advised by DWS Group), Magellan Development Group, and the Joseph P Kennedy Trust. The property, delivered in 2016, is a LEED Silver-certified building offering 509 units and is strategically located on the Wolf Point peninsula, providing excellent access to entertainment, retail, and major business districts.
