Upland Apartments’ Original Developer Divests for $48M

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 18 May 2026 21:40:05 +0000

SUMMARY

The original developer of Upland Village Green, a 24-building multifamily property in Upland, CA, has divested the asset for $48.5 million. The sale, brokered by Institutional Property Advisors, marks the first time the property has traded in over 40 years. The buyer, WMC Commercial Properties, is positioned to benefit from strong cash flow and opportunities for value-add enhancements and operational efficiencies.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Upland Village Green

Address: 1420 Chaffee St, Upland, CA*

Sale Price: $48.5M

Notable Features: 24-building multifamily property located on the eastern border of Upland near Rancho Cucamonga and Ontario.

Project Notes: Property was placed on the market for the first time in over 40 years by the original developer and operator.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: WMC Commercial Properties

SELLER BROKER: Institutional Property Advisors — Kevin Green, Executive Managing Director; Joseph Grabiec; Chris Zorbas; Gregory Harris

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The sale represents one of only four 100-plus unit value-add transactions in San Bernardino County in the past 24 months.
