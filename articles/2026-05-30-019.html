Manhattan DA indicts 18 people for alleged mortgage, deed fraud scheme

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 29 May 2026 20:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Manhattan District Attorney Alvin Bragg has announced the indictment of 18 individuals in connection with an alleged deed theft and mortgage fraud scheme. The elaborate plot involved stealing the deed to a Manhattan brownstone through identity theft, artificially inflating the property's value, and then securing a mortgage based on the fraudulent valuation. Prosecutors allege the defendants posed as heirs to the deceased owner, sold the property between straw buyers at an inflated price, and subsequently obtained a $1.6 million mortgage and construction loan.
