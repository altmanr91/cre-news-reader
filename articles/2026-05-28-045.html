Coastal NJ Apartments and Retail Sell in 1031 Exchange

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 27 May 2026 21:33:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Henshaw Realty acquired The Pavilion, a newly constructed 46-unit multifamily building, and an adjacent retail property in Long Branch, NJ, for a total of $19.35 million. The acquisition was part of a 1031 exchange for the buyer, while the seller, Jebara Partners, divested the assets to concentrate on core investments in Northern New Jersey. The transaction highlights continued strong investor demand for well-located multifamily assets in coastal New Jersey markets, particularly from 1031 exchange buyers seeking stable, income-producing opportunities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Pavilion

Address: 165 Pavilion Ave, Long Branch, NJ

Units: 46

Sale Price: $19.4M

Year Built: 2021

Notable Features: Newly constructed five-story residential building with modern finishes and private outdoor space.

Project Notes: The sale included an adjacent retail property at 231 Third Ave, Long Branch, NJ, which sold for $1.35 million. The buyer utilized a 1031 exchange after selling a New York City asset.
