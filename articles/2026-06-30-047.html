Charles Cohen Goes After Fortress For $204M After Paying Off Debt

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 14:37:49 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Billionaire developer Charles Cohen is suing Fortress Investment Group for $203.7 million, a week after paying off a $187 million judgment to the lender. This latest legal action is part of an ongoing dispute that began two years ago when Fortress sued Cohen for defaulting on a $534 million loan. Cohen alleges that Fortress mishandled property auctions, while Fortress claims Cohen engaged in fraudulent transfers of personal assets to avoid payment. The legal battle continues with Fortress pursuing additional suits against Cohen over loan guarantees and asset transfers.
