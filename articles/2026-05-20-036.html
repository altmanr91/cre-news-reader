Kygo's Music Festival Company Latest Brand To Adorn Miami Condos

DEVELOPMENT

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 19 May 2026 12:10:38 -0400

SUMMARY

Superstar DJ Kygo and his manager Myles Shear, through their company Palm Tree Crew, are partnering with developers Property Markets Group, Lion Development, Eden Residential, and Sterling Equities to develop Palm Tree Residences Miami, a 37-story condominium tower in Downtown Miami. The Kobi Karp-designed project will feature 483 short-term-rental-friendly units and is expected to open by 2030. This development highlights a growing trend in Miami's luxury real estate market where recognizable brands are used to differentiate projects and command a premium.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: Palm Tree Residences Miami

Address: 31 NW 10th St, Miami, FL

Units: 483

Completion: 2030

Notable Features: The project will host monthly electronic and house music events, and owners will receive priority access to Palm Tree Crew events and venues worldwide. Amenities include an infinity rooftop pool, padel court, wellness center, yoga studio, juice bar, and golf simulator.

Project Notes: Units are designed to be short-term-rental-friendly and will range in price from $500,000 to $1.8 million.

LEASING AGENT: PMG Residential

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Miami is the second-largest global market for branded residential projects, with developers reporting that a recognizable brand can command a 15% to 20% premium over non-branded projects.
