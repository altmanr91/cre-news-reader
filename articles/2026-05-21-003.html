Trump Administration Selects Development Team for $8B Penn Station Rebuild

DEVELOPMENT

Commercial Observer — Wed, 20 May 2026 19:40:35 +0000

SUMMARY

The Trump administration has selected Penn Transformation Partners, a joint venture between Halmar and Skanska, as the master developer for the $8 billion redevelopment of New York Penn Station. The plan aims to modernize the transit hub, increase track capacity, and revamp retail spaces while keeping Madison Square Garden in its current location with a refreshed facade. This federal takeover of the project follows years of stalled efforts by New York state to revitalize the station.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: transit hub

Property Name: Pennsylvania Station

Total Project Cost: $8.00B

Notable Features: Includes a major rebuild of the transit hub, increased track capacity, revamped retail, and fresh cladding for Madison Square Garden.

Project Notes: The Trump administration took control of the redevelopment from New York state and the MTA in April 2025. USDOT announced $4.7 billion in funding for Northeast Corridor transit improvements in April 2026.
