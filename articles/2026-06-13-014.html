Josh Flagg takes over listing for Diane Keaton’s former “Pinterest” home

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

The former Sullivan Canyon home of the late actress Diane Keaton, known for its unique design inspired by Pinterest, has been relisted for $22.9 million, a $4 million price reduction from its previous asking price. The 9,000-square-foot residence, situated on a 28,000-square-foot lot, was originally purchased by Keaton in 2011 for $4.7 million and extensively renovated. The property, which includes a guest house and pool, was first listed in March 2025 for $29 million and has been on and off the market since.
