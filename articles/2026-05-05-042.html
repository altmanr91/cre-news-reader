SMBC Scoops Up 200K SF For New HQ In Charlotte

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 04 May 2026 14:23:56 -0400

SUMMARY

Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corp. (SMBC) has signed a sublease for 200,000 square feet of office space at 301 S. College St. in Charlotte, establishing its second U.S. headquarters. The Tokyo-based financial services company plans to invest $50.5 million and create 2,000 jobs in the city over the next six years, supported by a $70 million Job Development Investment Grant from North Carolina. This significant transaction is expected to be the largest single leasing activity in Charlotte's office market for Q2, reflecting strong demand for top-tier Class A office buildings with amenities and convenient locations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: One Wells Fargo Center

Address: 301 S. College St., Charlotte, NC

Size: 200,000 SF

Notable Features: 42-story tower, previously known as One Wells Fargo Center.

Project Notes: SMBC will move into the building in Fall 2026. North Carolina's Economic Investment Committee approved a $70 million Job Development Investment Grant for the project over a 12-year period.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

TENANT: Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corp.

SUBLANDLORD: Wells Fargo & Co.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Charlotte's office market vacancy dropped to 16.6% in March 2026, the lowest rate since 2023, indicating a new phase of stability. Demand in Q1 2026 was concentrated on top-tier Class-A office buildings, with most tenants securing leases in the 20,000 SF range. JPMorgan finalized a 145,000 SF lease in SouthPark and Charles Schwab leased 51,500 SF in South End during Q1 2026.
