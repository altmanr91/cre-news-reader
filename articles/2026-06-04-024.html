South Florida’s top deals: Businessman nabs Palm Beach mansion for $43M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A trust tied to Philip and Catherine Korsant sold a nearly 9,000-square-foot mansion in Palm Beach for $43.3 million to Thomas Swan, co-CEO and director of The Swan Group. The 1928-built property, situated on a 0.8-acre lot, features eight bedrooms, nine bathrooms, a gym, pool, and guest house. The transaction highlights the continued strength in the luxury residential market, with the property selling for significantly more than its 2017 purchase price.
