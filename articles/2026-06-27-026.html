Simon plans “main street” mixed-use project at shuttered Sears site in Boca

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Simon Property Group is planning a significant mixed-use redevelopment at the former Sears site within the Town Center at Boca Raton. The project, which will replace a shuttered Sears store, includes a hotel, a multifamily building, and substantial retail space, designed to create a "main street" atmosphere. This initiative is part of a broader trend in South Florida to transform traditional mall properties into vibrant mixed-use destinations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Town Center at Boca Raton

Address: 5900 West Glades Road, Boca Raton, FL

Size: 157,000 SF

Units: 374

Keys: 197

Site Area: 31.6 acres

Notable Features: Includes an eight-story hotel, a seven-story apartment building, an eight-story parking garage, and eight retail buildings, with a landscaped 'main street' running through the redevelopment.

Project Notes: The 18.6-acre former Sears site was previously acquired for $23 million in 2025. The Sears store closed in 2018 and will be demolished.
