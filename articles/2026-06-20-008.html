Patriot Business Park in North Houston lands major tenant

LEASE

REJournals — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 13:03:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lindenmeyr Munroe, a leading distributor of printing and packaging papers, has leased 100,043 square feet in Building 3 at Patriot Business Park in North Houston. This lease marks the second major tenant for the newly developed three-building industrial campus, which was delivered in the first quarter of 2026. The rapid leasing activity underscores the quality of the development and the strong demand for institutional-grade industrial space in the North Houston market, according to the leasing agent.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Patriot Business Park, Building 3

Address: 10326–10330 Veterans Memorial Drive, Houston, TX

Size: 100,043 SF

Completion: Q1 2026

Notable Features: Building 3 offers 150,615 square feet with 36 dock-high doors and two drive-in ramps, 32-foot clear heights, and ample parking. The park is strategically located with direct access to Veterans Memorial Drive and proximity to Beltway 8 and Interstate 45.
