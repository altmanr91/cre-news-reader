Emerson, Philip Roper Deliver 201-Unit Apartment Community in Metro Richmond

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 14 May 2026 13:53:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Emerson Cos. and independent developer Philip Roper have completed The River Apartments, a 201-unit Class A multifamily community in Chester, VA. The property offers a range of floorplans and amenities, including a saltwater pool, fitness center, and coworking space. Burke & Herbert Bank provided construction financing for the project.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The River Apartments

Address: 1350 Meadowville Technology Parkway, Chester, VA

Units: 201

Notable Features: Class A community located near the James River, featuring a saltwater pool, fitness center, grilling stations, fire pits, resident lounge, and coworking/conference space.

Project Notes: Monthly rental rates range from $1,684 to $2,596.

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Emerson Cos.
