NAI Wisinski of West Michigan marks 15 years

REJournals — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

NAI Wisinski of West Michigan is celebrating its 15th anniversary, marking a significant milestone since the 2011 merger of NAI West Michigan and The Wisinski Group. The firm has grown to become the largest independently owned commercial real estate firm in West Michigan, emphasizing collaboration, local market expertise, and strong client relationships. With 25 licensed commercial real estate professionals facilitating approximately 400 transactions annually and a property management team overseeing over 6 million square feet, the company attributes its success to its people and a consistent focus on client service and adaptability.
