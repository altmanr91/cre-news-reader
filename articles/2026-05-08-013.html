Chicago’s FLOW Intelligence launches AI platform for multifamily owners, operators

REJournals — Fri, 08 May 2026 09:41:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

FLOW Intelligence, a Chicago-based technology company, has launched an AI-powered decisioning platform specifically designed for multifamily owners and operators. This platform integrates with existing property management, marketing, and leasing systems to connect performance data, provide AI-driven recommendations, and execute approved actions directly within those systems. Early engagements have shown significant revenue increases, cost savings, and operational efficiencies for users. The platform aims to prevent revenue loss from delayed decisions by leveraging trapped data within disparate systems, offering a closed-loop architecture that continuously learns and improves.
