Stream Realty Lists Single-Tenant Mission Viejo Office Property for Sale

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:47:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Stream Realty Partners has been appointed to market the sale of 20532 El Toro, a 61,418-square-foot office building in Mission Viejo, CA. The three-story property, formerly known as Mission Foothills Professional Building, has undergone extensive renovations, making it suitable for a single occupier or adaptable for a multi-tenant environment. The listing presents a flexible opportunity for various buyers, including potential condo conversion, medical-adjacent conversion, or long-term land banking, with reduced near-term execution risk due to prior capital investments.
