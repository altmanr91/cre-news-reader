Outrigger Industrial Sells 1 MSF Building at Generation Park in Northeast Houston

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 15 May 2026 14:29:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Outrigger Industrial has sold a 1 million-square-foot industrial building located within the Generation Park master-planned development in northeast Houston. The buyer and sales price were not disclosed. Additionally, Outrigger Industrial has secured a full-building lease with Representative Materials Co. for its other 255,871-square-foot industrial facility within the same development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 12515 Lockwood Road, Houston, TX

Size: 1,000,000 SF

Site Area: 72 acres

Notable Features: Features 40-foot clear heights, ESFR sprinkler systems, and abundant car and trailer parking.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Outrigger Industrial

SELLER BROKER: JLL

SELLER BROKER: NAI Partners — Jarret Venghaus; Jeff Venghaus; David Holland
