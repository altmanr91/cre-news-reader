Dallas-based Bray Real Estate joins Compass

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 18 May 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Bray Real Estate Group, a Dallas-based brokerage, has joined Compass, integrating over 100 agents across Dallas-Fort Worth, Austin, and Houston. This move significantly expands Compass's residential brokerage presence in key Texas markets, particularly in the North Texas suburbs, and follows Bray Real Estate's reported brokerage volume of over $475 million last year. The decision was driven by Compass's technology platform, according to Bray's co-founder.
