Access Industries scores $225M refi for Faena District

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 22:12:29 +0000

SUMMARY

Access Industries, through its real estate arm, secured a $225 million refinancing for its Faena District in Miami Beach, FL. J.P. Morgan increased the existing loan by $55 million, resulting in a reduced interest rate spread. The refinancing reflects the strong performance of Miami's luxury tourism and real estate markets, according to Access Real Estate's head. The Faena District includes the Faena Hotel, Faena Forum arts complex, and Casa Faena hotel.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Faena District

Address: 3201 Collins Ave, Miami Beach, FL*

Keys: 38

Loan Amount: $225.0M

Notable Features: Oceanfront luxury district including a 179-room hotel, an arts complex, and a 38-key hotel.

Project Notes: The Faena Hotel at 3201 Collins Avenue, Faena Forum at 3300 Collins Avenue, and Casa Faena at 3500 Collins Avenue are included in the refinancing. The Faena Hotel was formerly the Saxony Hotel, built in 1947 and reopened in late 2015 after renovation. The refinancing does not include Faena House or Aman Miami Beach.
