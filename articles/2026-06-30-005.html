HSF Kramer’s Seth Niedermayer On Architecting Private Equity’s Real Estate Rise

Commercial Observer — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 20:00:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article profiles Seth Niedermayer, a partner at HSF Kramer, highlighting his role in navigating complex real estate transactions, particularly those driven by private equity. Niedermayer's practice spans acquisitions, joint ventures, and finance across various asset classes, with a focus on highly structured deals, increased leverage, and a booming debt fund business. His ability to simplify complicated situations and find workable compromises is a key asset for his clients, who are increasingly selective about managers and assets, favoring high-returning sectors like data centers and senior living over traditional core investments. The article emphasizes how the current market environment, characterized by high interest rates and uncertainty, has led to longer and more intricate negotiations, a significant shift from earlier in his career.
