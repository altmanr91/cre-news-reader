AMAC Holdings Sells Adjacent East Village Buildings for $23.5M

SALE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 17:24:27 +0000

SUMMARY

AMAC Holdings has sold two adjacent multifamily buildings in Manhattan's East Village to AG Paratus for $23.5 million. The properties, located at 111 and 113 East 12th Street, were acquired by AMAC in June 2018 for $20 million. The transaction highlights continued investor interest in well-located urban residential assets.
