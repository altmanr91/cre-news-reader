Alaris Aerospace Inks Big Industrial Lease in Palm Beach County

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 28 May 2026 17:50:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Alaris Aerospace Systems has signed a 129,000-square-foot industrial lease at Corporate Logistics Center in Jupiter, FL, relocating its general warehousing, distribution, and office operations from Pompano Beach. Landlords Woodmont Industrial Partners and Butters Group announced the seven-year lease, which brings the recently completed facility to approximately 50% occupancy. The transaction is notable given the sluggish industrial leasing market in Palm Beach County, which saw limited net absorption in the first quarter of this year.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Palm Beach County's industrial leasing market recorded only 88,128 square feet in net absorption during the first quarter of this year. Asking rents remained steady at just under $14.20 per square foot per year.
