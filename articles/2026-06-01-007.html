Partnership Purchases 1.3 MSF Industrial Property in Phoenix

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:28:20 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture formed by Machine Investment Group, Miramar Capital, and Axonic Capital has acquired The Hub @ 202, a 1.3-million-square-foot industrial park in Phoenix. The new ownership plans to invest additional capital to create move-in ready industrial suites to meet market demand and attract a diverse tenant base. The transaction terms were not disclosed.

DATA POINTS

Property Name: The Hub @ 202

Size: 1,300,000 SF

Notable Features: Industrial park features 10 buildings ranging from 65,000 SF to 270,000 SF.
