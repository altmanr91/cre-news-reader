M2G Ventures Completes Redevelopment of 740,000 SF Industrial, Retail Property in Northwest Dallas

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 14:08:34 +0000

SUMMARY

M2G Ventures has completed the redevelopment of a 14-building campus in northwest Dallas, transforming it into a 740,000-square-foot industrial and retail property. The project, now known as the Inwood Design District for its showroom and light industrial components and Ace on Inwood for its retail and restaurant spaces, was acquired by M2G Ventures in early 2025.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Inwood Design District / Ace on Inwood

Address: 1110 Inwood Road, Dallas, TX

Size: 740,000 SF

Site Area: 38 acres

Notable Features: 14-building campus converted into industrial, retail, and showroom space.

Project Notes: Site previously known as the Inwood Design District. Acquired in early 2025.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: M2G Ventures
