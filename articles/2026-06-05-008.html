Red Oak Capital Provides $7.7M Acquisition Loan for Multifamily Community in Memphis

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 15:48:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Red Oak Capital Holdings has provided a $7.7 million acquisition loan for the Valley Forge Apartments, a 229-unit multifamily community in Memphis. The borrower, I Real Estate and Allstate Property Management, plans to use the financing for the acquisition and repositioning of the property, including repairs, capital improvements, and security upgrades. The loan has an initial 18-month term and a loan-to-stabilized value of 71.9 percent.
