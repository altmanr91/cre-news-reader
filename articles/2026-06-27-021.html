Queens man charged with deed theft from elderly woman with dementia

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 20:29:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A Queens man has been charged with 23 counts, including grand larceny and forgery, for allegedly stealing the deed to a Brooklyn home from an elderly woman with dementia. The man, Mark Salkey, is accused of forging documents to transfer ownership of the East Flatbush property to his company, Salkey Salkey & Associates, Inc., and then renting it out to multiple tenants while the rightful owner was still living there. He allegedly collected approximately $70,000 in rental payments and stole an additional $168,000 from the victim's savings and pension.
