DataBank Closes On $1.45B In Financing For 2 Deals: The DFW Deal Sheet

LOAN

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 15:08:36 -0400

SUMMARY

DataBank, a Dallas-based data center provider, has secured over $1.4 billion in new financing to bolster its capital structure and support ongoing expansion. The financing includes an $800 million revolving credit facility maturing in 2031 and a $650 million upsize to its existing construction financing for its 300-acre data center campus in Red Oak, Texas. This strategic financial move, which also incorporates DataBank's first private placement transaction, positions the company to capitalize on accelerating demand for data center capacity from hyperscale, cloud, and enterprise customers.
