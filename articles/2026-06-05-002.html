Paul Whalen of RAMSA: 5 Questions

Commercial Observer — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 21:40:29 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

Paul Whalen, a partner at Robert A.M. Stern Architects (RAMSA), discusses the current "golden age" of luxury residential development, particularly in South Florida and New York City. He highlights that clients are more knowledgeable, quality has significantly improved, and apartments are larger, leading to a resurgence in high-quality multifamily buildings not seen since before World War II. Whalen notes that residential buildings are now making architectural news, with developers increasingly hiring high-profile architects like RAMSA, whose brand recognition contributes to property value. He also details evolving amenity trends, such as above-ground pools and gymnasiums, and emphasizes the challenge of maintaining design quality throughout the development process while managing budgets and fostering team excitement.
