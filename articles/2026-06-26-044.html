Real Capital Solutions Debuts In Atlanta With $50M Purchase Of 101 Marietta

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 15:56:10 -0400

SUMMARY

Real Capital Solutions has acquired the 36-story, 674,000-square-foot office tower at 101 Marietta Street NW in Downtown Atlanta for $49.5 million, marking the firm's entry into the Atlanta market. The building, known for its distinctive chevron-shaped roof and proximity to Centennial Olympic Park, was purchased at a significant discount compared to its 2015 sale price, reflecting the challenging conditions in Downtown Atlanta's office market. The new owner plans to implement strategic leasing initiatives and build on existing improvements to increase occupancy and enhance the tenant experience.
