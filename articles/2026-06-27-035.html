Muslim community’s religious freedom lawsuit against state of Texas allowed to proceed

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 18:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A federal judge has allowed a religious freedom lawsuit filed by the East Plano Islamic Center (EPIC) against the state of Texas to proceed, rejecting the state's motion to dismiss. The lawsuit challenges a cease and desist order from the Texas funeral commission, which prohibited EPIC from performing traditional Muslim funeral rites at its Plano facility without a license. This legal battle is intertwined with broader political claims by Texas officials, including Governor Greg Abbott and Attorney General Ken Paxton, who have alleged that EPIC and a proposed master-planned community, The Meadow, are attempting to institute Sharia law in Texas, despite a U.S. Department of Justice probe finding no wrongdoing.
