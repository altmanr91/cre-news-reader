Vanderbilt Office, Hillwood And TriPost Capital JV Buys 1.4M SF Office Campus In DFW

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 15 May 2026 13:23:07 -0400

SUMMARY

A joint venture formed by Vanderbilt Office Properties, Hillwood Urban, and TriPost Capital Partners has acquired The Towers at Williams Square, a 1.4M SF Class-A office campus in Irving, TX. The iconic property, known for its distinctive architecture and the Mustangs of Las Colinas sculpture, has undergone over $30M in renovations and is home to a strong tenant mix. The buyers believe the campus is well-positioned to capitalize on the ongoing flight-to-quality trend in the office market, driven by its amenities, accessibility, and central location within the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: The Towers at Williams Square

Address: 5205 N O'Connor Blvd, Irving, TX*

Size: 1,400,000 SF

Year Built: 1980

Notable Features: Class-A office campus featuring a 26-story center high-rise, two 14-story towers, an adjacent five-story building, and the Mustangs of Las Colinas bronze sculpture.

Project Notes: Over $30M in renovations have been completed over the years. Vanderbilt Office Properties previously partnered with Apollo Global Real Estate to purchase the campus in 2016 for $330M, with Hillwood also involved in that prior deal.
