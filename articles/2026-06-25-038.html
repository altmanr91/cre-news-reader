Blackstone picks up Hyatt Regency San Francisco for $279M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Blackstone has acquired the 821-room Hyatt Regency San Francisco for $279 million from Sunstone Hotel Investors, further expanding its hotel portfolio in the city. The transaction, which values the property at nearly $340,000 per room, will see Hyatt continue to manage the hotel. This acquisition reflects Blackstone's strong confidence in San Francisco's economic rebound, driven by the booming artificial intelligence sector, which has made the city a global hub for AI innovation.
