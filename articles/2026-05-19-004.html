Tech Firm Profound Inks 30K-SF Deal at GFP’s 5-9 Union Square West

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 18 May 2026 18:50:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Marketing intelligence platform Profound has secured a 30,000-square-foot lease at GFP Real Estate's 5-9 Union Square West, also known as the Spingler Building. The deal encompasses two full floors in the eight-story office property, which overlooks Union Square Park. This new location marks Profound's second office on Union Square West, expanding its presence in the area.
