Harlingen city officials approve 120-day data center moratorium to modernize land regulations

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 26 May 2026 21:19:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

Harlingen city officials have approved a 120-day moratorium on data center development to evaluate land use regulations and assess the impact on public infrastructure, including electricity, wastewater, and water supply. This decision comes as the city faces a proposed 1,800-acre data center project by Eneus Energy just outside its limits, which city officials believe would strain current infrastructure. The moratorium aims to allow the city to modernize its regulations to accommodate future data center plans responsibly.
