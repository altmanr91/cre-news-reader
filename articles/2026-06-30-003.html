Citigroup Refis East Village Apartment Building With $45M CMBS Loan

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 20:37:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Benchmark Real Estate Group has secured a $44.5 million CMBS loan from Citigroup to refinance its 61-unit multifamily property at 194 East Second Street in Manhattan's East Village. The property, which also includes 15,450 square feet of retail space, underwent significant renovations after Benchmark acquired it in 2024. The refinancing highlights the strong demand for high-quality multifamily assets in Manhattan, driven by robust operating fundamentals and limited new supply, according to JLL, which brokered the deal.
