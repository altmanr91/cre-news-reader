Ted Turner leaves behind real estate, conservation legacy

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 07 May 2026 19:14:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Obituary

This article is an obituary highlighting the extensive real estate and conservation legacy of media mogul Ted Turner, who passed away at 87. Turner amassed a portfolio of over 2 million acres across the United States, becoming one of the country's largest landowners. His land holdings, which included luxury resorts and livestock businesses, were instrumental in his conservation efforts, particularly in boosting bison populations. Beyond his vast ranches, Turner also played a pivotal role in revitalizing Atlanta's Omni International complex, making it the home of CNN and transforming it into a significant destination.
