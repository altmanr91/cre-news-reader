“Idiots”: Russell Galbut blasts opponents of Galleria Mall megaproject

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 29 May 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Russell Galbut, co-founder and chairman of GFO Investments, unveiled updated renderings for the massive redevelopment of The Galleria Mall in Fort Lauderdale, a nine-tower megaproject. The plans include over 3,000 apartments, a 170-room hotel, office space, and retail, with a $100 million investment in the mall portion. Galbut's remarks at a Bisnow event, where he criticized project opponents as "idiots," drew strong reactions, including from Fort Lauderdale Mayor Dean Trantalis. The project, which utilizes Florida's Live Local Act to bypass local zoning, has faced significant community opposition over concerns about traffic, infrastructure strain, and displacement.
