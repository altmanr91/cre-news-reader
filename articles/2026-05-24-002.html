Kathie Lee Gifford’s headline listing and Greenwich’s lux surge

The Real Deal — New York — Sat, 23 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Kathie Lee Gifford has listed her waterfront estate in Riverside, CT, for $100 million, a price point that would make it one of the most expensive residential sales in Greenwich if achieved. While the property boasts significant acreage and water access, some market observers believe the asking price is aspirational and the property may trade closer to $40 million, especially given the likelihood of a teardown. The listing comes amidst a surge in Greenwich's luxury market, which has seen a significant increase in high-value home sales and a corresponding depletion of inventory.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Greenwich's luxury market has seen 39 homes trade for over $10 million last year, nearly double the previous year. Current inventory is significantly low, with only 100-105 homes on the market compared to 600-700 pre-pandemic. The Upper East Side townhouse market recorded nearly $283 million in deals during Q1 2026, an 80% increase year-over-year, with an average sale price of $16.6 million.
