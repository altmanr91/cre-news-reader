NAI Hiffman Achieves Full Occupancy at 137K-SF O’Hare Office Building

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 20:58:07 +0000

SUMMARY

NAI Hiffman has successfully brought the 136,973-square-foot office building at 5500 Pearl Street in Rosemont, Illinois, to 100% occupancy with a new 2,748-square-foot lease signed by Horn-McGowan Insurance Agency. This achievement marks the culmination of a multi-year repositioning strategy by owner Integris Ventures and NAI Hiffman, which acquired the property in 2018 with only 47.7% occupancy. The comprehensive upgrades, including new amenities and common area improvements, significantly boosted leasing activity and tenant interest.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 5500 Pearl Street, Rosemont, IL

Size: 2,748 SF

Current Occupancy: 100.0%

Project Notes: The 136,973-square-foot property was acquired in 2018 as a value-add investment with 47.7% occupancy. NAI Hiffman was engaged in 2019 to oversee repositioning efforts.
