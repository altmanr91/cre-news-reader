Douglas Elliman quietly posts $16M loss after last year’s rebound

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 11 May 2026 15:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Douglas Elliman reported a net loss of over $16 million in the first quarter, a reversal from its $69 million profit in the previous quarter. The firm's revenue declined to $214 million, attributed partly to an exceptionally strong first quarter in the prior year and the sale of its property management division. Despite the financial setback, CEO Michael Liebowitz expressed confidence in the company's future, citing positive momentum in agent recruitment and strategic growth in luxury markets.
