Arrow Lines Up Acquisition Financing for 7-Property Mixed-Use Portfolio

LOAN

Connect CRE — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:53:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Arrow Real Estate Advisors secured a $69.7 million acquisition bridge loan for TARGO Capital Partners to acquire a seven-property mixed-use portfolio in Manhattan. The portfolio, comprising 127 residential units and 13 retail units across 76,182 square feet, is located in the Lower East Side, East Village, and West Village submarkets. The financing was provided by Bridge Investment Group, enabling TARGO Capital Partners to expand its investment platform.
