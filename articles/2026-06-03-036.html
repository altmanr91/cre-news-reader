SummerHill plans for demo, townhome redevelopment in Cupertino

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

SummerHill Homes is planning to redevelop a nearly 1.6-acre commercial site in Cupertino, CA, into a 27-unit townhome community. The project, which will include five affordable housing units, is utilizing California's Senate Bill 330 for streamlined approval. The Cupertino City Council is scheduled to review the proposal.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 10268 Bandley Drive, Cupertino, CA

Units: 27

Site Area: 1.6 acres

Notable Features: Five of the 27 units will be deed-restricted for affordable housing for median- and moderate-income households.

Project Notes: The project is utilizing a streamlined approval process under Senate Bill 330.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: SummerHill Homes

ARCHITECT: William Hezmalhalch Architects

SPONSOR PIPELINE

20045 Stevens Creek Boulevard: 32 townhome-style apartments, with six units deed-restricted as affordable for moderate-income tenants, developed by Dividend Homes.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The City of Cupertino is required to entitle 4,588 new housing units by 2031 under its certified housing element.
