Greystone Provides HUD/FHA Loan for Central NJ Multifamily

LOAN

Connect CRE — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:04:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Greystone has provided a $47.25 million HUD/FHA-insured loan for The View at Middlesex, a 200-unit multifamily community in Middlesex, NJ. The financing was secured for Forte Real Estate Development, a prominent developer in Central New Jersey known for its expertise in acquiring, repositioning, and developing residential properties. This long-term, fixed-rate debt solution is expected to support the property's sustained success.
