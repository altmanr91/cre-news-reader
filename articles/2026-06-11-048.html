The Scion Group Continues Student Housing Spree With $1.5B Acquisition

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 11:41:19 -0400

SUMMARY

The Scion Group, the largest owner of student housing in the U.S., is set to acquire Atlanta-based Student Quarters for approximately $1.5 billion. This transaction will add 13,000 beds across 21 markets to Scion's portfolio, bringing its total to nearly 118,000 beds across 190 properties. This acquisition marks Scion's second significant expansion in recent months, following a $910 million deal for a 7,578-bed portfolio in May, and is part of a broader strategy to expand its national footprint and capital deployment, which has exceeded $5 billion over the last two years.
