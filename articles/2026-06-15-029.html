MCR Pursuing 1,000 New Miami Airport Hotel Rooms

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:49:05 +0000

SUMMARY

MCR, owner of the Hilton Miami Airport Blue Lagoon, is planning a significant expansion to add 1,000 new hotel rooms across two 14-story towers. The project will utilize existing surface parking space at the property, with the current hotel remaining operational during construction. The expansion will also include a new seven-story parking garage.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: Hilton Miami Airport Blue Lagoon

Address: 5101 Blue Lagoon Drive, Miami, FL

Keys: 1,000

Notable Features: Each new tower will feature a sixth-floor pool deck and amenity areas overlooking Blue Lagoon.

Project Notes: The 2.6-acre site was previously acquired for $118.3 million in 2023.
