MCEC Closes $43M C-PACE Loan for Marriott Downtown Frederick Hotel in Maryland

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 14 May 2026 13:46:49 +0000

SUMMARY

The Maryland Clean Energy Center (MCEC) has closed a $43 million C-PACE loan for the development of the Marriott Downtown Frederick at Carroll Creek, a 204-room hotel in Frederick, MD. Nuveen Green Capital provided the 30-year, fixed-rate financing on behalf of Plamondon Hospitality Partners. This transaction marks the largest C-PACE loan in Maryland to date, funding sustainable initiatives such as advanced insulation, energy-efficient HVAC systems, and LED lighting.

Project Notes: The C-PACE financing will fund sustainable initiatives including advanced insulation, roofing, windows, HVAC systems, LED lighting, low-flow water fixtures, and hot water systems.
