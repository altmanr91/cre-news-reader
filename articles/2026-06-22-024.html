Cushman & Wakefield Lands 955K-SF Tampa Industrial Leasing Assignment

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 14:26:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Leasing Assignment

Cushman & Wakefield has been selected by Link Logistics to manage the leasing of a 955,000-square-foot industrial portfolio in Tampa, FL. The portfolio comprises six buildings, offering a variety of suite sizes that are expected to attract a diverse tenant base, from local businesses to regional distributors, due to its strategic location with direct access to major thoroughfares.
