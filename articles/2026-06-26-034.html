Clarion pays $132M for a new manufacturing plant in Fremont leased by Tesla

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 20:03:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Clarion Partners has acquired a newly developed 267,100-square-foot industrial manufacturing facility in Fremont, CA, for $132.3 million. The property, known as Milmont Industrial, was sold by Hines and Oaktree Capital Management, who redeveloped the site after purchasing it in 2021. The facility is fully leased to an electric vehicle manufacturer, underscoring Fremont's growing significance as an industrial and manufacturing hub in the Bay Area.
