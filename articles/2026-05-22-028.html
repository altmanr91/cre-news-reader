Related Group scores rezoning for 20-story apartments in busy Riviera Beach

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 21 May 2026 17:42:04 +0000

SUMMARY

The Related Group and its partners secured zoning approval for Gallery at Marina Village, a 20-story, 418-unit market-rate apartment project in Riviera Beach. The development, which includes retail and office space, is part of the larger Marina Village redevelopment. The project is situated on a 2.5-acre site leased from the Riviera Beach Community Redevelopment Agency.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Gallery at Marina Village

Address: 1200-1300 Broadway, Riviera Beach, FL

Size: 531,000 SF

Units: 418

Site Area: 2.5 acres

Notable Features: The project will include a nine-level parking garage, 3,300 square feet of retail, and 3,000 square feet of offices facing the U.S. 1/Broadway thoroughfare.

Project Notes: The developers secured a 99-year lease for the site in June 2023. The project is part of the 80-acre Marina Village redevelopment area. The city's planning and zoning board had previously pushed for workforce housing at this project, but it was not included in the rezoning documents.
