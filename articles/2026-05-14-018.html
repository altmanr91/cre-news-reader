Sam Sprei, former Brooklyn judge arrested

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 13 May 2026 17:18:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A former Brooklyn judge, Edward Harold King, and real estate operator Sam Sprei have been arrested and charged with criminal wire fraud conspiracy. Prosecutors allege the pair diverted $6.5 million in investor escrow funds intended for a bankruptcy property sale in Freehold, New Jersey. The funds were supposedly for 'proof of liquidity' for a property purchase, but were instead allegedly siphoned off, with only a partial return to investors.
