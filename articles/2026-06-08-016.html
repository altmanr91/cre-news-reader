One-two-three punch of Mavs, Stars and Neiman bruises struggling Downtown Dallas

The Real Deal — Texas — Sun, 07 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Downtown Dallas is facing significant challenges with the departure of several key anchors. The Dallas Mavericks are relocating to Valley View, the Dallas Stars are leaving American Airlines Center, and the Neiman Marcus flagship store at 1618 Main Street is closing in September due to Saks Global's bankruptcy. These departures follow AT&T's earlier decision to move its 2 million-square-foot office footprint to Plano, further exacerbating the urban core's struggles. Meanwhile, Plano continues to attract major businesses, including Samsung's relocation of its U.S. operations from New Jersey.
