Costco, multifamily on tap as mixed-use projects power Pompano Beach

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 20:47:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Pompano Beach is experiencing a surge in mixed-use development, with two significant projects heading to the city's planning and zoning board for review. One proposal involves a new 163,000-square-foot Costco Wholesale store, complete with a tire center and gas station, to be built on an 18.7-acre parcel within The Pomp master-planned development. The other project, by Vera Fund, is a 132-unit multifamily building at 1600 South Federal Highway, which will also include 3,700 square feet of retail space and retain an existing office building on the site. These developments reflect Pompano Beach's growing appeal to developers seeking opportunities outside of more established South Florida markets.
