Ally Bridge Relocates U.S. Headquarters to Rudin’s 560 Lexington

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 19 May 2026 20:50:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Ally Bridge Group, a global investment management firm specializing in healthcare and life science, has signed a 10-year, 10-month lease to relocate its U.S. headquarters to 560 Lexington in New York City's Plaza District. The firm will occupy 6,440 square feet on the 18th floor of the 22-story office tower, with the move expected in early 2027. This long-term commitment underscores the Plaza District's continued appeal to the global investment community.
