SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 11 May 2026 21:20:10 +0000

SUMMARY

A banned financial adviser has found a buyer for his waterfront Miami home, which was asking $19.9 million. The 7,237-square-foot house sits on a nearly half-acre lot. This transaction is part of a broader trend of luxury home contracts signed in Miami-Dade County, with 22 such deals closing in the first week of May.
