Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

This article is a digest of top residential real estate transactions in South Florida. The most expensive sale was a 22,900-square-foot mansion in Manalapan, which traded for $70.9 million. Additionally, a 3.9-acre vacant waterfront lot in Manalapan sold for $32 million, with another portion of the same estate under contract for $35 million. A luxury condo in Coconut Grove also changed hands for $9.6 million.
