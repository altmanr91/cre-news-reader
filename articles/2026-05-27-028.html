Pending sale of McMackins’ $85M Manalapan estate tops signed contracts in Palm Beach

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 26 May 2026 19:26:59 +0000

SUMMARY

A lake-to-ocean estate in Manalapan, featuring a unique underground tunnel, is under contract for $85 million, marking the highest-priced pending sale in Palm Beach County last week. The 2-acre property, built in 2020, includes a 22,900-square-foot mansion with seven bedrooms and 11.5 bathrooms, along with an air-conditioned beach bungalow. This transaction was part of a broader trend of 15 luxury properties, including 10 single-family homes and five condos, going under contract in the county, totaling nearly $210 million in asking dollar volume.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Address: 1660 South Ocean Boulevard, Manalapan, FL

Size: 22,900 SF

Site Area: 2 acres

Notable Features: Lake-to-ocean estate with an underground tunnel connecting the mansion to an air-conditioned beach bungalow.

Project Notes: The sellers previously acquired the property for $38.9 million in 2020, the same year the house was built.
