Shallow Bay Industrial Portfolio Trades in East Bay

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 20:47:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Newmark arranged the sale of the Sierra Trinity industrial portfolio, an 11-building shallow bay industrial asset in Dublin, CA. The property was 97% leased to a diverse tenant mix at the time of sale, offering strong in-place cash flow and future upside through rent mark-to-market opportunities. The competitive marketing process for the portfolio highlighted robust demand for multi-tenant, shallow bay industrial product in infill locations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Sierra Trinity

Address: 6701 Sierra Ct., Dublin, CA

Occupancy at Sale: 97.0%

Notable Features: 11-building shallow bay industrial portfolio.

Project Notes: Average tenant commitment exceeding nine years.
