Investment Firm Acquires SoHo Mixed-Use for $43M

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 22 May 2026 20:08:11 +0000

SUMMARY

AV Management, a New York-based investment firm, has acquired the mixed-use property at 73-75 Sullivan St. in SoHo for $43.33 million from a long-term SoHo developer. The transaction was financed with a $21.6 million acquisition loan from Citizens Private Bank. AV Management noted that the acquisition represents an opportunity to purchase investment-grade assets at high yields by leveraging early-cycle market dynamics and the dislocation caused by recent interest rate hikes, with plans to hold the asset long-term for income.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 73-75 Sullivan St, New York City, NY

Sale Price: $43.3M

Year Built: 2016

Notable Features: Modern construction vintage with expansive layouts and long-term commercial tenancy.

Project Notes: Acquired at an opportunistic basis to be held long-term as a permanent income vehicle.
