Baptist Health sells Publix-anchored Cutler Bay development site for $30M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 19:58:02 +0000

SUMMARY

Baptist Health South Florida sold a 25.5-acre development site in Cutler Bay for a total of $29.8 million in two separate transactions. The site is planned for a mixed-use project, including a Publix-anchored shopping center and a multifamily development. This sale aligns with Baptist Health's strategy to divest surplus land, capitalizing on developer interest in South Miami-Dade for residential and retail projects.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Sale Price: $29.8M

Site Area: 25.5 acres

Project Notes: The site was sold in two transactions: 15 acres for residential development and 10.5 acres for retail development.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Baptist Health South Florida

BUYER: Kolter Group

BUYER: AEW Capital Management

LENDER: Santander Bank

TENANTS AT PROPERTY

Publix

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Publix has been actively acquiring shopping centers it occupies across Florida, spending $326.5 million on such acquisitions over the past two years. Recent purchases include Hammocks Town Center in Kendall for $71.9 million and Fountains of Boynton near Boynton Beach for $78 million.
