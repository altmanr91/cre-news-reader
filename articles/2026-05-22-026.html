Riviera Beach delays vote on impact fee hike after Gold Coast Builders challenge

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 21 May 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Riviera Beach officials have postponed a vote on a proposed 300% increase in development impact fees after the Gold Coast Builders Association raised concerns about the legality and potential negative impact on development. The city council deferred the decision to allow staff to meet with the builders' association, which argues that the proposed fees may not comply with a 2024 state law requiring inter-county agreements for transportation impact fees. City officials contend that the fee hike is necessary to fund infrastructure and services for an anticipated 4,500 new residential units by 2040, but the city attorney expressed uncertainty about the legal defensibility of the proposed fees.
