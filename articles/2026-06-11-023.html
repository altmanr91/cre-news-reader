DR Horton chases south Miami-Dade supply in $17M on Homestead land buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

D.R. Horton has acquired 97 residential lots across two parcels in Homestead, FL, for $16.9 million, continuing its strategy to secure developable land in south Miami-Dade. This acquisition, which closed at approximately $174,000 per lot, underscores the ongoing trend of homebuilders expanding into the region due to land scarcity and more favorable pricing compared to the county's urban core. The company had previously placed the site under contract in 2024 with a mortgage deposit.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Property Name: Aspen Estates East and Aspen Estates West

Units: 97

Sale Price: $16.9M

Project Notes: The site was under contract from M Aspen Homestead in 2024 through a $2.53 million mortgage deposit.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: D.R. Horton

SELLER: M Aspen Homestead LLC

SPONSOR PIPELINE

19300 and 19360 Southwest 344th Street: Proposed 247-unit project comprising 190 townhouses and 57 single-family homes on a 26.2-acre site.

19470 and 19400 Mowry Drive: Proposed development with 34 single-family homes on 7.37 acres.

Sandero Landing community: Planned 1,100 residential units, two- and three-story townhomes, a four-story mixed-use residential development and more than 190,000 square feet of commercial space on 97 acres at Southwest 328th Street and Southeast 6th Street.
