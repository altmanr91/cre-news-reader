IRES REIT: New Rent Rules Boosting Dublin Residential Investment

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 29 May 2026 08:09:19 -0400

SUMMARY

Irish Residential Properties REIT (IRES REIT) is optimistic about the growth opportunities in Ireland's residential rental market following recent regulatory reforms. The company reported strong Q1 2026 performance with over 99% occupancy and rent collection, and a net rental income margin of 78%. CEO Eddie Byrne noted that the new regulations are already positively impacting market conditions, leading to increased development activity, international capital allocation, and transaction volumes for apartment blocks. IRES REIT recently forward-purchased 77 apartments for €31.75M, its first acquisition in several years, and continues to recycle capital through disposals at premiums above book value.
