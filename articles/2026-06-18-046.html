Global Holdings Wraps Work on 99 Park Avenue Repositioning

Connect CRE — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 20:51:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Global Holdings has completed a $30 million repositioning of 99 Park Avenue, a 600,000-square-foot Class A office tower in New York City. The project, designed in collaboration with Vocon, focused on enhancing the building's arrival experience while preserving its Art Deco character. The building is currently 97% leased, reflecting strong tenant interest in the upgraded space.
