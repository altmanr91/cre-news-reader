Longacre nabs one of Houston’s “most f-cked up” apartments after years of distress

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 18:45:40 +0000

SUMMARY

Longacre Asset Management has acquired the 541-unit Villa Nueva Apartments in Houston, a property previously owned by SouthState Bank following foreclosure proceedings against Kalkan Capital. The New York-based firm plans to revitalize the "extremely distressed" complex, which had gained notoriety for its poor conditions and numerous tenant complaints. This acquisition highlights the ongoing challenges in the Class B and C multifamily sector across Texas, where many value-add strategies have faltered due to rising interest rates.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Villa Nueva Apartments

Address: 5300 West Gulf Bank Road, Houston, TX

Units: 541

Notable Features: Property was featured in a viral TikTok video for its poor conditions and topped the list of Houston apartment complexes for 311 complaints in 2022.

Project Notes: SouthState Bank seized control of the property from Kalkan Capital in January 2026 after initiating foreclosure proceedings in July 2025.
