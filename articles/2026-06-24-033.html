Frisco mansion with unique 3D-printed steel frame lists for $15M

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A luxury mansion in Frisco, TX, featuring a digitally designed and 3D-printed steel frame, has been listed for $15 million. The innovative construction method significantly reduced framing time. The 13,559-square-foot residence, expected to be completed in 2027, offers six bedrooms, six full bathrooms, a glass elevator, and an infinity-edge pool, with views of The Star and a nature preserve. The developer noted the steel frame provides enhanced wind resistance, a valuable feature in the region.
