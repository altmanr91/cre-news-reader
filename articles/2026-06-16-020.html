Haruvi family company files for bankruptcy, affecting nearly 500 apartments

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

REO / Foreclosure

Simry Realty Corp, a company linked to the Haruvi family, has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy, impacting nearly 500 apartments across seven Manhattan properties. The bankruptcy filing aims to halt a protracted legal battle with family member Michelle Haruvi, who claims she was unfairly excluded from a 2022 company restructuring. This litigation has reportedly hindered the company's ability to refinance its mortgages, which matured in June 2024.
