SUMMARY

A vacant development site in Greenpoint, Brooklyn, was sold for $25.6 million. The property, located at the corner of West and Milton Street, was acquired by Seventy West Realty LLC from an LLC associated with Pearl Realty Management. This transaction represents the largest commercial deal recorded in New York City during the specified period.
