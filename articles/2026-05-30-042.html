PCCP, Alliance Residential Snap Up Garden-Style Riverside Complex

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Fri, 29 May 2026 21:34:26 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between PCCP and Alliance Residential Company has acquired The Hawthorne, a 184-unit garden-style multifamily community in Riverside, CA, for $65.3 million. The property, delivered in 2023, features townhome-style architecture and a strong amenity package. This acquisition represents an opportunity to secure a Class A asset in a high-growth Southern California submarket with favorable long-term demographic and demand drivers.
