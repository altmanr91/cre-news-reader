Yorba Linda Parking Lot Sells for Townhome Redevelopment

SALE

Connect CRE — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 20:35:09 +0000

SUMMARY

A 1.9-acre parking lot in Yorba Linda, CA, was sold for $8.12 million to an Orange County-based housing developer. The buyer plans to redevelop the site into 40 townhomes, with site work expected to commence shortly. The transaction highlights the robust demand for residential development opportunities in North Orange County, a market characterized by limited land availability and high barriers to entry.
