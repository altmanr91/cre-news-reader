“Perpetual danger” for Fisher Island families: lawsuit filed over “secret” $400M fuel depot deal with Miami-Dade

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 28 May 2026 23:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Fisher Island residents have filed a lawsuit against developer HRP, alleging a "secret" $400 million deal with Miami-Dade County for a fuel depot site. The residents claim HRP reneged on an earlier agreement to demolish the existing fuel bunker, remediate the land, and develop luxury condominiums, instead pursuing a sale of the entire 9.6-acre property to the county. The lawsuit seeks to compel HRP to honor its original commitment, which included conveying 4 acres to the Fisher Island Community Association for development. Residents express concerns about the safety of the aging fuel depot, citing its proximity to homes and a school, and its location in a hurricane flood zone.
