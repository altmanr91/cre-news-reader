Personal Care Brand Athena Club Expands Headquarters with 140 Broadway Move

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 12 May 2026 22:19:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Athena Club, a rapidly expanding personal care brand, has signed an 11,857-square-foot office lease for its new headquarters at 140 Broadway in Downtown Manhattan. The move from its previous location at 915 Broadway accommodates the company's significant growth, which saw triple-digit expansion in 2025 and projected sales exceeding $100 million in 2026. The new space on the 44th floor of 140 Broadway is expected to support the brand's continued trajectory.
