Brookfield Sells Bethesda Tower For 87% Less Than It Paid In 2011

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 26 May 2026 10:47:03 -0400

SUMMARY

In-Rel Properties has acquired the ground lease for 3 Bethesda Metro Center, a 388,000-square-foot office building in Bethesda, MD, from Brookfield for $20 million. This transaction represents an 87% discount from the $150.1 million Brookfield paid for the ground lease in 2011. In-Rel plans a substantial capital improvement program for the 52% leased property, including facade enhancements, a redesigned lobby, and new amenities, aiming to reposition it for modern tenants. The acquisition complements In-Rel's existing office building across the street, with the two properties now forming "In-Rel Plaza."
