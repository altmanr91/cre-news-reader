Brookfield Asset Management Reports $67B in Fresh Capital in 2026 So Far

Commercial Observer — Fri, 08 May 2026 18:30:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Earnings Report

Brookfield Asset Management reported a strong first quarter in 2026, raising $67 billion in fresh capital year-to-date, driven by two flagship fund launches and a $40 billion insurance mandate. The asset manager's fee-related earnings increased by 11 percent year-over-year to $772 million, with distributable earnings rising to $702 million. CEO Connor Teskey expressed an optimistic outlook for 2026, anticipating a record year for fundraising and significant increases in real estate transaction activity and valuations, particularly in alternative sectors like hospitality, logistics, and housing.
