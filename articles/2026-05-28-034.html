Scott Everett’s S2 Capital faces $78M foreclosure amid REIT equity wipeout

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 27 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

S2 Capital is facing foreclosure on The Republic Apartments in Garland, TX, after allegedly defaulting on a $78.6 million loan provided by Benefit Street Partners in 2021. The 1,033-unit multifamily property, comprising Republic West and Republic East, is scheduled for a foreclosure auction, though S2 Capital's Scott Everett stated the property is under contract for sale. This distress comes as S2 Capital's REIT, formed to manage floating-rate debt, faces significant financial challenges, with equity investors warned to expect a full loss of capital.
