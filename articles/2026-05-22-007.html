CapRock Partners Breaks Ground on 250,000 SF Industrial Project in McKinney, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 21 May 2026 14:20:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CapRock Partners has commenced construction on McKinney Air Business Park, a two-building industrial development totaling 250,000 square feet in McKinney, Texas. The shallow-bay project, situated on a 15.3-acre site adjacent to McKinney National Airport, is expected to be completed in early 2027. Colliers is handling the leasing efforts for the property.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: McKinney Air Business Park

Address: 502 Bloomdale Rd, McKinney, TX*

Size: 250,000 SF

Completion: early 2027

Site Area: 15.3 acres

Notable Features: Two-building, shallow-bay development with 32-foot clear heights, ESFR sprinkler systems, ample dock-high loading, and 130-foot truck court depths, adjacent to McKinney National Airport.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
