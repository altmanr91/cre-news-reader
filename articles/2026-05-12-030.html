Law firm Hall Prangle jumps to La Caisse, Hines’ revamped Riverside Plaza

LEASE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 12 May 2026 00:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hall Prangle, a Chicago trial defense firm, has signed a long-term lease for approximately 29,000 square feet at 10 South Riverside Plaza, a move that highlights the ongoing flight to quality in the city's office market. The law firm will occupy the 12th floor of the 21-story building, which recently underwent a $75 million capital improvement project by joint owners Hines and La Caisse. The upgraded space is designed to accommodate the firm's growth, allowing them to increase their attorney roster without expanding their overall footprint, and better aligns with their collaborative culture.
