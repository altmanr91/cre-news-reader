Cain, Alchemy-ABR Secure $321M Refi for Newly Delivered Plaza District Offices

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Thu, 21 May 2026 22:00:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Cain and Alchemy-ABR Investment Partners have secured a $321 million refinancing package for 125 W. 57th St., a recently completed Class A office tower located in Manhattan's Plaza District. The financing was provided by JPMorgan Chase as the senior lender and Hudson Bay Capital as the mezzanine lender. This refinancing underscores the strong demand for high-quality assets in prime locations, reflecting the project's exceptional design and execution.
