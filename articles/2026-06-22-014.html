JLL Capital Markets closes $18.55 million sale of renovated office building in Shoreview

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 09:46:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has completed the $18.55 million sale of The Lex, a recently renovated 124,925-square-foot office building in Shoreview, Minnesota. The property, which underwent renovations in 2023, is 95% leased to a diverse tenant mix across financial services, healthcare, and technology sectors. The building's prime location within the Shoreview Corporate Center offers convenient access to retail, dining, and major interstates, enhancing its appeal to tenants and investors.
