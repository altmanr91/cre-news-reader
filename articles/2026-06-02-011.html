Boston suburb set for transformative development after rezoning approval

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 19:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Brookline, MA, has approved a rezoning measure for the Chestnut Hill Commercial Area Study, paving the way for a significant mixed-use development by City Realty Group. The project, planned for a five-acre site, will feature a 200-room hotel, 266 residential units (apartments or condos), medical office space, and retail. This approval, which passed with strong support, is expected to generate substantial commercial tax revenue and contribute to the town's affordable housing and transportation initiatives, transforming the Route 9 corridor.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 1280-13330 Boylston Street, Brookline, MA

Units: 266

Keys: 200

Site Area: 5 acres

Notable Features: Mixed-use complex including hotel, residential, medical office, and retail space.

Project Notes: Site previously acquired for $41 million in 2024. Developer is contributing $11 million to Brookline's Affordable Housing Trust Fund and $12 million to transportation and public safety. Construction is expected to begin in 2028, pending environmental and special permit approvals.
