Rubell family sells another Allapattah property to Neology for $24M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 20:33:29 +0000

SUMMARY

The Rubell family sold a 46,870-square-foot warehouse in Allapattah, Miami, to Neology Group for $24 million, marking a significant increase in value since its acquisition in 2022. This transaction is intended for phases two and three of a larger residential project. The deal follows a previous $15 million land sale to Neology for the first phase of the same development, highlighting the Rubell family's continued divestment of their Allapattah holdings to residential developers.
