Presidio Bay moves in on Mack Real Estate loan tied to historic downtown SF offices

LOAN

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 18 May 2026 19:28:08 +0000

SUMMARY

Presidio Bay Ventures is in discussions to acquire a nonperforming $100 million loan tied to the historic 301 Battery Street office building in San Francisco. The acquisition of this debt from Mack Real Estate, which originated the loan when RFR Holdings purchased the property in 2020, would position Presidio Bay to take control of the asset through foreclosure or a deed-in-lieu process. The potential deal, valued at approximately $70 million, reflects a significant discount from the property's last sale price and highlights the ongoing challenges in the downtown San Francisco office market.
