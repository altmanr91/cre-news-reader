Floyd Mayweather Jr. didn’t buy a stake in former Versace Mansion

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 29 May 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Boxing champion Floyd Mayweather Jr. did not proceed with his planned acquisition of a 20% stake in the Versace Mansion, also known as Villa Casuarina, in Miami Beach. Mayweather had made a $250,000 deposit in 2024 for the interest in the luxury boutique hotel, which is owned by real estate investor Eli Gindi and the Nakash family. Gindi confirmed that Mayweather decided not to close on the deal and his deposit was returned.
