Miamians sue to stop Trump presidential library in downtown Miami, again

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:15:01 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A group of Miami residents has filed a lawsuit to halt the development of a proposed Trump presidential library in downtown Miami, alleging the land transfer for the project is unconstitutional. This marks the second legal challenge against the project, with plaintiffs arguing that the inclusion of a potential hotel and the use of the Trump Organization's branding indicate an intent to monetize the skyscraper for personal profit, violating the Emoluments Clause. The lawsuit also cites concerns about the tower's impact on neighborhood views and living conditions.
