Union Square comeback continues with 14K sf Market Street museum lease

LEASE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 15 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Museum of Illusions has signed a 14,300-square-foot lease at 735 Market Street in San Francisco, occupying a space that has been vacant since 2020. This lease is a significant step in the ongoing revitalization of the Market Street corridor and the broader Union Square neighborhood, which has seen increased foot traffic and declining vacancies. The museum, known for its interactive and social-media-friendly exhibits, is part of an aggressive U.S. expansion strategy by its new investor group.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 735 Market Street, San Francisco, CA

Size: 14,300 SF

Notable Features: The space was formerly occupied by Decathlon and has been vacant since 2020.

Project Notes: Tenant improvements are estimated to cost $1.6 million.
