Gold Coast mansion relists for $9M after seller’s-remorse saga

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A Gold Coast mansion in Chicago, previously the subject of a "seller's remorse" lawsuit, has been relisted for $8.99 million. The owners, Jack and Julie McGinley, settled a lawsuit earlier this year with Jason and Courtney Kuhn, who had a binding contract to purchase the property for $7.4 million. The McGinleys had backed out of the prior sale, leading to the legal dispute. This relisting marks a significant price reduction from its original $14.5 million asking price a decade ago.
