Rivani sells waterfront Palm Island home to Jose Mas, partners for $25M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 15:07:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Commercial real estate investor Robert Rivani sold a waterfront mansion on Palm Island in Miami Beach for $25 million. The buyer was a company led by Jose Mas of the Mas family and architect Cesar Molina, who are actively acquiring and redeveloping homes in South Florida. Rivani had purchased the property six months prior for $22.9 million and undertook renovations, selling it for a $2.1 million profit.
