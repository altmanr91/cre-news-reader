GD Holdings buys 16 St. Regis Chicago condos from Sony for $21M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 26 May 2026 17:07:58 +0000

SUMMARY

GD Holdings has acquired a portfolio of 16 luxury condominiums, including penthouse units, within the St. Regis Chicago from Sony for $21 million. This transaction further solidifies GD Holdings' significant ownership stake in the Jeanne Gang-designed supertall, following a previous bulk purchase of 84 units from the original developer. Sony, an entertainment company, had only recently obtained these units from Dalian Wanda Group to settle an intellectual property dispute, indicating a strong motivation to quickly divest the real estate assets at a discount.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: St. Regis Chicago

Address: The St Regis Hotel, 401 E Wacker Dr, Chicago, IL*

Units: 16

Sale Price: $21.0M

Notable Features: Includes penthouse units on the 89th and 77th floors of the 101-story supertall tower.

Project Notes: Sony acquired these units in January from Dalian Wanda Group to settle a $49 million intellectual property dispute. GD Holdings previously acquired 84 unsold units from Magellan in late 2024 for $117 million.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Penthouse units at the St. Regis Chicago are currently leasing for around $35,000 per month, while half-floor condo units command roughly $25,000 a month.
