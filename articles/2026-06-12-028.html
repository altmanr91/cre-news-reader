RCI Marine, Suntex’s $80M marina redev plans head to Miami voters

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Miami commissioners have unanimously approved a measure to place an $80 million marina redevelopment proposal on the November ballot for voter approval. The proposal, from a joint venture between RCI Marine Group and Suntex Marinas, seeks to modernize the Rickenbacker Marina and Marine Stadium Marina on Virginia Key. This decision follows a court order compelling the city to advance the proposal after finding the city had improperly rejected the joint venture's prior bids. The proposed lease, which could extend up to 75 years, includes annual rent payments to the city starting at $2.2 million, plus a percentage of gross revenue.
