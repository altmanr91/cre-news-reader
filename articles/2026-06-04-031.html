How top South Side multifamily brokers became owners of $70M in real estate

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:52:55 +0000

SUMMARY

BSG, an investment firm formed by former Kiser Group brokers Noah Birk, Aaron Sklar, and Dan Gonzalez, has acquired a 16-unit multifamily property at 1552 North LaSalle Drive in Chicago for $6.2 million. The property, located in Old Town, recently underwent a gut renovation with condo-quality finishes. This acquisition adds to BSG's growing portfolio, which now totals approximately $70 million in Chicago-area real estate. The transaction highlights the firm's strategy of investing in stabilized, recently renovated buildings and boutique new construction, even as the broader South Side multifamily market, where the principals previously brokered many deals, faces increased financial distress.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Per-unit prices in Chicago's lower-cost multifamily neighborhoods surged to nearly $73,000 per door in 2024, up from less than $60,000 in 2019, according to inflation-adjusted DePaul University data. Cap rates in Chicago's South Side multifamily market previously reached double-digit figures, significantly higher than national averages of around or below 5 percent a few years ago.
