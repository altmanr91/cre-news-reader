KBS lists downtown high-rise as Austin office owners hunt for exits

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

KBS has listed its 26-story office tower at 515 North Congress Avenue in Downtown Austin for sale. The 267,956-square-foot building, constructed in 1975 and currently 82 percent leased, is being marketed by JLL Capital Markets. KBS acquired the property in 2015 for $112.5 million, and its sale comes as Austin's office market faces high vacancy rates and a slowdown in corporate relocations.
