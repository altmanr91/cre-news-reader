Brookfield Raises $900M For Fund Targeting CRE Liquidity Needs

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 12:58:53 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Fund Close

Brookfield Asset Management has successfully raised approximately $900 million for its Brookfield Real Estate Solutions II fund, which focuses on general partner-led recapitalizations and equity solutions for real estate assets. The fund, backed by a diverse group of global investors including public pensions and insurance companies, will target value-add returns across North America, the UK, Europe, and Australia. Its primary investment focus will be on the residential and logistics sectors, with potential allocations to self-storage, cold storage, and life sciences properties.
