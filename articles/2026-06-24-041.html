Cityview, Stockbridge Cut Ribbon on Gardena Multifamily

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 21:52:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Cityview and Stockbridge have celebrated the grand opening of Apollo, a 265-unit multifamily community in Los Angeles' South Bay. The project aims to address the region's housing shortage by providing new residences in a job-rich area, specifically within the aerospace corridor. Its strategic location offers convenient access to major employment centers, public transit, and key regional attractions.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Apollo

Address: 12888 Crenshaw Blvd., Los Angeles, CA

Units: 265

Notable Features: Located in the heart of the aerospace corridor, within five miles of over 260,000 jobs, and a short walk from the Metro Green Line.
