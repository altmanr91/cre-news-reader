Dezer targets broader market with North Miami residential development

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 21:31:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Dezer Development received unanimous planning board approval for Dezerland Park West, a 586-unit residential project in North Miami. The development will feature two buildings with apartments and townhouses, along with retail space and shared amenities. This project marks a strategic shift for Dezer, known for ultra-luxury, automobile-branded towers, as it targets a broader market segment.
