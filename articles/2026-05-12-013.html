The Missner Group begins construction on industrial-to-multifamily conversion in Chicago’s West Loop

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Mon, 11 May 2026 16:43:59 +0000

SUMMARY

The Missner Group and Base 3 have commenced construction on Oxxford Lofts, a $42 million adaptive reuse project in Chicago's West Loop. The historic industrial building, formerly owned by Oxxford Clothes, will be converted into a 112-unit multifamily community. The development will feature modern amenities, upgraded sustainability features, and 20% of the units will be designated as affordable housing.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Oxxford Lofts

Address: 1220 W. Van Buren Street, Chicago, IL

Units: 112

Total Project Cost: $42.0M

$/Unit: $375,000/unit (calculated)

Completion: Spring 2027

Notable Features: Historic industrial building conversion with upgraded sustainability features, rooftop deck, fitness center, coworking space, and secure bike storage. Located in West Loop with access to Fulton Market, public transit, and major highways.

Project Notes: 20% of units will be designated as affordable housing.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
