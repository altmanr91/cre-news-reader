DWS sells Jewel Osco-anchored shopping center for $70M

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 30 Jun 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hines has acquired a Jewel Osco-anchored shopping center in Chicago's Wicker Park neighborhood for $70 million from DWS Asset Management. The transaction highlights continued investor interest in grocery-anchored retail, which has demonstrated resilience post-pandemic due to its perceived immunity to e-commerce trends. While Chicago's urban retail transaction volume has slowed, suburban retail activity has increased in areas with population and multifamily growth.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 1341 North Paulina Street, Chicago, IL

Sale Price: $70.0M

Project Notes: DWS' RREEF Property Trust previously acquired the property in 2015 for $94 million, as part of a larger transaction that included additional properties along Milwaukee Avenue and Ashland Avenue.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Hines

SELLER: DWS Asset Management

TENANTS AT PROPERTY

Jewel Osco

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Grocery-anchored shopping centers have performed well since the pandemic, as investors view them as less susceptible to online shopping impacts. Retail transactions in Chicago have slowed recently, while suburban retail activity has increased in areas experiencing population growth and multifamily investment.
