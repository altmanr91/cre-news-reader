High Street, Tokyu Break Ground on 281-Unit Multifamily Project in Downtown Richardson, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 07 May 2026 14:49:43 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between High Street Residential and Tokyu Land Corp. has commenced construction on a 281-unit multifamily development in downtown Richardson, Texas. The project will offer a mix of studio, one-, and two-bedroom units with a variety of amenities, and is expected to be completed in late 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 110 E. Polk St., Richardson, TX

Units: 281

Completion: 4Q 2027

Notable Features: Amenities will include a pool, clubroom, coworking areas, grilling stations, a fitness center, dog wash station and a package room.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: High Street Residential

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Tokyu Land Corp.

ARCHITECT: WDG Architects

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Andres Construction
