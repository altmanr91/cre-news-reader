“Underhanded”: Michael Stern’s JDS sues lender back over foreclosure of Mercedes-Benz site

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 13 May 2026 16:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

JDS Development Group is suing its lender, Cottonwood Management, alleging an "underhanded proceeding" related to the foreclosure of the Mercedes-Benz Places condo site in Miami's Brickell. JDS claims Cottonwood initiated foreclosure without proper notification and in violation of a non-disclosure agreement, causing significant damage to the stalled nearly 800-unit, two-tower development. The developer asserts the foreclosure has jeopardized its public benefits agreement with the city, deterred buyers, and impeded efforts to secure new construction financing for the $2 billion project.
