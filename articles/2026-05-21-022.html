Mamdani targets Brooklyn corridors for major housing push

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 20 May 2026 17:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

New York City Mayor Zohran Mamdani's administration is initiating its first significant neighborhood rezoning effort, the "South of Prospect Plan," in Brooklyn. This plan targets commercial corridors along Coney Island and McDonald avenues, aiming to allow for taller buildings and thousands of new apartments. The initiative is a key part of the city's strategy for transit-oriented development, leveraging the area's proximity to subway lines and the proposed Interborough Express light rail. The Department of City Planning has begun public engagement, with a draft proposal expected next year, as the city seeks to address its housing shortage and create more affordable units.
