Jagged little sale: Alanis Morissette quickly unloads Lafayette home for more than $2M above asking

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

Singer Alanis Morissette has sold her personal residence at 3958 South Peardale Drive in Lafayette, CA for $9.6 million. The property, listed under Baz Trust, was purchased by Nicholas James Sunseri. The home was on the market for only a week, selling for $2.1 million above its asking price.

It also features a separate pool house with a full bathroom and sauna, a swimming pool, outdoor pavilion, exterior kitchen, sports court, and a custom treehouse.
