“Parcel manipulation”: Homestead homeowners sue over developer’s two-decade control of HOA

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Homeowners at Villa Portofino East, a 117-unit townhouse complex in Homestead, FL, have filed a lawsuit alleging that the developer, Prime Homes at Villa Portofino East (an affiliate of Prime Group), has illegally maintained control of the HOA board for two decades. The suit claims the developer has manipulated parcel calculations by including land transferred for a new hotel development as part of the residential community's total parcels, thereby artificially delaying the statutory 90% conveyance threshold for homeowner turnover. Residents also allege neglect of common area maintenance under the developer-controlled board.
