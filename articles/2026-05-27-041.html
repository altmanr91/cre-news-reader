Gastropub Operator Chooses NoMad for Fifth Manhattan Location

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 26 May 2026 22:34:39 +0000

SUMMARY

A gastropub operator has secured a 13,900-square-foot lease for its fifth Manhattan location at 373 Park Avenue South in the NoMad neighborhood. The space, formerly occupied by a long-standing restaurant, offers a significant footprint across the ground floor, lower level, and a mezzanine. This transaction highlights the ongoing demand from established food and beverage concepts for prominent, large-format restaurant spaces in Midtown South.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 373 Park Ave. South, New York City, NY

Size: 13,900 SF

Notable Features: Second-generation restaurant space with ground floor, lower level, and mezzanine components.

Project Notes: Space was formerly occupied by Dos Caminos for over 20 years.
