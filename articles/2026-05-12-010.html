Partnership Breaks Ground on Phase I of Affordable Housing Redevelopment Project in West Philadelphia

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 11 May 2026 14:21:16 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership between LMXD, MSquared, and the Philadelphia Housing Authority has broken ground on the first phase of the Westpark Apartments redevelopment in West Philadelphia. This initial phase involves constructing a new 145-unit midrise building with ground-floor retail, rehabilitating an existing 141-unit building, and adding 41 new units. The project emphasizes affordability, with units reserved for existing residents and new households earning between 20 and 80 percent of the area median income.
