Associated Bank Completes $23M Retail Redevelopment Loan in Buffalo Grove

LOAN

Connect CRE — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 20:05:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Associated Bank has provided a $22.6 million loan to Shorewood Development Group for the acquisition and redevelopment of a vacant 66,083-square-foot former grocery store in Buffalo Grove, Illinois. The project, named Chase Plaza, will transform the property into a two-tenant, grocery-anchored retail center. This marks the 13th loan Associated Bank has extended to Shorewood Development Group, highlighting a strong ongoing relationship between the two entities.
