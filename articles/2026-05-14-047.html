Compass Datacenters CEO On Walking Away From 12M SF Project: 'We'll Go To Another Spot'

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:49:14 -0400

SUMMARY

Development

Compass Datacenters has withdrawn from a massive 11.6M SF data center development in Prince William County, Virginia, after a rezoning approval was overturned and the county signaled its opposition to the project. CEO Chris Crosby stated the company spent $40M pursuing the project but will now seek other locations, emphasizing that the U.S. is large enough to find communities that welcome data center investment. The decision follows a legal challenge by preservation groups and a shift in local political sentiment against data center development near the Manassas National Battlefield Park.
