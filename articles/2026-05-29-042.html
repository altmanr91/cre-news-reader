Senior Living Facilities: Don’t Forget the Outdoor Space

Connect CRE — Fri, 29 May 2026 05:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article, authored by landscape architect David McCullough, argues that outdoor spaces in senior living facilities are as crucial as indoor amenities for residents' well-being and should be prioritized from the outset of design. McCullough contends that modern seniors are more active and healthier than previous generations, requiring outdoor environments that support activities like pickleball, cooking, and socialization. He emphasizes that while capital investments vary by care level and location, strategically planned outdoor spaces can offer a decent return on investment and significantly improve residents' physical and mental health, reducing isolation and stress.
