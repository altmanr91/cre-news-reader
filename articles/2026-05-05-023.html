📊 South Florida’s top deals: Cortland sells Boca Raton apartments for $240M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 04 May 2026 22:51:29 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Reserve acquired the Residences at Uptown Boca, a 456-unit apartment complex in Boca Raton, for $240 million from Cortland Partners. The transaction for the 643,000-square-foot property, situated on 16.3 acres, equates to approximately $526,000 per apartment. This sale marks a significant commercial deal in the South Florida market.
