Spanish developer fires back at Laura Tauber’s suit over Miami-Dade’s biggest Live Local project

The Real Deal — Miami — Sat, 09 May 2026 00:12:27 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Spanish developer has responded to a lawsuit filed by a former collaborator over a significant Live Local Act project in Miami-Dade County. The plaintiff alleges she was promised profit shares and sidelined from the development after working on it for three years without compensation. The developer, however, denies she was ever a partner, asserting her participation was contingent on her bringing in equity and sharing project costs, which she failed to do. The dispute centers on the nature of their agreement and the plaintiff's expected compensation for her contributions to the project.
