Basis Industrial Closes on Construction Loan for Wayne, NJ Self-Storage

LOAN

Connect CRE — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 20:00:58 +0000

SUMMARY

Basis Industrial has secured a $24 million construction loan for a new self-storage development in Wayne, NJ. The project, which will feature 85,330 square feet and 586 climate-controlled units, is expected to break ground in August 2026 and be delivered in February 2028. This development addresses the high demand for self-storage in a supply-constrained Northern New Jersey market with strong demographics.
