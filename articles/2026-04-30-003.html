Global Holdings, MAG Partners to Develop 149-Unit Resi Tower in Hudson Square

DEVELOPMENT

Commercial Observer — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 19:54:08 +0000

SUMMARY

Global Holdings and MAG Partners have formed a joint venture to develop a 149-unit residential tower at 122 Varick Street in Hudson Square, New York, under a long-term ground lease with Trinity Church. The 192,000-square-foot project, developed under New York State's 485x program, will include 25 percent permanently affordable units and 5,000 square feet of ground-floor retail. The developers emphasized building for the long term, given Trinity Church's centuries-long stewardship of the land, and highlighted the project's design-forward approach and commitment to civic stewardship.
