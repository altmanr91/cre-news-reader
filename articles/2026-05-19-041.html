Records Hint Dilweg Is Behind On Loan For Landmark Atlanta Skyscraper

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 18 May 2026 17:35:19 -0400

SUMMARY

Loan

The Dilweg Co. appears to be significantly behind on its mortgage for 101 Marietta St., a 36-story office tower in Downtown Atlanta, with an outstanding balance of $68.5M as of March 2025. The loan, originally $64.5M from Citizens Bank in 2015, matured in August 2025 and has not been satisfied or extended according to public records. The property, which Dilweg had previously marketed for sale, has since been pulled from the market, reflecting the challenging conditions in the Downtown Atlanta office sector.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Downtown Atlanta's office market experienced a vacancy rate exceeding 30% in Q1 2026, with 124K SF of negative absorption. In contrast, Midtown Atlanta's office submarket had a nearly 30% vacancy rate but saw over 300K SF of leasing activity in the same quarter. The $80M loan for 191 Peachtree Tower was placed on a watchlist last month by Morningstar Credit, and Fitch Ratings downgraded a Morgan Stanley CMBS pool partly due to this loan.
