Philippe Harari’s AquaBlue under contract to sell waterfront Venetian Islands homes asking $67M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 27 May 2026 20:35:11 +0000

SUMMARY

AquaBlue Group has secured a buyer for its two adjacent waterfront luxury homes on the Venetian Islands in Miami Beach, with an asking price of $67 million. The properties, totaling 21,000 square feet, are expected to be completed this summer and represent the top luxury home contract signed in Miami-Dade County for the week of May 18-24. The sale highlights continued activity in the high-end residential market, with luxury homes spending an average of 168 days on the market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Address: 340 and 320 West Di Lido Drive, Miami Beach, FL

Size: 21,000 SF

Sale Price: $67.0M

Completion: Summer 2026

Notable Features: Two adjacent waterfront homes with 120 feet of waterfront, each featuring six bedrooms, seven bathrooms, and one half-bathroom.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: AquaBlue Group — Philippe Harari

SELLER BROKER: One Sotheby's International Realty — Mirce Curkoski

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

26 luxury home contracts were signed in Miami-Dade County between May 18 and May 24, with an asking dollar volume of $333.5 million for 17 single-family homes and nine condos. Single-family homes had an average asking price of $15.6 million and spent an average of 168 days on the market. Condos had an average asking price of $7.6 million, spent 105 days on the market, and averaged $2,500 per square foot. In New York, 29 luxury home contracts were signed with a combined asking price of $228 million, spending an average of 601 days on the market.
