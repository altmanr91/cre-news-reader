Lee & Associates closes 26,707-square-foot industrial lease in Fort Worth

LEASE

REJournals — Wed, 27 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates Dallas-Fort Worth has successfully brokered a new lease for a 26,707-square-foot industrial facility in Fort Worth, Texas. The transaction involved Hawk Logistics as the tenant, securing space at Riverbend East Building 1. Brokers from Lee & Associates represented the tenant, while a team from CBRE Inc. and HPI Real Estate Services & Investments represented the landlord.
