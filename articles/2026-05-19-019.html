LISTEN: The exodus of wealthy tenants at a UES mainstay

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 18 May 2026 18:30:19 +0000

SUMMARY

The article discusses the predicament of luxury tenants at 800 Fifth Avenue on the Upper East Side, who are facing an exodus after the building was acquired by Miki Naftali for $800 million with plans to convert it into condominiums. This situation has forced these tenants into a competitive luxury rental market where prices have significantly increased, creating challenges for them and their brokers. The building's leasing office has closed, and tenants are expected to vacate by year-end.
