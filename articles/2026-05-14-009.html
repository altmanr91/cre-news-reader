Colliers Brokers Sale of 69,363 SF Industrial Building in Irving

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:37:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers has brokered the sale of a 69,363-square-foot industrial building in Irving, Texas. The property, Building 1 within TriStar Business Park, was sold by Hopewell Development to GEO Plastics. GEO Plastics plans to occupy the building and create approximately 50 new jobs in the area.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Building 1, TriStar Business Park

Address: 7815 Jetstar Drive, Irving, TX

Size: 69,363 SF

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Hopewell Development

BUYER: GEO Plastics

SELLER BROKER: Colliers — Greg Cannon
