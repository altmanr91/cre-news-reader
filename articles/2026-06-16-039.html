RADCO Obtains $53M Refi on Atlanta Hotel

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 13:26:23 +0000

SUMMARY

The RADCO Companies secured a $53 million refinancing loan for The American Hotel Atlanta Downtown, a 315-key hospitality asset in downtown Atlanta. The financing, arranged by Newmark, was provided by Structured Hotel Capital. The hotel recently underwent an $11.3 million renovation to modernize its facilities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: The American Hotel Atlanta Downtown

Address: 160 Ted Turner Drive NW, Atlanta, GA

Keys: 315

Loan Amount: $53.0M

Loan Per Key: $168,254/key (calculated)

Year Built: 1962

Notable Features: Affiliated with Hilton's Tapestry Collection, features 13,300 square feet of meeting space, an outdoor pool, and fitness center. Located in the Fairlie District, close to major attractions like the Georgia World Congress Center and Mercedes-Benz Stadium.

Project Notes: The hotel completed an $11.3 million renovation prior to this refinancing.
