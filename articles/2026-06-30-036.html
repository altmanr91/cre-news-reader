Trammell Crow Center gets boost from new, extended leases

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 17:46:11 +0000

SUMMARY

Stream Realty Partners has secured over 100,000 square feet in new and extended leases at the Trammell Crow Center in Downtown Dallas, significantly boosting the building's occupancy. The transactions include a full-floor lease by a major financial services institution, Stream Realty's own renewal, and expansions or new leases by several law firms. This leasing activity brings the 1.2 million-square-foot tower to 92% leased, providing a positive counter-narrative to recent concerns about Downtown Dallas's commercial real estate market. The building's prime location in the Arts District and its proximity to the Uptown financial district are cited as key attractions for tenants.
