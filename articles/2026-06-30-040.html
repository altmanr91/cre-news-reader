CREFC’s Lisa Pendergast Sees Industry Moving into “Constructive Period” (VIDEO)

Connect CRE — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 21:23:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Lisa Pendergast, CEO of CREFC, characterized the commercial real estate finance industry as entering a "constructive period" despite the ongoing challenge of high interest rates. She noted that billions of dollars in loans with 4% coupons are maturing, requiring refinancing at current rates of 6.5% to 7%. Pendergast expressed limited optimism for significant near-term rate cuts, but believes that once inflation subsides, refinancing will become more accretive.

DATA POINTS

Maturing loan coupon rates: 4%

Current refinancing rates: 6.5% - 7%
