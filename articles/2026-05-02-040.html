FIRST DRAFT LIVE: Newmark Capital Markets Head Chad Lavender Says CRE Debt Markets 'Almost Insatiable'

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 01 May 2026 15:48:01 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Newmark's President of North American Capital Markets, Chad Lavender, expressed optimism for the commercial real estate industry despite broader economic uncertainties and the Federal Reserve's decision to hold interest rates. He noted that investors are forward-looking and eager to deploy approximately $378 billion in dry powder. Lavender believes the anticipated "maturity wall" will not result in a wave of defaults but rather constructive refinances, sales, and workouts, supported by strong liquidity in debt funds and an "almost insatiable" repo lending market offering attractive rates.
