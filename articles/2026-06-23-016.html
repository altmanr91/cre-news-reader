Witness tampering claim fuels Ostad brothers’ real estate feud

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 20:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A legal dispute between the Ostad brothers, Steven, Michael, and Edward, over control of 10 rental properties and a lending arm called Flatiron Capital has escalated with allegations of witness tampering. Steven's attorney accused Michael and Edward of harassing a key witness, Amram Tehrani, who allegedly has firsthand knowledge of the brothers' scheme to collect cash rents off the books and fabricate rental records. Michael and Edward's counsel denied the allegations, calling them defamatory and unsubstantiated, while Steven's attorney requested a protective order for the witness.
