Naftali Credit Partners Arranges $75M Debt for Flatbush Condo Project

LOAN

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:26:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Naftali Credit Partners has arranged a $75 million financing package for New Empire's nine-story condominium development at 757 Flatbush Avenue in Brooklyn. The financing, which includes a $58 million senior loan from Madison Realty Capital and a $17 million mezzanine loan from Naftali Credit Partners, will refinance existing land debt and cover remaining construction costs. The project is well-aligned with strong demand for high-quality condominium projects in Brooklyn.
