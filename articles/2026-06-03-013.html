HSG Medical, Capital Healthcare Properties close $23.4 million sale of outpatient center in Naperville

SALE

REJournals — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 10:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

HSG Medical and Capital Healthcare Properties have completed the $23.4 million sale of the Advocate Health Care Cardiovascular ASTC & Outpatient Center in Naperville, Illinois. The 42,438-square-foot medical facility was acquired by Advocate Health and Hospitals Corporation, a subsidiary of Advocate Health. The property is a newly redeveloped, state-of-the-art cardiovascular ambulatory surgery treatment center and outpatient facility, purpose-built to extend Advocate's cardiology program.
