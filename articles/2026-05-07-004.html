Brooklyn Heights Walgreens to Become High-Rise After $30M Sale

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:29:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Rogers Equities has acquired a 6,000-square-foot development site at 120 Court Street in Brooklyn Heights, NY, from the Sorkin family for $30 million. The site, currently home to a Walgreens pharmacy, is slated for a 14-story, 75-unit mixed-use residential building with ground-floor retail. The transaction was facilitated by Newmark, representing both the buyer and seller, and marks a significant development in the upscale Brooklyn Heights neighborhood.

Plans include recreational space, 38-bike storage, and amenity spaces on the first and second floors with outdoor recreation on the roof.

Project Notes: The site is currently occupied by a Walgreens pharmacy, which will close in June 2026. The new development will be a high-end rental building under New York State's 485x property tax incentive program. Plans were filed in March 2026.
