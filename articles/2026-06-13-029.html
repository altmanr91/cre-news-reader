Unpaid contractors cloud Obama Center’s finish line

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 21:15:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

The Obama Presidential Center, nearing its opening, is facing allegations of unpaid invoices from several subcontractors, reportedly totaling millions of dollars. The project's construction cost has escalated significantly from an initial $300 million to $850 million, attributed to pandemic-related delays and a prolonged legal battle. While the general contractor, Lakeside Alliance, states that contractual closeout processes are normal for a project of this scale, some subcontractors are pursuing legal action to resolve payment disputes, with some expressing regret over their involvement.
