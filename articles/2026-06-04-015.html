CRESCO Real Estate lands industrial portfolio in Valley View

REJournals — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Cushman & Wakefield | CRESCO Real Estate has been retained to market the PAG Portfolio, an eight-building industrial flex portfolio totaling 400,000 square feet across three campuses in Valley View, Ohio. The properties, located along Rockside Road, represent a significant industrial flex opportunity in a submarket where CRESCO has a strong presence. The brokerage plans to reposition some existing office space within the buildings to better align with current industrial flex tenant demand.
