China Vanke cedes control of Bush Tower to lender

REO

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

China Vanke's American affiliate has transferred ownership of Bush Tower in Midtown Manhattan to its lender, United Overseas Bank, in a deal valued at $58.1 million. This transfer follows a $120 million loan provided by United Overseas Bank in 2018, indicating a significant decrease in the property's value. While Vanke will retain management responsibilities, the bank intends to sell the 29-story office building.

Project Notes: Vanke previously acquired a majority stake in the building for $125 million in 2015. The ground lease was acquired by Tribeca Associates and Meadow Partners in 2013 for $65 million. American Properties owns the land. WeWork previously leased 64,000 square feet but terminated the lease during bankruptcy restructuring. Home care solutions company HHAeXchange also recently vacated the building.
