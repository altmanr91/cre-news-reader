Massachusetts relaunches effort to redevelop Boston Government Center

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

The state of Massachusetts has relaunched its efforts to redevelop the 6.5-acre Boston Government Center complex, comprising the Erich Lindemann Mental Health Center and the Charles F. Hurley Building. The administration of Governor Maura Healey has issued an offering memorandum for the site, which is intended to create new housing and preserve mental health services. This marks a renewed push after a previous $1 billion mixed-use redevelopment plan for the Hurley Building, awarded in 2022, fell through due to a downturn in the life sciences market.
