The most talked about part of Mamdani’s housing plan wasn’t in his housing plan

The Real Deal — New York — Thu, 28 May 2026 16:14:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article clarifies a misunderstanding regarding Mayor Zohran Mamdani's housing plan, specifically addressing a Wall Street Journal report about potential rent freeze exemptions for affordable housing landlords. The administration stated that the practice of restructuring rents on vacant units in affordable housing is not a new policy within the housing plan, but rather a longstanding tool used by the Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) during loan modifications with affordable housing operators. This allows rents to be adjusted up to the level of a newly constructed affordable unit with similar income restrictions, but is used in minimal and rare situations.
