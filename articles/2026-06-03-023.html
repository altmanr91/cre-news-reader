South Florida’s top deals: MSCI chief pays $25M for Palm Springs home

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

This article is a roundup of top residential sales in South Florida. The most significant transaction involved Henry and Alexia Fernandez acquiring a 5,800-square-foot home at 134 Casa Bendita in Palm Beach for $25 million. Other notable sales included a waterfront home on the Venetian Islands for $15.5 million, a penthouse at Una Residences in Miami for $13.2 million, and a condo in Sunny Isles Beach for $10.2 million.
