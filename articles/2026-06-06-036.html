Hudson Pacific lists its emptiest SF offices in ongoing property sell-off

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 18:40:19 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Hudson Pacific Properties has listed its two-building office complex at 875-899 Howard Street in San Francisco's South of Market neighborhood for sale. The 282,828-square-foot property, which the firm acquired in 2007 for $46 million, is being offered for an undisclosed price. The listing is part of Hudson Pacific's strategy to reduce its San Francisco office portfolio amidst internal financial challenges, with the buildings currently representing the emptiest in their local holdings.
