Roller Rabbit skates past Nantucket chain store ban

LEASE

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 15 May 2026 18:45:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Roller Rabbit has signed a two-year lease for a new retail location at 44 Centre Street in Nantucket, MA, circumventing the town's "formula business" bylaw that restricts chain stores. The company rebranded the store as "Roller Rabbit General Store" and committed to selling at least half non-Roller Rabbit branded products to comply with local regulations. This move follows a previous closure of a Roller Rabbit store in Nantucket due to the same bylaw, and its return has sparked debate among local residents and businesses regarding potential loopholes for other chains.
