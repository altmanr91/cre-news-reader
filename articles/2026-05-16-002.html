Canero Flips Condo in Miami’s Coconut Grove to El-Ad for $46M

SALE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 15 May 2026 21:10:51 +0000

SUMMARY

Jose Canero has sold the Chateau Grove Condominium in Miami's Coconut Grove for $45.5 million to El-Ad National Properties. Canero had previously acquired the 25-unit building for $17.3 million and terminated its condo association, a move that grants the buyer full control for redevelopment. El-Ad National Properties intends to redevelop the property, recognizing Coconut Grove as a highly desirable and established neighborhood in Miami.
