Well-Located Bergen County Multifamily Trades for $29M

SALE

Connect CRE — Tue, 26 May 2026 21:59:34 +0000

SUMMARY

A 52-unit multifamily property located at 8 Tice Rd. in Franklin Lakes, NJ, has been sold for $29 million. Cushman & Wakefield represented both the seller, Adoni Property Group, and the buyer, Four Point Holdings, LLC, in the transaction. The property's modern amenities and strategic location in a desirable community, coupled with robust investor interest in well-located multifamily assets, contributed to a competitive sale process.
