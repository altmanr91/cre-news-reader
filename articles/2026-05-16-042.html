CIM Group Launches Platform To Invest In Rural QOZ Power Projects

LOAN

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 15 May 2026 12:48:54 -0400

SUMMARY

CIM Group has launched Permanent Power Co., a national platform focused on investing in rural qualified opportunity zones to develop power projects. This initiative aims to consolidate CIM Group's efforts in solar generation, energy storage, and transmission infrastructure, addressing the increasing power demands from sectors like artificial intelligence and data centers. The new company has secured $400 million in financing from HPS Investment Partners to advance its growth and accelerate the development of its initial solar projects in California's San Joaquin Valley.
