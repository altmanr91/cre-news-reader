Miami Beach advances Robert Rivani’s $50M office rooftop expansion

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Miami Beach officials have advanced Robert Rivani's proposed $50 million rooftop expansion for his mixed-use building, The Rivani, located at 1691 Michigan Avenue. The City Commission unanimously approved on first reading an extension of the ground lease for the city-owned land, pushing its expiration to 2132. In exchange for the extended lease and additional renewal options, Rivani is offering an annual rent increase of at least $500,000 to the city, along with a $1.3 million contribution for public benefits. The project involves adding 36,000 square feet of office space, three padel courts, and a 6,000-square-foot restaurant by redeveloping the building's sixth-level garage.
