Texas Stock Exchange locks in Y’all Street lease

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 13 May 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Texas Stock Exchange has confirmed its lease at the Bank of America Tower at Parkside in Dallas, establishing its new Texas Market Center. The space will include executive offices, a museum, and a broadcast studio, with a live stock ticker expected to be installed by Q1 2027. This move is part of Dallas's broader strategy to attract investment and jobs by offering a business-friendly environment with less regulation and no state income or corporate taxes, positioning the city as a growing financial hub.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Bank of America Tower at Parkside

Address: 1919 Woodall Rodgers Freeway, Dallas, TX

Size: 500,000 SF

Notable Features: 30-story tower located near Klyde Warren Park in Uptown, part of a burgeoning financial district known as Y’all Street.

Project Notes: The Texas Stock Exchange plans to launch trading in July, with its initial public offering set for 2027. Approximately $275 million has been earmarked for the exchange by a global group of investors.
