Bradford Allen celebrates grand opening of 301-unit apartment community in Arlington Heights

DEVELOPMENT

REJournals — Fri, 15 May 2026 16:09:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Bradford Allen has celebrated the grand opening of Arbor House, a new 301-unit luxury apartment community in Arlington Heights, IL. The eight-story development offers studio, one-, two-, and three-bedroom apartments with upscale finishes and extensive indoor and outdoor amenities, including a fitness center, resident lounge with golf simulator, coworking suites, and a private outdoor terrace with a pool. The community also features 26,000 square feet of ground-floor retail and serves as the anchor of Bradford Allen's larger 18-acre mixed-use Arlington Gateway district, which includes a renovated 150,000-square-foot wellness center.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Arbor House

Address: 25 E. Algonquin Road, Arlington Heights, IL

Units: 301

Notable Features: Luxury apartment community with 26,000 square feet of ground-floor retail, extensive amenities including a pool, spa, golf simulator, and coworking suites. Concrete construction provides sound insulation. Located at the southeast gateway to Arlington Heights with views of Busse Woods and the Chicago skyline.

Project Notes: Anchor of Bradford Allen's 18-acre mixed-use Arlington Gateway district, which includes the renovated 150,000-square-foot Arlington Med wellness center. Located near Busse Woods nature preserve, Jane Addams Memorial Tollway, and Arlington Heights Metra station.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Bradford Allen

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: Clark Construction
