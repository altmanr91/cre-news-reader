Tucker, Wingspan cash out with $133M Skokie refi

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 19:02:34 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Tucker Development and Wingspan Development Group has secured a $133 million refinancing for The Henry at Harms Woods, a 294-unit mixed-use apartment complex in Skokie, IL. The new first mortgage replaces the original $100 million construction debt, resulting in a $33 million cash-out for the developers. This refinancing underscores the strong demand for newly delivered multifamily supply in the suburban Chicago market, which has experienced a constrained development pipeline.

Project Notes: The developers previously acquired the 10.5-acre site for $13.3 million in 2023 and demolished a 130,000-square-foot office building to make way for the multifamily project. Original construction financing in October 2023 included a $77.3 million senior mortgage from Kennedy Wilson and mezzanine financing from Nationwide Insurance.
