Shai Wolkowicki hit with more lawsuits

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 28 May 2026 19:45:14 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Chicago real estate investor Shai Wolkowicki is facing two new lawsuits, adding to his ongoing legal battles. One lawsuit, filed by Andrew Oshay, alleges financial mismanagement and diversion of funds intended for a South Side multifamily portfolio near the Obama Presidential Center, leading to property deterioration and default on senior debt. Separately, Old National Bank has initiated a $5.8 million foreclosure lawsuit against a venture involving Wolkowicki and his former partner Mitch Goltz, targeting vacant land parcels in Orland Park and Joliet that were once slated for medical office development. These new legal actions highlight significant financial distress across multiple real estate ventures associated with Wolkowicki.
