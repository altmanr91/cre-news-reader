World’s largest bicycle manufacturer rides into Boulder

LEASE

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 07 May 2026 00:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Giant Group U.S.A., the American subsidiary of the world's largest bicycle manufacturer, has signed a 10-year lease for 44,000 square feet at 3825 Walnut Street in Boulder, CO, establishing its new U.S. headquarters. The move from Southern California to Boulder, a prominent cycling hub, will consolidate operations and includes plans for a nine-month renovation to create retail, showroom, and office space within the largely warehouse building. The relocation is expected to bring approximately 125 jobs to the area.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 3825 Walnut Street, Boulder, CO

Size: 44,000 SF

Notable Features: Building is almost entirely a warehouse with roughly 16,000 square feet of office space; will include retail space, a showroom, and office space.

Project Notes: The building owner previously planned to redevelop the property into a life sciences campus. Giant Group U.S.A. is moving from Newbury Park, CA, and plans to move next year following a nine-month renovation. The relocation deepens Giant's foothold in Boulder after its subsidiary SPIA Cycling acquired Stages Cycling out of bankruptcy in 2024; Stages Cycling is expected to vacate its headquarters at 5335 Sterling Drive and fold operations into Giant's new base.
