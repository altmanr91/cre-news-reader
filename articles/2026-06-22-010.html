KAI Engineering designs sustainable MEP systems for clinic in Fort Worth

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

KAI Engineering designed the sustainable Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, and Fire Protection (MEP/FP) systems for the new 39,000-square-foot JPS Health Network Medical Home Southwest Tarrant clinic in Fort Worth, TX. The clinic, which opened in February 2025, expands access to primary care and behavioral health services in a rapidly growing area, supporting JPS Health Network's strategy to meet increasing demand across Tarrant County. KAI's design focused on efficiency, comfort, and flexibility to support an integrated care model, incorporating distinct occupied and unoccupied operating modes and an economizer mode to maximize energy savings.
