State of California Secures 20K-SF Office at 1180 Avenue of the Americas

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 20 May 2026 21:38:45 +0000

SUMMARY

The State of California has secured a 20,000-square-foot office lease at 1180 Avenue of the Americas in Midtown Manhattan, relocating its Franchise Tax Board's field office from a nearby building. This transaction is part of a recent leasing surge at the Northwood Investors-owned tower, which has seen approximately 60,000 square feet of new leases and expansions since late April. Other recent deals include a law firm's expansion and new leases by a market research company and a staffing agency, underscoring the building's appeal due to its Midtown location and amenities.
