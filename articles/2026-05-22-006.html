JPI Begins Construction on 277-Unit Multifamily Project in The Colony, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 21 May 2026 14:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JPI has commenced construction on Jefferson Grandscape II, a 277-unit multifamily development in The Colony, Texas. The $77.8 million project, located within the larger Grandscape mixed-use development, will feature a five-story building with a diverse unit mix and amenities such as a pool and sky lounge. QuadReal Property Group and Nebraska Furniture Mart are providing construction financing, with initial units anticipated for occupancy in early 2028.
