JLL Closes Industrial Portfolio Sale to Two Separate Buyers

SALE

Connect CRE — Thu, 14 May 2026 22:40:38 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has completed the sale of Project Bronco, a five-asset industrial portfolio totaling 365,774 square feet across Los Angeles' San Gabriel Valley and Orange County submarkets. The portfolio was split between two buyers, with EQT Real Estate and Berkeley Partners each acquiring separate assets through distinct closings. The terms of the sale were not disclosed.
