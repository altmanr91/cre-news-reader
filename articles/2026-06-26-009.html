Cushman & Wakefield Negotiates 172,089 SF Office Lease in Irving

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 15:06:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Cushman & Wakefield has brokered a significant 172,089-square-foot office lease in Irving's Las Colinas district. Religious nonprofit organization Mercury One will fully occupy the building at 6655 N. MacArthur Blvd. Orion Properties, the owner, was represented by Cushman & Wakefield, who also represented the tenant in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 6655 N. MacArthur Blvd., Irving, TX

Size: 172,089 SF

Year Built: 1997

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LANDLORD: Orion Properties

TENANT: Mercury One

LEASING AGENT: Cushman & Wakefield — Matt Schendle; Mary Frances Burnette

Mercury One
