JLL seeks buyer for debt tied to Walnut Creek offices, opening door for possible deed-in-lieu sale

LOAN

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL is marketing for sale a $26.6 million loan secured by the 500 Ygnacio Valley Road office tower in Walnut Creek, CA. The Class A, 105,000-square-foot property, owned by Broadshore Capital Partners, faces a July loan maturity with an outstanding balance of $26.6 million. This offering could lead to a deed-in-lieu of foreclosure, reflecting the ongoing challenges in the East Bay office market where landlords are contending with high vacancy rates and declining property values.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Vacancy across the I-680 corridor reached 22.4% in Q1 2026, with downtown Walnut Creek at 22.6% and Ygnacio Valley at 12.2%. Nearby office properties, such as Ygnacio Center and 3003 Oak Road, have recently traded at significant markdowns, establishing new benchmarks for East Bay office valuations.
