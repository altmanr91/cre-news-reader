Westbridge Realty Files Plans for 99-Unit Residential Building in Washington Heights

Commercial Observer — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:50:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Westbridge Realty Group has filed plans with the New York City Department of Buildings for a 99-unit residential building at 4388 Broadway in Washington Heights, Manhattan. The proposed 16-story, 67,048-square-foot project would include ground-floor retail and 25 parking spaces. The 99-unit count suggests the developer may be aiming to utilize the 485x tax incentive program, which offers tax exemptions for residential projects in New York, while potentially avoiding the higher wage requirements for construction workers associated with projects of 100 units or more under the same legislation.
