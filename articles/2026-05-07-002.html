LGT Capital Partners Takes 42K SF at 1251 Avenue of the Americas

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 06 May 2026 21:26:15 +0000

SUMMARY

LGT Capital Partners, a global alternative investment specialist, has signed a 10-year lease for 41,600 square feet of office space at 1251 Avenue of the Americas in Midtown Manhattan. The firm is relocating its New York City office from 1133 Avenue of the Americas to this 54-story tower owned by Mitsui Fudosan America. This transaction was one of the largest office leases in Midtown during April, reflecting a strong leasing velocity in Manhattan.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Midtown office asking rents averaged $84.77 per square foot in April. Manhattan's office leasing velocity reached 3.61 million square feet in April, exceeding the 10-year monthly average of 2.78 million square feet and surpassing April 2025's activity by 6.9 percent.
