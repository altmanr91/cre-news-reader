Ulta Beauty inks $400M Times Square lease at Jeff Sutton’s 1551 Broadway

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Ulta Beauty has signed a significant 15-year lease for all four floors of the 26,000-square-foot retail building at 1551 Broadway in Times Square, owned by Jeff Sutton's Wharton Properties. The lease, valued at $400 million, also includes the building's prominent 250-foot LED sign. Ulta Beauty plans to open an experiential store at this flagship location, replacing the former tenant.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 1551 Broadway, New York City, NY

Size: 26,000 SF

Year Built: 2009

Notable Features: Includes a 250-foot LED sign; located in Times Square.

Project Notes: Building was developed by Wharton Properties and SL Green in 2009 after demolishing the former Times Square Howard Johnson's hotel.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Times Square's retail recovery is uneven, with leasing activity concentrated in specific categories and apparel tenants largely absent. Experiential concepts are helping to fill space. Foot traffic in Times Square remains strong, with approximately 222,000 pedestrians recorded in November, an 18% increase from two years prior.
