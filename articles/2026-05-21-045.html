Law Firm Jones Jones Relocates within Financial District

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 20 May 2026 20:27:46 +0000

SUMMARY

Law firm Jones Jones LLC has signed a 10-year, nine-month lease for 5,311 square feet at 44 Wall St. in Manhattan's Financial District. The firm is relocating from 5 Hanover Square, which is slated for residential conversion, choosing to remain in Lower Manhattan due to its accessibility, transportation options, and value for office tenants. The move reflects a broader trend in the Financial District where older office properties are being converted to residential use, prompting office tenants to seek quality space that offers both location and efficiency.
