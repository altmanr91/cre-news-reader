Spirit Airlines' South Florida HQ Scheduled For July Auction

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 13:55:21 -0400

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The former South Florida headquarters of Spirit Airlines, a four-building campus known as Spirit Central, is scheduled for auction in July following the airline's bankruptcy filing in late 2024. The 8.5-acre complex in Dania Beach, FL, which cost $275 million to develop and opened in 2024, includes a 180,000 SF office building, an amenity building, a training facility, and housing for visiting workers. Potential buyers can bid on the entire campus or individual portions, with Broward County commissioners reportedly considering a bid for the office building, parking garage, and training facility for up to $100 million to replace their current downtown Fort Lauderdale office.
