Grocery-anchored retail REIT moves to sell majority of properties

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Wheeler Real Estate Investment Trust is marketing 35 of its grocery-anchored retail properties for sale, representing a majority of its 59-asset portfolio. The portfolio is stabilized with a 94% occupancy rate. This move comes as the REIT faces significant stock price declines and recent net losses, despite the strong market demand for grocery-anchored retail assets.
