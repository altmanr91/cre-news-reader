LV Collective, Peninsula Break Ground on 922-Bed Student Housing Project in College Station, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 28 May 2026 14:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between LV Collective and Peninsula Investments has broken ground on Rambler Northgate, a 922-bed student housing project in College Station, TX, near Texas A&M University. The 24-story development will feature 342 units with bed-to-bath parity and a range of amenities including a pool, coworking spaces, and a fitness center. UMB is providing the construction financing, with completion anticipated in 2028.
