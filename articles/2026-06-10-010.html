Cronheim Arranges $33.2M in Financing for Shopping Center in New City, New York

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 14:38:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Cronheim Mortgage has arranged $33.2 million in financing for New City Center, a 128,801-square-foot shopping center in New City, New York. The recently renovated property is anchored by a grocer and features a strong tenant mix. The loan, secured through Voya Investment Management, has a three-year term with two one-year extension options, and was obtained by a joint venture between KABR Group and BTF Capital Fund.
