Zoox parks in Hayward with another 34K sf lease

LEASE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 01 May 2026 22:28:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Zoox, an Amazon-owned autonomous vehicle company, has leased a 34,000-square-foot industrial property in Hayward, CA, to serve as a logistics and staging hub for its robotaxis. This expansion complements its existing manufacturing plant across the street and other facilities in the region, supporting the company's growing production and service area. The landlord, Terreno Realty Corporation, noted the complexity of the lease, which involved a buyout and city coordination.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 25555 Clawiter Road, Hayward, CA

Size: 34,000 SF

Site Area: 10.2 acres

Notable Features: Functions as a logistics and staging hub for finished vehicles, located across the street from Zoox's manufacturing plant.

Project Notes: The deal required a complex lease buyout and coordination with the city.
