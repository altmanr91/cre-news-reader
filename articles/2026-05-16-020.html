Rabsky buys troubled Tribeca development site for $30M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 15 May 2026 20:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The Rabsky Group has acquired a long-troubled development site at 267 Broadway in Tribeca for $30 million, with plans to construct a luxury mixed-use condominium project. The site, which has approved plans for a 45-story condominium and hotel tower, faced financial difficulties when the previous owner defaulted on its mortgage and the lender subsequently collapsed. This acquisition marks another significant Manhattan investment for Rabsky, a firm known for its prolific development activity in Brooklyn.
