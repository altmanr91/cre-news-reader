“Podfather” drops $17M at Extell’s Lincoln Square tower

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 21:29:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Jon Rubinstein, a key engineer behind the original iPod, has acquired a luxury condominium at Extell Development's 50 West 66th Street in Lincoln Square for $17.1 million. The unit, spanning approximately 2,800 square feet, features three bedrooms, three bathrooms, and a private loggia with views of the Hudson River and Central Park. This sale is a positive indicator for the luxury market amidst concerns about a potential pied-à-terre tax, as the building is now over 80 percent sold.
