'We're In A Different Cycle': Fund Managers Try To Convince Investors OZ 2.0 Will Be Better

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 16:52:35 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Opportunity Zone fund managers are actively working to reassure investors that the upcoming OZ 2.0 program, with its revised structure and more favorable market timing, will yield stronger returns compared to the initial iteration. The first version of the program faced significant headwinds from the pandemic and rising interest rates, which impacted investor profitability. Key changes in OZ 2.0 include its permanence in the tax code, a new map of qualified census tracts, and enhanced tax incentives for investments in rural areas. Fund managers like GTIS Partners are emphasizing that the underperformance of some early OZ funds was primarily due to market cycle timing rather than inherent flaws in the program itself, and they are educating investors on the new rules and the current real estate cycle.
