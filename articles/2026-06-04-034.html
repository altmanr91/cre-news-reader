Meet the Becks, the owner of the Dallas Mavericks arena site at Valley View

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article profiles Beck Ventures, the long-time owner and developer of the Valley View Mall site in North Dallas, which is now the proposed location for the Dallas Mavericks' new arena. The firm, led by Jeff Beck and his sons Scott and Jarrod, acquired the 40-year-old mall in 2012 with ambitious plans for a $2 billion mixed-use redevelopment called Dallas Midtown. Despite securing $36 million in public tax incentives and holding a groundbreaking in 2016, the project has been plagued by delays, lawsuits, code violations, and a mysterious fire, with demolition of the old mall only recently completed. The Mavericks' plan to purchase 104 acres from Seritage Growth Properties for their arena and entertainment district will require a separate deal with Beck Ventures, which remains the largest landlord at the 110-acre site.
