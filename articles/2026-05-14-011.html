CBRE closes sale of 120-unit multifamily property in West Bend

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 14 May 2026 10:02:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CBRE has facilitated the sale of Trail's Edge Apartments, a 120-unit multifamily property in West Bend, Wisconsin. Wisconsin Lakefront Property Management LLC acquired the asset from an ownership group that included its original developer, American Construction Services. The property, built in 2022, offers a strong amenity package and convenient access to local attractions.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Trail's Edge Apartments

Address: 250 S. Forest Ave., West Bend, WI

Units: 120

Year Built: 2022

Notable Features: Amenities include a fitness center, community room, pet washing station, interior car wash bay, and keyless building access. Units feature quartz countertops. The property is located near restaurants, dog parks, and walking trails along the Milwaukee River.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Wisconsin Lakefront Property Management LLC

SELLER: American Construction Services

SELLER BROKER: CBRE — Sean Beuche; Matson Holbrook; Gretchen Richards
