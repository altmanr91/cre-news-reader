NYC’s top deals: Brooklyn Heights townhouse sells for $13M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Sat, 16 May 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Extell Development Company has acquired a significant development site at 405-407 Park Avenue in Midtown for approximately $451 million. The transaction involved multiple sellers, including Corem Property Group AB and a joint venture between Deutsche Bank's asset management arm and MRP Realty. This acquisition also includes air rights from the nearby Central Synagogue, indicating a strategic move for future development in a prime Manhattan location.
