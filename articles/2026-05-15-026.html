Rishi Kapoor to plead guilty in $85M federal fraud case

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 14 May 2026 21:53:54 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Rishi Kapoor, the former CEO of Location Ventures, is set to plead guilty to federal charges of money laundering and payroll tax conspiracy, stemming from an alleged $85 million fraud scheme. Prosecutors contend that Kapoor defrauded investors, misled banks, and evaded taxes, using misappropriated funds for personal luxury expenses. This plea follows a multi-year investigation and an indictment on 37 criminal counts, with Kapoor facing a minimum of 10 years in prison.
