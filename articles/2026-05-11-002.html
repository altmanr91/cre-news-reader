Commercial Observer — Sun, 10 May 2026 13:00:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Extell Development has acquired a significant development site at 405-417 Park Avenue in New York City for $500 million, with plans to potentially expand by acquiring two adjacent buildings. The developer is considering a project that could yield up to 700,000 square feet of rentable office space with air rights, or 527,000 square feet of as-of-right development. This acquisition underscores continued confidence in prime Manhattan development opportunities.
