Kairos tests SF’s recovering office market with 193K sf FiDi listing

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 11 May 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Kairos Investment Management has listed the 10-story office building at 550 Kearny Street in San Francisco's Financial District for sale, aiming for a price in the low- to mid-$300-per-square-foot range. This potential sale would value the 193,000-square-foot property in the high-$50 million to high-$60 million range, significantly higher than the firm's acquisition value two years prior. Kairos had acquired the distressed loan for $35.4 million in May 2024 before taking control of the asset, which is now being marketed as a value-add opportunity with a current occupancy of 28.6 percent.
