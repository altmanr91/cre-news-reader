PCCP, The Dinerstein Companies acquire student housing communities in College Station

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 10:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between PCCP and The Dinerstein Companies has acquired two adjacent student housing communities in College Station, TX, totaling 1,128 beds. The properties, formerly known as 2818 Apartments and Parkway Place, have been rebranded as Sterling College Station. The new owners plan a comprehensive renovation and operational improvement program, including upgraded unit interiors, fitness center improvements, and new pickleball courts, to enhance the community's appeal to Texas A&M University students.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Property Name: Sterling College Station

Address: 1300 Harvey Mitchell Parkway South, College Station, TX

Units: 350

Beds: 1,128

Site Area: 44 acres

Notable Features: Low-density cottage- and garden-style communities offering larger layouts and expansive indoor and outdoor amenity spaces, served by the AggieSpirit bus system.

Project Notes: Properties delivered in 2008 and 2009. The joint venture plans to combine operations into a single community and implement in-house property management.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: PCCP

BUYER: The Dinerstein Companies
