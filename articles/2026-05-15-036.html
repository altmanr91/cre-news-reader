Houston mansion asking $22M is city’s priciest sale in over a year

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

An energy executive sold a 15,800-square-foot mansion in Houston's River Oaks neighborhood to a private couple. While the exact sale price was undisclosed, the property was listed for $21.5 million, making it the city's most expensive residential sale in over a year. The home, situated on a 1.2-acre lot, had seen several price reductions from its initial asking price of $27.5 million in January 2024.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Address: 3 Briarwood Court, Houston, TX

Size: 15,800 SF

Site Area: 1.2 acres

Notable Features: Features include an elevator, an outdoor putting green, an indoor golf room, and a four-car garage.

Project Notes: The home was listed for $27.5 million in January 2024, reduced to $23.5 million in April 2025, and then to $21.5 million in January 2026.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Individual — Neil Kelley, Energy executive

SELLER BROKER: Compass — Robert Bland
