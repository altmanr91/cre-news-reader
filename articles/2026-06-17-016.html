Cress Capital offloads Denver-area office building at 32% discount

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 15:32:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Dunton Commercial and Laminar Real Estate have acquired Union Tower, a 205,800-square-foot office building in Lakewood, CO, for nearly $17.3 million. The seller, Cress Capital, offloaded the property at a 32% discount from its 2017 sale price. The buyers plan to renovate the 73% occupied, 10-story building with new amenities and spec suites, aiming to capitalize on the market's flight-to-quality trend.
