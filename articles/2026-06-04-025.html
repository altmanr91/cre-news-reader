Flow affiliate sells unfinished Miami Worldcenter office, retail building for $27M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 21:14:49 +0000

SUMMARY

An affiliate of Flow, led by Adam Neumann, sold an unfinished three-story retail and office building at Miami Worldcenter for $26.6 million in an all-cash transaction. The 60,000-square-foot property, which is 80 percent complete, was acquired by Daniel Cardenas and Michael Sullivan of Vertical Real Estate, along with Brazilian investor Gustavo Agostini. Merrimac Ventures will continue as a development partner on the project, which is expected to be completed this year, with the buyers actively seeking financing for the remaining construction and stabilization.
