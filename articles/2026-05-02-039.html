Blackstone's QTS Files Appeal To Save Massive Virginia Data Center Campus

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 01 May 2026 12:52:24 -0400

SUMMARY

Development

Blackstone's data center arm, QTS, has filed a last-minute appeal to challenge a Virginia court decision that blocked the rezoning of the 2,100-acre PW Digital Gateway in Prince William County. The company plans to develop 7.9M SF of data centers on an 812-acre portion of the site, a project that would bring significant investment and tax revenue to the county. This appeal to the Virginia Supreme Court is the final legal avenue for QTS after lower courts sided with groups seeking to prevent data center construction near the Manassas National Battlefield Park.
