Newmark closes 72,745-square-foot industrial lease in ALgonquin

LEASE

REJournals — Tue, 26 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Fromm International, a manufacturer of salon tools, has leased 72,745 square feet of industrial space at Algonquin Corporate Center in Algonquin, IL, relocating its national distribution operations from the O'Hare submarket. The Class A facility, developed by NorthPoint Development, offers modern industrial amenities and strategic access to I-90 and a skilled labor pool. Newmark represented the tenant, while CBRE and Stream Realty represented the landlord.
