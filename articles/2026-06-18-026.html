South Florida’s top deals: Edgewater development site trades for $43M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Cain and Kushner acquired a 1.5-acre apartment development site in Miami's Edgewater neighborhood for $43.1 million from a joint venture between OKO Group and Cain. The buyers plan to develop a 40-story, 364-unit luxury residential project on the site. This transaction represents a significant investment in the area's growing multifamily market.
