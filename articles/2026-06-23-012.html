NY Dirt: Real estate gives big in key Albany legislative races

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article details the significant financial contributions from prominent real estate figures and firms to key legislative races in New York's primary elections. The industry is actively supporting incumbent candidates against progressive challengers endorsed by groups like the Working Families Party and Democratic Socialists of America, aiming to influence the general election ballot. Major developers and real estate companies are channeling funds into various campaigns and independent expenditure committees to back candidates who align with their interests, particularly concerning housing policy and development across New York City neighborhoods.
