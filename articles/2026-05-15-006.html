Hillwood Underway on Two Spec Industrial Buildings Totaling 769,858 SF in North Fort Worth

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 14 May 2026 13:56:22 +0000

SUMMARY

Hillwood is developing two speculative industrial buildings, Alliance Gateway 70 and Alliance Gateway 71, totaling 769,858 square feet within its AllianceTexas master-planned community in North Fort Worth. The projects are being financed by Enterprise Bank and Comerica Bank, with completion anticipated by the end of the year. These developments aim to meet the strong demand for industrial space in the region.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Alliance Gateway 70 and Alliance Gateway 71

Size: 769,858 SF

Completion: end of 2026

Notable Features: Alliance Gateway 70 will feature 36-foot clear heights, 47 dock doors, and 270 car parking spaces. Alliance Gateway 71 will feature 40-foot clear heights, 105 dock doors, and 294 car parking spaces. Both buildings will include speculative tenant improvements such as office space and LED warehouse lighting.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Hillwood

LENDER: Enterprise Bank

LENDER: Comerica Bank

ARCHITECT: RGA Architects

ENGINEER: Westwood
