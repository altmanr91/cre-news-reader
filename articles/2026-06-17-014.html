Apollo lending REIT to dissolve after $9B offload

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 17:31:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance, a lending REIT and subsidiary of Apollo Global Management, is dissolving and liquidating its holdings after offloading nearly all of its loan commitments. The move follows the sale of a $9 billion loan portfolio to Athene Holding, another Apollo subsidiary, earlier this year. The sale, which closed in April, left the REIT with approximately $2.2 billion in assets, primarily cash, and only two remaining loans totaling $146 million. The company's CEO stated that the dissolution is in the best interest of the company, highlighting a trend where insurance companies are becoming significant capital providers in the real estate industry.
