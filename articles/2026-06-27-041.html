Bronx Bank’s Flagship Branch Goes for $16M in 1031 Exchange

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 20:00:59 +0000

SUMMARY

The flagship branch and headquarters of Ponce Bank in the Bronx has been sold for $16.25 million in an all-cash 1031 exchange. The net-leased bank property, which Ponce Bank has occupied for 25 years, attracted significant buyer interest due to its management-free triple-net lease, annual rent increases, and future development potential given its large parcel size and proximity to the subway.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 2244 Westchester Ave, Bronx, NY

Sale Price: $16.2M

Notable Features: Net-leased bank property with annual rent increases and significant future development potential, located near a subway station.

Project Notes: Ponce Bank has occupied the property for 25 years. The buyer completed a 1031 exchange.
