Habitat, M/I Homes of Chicago to develop 227-residence community on former Solo Cup site in Highland Park

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Wed, 20 May 2026 09:57:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Habitat and M/I Homes of Chicago has acquired the former Solo Cup Company site in Highland Park, Illinois, with plans to develop The Bowery of Highland Park, a 227-residence for-sale townhome community. The 28-acre vacant site will be transformed into a residential neighborhood featuring townhomes, preserved open space, landscaped parkways, and community amenities. The developers are moving forward with permitting and pre-construction activities, with groundbreaking anticipated for summer 2026, addressing the high demand for new-construction housing in established suburban locations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: townhome

Property Name: The Bowery of Highland Park

Address: 1700 Old Deerfield Road, Highland Park, IL

Units: 227

Site Area: 28 acres

Notable Features: Community amenities include a resident clubhouse with an outdoor pool, a dog park, a publicly accessible tot lot, and pedestrian paths. The site is bordered by preserved wetlands and mature trees.

Project Notes: 34 homes will be offered to qualifying individuals and families in accordance with Highland Park’s Inclusionary Housing Plan. The site was previously the Solo Cup Company site.
