Real Capital Solutions Acquires 101 Marietta Office Tower in Downtown Atlanta for $49.5M

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 12:24:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Real Capital Solutions has acquired the 36-story 101 Marietta office tower in downtown Atlanta for $49.5 million. The 673,819-square-foot building was 54 percent leased at the time of sale and is situated adjacent to the Centennial Yards development. The new owner plans to continue investing in capital improvements to enhance leasing and the tenant experience.
