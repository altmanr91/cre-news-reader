Holt Lunsford Negotiates 39,860 SF Industrial Lease in Carrollton, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 14:54:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Holt Lunsford Commercial has brokered a 39,860-square-foot industrial lease in Carrollton, Texas. The firm represented the landlord, Taurus Investment Holdings, while Rich Young Co. represented the tenant, Turnkey Overstock Inc., in the transaction.

Property Type: industrial

Address: 2425 Camp Ave, Carrollton, TX

Size: 39,860 SF
