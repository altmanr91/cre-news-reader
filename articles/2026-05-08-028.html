Chicago’s medical office market continues hot steak with $18M Hinsdale deal

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 07 May 2026 23:57:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Meadows & Ohy, an Atlanta-based firm, has acquired a medical office property in Hinsdale, Illinois, for $18 million. The University of Chicago primary care office building was purchased from Chicago-based MedProperties Group, which had owned the asset since 2013. The acquisition was financed with a $12 million mortgage from Renasant Bank. This transaction continues a strong trend of medical office deals in the Chicago area, as investors increasingly favor healthcare-related properties amidst a challenging market for traditional office assets.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: medical office

Address: 11 Salt Creek Drive, Hinsdale, IL

Sale Price: $18.0M

Loan Amount: $12.0M

Project Notes: Seller MedProperties Group previously acquired the building for $15 million in 2013.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Meadows & Ohy

SELLER: MedProperties Group

LENDER: Renasant Bank

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Chicago area medical office market has seen over $600 million in transactions since the beginning of the year, reflecting investor preference for stable assets amid a depressed traditional office market and competitive multifamily sector.
