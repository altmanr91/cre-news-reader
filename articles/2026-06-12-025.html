South Florida’s top deals: Rubells sell more property to Neology for $10M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Neology acquired an industrial property in Miami for $10.1 million from an LLC tied to the Rubell family. The 47,000-square-foot building traded at approximately $215 per square foot. This transaction follows a recent $15 million sale of a vacant development site by the Rubells to Neology, indicating a continued strategic acquisition by the buyer in the area.
