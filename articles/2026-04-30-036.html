Mill Creek Residential Breaks Ground on Apartments at Westfield Garden State Plaza

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 20:39:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Mill Creek Residential has broken ground on Modera Garden State Plaza, a significant mixed-use apartment community that will feature 575 units across two buildings. This development marks the initial phase of a broader redevelopment effort to transform the Westfield Garden State Plaza into a vibrant town center. The project will offer residents direct access to diverse retail and dining options within the larger mixed-use environment, with first move-ins anticipated in mid-2028.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Modera Garden State Plaza

Size: 48,000 SF

Size: 575 units

Completion: mid-2028

Notable Features: Part of a larger mixed-use redevelopment of Westfield Garden State Plaza, converting the mall into a town center.
