Rivani’s $50M South Beach office building expansion inches closer to referendum

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 07 May 2026 14:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Robert Rivani's firm is seeking to expand its South Beach office building, The Rivani, by adding 36,000 square feet of office space, three rooftop padel courts, and a 6,000-square-foot restaurant. The $50 million project also involves reducing garage parking by 250 spaces and extending the ground lease at 1691 Michigan Avenue by 40 years to 2132. The Miami Beach Planning Board has recommended approval, moving the proposal to city commissioners and potentially a November referendum for voter approval.
