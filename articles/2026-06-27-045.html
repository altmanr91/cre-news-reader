Hillwood Urban Considering Sale Or Refi Of Trophy Office Building On Y'all Street

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 13:30:49 -0400

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Hillwood Urban is exploring monetization options for its 364,000-square-foot Victory Commons One office tower in Dallas, including a potential sale, refinancing, or bringing on a new partner. The four-year-old trophy asset, developed in partnership with Affinius Capital, is 93% leased with a weighted average lease term of 12.2 years. The building is strategically located in the Victory Park neighborhood, adjacent to the American Airlines Center and within the burgeoning "Y'all Street" financial district, which has attracted significant investment from major financial institutions.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Dallas financial district, dubbed "Y'all Street," is attracting major financial players, including the planned Texas Stock Exchange. NYSE reincorporated its Chicago branch in Dallas, and Nasdaq plans a regional headquarters in the metro area. Goldman Sachs is building an 800,000 SF, $500 million office campus at 2323 N. Field St., expected to open in 2028 and house over 5,000 employees. Morgan Stanley is considering a $684 million investment for a new high-rise at 2401 McKinney Ave. Stonelake Capital Partners plans a 17-story, 174,000 SF speculative office tower at 2626 McKinney Ave.
