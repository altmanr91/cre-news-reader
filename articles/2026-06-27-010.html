Berkadia Arranges $100.4M Refinancing for Office Campus in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 14:19:51 +0000

SUMMARY

Berkadia has secured a $100.4 million floating-rate loan for the refinancing of DiVosta Towers, a two-building Class A office campus in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. The three-year loan was provided by Cirrus Real Estate to the borrower, Gatsby Florida, for the property which was fully delivered in 2020 and features a strong tenant mix.
