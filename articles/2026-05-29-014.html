Marcus & Millichap negotiates sale of Comfort Suites property in Johnson Creek

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 28 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of the 100-room Comfort Suites Johnson Creek Conference Center in Johnson Creek, Wisconsin. The property, built in 2005, is strategically located off Interstate 94, benefiting from its proximity to local attractions and a strong conference facility.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: Comfort Suites Johnson Creek Conference Center

Address: 725 Paradise Lane, Johnson Creek, WI

Keys: 100

Year Built: 2005

Site Area: 4.92 acres

Notable Features: Features an indoor pool, fitness and business centers, and a conference facility with meeting spaces accommodating over 500 guests. Located near Johnson Creek Premium Outlets and Milford Hills Hunt Club.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Ebrahim Valliani, Investment Specialist; Kristen Blackshaw, Investment Specialist; Todd Lindblom, Wisconsin Broker of Record; Allan Miller; Chris Gomes
