Mandrake Inks Refi on Savannah-Area BTR Community

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:51:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Mandrake Capital Partners has secured a $34.5 million refinancing loan for Easthaven, a 143-unit build-to-rent townhome community in Poller, GA. Bridge Investment Group provided the financing, which was arranged by JLL Capital Markets. The community, completed in March 2025, offers three-bedroom townhomes and is strategically located near major employment hubs and the Port of Savannah.

DATA POINTS

Property Name: Easthaven

Address: 101 Petworth Pl., Poller, GA

Units: 143

Loan Amount: $34.5M

Notable Features: Resort-style amenities include a pool and dog park, with easy access to golf courses and parks. Located near Hyundai's EV plant, Gulfstream's headquarters, and the Port of Savannah.
