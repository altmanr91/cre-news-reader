Three Tenants Sign Office Leases Totaling 36,548 SF at Frisco Station

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 22 May 2026 15:36:08 +0000

SUMMARY

Three new tenants have signed office leases totaling 36,548 square feet at Offices Three within Frisco Station, a 242-acre mixed-use development north of Dallas. The new leases include an architecture and engineering firm, a cement manufacturer, and a financial advisory group, adding to the diverse tenant mix at the property.
