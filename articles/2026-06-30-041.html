Private Equity Firm Signs Full-Floor Lease at Marx Realty’s 545 Madison

LEASE

Connect CRE — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 21:05:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Niobrara Capital, a private equity firm, has signed an 11,000-square-foot full-floor lease at Marx Realty's 545 Madison in Midtown Manhattan. The building, also known as the Baccarat Building due to a co-branding partnership, continues to attract premier firms seeking a distinctive, hospitality-infused workplace experience in the Plaza District.
