Alston & Bird Relocates NYC Offices to 51W52 in 170K-SF Lease

LEASE

Connect CRE — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 20:30:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Alston & Bird has signed a significant lease for 170,000 square feet at 51W52 in New York City, relocating its offices to the trophy property. This transaction brings the building to full occupancy, underscoring the continued strength of the New York office market for premier assets in prime locations.
