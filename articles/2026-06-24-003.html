TA Realty Buys Two Staten Island Retail Buildings for $79M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 20:05:26 +0000

SUMMARY

TA Realty has acquired two retail buildings totaling 184,204 square feet within the Tysens Park Shopping Center in Staten Island, New York, for $79.2 million from the Bregman Organization. The transaction includes significant air rights, providing the new owner with substantial redevelopment potential. This acquisition represents a notable expansion into the retail sector for TA Realty, which is primarily known for its industrial portfolio.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Tysens Park Shopping Center

Address: 2712 Hylan Boulevard and 2754 Hylan Boulevard, Staten Island, NY

Size: 184,204 SF

Sale Price: $79.2M

Notable Features: Includes 632,284 square feet of air rights, offering 818,733 square feet of total buildable space.
