Cousins Properties plans to dump aging office in Downtown Austin

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 01 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Cousins Properties is planning to sell One Eleven Congress, a 30-story office tower in Downtown Austin, with the sale expected to close in the third quarter. The 500,000-square-foot building, constructed in 1987, was most recently appraised at $192 million and was 84 percent occupied as of June. This move follows Cousins' acquisition of the newly-built Sail Tower downtown last year for $522 million.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Sail Tower: Acquired for $522 million, or almost $650 per square foot. The 35-story, 804,000-square-foot office tower was delivered in 2022.

Frost Bank Tower: Office property in Downtown Austin.

The Domain: Office property in Austin.

The Link at Uptown: Acquired for $218 million, or $747 per square foot. The 25-story, 292,000-square-foot office building at 2601 Olive Street in Dallas.
