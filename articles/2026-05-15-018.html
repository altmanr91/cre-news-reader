Quarterra seeks to sell 3,700-unit national portfolio

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 14 May 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Quarterra, the multifamily arm of Lennar, is marketing a 10-property, 3,746-unit national portfolio for sale, either as a whole or individually. The properties, averaging four years old, are expected to sell below replacement cost. This offering follows Quarterra's recent divestment activities, including the sale of a 326-unit complex in Katy, TX, and a 212-unit property in Elmhurst, IL, as the company navigates market conditions marked by rising construction costs and oversupply.
