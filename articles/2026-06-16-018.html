NYC’s top deals: Stalled Brookland Capital site trades for $8M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

KG Atlantic Realty Holding Inc. has acquired a stalled residential development site at 929 Atlantic Avenue in Clinton Hill for $7.7 million. The property, which was previously owned by Brookland Capital and later taken over by Amos Financial through foreclosure, is now under the ownership of KG Atlantic Realty Holding Inc., led by president Fei Guo.
