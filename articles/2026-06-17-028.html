Related nears marquee tenant for office skyscraper at Fulton Market’s gateway

LEASE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 17:50:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Law firm Sidley Austin is reportedly nearing a deal to lease over half of a planned 45-story office skyscraper at 725 West Randolph Street in Chicago's Fulton Market area. The building, developed by Related Midwest, is slated for completion in 2030 and will span 968,000 square feet. This potential lease would make the law firm the anchor tenant, signaling confidence in new, high-quality office space despite broader market trends.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 725 West Randolph Street, Chicago, IL

Size: 968,000 SF

Completion: 2030

Notable Features: 45-story office skyscraper located near Fulton Market.

Project Notes: Developer pivoted from original mixed-use apartment and hotel plans in 2023 to an office tower. The updated proposal for the office tower has not yet been approved by the Chicago City Council.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LANDLORD: Related Midwest

TENANT: Sidley Austin — Brian Fahrney, executive committee chair

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Office space availability in Chicago was at an all-time high in 2023, though the Fulton Market area continued to attract office and retail tenants.
