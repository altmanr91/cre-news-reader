SUMMARY

Arthaus Partners, an Oakland-based investor, has acquired the 12-story mixed-use property at 1330 Bush Street in San Francisco's Nob Hill neighborhood for $25 million. The property, which includes 117 residential units and over 16,000 square feet of commercial space, was sold by Equity Residential, which incurred a loss on the sale compared to its 2011 acquisition price. BrightSpire Capital provided a $24 million loan for the acquisition, marking a significant transaction in a surging San Francisco market driven by record rents and strong leasing activity.
