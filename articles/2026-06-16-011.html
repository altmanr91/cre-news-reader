Sullivan Development to develop 200-unit affordable housing development in Indianapolis

DEVELOPMENT

REJournals — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Sullivan Development has secured financing for Junction at Fall Creek, a 200-unit affordable housing development in Indianapolis. The project, with an estimated total development cost of $50 million, will transform a long-vacant 6-acre former rail yard site into high-quality apartment homes for residents earning 60% or less of the Area Median Income. Construction commenced in May 2026, with completion expected in late 2027, aiming to address the critical need for workforce housing and contribute to neighborhood revitalization.

Project Notes: This project is the first phase of the broader Monon 35 master-planned development. It will provide financial literacy, homeownership planning, workforce development, and food access programming for residents.
