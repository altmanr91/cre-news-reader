Newcor Completes 617-Unit Self-Storage Facility in Tomball, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 14:49:29 +0000

SUMMARY

Newcor Commercial Real Estate has completed a 617-unit self-storage facility in Tomball, Texas, northeast of Houston. The facility, spanning 87,970 net rentable square feet, includes climate-controlled, non-climate-controlled, and dedicated boat and RV storage spaces. RightMove Storage will operate the newly completed facility.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: self-storage

Address: 11110 Hufsmith Road, Tomball, TX

Size: 87,970 SF

Units: 617

Site Area: 5.1 acres

Notable Features: Includes 510 climate-controlled units, 93 non-climate-controlled units, and 14 boat/RV storage spaces.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Newcor Commercial Real Estate

OWNER: RightMove Storage
