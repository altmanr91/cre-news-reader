Equity Residential, AvalonBay to Merge for $52B Apartment REIT

Connect CRE — Thu, 21 May 2026 10:50:15 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Apartment REITs Equity Residential and AvalonBay Communities, Inc. have announced an all-stock merger of equals, creating a combined company with a pro forma equity market capitalization of approximately $52 billion and a total enterprise value of approximately $69 billion. The new entity will manage over 180,000 rental apartments and have nearly 11,000 units in its construction pipeline. Benjamin Schall, current president and CEO of AvalonBay, will lead the combined company, which will maintain dual headquarters in Chicago and Arlington, VA. The merger is anticipated to close in the second half of 2026, subject to shareholder approvals.
