CIM Group shops SoMa office buildings home to iHeartMedia

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

CIM Group has listed a two-building office complex totaling 179,000 square feet in San Francisco's South of Market (SoMa) neighborhood. The property, located at 330 and 340 Townsend Street, is 95 percent occupied, with one building fully leased to a subsidiary of iHeartMedia until late 2028. CIM Group acquired the buildings in 2013 for $30 million and had previously explored redeveloping the site into either a residential or office tower, leveraging the area's 2016 rezoning for high-rise development.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Central SoMa Plan area was rezoned in 2016 from industrial to accommodate high-rise office, residential, and commercial buildings. Developers like Kilroy Realty and Alexandria Real Estate Equities have been active in the neighborhood, though development has slowed due to elevated construction costs and high office vacancy rates. Legislation passed last year allows megaproject developers to incorporate more housing and removes office space requirements.
