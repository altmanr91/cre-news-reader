CBRE closes sale of 142,400-square-foot industrial portfolio in Beavercreek

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 14:05:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CBRE has facilitated the sale of a two-building industrial and research and development portfolio in Beavercreek, Ohio, for $12.5 million. The fully leased portfolio, totaling 142,400 square feet, was acquired from 2060 Heller LLC and 2070 Heller LLC. The properties are strategically located within the Cincinnati–Dayton Interstate 75 corridor, offering a strong position for industrial operations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 2060 and 2070 Heller Drive, Beavercreek, OH

Size: 142,400 SF

Sale Price: $12.5M

Notable Features: Each building includes a mix of office and production space designed to support specialized aerospace manufacturing and research functions, located within the Cincinnati–Dayton Interstate 75 corridor.
