Tishman Speyer Holds First Close on Fund Targeting Multifamily in Seoul

Connect CRE — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 11:23:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Fund Close

Tishman Speyer has achieved the first close for its Korea Living Venture (KLV) fund, securing $300 million in third-party equity commitments from two European pension funds. The fund is dedicated to acquiring, repositioning, and developing multifamily and accommodation properties across Seoul and the broader capital region. This initiative aims to capitalize on the under-institutionalized living sector in Korea, driven by increasing demand and limited supply, while expanding Tishman Speyer's global portfolio.
