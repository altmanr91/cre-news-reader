Pac Heights mansion sells for $28M as spring market heats up

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 04 May 2026 22:45:39 +0000

SUMMARY

A Georgian Colonial mansion in San Francisco's Pacific Heights, originally listed for $35 million in 2023, has sold for $27.5 million. The seller, an entity tied to Hennessey Capital President Rajiv Ghatalia, had purchased the property in 2010 for $8.4 million. The buyer is an LLC linked to a San Francisco financial services firm, though the specific identity remains undisclosed. This transaction reflects a heating luxury market in the city, driven by tech wealth and limited inventory.
