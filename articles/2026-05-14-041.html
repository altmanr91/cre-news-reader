W Magazine Signs Direct Lease at 1 World Trade Center

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 13 May 2026 22:39:30 +0000

SUMMARY

W Magazine has signed a direct, long-term lease for its new headquarters at 1 World Trade Center in Lower Manhattan. The publication will occupy 12,456 square feet on the 46th floor, establishing a permanent, high-quality office environment to reflect its brand and host clients. This move signifies a broader trend of companies recommitting to long-term office spaces in New York City.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 1 World Trade Center

Address: 285 Fulton St, New York City, NY

Size: 12,456 SF

Project Notes: W Magazine previously operated out of a sublease.
