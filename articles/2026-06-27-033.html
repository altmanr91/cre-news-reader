Cedar Park greenlights Texas Materials HQ move with $14M incentive deal

LEASE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Texas Materials, a construction materials producer and supplier, is relocating its headquarters to the new Cedar Park Town Center in Cedar Park, TX. The move is supported by a $13.8 million incentive package from the Cedar Park City Council, contingent on the company doubling its headquarters employee count to over 169. Texas Materials will occupy 48,000 square feet in the Balcones Real Estate-developed office facility, with the move expected by May 2028.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Cedar Park Town Center

Address: 701 Central Park Drive, Cedar Park, TX

Size: 48,000 SF

Project Notes: The Cedar Park Town Center is currently under construction, with future development phases planned over the next few years. The city expects 300,000 square feet of commercial development in the area by 2032. Texas Materials is eligible for sales tax revenue rebates and a $10,000 incentive (capped at $500,000) for each employee who purchases a home or residential lot in Cedar Park.
