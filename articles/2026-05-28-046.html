Blackstone, Brookfield Are Betting On Custom AI — And Proptech Could Pay The Price

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 27 May 2026 17:11:36 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Wall Street titans Blackstone and Brookfield are investing heavily in custom AI solutions, signaling a shift that could disrupt the proptech sector. These firms are partnering with AI developers like Anthropic and OpenAI to create bespoke enterprise tools for real estate operations, potentially reducing the need for third-party proptech platforms. While the full impact will take time to materialize, the trend suggests a return to customized operational systems, with proptech companies needing to reposition themselves as essential data layers and systems of record to remain relevant.
