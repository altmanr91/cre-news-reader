Seth Hecht Returns to Colliers From JLL as New Senior Vice President

Commercial Observer — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 18:31:29 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Seth Hecht has rejoined Colliers as a Senior Vice President on its office leasing team in New York City, returning after eight years at JLL and three years at Cushman & Wakefield. In his new role, Hecht will provide advisory services to both tenants and landlords for their leasing activities. His return is seen as a strategic move to leverage his extensive experience and industry relationships in an evolving market.
