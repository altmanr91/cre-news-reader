ACORE Capital Provides $110M Loan for Refinancing of Midtown Manhattan Office Building

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 14:50:02 +0000

SUMMARY

ACORE Capital has provided a $110 million loan for the refinancing of 370 Lexington Avenue, a 317,000-square-foot office building in Midtown Manhattan's Grand Central submarket. The loan was part of a larger $138 million recapitalization for the property, which recently underwent significant renovations to its common areas and building systems. The sponsor for this refinancing is a partnership between Broad Street Development and KSR Capital.
