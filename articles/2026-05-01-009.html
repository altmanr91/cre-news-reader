Berkadia Originates Three HUD-Insured Loans for Multifamily Properties in Virginia

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:42:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Berkadia has originated three HUD 223(f) loans totaling $32.4 million for the refinancing of three garden-style apartment communities in Virginia. The loans were secured on behalf of Fralin Cos. for properties located in Hardy, Covington, and Christiansburg, demonstrating continued lender confidence in the multifamily sector.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LENDER: Berkadia — Amy Gay

SPONSOR: Fralin Cos.
