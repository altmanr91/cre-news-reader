Fort Myers portfolio trades for $48M in region’s biggest industrial sale this year

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 19:31:05 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Geis Companies and Westminster Capital sold the newly constructed Meridian Business Campus in Fort Myers, FL, for $48.4 million to Capital Partners. This four-building industrial portfolio, completed in 2023 and spanning 208,400 square feet, represents the largest industrial sale in Southwest Florida this year. The property is 96.5 percent leased to a diverse mix of tenants, reflecting strong demand for modern industrial facilities despite rising regional vacancy rates.

SELLER: Geis Companies

SELLER: Westminster Capital

SELLER BROKER: JLL Capital Markets

LENDER: CBRE Group

FINANCING

$30.5 million 10-year fixed-rate loan arranged by CBRE Group.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Southwest Florida's industrial market is among the fastest growing in the state by inventory, with 2.2 million square feet currently under construction. The regional vacancy rate rose to nearly 10 percent at the end of last year, with older facilities facing longer downtime and concession pressure, while modern buildings are leasing steadily.
