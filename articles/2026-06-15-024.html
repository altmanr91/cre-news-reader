NexPoint gears up for $445M Cityplace Tower overhaul

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

NexPoint is set to commence a significant $445 million redevelopment of the 42-story Cityplace Tower in Dallas, TX, with work on the initial phase, "The Apron," expected to begin before the end of 2026. This ambitious project involves converting 27 office floors into 970 apartment units, with 194 designated as income-restricted, and transforming eight floors into a 221-key hotel. The first phase also includes six new eight-story retail buildings on the surrounding five acres, with the entire redevelopment anticipated to conclude in 2029. The redevelopment was prompted in part by the departure of a major corporate tenant.
