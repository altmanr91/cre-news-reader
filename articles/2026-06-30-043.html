Crown Heights Office/Flex Property Garners Bridge Loan for Refi

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 20:47:43 +0000

SUMMARY

Arrow Real Estate Advisors secured a $22 million bridge loan for the refinance of 1000 Dean St., a creative office and flex property in Brooklyn's Crown Heights/Prospect Heights submarket. The financing, provided by Genesis Credit LLC, addresses the transitional nature of the 129,389-square-foot asset and supports its ongoing lease-up strategy. The broker noted that Genesis Credit LLC quickly recognized the property's strong location, collateral quality, and the sponsor's business plan, providing a flexible solution for stabilization.
