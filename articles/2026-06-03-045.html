Coffee Shops' Desire For Drive-Thrus Is Reaching A Boiling Point

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 16:31:39 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The coffee shop market is experiencing a significant shift, with smaller, drive-thru focused chains rapidly expanding and challenging the dominance of established giants like Starbucks and Dunkin'. These emerging players, including Dutch Bros and 7 Brew, are prioritizing smaller building footprints combined with extensive drive-thru capabilities, requiring larger outdoor spaces and community collaboration. This strategy allows for rapid expansion, particularly in suburban markets, where they are acquiring numerous sites despite individual deals being smaller in scale. The success of these chains is also attributed to their diversified beverage menus, which extend beyond traditional coffee to include customizable cold and energy drinks, appealing to a broader customer base.
