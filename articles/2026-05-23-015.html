Starwood lending arm trying to flip Back Bay office

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 22 May 2026 16:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

LNR Partners, the lending arm of Starwood Property Trust, is marketing the Park Square Building in Boston's Back Bay for sale, just two months after acquiring it at auction for $95 million. The 503,000-square-foot office property is being pitched with significant redevelopment potential, including a possible mixed-use conversion. The building is currently 35.8 percent leased with a weighted average remaining lease term of less than four years, making it an attractive candidate for repositioning given the city's incentives for office-to-residential conversions.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Office-to-residential conversions are a growing trend in the Boston office market, supported by a city program that offers expedited project approvals and up to 75 percent in tax abatements. The Boston Planning & Development Agency recently approved four such conversions.
