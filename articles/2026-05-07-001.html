Insurance Broker Howden Inks 32K-SF Deal at Durst’s 151 West 42nd Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 07 May 2026 11:00:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Global insurance broker Howden has signed a new lease for 31,519 square feet, encompassing the entire 53rd floor, at The Durst Organization's One Five One office tower in Times Square. The building's sustainable design, state-of-the-art amenities, and proximity to Bryant Park were key factors in attracting the tenant, as the firm sought a premium workspace in the heart of New York's financial markets.
