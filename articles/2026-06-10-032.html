Waterfront Lake Geneva estate hits market for $11M as luxury hotspot’s priciest listing

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:18:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A 9.2-acre lakefront estate in Lake Geneva, Wisconsin, has been listed for sale at nearly $11 million, making it the area's most expensive active listing. The property, which includes 160 feet of shoreline on Geneva Lake and is divided into five parcels, features a main home with five bedrooms. The listing agent believes the luxury market remains strong despite recent price adjustments on other high-end properties, attributing perceived sluggishness to a lack of inventory rather than diminished demand.
