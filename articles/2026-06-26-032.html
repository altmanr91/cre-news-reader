Ryan Seacrest sells Wine Country estate for under $19M after price cuts

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 23:07:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

TV host Ryan Seacrest has sold his 40-acre estate in Napa Valley for $18.5 million, following several price reductions from its initial listing of $22 million two years prior. The sprawling property, which includes a 10,750-square-foot main house and guest cottage, is surrounded by vineyards and mountains in St. Helena. Seacrest had acquired the estate in September 2020 for $14 million and undertook renovations in 2024.
