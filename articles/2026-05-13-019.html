NYC’s top construction permits: Week ending May 8, 2026

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 12 May 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Permits

Master GC Management Corp. filed plans for the largest new building application in Astoria, proposing a 60,473-square-foot, eight-story residential building with 62 units at 30-75 21st Street. Other significant new building permits include a 10-story, 52,300-square-foot residential building with 55 units in East Williamsburg by Mendy Schwimmer, and a six-story, 44,475-square-foot residential complex with 60 units in Bushwick for owner Jerome Zwick. The top alteration permit was filed by a company tied to Shulem Rabi to convert a portion of a commercial property at 311 West 43rd Street into 14 residential units.
