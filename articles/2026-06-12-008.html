Colliers Brokers Sale of 52,889 SF Shopping Center in South Houston

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 14:57:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Colliers has brokered the sale of Parks at Boulder Creek, a 52,889-square-foot shopping center in South Houston. The property, anchored by a prominent wine and spirits retailer and featuring a strong tenant mix, was sold by an undisclosed seller to an undisclosed buyer. A Colliers broker represented the seller, while a broker from S+P Interests represented the buyer.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Parks at Boulder Creek

Address: 11575 Pearland Pkwy, Houston, TX*

Size: 52,889 SF

Site Area: 4.8 acres

Notable Features: Anchored by a major wine and spirits retailer.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
