Union Investment, Nuveen Sell Upper East Side Retail

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 20:24:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Union Investment and Nuveen Real Estate have sold their 1511 Third Ave. retail property on Manhattan’s Upper East Side to Stockbridge. The fully leased property spans approximately 17,500 square feet across four floors and a basement. The sale was executed for strategic portfolio reasons and to enhance the fund's liquidity, amidst a transaction market characterized by limited activity and high interest rates.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 1511 Third Ave., New York City, NY

Size: 17,500 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Notable Features: Fully leased property spanning four floors plus a basement.

Project Notes: Union Investment acquired the retail property in 2016 as part of a closed joint venture deal with Nuveen Real Estate for its open-ended real estate fund Unilmmo: Global, which held a 49% stake.
