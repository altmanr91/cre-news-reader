Long-operating Garden State REIT calls it a day

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 18 May 2026 17:40:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

The First Real Estate Investment Trust of New Jersey (FREIT) has announced a plan for voluntary liquidation, intending to sell its entire portfolio of over a dozen properties, primarily in New Jersey, over the next 24 months. The move is expected to return significant capital to stockholders, with estimated net proceeds ranging from $24.44 to $30.03 per share, compared to a pre-announcement stock price of $15.25. The portfolio includes multifamily complexes, shopping centers, and vacant land, as well as a property in New York.

DATA POINTS

FREIT stock price before liquidation announcement: $15.25 per share

Expected net proceeds from asset sales: $24.44 to $30.03 per share

Number of multifamily complexes in portfolio: 6

Number of shopping centers in portfolio: 5

Number of vacant parcels in portfolio: 3
