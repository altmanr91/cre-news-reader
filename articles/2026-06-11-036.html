Housing on high: Mission District church-turned-condo up for sale

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A unique 5,300-square-foot condominium, converted from a former Christian Science church known as The Light House, is on the market in San Francisco's Mission District for $6.8 million. The three-bedroom, three-bathroom residence, which retains many original architectural features, is being sold by John Nichols, co-founder of 1-800-Contacts. The property is expected to attract empty nesters, artists, and founders, according to the listing agents.
