Sachem Capital, IRG to Form $3.4B Industrial REIT

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Mon, 18 May 2026 11:37:09 +0000

SUMMARY

Sachem Capital Corp., a mortgage REIT, and Industrial Realty Group (IRG) have entered into a definitive contribution agreement to form IRG Realty Trust, Inc. (IRGT). IRG will contribute 98 industrial assets from its portfolio to Sachem, creating a combined entity with an implied enterprise value of approximately $3.4 billion. The new REIT will focus on mission-critical industrial infrastructure, supporting manufacturing and distribution users, and is expected to become a top-10 publicly listed industrial REIT by enterprise value. Sachem's CEO noted that the transaction provides a powerful industrial platform with greater scale and a strategy for sustained growth, while also allowing Sachem's mortgage capabilities to continue providing capital solutions.
