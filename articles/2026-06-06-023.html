MG, Vertical score $100M construction loan for Coral Gables mixed-use

LOAN

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

MG Developer and Vertical Developments secured a $100 million construction loan for Alhambra Parc, a luxury mixed-use condominium project in Coral Gables. The financing, provided by Benmark Capital and arranged by Berkadia, will facilitate the development of 74 condos, 18,000 square feet of retail, and nearly 14,000 square feet of office space. This project marks MG Developer's tenth venture in Coral Gables, where the firm has been a prominent luxury residential developer.
