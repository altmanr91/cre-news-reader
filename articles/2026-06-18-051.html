Hackman Capital Faces $258M Default

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 14:49:59 -0400

SUMMARY

REO / Foreclosure

Hackman Capital Partners has defaulted on a $257.6 million loan from Deutsche Bank for its MBS Media Campus in Manhattan Beach, CA, potentially leading to foreclosure. This default is part of a broader trend affecting Hackman Capital's entertainment-focused properties, which have been impacted by a significant slowdown in filming and television production in Southern California. The company has faced multiple defaults and foreclosures on other studio assets in recent months, reflecting the challenging market conditions for studio owners.
