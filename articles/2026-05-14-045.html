Advance Realty Launches 1.1M-SF Life Science Campus in Bridgewater

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Wed, 13 May 2026 21:50:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Advance Realty Investors has launched Continuum, a new 1.1 million-square-foot life science and advanced manufacturing campus in Bridgewater, NJ. The project, situated on over 60 acres, offers build-to-suit opportunities and is supported by an approved site plan and a long-term PILOT agreement with the township. The developer aims to create a connected ecosystem for research, development, manufacturing, and logistics, enhancing efficiency and collaboration for users.
