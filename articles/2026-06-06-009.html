Creative Action to Open 25,000 SF Headquarters in Austin’s Mueller District

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 14:55:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Creative Action, a youth arts education organization, is set to open a new 25,000-square-foot headquarters in Austin's Mueller district. The facility will be split across two levels, dedicating 15,000 square feet to performing arts and communal gatherings, with the remaining 10,000 square feet allocated for administrative and programming functions. This move signifies a significant expansion for the organization within a prominent mixed-use redevelopment.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Size: 25,000 SF

Notable Features: Located within Mueller, a 700-acre mixed-use district redeveloped from a former municipal airport.
