Cost overruns, delays: Atlantic Pacific seeks affordable housing loan from Miami-Dade

LOAN

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 01 May 2026 21:00:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Atlantic Pacific Companies is seeking a $3.8 million loan from Miami-Dade County to cover unexpected construction cost overruns and delays across three affordable housing projects totaling 470 units. The financing, which would be redeployed from funds Atlantic Pacific previously repaid to the county, is intended to ensure the affordability of units for low-income tenants. The loan features zero interest for the first two years, followed by a low 0.75 percent interest rate over a 30-year term.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Size: 470 units

Loan Amount: $3.8M

Loan Per Unit: $8,085/unit (calculated)

Notable Features: Units across the three projects are reserved for households earning between 30 percent and 80 percent of the area median income.

Project Notes: The loan proceeds are being redeployed from Atlantic Pacific's repayment of a $3.8 million surtax from the completed Amber Garden development. The Brownsville Village V project faced a nearly two-year delay due to issues with the ground lease between the developer and Miami-Dade County.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SPONSOR: Atlantic Pacific Companies — Kenneth Naylor

LENDER: Miami-Dade County

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Perrine Apartments II: 150-unit age-restricted affordable housing project at 10060 West Evergreen Street, West Perrine, Miami-Dade. Expected completion Q3 2026.

Brownsville Village V: 120-unit affordable housing project at 5115 Northwest 29th Avenue, Brownsville, Miami-Dade. Expected completion Q4 2026.

Northside Property III: 200-unit affordable housing project at 3180 Northwest 79th Street, Miami-Dade. Expected completion Q4 2026.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Approximately 10,000 units in Rental Assistance Demonstration (RAD) projects have been approved in Miami-Dade in the past year, allowing developers to lease public housing complexes from the county in exchange for building affordable and workforce units.
