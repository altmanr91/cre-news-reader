Once distressed office building in Rosemont lists following tenant boost

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A previously distressed, 255,486-square-foot office building in Rosemont, IL, is being marketed for sale by its special servicer following a significant increase in occupancy. The nine-story Columbia Centre III, located near O'Hare International Airport and major interstates, saw its occupancy rise to 75% with a weighted average lease term of approximately five years after a major tenant committed to relocating its headquarters to the property. The building was taken over by the lender in 2025 through a deed in lieu of foreclosure from its previous owner.
