Content Creation Platform Spotter Takes 17K SF at Thor’s 25 West 39th Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:06:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Spotter, a content creation platform, has signed a 17,000-square-foot lease for the entire 14th floor at 25 West 39th Street in Midtown Manhattan. The three-year lease was secured with landlord Thor Equities, with an asking rent of approximately $70 per square foot. This transaction marks a significant office commitment for the Los Angeles-headquartered company in New York City.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 25 West 39th Street

Address: 25 West 39th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 17,000 SF

Rental Rate: $70.00/SF/yr

Year Built: 1907

Notable Features: 16-story office building located one block south of Bryant Park.

Project Notes: Building was originally the Engineering Society's building until its conversion to office in 1961. Thor Equities acquired the building in 2006 for $82 million.
