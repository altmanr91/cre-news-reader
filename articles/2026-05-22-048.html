Choice Hotels International CEO Steps Down

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 21 May 2026 11:57:35 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Patrick Pacious has stepped down as president and CEO of Choice Hotels International after leading the company since 2017 and serving for over two decades. Dominic Dragisich, Chief Growth and Strategy Officer, has been appointed interim CEO while the board conducts a search for a permanent replacement. The transition is described as amicable, with Pacious remaining in an advisory role through August. Choice Hotels reported strong first-quarter revenue, exceeding analyst estimates, despite slight year-over-year declines in revenue per available room, average daily rate, and occupancy.
