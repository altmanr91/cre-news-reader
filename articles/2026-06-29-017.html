Austin’s top residential agent is in hot water

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Sun, 28 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Morgan Stanley is partnering with Trammell Crow to develop a new built-to-suit office at 2401 McKinney Avenue in Dallas, with the bank committing $700 million and Trammell Crow contributing $650 million. The city of Dallas has approved an $18.5 million incentive package for the project, which is expected to open in 2031. In the interim, Morgan Stanley has signed a 255,000-square-foot lease at Fountain Place downtown, providing a temporary boost to the area.
