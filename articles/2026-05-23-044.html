Kayne Anderson Raises Record $5B For Latest Alternatives Fund

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 22 May 2026 11:53:32 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Fund Close

Kayne Anderson Real Estate, the real estate private equity arm of Kayne Anderson Capital Advisors, has successfully closed its seventh fund, Kayne Anderson Real Estate Partners VII, with a record $5.12 billion in capital raised. This significantly exceeded its $3 billion target, demonstrating strong investor confidence in alternative real estate sectors. The fund will strategically focus on investments in medical office, senior housing, student housing, and light industrial assets, which the firm's CEO believes are poised for substantial growth driven by long-term demographic and e-commerce trends.
