Vornado exec says they are “ready to roll” on 350 Park

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 06 May 2026 21:39:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Vornado Realty Trust is moving forward with its 2 million-square-foot office tower at 350 Park Avenue in New York City, despite speculation that Citadel might withdraw from the project. A Vornado executive confirmed that demolition has begun and the firm is "ready to roll," with a mid-July deadline to commit fully to the joint venture or sell its stake. The executive indicated Vornado's intention to remain involved, noting strong tenant interest years before the building's completion, reflecting a tight market for high-quality office space. The project is a joint venture with Rudin and Citadel.
