From “Pompa-no to Pompa-now”: Mapping Pompano’s latest development boom

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 19:14:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Development Pipeline

Pompano Beach is experiencing a significant development boom, transforming from a quiet town into a luxury destination with numerous high-end residential and hospitality projects. Developers are drawn to the area's oceanfront land availability and cooperative city environment, offering a more accessible price point compared to neighboring South Florida markets. The influx of branded residences and large-scale apartment complexes is attracting buyers from high-tax states and international markets, signaling a new era of growth and investment for the city.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Pompano Beach is seeing a surge in luxury developments, including branded residences like Ritz-Carlton and Waldorf Astoria. The city's oceanfront land availability and developer-friendly environment are key drivers. Average sales prices for luxury condos are around $1,600 per square foot. The market is attracting buyers from California, New York, and Canada.
