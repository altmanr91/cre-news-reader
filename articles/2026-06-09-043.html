Walker & Dunlop, Pretium Close on $76M Bridge Loan for Chelsea Apartments

LOAN

Connect CRE — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 21:41:47 +0000

SUMMARY

Walker & Dunlop and Pretium, through their affordable housing bridge lending joint venture, have closed a $75.7 million bridge loan for a 174-unit affordable multifamily community in Manhattan's Chelsea neighborhood. This financing will provide short-term interim capital, supporting the property until it secures a permanent refinance through the HUD 223(f) program. The transaction underscores the venture's commitment to preserving affordable housing and offering tailored financing solutions during transitional periods for property owners.
