Pharrell Williams’s Nami Nori Hand Roll Concept to Open in West Palm Beach

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 19:25:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Launchpad Hospitality, the firm behind the Nami Nori hand roll restaurant concept, has signed a 4,300-square-foot lease in West Palm Beach, Florida. The restaurant, backed by musician Pharrell Williams, will open in the new Nora District at 855 North Railroad Avenue, featuring an outdoor patio. The landlord, a joint venture led by Place Projects, noted that Nami Nori's exceptional food and high-design environment align perfectly with the district's vision.
