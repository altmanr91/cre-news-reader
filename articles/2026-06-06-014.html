Venture One Real Estate acquires 59,611-square-foot industrial building in Mundelein

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Venture One Real Estate, through its fund VK Industrial VII, LP, has acquired a 59,611-square-foot multi-tenant industrial building in Mundelein, Illinois. The property was fully leased at the time of closing, indicating strong market demand for industrial space in the area. This acquisition expands Venture One's industrial portfolio with a well-occupied asset.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 1150-1170 Allanson Road, Mundelein, IL

Size: 59,611 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Year Built: 1977

Notable Features: Multi-tenant building demised into three units, featuring four docks and two drive-in doors.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Venture One Real Estate

SELLER BROKER: Colliers International — Jeff Devine; Tyler Ziebel
