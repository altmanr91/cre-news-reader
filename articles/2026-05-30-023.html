Tavistock adds resi, grocery store, promenade in Pier Sixty-Six’s next phase

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 29 May 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Tavistock Development is embarking on the second phase of its Pier Sixty-Six redevelopment in Fort Lauderdale, proposing four mixed-use buildings that will add 339 residential units, a grocery store, and significant retail and office space. This expansion, spanning 8 acres across both sides of Southeast 17th Street, aims to further integrate the recently reopened resort and superyacht marina with the surrounding waterfront neighborhood. The project includes a large waterfront promenade, an acre-sized water basin, and public improvements, building on the developer's substantial prior investment in renovating the iconic property.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Resort Residences: Two-building, 31-unit project completed in 2024.

Azul: 11-story condominium tower with 29 units, certificate of occupancy secured in 2024.

Indigo: 11-story tower with 28 units, completed in 2025.
