Prologis’ $352.2 million Broward County industrial buy is biggest this year

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 21:42:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Prologis has acquired the Davie Business Center, a seven-warehouse industrial portfolio in Broward County, Florida, from IDI Logistics for $352.2 million. This acquisition, totaling 1.2 million square feet, represents the largest industrial transaction in South Florida this year. The portfolio, built between 2013 and 2022, is 97% leased to a diverse tenant mix, significantly expanding Prologis' presence in the region.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Davie Business Center

Address: 3300, 3350, 3360, 3370, 3380, 3410 and 3430 Davie Road, Davie, FL

Size: 1,200,000 SF

Sale Price: $352.2M

Occupancy at Sale: 97.0%

Project Notes: The warehouses were built between 2013 and 2022.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Prologis — Alex Vargas Vila, Vice President and Investment Officer

SELLER: IDI Logistics

SELLER BROKER: CBRE National Partners

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Broward County's industrial market is showing signs of stabilization, with vacancy holding steady at 5.5% in Q1 and leasing volume increasing by 24%. Average rents in Q1 were $17.50.
