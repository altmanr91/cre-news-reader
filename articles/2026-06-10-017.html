Trammell Crow, PCCP secure Denver area’s largest industrial lease in years

LEASE

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Trammell Crow Company and PCCP have secured the largest industrial lease in the Denver area since 2024, with an undisclosed tenant committing to a nearly 410,000-square-foot building at the under-construction Crossroads 25 industrial complex in Thornton. This significant lease brings the first phase of the 1.1 million-square-foot development to 49 percent pre-leased, demonstrating strong demand for modern industrial space in the market despite a recent period of negative absorption.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Crossroads 25

Size: 410,000 SF

Notable Features: Part of a 76-acre industrial complex planned for 1.1 million square feet.

Project Notes: The 2.6-acre site was previously acquired by Trammell Crow and PCCP for $18.9 million from an affiliate of Noddle Companies, which had purchased it in 2020 for $19.5 million as part of a larger acquisition. The first phase of 828,000 square feet across four buildings is expected to be delivered in Q3 2027, with full complex completion projected for 2030.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LANDLORD: Trammell Crow Company

LANDLORD: PCCP

SELLER BROKER: CBRE — Tyler Carner

SELLER: Noddle Companies

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Denver metro's industrial market had a direct vacancy rate of 9.3 percent in Q1 2026, with over 4 million square feet under construction and negative absorption following substantial completions in 2025.
