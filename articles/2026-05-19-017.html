NYC’s top deals: Hennick Group buys Midtown property for $66M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 19 May 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Hennick Group, a Toronto-based firm, acquired a 14-story commercial building at 13 West 56th Street in Midtown, New York City, for $66.2 million. The nearly 55,000-square-foot property was sold by an LLC associated with BD Hotels. This transaction represents the top commercial deal recorded in the city during the reporting period.
