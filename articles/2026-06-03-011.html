Marcus & Millichap negotiates sale of 16,933-square-foot Walgreens property in Appleton

SALE

REJournals — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of a 16,933-square-foot Walgreens property in Appleton, Wisconsin, for $3.15 million. The buyer, a Chicago-based family office, acquired the asset as part of a 1031 exchange and intends to hold it as a long-term investment.
