Hines scores $142M refi for Wolf Point West rental tower

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 18 May 2026 17:24:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Hines and its partners have secured a $141.8 million refinancing for Wolf Point West, a 509-unit luxury rental tower located on the Chicago River. The three-year, floating-rate loan was provided by New York Life Insurance Company and arranged by JLL Capital Markets. This transaction marks the second significant debt deal for the ownership group at the riverfront site within the past year, reflecting strong institutional investor interest in Chicago's multifamily market due to limited new supply and robust rent growth.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Chicago rents are growing at 4.2 percent annually, significantly higher than the 0.4 percent national average. Chicago continues to be a major destination for corporate relocation. The owners of the luxury Aqua tower in Lakeshore East secured a $144 million refinancing package in April.
