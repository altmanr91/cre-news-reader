South Florida’s top deals: Hillsboro Beach mansion trades for $28M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 28 May 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

An affiliate of TA Realty acquired an industrial building at 7200 Northwest 25th Street in Miami for $47.6 million. The seller, an affiliate of AEW Capital Management, had previously purchased the nearly 179,000-square-foot property in 2019 for $25 million. This transaction represents the top commercial deal in the tri-county region, with the buyer having prior ownership of the complex.
