Property Owners' Efforts To Green Their Buildings Are Hitting A Wall

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 14:58:44 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Commercial property owners are encountering significant obstacles in their efforts to further green their buildings, despite prior progress driven by local regulations, tenant demand, and lender requirements. Industry experts note that physical building constraints, the continued reliance on fossil fuels by the national power grid, and recent federal policy changes impacting renewable energy incentives are collectively hindering landlords' ability to reduce emissions. This stagnation in sustainability efforts could lead to a devaluation of assets, as green improvements have historically commanded a value premium. While initial steps like LED conversions and insulation have been widely adopted, more substantial retrofits involving core infrastructure changes are proving challenging due to space limitations and the carbon intensity of the broader energy supply.
