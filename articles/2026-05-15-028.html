One Sotheby’s expands to the Northeast with New Jersey acquisition

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 14 May 2026 19:36:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

One Sotheby's International Realty, a Coconut Grove-based brokerage, has expanded its operations into the Northeast through the acquisition of Callaway Henderson Sotheby's International Realty in New Jersey. This strategic move brings 135 agents under the One Sotheby's umbrella and marks a significant step in the firm's out-of-state growth ambitions, as articulated by its new CEO. Callaway Henderson, founded in 1953, was a top-ranked firm in Mercer County, New Jersey, and also active in surrounding counties in New Jersey and Pennsylvania, having closed nearly $800 million in sales in 2025.
