Lee & Associates brokers 40,873-square-foot industrial lease in Elgin

LEASE

REJournals — Tue, 05 May 2026 10:04:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates has brokered a new lease for a 40,873-square-foot industrial unit at the Fox River Business Center in Elgin, Illinois. The tenant, a specialty pharmaceutical service provider, was represented by Cushman & Wakefield, while Lee & Associates represented the landlord, High Street Logistics Properties. The property offers high visibility and is strategically located near a major tollway.
