JLL Capital Markets closes sale of 1.74-acre redevelopment site in Dallas

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 04 May 2026 10:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has facilitated the sale of Monticello West, a 1.74-acre redevelopment site in Dallas, TX, to McKinney Webb Holdings, LLC. The property, sold by Lakemore LCS Leasing MW LLC, is strategically located between Highland Park and the Knox-Henderson Avenue corridor, offering significant development potential for multifamily, office, or hotel uses due to its flexible zoning and proximity to amenities like the Katy Trail.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: land

Property Name: Monticello West

Address: 5114 McKinney Ave., Dallas, TX

Site Area: 1.74 acres

Notable Features: Zoned PD 193 LC allowing for multifamily, office, or hotel development with a maximum height of 240 feet and a 4.5:1 FAR. Located between Highland Park and Knox-Henderson Avenue corridor, 300 feet from the Katy Trail.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Lakemore LCS Leasing MW LLC

BUYER: McKinney Webb Holdings, LLC

SELLER BROKER: JLL Capital Markets — Jonathan Carrier, Managing Director; Michael Swaldi, Senior Managing Director; Adam McDaniel, Associate
