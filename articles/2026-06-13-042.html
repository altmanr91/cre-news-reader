Former Performing Arts School Sells to Summa Academy as Its Third Bay Area Campus

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 20:45:46 +0000

SUMMARY

Summa Academy, a non-public school for students with autism and special needs, acquired a 43,702-square-foot former performing arts school facility at 2730 Mitchell Drive in Walnut Creek, CA, for $7.1 million. This acquisition marks Summa Academy's third and largest campus in the East Bay, providing expanded space for individualized education and life-skills development, including a mock apartment. Brokers involved in the transaction emphasized the importance of preserving the site for educational use within the community.
