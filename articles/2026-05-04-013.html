The Closing: Jonathan Landau

LOAN

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 04 May 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Jonathan Landau, founder of Landau Properties, recently secured $213 million in financing for a planned 47-story residential tower at 205 Montague Street in Brooklyn. This project marks a significant step for his new firm, which he co-founded with his daughter, son, and son-in-law after departing Fortis in 2022. Landau aims to develop properties that are "a little bit better" than competitors, drawing on his experience with successful projects like Olympia Dumbo, a 33-story condo tower that set Brooklyn sales records. The Montague Street development is strategically located at the intersection of Downtown Brooklyn and Brooklyn Heights, an area Landau considers prime for development.
