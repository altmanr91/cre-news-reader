DuWest Realty, DBA Commercial Real Estate form Dallas-based DUWEST

REJournals — Fri, 15 May 2026 10:03:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

DuWest Realty and DBA Commercial Real Estate, both Dallas-based firms, have merged to form DUWEST, creating a unified commercial real estate platform focused on retail and land brokerage, tenant representation, investment sales, development services, and property management throughout Texas. The combined entity will leverage the strengths of both legacy companies, enhancing its statewide presence and service offerings. The new firm aims to be a dynamic and well-regarded entity in the state, emphasizing experienced leadership and a deep understanding of retail real estate economics.
