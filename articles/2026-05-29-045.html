Downtown Atlanta Is Poised For Revival. Businesses Are Still Leaving

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 28 May 2026 16:51:30 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Downtown Atlanta is undergoing a significant revitalization driven by billions in new development, including CIM Group's Centennial Yards, CP Group's rebranded CNN Center, and Atlanta Ventures' South Downtown mixed-use project. These initiatives aim to attract visitors and consumers, but the office market continues to struggle with a net loss of 2 million square feet since 2020. The exodus of "legacy tenants" to submarkets like Midtown is attributed to aging office stock, landlord struggles, and perceptions of crime and vagrancy. However, some experts believe the office ecosystem is transforming, with a potential for new "urban pioneer" tenants and government entities drawn by affordable rents, though the lack of dense residential population remains a critical challenge for sustained growth.
