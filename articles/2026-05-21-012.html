JLL: Commercial real estate investors are back in spend mode

REJournals — Thu, 21 May 2026 10:08:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Investment Report

Commercial real estate investors are increasingly confident and actively deploying capital, with a notable shift towards markets exhibiting strong long-term fundamentals such as population growth, infrastructure investment, and diversified economies. This trend is evidenced by a significant increase in global and U.S. transaction volumes in Q1 2026, with specific U.S. markets like San Francisco, Chicago, and Atlanta experiencing substantial surges in investment activity. Investors are prioritizing future growth potential over immediate yields, leading to a fundamental change in capital allocation strategies. The office sector, in particular, has shown a strong rebound, leading investment-sales growth globally and in the U.S., driven by large transactions and limited new supply.
