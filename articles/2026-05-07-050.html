Pablo Castro Denies Laura Tauber Was Ever His Partner On 4,000-Unit HueHub Project

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 06 May 2026 16:31:49 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A legal dispute has emerged over the HueHub project, a large-scale housing development in Miami. Developer Pablo Castro is refuting claims by Laura Tauber, who alleges she was a partner in the project, instrumental in securing approvals and union agreements, and is owed compensation and equity. Castro contends that Tauber was never a partner and her involvement was solely to learn about workforce housing development, with any future benefit contingent on her investment, which he claims never materialized. The project, which utilizes Florida's Live Local Act, has secured approval for its first phase, comprising over 4,000 units across six towers, with a significant portion dedicated to affordable housing for union members.
