Starwood Property Trust Q1 Rental Income Shoots Up While It Settles Troubled Assets

Commercial Observer — Fri, 08 May 2026 18:55:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Earnings Report

Starwood Property Trust reported a mixed first quarter for 2026, with net income declining despite increased revenue and a significant rise in rental income from its property and net-lease holdings. The mortgage REIT continued to resolve legacy assets, including foreclosures on mixed-use and multifamily properties in Dallas and Phoenix, and a third-party sale of the Scribner Building in Manhattan. The firm deployed $2.5 billion in new investments during the quarter, focusing on disciplined origination and balance sheet optimization, with a strategic shift away from office properties towards multifamily, industrial, net lease, and data centers.
