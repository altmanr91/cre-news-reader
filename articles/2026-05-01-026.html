Trading card mogul sells Mag Mile condo for $5M

SALE

Chicago Real Estate News - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Matthew Bayer, CEO of MJ Holding Company, sold his duplex condo at One Magnificent Mile for $5.1 million. The two-floor, 9,000-square-foot unit, located on the 46th and 47th floors, features six bedrooms and 11 bathrooms and underwent a significant remodel last year. The sale reflects an uptick in activity within the downtown condo market, which has experienced lagging prices and slow sales in recent years.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: One Magnificent Mile

Address: 950 North Michigan Avenue, Chicago, Illinois

Size: 9,000 SF

Sale Price: $5.1M

$/SF: $567/SF (calculated)

Notable Features: Duplex unit on 46th and 47th floors with a great room, wet bar, wine storage, private terrace, and wellness and recreation rooms. Designed by Wheeler Kearns Architects.

Project Notes: The sellers previously acquired the condo in two stages between 2010 and 2014 for a combined $2 million. The unit was initially listed in September for just under $6 million, with the price later reduced to $5.5 million in January before going under contract in March.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: MJ Holding Company — Matthew Bayer, CEO; Joyce Bayer

SELLER BROKER: Jameson Sotheby’s International Realty — Ryan Preuett; Tim Salm

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The downtown Chicago condo market is experiencing an uptick in activity after years of lagging prices and slow sales. This is the third downtown condo to sell for over $5 million so far this year, following a $5.4 million unit sale at the St. Regis Chicago and a $5.5 million sale in the Palmolive Building, both in March.
