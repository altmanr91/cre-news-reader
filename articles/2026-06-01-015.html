Seniors housing community opens in St. Francis

REJournals — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 10:03:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

A new senior living community, Vista Prairie at Eagle Pointe, has officially opened in St. Francis, Minnesota. The community offers independent living, assisted living, memory care, and care suites, providing a range of housing and care options for older adults. Located at 23440 Ambassador Boulevard, the development aims to address the increasing demand for senior housing in the area with a focus on resident wellness, comfort, and social connection.
