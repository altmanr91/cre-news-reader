601W Companies seeking three-year extension on $536M Aon Center loan

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

601W Companies is seeking a three-year extension on its $536 million senior loan for the Aon Center in Chicago, citing an inability to pay off the mortgage by its July 1 maturation date. This marks the second time the loan has been extended, and the property recently returned to special servicing after the owner allegedly missed a payment for tenant improvements. The building's occupancy has declined significantly, and its appraised value was nearly halved in 2023.
