REBNY legislative lead resigns after tearing down political signs

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 22:22:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

Dev Awasthi, REBNY's vice president of legislative affairs for New York, resigned after being filmed removing and trashing campaign signs for congressional candidate Jack Schlossberg. REBNY stated that Awasthi's actions were inappropriate and did not meet their employee standards, leading to his immediate termination. The incident occurred near Third Avenue and 32nd Street in Manhattan, within the 12th Congressional district.
