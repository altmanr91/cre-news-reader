Marcus & Millichap Brokers $14M Sale, Financing of Indiana Property

SALE

Connect CRE — Thu, 21 May 2026 21:38:49 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap facilitated the $14.1 million sale of The Villas at Green Valley, an 84-unit multifamily property in New Albany, Indiana. The buyer, a California-based investor, utilized a 1031 exchange to consolidate multiple California properties into this newer, larger community. Marcus & Millichap Capital Corporation also arranged $9.2 million in acquisition financing for the transaction.
