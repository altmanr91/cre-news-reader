Streetlights Residential developing 22-story multifamily development in Plano

DEVELOPMENT

REJournals — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

StreetLights Residential is developing a 22-story luxury multifamily high-rise and three adjacent townhomes in Plano, TX. The project, located at 6501 Legacy Drive, will feature 261 high-rise residences and three townhomes for lease, with construction slated to begin in June 2026 and completion expected in February 2029. The development aims to offer a refined, hospitality-driven living experience with extensive amenities, appealing to discerning renters seeking larger floorplans and a maintenance-free lifestyle in a connected outdoor environment.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 6501 Legacy Drive, Plano, TX

Units: 264

Completion: February 2029

Notable Features: 22-story luxury high-rise with three adjacent townhomes, located within the 100-acre former JCPenney headquarters campus, offering walkable access to Legacy West and immediate access to walking trails, city parks, dog parks, and pickleball courts.

Project Notes: High-rise residences will range from 682 to 2,843 square feet. Townhomes will be three-bedroom, three-and-a-half-bath layouts ranging from 3,283 to 3,316 square feet. Construction is scheduled to begin in June 2026.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: StreetLights Residential — Greg Coutant, Senior Vice President of Development
