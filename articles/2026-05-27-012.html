Trammell Crow Company breaks ground on 788,320-square-foot business center in Plainfield

CONSTRUCTION

REJournals — Tue, 26 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Trammell Crow Company has broken ground on Building 2 of the Plainfield Business Center, a 788,320-square-foot speculative warehouse in Plainfield, Illinois. This industrial facility is expected to be completed by the third quarter of 2027 and is being marketed for sale or lease. The broader Plainfield Business Center is planned to be a significant industrial development, ultimately comprising over 8 million square feet.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Plainfield Business Center, Building 2

Address: 26230 W. 143rd St., Plainfield, IL

Size: 788,320 SF

Completion: Q3 2027

Total Project (All Phases): over 8 million square feet

Site Area: 46 acres

Notable Features: Features 40-foot clear heights, 80 dock doors expandable to 156, and 211 trailer parking stalls. Located with proximity to Interstate 55 and I-80.

Project Notes: Building 1, also 788,320 square feet, was delivered earlier in 2026 and is fully leased.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Trammell Crow Company

LEASING AGENT: CBRE — Matt Mulvihill; Phil DeBoer

ARCHITECT: Harris Architects

ENGINEER: Kimley-Horn and Associates

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: FCL Builders
