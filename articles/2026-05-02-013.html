Associated Bank closes $22.6 million acquisition loan for 66,083-square-foot former grocery store in Buffalo Grove

LOAN

REJournals — Fri, 01 May 2026 15:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Associated Bank provided a $22.6 million acquisition and renovation loan to Shorewood Development Group for a vacant 66,083-square-foot former grocery store in Buffalo Grove, IL. The property, located at a prominent intersection, will be redeveloped into Chase Plaza, a two-tenant retail center anchored by a grocery store and a fitness center. This project marks the 13th loan Associated Bank has provided to Shorewood Development Group, highlighting a strong ongoing relationship.
