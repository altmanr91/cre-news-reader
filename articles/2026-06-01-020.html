Editor’s note: Almost human

The Real Deal — National — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Editorial

This editorial discusses the impact of artificial intelligence on the real estate industry, highlighting both its potential and the anxieties it creates. The author notes that while AI bots can perform various tasks, they still exhibit some limitations. The article explores how AI is influencing residential real estate brokers by empowering clients and agents, potentially increasing workloads and complicating deals. It also touches on the demands of AI startup founders in the office leasing market and features a debate on whether AI will ultimately harm the industry. The piece also previews other topics in the June issue, including the Hamptons market and developer practices.
