Hospital adds massive land buy to $300M building expansion in Denver suburb

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 12 May 2026 15:09:10 +0000

SUMMARY

AdventHealth, a Florida-based nonprofit healthcare system, has acquired a 44-acre parcel of land in Parker, CO, for $16.5 million from Bowey Family Partnership LLLP. The land is adjacent to AdventHealth Parker, where the system is currently undertaking a $300 million, 186,000-square-foot expansion. While specific plans for the newly acquired land have not been disclosed, the purchase reflects the growing demand for healthcare services in Denver's rapidly expanding suburbs.
