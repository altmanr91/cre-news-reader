Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 12,000-square-foot industrial property in Midland

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 18 May 2026 09:44:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Coastal Pump & Equipment, a 12,000-square-foot industrial property in Midland, Texas. The single-tenant asset, built in 2019, sits on 6.66 acres and is leased to a water transfer equipment and service provider with approximately four years remaining on a triple-net lease featuring 2% annual rent increases. The transaction closed at 96% of the original list price, attracting multiple offers from out-of-state investors.
