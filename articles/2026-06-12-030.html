Prada-anchored Oak Street retail building hits market with $30M target

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

J. Safra Real Estate has listed its Prada-anchored retail building on Oak Street in Chicago's Gold Coast for sale, with an expected price of around $30 million. The property, located in a premier high-end shopping district, features 11,000 square feet of retail space. The listing comes as Oak Street maintains low vacancy rates, prompting several owners to test the market.
