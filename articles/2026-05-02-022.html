Steve Madden walks into 60K sf at ESRT’s 501 Seventh Ave

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 01 May 2026 15:37:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Steve Madden has signed a 13-year lease for 60,000 square feet at Empire State Realty Trust's 501 Seventh Avenue in the Garment District. The shoe and accessories retailer is relocating its corporate headquarters from Long Island City to this Manhattan property. The deal was disclosed in ESRT's first-quarter earnings report, with asking rents in the low $60s per square foot.
