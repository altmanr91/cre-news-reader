Continental Spins Off 95K-SF Mount Pleasant Retail Center

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:35:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Continental Realty Corporation (CRC) has sold Sweetgrass Corner and The Shops at Towne Centre Way, two fully leased retail properties totaling 95,370 square feet in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina. CRC had acquired these assets in 2022 as part of a strategy to improve the retail environment around its adjacent Mount Pleasant Towne Centre. Notably, Sweetgrass Corner was transformed from a largely vacant center, including a long-empty grocery store, into a fully occupied retail destination.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Sweetgrass Corner and The Shops at Towne Centre Way

Address: 1903 and 1909 Highway 17 North, Mount Pleasant, SC

Size: 95,370 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Project Notes: Sweetgrass Corner was previously acquired for $17 million and The Shops at Towne Centre Way for $3.9 million in 2022. Sweetgrass Corner was approximately 33% leased at the time of acquisition and included a 59,824-square-foot former BI-LO grocery store that had been vacant for over two years.
