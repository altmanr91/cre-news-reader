Moonwater Capital Breaks Ground on 220,000 SF Halo Tower in Southwest Las Vegas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 13:19:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Moonwater Capital has commenced construction on Halo Tower, an eight-story Class A office building in southwest Las Vegas, with completion anticipated in the third quarter of 2028. The project will feature 220,000 square feet of modern office space, including high lobby ceilings and a rooftop amenity deck offering panoramic views. CBRE has been appointed to manage the marketing and leasing efforts for the property.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Halo Tower

Size: 220,000 SF

Completion: 3Q 2028

Notable Features: Class A office property with 15- to 17-foot lobby heights and an indoor-outdoor rooftop boardroom and amenity deck with panoramic views.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
