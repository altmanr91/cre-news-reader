Core Spaces to develop three new student housing developments near University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

DEVELOPMENT

REJournals — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Core Spaces, a prominent student housing developer, has announced plans for three new purpose-built student housing developments near the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. These projects are expected to deliver over 3,500 beds between 2028 and 2031, significantly expanding the firm's presence in the market. The expansion builds on Core Spaces' existing portfolio in Champaign, which includes previous developments and recent acquisitions along Green Street.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Property Name: Hub Champaign Green Street

Units: 490

Beds: 1,577

Completion: Summer 2028

Project Notes: This project is the first of three new developments, with the remaining two planned to deliver between 2028 and 2031, totaling over 3,500 beds across all three projects.
