Former IHOP Site in Pacific Beach Trades as Redevelopment Opportunity

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 21:35:45 +0000

SUMMARY

A former IHOP site in Pacific Beach, San Diego, has been sold for $8 million, presenting a rare redevelopment opportunity. The buyer, 856 Grand Ave LLC, plans to explore multifamily redevelopment for the property, capitalizing on its prime coastal location in a supply-constrained neighborhood. The acquisition is significant due to the scarcity of development sites of this scale along Grand Avenue and its proximity to the beach.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: development site

Address: 856 Grand Ave, San Diego, CA

Sale Price: $8.0M

Notable Features: Located approximately one block from the Pacific Ocean in a highly visible coastal area.

Project Notes: Site was formerly occupied by IHOP.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
