Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of 1,087-Unit Self-Storage Portfolio in Elgin, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 14:51:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of the Solid Ground Storage Portfolio, comprising two self-storage facilities in Elgin, Texas. The portfolio totals 126,038 net rentable square feet across 1,087 units. Both the seller and buyer requested anonymity in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: self-storage

Property Name: Solid Ground Storage Portfolio

Address: 505 and 1210 W. U.S. Highway 290, Elgin, TX

Size: 126,038 SF

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Dave Knobler; Mixson Staffel; Charles LeClaire; Jon Danklefs
