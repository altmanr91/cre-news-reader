Advanced Real Estate Acquires, Rebrands Rowland Heights Apartments

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 21:05:51 +0000

SUMMARY

Advanced Real Estate has acquired the 126-unit Casa La Paz apartment community in Rowland Heights, rebranding it as The Rowland. The property will undergo extensive renovations, including exterior, interior, and amenity upgrades. This acquisition, the fifth for Advanced Fund 24-3 in eight months, utilized a 721 tax-deferred exchange structure, allowing the former family ownership to contribute the property into the fund for partnership interests while also providing cash payouts to some partners.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Rowland (formerly Casa La Paz)

Units: 126

Project Notes: Acquired using a 721 tax-deferred "exchange and contribution" structure, allowing former owners to contribute the property into the fund in exchange for partnership interests.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Advanced Real Estate — Richard Julian, CEO

SELLER: Former ownership group
