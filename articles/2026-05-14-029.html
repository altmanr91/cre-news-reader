R.I.G. Capital snags O’Hare area apartment complex for record $167M deal

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 13 May 2026 21:33:45 +0000

SUMMARY

R.I.G. Capital has acquired the 1,115-unit Pavilion Apartments complex near O'Hare International Airport from Brookfield Asset Management for $167 million. This transaction represents the largest multifamily sale in Chicago for 2026 to date. The property's strategic location on Chicago's Far Northwest side allows it to compete with both urban and suburban apartment complexes, a factor highlighted by the buyer as a rare opportunity given the asset's scale and prior capital investments by the seller.
