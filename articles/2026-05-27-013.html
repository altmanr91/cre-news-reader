Roseville’s Lupe Development Partners name president, COO

REJournals — Tue, 26 May 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Lupe Development Partners, a Roseville, Minnesota-based developer, has appointed Aaron Diederich as its new president and chief operating officer. Diederich, who previously served as vice president of construction, will now lead the company, which has developed over 2,200 apartment and condominium units and more than one million square feet of retail, residential, and industrial property since its founding in 1989. Former President Lucy Brown and former Vice President Steve Minn are retiring from full-time roles but will remain on the Board of Directors, with Brown as Executive Chairwoman, and will transition from their ownership roles over the next few years.
