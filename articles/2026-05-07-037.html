Nate Paul’s Downtown Austin IHOP sells after bidding war at foreclosure auction

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 06 May 2026 18:57:59 +0000

SUMMARY

A downtown Austin IHOP property, previously owned by Nate Paul's World Class Holdings, was sold at a foreclosure auction for approximately $12.7 million to Travis County Exchange Corporation. The 0.8-acre site, located in the Rainey Street District, traded for about half its appraised value, presenting a redevelopment opportunity despite an existing tenant lease extending until at least June 2029. The sale is part of the ongoing disposition of Paul's portfolio following his legal issues.
