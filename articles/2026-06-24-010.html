Burlington Debuts 2 MSF Distribution Center in Ellabell, Georgia

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 14:48:41 +0000

SUMMARY

Burlington Stores Inc. has opened its first distribution center in Georgia, a 2 million-square-foot facility in Ellabell, Bryan County. This new center is expected to create 1,500 jobs at full capacity and includes extensive office space and employee amenities. The facility will support Burlington's expanding network of discount apparel stores, with the company planning to open approximately 115 net new stores nationwide by early 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Size: 2,000,000 SF

Notable Features: Includes 40,000 square feet of office space with a full-service cafeteria, fitness center, locker room with showers, meditation room, and maternity room.
