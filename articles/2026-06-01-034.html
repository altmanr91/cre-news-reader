AT&T adds insult to injury with replica of Dallas’ Reunion Tower on Plano campus

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Sun, 31 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

AT&T is developing a 54-acre campus in Plano, Texas, which will include a 280-foot replica of Dallas' Reunion Tower, serving as a communications antenna. This move follows AT&T's decision to relocate its headquarters from Downtown Dallas, a move that will leave a significant vacancy in the city's office market. The Plano campus, which received a $20 million incentive package from the city, is expected to be completed in the second half of 2028.
