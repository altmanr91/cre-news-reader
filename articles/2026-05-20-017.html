Brooklyn luxury contracts come back to earth with $80M week

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 19 May 2026 15:22:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Brooklyn's luxury residential market experienced a significant slowdown last week, with contract volume dropping to $80 million across 25 deals, following two consecutive weeks exceeding $150 million. The median asking price for these contracts was $2.5 million, with homes spending an average of 62 days on the market. The priciest contract was for a Carroll Gardens townhouse at 181 President Street, asking $8.3 million, while a unit at 110 Boerum Place in Cobble Hill secured the second-highest price at $5.6 million.
