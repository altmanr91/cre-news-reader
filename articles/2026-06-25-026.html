Will Lincoln Road reclaim its crown? 18K sf food hall leads recent leasing surge

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Leasing Activity

Lincoln Road in Miami Beach is experiencing a significant leasing surge, led by an 18,000-square-foot food hall, signaling a potential comeback for the pedestrian promenade. This activity supports the thesis of an investor group, including developer Michael Comras, who recently acquired 11 storefronts for $140 million and is working on the NoLi District revitalization. The resurgence is further bolstered by a $29.4 million infrastructure improvement project by the city and intensive marketing efforts by the Lincoln Road Business Improvement District.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Lincoln Road's asking retail rates were previously among South Florida's highest at $325 per square foot. Other districts like Brickell, City Centre, the Miami Design District, and Wynwood have risen in prominence, increasing competition for Lincoln Road.
