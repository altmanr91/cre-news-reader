Blackstone Levels Up San Francisco Footprint With $279M Hyatt Regency Purchase

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 11:44:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Blackstone has acquired the 821-key Hyatt Regency Embarcadero in San Francisco from Sunstone Hotel Investors for approximately $279 million. This acquisition reflects Blackstone's continued confidence in San Francisco's economic recovery, particularly driven by significant investment in the artificial intelligence sector, which is boosting business travel and the hospitality market. Hyatt will continue to manage the luxury waterfront hotel, which features extensive meeting space and was renovated in 2022.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

San Francisco boasts the highest concentration of AI firms globally, with 60 percent of new AI investment happening in the city. The city is also attracting the bulk of venture capital funding, driving business travel and making San Francisco the fastest-growing lodging market in the U.S. Office market activity is up 48 percent year-over-year.
