Belmont developer plans 202 apartments in neighboring San Mateo

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 19:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

The McLellan Company is moving forward with plans to develop a six-story, 202-unit multifamily building at 4095 Pacific Boulevard in San Mateo, CA. The project will include 25 deed-restricted affordable units and will benefit from state density bonus laws and streamlined review under Senate Bill 330. The development will replace an existing gas station and shopping center, contributing to San Mateo's state-mandated housing goals.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The City of San Mateo must plan for 7,015 new housing units by 2031. Other recent multifamily developments in San Mateo include Prometheus Real Estate Group's 128-unit Gateway project at 668 East Third Avenue, Align Real Estate's 396-unit project at 1655 South El Camino Real, and Tourbineau Real Estate Partners' 144-unit office-to-residential conversion at 2121 South El Camino Real.
