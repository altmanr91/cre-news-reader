Kalthia Group buys another San Jose hotel as Bay Area lodging market navigates defaults, foreclosures

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Kalthia Group Hotels has acquired the 88-room Super 8 by Wyndham in San Jose, CA, for $8 million. This purchase marks another addition to the firm's growing hotel portfolio in Silicon Valley, following two Sonesta ES Suites acquisitions in late 2025. The transaction occurs amidst a challenging Bay Area lodging market, characterized by increasing loan defaults and foreclosures, particularly among Super 8 properties and other hotels in San Jose.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Bay Area lodging market is experiencing loan defaults and foreclosures, with several Super 8 by Wyndham properties and other hotels in San Jose facing distress. New hotel construction in the region saw a 34.3% drop in rooms and a 28.6% decline in hotels year over year in 2025, with only 15 hotels totaling 1,610 rooms under construction.
