Long-awaited Four Seasons Lake Austin condo project lands $870M construction loan

LOAN

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 17:05:18 +0000

SUMMARY

The Four Seasons Private Residences Lake Austin, a highly anticipated luxury condominium project, has secured an $870 million construction loan from TYKO Capital. This significant financing, arranged by Adelaide Real Estate, Cobalt Equities, and JLL, is one of the largest ever for a Texas residential development. The project, developed by Austin Capital Partners and Lincoln Property Company, will feature private residences, villa lots, a private marina, and extensive amenities managed by Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: Four Seasons Private Residences Lake Austin

Address: 6507 Bridge Point Parkway, Austin, TX

Loan/Acre: $4,142,857/acre (calculated)

Loan/Land SF: $95.11/land SF (calculated)

Site Area: 210 acres

Notable Features: Features nearly one mile of Lake Austin shoreline, a restaurant by Michelin-starred chef Daniel Boulud, a private theater, sports lounge, infinity pool, spa, nature trails, clubhouse, and a working produce farm.

Project Notes: The project will include 28 villa lots and a private marina with 50 boat slips. Austin Capital Partners has cycled through three development partners since the project's 2021 announcement: Hines, Turnbridge Equities, and now Lincoln Property Company.
