Reschke tees up 55 West Monroe purchase in latest Loop office bargain

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 21 May 2026 16:38:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Prime Group, led by Mike Reschke, has acquired the 40-story office tower at 55 West Monroe Street in Chicago for approximately $25 million. The 815,000-square-foot building, which was previously purchased for $243 million in 2014, is currently over 80 percent vacant due to the impact of remote work. Reschke plans to maintain the property as a Class A office building for now, but is considering a future mixed-use conversion to include residential and hotel components, aligning with his strategy to revitalize the Loop through such developments.
