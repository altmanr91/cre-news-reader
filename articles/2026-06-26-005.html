Law Firm Alston & Bird Takes 170K SF at 51 West 52nd Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 16:44:02 +0000

SUMMARY

International law firm Alston & Bird has signed a 15-year lease for 169,664 square feet at 51 West 52nd Street in Midtown Manhattan, relocating its offices from 90 Park Avenue. The transaction brings the Harbor Group International-owned trophy property to full occupancy, underscoring the continued strength of New York City's prime office market. The landlord's CEO expressed confidence in the resilience of the market for top-tier assets in desirable locations.
