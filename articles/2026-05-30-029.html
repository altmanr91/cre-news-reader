Chicago’s Trump Tower lands first riverfront tenant in 17 years

LEASE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 29 May 2026 15:28:13 +0000

SUMMARY

Prasino Cafe has signed a lease for 2,800 square feet at the riverwalk level of Trump International Hotel & Tower in Chicago, marking the first retail tenant for this space in 17 years. The deal fills a small portion of the tower's 70,000 square feet of retail space, which has largely remained vacant since the building opened in 2009. Despite the challenges associated with the building's namesake, the broker involved in the transaction emphasized the prime location of the space along the Chicago River.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Trump International Hotel & Tower

Address: 401 North Wabash Avenue, Chicago, IL

Size: 2,800 SF

Year Built: 2009

Completion: spring 2027

Notable Features: Located at the riverwalk level of a 98-story mixed-use tower with 486 luxury condos and a 339-key hotel.

Project Notes: The retail space has been vacant since the tower opened in 2009. The building has a total square footage of 2.6 million square feet.
