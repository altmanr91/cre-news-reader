Brexit Slowly But Surely Remade London Commercial Real Estate. Now The Future Is Even Less Certain

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 07:13:57 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

A decade after the Brexit vote, London's commercial real estate market has been fundamentally reshaped, with investment volumes and leasing activity failing to return to pre-2016 levels. The political instability unleashed by Brexit, coupled with subsequent geopolitical and macroeconomic shocks, has made buyers wary and shifted the investor landscape, with institutional buyers decreasing significantly. While London retains its appeal and has seen strong rent growth for prime offices and an increase in office jobs, the market's future remains deeply uncertain amidst higher interest rates and slower growth compared to continental European counterparts.
