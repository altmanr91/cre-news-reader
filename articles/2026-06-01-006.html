JLL Negotiates Sale of 838,446 SF Industrial Facility in Katy, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 15:28:48 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has facilitated the sale of an 838,446-square-foot industrial facility in Katy, Texas. The property, developed as a build-to-suit for a building materials supplier, was acquired by Goldman Sachs from a partnership between Pinpoint Commercial and Senterra. This transaction represents the first phase of the larger Grand Central West development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Grand Central West

Address: 22206 Beckendorff Rd, Katy, TX*

Size: 838,446 SF

Year Built: 2024

Notable Features: Features 40-foot clear heights, 136 dock doors, 20 loading ramps, three drive-in doors, 185-foot truck court depths, and 31,065 square feet of office space.

Project Notes: Constructed as a build-to-suit for Builders FirstSource. This facility is Phase I of the Grand Central West development.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
