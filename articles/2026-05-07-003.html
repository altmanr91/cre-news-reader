Catalfumo Lands $401M to Finish Ritz-Carlton Condos in Palm Beach Gardens

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Wed, 06 May 2026 21:15:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Catalfumo Companies has secured a $401 million bridge loan from Northwind to complete its Ritz-Carlton Residences condominium project in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. The financing will refinance a previous $340 million construction loan and carry the project through its sellout phase. The development, which is already 35% presold, is expected to benefit from strong demand in South Florida.

Notable Features: Waterfront property with 1,040 feet of Intracoastal Waterway frontage, private 29-boat slip marina, 20,000 square feet of amenities including a waterfront restaurant, pool, spa, wellness facilities, fitness center, pickleball court, and clubhouse. Units are three- and four-bedroom floor-through residences.

Project Notes: The loan refinances a $340 million construction loan issued three years prior. Prices range from $4 million to over $10 million. 35% of units are presold.
