Spirit Airlines ghosted Dania Beach HQ, but potential buyers are waiting in the wings

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:31:07 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Following the failure of Spirit Airlines, its recently completed 180,000-square-foot headquarters in Dania Beach, FL, is now vacant and being considered for acquisition by Broward County. The county commission will evaluate purchasing the six-story building for a new governmental center, potentially saving significant costs compared to new construction. The property, part of the larger Dania Pointe development, is expected to attract strong interest from other corporate headquarters users, particularly in the aviation sector, or investors with large tenants, due to its prime location near Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport and the scarcity of large Class A office space in South Florida.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

There is a significant demand for new, large Class A blocks of office space in South Florida, which is generally a scarce commodity. Publicly funded financial incentives could be offered to a new occupant of the Spirit headquarters.
