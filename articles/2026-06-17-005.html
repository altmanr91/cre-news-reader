Delshah Capital Buys 227 and 456 Grand Street in Williamsburg for $85M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 21:19:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Delshah Capital has acquired two mixed-use residential properties in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, from Bronstein Properties for a combined $85 million. The transaction included 227 Grand Street and 456 Grand Street, with the latter benefiting from a 421-a tax abatement. The acquisition was financed with a $60.5 million loan from Prospect Ridge.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 227 Grand Street, Brooklyn, NY

Units: 93

Sale Price: $85.0M

Loan Amount: $60.5M

Notable Features: 456 Grand Street includes a 421-a tax abatement expiring in 2030.

Project Notes: 456 Grand Street was constructed in 2015 under New York City's PlaNYC Brownfield Cleanup Program. Bronstein acquired 227 Grand Street in a 2022 bankruptcy sale.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Delshah Capital — Michael Shah

SELLER: Bronstein Properties

LENDER: Prospect Ridge
