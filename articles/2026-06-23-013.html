NYC’s top deals: UES townhouse trades for $22M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Olmstead Properties and Vertex Properties have acquired the 16-story office building at 19 West 44th Street in Midtown from Savanna. The Class B property, built in 1917, spans 303,000 square feet and is approximately 80 percent leased. Savanna had previously invested $20 million in upgrades after purchasing the building in 2017.
