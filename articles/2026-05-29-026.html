Chicago’s top construction permits: Week ending May 27, 2026

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 28 May 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Construction

This article details the top construction permits issued in Chicago for the week ending May 27, 2026. The largest permit, valued at $21 million, was granted to the Art Institute of Chicago for interior alterations to the Rice Building, creating the new Grainger Center for Conservation & Science. Other significant permits include a $13.8 million interior renovation for an airline lounge at O'Hare International Airport and a $7 million conversion of a bank into a medical care facility in Bronzeville. The week's activity saw a slight decrease in total value compared to the previous week.
