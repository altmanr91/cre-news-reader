Sueba USA Completes 353-Unit Apartment Community in Katy, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 14:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Sueba USA has completed Boardwalk Square, a 353-unit apartment community in Katy, Texas, west of Houston. The midrise development offers a variety of floor plans with modern finishes and a comprehensive amenity package, including a CrossFit-inspired athletic club and a recovery room. Construction on the project commenced in spring 2024.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Boardwalk Square

Address: 125 Prairie Pkwy, Katy, TX*

Units: 353

Completion: June 2026

Notable Features: Midrise building with 20 open-concept floor plans, quartz countertops, stainless steel appliances, full-size washers and dryers, and walk-in closets. Amenities include a CrossFit-inspired athletic club, recovery room with dry sauna and cold plunge, pool with tanning ledges and poolside cabanas, pet spa, catering kitchen, and coffee bar.

Project Notes: Construction began in spring 2024.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
