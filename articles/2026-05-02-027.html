Spear Street swaps Loop headaches for Fulton Market

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 01 May 2026 16:47:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Spear Street Capital is under contract to acquire the 146,000-square-foot office building at 1201 West Lake Street in Chicago's Fulton Market for approximately $40 million, or $275 per square foot. The seller, McCaffery Interests, developed the seven-story building, which was completed in 2019 and is currently 85 percent leased. This acquisition marks a strategic pivot for Spear Street Capital, which has faced challenges with larger office properties in the Loop area, including a default on a $270 million loan at 500 West Monroe Street.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The price of $275 per square foot for 1201 West Lake Street is below pre-pandemic peaks but represents a significant premium over recent downtown office sales, such as 161 North Clark Street and 401 North Michigan Avenue, which traded for less than $200 per square foot earlier this year. This transaction marks the highest per-square-foot rate for a downtown office building since a stake in the Bank of America Tower sold in 2022.
