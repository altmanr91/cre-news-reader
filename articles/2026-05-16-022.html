Tavros Capital starts reimagining of beleaguered 250 Water

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 15 May 2026 18:13:08 +0000

SUMMARY

Tavros Capital is moving forward with its redevelopment of 250 Water Street in the Seaport District, securing a significant lease with Chelsea Piers Fitness for a 76,000-square-foot health and wellness facility. This lease marks a key step in the project's progress since Tavros and Atlas Capital acquired the site last year. The development is envisioned as a comprehensive neighborhood asset, incorporating residential, commercial, and outdoor spaces.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 250 Water Street, New York City, NY

Size: 76,000 SF

Notable Features: Five-story health and wellness facility with indoor and outdoor spaces.

Project Notes: The site was previously acquired by Tavros and Atlas Capital for $150 million. The overall development will include 600 apartments, with 25% designated as affordable, along with commercial and outdoor space. Atlantic Retail is marketing 35,000 square feet of ground-floor retail space.
