Alchemy Properties Plans Resi Conversion of Vacant Tribeca Garage

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Wed, 06 May 2026 21:37:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Alchemy Properties, in partnership with Daishin America and Takamatsu Construction Group USA, has acquired a vacant five-story garage at 60 N. Moore St. in Tribeca for $57 million. The partnership plans a gut renovation and conversion of the 1914-vintage property into an ultra-luxury condominium, including the addition of two Landmarks-approved floors. This transaction highlights strong demand for high-end residential housing and growing investor confidence in the Manhattan market.
