Onyx+East acquires 31 acres for development of housing community north of Cincinnati

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Onyx+East has acquired 31.9 acres in Liberty Township, north of Cincinnati, for the development of Parkmont, a 90-home single-family community. Construction is slated to begin this summer, with home sales launching in spring 2027. The community will feature a walkable layout, timeless architecture, and amenities such as pickleball courts and walking trails, appealing to various buyers seeking low-maintenance living in a connected suburban setting.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Property Name: Parkmont

Address: 4231 Parkmont Dr, Cincinnati, OH*

Units: 90

Site Area: 31.9 acres

Notable Features: Walkable community with pickleball courts, walking trails, a pavilion, and fire-pit gathering spaces, located minutes from Liberty Center.

Project Notes: Homes will offer 3+ bedrooms, optional first-floor primary suites, 10-foot ceilings, and 1,862-2,900 SF of living space, with optional basements and upgraded outdoor living areas.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Onyx+East
