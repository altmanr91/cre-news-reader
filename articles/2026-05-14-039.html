SF Decorator Showcase home in Pac Heights lists for first time since 1960s

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 13 May 2026 17:59:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A historic Queen Anne-style mansion in San Francisco's Pacific Heights, originally built around 1900, has been listed for sale at $25 million. The 9,300-square-foot property, which served as this year's San Francisco Decorator Showcase home, is being sold by the widow of the late preservation architect Herbert McLaughlin, who acquired it in 1969 and spent decades restoring its original features. The listing comes amidst a strong luxury housing market in Pacific Heights, fueled by demand from the booming artificial intelligence sector.
