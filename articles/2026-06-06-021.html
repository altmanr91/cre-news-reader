Live Local Act megaproject developer sued again – by someone he says he’s never met

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Spanish developer Pablo Castro is facing a third lawsuit concerning his planned HueHub project, a large Live Local Act development in Miami-Dade County. The Miami Gardens Chamber of Commerce alleges that Castro and his Digital Housing Platform entity misappropriated trade secrets, including strategies for securing government worker leases to collateralize financing. Castro denies any knowledge of the chamber or its representatives, calling the lawsuit false and defamatory. This litigation follows two previous lawsuits filed this year against Castro related to the HueHub project, one for unpaid architectural work and another from a former partner alleging she was frozen out of the development after contributing significant work.
