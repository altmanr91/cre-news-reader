Texas Icon Heather Nguyen: Curating global retail concepts into lasting U.S. destinations

REJournals — Mon, 18 May 2026 09:46:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article profiles Heather Nguyen, Managing Director of Asia Pacific Advisory Services and Executive Vice President at NewQuest, highlighting her unique approach to retail development. Nguyen specializes in identifying emerging retail concepts from Asia and curating them into successful, culturally rich destinations across U.S. markets. Her strategy focuses on creating platforms for Asian brands that resonate with mainstream audiences, bridging cultural nuances with market demands. Over her 30-year career, she has facilitated over $10 billion in transactions, emphasizing relationship-driven collaboration with entrepreneurs and international brands entering the U.S. market.
