Shirley Aninias School Signs 23K-SF Lease at 30 Wall Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 20 May 2026 21:33:04 +0000

SUMMARY

The Shirley Aninias School, a private special education program, has signed a 15-year lease for 23,000 square feet across two floors at 30 Wall Street in New York City's Financial District. This transaction represents the largest lease ever signed at the property and will serve as a relocation and expansion for the school. The space will feature an internal staircase and a dedicated ground-floor entrance, creating a self-contained environment tailored for the school's neurodiverse students and staff.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Rental Rate: $67.50/SF/yr

Completion: September 2026

Notable Features: The leased space includes an internal staircase connecting the second and third floors and a dedicated ground-floor entrance, providing a self-contained environment.

Project Notes: The Shirley Aninias School is relocating and expanding from its previous location at 291 Broadway in Tribeca.
