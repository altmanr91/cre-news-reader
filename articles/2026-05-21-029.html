Sightwell scraps East Dundee lumberyard redevelopment plan

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 21 May 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Sightwell has withdrawn its plans for a mixed-use redevelopment of the former lumberyard at Heritage Station in East Dundee, citing economic constraints. The proposed $18 million project, which included retail, lodging, and townhomes, faced challenges with the allocated $2.5 million in special tax funding primarily covering site preparation rather than construction. Despite this setback, the village of East Dundee remains committed to the overall development vision for the area, including the ongoing construction of a performing arts center and plans to seek new bids for the lumberyard site's phased development.
