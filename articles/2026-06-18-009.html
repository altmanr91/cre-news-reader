Telecommunications Firm Signs 42,000 SF Lease at Flex Building in Richardson, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 14:42:39 +0000

SUMMARY

A telecommunications hardware manufacturer has signed a 42,000-square-foot lease for a flex building in Richardson, Texas. The company is relocating from a nearby facility and plans to invest over $2 million in tenant improvements and new equipment for its new space, which will accommodate office, lab, manufacturing, and engineering functions.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: flex

Address: 2920 Telecom Parkway, Richardson, TX

Size: 42,000 SF

Project Notes: Tenant plans to invest over $2 million in tenant improvements and new furniture, fixtures, and equipment.
