King Ranch scion sells historic Houston home

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

John Steen III and Mary Steen sold their historic 1931 home at 1419 Kirby Drive in Houston to attorney Ryan Pigg. The five-bedroom, seven-bathroom residence spans 6,300 square feet on a half-acre lot in the River Oaks neighborhood. The property, which was once considered for city landmark status, went under contract in just 14 days after being listed for $6.5 million, making it one of Houston's top residential sales in May.
