“Perception becomes reality”: Miami-Dade office rents enter new era at over $200 psf

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 18 May 2026 14:30:02 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Miami-Dade County's prime office market is experiencing a significant surge in rents, with some deals now surpassing $200 per square foot, a substantial increase from the $100 per square foot records set during the pandemic. This escalation is largely driven by the continued influx of high-net-worth individuals and their associated firms, particularly from out-of-state, seeking bespoke office spaces near their residences. While this trend primarily impacts boutique, high-end buildings and does not reflect the broader market's occupancy or lease rates, it signifies a new era for luxury office properties in the region, with developers responding by offering turnkey spaces and generous tenant allowances.
