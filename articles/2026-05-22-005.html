Marcus & Millichap Expands to 41K SF at AmTrustRE’s 260 Madison Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 21 May 2026 16:16:26 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has expanded its footprint at 260 Madison Avenue in Midtown Manhattan, adding 5,000 square feet to its existing 36,000-square-foot office space, bringing its total to 41,000 square feet. The real estate advisory firm also extended its lease at the property, which is owned by AmTrustRE. The landlord noted its commitment to investing in and repositioning New York City office assets, citing the retention of a high-quality tenant like Marcus & Millichap as a testament to its strategy.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 260 Madison Avenue

Address: 260 Madison Avenue, New York City, NY

Size: 41,000 SF

Notable Features: 22-story office building near Grand Central Terminal.

Project Notes: AmTrustRE acquired the building in October 2025 for $217 million. Marcus & Millichap originally signed a 10-year lease in 2015.
