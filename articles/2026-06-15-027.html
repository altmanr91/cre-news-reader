Ken Mattson is going to trial

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Sun, 14 Jun 2026 23:16:52 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Sonoma developer and investor Ken Mattson, who was charged with orchestrating a $100 million Ponzi scheme, has decided to proceed with a jury trial after initially agreeing to a guilty plea. Mattson faces nine felony counts, including money laundering, obstruction of justice, and wire fraud, each carrying a maximum 20-year prison sentence. The decision to reverse his plea and maintain innocence comes after prosecutors presented evidence against him.
