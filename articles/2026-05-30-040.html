Covina Apartments Trade on In-Place Cash Flow, Rental Upside

SALE

Connect CRE — Fri, 29 May 2026 21:59:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Vincent Village, a 40-unit apartment property in Covina, CA, has been sold for $8.2 million. The transaction was driven by the property's strong in-place cash flow, significant rental upside potential, and the possibility of adding accessory dwelling units, which generated substantial buyer interest despite local rent control limitations. The sale was brokered by Marcus & Millichap.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Vincent Village

Address: 4810 N. Vincent Ave., Covina, CA

Units: 40

Sale Price: $8.2M

$/Unit: $205,000/unit (calculated)

Year Built: 1984

Notable Features: Features 38 two-bedroom, one-bathroom units and two non-conforming studio units.

Project Notes: Located in unincorporated Los Angeles County with annual rent increases limited to 60% of the Consumer Price Index. The property offers approximately 29% rental upside and potential to construct multiple accessory dwelling units.
