Cushman & Wakefield brokers $12.25 million sale of mixed-use property in Carmel

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 16:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Cushman & Wakefield brokered the $12.25 million sale of Monon & Main, a four-story mixed-use office and retail property in Carmel, Indiana. The transaction, priced at $354 per square foot, established a new record for office sales in the state. The property was fully leased at the time of sale, reflecting strong demand for Class-A+ assets in the Carmel Arts & Design District.
