Lee & Associate closes lease that brings Elgin industrial building to full occupancy

LEASE

REJournals — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 16:12:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Lee & Associates has successfully brokered a 64,624-square-foot industrial lease at 2801 Alft Lane in Elgin, Illinois. This transaction brings the property to full occupancy, underscoring strong demand for industrial space in the market. Premier Handling Solutions, a manufacturer of supply-chain equipment, is the new tenant.
