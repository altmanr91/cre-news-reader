DTLA’s Bank of America Plaza Trades for $210M in Receivership Sale

SALE

Connect CRE — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 21:05:03 +0000

SUMMARY

The Bank of America Plaza, a 1.4-million-square-foot office tower in Downtown Los Angeles, has been sold for $210 million in a receivership sale. Capital Group, a long-term anchor tenant in the building, acquired the property. This transaction marks the largest office sale in Los Angeles in 2026 and the largest in Los Angeles County since 2023. Capital Group plans to expand its occupancy within the building, consolidating its local workforce and making significant investments to enhance the workplace experience.
