Newmark Negotiates $64M Sale of Apartment Community in Huntsville

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 06 May 2026 14:23:39 +0000

SUMMARY

Newmark has brokered the $64 million sale of The Liam at Hays Farm, a 329-unit apartment community in Huntsville, AL. Greystone acquired the property from a joint venture between Bomasada Group and an affiliate of The Wolff Co. This transaction represents the largest multifamily deal in the Huntsville market since 2023.
