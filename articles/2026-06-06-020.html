Paul Massey sells B6 brokerage to Boston-based Horvath & Tremblay

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 20:34:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Paul Massey has sold his brokerage, B6 Real Estate Advisors, to Boston-based Horvath & Tremblay. This acquisition provides a new direction for B6, which had faced financial challenges and broker departures. Horvath & Tremblay, a national brokerage with 17 offices, gains a stronger presence in the New York market and benefits from Massey's extensive industry reputation and experience.
