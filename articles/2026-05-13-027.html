TikTok’s “Trump whisperer” drops $17M on house in Mar-a-Lago Security Zone

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 12 May 2026 13:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

Tony Sayegh, a former Trump administration official, and his wife, Maria Sayegh, purchased a 6,800-square-foot house in the Mar-a-Lago Security Zone in Palm Beach for $17.1 million. The sellers were hedge funder Peter Gerhard and his wife, Kristen Gerhard. The property, built in 2017, was previously acquired by the Gerhards in 2013 for $2.3 million.
