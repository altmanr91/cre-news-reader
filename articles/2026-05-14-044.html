Newly Built Pollo Campero Sells at Four-Year-Low Cap Rate

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 13 May 2026 22:08:50 +0000

SUMMARY

A newly constructed Pollo Campero ground lease in Lake Elsinore, CA, was sold for $2.925 million, achieving the lowest cap rate for the brand nationwide in nearly four years. The transaction involved a private investor acquiring the property from developer Evergreen Devco, with brokers noting strong buyer interest and an all-cash 1031 exchange. The sale occurred before the anchor tenant, Stater Bros., began construction, highlighting the strength of the location and the tenant's appeal.
