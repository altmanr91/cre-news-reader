No More Mesh Cages: High-Security On-Site Storage as a Multifamily Amenity

Connect CRE — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 05:01:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article argues that high-security, on-site storage has become a necessity rather than a luxury amenity in multifamily properties, driven by smaller unit sizes, longer rental tenures, and residents accumulating more belongings. The author contends that traditional mesh cages are inadequate and that fully enclosed, secure storage units improve the resident experience, reduce turnover, and generate recurring revenue for owners. Planning for such amenities should occur during the design phase for new developments or by repurposing underutilized spaces in existing properties.

DATA POINTS

Multifamily unit size reduction (past 10 years vs. 2005-2014): approximately 30 square feet smaller

Multifamily unit size reduction (past 10 years vs. early 2000s): 75 square feet smaller

Potential rental income from on-site storage: up to $5 per square foot
