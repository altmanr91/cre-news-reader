Collies adds managing director of real estate services in Kansas City office

REJournals — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Promotion

Colliers has appointed Jeannette Vahle as managing director of Real Estate Management Services in its Kansas City office. Vahle brings over 30 years of experience in commercial real estate, including property management, asset services, and operations leadership. In her new role, she will be responsible for overseeing the firm's REMS platform, focusing on property operations, client relations, team leadership, and strategic growth initiatives within the Kansas City market.
