Craig International wins bid for former Globe Life campus, eyes $200M-plus redevelopment

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Craig International has won the bid to redevelop the former Globe Life Corporate Headquarters in McKinney, Texas. The 58-acre site, which includes over 300,000 square feet of existing office space, will be rebranded as "Rowlett Station" and transformed into a mixed-use development. The initial redevelopment plans are projected to exceed $200 million, with a focus on repurposing existing structures and integrating new master-planned elements, hike-and-bike trails, and connectivity to adjacent retail.
