Karney Snags $34M Industrial Refi in Southern California

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 19:30:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Karney Properties, a prominent industrial operator, has secured $33.5 million in fixed-rate refinancing from Nationwide for a 178,454-square-foot Class A industrial facility in Cerritos, CA. The property, located at 15959 Piuma Avenue, is a single-tenant building adjacent to Interstate 605, featuring 32-foot clear heights and excess trailer storage. The financing was arranged by JLL Capital Markets.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Greater Los Angeles industrial market saw 13 move-ins versus five move-outs for spaces 100,000 square feet or more in Q1. The broader market experienced negative 765,919 square feet of year-to-date net absorption, with total vacancy at 7.3 percent and average asking rent at $1.38 per square foot per month.
