Jury acquits George Pino in boat crash death of 17-year-old

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 23:50:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

A Miami-Dade County jury acquitted commercial real estate broker George Pino of manslaughter and boat vessel homicide charges in the 2022 boat crash that resulted in the death of 17-year-old Luciana Fernandez. The defense argued that the incident, while tragic, amounted to ordinary negligence rather than the "gross and flagrant" conduct required for a criminal conviction, emphasizing that not every mistake constitutes a crime. Prosecutors contended Pino's "complete lack" of responsibility as captain, including speeding and ignoring navigational rules, directly led to the fatality.
