Crowns sell Water Tower Place jewel for nearly $6M

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 20:30:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Lester and Renee Crown sold their 6,200-square-foot condominium on the 37th floor of Water Tower Place in Chicago for $5.5 million. The five-bedroom unit, which the Crowns had assembled from two separate units purchased in 2015 and 2016, was recently renovated by Kara Mann Interiors. The buyer of the luxury residence was not identified in public records.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Property Name: Water Tower Place

Address: 835 North Michigan Avenue, Chicago, IL

Size: 6,200 SF

Sale Price: $5.5M

$/SF: $887/SF (calculated)

Notable Features: Recently renovated by Kara Mann Interiors, features a walk-in coat closet, multiple wet bars, a nursery, and a breakfast room overlooking Lake Michigan.

Project Notes: The unit was purchased in chunks by the Crowns in 2015 and 2016 for $2.7 million and $3.3 million respectively, then combined. The property had a $60,000 property tax bill in 2024 and $56,500 in annual HOA dues.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Crown Family — Lester Crown; Renee Crown

SELLER BROKER: Malone Residential — Kathleen Malone, Listing Agent

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Water Tower Place is undergoing a $170 million revamp by MetLife, with upper floors being converted into medical office space. The Magnificent Mile commercial district is experiencing renewed excitement with dropping retail vacancy and high-profile tenants like American Eagle, Nike, and Levi's looking to shuffle around the area.
