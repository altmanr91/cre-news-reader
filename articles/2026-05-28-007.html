RealSource Arranges $5.4M Sale of Single-Tenant Retail Building in Kemah, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 27 May 2026 15:17:19 +0000

SUMMARY

RealSource Group has facilitated the $5.4 million sale of a single-tenant retail building in Kemah, Texas, which is net leased to Mister Car Wash. The property, constructed in 2021, features a fully guaranteed corporate lease. The transaction involved a local private investor as the seller and an undisclosed buyer.
