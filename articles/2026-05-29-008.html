Portman to Bring 30,000 SF Food Hall to Savona Mill Redevelopment in Charlotte

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 28 May 2026 14:28:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Portman is developing Savona Food Works, a 30,000-square-foot food hall, as the anchor for its Savona Mill adaptive reuse project in Charlotte's West End district. The food hall, led by veteran operator Robert Montwaid, will feature 25 food stalls, a full-service restaurant, coffee shop, flower market, and a rooftop bar. This dining installation is part of a larger 27-acre mixed-use development that includes creative office space and multifamily units, with additional residential phases planned.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Savona Food Works

Address: 4321 Colwick Rd, Charlotte, NC*

Size: 30,000 SF

Completion: 2027

Site Area: 27 acres

Notable Features: Located in the historic "Weave building" dating back to the 1900s; adjacent to the Stewart Creek Greenway, connecting to LYNX Light Rail and Blue Line Rail Trail.

Project Notes: Part of the larger Savona Mill adaptive reuse project, which includes 200,000 square feet of creative office space and 319 apartments, with more multifamily units planned for a future phase.
