PolicyPro: COPA momentum builds, Mamdani picks new BSA chair

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 23:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

New York City Council member Carl Wilson has publicly endorsed the Community Opportunity to Purchase Act (COPA), a bill that would grant city-designated nonprofits the first right to purchase distressed multifamily buildings. This endorsement, while not decisive for the bill's passage, aligns Wilson with the mayor's housing agenda and the Council's progressive bloc. Separately, Mayor Zohran Mamdani nominated John Mangin, a veteran housing policy expert and key architect of significant housing reforms, to lead the Board of Standards and Appeals. Mangin's appointment is expected to enhance the BSA's role in fast-tracking affordable housing developments by leveraging new charter changes that allow certain projects to bypass traditional City Council review.
