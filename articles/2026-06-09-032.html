Austin’s luxury market is regaining speed

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 21:23:17 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Austin's luxury housing market is experiencing a significant turnaround, moving from being one of the slowest in the country last year to one of the swiftest this spring. Luxury homes in Austin are now going under contract in a median of 57 days, 16 days faster than the previous year, indicating a strong resurgence in buyer interest. This shift is attributed to more realistic seller expectations and a limited supply of move-in ready luxury homes, leading to an increase in cash transactions and quicker closings.

DATA POINTS

Median days on market for Austin luxury homes (Spring): 57 days

Year-over-year decrease in median days on market (Austin): 16 days faster

Median days on market for Austin luxury homes (October last year): 104 days
