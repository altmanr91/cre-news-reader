Irene Pritzker buys Crown family’s Water Tower condo for nearly $6M

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 17:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Irene Pritzker, a member of the prominent Chicago Pritzker family, has acquired a luxury condominium at the Water Tower Residences in Streeterville for $5.5 million. The seller was billionaire philanthropists Lester and Renee Crown, who had purchased the property in two separate transactions in 2015 for a combined $6.02 million. The 6,200-square-foot condo, located on the 37th floor, recently underwent a renovation and offers views of Lake Michigan.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: Water Tower Residences

Address: 835 North Michigan Avenue, Chicago, IL

Size: 6,200 SF

Sale Price: $5.5M

$/SF: $887/SF (calculated)

Notable Features: Recently renovated by Kara Mann Interiors, with a walk-in closet, a bedroom converted into a nursery, and a breakfast room with a view of Lake Michigan.

Project Notes: The Crowns acquired the property in 2015 in two pieces for $6.02 million. The building is set to receive a $170 million revamp from MetLife, with portions of upper floors converted to medical office space.
