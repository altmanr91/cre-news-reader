MMCC Arranges $54M HUD-Insured Loan for Refinancing of Metro Houston Apartment Community

REFINANCE

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 15:14:32 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap Capital Corp. (MMCC) has arranged a $54 million HUD-insured loan for the refinancing of Lakeview at Westpark, a 298-unit apartment community in Richmond, Texas. The loan, secured through KeyBank on behalf of Rockstar Capital, features a fixed interest rate of 5.3 percent and a 35-year amortization schedule. This refinancing provides long-term, stable financing for the recently built multifamily asset.
