Bellevue self-storage facility trades for $51M amid job growth

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Seattle — Tue, 12 May 2026 23:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Premier Storage Investors has acquired the West Coast Self-Storage facility in Bellevue, WA, for nearly $51 million from Commune Capital. The 140,000-square-foot property, built in 2016, features 1,000 storage units on a 1.2-acre site. The transaction reflects the strong demand for self-storage properties in markets experiencing significant job and population growth, particularly from the tech sector.

The self-storage sector is projected to continue growing nationwide due to population growth and demand for storage units outpacing limited supply.
