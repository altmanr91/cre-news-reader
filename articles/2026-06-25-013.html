Marcus & Millichap brokers sale of retail strip in Schiller Park

SALE

REJournals — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 16:13:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A six-suite retail strip property in Schiller Park, Illinois, was sold for $3.5 million to a local private investor completing a 1031 exchange. The 23,000-square-foot center, formerly a warehouse, was acquired for its long-term potential despite short remaining lease terms, benefiting from its conversion to multi-tenant retail and proximity to Rosemont's convention and corporate activity.
