Law Firm Fenwick & West Signs Lease Extension, Expansion

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 21:19:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Law firm Fenwick & West, LLP has expanded its office footprint at 902 Broadway in New York City, signing an 11-year lease extension and expansion. The firm added 18,200 square feet, bringing its total occupancy to 72,800 square feet across four floors. The expansion reflects the firm's continued growth and need for additional space in the city, with ownership noting strong interest from professional organizations in premier locations with efficient floorplates.
