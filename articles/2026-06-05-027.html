Looksmaxxing influencer Clavicular rents downtown Miami penthouse

LEASE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Controversial influencer Braden Peters, known as Clavicular, has leased a penthouse unit at Paramount Miami Worldcenter in downtown Miami for a one-year term. The 6,500-square-foot, five-bedroom, seven-bathroom condo is currently on the market for sale with an asking price of $9.5 million. The building's developer, Dan Kodsi, owns the specific unit through an LLC.
