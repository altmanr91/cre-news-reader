Distressed Hawthorne Race Course gets green light to subpoena Illinois officials

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 28 May 2026 21:01:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Hawthorne Race Course, currently undergoing bankruptcy sale proceedings, has received federal court approval to subpoena Illinois state officials. The track's owners allege that the Illinois Department of Agriculture and its director improperly diverted $317,000 in state funds from Hawthorne while increasing funding to a direct competitor, Fairmount Park Casino & Racing. This legal action is part of the ongoing Chapter 11 bankruptcy case filed in February, which has seen the property listed for sale, including a reported $107 million offer last year. The track's financial distress has also been exacerbated by $6.7 million in contractor liens and a suspended harness racing license due to payroll violations.
