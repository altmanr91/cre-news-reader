Monty Bennett’s Ashford leads bid for bankrupt River Walk hotel amid Braemar divorce

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 17:10:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Ashford Hospitality is poised to acquire the bankrupt 145 Navarro Street property in San Antonio for $32 million, with GrayStreet Partners serving as a backup buyer at $30 million. The former office building was undergoing a conversion into a luxury Marriott Autograph Collection hotel, but the project faced significant delays and cost overruns due to extensive flood and mold damage, leading the owner, Blueprint Hospitality, to file for Chapter 11 bankruptcy. A new owner is expected to be finalized by mid-July.

Project Notes: The property was previously acquired from CPS Energy and was undergoing conversion into a Marriott Autograph Collection hotel. The project faced significant water damage and mold issues, leading to bankruptcy. GrayStreet affiliate ERC Acquisitions IV LLC was a lender for the project.
