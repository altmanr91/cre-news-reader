Bixby, Franklin team up again to nab Accesso’s distressed Loop office loan

ACQUISITION

Chicago Real Estate News - The Real Deal — Wed, 29 Apr 2026 21:30:50 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Bixby Bridge Capital and Franklin Partners acquired a distressed loan on the 23-story office building at 200 West Monroe Street in Chicago's Loop for $16 million. This transaction marks the third distressed office deal in two years for the partnership, which specializes in acquiring troubled assets at a significant discount to their original loan values. The previous owner, Accesso Partners, had taken out a $75 million loan on the 536,000-square-foot property in 2014, which subsequently faced foreclosure due to reduced occupancy and tenant consolidations.
