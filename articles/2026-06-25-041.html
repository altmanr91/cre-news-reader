Prado, SKS put more resi plans in play in at former UCSF Laurel Heights campus

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Prado Group and SKS Partners are moving forward with the second phase of their Presidio Heights master-planned development at the former UC San Francisco Laurel Heights campus. This phase involves the construction of 220 apartments across three buildings, with an estimated cost of $196 million. The overall 10-acre project is slated to include 744 apartments, retail space, a childcare center, and extensive open space.

SPONSOR PIPELINE

3700 California Street: Redevelopment of the 5-acre former California Pacific Medical Center in Presidio Heights into 530 residential units.
