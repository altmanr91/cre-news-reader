Marcus & Millichap closes $8.15 million shopping center sale in Stevens Point

SALE

REJournals — Fri, 29 May 2026 14:52:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Northpoint Shopping Center, an 11-suite retail strip in Stevens Point, Wisconsin, for $8.15 million. The fully occupied property, built in 1968, is strategically located near the University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point, offering a strong tenant mix.
