Scott Rechler made it to the other side of the downturn. At what cost?

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 14:49:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article provides a retrospective analysis of RXR's performance and strategic adjustments in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic and rising interest rates, which significantly impacted the New York City office market. CEO Scott Rechler's "Project Kodak" initiative aimed to differentiate viable office assets from obsolete ones, but the firm has faced a complex reality, including substantial losses on several property sales and a mortgage default at Worldwide Plaza. Despite these challenges, RXR has adapted by diversifying its funding sources, partnering with opportunistic investment firms, and pursuing new development projects, such as a large-scale project at 175 Park Avenue, demonstrating a rebound from the downturn.
