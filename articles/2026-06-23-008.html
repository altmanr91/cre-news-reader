Cawley Commercial Real Estate closes sale of maintenance facility in Romeoville

SALE

REJournals — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 09:35:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Cawley Commercial Real Estate has completed the direct sale of a newly constructed Class-A maintenance facility in Romeoville, Illinois. The transaction involved the disposition of a 27,500-square-foot industrial property on a four-acre parcel, which was acquired by MW Express for its expansion in the Chicago market. A Cawley CRE broker represented both the buyer and the seller, having also brokered the original land sale and overseen the property's development.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 1385 Joliet Road, Romeoville, IL

Size: 27,500 SF

Completion: Q4 2025

Site Area: 4 acres

Notable Features: The facility includes 19,950 square feet of warehouse space and 7,550 square feet of office space, featuring 10 drive-in bays, heavy power, and triple catch basin drainage, with immediate access to I-55 and I-355.

Project Notes: The 4-acre land parcel was previously acquired by KAB Properties through a brokerage facilitated by Cawley CRE.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: MW Express

SELLER: KAB Properties

SELLER BROKER: Cawley Commercial Real Estate — Matt Garland; Andrew Maletich, SIOR; Houston Brooke

SELLER BROKER: Brick-by-Brick Realty — Jandra Zitkevicius
