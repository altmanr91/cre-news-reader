NYC’s top deals: Omnia buys Gramercy Park office for $14M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Omnia Properties has acquired a six-story, 19,600-square-foot office building located at 108 East 16th Street in Gramercy Park, New York City, for $13.8 million. The seller, Rashid Realty, had owned the property since the 1970s. This transaction represents the top commercial real estate deal recorded in New York City during the specified 24-hour period.
