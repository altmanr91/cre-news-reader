Interim Chicago Housing Authority head Matthew Brewer considering mayoral run

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 22 May 2026 16:01:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

Matthew Brewer, the interim operating chairman of the Chicago Housing Authority (CHA), is reportedly considering a mayoral run in Chicago. Brewer has been engaging with prominent business figures, including the board of One Future Illinois, a non-profit that has previously clashed with incumbent Mayor Brandon Johnson over corporate and real estate transfer taxes. This potential political move comes amidst an ongoing power struggle between Brewer and Mayor Johnson regarding the leadership of the CHA, an agency with a $1.4 billion operating budget overseeing over 65,000 households.
