Indianapolis-Based Ambrose Executes 190K Square Feet of Industrial Leases in Several States

LEASE

Connect CRE — Fri, 08 May 2026 20:15:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Ambrose has executed three industrial leases totaling over 190,000 square feet across facilities in Cincinnati, Denver, and Orlando. The transactions include a catering logistics provider taking space at Ascent Commerce Center in Denver, a manufacturing firm leasing at Cincinnati Logistics Park – Airport West, and a technology subsidiary securing a full building at Orlando Logistics Park at LeeVista. These leases underscore continued demand for modern industrial facilities in key logistics markets.
