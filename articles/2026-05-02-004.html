Events Company Skylight Expands By 11K SF at Two Trees’ Refinery at Domino

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:24:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Two Trees Management has achieved 90 percent occupancy at The Refinery at Domino in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, following two new lease deals. Event space operator Skylight expanded its footprint by 11,000 square feet, bringing its total presence to 13,600 square feet across the 14th and 15th floors. Additionally, job search platform Bandana signed a new 10,380-square-foot lease on the 14th floor, relocating from nearby offices. The building's historical significance and distinctive architecture were cited as key factors in attracting tenants.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: The Refinery at Domino

Address: 300 Kent Avenue, Brooklyn, NY

Size: 11,000 SF

Current Occupancy: 90.0%

Completion: 2023

Notable Features: Redeveloped 19th-century sugar refinery into a Class A office tower along Brooklyn’s waterfront.

Project Notes: Skylight's initial lease was 2,600 square feet in April 2024, now expanded to 13,600 square feet. Asking rents at the property ranged between $58 and $80 per square foot in 2024.
