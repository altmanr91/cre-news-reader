The next frontier in data center real estate? Space

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:55:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Elon Musk's SpaceX and Google are reportedly in discussions to launch data centers into orbit, a novel and unproven concept. This initiative, which Google is also exploring with other rocket companies, aims to address growing environmental and logistical challenges faced by terrestrial data centers, such as water usage, energy consumption, and local opposition. SpaceX has already filed an application to launch up to 1 million satellites for this purpose, with AI firm Anthropic expressing interest in collaboration. While the feasibility and geopolitical implications of orbital data centers remain significant hurdles, proponents believe this could become a more common development method in the future.
