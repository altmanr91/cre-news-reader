Continental Battery Systems to Open 25,000 SF Headquarters in Irving

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:57:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Continental Battery Systems, a supplier of batteries for various sectors, has signed a 10-year lease for a new 25,793-square-foot headquarters in Irving, TX. This move is expected to create approximately 95 new jobs in the local economy. The company plans to occupy the space in the coming weeks.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 750 N. John Carpenter Freeway, Irving, TX

Size: 25,793 SF
