Downtown Dallas flagship Neiman Marcus to shutter

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 20:52:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Retail Closure

Saks Global has announced the permanent closure of the flagship Neiman Marcus department store in Downtown Dallas, effective at the end of September. This decision follows months of uncertainty and a previous reprieve that kept the store open through the holiday season. The closure marks a significant loss for the downtown area, which is also contending with the Dallas Mavericks' decision to build a new stadium outside of the city center.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Neiman Marcus NorthPark location generates more than 10 times the sales of the downtown store. Saks Global has been closing or selling Neiman Marcus stores nationwide, including locations in Plano, San Francisco, and Beverly Hills.
