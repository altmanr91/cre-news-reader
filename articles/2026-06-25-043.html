C&C Development Opens Affordable Housing in Irvine

DEVELOPMENT

Connect CRE — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 21:38:20 +0000

SUMMARY

C&C Development, in partnership with Riverside Charitable Corporation, has opened Cartwright Family Apartments, a 60-unit affordable housing community in Irvine, CA. The development is designed for households earning between 30% and 80% of the Orange County area median income, with a specific allocation for veterans. This project represents C&C Development's second affordable community in Irvine and is fully occupied at its opening, supporting the city's goals for housing diversity and inclusion.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Cartwright Family Apartments

Address: 17861 Cartwright Rd, Irvine, CA

Units: 60

Current Occupancy: 100.0%

Site Area: 1.6 acres

Notable Features: A portion of units are set aside for veterans, and there is a leasing preference for current Irvine residents or those employed in the city.

Project Notes: This is C&C Development's second affordable community in Irvine.
