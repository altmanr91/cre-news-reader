Partners Real Estate Brokers Sale of 114,525 SF Office Building in Northeast Dallas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 26 May 2026 14:28:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Partners Real Estate has brokered the sale of a 114,525-square-foot office building in northeast Dallas. The undisclosed buyer plans to implement capital improvements to the property. The seller and sales price were not disclosed.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 6500 Greenville Ave, Dallas, TX

Size: 114,525 SF

Year Built: 1981

Notable Features: Seven-story building renovated in 2000.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER BROKER: Partners Real Estate — Justin Utay; Jeremy Brubaker
