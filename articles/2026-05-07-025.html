Cobble Hill’s most expensive listing finds a buyer after a $5M price cut

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 06 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A townhouse at 205 Clinton Street in Cobble Hill, Brooklyn, has found a buyer after its asking price was reduced by $5 million from its initial $22 million. The property, which underwent over two years of renovations, was listed at $17 million. If it closes near this price, it is expected to be among the most expensive single-family home sales in the borough this year, potentially surpassing a $16.3 million penthouse sale in Dumbo.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Address: 205 Clinton Street, Brooklyn, NY

Notable Features: 25-foot-wide home with seven bedrooms, seven bathrooms, an elevator, finished cellar, outdoor kitchen, and a top-floor 'clubhouse' with a terrace. Renovations maintained original features while adding modern amenities.

Project Notes: Property was previously acquired for $3.9 million in 2020. The asking price was initially $22 million in October, cut to $18.5 million in November, and then to $17 million in April.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: Vega Management — Brandon Hornbeck

SELLER BROKER: Compass — Lindsay Barton Barrett

ARCHITECT: Mike Ingui Architect — Mike Ingui
