Hassan Whiteside scores buyer for waterfront Miami Beach home

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 15:02:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Former Miami Heat player Hassan Whiteside has found a buyer for his waterfront Miami Beach home, which was listed for $14.8 million. This pending deal was the second-priciest among 16 luxury home contracts signed in Miami-Dade County between June 7 and June 14, according to a weekly report by Douglas Elliman's Eklund-Gomes team. The property, built in 2014, spans over 5,400 square feet on a 0.4-acre lot.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Between June 7 and June 14, 16 luxury home contracts were signed in Miami-Dade County for properties asking $4 million and up, totaling $136.8 million in asking dollar volume. Single-family homes averaged an asking price of $7.9 million and spent 260 days on the market. Condos averaged an asking price of $11.5 million, spent 71 days on the market, and averaged $3,021 per square foot.
