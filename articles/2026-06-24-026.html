Blue Road, Ilia cut hotel rooms, win resi upsize for North Miami Beach project

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 19:35:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Blue Road and Ilia Development Group received approval from North Miami Beach officials to increase the residential unit count for their condo tower, The William, from 344 to 374 units, while eliminating 30 planned hotel rooms. The 25-story project, located at 2040 Northeast 163rd Street, will also include 4,500 square feet of retail and is slated to begin construction by early next year. The developers have already presold 23 percent of the units, indicating strong market interest in the area's evolving landscape.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: The William

Address: 2040 Northeast 163rd Street, North Miami Beach, FL

Size: 650,384 SF

Units: 374

Completion: early 2027

Original Plan: 23-story building with 334 residential units, 30 hotel rooms, 7,300 square feet of retail and 410 parking spaces

Site Area: 1.1 acres

Notable Features: Features a pickleball court on the 25th-floor roof deck, a ninth-floor amenity deck with a pool, club room, office areas, and a gym.

Project Notes: The 1.1-acre property was previously acquired for $7.5 million in November 2022. The development replaces a two-story office building completed in 1968. Presales prices for condo units ranged between $380,000 and $1 million at launch in September 2026.
