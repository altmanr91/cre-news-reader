Disney Investment Group Negotiates $82.2M Sale of Retail Power Center in Norman, Oklahoma

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 19 May 2026 14:33:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Disney Investment Group has negotiated the $82.2 million sale of University Town Center, a 416,766-square-foot retail power center in Norman, Oklahoma. The property, built in 2009 and 99% leased at the time of sale, was acquired by a partnership between Mazaheri Properties and Champion Hotels from an affiliate of Rainier Cos. Disney Investment Group also brokered the previous sale of the property in 2019.
