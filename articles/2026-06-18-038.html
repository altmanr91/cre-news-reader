Pearson Ranch flattens with Tri Pointe development agreement

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 17:23:24 +0000

SUMMARY

Tri Pointe Homes has secured approval from Williamson County to develop 95 acres of the 156-acre Pearson Ranch, a significant shift from the project's initial vision as a dense mixed-use community. The homebuilder's plans for residential neighborhoods will largely replace the previously proposed commercial components, including office space, hotels, and retail. While the original developer, Inspire Development, still owns the land, Tri Pointe is set to take control of nearly two-thirds of the site for its residential build-out. This development marks a pivot towards more horizontal residential growth in a project initially envisioned with a $2 billion mixed-use scope.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Property Name: Pearson Ranch

Address: 15321 Fernhill Dr, Austin, TX*

Site Area: 95 acres

Notable Features: Located approximately 20 miles north of Downtown Austin, with nearby employers including Dell and Apple.

Project Notes: The 156-acre site was initially proposed in January 2022 as a $2 billion mixed-use development by Inspire Development, including a 48-acre office campus, two hotels, 200,000 SF of retail, and 2,500 multifamily units. The Eden, a 306-unit multifamily community, began construction in 2022 as part of the original plan.
