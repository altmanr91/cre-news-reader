Miami-Area Warehouse Trades for $56M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 20:50:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Terreno Realty has acquired a 98,000-square-foot industrial distribution building in Hialeah Gardens, FL, for $56.3 million from Clarion Partners. The property, completed in 2022, is fully leased to an e-commerce firm and features high ceilings and extensive loading capabilities. This acquisition expands Terreno Realty's significant industrial portfolio in the Miami market.
