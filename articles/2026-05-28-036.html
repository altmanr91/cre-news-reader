End of The Line? The Line Austin faces foreclosure after lenders took back DC and LA hotels

FORECLOSURE

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 27 May 2026 13:58:23 +0000

SUMMARY

The Line Austin hotel is facing foreclosure proceedings, with a June auction scheduled due to an alleged default on a $172 million loan originated by JP Morgan in 2023. This follows similar foreclosure actions at other Line hotel properties in Washington, D.C. and Los Angeles, where lenders took control of the assets. The Line Austin, developed by Sydell Group and opened in 2018, is a 428-key boutique hotel overlooking Congress Bridge and Lady Bird Lake.
