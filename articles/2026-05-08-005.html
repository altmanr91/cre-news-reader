S3 Capital Closes Fund Focused on Multifamily Lending at $1.3B

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Thu, 07 May 2026 19:50:41 +0000

SUMMARY

S3 Capital has announced the final close of its S3 LB RE Credit Fund III, a closed-end fund that secured $1.3 billion in total investable capital, exceeding its initial target. The fund is dedicated to first-lien construction lending, primarily targeting multifamily residential developments in markets facing housing shortages. This capital is expected to support approximately $4.3 billion in loan originations, addressing the current imbalance between housing demand and available construction financing, particularly as regional banks have scaled back their lending activities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Loan Amount: $4.30B

Project Notes: Fund closed with $850 million of discretionary fund commitments and $465 million of co-investment commitments, exceeding its initial $650 million target. Since its initial close in November 2024, the fund has originated over $2.3 billion of whole loans.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

LENDER: S3 Capital — Robert Schwartz, Co-founder and Principal; Michelle Fang, Head of Marketing and Investor Relations
