Johnson Development lines up first phase of $5B Denton master-planned community

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 17:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Johnson Development Services is set to break ground on the initial phase of Cole Ranch, a 3,169-acre master-planned community near Denton, Texas. This $5 billion development will introduce 4,365 single-family homes, a 55-acre business park, 156 acres of commercial space, and 200 acres for new schools. The first phase will focus on infrastructure and model homes, with home sales projected to commence in late 2027, addressing Denton's significant population growth.

Project Notes: The site has been owned by the Cole family since the 1930s. Planning for the project spanned over a decade, with initial public hearings in the mid-2010s and a municipal management district approved in 2019.
