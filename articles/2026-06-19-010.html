Nuveen Provides $38.6M in C-PACE Financing for Seniors Housing Development in Central Texas

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 15:22:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Nuveen Green Capital has provided $38.6 million in C-PACE financing for The Statesman at Horseshoe Bay, a new seniors housing community under development in Horseshoe Bay, Texas. The project, developed by Falcon Senior Housing, will offer 180 independent living, assisted living, and memory care units across 313,714 square feet. The financing package also includes EB-5 immigration investment funds.
