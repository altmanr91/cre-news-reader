Investors eye half-acre site in downtown San Jose for 136-unit development

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 23:26:24 +0000

SUMMARY

An investor group led by Anthony Ho has submitted preliminary plans for an eight-story, 136-unit multifamily development at 945 West Julian Street in San Jose, CA. The project, which includes studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments, is notable for being advanced by individual investors rather than a traditional development firm. The developers plan to utilize Senate Bill 330 to streamline the approval process, aiming to contribute to San Jose's housing goals.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 945 West Julian Street, San Jose, CA

Units: 136

Site Area: 0.5 acres

Notable Features: Located within walking distance of Diridon Station and the SAP Center.

Project Notes: Developers intend to use provisions of Senate Bill 330 to streamline the approval process.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Investor Group — Anthony Ho

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The City of San Jose must plan for 62,200 new housing units by 2031 as part of its state-mandated housing construction requirements.
