Jamie Katcher of JLL: 5 Questions

Commercial Observer — Mon, 04 May 2026 11:00:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

JLL's Tech Practice Group, led by Jamie Katcher, was formed in 2025 to support clients navigating the rapid growth of artificial intelligence companies in New York City. AI companies significantly increased their leasing activity in Q1 2026, nearly matching their entire 2025 footprint. This surge is tightening the market for high-quality office assets, particularly in Midtown South, as AI firms prioritize premium spaces for recruitment and client engagement. Katcher notes that this wave feels more grounded than previous tech booms, driven by well-capitalized companies adapting to AI, and anticipates a 'musical chairs' effect as demand for top-tier buildings intensifies.
