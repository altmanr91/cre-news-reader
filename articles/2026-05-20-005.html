Arixa Capital Lends $28M on Hawaii Build-for-Sale Luxury Homes

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Tue, 19 May 2026 20:11:37 +0000

SUMMARY

West Point Investment Corporation has secured $27.6 million in construction financing from Arixa Capital for its Hapuna Estates project, a luxury single-family home development within the Mauna Kea Resort on Hawaii's Big Island. The project will feature seven for-sale homes on lots ranging from 0.39 to 0.75 acres, with construction expected to begin in early 2027 and delivery slated for 2028. Berkadia arranged the competitive financing, noting strong sales velocity and high demand for the limited remaining custom home lots in this established resort community.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Units: 7
