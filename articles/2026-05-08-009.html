Franklin Street Arranges Sale of 12,705 SF Office Building in Pflugerville, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 07 May 2026 14:31:50 +0000

SUMMARY

Franklin Street has facilitated the sale of a 12,705-square-foot office building in Pflugerville, Texas. The property, built in 2013, was acquired by the Cameron Road Church of Christ, which plans to use it as its new home. The seller was Government Leases LLC.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 508 Old Austin Hutto Road, Pflugerville, TX

Size: 12,705 SF

Year Built: 2013

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Cameron Road Church of Christ

SELLER: Government Leases LLC

BUYER BROKER: Franklin Street — Marshall Durrett; Laura Fretwell
