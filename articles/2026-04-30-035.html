Gindis offload pair of luxury retail hubs in Boston

SALE

Boston - The Real Deal — Wed, 29 Apr 2026 20:43:18 +0000

SUMMARY

ASG Equities, an entity of the Gindi family, sold two luxury retail properties on Newbury Street in Boston for a combined $113.5 million to a joint venture between Acadia Realty Trust and Osiris Ventures. The properties, totaling 29,000 square feet, are fully occupied by high-end luxury retailers. This transaction reflects the strong demand and low vacancy rates in Boston's downtown retail market, which is experiencing a period of thriving activity and anticipated boutique and luxury expansions.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Boston's downtown retail submarket had a vacancy rate of only 3 percent at the start of the year, driven by high demand and low supply. Brokerage firms forecast boutique and luxury expansions in this submarket.
