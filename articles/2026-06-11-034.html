Embattled Silver Star files for Chapter 11 bankruptcy for second time in four years

The Real Deal — Texas — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 19:42:42 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Silver Star Properties, a Houston-based REIT, has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy for the second time in four years, citing $75 million in liabilities against $100 million in assets. The company, which attempted to pivot from office investments to self-storage, is facing defaults on four loans and a foreclosure on a self-storage facility. This latest filing follows a previous bankruptcy in September 2023, which involved the sale of its 35-property office portfolio to address $217 million in debt and a contentious legal battle with its former CEO over alleged mismanagement.
