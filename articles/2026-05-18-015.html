Chikoo Patel strikes back against Shai Wolkowicki in battle over Chicago multifamily meltdown

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 18 May 2026 11:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A legal dispute is escalating between former partners Chikoo Patel and Shai Wolkowicki over a collapsed multifamily portfolio in Chicago. Patel, the former head of CKO Real Estate, is counter-suing Wolkowicki and his own former attorney, Animesh Ravani, alleging they fraudulently induced him to sign an "Interim Settlement Agreement" that stripped him of his 40% equity stake in 46 property-owning entities. Patel claims he was misled into forfeiting his equity and agreeing to pay $2 million based on a false verbal promise of future property sale offsets, while also accusing his attorney of professional misconduct for drafting an "unconscionable" settlement that benefited Ravani, who was also an investor. Wolkowicki's camp, however, argues Patel's counterclaims are a desperate attempt to reverse a legally binding agreement where Patel admitted sole responsibility for unauthorized fund transfers.
