Century Fire Protection Signs 25,798 SF Industrial Lease in Grand Prairie, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 14:43:30 +0000

SUMMARY

Century Fire Protection, a provider of fire safety systems, has secured a 25,798-square-foot industrial lease in Grand Prairie, Texas. The transaction involves a building constructed in 1984, with the tenant's representation handled by KBC Advisors and the landlord represented by Holt Lunsford Commercial.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 1111 W. Carrier Parkway, Grand Prairie, TX

Size: 25,798 SF

Year Built: 1984
