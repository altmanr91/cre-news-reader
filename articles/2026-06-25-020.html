Brooklyn developer scores financing on one-time David Werner conversion target

LOAN

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 16:17:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Blue Sky Builders has secured $102 million in construction financing from S3 Capital for the office-to-residential conversion of 311 West 43rd Street in Hell's Kitchen. The project, known as the Press Building, will be redeveloped into a 160-unit rental property with 40,000 square feet of commercial space. The developer plans to utilize the 467-m tax abatement program, designating 25 percent of the residential units for affordable housing.

Project Notes: The property was previously under contract to David Werner for over $40 million, but that deal did not close. DivcoWest previously paid $131 million for the property in 2018. A large portion of the building was formerly leased to a coworking company, which allegedly abandoned the location in 2023.
