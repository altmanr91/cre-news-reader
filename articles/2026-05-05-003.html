Sea Fire Grill Relocating to 12K-SF Restaurant Space at 216 East 49th Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 04 May 2026 20:56:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Benjamin Restaurant Group's Sea Fire Grill is relocating its Midtown East seafood restaurant to a 12,000-square-foot space at 216 East 49th Street. The new location, owned by Shaban Sinanaj, is just a block from its current spot and will feature six private dining rooms, a new bar, and a main dining room with 24-foot ceilings, allowing the restaurant to evolve its brand while retaining its existing clientele.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 216 East 49th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 12,000 SF

Notable Features: New location features six private dining rooms, a stunning bar, and a dramatic main dining room with 24-foot ceilings.

Project Notes: The building owner previously acquired the property for $9.1 million in June 2024.
