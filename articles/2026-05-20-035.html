ZOM Ordered To Pay $323M For Circumventing Partner In Arizona Multifamily JV

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 19 May 2026 13:31:36 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Maricopa County jury has ordered ZOM Living to pay $323 million in damages to Gray Development Group for breach of contract and implied good faith and fair dealing. The dispute arose from a 2019 joint venture agreement where Gray Development shared proprietary information for five planned multifamily projects in Arizona. Gray Development alleged that ZOM Living subsequently circumvented the agreement, using Gray's confidential data to develop the projects independently after Gray ceased operations in 2020. The projects, consisting of four apartment communities in Phoenix and Scottsdale, broke ground last year and are slated for 2027 delivery.
