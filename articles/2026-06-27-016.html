Emergency declaration pushes Philly affordable project forward

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Philadelphia Mayor Cherelle Parker issued an emergency declaration to expedite the demolition of 44 vacant buildings at Bartram Village, clearing the way for a significant affordable housing redevelopment. The Philadelphia Housing Authority will replace the aging structures with 514 new public housing units as part of a larger $425 million project. This initiative aims to remove hazards, create new housing opportunities, and revitalize the Southwest Philadelphia complex, with current residents having the right to return upon completion.
