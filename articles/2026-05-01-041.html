EastBanc's New CEO On 'The Opportunity Of Our Generation'

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 13:01:21 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Philippe Lanier, the new CEO of EastBanc, is leading the 40-year-old development firm into what he describes as a generational buying opportunity in the Washington D.C. office market. After a five-year hiatus from acquisitions, EastBanc plans to capitalize on deeply discounted "truly broken office" properties, aiming for six deals by the end of 2027. Lanier believes that by repositioning these assets, which are trading at significantly below replacement cost, the company can offer investors attractive returns of 20% to 30%, despite the current challenging capital environment for D.C. commercial real estate.
