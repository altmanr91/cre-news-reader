JLL Negotiates Sale of 357,570 SF Distribution Center in Northwest Houston

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 26 May 2026 14:43:30 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has brokered the sale of Fairbanks Northwest Distribution Center, a 357,570-square-foot industrial property in northwest Houston. The two-building, cross-dock complex, completed in 2023, was 80 percent leased at the time of sale. The buyer was a fund backed by Ares Real Estate, acquiring the asset from seller Triten Real Estate Partners.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Fairbanks Northwest Distribution Center

Size: 357,570 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 80.0%

Year Built: 2023

Site Area: 23.4 acres

Notable Features: Two-building, cross-dock complex with 32-foot clear heights, 72 dock doors, eight drive-in doors, and 10,471 square feet of office space.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Ares Real Estate

SELLER: Triten Real Estate Partners

SELLER BROKER: JLL — Trent Agnew; Charles Strauss; Lance Young; Brooke Petzold
