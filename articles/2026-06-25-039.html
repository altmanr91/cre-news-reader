Investors double down on SF offices, tracking market to beat 2025

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Investment Report

San Francisco's office market is experiencing a significant resurgence in investment activity, with $1.7 billion in office building deals recorded in the first half of the year, nearly matching the $1.9 billion invested in all of last year. This renewed interest, particularly from institutional investors, is driven by growing confidence in the city's artificial intelligence sector. The market is on track to surpass investment volumes from 2020, 2021, and 2013, with increased activity in individual buildings and interest from both regional and larger institutional groups.
