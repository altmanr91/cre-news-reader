Braemar finds buyer for Colorado resort

SALE

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Braemar Hotels and Resorts, a Dallas-based hospitality REIT, has agreed to sell the 193-room Park Hyatt Beaver Creek Resort & Spa in Beaver Creek, Colorado, to Apres Owner LLC for $176 million. This transaction is part of Braemar's broader strategy to liquidate its 14-property portfolio and pay down mortgage debt, following a plan initiated last August. The sale price equates to approximately $912,000 per key and includes a $6.5 million non-refundable deposit.
