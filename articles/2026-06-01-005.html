S3 Capital Lends $132M on Washington Heights Student Housing Development

LOAN

Commercial Observer — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 10:00:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Edge Property Group secured $131.5 million in construction financing from S3 Capital for a 28-story student housing development in Washington Heights, New York City. The loan will fund the construction of 276 units and 321 beds, and also refinance existing debt on the project. S3 Capital noted the severe supply-demand imbalance in the Washington Heights student housing market, which has a very low vacancy rate and limited new deliveries.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: student housing

Address: 465 West 165th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 148,074 SF

Units: 276

Beds: 321

Loan Amount: $131.5M

Completion: fall 2028

Notable Features: The building will include over 11,000 square feet of amenity space, such as private study pods, quiet lounges, a fitness center, a golf simulator, and an outdoor terrace.

Project Notes: The loan also refinances past debt on the property.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SPONSOR: Edge Property Group

LENDER: S3 Capital — Steven Jemal, managing director of origination; Robert Schwartz, co-founder and principal

MORTGAGE BROKER: Institutional Property Advisors — Max Herzog; Eric Toddy

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Washington Heights is one of the most supply-constrained neighborhoods in New York City for residential housing, with a vacancy rate of 0.8 percent as of Q2 2025. Only 14 units were delivered in the prior 12 months.
