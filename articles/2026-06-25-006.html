Hillwood Breaks Ground on 1.2 MSF Spec Industrial Project at AllianceTexas in North Fort Worth

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 14:46:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Hillwood has commenced construction on Alliance Westport 16, a 1.2 million-square-foot speculative industrial project located within its AllianceTexas master-planned community in North Fort Worth. The facility is designed with modern industrial features, including high clear heights and extensive trailer parking, with potential for further expansion and flexible loading configurations. Fifth Third Bank is providing the construction financing for the project, which is expected to be completed by summer 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Alliance Westport 16

Size: 1,200,000 SF

Completion: summer 2027

Notable Features: 40-foot clear heights, 276 trailer parking spaces (expandable to 504), potential for tri-load configuration and 190-foot truck court depths.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Hillwood

ARCHITECT: GSR Andrade

ENGINEER: Westwood

LENDER: Fifth Third Bank
