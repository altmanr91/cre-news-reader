Anaheim Industrial Outdoor Storage Site Secures Bridge Loan

LOAN

Connect CRE — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:58:57 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has secured $26 million in bridge financing for North Palisade Partners for a 7.75-acre industrial outdoor storage facility in Anaheim, CA. The Class A property is fully leased to a Fortune 100 logistics company, which utilizes the site for mission-critical secured parking to support its nearby distribution centers. The facility's strategic location offers excellent access to major Southern California transportation corridors and the Ports of Long Beach and Los Angeles.

Notable Features: Class A industrial outdoor storage facility with 161 trailer parking stalls and two points of ingress and egress. Located 1.1 miles from State Route 91, 2.5 miles from State Route 57, and three miles from State Route 90, providing connectivity to Orange County, Los Angeles, and the Inland Empire.

Project Notes: Non-recourse, three-year, floating-rate loan.
