Mansueto’s Chicago Fire inks McDonald’s for new stadium naming rights

The Real Deal — Chicago — Wed, 13 May 2026 19:03:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

Chicago Fire FC, owned by Joe Mansueto, has secured McDonald's as the naming rights sponsor for its new $750 million privately funded stadium, to be known as "McDonald's Park." The Major League Soccer team will relocate to the new venue at The 78 development in Chicago in 2028, serving as a key anchor for Related Midwest's larger $8 billion project. The sponsorship deal with McDonald's is expected to run through 2040 and includes a 20,000-square-foot restaurant at the stadium.
