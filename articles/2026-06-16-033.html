Former Spurs star LaMarcus Aldridge buys another Westlake home

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 19:43:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Former NBA player LaMarcus Aldridge, through his LLC, has acquired a 10,466-square-foot mansion in Westlake, Texas, from Larry and Maria Smith. The property, initially listed for $10.9 million, was under contract at $10 million. This acquisition further expands Aldridge's real estate portfolio, which is primarily concentrated in the Dallas-Fort Worth area.
