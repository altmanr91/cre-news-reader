Gencom's Karim Alibhai Looks West After $1B East Coast Buying Spree

ACQUISITION

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:57:36 -0400

SUMMARY

Gencom, a hospitality-focused investment and development firm, has acquired five luxury hotels in New York and Miami over the past two years, totaling over $1 billion. The firm recently purchased the 253-room Ritz-Carlton New York, Central Park for $320 million in February, following its $300 million acquisition of the Thompson Central Park and a $230 million joint venture for the InterContinental New York Times Square in late 2024. Gencom is now shifting its focus to distressed acquisition opportunities on the West Coast, particularly in California, while also planning significant renovations for its newly acquired and re-acquired properties.
