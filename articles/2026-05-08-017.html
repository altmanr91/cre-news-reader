Zillow posts $46M profit in first quarter

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 07 May 2026 14:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Earnings Report

Zillow Group reported a significant increase in first-quarter net income, reaching $46 million, nearly six times its earnings from the previous year. The Seattle-based real estate technology company saw its revenue climb 18% year-over-year to $708 million, driven by strong performance in its mortgages and rentals segments. Mortgages revenue surged 56% to $64 million, while rentals revenue increased 42% to $183 million, with multifamily revenue up 57%. The company also highlighted its focus on AI integration across its products and operations, including the recent launch of Zillow AI mode, and a new partnership with Realtor.com to syndicate pre-marketed listings.
