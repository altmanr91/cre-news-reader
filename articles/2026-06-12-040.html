Historic Catholic residences in Los Altos trade for $43M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 22:39:59 +0000

SUMMARY

A Silicon Valley entity linked to entrepreneur Nicole Shanahan's Bia-Echo Foundation has acquired the historic Maryknoll Residence in Los Altos for $43 million in an all-cash transaction. The 29.2-acre property, formerly a seminary and retirement home for the Catholic Foreign Mission Society of America, was sought by the buyer for its potential to support a special education school with a faith-based component. This sale reflects a broader trend of religiously affiliated campuses trading hands in Silicon Valley.
