Saunders partners with Premier Estate Properties to link ultralux markets

The Real Deal — New York — Wed, 20 May 2026 16:41:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Saunders & Associates, a Hamptons-based brokerage, and Premier Estate Properties, a South Florida firm, have formed a strategic partnership to connect their respective luxury real estate markets. This alliance will facilitate referral sharing, listing information exchange, and joint networking events, aiming to expand their reach in an increasingly consolidated industry. The collaboration leverages the strong symbiotic relationship between the Hamptons and Palm Beach, which are key markets for high-net-worth individuals.
