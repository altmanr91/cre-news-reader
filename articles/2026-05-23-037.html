Sprawling Sonoma County estate flirts with local price record

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 22 May 2026 16:31:36 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Ghost Donkey Ranch, a 227-acre estate in Knights Valley, Sonoma County, is being listed for $35 million. The property, which includes a 4,200-square-foot main house and two guest houses, is a working cattle and agricultural ranch. The seller, Adam Gordon, managing partner of Wildflower, acquired the land in 2011 and developed the estate, which also serves as the base for his Wagyu beef company.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The priciest home sale ever recorded in Sonoma County was $30 million in 2010.
