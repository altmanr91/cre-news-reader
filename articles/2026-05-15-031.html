“Development in a box”: Time Equities lists historic South Loop office-to-resi play

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Time Equities and JK Equities are listing a historic 80,500-square-foot office building at 1006 South Michigan Avenue in Chicago's South Loop for sale. The property is being offered as a fully entitled office-to-residential conversion opportunity, with zoning, land use approvals, and floorplan designs already secured. The sellers anticipate obtaining building permits prior to closing, allowing a buyer to commence construction almost immediately on the planned 49-unit apartment conversion, which will also include ground-floor retail.
