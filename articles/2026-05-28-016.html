Centerbridge eyeing minority stake in industrial firm

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 27 May 2026 20:16:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Centerbridge Partners is reportedly in advanced discussions to acquire a minority stake in Merritt Properties, a Maryland-based industrial and office firm with a portfolio exceeding 20 million square feet. The private equity group is expected to purchase approximately one-third of the business from Almanac Realty Investors, the real estate investment division of Neuberger Berman. The Merritt family will retain a majority ownership in the company they founded in the 1960s. This potential acquisition highlights Centerbridge's growing investment in the real estate sector, following the close of its $2.3 billion second real estate fund in early 2022.

DATA POINTS

Merritt Properties portfolio value: $3 billion

Centerbridge Partners Real Estate Fund II capital commitments: $2.3 billion

Centerbridge Partners capital under management (end of 2021): $33 billion
