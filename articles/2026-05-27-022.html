Danny Meyer heads to Hotel Bossert

LEASE

The Real Deal — New York — Tue, 26 May 2026 21:38:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Union Square Hospitality Group, led by Danny Meyer, will open its first full-service Brooklyn restaurant at the historic Hotel Bossert in Brooklyn Heights. The restaurant will occupy 3,200 square feet on the ground floor of the landmarked building, which is currently being redeveloped into condominiums. The property was acquired by SomeraRoad last year for $100 million after a period of development challenges and a foreclosure auction.

Notable Features: Historic landmarked building, formerly known as Brooklyn's Waldorf-Astoria.

Project Notes: Building was previously acquired by SomeraRoad for $100 million last year after a foreclosure auction. Original plans by Chetrit Group and David Bistricer in 2013 included 78 guest rooms, a restaurant, and a rooftop bar.
