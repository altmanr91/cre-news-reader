How New Yorkers Erik Rutter and David Weitz became Miami’s newest up-and-comers

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 04 May 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Oak Row Equities, led by Erik Rutter and David Weitz, acquired a 4.25-acre bayfront development site in Miami's Brickell district for a record $520 million. The firm partnered with OKO Group to develop a luxury hotel and branded condominium project on the site. This acquisition marks a significant move for the relatively young firm, which has rapidly expanded its presence in Miami's prime development areas since its inception eight years ago, backed by substantial family capital.
