“Behind our backs”: Ex-Lt Gov, Florida Rep Carlos Lopez-Cantera, father sued over Miami-Dade land deal

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 21:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A lawsuit has been filed against former Florida politician Carlos M. Lopez-Cantera and his father, Carlos C. Lopez-Cantera, alleging they conspired to defraud investment partners in a Miami-Dade County land deal. The suit claims the Lopez-Canteras misled partners about the value of an 8.3-acre vacant tract, inducing them to sell it for $40,000 to Quarry Real Estate Holdings, an entity led by the younger Lopez-Cantera. The plaintiffs allege the Lopez-Canteras concealed negotiations to lift a conservation easement that would significantly increase the property's value, intending to flip the land for millions and deprive investors of higher profits.
