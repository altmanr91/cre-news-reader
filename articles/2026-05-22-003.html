Design Firm GKV Architects Signs 6K-SF Lease at AmTrustRE’s 360 Lexington Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 21 May 2026 17:06:49 +0000

SUMMARY

GKV Architects, an architecture and interior design firm, has signed a 5,754-square-foot lease for a portion of the 14th floor at 360 Lexington Avenue in Midtown East, relocating from its previous offices at 675 Third Avenue. The building, acquired by AmTrustRE in 2024, is seeing active leasing, with the landlord noting GKV's prior collaboration on several of its projects. This lease follows other recent transactions at the property, indicating strong tenant interest in the repositioned asset.
