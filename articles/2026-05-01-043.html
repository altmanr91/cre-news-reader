REPORT: Anthropic Closing In On Full-Building Lease In Hudson Square

LEASE

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 11:35:38 -0400

SUMMARY

Artificial intelligence firm Anthropic is reportedly close to finalizing a full-building lease for 330 Hudson Street in Manhattan's Hudson Square. The San Francisco-based company, which has been actively seeking a significant expansion in New York City, is expected to occupy the entire 466,000-square-foot property owned by AEW Capital Management. This move aligns with a broader trend of AI companies expanding their footprint in Manhattan, often securing space for future growth rather than immediate staffing needs, as evidenced by the substantial leasing activity in the sector.
