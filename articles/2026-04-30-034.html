San Francisco is only US gateway market to eclipse pre-pandemic office demand

San Francisco - The Real Deal — Wed, 29 Apr 2026 17:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Office Demand Report

San Francisco has emerged as the sole U.S. gateway market to surpass its pre-pandemic office demand, driven largely by the booming artificial intelligence sector. According to VTS, national office demand in Q1 2026 reached its highest point since 2020, with San Francisco's demand exceeding its 2018-2019 average by 14 percent. This surge is attributed to a significant influx of AI companies seeking space, indicating a shift away from remote work preferences in the tech industry and a strong market sentiment despite broader economic and geopolitical uncertainties.
