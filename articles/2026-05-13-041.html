Rampant Mismanagement Alleged At Appraisal Institute In Bombshell Defamation Suit

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 12 May 2026 14:40:51 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A former executive at the Appraisal Institute has filed a defamation lawsuit against the organization and several current and former members, alleging rampant mismanagement, financial malfeasance, and a culture focused on internal power struggles. The lawsuit claims the executive was forced out after pushing for internal changes and that sexual harassment allegations against him were unsubstantiated. The complaint details accusations of inflated membership counts, misreported program success, and non-competitive contracts awarded to insiders, with some alleged financial mismanagement dating back to 2012.
