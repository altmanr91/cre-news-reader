Farallon Partnership Trades Historic Hawthorne Hangar Campus

SALE

Connect CRE — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 21:07:32 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership led by Farallon Capital Management, Jofa Capital, and Urban Investment Research Corporation (URC) has sold the historic Hawthorne Hangar Campus in Hawthorne, CA, for $32.5 million. The industrial flex/R&D property, which served as the original office and airport hangar for aviation pioneer Jack Northrop, was acquired by the selling partnership in 2023 for $13.35 million out of bankruptcy. The sellers secured a long-term lease with a Fortune 100 tenant prior to the sale, aligning with their investment strategy for unique, fragmented assets.
