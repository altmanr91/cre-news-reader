Gatsby lands $100M refi for Palm Beach Gardens offices

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Gatsby Florida secured a $100.4 million refinancing loan for DiVosta Towers, a 220,000-square-foot Class A office campus in Palm Beach Gardens, FL. Cirrus Real Estate Partners provided the three-year, floating-rate loan, which will be used to refinance a previous mortgage. The transaction highlights the increasing demand for suburban office space in Palm Beach County, as companies seek more affordable leases compared to downtown West Palm Beach.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Palm Beach Gardens office landlords reported 24,400 square feet of positive net absorption and 13,800 square feet of leasing activity in Q1. The vacancy rate in Palm Beach Gardens stood at 8.6 percent, with average asking rents at $52.50 per square foot overall and over $60 per square foot for Class A offices. More companies are looking to move to suburban submarkets in Palm Beach County due to high rents in downtown West Palm Beach, which can exceed $100 per square foot.
