Amazon’s massive retail store development in Oak Brook passes first test

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 15 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Amazon is moving forward with plans for a large-format retail store in Oak Brook, Illinois, after the village panel recommended approval for the project. The development, which involves rezoning and the demolition of existing office buildings, represents a strategic shift for Amazon into physical retail, moving beyond its previous smaller-format store concepts. The company aims to create a unique shopping experience, with completion anticipated by October 2028.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Size: 225,000 SF

Completion: October 2028

Site Area: 27 acres

Notable Features: Costco-style grocery and merchandise store, one of the first of its kind in the country.

Project Notes: Requires zoning changes from office to retail. Involves the demolition of seven office buildings totaling approximately 600,000 square feet.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Amazon

OWNER: Inland Real Estate

SPONSOR PIPELINE

Orland Park, IL: 229,000-square-foot store on a 35-acre plot, planned for 2027.
