Colliers closes sale of 246,446-square-foot logistics facility in Elgin

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Venture One has sold a 246,446-square-foot logistics facility in Elgin, Illinois, to an undisclosed publicly traded real estate investment trust. The cross-docked distribution center, built in 2015, is fully leased and benefits from its strategic location near the I-90 and Randall Road interchange, providing excellent access to a key freight corridor for regional distribution and last-mile delivery.

Size: 246,446 SF

Notable Features: Cross-docked distribution facility located in a fully developed business park, offering immediate access to I-90 and Randall Road.
