Skanska Completes 250,000 SF Academic Project at University of Texas at Tyler

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 15:05:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Skanska has completed a 250,000-square-foot academic building for the University of Texas at Tyler, which will serve as the university's new medical school. The five-story facility includes classrooms, advanced teaching laboratories, and collaborative training spaces. The project also features a seven-story parking garage with an integrated facilities office and a skybridge connecting to the existing hospital.
