Walker & Dunlop Leads $16.5B Fannie Mae Lending Market

Commercial Observer — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 12:47:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Lending Report

Walker & Dunlop has emerged as the leading Fannie Mae multifamily lender for 2026 year-to-date, originating $2.18 billion across 110 loans, according to CRED iQ's proprietary loan analytics. The firm's performance highlights a concentrated agency lending market where the top 10 originators captured approximately 78% of the total $16.5 billion volume through mid-May. The market is heavily driven by refinancing activity, which accounts for 62.8% of the volume, as borrowers address upcoming maturities and replace bridge debt. Gateway and Sun Belt metros, including New York, San Jose, Los Angeles, Phoenix, Miami, and Dallas, are the primary destinations for this agency capital.
