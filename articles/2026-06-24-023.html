Miami Design District brings another marquee mixed-use project

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 23:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Miami Design District Associates and Raycliff Capital are partnering to develop Sweetbird North, an eight-story, 120,500-square-foot mixed-use project in the Miami Design District. The development will feature over 92,000 square feet of office space targeting creative industries and 22,500 square feet of ground-floor and second-story retail. Construction is slated to begin in August 2026 and conclude in early 2028, with the design by globally recognized architecture firm Snøhetta.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Sweetbird North

Size: 120,500 SF

Completion: early 2028

Notable Features: Features a layered facade of mesh screens and planted terraces; office space targets creative industries.

Project Notes: Developed on a 17,000-square-foot site.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Developers in the Design District are relying on Miami's continued influx of companies and executives to drive demand for new office space, offering an alternative to established business hubs like downtown, Brickell, and Wynwood. Nearly 1,000 residential units are planned in the neighborhood.
