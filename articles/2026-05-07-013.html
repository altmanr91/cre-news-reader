Marcus & Millichap closes sale of 20-townhome portfolio in New Richmond

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 07 May 2026 09:37:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A 20-townhome portfolio in New Richmond, Wisconsin, has been sold for $4.6 million. The transaction was brokered by Marcus & Millichap, representing a Wisconsin-based investment group as the seller and procuring a Wisconsin-based syndication as the buyer. The portfolio consists of three-bedroom, two-bathroom homes built in 2016, located approximately 40 miles from Minneapolis–Saint Paul.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Units: 20

Sale Price: $4.6M

Year Built: 2016

Notable Features: All units are three-bedroom, two-bathroom homes.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Wisconsin-based syndication
