Cushman & Wakefield Brokers $6.9M Sale of Delray Beach Medical Pavilion in South Florida

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:41:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Benlee Asset Management has acquired the Delray Beach Medical Pavilion, a 36,041-square-foot healthcare facility in Delray Beach, Florida, for $6.9 million. The property, located at 14000 S. Military Trail, was 63.4 percent leased at the time of sale. Cushman & Wakefield facilitated the transaction, representing the seller.
