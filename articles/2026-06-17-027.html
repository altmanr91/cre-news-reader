Landmark designation of Bryn Mawr Avenue building stalling demolition, development plans

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 20:08:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

A proposed 95-unit mixed-use apartment complex in Chicago's Edgewater neighborhood faces a significant hurdle as the Commission on Chicago Landmarks has issued a preliminary landmark recommendation for the Bryn Mawr Avenue area. Developer Harlem Irving Companies, which had planned to demolish three existing buildings for the project, has had one demolition permit denied due to the building's historic significance. The developer has already invested $6 million in the project since acquiring the site in 2020, and the final decision on the demolition permit is expected in July.
