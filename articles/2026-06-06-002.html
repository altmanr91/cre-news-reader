Targo Capital Partners Buys 185 East Houston Street, Part of $81M Portfolio

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 17:53:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Targo Capital Partners has acquired 185 East Houston Street, a six-story, 31-unit apartment building on Manhattan’s Lower East Side, for $30.8 million. This transaction is part of a larger $81 million portfolio acquisition by Targo Capital Partners, comprising seven prewar residential properties from S&H Equities. The portfolio includes 125 residential units and ground-floor retail space across various Lower Manhattan neighborhoods.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 185 East Houston Street, New York City, NY

Sale Price: $30.8M

Notable Features: Prewar residential property with ground-floor retail.

Project Notes: Part of a larger $81 million portfolio acquisition of seven properties totaling 125 residential units. The other properties are located at 90 Rivington Street, 96 Orchard Street, 156 Orchard Street, 126 First Avenue, 153 Avenue C and 38 Carmine Street.
