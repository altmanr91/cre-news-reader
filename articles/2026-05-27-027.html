Boucher Brothers, city of Miami Beach finalize $3M settlement with Nikki Beach operator

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 26 May 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A three-year legal dispute between the city of Miami Beach, Boucher Brothers, and the operator of Nikki Beach has concluded with a $3 million settlement. The lawsuit, filed by Penrod Brothers, alleged that city officials favored Boucher Brothers and Major Food Group's bid to redevelop the city-owned beachfront site at 1 Ocean Drive. The settlement clears the way for Boucher Brothers and Major Food Group to proceed with their plans for a new development, Boucher Brothers Pier Park, which will include multiple restaurants, a pool, retail, and other amenities.
