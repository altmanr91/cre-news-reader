Thiago da Costa of Procore: 5 Questions

Commercial Observer — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 13:00:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Feature / Profile

This article is a Q&A interview with Thiago da Costa, Senior Vice President of AI and Data at Procore, discussing the integration of Datagrid Intelligence's agentic artificial intelligence into Procore's platform. The expanded Procore AI platform aims to automate high-friction construction workflows such as submittals, RFIs, and contract reviews, by enabling the AI to execute tasks directly within the platform. Da Costa emphasizes that this new agentic AI goes beyond previous search capabilities by actively performing work, leveraging multimodal intelligence to process visual data from job sites, and ultimately helping to upskill the construction workforce by providing immediate access to project knowledge.
