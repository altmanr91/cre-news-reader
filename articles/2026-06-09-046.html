Charlotte Approves 150-Day Pause On New Data Center Projects

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 09:00:50 -0400

SUMMARY

Infrastructure Investment / Government Policy

The Charlotte City Council has unanimously approved a 150-day moratorium on new data center development to study the infrastructure and environmental impacts of these facilities. This pause will allow the city to gather technical and scientific information to inform future policy decisions regarding the rapidly growing data center industry. The moratorium will not affect projects that have already received valid building permits or have complete applications for developmental approval.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

A city in Southern California recently passed the nation's first permanent citywide data center ban. New York state is considering a one-year moratorium. Durham, NC, passed a 60-day moratorium in May, and Apex, NC, passed a one-year moratorium in April.
