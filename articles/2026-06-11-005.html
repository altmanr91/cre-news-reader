Bay Club Company Buys El Segundo, Calif., Property for $42M: Updated

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 18:45:55 +0000

SUMMARY

Bay Club Company has acquired the 5.4-acre campus housing its Bay Club El Segundo facility for $42 million, completing a long-term strategy with seller Continental Development Corporation. This transaction gives Bay Club full ownership of both the buildings and the underlying land at the property, solidifying its presence in the South Bay area as a premier athletic and recreation club destination. The company's CEO noted that controlling the real estate provides permanent control over their future and the foundation for continued investment in the club and community.
