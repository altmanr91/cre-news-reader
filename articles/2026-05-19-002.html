Gigi Hadid’s Guest in Residence Brand Leases Second NYC Store at 1061 Madison Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 18 May 2026 20:21:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Gigi Hadid's luxury cashmere brand, Guest in Residence, has secured a 3,550-square-foot retail lease at 1061 Madison Avenue on Manhattan's Upper East Side. This marks the brand's second New York City location, expanding its brick-and-mortar presence. The lease, finalized in February, includes ground-floor, second-floor, and basement space, with an asking rent of $750,000 annually.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 1061 Madison Avenue, New York City, NY

Size: 3,550 SF

Rental Rate: $211.27/SF/yr

Notable Features: Located one block from Central Park and the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Project Notes: The space was previously occupied by activewear retailer Bandier.
