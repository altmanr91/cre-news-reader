Dollar Stores Have Been On a Tear — Here’s What Could Spoil the Party

Commercial Observer — Mon, 18 May 2026 12:17:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The dollar store sector, including Dollar General, Dollar Tree, and Five Below, experienced a strong performance in 2025, driven by consumers across all income brackets seeking value. These retailers announced significant expansion plans for 2026, including new store openings and extensive renovations, with Dollar General planning 450 new locations and over 4,000 remodels, and Dollar Tree aiming for 400 new stores and converting existing ones to a multi-price format. However, rising gas prices and broader economic pressures are expected to challenge this momentum, potentially shifting consumer spending away from discretionary items, as evidenced by recent declines in foot traffic for some chains like Dollar Tree, while others focused on necessities, like Dollar General, have maintained steady visitation.
