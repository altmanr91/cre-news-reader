Fraud Prevention Startup 100 Acquires Cobblestone

Commercial Observer — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 12:00:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Proptech startup 100 has acquired Cobblestone, a fraud-detection and income-verification company specializing in the multifamily market. The acquisition, funded by a mix of cash and equity from existing revenue, integrates Cobblestone's fintech-grade fraud prevention technology into 100's existing verification and underwriting platform. This strategic move aims to enhance 100's end-to-end fraud prevention capabilities, particularly in income automation, leveraging advanced AI to combat increasingly sophisticated fraudulent applications in the real estate industry.
