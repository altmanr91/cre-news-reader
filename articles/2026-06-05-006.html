Newmark Brokers Sale of 332,309 SF Shopping Center in Newport, Kentucky

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 15:56:06 +0000

SUMMARY

Newmark has brokered the sale of Newport Pavilion, a 332,309-square-foot shopping center in Newport, Kentucky. The property, co-anchored by Kroger and Target, was fully leased at the time of sale. Newmark represented the seller, The Ranier Cos., in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Newport Pavilion

Address: 102-114 Pavilion Pkwy, Newport, KY*

Size: 332,309 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 100.0%

Site Area: 55 acres

Notable Features: Co-anchored by Kroger and Target; benefits from a long-term PILOT agreement through 2037.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
