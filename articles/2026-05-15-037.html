SpaceX well into building out 1M sf Bastrop solar cell factory

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 14 May 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

SpaceX is reportedly well into construction on a 1.1 million-square-foot solar cell factory in Bastrop, Texas. The facility, located south of the company's Starlink construction facility, is intended to produce solar cells for large-scale data centers in Earth's orbit, supporting the growing demand for AI infrastructure. This development is part of Elon Musk's broader strategy to expand tech infrastructure for his various companies in the Austin area.
