Mavrek, Acres join forces for second $90M Loop office-to-residential conversion

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 17:37:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Mavrek Development and Acres Capital are partnering for a second time to convert a largely vacant office building at 209 West Jackson Boulevard in Chicago's Loop into approximately 180 residential apartments. This $90 million renovation project is part of a broader trend of office-to-residential conversions in Downtown Chicago, driven by high office vacancy rates and strong demand for rental housing. The project is notable for being privately funded, distinguishing it from many other conversions in the city that rely on public subsidies.
