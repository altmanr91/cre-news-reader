Jack Nicklaus plans Lake Worth private golf club

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Golf legend Jack Nicklaus and his family, in partnership with Mark Walter's TWG Global, are developing Nokosi, a private members-only golf club in Lake Worth, FL. The project involves redeveloping the former Sherbrooke Golf & Country Club site, which was acquired for $15 million in October. Plans include a redesigned golf course, dining, fitness, wellness, event, and golf training facilities, with the course expected to be completed later this year and clubhouse construction to follow.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: golf course

Property Name: Nokosi

Address: 6270 Lyons Road, Lake Worth, FL

Site Area: 153 acres

Notable Features: Private members-only club with a redesigned golf course and comprehensive amenities.

Project Notes: The 153-acre golf course was previously acquired for $15 million in October by GNP Lantana, a Delaware LLC.
