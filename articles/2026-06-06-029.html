Nuveen snaps up industrial facility in San Antonio

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 05 Jun 2026 21:10:50 +0000

SUMMARY

Nuveen Real Estate has acquired Phase I of Koontz's Westport Industrial Park, a Class A industrial facility on San Antonio's far West Side. The two-building, 187,200-square-foot property, which is 79 percent leased, was valued at nearly $17 million by the Bexar Central Appraisal District, though the sale price was likely higher. Koontz will retain ownership of the adjacent Phase II, which is slated for completion in late 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Westport Industrial Park Phase I

Address: 438 South Callagahan Road, San Antonio, TX

Size: 187,200 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 79.0%

Site Area: 17 acres

Notable Features: Class A industrial buildings with direct access to Hwy 151, located in an area of residential expansion.

Project Notes: The Bexar Central Appraisal District valued the buildings at almost $17 million, but the sale price was likely much higher. Koontz will retain ownership of Phase II, an adjacent site set to be completed in Q4 2027. The total project is planned for four buildings.
