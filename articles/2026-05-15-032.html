Lake Forest mansion fetches $6M, below 2017 sale

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Thu, 14 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A Lake Forest mansion, designed by architect Darcy Bonner and built in 2010, recently sold for $6.35 million. The 11,500-square-foot property, located on a nearly 3-acre gated lot, was on the market for almost two years and sold for less than its 2017 purchase price. The transaction highlights the current market dynamics in Lake Forest, with several high-value residential sales occurring in the area recently.

Project Notes: The property was previously acquired for $6.4 million in 2017, resulting in a $50,000 loss for the sellers. It was originally listed for $7.5 million in August 2024 and underwent multiple price reductions.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Lake Forest market has seen several high-profile sales recently, including an $8.3 million lakefront estate on Bluffs Edge Drive in March and the former Kraft and Quaker chief executive Robert Morrison's home for nearly $6.3 million in April. The priciest sale in the last three years was $11 million for a house at 930 East Rosemary Road in October.
