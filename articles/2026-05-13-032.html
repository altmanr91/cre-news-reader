Peninsula Land ventures into Campbell with bargain-basement office buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 12 May 2026 20:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Peninsula Land & Capital has acquired Lincoln Court, a 123,800-square-foot office building in Campbell, CA, for $24.6 million. The property was sold by an affiliate of Pacific Oak Capital Advisors at a significant discount to its prior assessed value, reflecting the ongoing recalibration of Bay Area office values. The transaction drew interest from multiple potential buyers, indicating a growing appetite for suburban multi-tenant office assets despite broader market distress.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Bay Area office values continue to fall due to higher interest rates, soft tenant demand, and elevated vacancies. Deeply discounted trades are expected to pressure future tax assessments for local governments.
