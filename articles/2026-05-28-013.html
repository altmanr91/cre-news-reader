Essex Realty Group closes sale of six-unit multifamily property in Chicago’s Lincoln Park neighborhood

SALE

REJournals — Wed, 27 May 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Essex Realty Group has facilitated the sale of a six-unit multifamily property located at 1948 North Lincoln Avenue in Chicago's Lincoln Park neighborhood. The property, featuring one-bedroom, one-bathroom apartments with modern finishes and in-unit amenities, offers investors strong in-place rents and significant upside potential through continued rental growth and potential short-term rental income. Its prime location provides excellent access to transportation, dining, shopping, and entertainment, benefiting from proximity to major attractions and DePaul University, which drives consistent rental demand.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 1948 North Lincoln Avenue, Chicago, IL

Units: 6

Notable Features: Each apartment features modern finishes including hardwood floors, stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, tiled backsplashes and bathrooms, individual HVAC systems, in-unit laundry, and private rear decks. Located less than one mile from Sedgwick and Armitage CTA Stations, with convenient access to Lake Shore Drive and multiple bus routes.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Essex Realty Group, LLC

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Lincoln Park remains one of Chicago’s most historically significant and desirable neighborhoods due to its proximity to downtown Chicago, Lake Michigan, and the city’s lakefront amenities. Its combination of historic charm, vibrant retail corridors, and steady residential demand continues to make the neighborhood a premier destination for multifamily investment. DePaul University, with over 25,000 students, continues to drive strong rental demand throughout the neighborhood.
