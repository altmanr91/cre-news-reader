Henderson Park, Pyramid Global Acquire Hyatt Regency Grand Reserve in Puerto Rico

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 21:12:05 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between international private equity real estate firm Henderson Park and hospitality management company Pyramid Global Hospitality has acquired the Hyatt Regency Grand Reserve in Río Grande, Puerto Rico, for $190 million. This transaction marks the entry of both firms into the Puerto Rican hospitality market, which is experiencing strong growth with a significant increase in luxury hotel rooms and overseas arrivals. The new owners plan to undertake capital investments to upgrade the resort for both group and leisure segments, leveraging the island's compelling market fundamentals and favorable tax incentives.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

Puerto Rico's hotel market is considered one of the strongest in North America, with a 25% increase in luxury hotel rooms since early 2024. Overseas arrivals to Puerto Rico were up 82% from pre-pandemic levels as of January 2026, while the U.S. overall saw 17% fewer visitors.
