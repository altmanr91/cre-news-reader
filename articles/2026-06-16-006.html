Partnership Breaks Ground on 78-Unit Multifamily Project in Long Branch, New Jersey

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 14:21:12 +0000

SUMMARY

A partnership between Downeaster Development and TANTUM Real Estate has commenced construction on The Elbie, a 78-unit multifamily project in Long Branch, New Jersey. The four-story building will also feature 4,000 square feet of ground-floor retail space and a variety of amenities. Initial occupancy is anticipated in late spring 2027.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: The Elbie

Address: 305 Broadway, Long Branch, NJ*

Size: 4,000 SF

Units: 78

Completion: late spring 2027

Notable Features: Includes one- and two-bedroom units, a fitness center, clubroom, game room, recording and podcast studio, and outdoor grilling and dining stations.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.
