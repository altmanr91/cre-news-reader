CRC closes acquisition of 14-property retail portfolio spanning seven states

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Wed, 06 May 2026 19:33:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Continental Realty Corporation (CRC) has completed an off-market acquisition of a 14-property retail portfolio from US Properties Group. The portfolio, spanning over 2 million square feet across seven states, includes a mix of well-located retail assets with strong market fundamentals and high occupancy. This strategic acquisition expands CRC's national shopping center portfolio and increases its assets under management to nearly $5 billion, broadening its footprint to 16 states.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Size: 2,000,000 SF

Occupancy at Sale: 93.0%

Notable Features: Portfolio includes a lifestyle center, Streets of Indian Lake, in Nashville, TN, and four assets in Ohio, including the Kroger-anchored Delaware Community Plaza.

Project Notes: This off-market transaction is the third largest portfolio acquisition of the year according to CoStar Group.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Continental Realty Corporation

SELLER: US Properties Group

SELLER BROKER: CBRE — Chris Decoufle; Kevin Hurley

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The acquired portfolio is located in markets with an average retail occupancy of 97 percent. Delaware County, part of the Columbus metropolitan area, experienced 12.54% population growth over the past five years, and the Columbus market added over 21,000 residents in 2025.
