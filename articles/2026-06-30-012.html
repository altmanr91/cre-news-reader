McGuireWoods advises Chevron in natural gas power facility deal in West Texas

DEVELOPMENT

REJournals — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 16:47:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Chevron U.S.A. Inc., through its subsidiary Energy Forge One LLC, has partnered with Microsoft Corp. to develop Project Kilby, a natural gas power facility in West Texas. This facility will provide dedicated electricity to a co-located Microsoft-operated data center under a 20-year power purchase agreement. The project, developed in collaboration with investment firm Engine No. 1, is designed to deliver approximately 2.67 gigawatts of power generation capacity through a phased, modular approach, supporting AI growth and reducing strain on regional electricity grids.
