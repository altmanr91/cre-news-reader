Mapletree Acquires Central NJ Land for 953K-SF Logistics Development

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Tue, 12 May 2026 21:18:48 +0000

SUMMARY

Mapletree Investments has acquired a 96.8-acre parcel in Manalapan, NJ, where it plans to develop a nearly 953,000-square-foot logistics facility. The project, expected to be completed in the first quarter of 2028, will feature two warehouse buildings with 40-foot clear heights, ample parking, and modern amenities including EV charging infrastructure. This acquisition reinforces Mapletree's commitment to expanding its logistics portfolio in the U.S., particularly in the strategic Central New Jersey market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 370 Smithburg Rd, Manalapan, NJ

Size: 952,720 SF

Completion: Q1 2028

Site Area: 96.8 acres

Notable Features: Two warehouse buildings with 40-foot clear heights, 366 car parking spaces, 141 trailer parking spaces, 172 dock doors, EV charging infrastructure, and smart metering technology.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Mapletree Investments — Richard Prokup, CEO, U.S.; Chiagorom Osu, Head of U.S. Logistics Development
