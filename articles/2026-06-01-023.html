Inside the wellness amenity business in the Hamptons

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The Hamptons real estate market has become a laboratory for integrating wellness amenities into luxury homes, driven by a cultural shift among wealthy homeowners seeking calm and restoration. New builds are allocating significant budget to features like infrared saunas, cold plunge pools, and meditation rooms, reflecting a growing demand from buyers across different age cohorts. Beyond specific amenities, architects and designers are focusing on creating peaceful, human-scaled spaces with natural materials, biophilic design, and advanced environmental systems like enhanced air filtration and circadian lighting, emphasizing that the overall architecture and flow contribute as much to wellness as individual features.
