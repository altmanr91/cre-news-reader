South Florida Dirt: In Brickell, Michael Stern fights foreclosure. In Miami Beach, his proposed tower moves on without him

The Real Deal — Miami — Sun, 17 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

This article details the contrasting fates of two major South Florida development projects by Michael Stern's JDS Development Group. In Miami Beach, JDS transferred its ownership stake in the 1250 West Avenue condo tower project to Terra, which subsequently secured unanimous design approval for the 28-story building after JDS's earlier efforts to obtain an upzoning. Meanwhile, in Brickell, JDS is embroiled in a foreclosure lawsuit with its new lender, Cottonwood Management, over the stalled Mercedes-Benz Places development, with Cottonwood alleging default and JDS accusing the lender of violating a non-disclosure agreement.
