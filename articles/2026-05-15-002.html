Chelsea Piers Fitness Pre-Leases 76K SF at Tavros’ 250 Water Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 14 May 2026 20:18:21 +0000

SUMMARY

Chelsea Piers Fitness has pre-leased 76,000 square feet for a five-story fitness center at Tavros's planned mixed-use tower at 250 Water Street in Manhattan's Seaport District. This anchor retail lease is a significant step for the development, which aims to deliver a comprehensive neighborhood asset including housing and community amenities. The developers acquired the site in February for $150.5 million after previous owners faced challenges with a controversial high-rise project.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Address: 250 Water Street, New York City, NY

Size: 76,000 SF

Notable Features: Five-story fitness center within a planned 600-unit mixed-use development.

Project Notes: The 1-acre development site was previously acquired for $150.5 million in February. The prior owner, Seaport Entertainment Group, acquired the property for $180 million in 2018 and spent $40 million on air rights, facing community opposition to development plans.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The median retail asking rent in the nearby Financial District was $248 per square foot in the second half of 2025. Lower Manhattan is identified as one of the fastest-growing residential neighborhoods in New York City.
