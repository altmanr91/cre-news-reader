Weston Urban Completes 290-Unit Multifamily Project in Downtown San Antonio

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 11 May 2026 14:58:33 +0000

SUMMARY

Weston Urban has completed The Continental Residences, a 290-unit multifamily project in downtown San Antonio. The 16-story building offers a mix of studio, one- and two-bedroom apartments, and two-story townhomes. Amenities include a sixth-floor clubroom, coworking space, fitness center, outdoor pool deck, and a rooftop lounge.

DATA POINTS

Address: 110 S Laredo St, San Antonio, TX*
