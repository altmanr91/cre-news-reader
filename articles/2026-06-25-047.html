Brokerages Are Racing To Adopt AI. Costs And Headaches Are On The Rise

Bisnow News Feed — Wed, 24 Jun 2026 17:34:20 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Commercial real estate brokerages are rapidly adopting artificial intelligence, with 92% of firms now running AI pilots, up from 5% three years ago. However, many firms are struggling to achieve their program goals, with only 5% reporting success, and are facing rising costs for development, integration, and ongoing support. The industry is also grappling with 'shadow AI,' where employees use unsanctioned tools, leading to concerns about data security and the accuracy of AI-generated content. Despite these challenges, some brokers are finding AI beneficial for mundane tasks and quick data analysis, while firms like JLL are investing in AI startups to facilitate adoption and innovation.
