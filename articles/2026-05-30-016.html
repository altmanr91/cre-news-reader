Aligned Data Centers plots suburban complex after Phoenix restrictions

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 29 May 2026 16:35:35 +0000

SUMMARY

Aligned Data Centers is planning a two-building data center campus spanning 916,000 square feet on a 95-acre site in suburban Peoria, Arizona. The company acquired the property in October 2024 for $43.2 million. This development follows recent restrictions imposed by the City of Phoenix on data center growth, which led Aligned to seek compensation for a previously planned project in Phoenix.
