Marcus & Millichap brokers sale of 7,295-square-foot retail property in Anna

ACQUISITION

REJournals — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 09:52:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A Dallas-area private partnership has acquired a 7,295-square-foot multi-tenant retail property in Anna, Texas, as part of a 1031 exchange. The all-cash transaction closed in less than 45 days, with the buyer strategically reallocating equity from an alternative property type into triple-net retail assets within high-growth DFW submarkets. The acquisition was part of a two-property portfolio that included an adjacent shopping center.
