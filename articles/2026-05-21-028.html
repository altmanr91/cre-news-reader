Dingle brothers sell waterfront spec mansion for $75M in Boca Raton record

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 20 May 2026 17:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

SRD Building, led by Steve and Scott Dingle, sold a newly constructed waterfront spec mansion in Boca Raton for $75 million, setting a new record for home sales in the city and within the exclusive Royal Palm Yacht & Country Club. The 18,300-square-foot estate, situated on a 0.8-acre corner lot with extensive water frontage, features eight bedrooms and a comprehensive wellness wing. The sale underscores the continued strength of South Florida's ultra-luxury residential market, with the broker noting that buyers at this price point seek premium, completed products.
