OHT Partners plans $48M multifamily project in Farmers Branch

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Texas — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

OHT Partners is planning a new Class A multifamily development, Lenox Alpha, in Farmers Branch, a suburb of Dallas. The project, estimated to cost $48 million, will feature 391 units across five stories and is slated to begin construction in September 2026. The developer acquired the five-acre site in 2022, which is strategically located near the Dallas North Tollway.
