Waterton sells the Grand Central in South Loop for $22M premium over 2023 purchase

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 20:37:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Waterton has sold the Grand Central, a 346-unit apartment complex in Chicago's South Loop, to RPM Living for $103.5 million. This transaction represents a significant increase in value, with the sale price nearly $22 million higher than Waterton's acquisition cost in 2023. The heightened sale value reflects Chicago's strong rent growth and limited new construction, which has encouraged landlords to bring properties to market.
