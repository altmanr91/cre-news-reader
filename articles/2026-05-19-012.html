Cawley Commercial Real Estate closes 244,343-square-foot industrial lease in Joliet

LEASE

REJournals — Tue, 19 May 2026 09:58:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Cawley Commercial Real Estate facilitated a 244,343-square-foot industrial lease for Coda Resources at a newly constructed facility in Joliet, Illinois. The long-term lease, spanning 75 months, provides the tenant with a modern industrial space featuring intermodal access, 50 dock doors, and 36-foot clear height, supporting the company's operational growth and efficiency. The tenant is expected to occupy the space in September 2026.
