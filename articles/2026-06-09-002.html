In Life Sciences Real Estate, Times Are Tough. That Spells Opportunity for Some.

Commercial Observer — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 10:00:54 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

The life sciences real estate market, once booming during the pandemic, is now experiencing a downturn with high vacancy rates, creating a unique opportunity for investors. While tenants are increasingly building their own facilities, investors are acquiring properties at discounted prices, anticipating a market rebound. Despite the perceived stagnation, significant investment activity continues, with venture capital funding in the sector showing strong growth in late 2025. The specialized infrastructure of lab spaces makes conversions to other uses costly, but some experts see potential for repurposing them as data centers due to their robust power and HVAC systems.
