Kayne Anderson crushes goal with $5B real estate fundraise

The Real Deal — National — Tue, 26 May 2026 18:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Capital Raise

Kayne Anderson Real Estate has successfully closed its latest opportunistic real estate fund, Kayne Anderson Real Estate Partners VII LP, raising $5.12 billion. This significantly exceeded its initial $3 billion target and a $4 billion cap, marking the firm's largest real estate fundraise to date. The fund will strategically focus on investments in student housing, healthcare, and industrial properties, particularly last-mile logistics, with executives expressing strong conviction in the long-term demand and limited supply within these sectors.
