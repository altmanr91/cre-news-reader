JLL Capital Markets closes sale of 1.28-acre development site in Dallas

SALE

REJournals — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 14:44:00 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL Capital Markets has successfully brokered the sale of a 1.28-acre development site located at 3601 Cedar Springs in Dallas, TX. The property, sold by Bill Y Bob Partners LLC to KC Turtle Creek Owner LLC, offers significant development flexibility due to its PD 193 (O-2) zoning, allowing for multifamily, office, or hotel uses with a maximum height of 240 feet and a 4.5:1 floor area ratio. The site's prime location in the Turtle Creek/Oak Lawn neighborhood provides excellent access to local amenities and strong area demographics.
