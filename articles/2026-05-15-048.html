Blackstone's Data Center REIT Hits Its $1.75B Initial Target In Debut

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 14 May 2026 12:14:05 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Capital Markets

Blackstone's new data center REIT, Blackstone Digital Infrastructure Trust (BXDC), successfully raised $1.75 billion in its initial public offering on the U.S. stock market. The REIT aims to acquire newly built data centers, primarily occupied by hyperscalers, with individual asset values ranging from $250 million to $1.5 billion. This IPO reflects the growing institutional investor interest in data centers, driven by the artificial intelligence boom and the increasing demand for compute and power capacity.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

More than half of investors surveyed by CBRE plan to increase their data center outlay by at least 10% in 2026, with 55% indicating an even greater increase. Half of these investors are seeking investments in turnkey-wholesale, hyperscale, and powered-shell data centers. JLL Vice Chairman Curt Holcomb noted that the data center industry is in a 'supercycle development process' where demand significantly outstrips supply.
