The Plan: 212 West 72nd Street Goes Modern on Historic Upper West Side

SALE

Commercial Observer — Wed, 20 May 2026 10:00:21 +0000

SUMMARY

Centurion Property Investors is nearing completion of sales at 212 West 72nd Street, a luxury condominium conversion on Manhattan's Upper West Side. The project, which transformed a former rental building, features 106 residences designed by CetraRuddy, with 14 units remaining. The building introduces a modern, curved glass aesthetic to the historic neighborhood, offering unique layouts, high-end finishes, and a suite of amenities.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: 212 West 72nd Street

Address: 212 West 72nd Street, New York City, NY

Units: 106

Notable Features: Modern curved glass tower; residences feature unique layouts, oversized windows, white oak floors, Miele appliances, wine fridges, Waterworks and Kohler fixtures, and Venus White marble floors. Amenities include a landscaped rooftop terrace with outdoor kitchen, fitness center with outdoor meditation space, residents lounge, and children's play area.

Project Notes: Conversion from a rental building. Prices for available condos range from $4.5 million for a three-bedroom to $13.5 million for a penthouse.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Centurion Property Investors

ARCHITECT: CetraRuddy

LEASING AGENT: Douglas Elliman — Ammanda Espinal, Sales Director
