MRC Provides $480M in Financing for Office-to-Residential Conversion Project in Midtown Manhattan

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 19 Jun 2026 13:50:10 +0000

SUMMARY

Madison Realty Capital has provided $480 million in financing for the conversion of a 27-story office building in Midtown Manhattan into a 420-unit multifamily property. The project at 1740 Broadway will feature 238 apartments and 182 condominiums, along with 60,000 square feet of amenities. The financing was arranged by Ackman-Ziff on behalf of Yellowstone Real Estate Investments, with completion anticipated in the third quarter of 2029.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 1740 Broadway, New York City, NY

Units: 420

Loan Amount: $480.0M

Loan Per Unit: $1,142,857/unit (calculated)

Completion: 3Q 2029

Notable Features: The converted building will include 60,000 square feet of amenities, such as a 22,000-square-foot sporting club, a spa with a lap pool, and a speakeasy bar in the original bank vault.

Project Notes: The project involves the conversion of an existing 27-story office building into residential use.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE
