NYC’s top deals: SL Green sheds portions of FiDi building for $223M

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 11:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

SL Green Realty sold the residential and retail components of 7 Dey Street in the Financial District for $222.6 million to GO Residential. SL Green will retain ownership of the office space within the 34-story, 260,000-square-foot building. Additionally, two mixed-use properties at 111 and 113 East 12th Street in the East Village were sold for $23.5 million to an affiliate of AG Paratus by LLCs tied to Maurice Kaufman. These properties include 13 apartments and ground-floor retail.
