First America Homes expands north with Anna project

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Texas — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 13:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

First America Homes, the homebuilding division of The Signorelli Company, has expanded its operations into the Dallas-Fort Worth market, establishing a new divisional office and acquiring 81 homesites within the Leonard Trails development in Anna, TX. This move marks a significant expansion for the company, which has historically focused on the Houston and San Antonio areas. Construction on the new single-family homes, which will range from 1,700 to 3,200 square feet and start in the $300,000s, is slated to begin next month.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: single family

Property Name: Leonard Trails

Address: 912 Fairfield Ave, Anna, TX*

Units: 81

Notable Features: Homes will range from 1,700 to 3,200 square feet with one- and two-story plans, on lots ranging from 30 to 40 feet wide.

Project Notes: Prices will start in the $300,000s. Construction is scheduled to start in July 2026.
