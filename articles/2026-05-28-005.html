Topeka’s Housing Market Builds Momentum as Demand Outpaces Supply

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 28 May 2026 12:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Topeka, Kansas, is experiencing a highly competitive and resilient housing market, driven by strong demand and undersupply. The market is characterized by rapid home sales, with properties selling at or above list price, and a median home price significantly lower than the national average. This demand is fueled by job opportunities, affordability, and regional connectivity, attracting developers to address the housing shortage. Key projects underway include The Annex Group's 250-unit affordable housing development, Union at Tower District, and Flaherty & Collins Properties' 192-unit market-rate apartment community, The Hutch, both aiming to diversify the city's housing stock.
