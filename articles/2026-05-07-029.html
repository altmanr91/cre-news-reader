David MacNeil sells another Fort Lauderdale mansion for $26M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 06 May 2026 20:07:42 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

David MacNeil, founder of WeatherTech, sold a beachfront mansion in Fort Lauderdale, FL for $26 million to Pablo Umansky. This marks the second time MacNeil has sold the property in three years, having previously reacquired it through a foreclosure suit against the prior owner, Jack Owoc, who had financed his initial purchase with a loan from MacNeil. The property features five bedrooms, six bathrooms, four half-bathrooms, a pool, and 100 feet of beachfront.
