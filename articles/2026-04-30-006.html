Signorelli Breaks Ground on 328-Acre Mixed-Use Expansion Project in New Caney, Texas

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 14:50:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Signorelli Co. has commenced construction on the Azalea District, a 328-acre mixed-use expansion within its Valley Ranch master-planned community in New Caney, Texas. The project will feature 359 single-family homes and a 125-acre commercial component primarily dedicated to healthcare, with potential for retail, restaurant, hospitality, and other wellness amenities. The initial phase, comprising 156 single-family homes by First America Homes, is slated for delivery in summer 2026.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: Azalea District at Valley Ranch

Size: 359 units

Phase I: 156 single-family homes

Site Area: 328 acres

Notable Features: Mixed-use expansion within a master-planned community, with a significant healthcare-focused commercial component.

Project Notes: Residential component spans 203 acres; commercial component spans 125 acres.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

DEVELOPER/SPONSOR: Signorelli Co.

GENERAL CONTRACTOR: First America Homes
