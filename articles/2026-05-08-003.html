Consulting Firm Council Advisors Renews 18K SF at BGO’s 685 Third Avenue

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 07 May 2026 20:35:12 +0000

SUMMARY

Council Advisors, an executive advisory firm, has renewed its 17,685-square-foot office lease at 685 Third Avenue in Midtown East for an additional 10 years. The firm has occupied space in the BGO-owned building since 2015. The tenant's broker noted that the building's prime location, strong amenity package, and institutional ownership were key factors in the decision, aligning with the firm's long-term needs.
