Foreclosed Gold Coast Hilton hits market as conversion candidate

The Real Deal — Chicago — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

The Hilton Suites at 198 East Delaware Place in Chicago's Gold Coast, a 30-story, 267,800-square-foot hotel, has been put on the market for sale by LNR Partners, representing bondholders who foreclosed on the property three years ago. The previous owner, Procaccianti, surrendered the hotel via a deed-in-lieu of foreclosure related to nearly $70 million in debt. JLL is marketing the property as a prime candidate for residential conversion, citing existing zoning, or a potential rebranding opportunity, as the Hilton contract expires at the end of 2026.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

CoStar data values the building at $48 million. Chicago's housing market is experiencing a construction pipeline slowdown, leading developers to consider converting unused office space to hotels or residences. The Magnificent Mile is seeing a shift in retail, with more experiential retail and lower-dollar apparel stores emerging alongside luxury brands.
