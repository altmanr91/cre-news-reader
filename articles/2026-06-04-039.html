Investors buy another San Jose office building for nearly $14M

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 21:36:28 +0000

SUMMARY

A group of investors led by Silicon Valley executive Shri Chawla acquired an approximately 58,000-square-foot office building at 110 Baytech Drive in San Jose, CA, for $13.5 million. The property, currently occupied by green tech company Lyten, was sold by an affiliate of Money360, which had foreclosed on the asset in 2024. The purchase price represents a 6% discount from the building's foreclosure valuation.
