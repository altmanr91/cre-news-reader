$46M Bridge Loan Refinances Newly Built Moreno Valley Apartments

REFINANCE

Connect CRE — Tue, 26 May 2026 22:08:40 +0000

SUMMARY

Dwight Mortgage Trust has provided a $46 million bridge loan to Latco Enterprises for the refinancing of Villa Annette, a newly constructed 220-unit garden-style apartment community in Moreno Valley, CA. The financing will be used to retire existing construction debt, cover closing costs, and return equity to the borrower. The property offers convenient access to major regional transportation corridors, connecting residents to key employment centers across Southern California.

DATA POINTS
