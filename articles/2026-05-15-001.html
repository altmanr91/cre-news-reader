UBS Refis Chelsea Office Building With $161M Loan

REFINANCE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 14 May 2026 22:23:49 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Vornado Realty Trust and Aurora Capital Associates has secured a $161 million interest-only loan from UBS to refinance their 194,000-square-foot office and retail building in Chelsea, Manhattan. The new financing replaces a $155 million loan that was set to mature in November 2026. The property, known as the Yext Building, is strategically located across from Chelsea Market and near the Meatpacking District.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Yext Building

Address: 61 Ninth Avenue, New York City, NY

Size: 194,000 SF

Loan Amount: $161.0M

Loan Per SF: $830/SF (calculated)

Notable Features: Located across from Chelsea Market and a block north of Manhattan’s Meatpacking District.

Project Notes: The loan has a maturity date of March 2029 and carries an interest rate of SOFR plus 3% for the first year, SOFR plus 3.35% for the second year, and SOFR plus 3.85% for the remaining term. It replaces a previous $155 million loan with an interest rate of SOFR plus 2.45%.
