Another Bay Area mansion is available for purchase using AI stock

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 19:21:45 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A 2,495-square-foot home in San Francisco's Duboce Triangle is listed for nearly $3 million, with the seller accepting payment in either cash or stock from AI companies Anthropic or OpenAI. The unidentified developer seller is confident in the future of these AI giants, noting the high demand for housing among their employees and investors in the competitive Bay Area market. This offering follows a similar listing in Mill Valley that also sought Anthropic shares as payment.
