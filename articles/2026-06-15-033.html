Investors Say They Were Duped Into Buying Shares Of Firm With $4B Atlanta Development Dreams

Bisnow News Feed — Sun, 14 Jun 2026 18:47:18 -0400

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Investors are alleging that they were defrauded by a consultant raising funds for Webstar Technology Group's ambitious $3.8 billion Forge Atlanta mixed-use development. The investors claim they paid for shares in Webstar but never received proof of purchase or refunds, leading to complaints filed with the FBI, SEC, and other authorities. Webstar's corporate attorney stated that the consultant's actions were private and outside the company's business, while the consultant dismissed the complaints as unfounded. The project, which received preliminary approval for a nearly $10 million tax break from the Fulton County Development Authority, aims to revitalize South Downtown Atlanta with a large-scale mixed-use development, despite Webstar's reported financial struggles and a default on its land loan.
