Two prominent executives are selling their Marin homes, capitalizing on “gnarly” buyer market driven by AI

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Fri, 29 May 2026 15:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Celebrity Residential / Non-CRE

Two prominent executives are selling their personal residences in Marin County, California, capitalizing on a competitive buyer's market fueled by the region's artificial intelligence boom. The properties, an architecturally significant estate in Ross and a modern masterpiece in Tiburon, are listed for just under $13 million and nearly $20 million, respectively. The limited inventory and rising prices in San Francisco are driving homebuyers and investors to seek value in the exclusive northern enclaves, attracting both local families and international buyers.
