Developer Michael Klinger sells waterfront Bal Harbour mansion for $43M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 18 Jun 2026 15:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Developer Michael Klinger, through his firm Saber South Real Estate, has sold a waterfront mansion at 56 Bal Bay Drive in Bal Harbour, FL for nearly $43 million. This transaction marks the highest-priced home sale in the village this year, significantly exceeding Klinger's initial $30 million sales expectation after an extensive renovation of the property, which he acquired in 2022 for $15.4 million. The sale highlights the continued strength and appreciation in South Florida's luxury residential market, attracting buyers from high-tax states.
