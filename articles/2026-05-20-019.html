PMG, partners launch Palm Tree-branded Miami condo project

DEVELOPMENT

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 19 May 2026 16:10:56 +0000

SUMMARY

PMG, Lion Development Group, Eden Residential, and Sterling Equities have partnered to launch Palm Tree Residences Miami, a 37-story, 483-unit condominium project in Miami. This development marks the first branded residence for Palm Tree Crew, the music festival company co-founded by DJ Kygo. The project is designed to be short-term rental friendly, catering to investor demand in the Miami market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: condo

Property Name: Palm Tree Residences Miami

Address: 31 Northwest 10th Street, Miami, FL

Units: 483

Notable Features: First branded residence for Palm Tree Crew; units are fully furnished and short-term rental friendly; amenities include a pool, fitness center, padel court, spa facilities, golf simulator, and juice bar.

Project Notes: Prices for units range from $500,000 to $1.8 million. Palm Tree Crew will operate a dining and nightlife venue on the ground floor.

Miami has seen a recent surge in short-term rental-friendly condo projects, including Forth Development and MICL's Botanic Residences (51 units) and Wave Group Development, Vitrium Capital, and Zambala Luxury Apartments' Duos Wynwood (49 units).
