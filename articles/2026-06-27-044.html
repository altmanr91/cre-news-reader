Casey's Growing Into New Markets With 400-Store Expansion

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 26 Jun 2026 12:19:16 -0400

SUMMARY

Market Research / Retail Expansion

Casey's General Stores, a major convenience store and pizza chain, announced plans to open 400 new stores over the next three years. This expansion will be achieved through a combination of acquisitions and new developments, building on its previous growth of over 500 stores in the last three years, including a significant acquisition in 2024. The company, which owns all its existing locations, will prioritize expanding its food business, particularly made-to-order pizza and chicken wings, and leveraging technology for efficiency.
