Bass Pro Shops rings up Cheeca Lodge resort in Florida Keys

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 17 Jun 2026 21:19:28 +0000

SUMMARY

Bass Pro Shops has acquired the iconic Cheeca Lodge & Spa, a luxury fishing and sporting resort in Islamorada, Florida, from Northwood Investors. The 27-acre property, known for its rich history and amenities, was previously marketed for $300 million. Northwood Hospitality will continue to manage the resort, which has undergone significant upgrades since its acquisition by Northwood Investors in 2011, including the addition of oceanfront suites, luxury casitas, and a conference center.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: Cheeca Lodge & Spa

Address: 81801 Overseas Highway, Islamorada, FL

Keys: 254

Site Area: 27 acres

Notable Features: Luxury fishing and sporting resort with three restaurants, a store, a spa, a kids camp, six tennis courts, and a 525-foot fishing pier. It is one of the most famous fishing resorts in the country.

Project Notes: Northwood Investors previously acquired the property in 2011 and expanded it in 2020 with the acquisition of neighboring Casitas and Morada Bay properties.
