Santana Row offices reach 100% occupancy with 11K sf PNC Bank lease

LEASE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 06 May 2026 21:04:04 +0000

SUMMARY

PNC Bank has signed an 11,000-square-foot lease at the One Santana West building, bringing the entire Santana Row mixed-use district's office space to 100% occupancy. This achievement is particularly notable given the elevated office vacancy rates in the broader Silicon Valley market. The developer, Federal Realty Investment Trust, highlighted the strong leasing momentum, which reflects a broader resurgence in the Silicon Valley office market driven by technology and AI tenants.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Address: 3155 Olsen Drive, San Jose, CA

Size: 11,000 SF

Notable Features: On-site amenities include a fitness center, golf simulator, and pickleball courts.

Project Notes: The One Santana West building spans 375,000 square feet and is part of the larger Santana Row mixed-use district, which now has approximately 1 million square feet of fully leased office space.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The Silicon Valley office market recorded a post-pandemic high of 3.2 million square feet in leasing last quarter, a 1.7-million-square-foot increase from the previous quarter. Downtown San Jose had the highest office availability rate last quarter with 36.2 percent, North San Jose at 24.6 percent, South San Jose at 9.7 percent, and Santa Clara at 19.4 percent.
