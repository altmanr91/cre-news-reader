Inside Prospect Acquisitions’ Investment Strategy

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 04:00:20 +0000

SUMMARY

Prospect Acquisitions, a New York City-based investment firm, specializes in sourcing off-market multifamily and mixed-use properties in prime Manhattan and Brooklyn neighborhoods. The firm's strategy focuses on direct owner outreach and relationships to identify opportunities before they are publicly listed, as exemplified by its recent acquisition of 129 West 85th Street. Prospect's investment philosophy centers on acquiring well-located, value-add assets with potential for renovation, rent increases in free-market units, and operational improvements. The firm launched a $100 million fund in 2024 to pursue these opportunities, with an additional funding round now open due to strong investor demand.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Address: 129 West 85th Street, New York City, NY

Units: 10

Notable Features: Free market multifamily building located on Manhattan's Upper West Side.

Project Notes: Acquired off-market without a formal marketing process or bidding war.
