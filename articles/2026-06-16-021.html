Hawkins Way expands student housing bet with UWS buy

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 19:34:44 +0000

SUMMARY

Hawkins Way Capital has acquired the Stratford Residence Hall, a student housing property on Manhattan's Upper West Side, from AMDA College of the Performing Arts for $80 million. The performing arts college will lease back the 382-unit property for the next 30 years. This transaction marks another significant student housing investment for Hawkins Way Capital in the New York City market, following several other notable acquisitions and dispositions in the sector.
