Monty Bennett To Collect $480M Breakup Fee As Luxury Hotel REIT Goes Independent

Bisnow News Feed — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 12:56:49 -0400

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Braemar Hotels & Resorts, a luxury hotel REIT, is becoming a self-managed entity and terminating its advisory agreements with Monty Bennett's Ashford Inc. This decision follows a contentious and ultimately failed attempt to sell Braemar, which would have resulted in a significant payout to Bennett. To fund the $480 million termination fee owed to Ashford, Braemar is selling three of its hotels, including the Ritz-Carlton in Sarasota, Florida, for $437.5 million, and plans to divest additional assets. The move aims to simplify Braemar's corporate structure, enhance operational efficiency, and create long-term shareholder value, with new independent board members to be appointed.
