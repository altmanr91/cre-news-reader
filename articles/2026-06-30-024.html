Waterfront estate of Palm Beach’s oldest resident sells for $35M months after her death

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 22:42:16 +0000

SUMMARY

The waterfront estate of the late Jane Smith, Palm Beach's oldest resident, has sold for $35 million. The 6,700-square-foot home, marketed as a teardown, was acquired by a trust managed by a West Palm Beach attorney, suggesting an all-cash transaction. The property is expected to be redeveloped, leveraging its direct Intracoastal Waterway frontage and views of the Everglades golf course.
