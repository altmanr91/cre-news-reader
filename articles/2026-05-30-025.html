Wynwood site sells at loss for under $15M to Turkish buyers

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 29 May 2026 20:42:52 +0000

SUMMARY

A Turkish company, Terminal Wynwood LLC, acquired a 0.76-acre mixed-use development site in Miami for $14.5 million in cash. The property, located at the intersection of Wynwood, Midtown, and Edgewater, includes a 23,150-square-foot creative office building. The sale price represents a 23% discount from its last sale in 2022, reflecting the ongoing repricing of development sites due to higher borrowing and construction costs. The buyer plans to develop a transit-oriented, mixed-use tower with apartments, condos, and retail.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Address: 85 Northeast 27th Street, Miami, FL

Size: 23,150 SF

Sale Price: $14.5M

Site Area: 0.76 acres

Notable Features: Site is near the proposed Wynwood Tri-Rail station and sits at the crossroads of Wynwood, Midtown, and Edgewater.

Project Notes: The seller previously acquired the property for $18.75 million in 2022. The existing creative office building housed a coworking concept that struggled to compete with newer office developments.
