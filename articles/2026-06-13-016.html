Blackstone eyeing Canadian REIT acquisition

The Real Deal — National — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 18:10:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Blackstone is reportedly in preliminary, non-exclusive discussions with Canadian REIT H&R for a potential acquisition of either the entire company or certain assets. This follows a previous attempt by a consortium including Blackstone to acquire H&R last year, which ultimately fell through. H&R, which manages over C$8 billion in assets, has been under pressure from an activist hedge fund to consider a sale due to its stock trading at a discount to its real estate value. The REIT has been strategically rebalancing its portfolio, divesting from retail and office properties to focus on multifamily and industrial assets across Canada and the United States.
