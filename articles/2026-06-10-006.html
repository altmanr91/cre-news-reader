Craig International Unveils Plans for $200M Mixed-Use Redevelopment in McKinney, Texas

DEVELOPMENT

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 09 Jun 2026 15:22:16 +0000

SUMMARY

Craig International has announced plans for Rowlett Station, a $200 million mixed-use redevelopment in McKinney, Texas. The project will transform the 58-acre former Globe Life office campus into a vibrant destination featuring multifamily, office, and retail spaces, complemented by walking and biking trails. This significant undertaking aims to revitalize a key site in the northern Dallas suburb.
