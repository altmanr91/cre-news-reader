Miami commission to revisit $80M plan to redevelop neglected Virginia Key marinas

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 08 Jun 2026 18:25:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Development

The Miami City Commission is set to consider an $80 million redevelopment plan for the Virginia Key marinas, which would allow Virginia Key LLC, a joint venture of RCI Marine Group and Suntex Marinas, to lease and modernize the facilities. The proposal, which includes upgraded marina infrastructure, boat storage, restaurants, retail, and public parking, follows a court order for a public referendum after a decade of legal and political disputes. The developers would pay the city a minimum of $2.2 million annually, plus a percentage of gross revenue, under a proposed 45-year lease with two 15-year renewal options.
