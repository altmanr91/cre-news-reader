Showing Up for Community Through the REBNY Fellows Civic Day of Service

Commercial Observer — Tue, 30 Jun 2026 04:00:23 +0000

SUMMARY

Association / Leadership News

The Real Estate Board of New York (REBNY) Fellows Leadership Program recently concluded its sixth cohort with a Civic Day of Service at the Fisher House Foundation in the Bronx. This initiative prepares emerging real estate leaders to understand their civic responsibilities within New York City. The Fellows engaged in hands-on volunteer work, including sprucing up an outdoor space, preparing meals, and assembling toiletry kits for military and veteran families staying at Fisher House while loved ones receive medical care. This service day underscored the program's emphasis on community support and the broader impact of the real estate sector beyond property lines.
