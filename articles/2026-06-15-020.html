Movers & Shakers: Miami broker leaves Compass for Serhant

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Guillermo Freixas, a Miami-based broker, has departed Compass to establish his own team, My Block Group, at Serhant. He will serve as president, with his brother Jose Daniel Freixas overseeing operations and media strategy. Separately, Pittsburgh-based NAI Burns Scalo has acquired Fort Myers-based Commercial Property Southwest, a commercial real estate firm, marking NAI Burns Scalo's first Florida acquisition and expanding its presence along the state's Gulf Coast. Additionally, veteran land planner Frank Arbelaez has joined Concord Crest Real Estate as director of planning, and real estate attorney John Paul Croake has joined Weiss Law as senior counsel.

DATA POINTS

Project Notes: NAI Burns Scalo previously acquired rights to operate across 13 counties along Florida's Gulf Coast in 2025.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: NAI Burns Scalo — Brian Walker, President; Tim Rivers, Florida Executive Managing Director

SELLER: Commercial Property Southwest — Gary Tasman

TENANT REP: Serhant — Guillermo Freixas, President, My Block Group; Jose Daniel Freixas

ARCHITECT: Concord Crest Real Estate — Frank Arbelaez, Director of Planning
