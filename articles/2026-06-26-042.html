Have We Reached The End Of The Bid-Ask Standoff?

Bisnow News Feed — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 13:28:54 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article discusses the apparent end of the bid-ask standoff in commercial real estate, with institutional capital beginning to deploy more actively. Affinius Capital's $3.4 billion take-private acquisition of Veris Residential is highlighted as a significant transaction signaling this shift. A partner at Affinius Capital noted that the deal represents a change in investment thesis, moving multifamily investment back to its core principles of income production, downside protection, and inflation hedging, rather than tactical, opportunistic plays. He also observed that historical market declines similar to recent years have often been followed by periods of strong investor returns.
