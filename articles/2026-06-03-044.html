Old Town Multifamily Property Changes Hands

SALE

Connect CRE — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 21:13:48 +0000

SUMMARY

An eight-unit multifamily property located at 1503 North Wieland Street in Chicago's Old Town neighborhood has been sold for $2.5 million. The transaction was facilitated by Marcus & Millichap, with strong investor interest driven by the property's prime location, desirable unit mix, and rental stability. The buyer, a 1031 exchange investor, sought a well-positioned asset in a supply-constrained submarket.
