CS Ventures scores $22M loan to buy Frisbie properties in Palm Beach

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — Miami — Tue, 02 Jun 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CS Ventures secured a $22 million acquisition loan from Ocean Bank to purchase a portfolio of properties in Palm Beach, FL, from the Frisbie Group. The portfolio includes a 20,000-square-foot office and retail building, an 11,300-square-foot retail building, and three adjacent parking lots totaling 44,000 square feet. This acquisition follows the withdrawal of the seller's redevelopment plans for the site due to anticipated community opposition.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office / retail / parking

Property Name: former IberiaBank building

Address: 180 Royal Palm Way, Palm Beach, FL

Size: 31,300 SF

Loan Amount: $22.0M

Project Notes: The seller and partners previously acquired the properties for $26 million in 2021. Redevelopment plans for six luxury residences and renovations to two existing buildings were withdrawn in March 2026 due to anticipated resident opposition.
