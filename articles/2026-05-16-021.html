SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article summarizes key discussions from The Real Deal's NYC Real Estate Forum, highlighting various industry leaders' perspectives on critical issues. Related Companies CEO Jeff Blau defended New York City's market strength, citing Hudson Yards' success while advocating for tax and regulatory reform. Real estate brokerage leaders Pam Liebman and Bess Freedman debated transparency in listings, with Freedman pushing for industry-wide openness and Liebman supporting seller discretion. Vornado's Glen Weiss reaffirmed the firm's commitment to a major development project, echoing sentiments about political challenges. City officials, including HPD Commissioner Dina Levy and Deputy Mayor for Housing Leila Bozorg, addressed landlord relations and rent regulation, respectively. Bally's chairman Soo Kim discussed the company's successful casino bid in the Bronx, attributing it to strategic planning and local connections.
