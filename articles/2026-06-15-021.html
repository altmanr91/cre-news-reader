South Florida’s top deals: Miami Beach sales dominate priciest trades

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Market Research / Top Deals Roundup

This article is a roundup of the top residential sales in South Florida, with Miami Beach dominating the priciest transactions. The most significant sale involved a 9,200-square-foot waterfront mansion at 16 Palm Avenue, which traded for $25 million. The seller was a commercial real estate investor, and the buyer was an entity linked to a prominent billionaire family and an architect. Another notable transaction was a condo at Faena House, selling for $20 million, or approximately $4,500 per square foot.
