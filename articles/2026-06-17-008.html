Outdoor Wireless Networks Signs 42,362 SF Industrial Lease in Richardson, Texas

LEASE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 15:02:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Outdoor Wireless Networks LLC has secured a 42,362-square-foot industrial lease at Parc North East in Richardson, Texas. This transaction provides the tenant with significant industrial space within a modern, four-building development in a key Dallas suburb. The lease was facilitated by Colliers representing the tenant and Holt Lunsford Commercial representing the landlord.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Parc North East

Size: 42,362 SF

Notable Features: Part of a four-building, 434,640-square-foot industrial development.
