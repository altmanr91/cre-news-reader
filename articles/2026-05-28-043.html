Lee NYC Tapped as Leasing Agent for Retail at Seward Park Cooperative

Connect CRE — Wed, 27 May 2026 22:10:27 +0000

SUMMARY

Leasing Agent Assignment

Lee & Associates NYC has been appointed as the exclusive leasing agent for the retail portfolio at Seward Park Cooperative, a significant residential property in Lower Manhattan. The multi-year assignment involves managing the leasing strategy and representation for approximately 25 retail spaces totaling over 50,000 square feet. This initiative is part of a broader effort by Seward Park Cooperative to modernize and reposition its retail offerings amidst the ongoing evolution of the Lower East Side neighborhood, leveraging the area's dense residential population, strong foot traffic, and transit accessibility.
