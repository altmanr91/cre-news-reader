Ellis Partners, Baupost Group make priciest Silicon Valley office buy so far this year

ACQUISITION

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Tue, 26 May 2026 18:44:30 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between Ellis Partners and Baupost Group has acquired the Great America Commons office complex in Santa Clara, marking the largest office transaction in Silicon Valley so far this year. The two-building, 637,100-square-foot property traded for $310 million. This significant sale suggests a potential shift in the South Bay office market, which has seen a rebound in leasing activity during the first quarter.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: Great America Commons

Address: 4555 and 4655 Great America Parkway, Santa Clara, CA

Size: 637,100 SF

Sale Price: $310.0M

Notable Features: Located next to California's Great America amusement park.

Project Notes: The purchase price was 42.4 percent higher than the assessed value of $217.7 million in January 2025.

MARKET INTELLIGENCE

The sale is the largest office property transaction in Santa Clara County in 2026 and the second-largest in the Bay Area by dollar amount, following the $691.6 million sale of the Transamerica Pyramid in San Francisco in March.
