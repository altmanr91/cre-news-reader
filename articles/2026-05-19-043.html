Coffee, Chicken And Clinics Fuel Chicago's Post-Covid Resurgence

Bisnow News Feed — Mon, 18 May 2026 16:32:58 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Chicago's retail sector has experienced a significant post-pandemic recovery, largely fueled by the robust performance of quick-service restaurants, particularly coffee and chicken concepts, and medical retail. Panelists at a recent summit highlighted a dramatic shift from the challenging conditions during the pandemic, with key shopping corridors now exhibiting tight vacancy rates and a highly competitive leasing environment. Brokers note the difficulty in securing space for new tenants in popular areas due to limited availability and exclusive landlord deals, while also observing a strong entrepreneurial drive among prospective local tenants seeking discounted sublease opportunities.
