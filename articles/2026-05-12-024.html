Maverick blocked from auctioning former Flushing theater site

The Real Deal — New York — Mon, 11 May 2026 18:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

REO / Foreclosure

Maverick Real Estate Partners' attempt to auction the former KRO Keith's Theater site in Flushing, Queens, was halted after the co-founder of owner Xin Development's parent company filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. This move, made just one day before a scheduled foreclosure auction, marks the latest development in a prolonged dispute over the property. Maverick had acquired and increased a pre-development loan on the site, initiating foreclosure proceedings after Xin allegedly defaulted on real estate taxes and debt service payments.
