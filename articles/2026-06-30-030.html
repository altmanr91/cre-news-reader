Compass snags Ottenheimer Group to strengthen North Shore market

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 22:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

The Ottenheimer Group, a residential real estate team led by Scott Ottenheimer, has rejoined Compass Inc., bolstering the brokerage's presence in Chicago's North Shore market. This move, which includes Ottenheimer's eight-member team and new addition Tom Gorges, signifies Compass' continued national expansion and its strategy to attract experienced agents. The North Shore market is characterized by low inventory and high prices, creating a challenging environment for homebuyers, where seasoned agents are particularly valuable.
