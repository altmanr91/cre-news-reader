Moinian Group Secures Quartet of Leases in Midtown South

LEASE

Connect CRE — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 21:58:07 +0000

SUMMARY

The Moinian Group has secured four new leases totaling 70,000 square feet at 60 Madison Avenue in Midtown South. The transactions underscore the building's appeal to innovative and expanding companies seeking premium office space in a vibrant Manhattan business district, according to the landlord.
