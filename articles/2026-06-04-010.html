Marcus & Millichap Brokers Sale of Retail Strip Center in Balch Springs, Texas

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 14:37:31 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of Imperial Square, a retail strip center located in Balch Springs, Texas. The property, built in 2017, features seven suites occupied by service-oriented retailers. The buyer and seller were not disclosed in the transaction.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Imperial Square

Address: 3534 Hickory Tree Rd, Balch Springs, TX*

Year Built: 2017

Notable Features: Comprises seven suites leased to a mix of service-oriented retailers.

* Address sourced separately, not in original article.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Philip Levy; Jonathan Paredes
