Marcus & Millichap brokers sale of waterpark hotel in Wisconsin Dells

SALE

REJournals — Thu, 11 Jun 2026 15:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Marcus & Millichap has brokered the sale of the Grand Marquis Waterpark Hotel & Suites, a 93-room hotel in Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin. The property, which includes extensive indoor and outdoor waterpark facilities, was sold to an experienced hotel operator who plans to implement new revenue management and operational strategies to improve performance.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: Grand Marquis Waterpark Hotel & Suites

Address: 840 Wisconsin Dells Parkway South, Wisconsin Dells, WI

Keys: 93

Year Built: 1994

Site Area: 3.19 acres

Notable Features: Features an expansive indoor and outdoor waterpark with 10 water slides, family-style suites, meeting and event space, an indoor hot tub, sauna, and heated outdoor pool.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER BROKER: Marcus & Millichap — Jon Ruzicka; Raj Patel

BUYER: Experienced Hotel Operator
