LA Condo Property Sells to Its Original Developer

SALE

Connect CRE — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 20:56:14 +0000

SUMMARY

A 48-unit condominium property in Los Angeles's Valley Village neighborhood has been sold to its original developer. The transaction, brokered by Marcus & Millichap, saw the condominiums trade for an average price of $331,250 per unit. The property's individually parceled units offer the buyer flexibility for various exit strategies in a highly sought-after residential market.
