JLL Negotiates Sale of Retail Strip Center Portfolio in Dallas-Fort Worth, Waco

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Tue, 16 Jun 2026 15:04:33 +0000

SUMMARY

JLL has brokered the sale of a five-property retail strip center portfolio located across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex and Waco. N3 Real Estate sold the assets to Curbline Properties Corp. The transaction highlights continued investor interest in well-located retail properties within Texas markets.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SELLER: N3 Real Estate

BUYER: Curbline Properties Corp.
