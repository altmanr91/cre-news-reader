Rishi Kapoor admits to stealing $85M from investors, vows to make it his “life’s work” to repay them

The Real Deal — Miami — Fri, 15 May 2026 20:45:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Rishi Kapoor, founder of Location Ventures, has admitted to misappropriating $85 million from investors, including siphoning funds for personal use, as part of a plea deal with federal prosecutors. He pleaded guilty to two charges of money laundering and payroll tax evasion conspiracy. The scandal, which began three years ago, involved Kapoor unlawfully using deposits from pre-construction condo unit buyers and paying himself millions without shareholder approval. A court-appointed receiver is currently selling Location Ventures' assets to repay creditors.
