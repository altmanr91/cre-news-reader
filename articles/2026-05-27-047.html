Institutional Investors Are Following Each Other Back Into The Office Market

Bisnow News Feed — Tue, 26 May 2026 15:51:03 -0400

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

Institutional investors and large publicly traded companies are re-entering the office market, driving a significant increase in investment sales volume. This shift is attributed to stabilizing fundamentals, including improved return-to-work efforts, increased leasing activity, and rising rents in trophy office spaces, making the asset class more appealing to risk-averse investment committees. The renewed confidence from major players like SL Green and Vornado is encouraging other institutional investors to follow suit, leading to both acquisitions and new trophy office developments in key markets.
