Lease roundup: SPOT Market grocery/cafe launches in Fort Lauderdale, Davie

LEASE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 11 May 2026 13:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Affiliated Development secured a 4,000-square-foot lease with SPOT Market, a new grocery and cafe concept, at its under-construction mixed-income apartment project, The Era, in Fort Lauderdale. The retail space, which includes an outdoor patio, is expected to open next year following the project's completion in the first quarter. Meanwhile, 13th Floor Investments fully pre-leased the retail component of its Davie South mixed-use development in Davie, securing leases with Haraz Coffee House, a Vietnamese restaurant, Dumpling X, and another SPOT Market location. These leases highlight continued retail and food and beverage expansion in South Florida's growing mixed-use developments.
