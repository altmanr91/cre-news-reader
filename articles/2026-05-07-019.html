Starwood faces default on $265M hotel portfolio loan

LOAN

The Real Deal — National — Wed, 06 May 2026 18:52:35 +0000

SUMMARY

A $265 million commercial mortgage-backed securities loan secured by a 22-property hotel portfolio owned by an affiliate of Starwood Capital Group has been transferred to special servicing due to an imminent monetary default. The portfolio, comprising 2,943 keys across 12 states, has seen its financial performance significantly deteriorate, with a debt service coverage ratio of 0.64 and occupancy at 63 percent. Starwood has submitted a modification proposal to the noteholders to address the distress.
