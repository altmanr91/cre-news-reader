Walmart Supercenter Opens 200,000 SF Store Within Shawnee Trail in Celina, Texas

CONSTRUCTION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 04 May 2026 14:49:59 +0000

SUMMARY

Walmart has opened a new 200,000-square-foot Supercenter in Celina, Texas, within the 150-acre Shawnee Trail mixed-use development. This opening marks a significant retail anchor for the project, which is a collaboration between Trademark, Glendenning 1887, and the Celina Economic Development Corp. The broader development's first phase, including additional retail, dining, entertainment, and public spaces, is slated to begin construction next year.
