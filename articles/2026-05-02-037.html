Logistics Firm Signs for 156K SF with CIM Group

LEASE

Connect CRE — Fri, 01 May 2026 20:20:52 +0000

SUMMARY

A logistics firm has signed a 68-month lease for a 156,096-square-foot industrial property in Fullerton, CA. The lease, valued at over $13.6 million, facilitates the company's relocation and expansion from its previous Gardena location, consolidating operations to enhance efficiency and support continued growth. The new facility provides the necessary scale and functionality for the firm's next growth phase.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 691-701 Burning Tree Rd., Fullerton, CA

Size: 156,096 SF

Notable Features: The facility includes 11,575 square feet of office space, 134 parking spaces, 12 dock-high loading positions, four ground-level doors, 28-foot clear height, and an expansive truck court.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

TENANT: Columbia Container Lines LAX, Inc.

LANDLORD: CIM Group
