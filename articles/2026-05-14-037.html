VC investor’s Pac Heights mansion sells above asking days after listing

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 13 May 2026 23:32:19 +0000

SUMMARY

A modern mansion in San Francisco's Pacific Heights neighborhood, owned by retired venture capitalist Mark Perry and his wife Mauree Jane Perry, sold for $24 million, exceeding its asking price. The property, known as "the Perry House," was on the market for only a few weeks before closing. The buyer is an undisclosed entity, 2606 Jackson LLC. This transaction reflects a broader trend of rebounding luxury home sales in San Francisco, driven by affluent buyers, particularly those benefiting from the artificial intelligence sector.
