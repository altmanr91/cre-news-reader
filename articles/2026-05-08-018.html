Compass stock surges 30% post earnings

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 07 May 2026 13:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Compass' stock surged by 30% following its first earnings call as a holding company after acquiring Anywhere Real Estate, which includes brands like Corcoran, Coldwell Banker, and Century 21. The company reported a surprise profit of $22 million and revenue of $2.7 billion, both exceeding analyst expectations. Investors were particularly enthusiastic about Compass' increased projection for cost savings, now targeting $200 million in 2026, double its initial estimate. CEO Robert Reffkin also highlighted strategic partnerships with Redfin and Midwest Real Estate Data to enhance listing access and differentiate its offerings.
