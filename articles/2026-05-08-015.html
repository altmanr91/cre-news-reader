Critical Philly development site in Market East up for grabs

The Real Deal — National — Thu, 07 May 2026 20:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

A critical development site in Philadelphia's Market East neighborhood, located at 1301 Market Street, has been listed for sale by the estate of Samuel Rappaport with an asking price of $27.5 million. The surface parking lot offers favorable zoning for either commercial or high-density residential development and currently generates $800,000 in annual revenue. The property is seen as a cornerstone for the revitalization of the Market East corridor, an area the city is keen to improve.
