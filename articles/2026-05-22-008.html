Eastham Capital Sells 194-Unit Apartment Complex in Southeast Houston

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Thu, 21 May 2026 14:10:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Eastham Capital has sold University Green Apartments, a 194-unit multifamily complex in southeast Houston. The property was approximately 97 percent occupied at the time of sale. Eastham Capital had acquired the asset in 2018 in a joint venture with Mosaic Residential and implemented capital improvements during its ownership.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: University Green Apartments

Address: 1620 Bay Area Blvd, Houston, TX*

Units: 194

Occupancy at Sale: 97.0%

Notable Features: Amenities include a pool, fitness center, and a dog park.

Project Notes: Eastham Capital acquired the asset in 2018 in a joint venture with Mosaic Residential and subsequently implemented capital improvements.
