Will Becerra, Hilton bring real estate backers to November runoff?

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 01:46:25 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Political & Crime News

This article discusses the California gubernatorial race, focusing on which candidate the real estate industry is backing. While their initial preferred candidate, Matt Mahan, did not advance, Republican Steve Hilton secured a spot in the November runoff against Democrat Xavier Becerra. The article highlights the real estate donors who have supported Hilton and notes the significant financial backing Becerra has received from the California Association of Realtors. The central question is whether Mahan's former real estate industry supporters will now shift their contributions to either Hilton or Becerra, or refrain from further political spending.

DATA POINTS

Xavier Becerra campaign funding from California Real Estate Independent Expenditure Committee – California Association of Realtors: $2.8 million

Matt Mahan primary vote share: 4 percent
