Guardian Acquires 332-Unit Ladd Tower Apartment Building in Downtown Portland

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 15 May 2026 13:30:15 +0000

SUMMARY

Guardian has acquired Ladd Tower, a 332-unit Class A apartment building in downtown Portland, from an undisclosed seller. The property's prime location adjacent to the South Park Blocks and its proximity to Portland State University, public transit, and major employers were key attractions for the buyer.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Ladd Tower

Address: 1300 S.W. Park Ave., Portland, OR

Units: 332

Notable Features: 23-story Class A apartment building adjacent to the South Park Blocks and within walking distance of Portland State University, public transit, and major downtown employers.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: Guardian

SELLER BROKER: CBRE — Josh McDonald; Joe Nydahl
