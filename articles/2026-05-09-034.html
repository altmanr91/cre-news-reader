Skanska’s long-awaited Montrose tower pegged at $110M-plus

CONSTRUCTION

The Real Deal — Texas — Fri, 08 May 2026 19:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Skanska USA is moving forward with its long-anticipated mixed-use development in Montrose, with state construction records indicating a total project cost exceeding $110 million for the multifamily component. The 31-story apartment tower, named Starling, is slated to break ground in October and deliver in early 2029. The project will also include 30,000 square feet of retail space, aiming to enhance the increasingly walkable and upscale character of the Lower Westheimer corridor.

Notable Features: 31-story apartment tower with 30,000 square feet of retail space, including multiple patio areas for restaurant and bar tenants.

Project Notes: The 2.9-acre site was previously acquired by Skanska in 2020 for $27 million, replacing a low-slung retail center. The project's core and shell are projected to cost $66.8 million, with an additional $44.2 million for interior buildout.
