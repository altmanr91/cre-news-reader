Machinery Company Acquires Suburban Chicago Office, Warehouse Space

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Wed, 20 May 2026 21:00:14 +0000

SUMMARY

WSI Machinery, a provider of woodworking machinery and supplies, has acquired a 28,300-square-foot industrial building in Wauconda, Illinois. The company plans to renovate both the office and warehouse spaces to support its continued growth and operational expansion. This acquisition also provides future expansion capability through the opportunity to lease a secondary unit.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 280-290 Industrial Drive, Wauconda, IL

Size: 28,300 SF

Notable Features: Free-standing facility with 19-foot clear height, two exterior docks, two drive-in doors (12’ x 14’), and a triple catch basin system.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

BUYER: WSI Machinery

BUYER BROKER: Entre Commercial Realty — Elisabeth Lazzara; Mike DeSerto; Cory Kay

SELLER BROKER: Premier Commercial Realty — Bruce Kaplan
