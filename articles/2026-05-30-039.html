Koeppel Rosen Negotiates Five Leases in Midtown South

LEASE

Connect CRE — Fri, 29 May 2026 22:14:14 +0000

SUMMARY

Koeppel Rosen LLC, acting as the leasing and management agent for the Rosen family portfolio, successfully negotiated five new office leases totaling 15,474 square feet at 1261 Broadway in Manhattan's Midtown South. The transactions involved a diverse group of tenants, including law firms, a real estate appraisal company, a wholesale jewelry distributor, and an investment management firm. Max Koeppel, director of leasing at Koeppel Rosen, noted that the building's efficient workspace, accessibility, and strong value proposition are attracting a variety of tenants to the Midtown South market.
