NewMark Merrill’s Future San Bernardino County Shopping Hub Signs 11 Tenants

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Thu, 04 Jun 2026 22:55:28 +0000

SUMMARY

NewMark Merrill Companies, in a joint venture with World Premier Investments and RY Properties, has secured 11 new leases for Desert Sky Plaza II, a 297,363-square-foot shopping center in Victorville, CA. The project, which is the second phase of the existing Desert Sky Plaza, is nearly 85% pre-leased ahead of its construction start next month. The strong tenant demand reflects the growth opportunities for retailers in Victorville and the broader high desert region of Southern California, driven by affordable housing and convenient access.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Desert Sky Plaza II

Size: 297,363 SF

Current Occupancy: 85.0%

Site Area: 30 acres

Notable Features: Second phase of the existing Home Depot-anchored Desert Sky Plaza, part of the 100-acre commercial development known as The Desert Plazas.

Project Notes: Construction is scheduled to begin next month.
