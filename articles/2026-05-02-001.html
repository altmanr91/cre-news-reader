AI Firm Sierra Signs 94K-SF Deal at Rockrose’s 11 East 26th Street

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 01 May 2026 19:52:03 +0000

SUMMARY

An artificial intelligence firm has signed a 94,145-square-foot lease at 11 East 26th Street in Midtown South, marking its second office location in New York City. The deal was one of the largest office leases in Manhattan in April, reflecting continued demand for prime office space.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: office

Property Name: 11 East 26th Street

Address: 11 East 26th Street, New York City, NY

Size: 94,145 SF

Notable Features: 22-story office building located between Madison and Fifth avenues.

Project Notes: Asking rent in the building was $145 per square foot in February.
