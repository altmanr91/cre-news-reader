Zillow takes Compass, Chicago-area MLS to federal court over private listings

The Real Deal — Chicago — Tue, 12 May 2026 17:20:35 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Zillow has filed a federal antitrust lawsuit against Midwest Real Estate Data (MRED) and Compass International Holdings, alleging a conspiracy to restrict Zillow's access to listings and force it to display properties according to Compass' private marketing plan. The lawsuit, filed in the Northern District of Illinois, claims MRED and Compass have taken actions to choke off Zillow's listing access, including MRED's demand for Zillow to reinstate Compass private listings and Compass' termination of direct listing feed agreements. Zillow argues that MRED's partnership with Compass distorts the MLS's role as a neutral marketplace, ultimately harming consumers and competition.
