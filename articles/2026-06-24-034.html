HGI scores $124M refi deal for one of Irving’s largest apartment complexes

REFINANCE

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 18:20:53 +0000

SUMMARY

Harbor Group International has secured a $124.7 million refinancing loan for Alesio Urban Center, a large apartment complex in Irving, TX. The financing, provided by Blackstone, will be used to retire the existing mortgage and cover closing costs for the 908-unit property. This marks the third financing event for the property since Harbor Group International acquired it in 2021, demonstrating ongoing capital management for the asset.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: multifamily

Property Name: Alesio Urban Center

Address: 385 East Las Colinas Boulevard, Irving, TX

Units: 908

Loan Amount: $124.7M

Loan Per Buildable Unit: $137,335/buildable unit (calculated)

Loan/Acre: $7,335,294/acre (calculated)

Loan/Land SF: $168.40/land SF (calculated)

Site Area: 17 acres

Notable Features: Property includes 14 residential buildings and 23 commercial buildings with retail space, five swimming pools, a gym, yoga studio, conference facilities, and a running trail.

Project Notes: Property was built in 1987 and 1995. Harbor Group International previously acquired the property in 2021 with a $117 million loan and refinanced it in 2023 with a $122 million fixed-rate loan while conducting $4.5 million in renovations.
