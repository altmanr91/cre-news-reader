Coral Gables’ possible medical office buy precedes historic City Hall renovations

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 27 May 2026 18:00:03 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Government Use

The City of Coral Gables is considering the acquisition of the Biltmore Medical Center, a 55,000-square-foot medical office building, from Georgia-based ShareMD. This potential purchase comes as the city prepares for a $25 million to $30 million renovation of its historic City Hall, located nearby. The city is exploring using the medical office building to house various departments, including development services, parking, and human resources, to address its need for additional space during and after the City Hall renovations.
