SakeBrooklyn to Open 7K-SF Brewery at Property Markets Group’s Society Brooklyn

LEASE

Commercial Observer — Fri, 15 May 2026 17:58:36 +0000

SUMMARY

SakeBrooklyn has leased 6,500 square feet for a new brewery and taproom at Society Brooklyn, a mixed-use multifamily development in Gowanus, Brooklyn. The new location at 500 Degraw Street will feature a full-scale brewing operation and a public-facing taproom, adding to the growing retail landscape along the Gowanus Canal. The tenant's broker noted that the increasing residential density in Gowanus is driving demand for thoughtful retail activations that create destinations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: retail

Property Name: Society Brooklyn

Address: 500 Degraw Street, New York City, NY

Size: 6,500 SF

Completion: early 2027

Notable Features: Located on the northern corner of a 605,000-square-foot multifamily development with 517 apartments, including market-rate and affordable housing units, and ground-floor retail spaces along Bond, Union, and Degraw streets, with a public plaza along the Gowanus Canal.
