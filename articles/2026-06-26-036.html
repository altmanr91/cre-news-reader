Two more Presidio Heights homes reach $10M range as luxury supply dwindles

SALE

The Real Deal — San Francisco — Thu, 25 Jun 2026 13:50:15 +0000

SUMMARY

Two luxury homes in San Francisco's Presidio Heights neighborhood recently sold for $9.2 million and $10 million, respectively, highlighting the dwindling supply of high-end housing in the city. The sales reflect the significant wealth being generated by the artificial intelligence industry, attracting affluent buyers to the area. The scarcity of mansions in San Francisco, coupled with anticipated IPOs from major AI companies, is contributing to a competitive luxury market.
