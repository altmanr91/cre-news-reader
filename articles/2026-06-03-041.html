Kayne Anderson, BKM Acquire $1.8B Portfolio in Largest Light Industrial Deal Since 2022

ACQUISITION

Connect CRE — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 13:00:34 +0000

SUMMARY

A joint venture between BKM Capital Partners and Kayne Anderson Real Estate has acquired an 8.5-million-square-foot light industrial portfolio from Link Logistics for $1.81 billion. This transaction, reportedly the largest light industrial deal since 2022, significantly expands the joint venture's presence in key U.S. markets, bringing their total managed portfolio to 15 million square feet. The acquisition includes 51 multi-tenant properties across California, Washington, Texas, and Georgia, along with a dedicated operating infrastructure and 40 employees. The buyers plan to implement targeted upgrades and reconfigurations to the properties.
