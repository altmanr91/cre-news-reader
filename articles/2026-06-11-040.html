Vornado Brings Redeveloped PENN 2 to 90% Leased

LEASE

Connect CRE — Wed, 10 Jun 2026 21:30:56 +0000

SUMMARY

Vornado Realty Trust has achieved 90% occupancy at its redeveloped PENN 2 office tower in New York City's Penn District, following two new full-floor lease agreements. Altana AI signed a 10-year lease for 62,309 square feet on the 21st floor, establishing its new headquarters, while Veeva committed to a 12-year lease for 62,223 square feet on the 11th floor. The successful leasing reflects Vornado's strategy to transform the legacy building into a modern, world-class office environment, leveraging its prime location above a major transportation hub.
