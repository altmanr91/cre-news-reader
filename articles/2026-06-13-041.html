Pet Food Manufacturer Signs 163K-SF Lease in Ontario

LEASE

Connect CRE — Fri, 12 Jun 2026 20:48:37 +0000

SUMMARY

Hoa’s Global Pet Nutrition Inc. has signed a 163,336-square-foot industrial lease at 1930 S. Parco Ave. in Ontario, CA. The facility's unique features, including heavy power, a Class 8 Clean Room, and strategic access to freeways and airports, were key factors in securing the deal. This lease supports the tenant's expansion plans and highlights the property's versatility for various industrial operations in the Inland Empire market.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 1930 S. Parco Ave., Ontario, CA

Size: 163,336 SF

Site Area: 8.21 acres

Notable Features: M2-zoned property with heavy power, fully fenced perimeter, fully built-out office space, conference rooms, training room, and a 10,000-square-foot Class 8 Clean Room. Offers flexibility for warehousing, distribution, manufacturing, and logistics.
