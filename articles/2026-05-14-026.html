Rishi Kapoor’s former right hand Daniel Motha faces criminal conspiracy charge

The Real Deal — Miami — Wed, 13 May 2026 14:30:42 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

Daniel Motha, former CFO of the defunct real estate development firm Location Ventures, has been charged with felony conspiracy for allegedly assisting Rishi Kapoor in concealing and diverting over $1.3 million in payroll taxes owed to the IRS. Motha, a former college friend and business partner of Kapoor, is accused of making false representations to investors and lenders, altering accounting software, and diverting corporate funds for bonuses and profit distributions instead of paying taxes. This criminal charge follows a civil lawsuit filed by the SEC last year, which Motha settled without admitting wrongdoing, regarding his alleged role in a $93 million real estate investment fraud scheme.
