Josh Flagg’s family firm looking to flip Soho retail property for $12M

The Real Deal — New York — Fri, 15 May 2026 20:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Property Listing / For Sale

Flagg Family Capital, the investment firm owned by the family of residential broker Josh Flagg, is marketing a renovated retail property in Soho for just under $12 million. The firm, in partnership with Andrew Aryeh and David Elbaz, acquired three floors of retail space at 170 Mercer Street less than two years ago for an undisclosed price, though public records indicate prior transactions totaling $4 million. The property has been redeveloped into a two-story retail space with high ceilings and is fully leased to a fashion house on a 10-year term with annual escalations.
