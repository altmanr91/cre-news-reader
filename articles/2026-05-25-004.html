South Florida Dirt: Miami jury sends a $48M message with commission suit verdict

The Real Deal — Miami — Sun, 24 May 2026 17:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

CRE Legal / Regulatory

A Miami-Dade County jury awarded a real estate broker $47.8 million in damages, including punitive and compensatory damages, after finding that his client, a jewelry store owner, cheated him out of an $84,000 commission on a $2.8 million home sale. The broker had negotiated an offer for the Golden Beach waterfront home, but the client later claimed disinterest before secretly purchasing the property and paying his sister a nominal commission as a straw broker. The verdict, which is currently being challenged as excessive, is seen by many in the South Florida real estate community as a strong message against clients attempting to circumvent broker commissions.
