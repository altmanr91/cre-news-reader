Capstone Equities Acquires 140 Crosby Street for $51M

ACQUISITION

Commercial Observer — Tue, 26 May 2026 19:28:34 +0000

SUMMARY

Capstone Equities, through its entity CE 140 Crosby Owner, has acquired the 35,000-square-foot office and retail building at 140 Crosby Street in SoHo, New York City, for $51.4 million. The acquisition follows Capstone's foreclosure proceedings against the previous co-owners, Madison Capital and Vornado Realty Trust, who had defaulted on the property's $75 million mortgage debt. Capstone had acquired the defaulted mortgage in July of last year, and the transaction was finalized this month with new financing from Prime Finance.
