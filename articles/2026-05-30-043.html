Tuxedo Reserve Raises Roof on Destination Food Market’s Anchor

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Fri, 29 May 2026 21:22:57 +0000

SUMMARY

Related Companies and Lennar have achieved a significant construction milestone with the "raising of the Barn" at Market Square, which will serve as the anchor for a destination food market within The Village at Tuxedo Reserve. This custom post-and-beam structure is part of a larger 1,200-acre mixed-use downtown district in Tuxedo, NY, designed to offer shops, restaurants, luxury rental residences, and experiential gathering spaces. The development aims to create a unique Hudson Valley destination, providing both leisure opportunities and everyday conveniences that have been lacking in the area.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: mixed-use

Property Name: The Barn at Market Square / The Village at Tuxedo Reserve

Completion: Fall 2026

Site Area: 1200 acres

Notable Features: Custom post-and-beam structure anchoring a destination food market.

Project Notes: The Village at Tuxedo Reserve is the heart of the larger Tuxedo Reserve mixed-use district. The first hamlet, West Terrace, is now selling.
