$85M Manalapan mansion with tunnel to the ocean finds buyer

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Thu, 21 May 2026 21:03:13 +0000

SUMMARY

A sprawling lake-to-ocean estate in Manalapan, featuring a unique tunnel to the beach, has found a buyer. The 22,900-square-foot mansion, owned by Cindy and Ron McMackin of Pan-Pacific Mechanical, was built in 2020 and includes seven bedrooms and 11.5 bathrooms. The property was listed for $85 million and is notable for its direct, air-conditioned tunnel access to the ocean, a rare feature in the area.
