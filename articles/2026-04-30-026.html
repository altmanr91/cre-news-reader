Alvarez & Marsal gives up third distressed Chicago office in a month

SALE

Chicago Real Estate News - The Real Deal — Wed, 29 Apr 2026 22:15:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Alvarez & Marsal's real estate investment arm has divested three distressed office properties in Chicago this month, highlighting the ongoing challenges in the downtown office market. The firm relinquished 205 West Wacker Drive to lender A10 Capital via a deed in lieu of foreclosure, with the property having been only 43% leased. Separately, Alvarez & Marsal sold 542 South Dearborn Street and 459 West Erie Street to a joint venture of Concord Capital and QMR Capital Partners for $8.5 million, a significant discount from its 2018 purchase price, with the buyers planning an office-to-residential conversion.
