Ambrose Breaks Ground on 16-Acre Indianapolis Industrial Park

CONSTRUCTION

Connect CRE — Wed, 03 Jun 2026 20:25:38 +0000

SUMMARY

Ambrose has commenced construction on Indianapolis Logistics Park – South, a two-building speculative industrial development spanning 16 acres in Greenwood, Indiana. The park will feature a 105,000-square-foot building and a 150,000-square-foot building, both designed with 32-foot clear heights to meet the growing demand for modern logistics, distribution, and manufacturing space in the Indianapolis market. The project is being financed through a construction loan from Pinnacle Bank.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Property Name: Indianapolis Logistics Park – South

Size: 255,000 SF

Site Area: 16 acres

Notable Features: Two speculative buildings with 32-foot clear heights; Building I includes 2,900 SF office, Building II includes 3,200 SF office. Approximately 25 minutes from Indianapolis International Airport and the world's second-largest FedEx hub.
