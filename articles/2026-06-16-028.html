Highland Park lakefront mansion fetches highest price since Jordan estate

SALE

The Real Deal — Chicago — Mon, 15 Jun 2026 19:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

A lakefront residence in Highland Park, IL, was sold by Jerry and Naomi Senser for $6.5 million, marking the highest sale price in the suburb since Michael Jordan's estate traded. The 9,750-square-foot home, situated on 1.18 acres, features a private beach, boathouse, and deck, with significant restoration work completed on the shoreline. The property was on the market for approximately one year, closing at 74% of its initial asking price.
