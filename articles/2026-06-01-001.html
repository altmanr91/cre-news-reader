SL Green to Sell 10 East 53rd Street to Meadow Partners for $312M

SALE

Commercial Observer — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 13:49:39 +0000

SUMMARY

SL Green Realty is selling 10 East 53rd Street, a 37-story Midtown East office tower, to Meadow Partners for $312.2 million. The transaction, expected to close in Q3 2026, is part of SL Green's strategic disposition plan and will generate approximately $100 million in net cash proceeds for corporate debt repayment. SL Green will continue to manage the 390,000-square-foot Class A asset, which is currently 92% leased.
