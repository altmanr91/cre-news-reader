Ryan Serhant lassos the Lone Star State with four Texas offices

The Real Deal — Texas — Tue, 23 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Business News

Ryan Serhant's eponymous brokerage is expanding its operations into Texas, opening four new offices across Houston, Dallas, Austin, and San Antonio. This move marks a significant growth phase for the firm, which launched in 2020 and began its national expansion in 2023. The company aims to leverage its proprietary AI platform, S.MPLE, and a founding team of experienced agents to establish a strong presence in the competitive Texas luxury real estate market, which is benefiting from corporate relocations and a growing luxury condo sector.
