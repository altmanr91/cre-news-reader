Hunter Advisors Arranges Sale of 136-Room Hotel in Irving

SALE

REBusinessOnline — Wed, 06 May 2026 14:40:52 +0000

SUMMARY

Hunter Advisors has arranged the sale of the 136-room Aloft Hotel Las Colinas in Irving, Texas. The buyer, whose identity was not disclosed, plans to implement capital improvements to the property, which was originally built in 2008.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: hotel

Property Name: Aloft Hotel Las Colinas

Keys: 136

Year Built: 2008

Notable Features: Amenities include an outdoor pool, fitness center, business center, meeting space, and an onsite bar and restaurant.
