David Edelstein pays $26M for waterfront home in Miami Beach

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 11 May 2026 22:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Developer David Edelstein, a principal at TriCap, has acquired a waterfront home in Miami Beach for $25.5 million. The 4,500-square-foot Mid-Century house, built in 1951 on a 0.6-acre lot, was sold by Richard and Barbara Lane, who had purchased the property in 2020 for $9 million. This transaction marks Edelstein's first personal residence in Miami, despite his extensive real estate development history in South Florida.
