South Florida’s top deals: Wellington estate trades for $17M

SALE

The Real Deal — Miami — Mon, 01 Jun 2026 12:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

This article is a roundup of top real estate transactions in South Florida. The top commercial deal was the sale of a 207,000-square-foot truck stop in Medley for $23.6 million. Additionally, another truck stop in Hialeah Gardens traded for $20 million, and a portfolio of industrial properties and vacant land in Fort Lauderdale sold for $11.3 million. The article also notes a trend in residential mortgage activity, with refinancing originations surpassing new purchase loans for the second consecutive quarter.
