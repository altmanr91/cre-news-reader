William Passo, Passco Companies Founder, Passes Away at 84

Connect CRE — Mon, 22 Jun 2026 13:12:08 +0000

SUMMARY

Non-CRE / Obituary

William "Bill" O. Passo, founder and CEO of Passco Companies, passed away at the age of 84. Passo was a real estate investor for five decades, specializing in real estate syndications and making his first multifamily investment in 1976. He founded Passco Companies in 1998 and was a pioneer in the tenant-in-common (TIC) investment structure, which he presented to the IRS, leading to its validation in 2002. He also played a significant role in developing the Delaware Statutory Trust (DST) market, directing the formation of over 200 investment programs and the acquisition, management, and disposition of more than 250 retail, office, multifamily, and land properties nationwide, raising over $1.4 billion in investor funds.
