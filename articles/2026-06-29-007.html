Cushman & Wakefield Arranges Financing for IOS Facility in South Plainfield, New Jersey

ACQUISITION

REBusinessOnline — Mon, 29 Jun 2026 13:00:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Cushman & Wakefield has arranged acquisition financing and joint venture equity for Ridgecut Road to acquire an 8-acre industrial outdoor storage (IOS) facility in South Plainfield, New Jersey. The property includes two maintenance facilities totaling 30,000 square feet. This transaction highlights continued investor interest in the IOS sector, driven by its critical role in logistics and supply chain operations.

DATA POINTS

Property Type: industrial

Address: 200 Saint Nicholas Ave, South Plainfield, NJ

Size: 30,000 SF

Site Area: 8 acres

Notable Features: Industrial outdoor storage (IOS) facility with two maintenance buildings.

Project Notes: Acquisition financing and joint venture equity were arranged.

COMPANIES/PEOPLE

SPONSOR: Ridgecut Road

MORTGAGE BROKER: Cushman & Wakefield — John Alascio; T.J. Sullivan; Chris Meloni
