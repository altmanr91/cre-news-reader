“We have done nothing wrong”: Texas judge sides with developer of Muslim-centric neighborhood

Texas - The Real Deal — Thu, 30 Apr 2026 21:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

Development / Legal Dispute

Community Capital Partners secured a legal victory in its ongoing dispute with the Texas Workforce Commission regarding The Meadow, a master-planned community. A Travis County judge issued an injunction compelling the agency to review the project's updated housing policies, upholding a previous settlement. This ruling marks another step forward for the developer, who has faced accusations of discrimination from state and federal politicians, despite previous inquiries by the U.S. Department of Justice and the Texas Workforce Commission being closed.
