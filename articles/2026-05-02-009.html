Northmarq Arranges $20M in Financing for Castle Rock Industrial at The Meadows in Colorado

LOAN

REBusinessOnline — Fri, 01 May 2026 14:09:04 +0000

SUMMARY

Northmarq has secured $20 million in permanent-fixed financing for Castle Rock Industrial at The Meadows, an industrial distribution and warehouse facility in Castle Rock, Colorado. The financing, arranged through an insurance company lender, features a seven-year term with 3.5 years of interest-only payments and a 30-year amortization schedule. The property, built in 2023, is fully occupied under triple-net leases and was part of a 1031 exchange for the borrower.
