The Daily Dirt: Waiting for 485x reform? Mamdani admin says wait longer

SALE

The Real Deal — New York — Sat, 09 May 2026 11:30:00 +0000

SUMMARY

New York City officials indicate that reforms to the 485x tax abatement program, particularly regarding construction wage standards, are not imminent. Despite calls from both developers seeking weaker wage requirements and labor unions advocating for stronger ones, the Mamdani administration believes the 10-year program needs more time to mature. Deputy Mayor Leila Bozorg and Housing Preservation and Development Commissioner Dinah Levy expressed that developers have yet to provide sufficiently detailed underwriting to demonstrate where the program is falling short, making it premature to assess its effectiveness or propose changes.
