Commercial Real Estate Finance Is Entering the Crypto Age

Commercial Observer — Tue, 30 Jun 2026 10:30:01 +0000

SUMMARY

Opinion / Market Commentary

This article discusses the increasing integration of cryptocurrency, tokenization, and stablecoins into commercial real estate finance, driven by major financial institutions. Goldman Sachs recently partnered with Apex Group and Archax to launch a tokenized commercial real estate fund using its blockchain platform. This move follows similar initiatives by other large banks like J.P. Morgan Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, and Citibank, which are developing a shared tokenized deposit network. Experts suggest that while smaller players initially experimented with crypto finance, the growing stability and acceptance of stablecoins, which are pegged to real-world assets, are now attracting mainstream financial institutions. The Deloitte Center for Financial Services projects a significant increase in tokenized commercial real estate transactions, from less than $300 billion in 2024 to $4 trillion by 2035, indicating a modernization of real estate ownership and financing methods.

Opinion / Market Commentary

Executives from Microsoft and Oracle argue that the data center industry must fundamentally change its approach to design and development to overcome growing local opposition. SUMMARY

Empire State Development and the Atlantic Yards Community Development Corporation board have proposed a $5 billion plan to complete the Pacific Park megaproject in Brooklyn, New York City. The ambitious buildout, which has been delayed for over two decades, includes more than 5,600 residential units, with over 1,200 designated as income-restricted. The plan also features the decking over of railyards, exceeding previously approved open space requirements, and the construction of 1,000 condo units across three buildings, all to be built with union labor. A joint venture between Cirrus Real Estate Partners and LCOR, which acquired the remaining sites in a foreclosure auction, is slated to lead the development, leveraging their experience with large-scale projects and capacity to secure financing.
